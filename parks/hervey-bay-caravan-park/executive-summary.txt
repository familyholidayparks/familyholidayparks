PARK: Hervey Bay Caravan Park
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 269

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (13/20) — Reviews mention a pool and BBQ area but limited kids entertainment facilities; one review noted "pool arcade for the kids" but most describe it as a quiet park with basic amenities rather than dedicated play equipment or activities; "Would not take kids here necessarily as no facilities other than a pool for entertainment"

Nature (16/20) — Located near beaches with walking distance access (15-20 minutes), adjacent to Botanical Gardens, access to 16km esplanade bike paths, off-leash dog beach nearby, Mary to Bay Rail trail; reviewers note "relaxing sound of birds singing" and "millions of flying foxes at dusk" but not waterfront location

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding performance with consistent praise: "cleanest facilities and best kept site we've seen on the whole trip", "amenities are spotless", "immaculately clean", "amenities block is spotless", "we have never seen cleaner amenities blocks", "best I've seen on my trip around Australia"

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing; some note "rates won't break the bank balance", others mention "too expensive" for coastal parks, "reasonably priced park", one reviewer complained about $57/week storage fees and found policies restrictive; generally seen as fair but not exceptional value

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised: "spacious sites", "huge sites", "sites are a generous size", "large flat sites", "generous sized camping and caravan bays", "plenty of room for our big rig"; some noted tightness for dual axle caravans but managers help with parking

Sentiment (9/10) — Very positive with high repeat visitation: "been going for the last 7 years", "27th year in this park", "stayed here many times", "will definitely be back", "would happily stay again"; some negative reviews about previous management but recent management (Sheree, Dave, Georgia) praised extensively

Location (4/10) — Not beachfront (15-20 minute walk to beach), described as "a bit far from things, you will need to drive", "a little out of the way", but close to Aldi and Woolworths (5 minute walk), McDonald's, KFC; "tucked away off the main road" and "quiet location"; good for Fraser Island tour pickups

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited on-site kids entertainment facilities beyond a pool - no playground, waterpark, or structured activities programs that families with young children expect from top-tier holiday parks
2. Location is not beachfront and requires 15-20 minute walk or drive to reach the beach, reducing convenience for families with young children wanting easy beach access
3. Some reviews mention restrictive policies, strict rules, and past management issues that created unwelcoming experiences, plus inconsistent WiFi performance noted by several guests
