One 2023 review praised cleanliness of specific cabins, but recent reviews (2025-2026) consistently report poor maintenance. Mixed reports on bathroom cleanliness with some finding them "super clean" and others noting "very poor lighting" and missing amenities.

Value (3/15) — Poor value with "$50 per night" described as "staggering" for basic powered sites. Multiple reports of unfair charges, no refunds when guests left early due to conditions, and management "keeping your money falsely." Reviews indicate prices "not lower than beachside" despite inferior facilities.

Site Size (3/10) — One family reviewer reported inadequate sleeping arrangements with insufficient beds despite booking for specific numbers. Reception warned guests their "van was probably too big for the site allocated" after booking. Minimal evidence of spacious sites for families with large rigs.

Sentiment (2/10) — Overwhelmingly negative family sentiment with multiple warnings to "give this place a huge miss," "DO NOT STAY HERE," and "if you have a young family I would steer clear." Most recent reviews (2025-2026) consistently report rude management, particularly staff member "Sue," and hostile atmosphere from long-term residents. One glowing 5-star family review from 2023 is vastly outweighed by consistent negative experiences.

Location (6/10) — Convenient access to "transport n major shopping arcades" with Woolworths "only a few minutes away." "Great for the sports stadium" and close to Surfers Paradise. However, Gold Coast Hinterland location means not directly on beach, which may disappoint families expecting coastal access.

The park has significant cleanliness and maintenance issues with reports of rats, rundown caravans, clutter, and facilities needing upgrades that make it unsuitable for family holidays.
