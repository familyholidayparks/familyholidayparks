PARK: Discovery Parks - Airlie Beach
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-18
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 177

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Reviews consistently highlight excellent kids facilities including waterpark/splash pad, jumping pillow, playground, basketball, games room, outdoor movies, and daily lorikeet feeding at 8am. Families report children being "drawn to the waterpark" and having "lots of activities around the park." Recent upgrades to splash pad mentioned positively. Some older reviews noted tired waterpark equipment before renovation.

Nature (16/20) — Guests rave about the tropical bush setting with forest surroundings, describing it as "felt like we were in the rain forest" and waking to birdsong. Wildlife includes cockatoos, lorikeets, rainbow parrots, and water monitors. The natural environment provides a peaceful escape while being close to Whitsundays. Not beachfront but close to Airlie Beach and boat access.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Strong consensus on clean facilities with phrases like "spotless," "very clean," "well maintained." Amenities, showers, camp kitchens, and pool areas consistently praised as clean and modern. Some isolated complaints about ants, cockroaches in drawers, and specific cabin cleanliness issues (coffee stains, musty smell) prevent top marks.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback on value with many noting "reasonable prices," "Best Value for Money," and "decent value." However, several recent reviews mention expensive pricing for what's offered, with one German reviewer calling it "sehr schlecht" (very poor) value at 200+ AUD for smallest cabin. Some guests felt facilities didn't match premium pricing.

Site Size (7/10) — Positive comments about "spacious sites" and "large site" from caravan guests, though several complaints about tight sites, difficult reversing, unsuitable site sizes for larger vans (23ft van in 19ft site), and "very tight" powered sites. Some guests noted sloping sites and cramped layout in sections.

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive with high repeat visitation intent ("will definitely be back," "booked for Easter 2026 while there"). Strong family satisfaction evident. However, multiple 1-star reviews citing poor service, communication issues, refund disputes, and facility problems prevent higher score. Staff praised by name (Chris, Kristy, Ian, Amanda) frequently.

Location (5/10) — Situated on Shute Harbour Road, approximately 2.6km from Airlie Beach town centre. Bus stop directly outside park with convenient $1 day pass service receives praise. Tour pickups available. However, multiple reviews note it's "far away from everything," "a distance to the hub," and "out of the way from town." Good for Whitsundays access but not walkable to town attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Location is 2-3km from town requiring bus or car, limiting convenience for families wanting walkable beach/town access - multiple reviews specifically note this distance as a drawback.
2. Serious midge/sandfly problem extensively documented, with guests reporting being "attacked" day and night, "covered all over with bites," describing them as "outta control" and reason to "leave early" - this significantly impacts outdoor enjoyment.
3. Site size inconsistency with multiple complaints about tight, unsuitable sites for larger caravans, difficult manoeuvring, sloping sites, and only one amenities block requiring long walks from back sites (200+ metres mentioned).
