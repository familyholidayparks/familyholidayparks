PARK: Stansbury Caravan Park
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-06-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 445

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews mention a small playground and occasional live music/food truck events at Easter, but no water park, jumping pillow, or dedicated kids' entertainment facilities. Families report children enjoyed beach activities, fishing, crabbing, dolphins, pelicans, and seals more than structured entertainment. Some reviews note "limited options for children" and "not a lot for the kids" compared to modern resort parks.

Nature (19/20) — Outstanding natural environment with direct beachfront access, spectacular ocean views, and abundant wildlife including dolphins, pelicans, and resident seals at the jetty. Reviews consistently praise "right on the beach," "waterfront sites," "fishing off the beach," "beautiful coastal walks," and "amazing birdlife." Easy beach access for swimming, crabbing, and pontoon use. 

Cleanliness (15/15) — Exceptional cleanliness across all reviews. Consistent phrases include "spotlessly clean," "immaculate amenities," "always clean," "best kept park in 51 years of camping," and "facilities are brilliant & superbly maintained." Amenities, camp kitchen, cabins, and grounds all described as pristine and well-maintained.

Value (13/15) — Generally positive value comments with "well priced," "very affordable," and "reasonable rates" mentioned frequently. Some reviews note it's comparable in price to parks with pools/splash zones despite lacking those facilities. Off-peak specials praised. One negative mentioned unpowered Oyster Point site "not value for money" at peak time.

Site Size (7/10) — Mixed feedback on site size. Beachfront sites praised for views but described as "tight," "very tight fit," "smaller sites," "narrow roads," and "difficult getting on site." One 1-star review specifically cited site too small for awning. Staff offer tractor assistance for positioning vans. Some grass sites described as spacious, particularly at Oyster Point.

Sentiment (10/10) — Overwhelmingly positive sentiment with massive repeat visitation. Reviews mention returning for 15-60 years, "always coming back," "our happy place," "home away from home," "favourite caravan park," and "wouldn't stay anywhere else." Managers Anita and Ash frequently praised by name for exceptional customer service.

Location (10/10) — Perfect beachfront location with easy walking distance (5 minutes) to town shops, pub, cafe, supermarket, and jetty. Central base for exploring southern Yorke Peninsula. Reviews praise "ideal location," "fantastic location," "perfect location," and "well situated to explore." Beach access directly from sites.

WHY NOT 100:
1. No water play facilities (pool, splash park, or waterslides) and limited structured kids' entertainment compared to modern family resort parks, with only a small playground and occasional special events rather than daily activities.
2. Site size constraints with tight access, narrow roads, and some sites too small for large rigs with awnings out, particularly when parks at capacity with boats and vehicles, requiring staff tractor assistance for positioning.
3. Split facility confusion with separate Oyster Point location causing booking allocation issues, incomplete maps, and inconsistent site quality (some unpowered sites on dirt vs grass), plus wind exposure on beachfront sites requiring proper camping preparation.
