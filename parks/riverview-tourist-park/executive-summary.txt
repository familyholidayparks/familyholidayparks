PARK: Riverview Tourist Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 154

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — No dedicated kids' entertainment facilities, playground, or structured activities mentioned. Families praised canoe hire on the river and wildlife feeding opportunities (ducks, parrots) as the primary entertainment, with multiple reviews noting "canoes for hire is great for kids" and children loving to watch and feed the abundant birdlife.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding natural setting directly on Margaret River with walking trails, National Park access, and exceptional wildlife encounters. Reviews consistently highlight "surrounded by the local wildlife," "right on the river with the ducks," parrots eating from hands, and peaceful bush surroundings. Minor deduction for no beach access despite beautiful riverside location.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Generally strong cleanliness with many reviews praising "exceptionally clean and tidy cabin" and "very clean" facilities. However, multiple recent reviews note concerns: "bathroom was not very clean kinda gross around the toilet," "floors including bathroom were extremely dirty," and "BBQ pit not so clean so we never use it." Amenities blocks received consistent praise.

Value (13/15) — Good value for the region with reviews stating "reasonable value accommodation," "great value for money," and "very competitive rates." Family of four found it "very value for money." Some criticism that it's "a bit expensive for what you get" particularly for cramped camping sites, though cabin pricing considered fair.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant criticism for caravan/camping sites: "sites are very small, the whole park is very pokery they really pack everything in," "smallest I've ever seen," "tiny and crammed together," and "terrible access for vans to get into." Cabins received better feedback for being "spacious" and having adequate room.

Sentiment (8/10) — Positive overall sentiment with many repeat visitors and recommendations. Phrases like "will definitely be back," "we've been coming here for years," and "would highly recommend" common. One significant negative about "really poor customer service" on phone booking. Most guests report peaceful, relaxing stays with helpful staff.

Location (9/10) — Excellent proximity to Margaret River town with "walking distance to town" (10-15 minutes) and "2-minute drive from town center." Close to Woolworths, cafes, and restaurants. Located on river with walking trails. Beach is "only a few minutes drive away." Slight deduction for hilly walk into town mentioned by accessibility-challenged guests.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of on-site kids' entertainment facilities (no playground, no jumping pillow, no kids' club, no pool) forces reliance solely on natural activities which won't suit all families with young children.
2. Caravan and camping sites are severely undersized and cramped with multiple reviews describing them as the "smallest ever seen" with difficult access for larger rigs, creating frustration for caravanners.
3. Inconsistent maintenance standards with recurring issues including hot water systems not working properly, aging cabin infrastructure, dirty floors and bathrooms in some units, and one major customer service complaint about rude phone handling.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"kids absolutely loved watching and feeding" the ducks and birds, "canoes for hire is great for kids," "right on the river with the ducks," "sites are very small, the whole park is very pokery," "exceptionally clean and tidy cabin," "water heater not working properly," "walking distance to Margaret River main street," "reasonable value accommodation in the area"
