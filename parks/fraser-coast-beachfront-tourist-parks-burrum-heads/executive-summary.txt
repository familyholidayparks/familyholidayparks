PARK: Fraser Coast Beachfront Tourist Parks | Burrum Heads
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 190

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — No onsite kids entertainment, playground, or pool facilities mentioned across all reviews; families relied entirely on beach access and natural play opportunities with no structured activities or kids' clubs available.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding beachfront location with direct beach access, calm ocean swimming perfect for young children, beautiful sunsets, dolphin sightings, pleasant river walks at Burrum Heads location, and sheltered beach environment; "absolute beachfront," "right on the water," and "wake up to the waves" repeatedly praised.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently described as "spotless," "immaculate," "exceptionally clean" throughout recent reviews; management teams (Sean & Kym, Nigel & Fiona, Justin & Megan) praised for meticulous daily cleaning of amenities, grounds, and facilities; recent renovations elevated cleanliness standards significantly.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback with some noting "pricier" rates ($70/night mentioned) but most agreed "you get what you pay for" with quality facilities; beachfront sites at $56/night considered excellent value; some concern about paying full price during construction periods.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant concerns about tight sites, especially pre-renovation; "packed like sardines," "sites are quite small," "very hard to get van into" frequently mentioned; newer renovated areas have "quite large" and "spacious sites" but older sections remain cramped with difficult backing access.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive repeat visitation with families returning annually for 16-30+ years; management teams consistently praised as "legends," "wonderful," "fantastic"; strong loyalty despite some site allocation frustrations and construction disruptions.

Location (5/10) — This score reflects the park's ACCESSIBILITY rather than beach position (which is covered in Nature). Located at Burrum Heads/Torquay on Fraser Coast, not in a major hub; "away from the busy part of Hervey Bay" noted; shops, bakery, butcher across road convenient but limited overall town facilities; good for Fraser Island day trips but not centrally located for broader family attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of kids' entertainment facilities (no playground, pool, waterpark, or kids' club) limits appeal for families wanting structured activities beyond beach play.
2. Site size issues persist in older sections with tight, cramped spaces difficult for larger rigs, limited car parking, and challenging reversing despite helpful management assistance.
3. Road noise from esplanade traffic with "hoons racing" at night, some sites only 2-3 metres from footpath/road, and construction noise during ongoing renovations detract from the peaceful experience.
