PARK: BIG4 Tasman Holiday Parks - Nambucca Heads
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-06-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 133

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Reviews consistently praise the waterslides, jumping pillow, pool facilities, and playground that keep kids entertained for hours. Families highlight daily activities during school holidays including scavenger hunts, rock art, disco nights, and the Goanna Cave kids zone. Multiple reviews note children playing together freely across age groups, though some mention the games room has broken equipment and limited operational hours for some facilities.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional beachfront location with multiple beach access points just 50 metres from accommodation receives universal acclaim. Reviewers love the tropical vegetation, palms, shaded sites, peaceful natural surroundings, and opportunities to spot kangaroos, dolphins at sunrise, and diverse birdlife. The natural setting and beautiful white sand beach create a true coastal retreat atmosphere.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Significant concerns emerge with multiple reviews reporting maggots in toilet amenities, mould in bathrooms and on blinds, filthy AC filters, dirty pool conditions, mouse droppings, and soap buildup. While some guests found cabins and sites very clean, the inconsistency is striking with several families reporting disgraceful conditions that prompted early departures. Recent reviews suggest ongoing maintenance challenges.

Value (9/15) — Pricing generates substantial criticism with families citing $249 per night for powered sites and $179 for tent sites as excessive. Multiple reviews note the park is overpriced for the condition received, with dated cabins and poor maintenance not justifying premium BIG4 pricing. Some guests found reasonable value, but the majority express disappointment relative to cost.

Site Size (7/10) — Mixed feedback on site dimensions with some praising spacious sites with artificial grass, while others report narrow sites, tight parking between cabins, sloped sites on blind corners unsafe for young children, and difficulty maneuvering larger vans. Ensuite sites receive positive comments for space, but the park is described as "small" and "squished" by several families.

Sentiment (7/10) — Polarized guest sentiment with loyal repeat visitors contrasting sharply against families vowing "never again" after poor experiences. While many families express love for the park and intentions to return, recent negative reviews about cleanliness, water disruptions, uncomfortable beds, and management issues significantly impact overall sentiment. Staff friendliness receives consistent praise, though management practices draw criticism.

Location (9/10) — The beachfront position earns near-universal approval, with beach access steps away and a quiet, secluded setting away from traffic. Reviewers note it's 5 minutes drive from shops and cafes, making it convenient yet peaceful. Some mention it's a good halfway point between Sydney and Brisbane. A few note the distance to shops as inconvenient without a vehicle.

WHY NOT 100:
Serious and recurring cleanliness issues including maggots in amenities, mould, filthy conditions, and maintenance failures significantly compromise the guest experience and safety standards expected of a BIG4 park. Multiple water supply disruptions affecting showers and laundry, uncomfortable beds requiring replacement, and inconsistent facility maintenance (broken games room equipment, deflated jumping pillow, AC not working for 5 days) demonstrate operational challenges. Premium pricing of $179-$249 per night creates poor value perception when combined with dated, poorly maintained accommodation units that don't meet the standard implied by BIG4 branding and cost.
