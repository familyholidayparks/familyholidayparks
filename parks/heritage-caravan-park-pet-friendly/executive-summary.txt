PARK: Heritage Caravan Park - Pet Friendly
SCORE: 48/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-19
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 70

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Very limited on-site kids entertainment; reviews mention a pool that kids enjoyed but note playground equipment is often shut or closed. One family mentioned "The kids loved the pool size was in it every day" but minimal structured activities for children. No waterpark, kids club, or organized entertainment mentioned.

Nature (11/20) — Decent natural setting with "nice shady lawns," "large bushy sites," "great walk through the bush section," and grounds described as "birdy." Located just out of Alice Springs town center providing some natural buffer, though not near significant natural attractions like beaches or creeks.

Cleanliness (7/15) — Highly inconsistent cleanliness standards across reviews. Recent negative reviews cite "mouldy dirty," "disgusting" fridges, "bedsheet unwashed," "big stains on sheets," and mold issues. However, multiple positive reviews note "clean facilities," "very clean amenities," and "everything is so clean." Amenities are "old but clean" according to several guests, suggesting dated but maintained communal areas.

Value (9/15) — Mixed value perception. Multiple reviews praise "very reasonable price," "great prices," "affordable," and "budget-friendly." However, recent cabin guests felt "ripped off" at "$140 per night," describing price as "ridiculous for what we got." Value appears better for caravan sites than cabin accommodation.

Site Size (2/10) — Minimal family site size evidence. One review noted "unpowered site was spacious," another mentioned "large bushy sites" and "excellent wide caravan sites, level, grassed." However, powered sites described as "very long and narrow," and cabins/dongas are "very small" shipping containers. Limited evidence of family-sized, spacious sites.

Sentiment (6/10) — Polarized sentiment with many repeat visitors but significant recent negative experiences. Positive reviews show loyalty: "always stay here," "been here 6 times," "stayed here a few times." However, multiple recent 1-star reviews state "won't be staying here again," "didn't even stay," "worst night of my life." Management transitions appear to have affected consistency.

Location (7/10) — Good out-of-town location about 5-10km from Alice Springs CBD, described as "away from all the issues," "quiet part of Alice Springs," and "just out of town." Walking distance to Alice Springs Brewing Co. However, no proximity to beaches, major attractions, or natural landmarks beyond basic town access.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severely limited kids entertainment and recreation facilities - playground frequently closed, no kids club, minimal organized activities, and only basic pool amenities fail to meet family holiday needs.
2. Major cleanliness and maintenance issues in cabins - multiple recent reviews document mold, unwashed sheets, disgusting fridges, stained bedding, sewerage smells, and poor cabin conditions that are unacceptable for families with young children.
3. Dated, cramped accommodation unsuitable for families - cabins described as "old disgusting" shipping container dongas that are "very small," with poor quality beds, collapsing floors, and inadequate space for family stays.
