PARK: Hydeaway Bay Caravan & Camping Park
SCORE: 52/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-06-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 126

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — No kids entertainment, no playground, no activities. Multiple reviews state "not family friendly at all", "don't bring kids", children told off for normal play. Management actively discourages children with rules against splashing, noise, playing. One family reported kids made to cry. This is not a park for families with young children.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting in the Whitsundays with "pristine beaches", "beautiful white sandy beaches close by", "crystal clear water". Walking distance to Hideaway Bay beach and Dingo Beach. "Amazing bird life" noted. Quiet bush setting away from crowds. Beach access and natural environment are the park's strongest assets.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Generally "immaculately kept", "spotless amenities", "very very clean". Camp kitchen described as "amazing, chef standards". However, some reviews mention "bathrooms are so dirty", "not really clean and a bit old", inconsistent hot water in showers, and lack of hand towels/dryers. Maintenance standards appear variable.

Value (6/15) — Pricing criticized as "ridiculous" at $43-$63 for unpowered sites with limited facilities. Multiple reviews call it "very expensive" and "not worth it" given the unwelcoming atmosphere. "Miles from everything" with no shops nearby. Poor value for families given the hostile environment and lack of activities.

Site Size (9/10) — "Large sites", "spacious", "big sites", "plenty of room to set up". Mix of grass and concrete slabs. Sites described as "well kept" and "drive through". Some powered sites "crammed in like sardines" but unpowered sites are generous. Road noise noted on some sites.

Sentiment (2/10) — Extremely polarized reviews with recent family travellers overwhelmingly negative. "Worst caravan park I have ever stayed at", "would rather sleep in the car", "will never be back", "avoid this place". Management described as "rude", "aggressive", "arrogant", treating guests "like criminals". Many families report being watched, filmed, yelled at. Repeat visitors tend to be older guests without children.

Location (2/10) — While naturally beautiful, location is remote with nearest shops 20-30 minutes away in Proserpine. "Miles from everything", limited dining options nearby. 40+ minutes from Airlie Beach. Beautiful beaches are the only nearby attraction. Isolation limits family appeal despite natural beauty.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Management style is toxic for families—multiple reviews document aggressive enforcement of excessive rules, owners yelling at children, filming guests, creating a "detention center" atmosphere that ruins holidays for families with kids.
2. Zero entertainment or activities—no playground, no kids club, no organized activities, and children actively discouraged from normal play (no splashing, no noise, strict curfews, told off for riding bikes).
3. Poor value and remote location—expensive for what's offered ($43-$63 unpowered), 30+ minutes from shops/supplies, very limited nearby attractions beyond beaches, strict cancellation policy with no compassion for illness.
