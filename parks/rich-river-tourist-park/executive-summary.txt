PARK: Rich River Tourist Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 101

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note this is a quiet park with limited children's entertainment. There is a games room with pool tables and table tennis mentioned, but multiple reviews explicitly state "no big park extras" and "probably not as good for families/kids." No mention of playgrounds, jumping pillows, or organized kids activities.

Nature (14/20) — The park benefits from proximity to the Murray River with "short walk to the river" and "2 min walk down to the river" mentioned frequently. Reviews highlight "pleasant flower/shrub gardens," "well looked after gardens," and access to riverside walking areas. However, the nearby abattoir creates occasional odour issues that detract from the natural experience.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed reviews on cleanliness. Many praise "spotless amenities," "immaculately clean cabin," and "clean facilities," while others report significant concerns: "amenities are very bad condition," "pool with a brown scum line," dated facilities needing renovation, and maintenance issues in some cabins including dirty microwaves and torn flyscreens.

Value (11/15) — Reviews mention "good nightly rates," "very well priced," "great value," and "reasonable price." However, one review states "price over the roof" and suggests spending more money elsewhere for a proper holiday destination rather than just an overnight stop.

Site Size (8/10) — Reviews consistently mention "spacious sites," "generous sites," "big sites," and "plenty of room." The artificial grass covering is praised repeatedly. One review notes sites "can be a little tight to get into" and another mentions the park is "getting a bit small for the large houses that people drag around these days."

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation with many regulars saying "we have been coming for several years," "always a pleasant experience," and "our first stop whenever we are in town." However, sentiment is heavily skewed toward pet owners and couples rather than families with young children. Some critical reviews express disappointment for those seeking a holiday destination.

Location (8/10) — The park is 2km from Echuca town centre ("couple of minutes from the centre of town" by car, "40 min walk" on foot). Close to the Murray River and port area. Reviews note it's "close to everything" but "further from town than we thought" for walking to pubs. Quiet location away from main roads praised, though proximity to abattoir is a negative.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severely limited children's entertainment and activities - multiple reviews explicitly state this park is not suitable for families with kids, with no playground equipment, no waterpark features, and minimal organized activities beyond a basic games room.
2. Significant cleanliness and maintenance concerns - while some areas are praised, multiple reviews from 2024-2026 cite "very bad condition" amenities needing renovation, pool maintenance issues, dated facilities, and cabin maintenance problems including dirty appliances and broken fixtures.
3. Unpleasant odours from adjacent abattoir - several reviews mention the smell from the nearby slaughterhouse as a significant nuisance "when the wind blows your way," which fundamentally impacts the holiday experience for families.
