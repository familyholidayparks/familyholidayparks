PARK: River Retreat Home & Holiday Park
SCORE: 51/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-03-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 58

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (3/20) — Reviews consistently note there is no entertainment for children, with management specifically telling guests "This is NOT a fun park." The park has a "very small pool" and no playground, kids club, or activities. Multiple families with children (8-10 years) reported feeling unwelcome and constantly watched, with virtually no facilities to keep kids entertained.

Nature (14/20) — The park sits alongside the Tweed River with "lovely walk along the river through mangrove forests" and fishing opportunities "straight across the road." Guests encountered kookaburras, bush lizards, ibis, and black swans. However, the natural experience is severely compromised by constant aircraft noise as the park is "directly under the flight path" with planes "flying quite low" and "overhead every 5 to 10 minutes."

Cleanliness (12/15) — The amenities receive consistently positive feedback as "very clean," "spotless," "extremely clean," and "brilliantly clean and new." Reviews repeatedly praise the "lovely and clean" bathrooms, showers, and laundry facilities. Some sites had maintenance issues with "tree stumps scattered throughout," "pile of garden waste and rubble," and "overflowing bins," plus poor drainage creating "at least 3 inches of water" after rain.

Value (8/15) — Most reviews mention "reasonable prices" and describe it as "great value for money" at around $50-57 per night. However, value is undermined by strict no-refund policies that left multiple guests disappointed, extra hidden fees ($25 fines for not emptying bins), insufficient towels requiring paid laundry use, and the overall lack of facilities compared to typical holiday parks.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews are mixed with some praising "massive sites," "huge sites," and "roomy and level" powered sites with concrete slabs, while others complained of "cramped" conditions, "small sites making manoeuvring your rig difficult," sites where "hoses and cables simply couldn't reach" utilities, and placement under "large, older gum trees" with falling branches that damaged one guest's awning.

Sentiment (3/10) — Family sentiment is overwhelmingly negative with multiple reviews stating they "will not be returning," "would not recommend," and "worst caravan park we stayed at." Families with children felt "constantly watched," "intimated," and "under constant surveillance." However, some couples and solo travellers without children had positive experiences. The hostile reception, particularly from the female manager described as "rudest manager we have ever experienced" and "so rude," severely damaged family sentiment.

Location (4/10) — Located on the Tweed Coast near Tweed Heads South, the park is "close to Tweed Mall and Tweed City Shopping Centres" and a "short drive to Coolangatta" and beaches. However, being "straight under flight path from airport" with constant low-flying aircraft noise is a major detrimental factor. The riverside location is peaceful but "off the beaten track" and the constant plane noise makes it feel less like a holiday destination.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Extremely poor customer service and unwelcoming management attitude toward families with children - multiple reviews describe being treated rudely, feeling "constantly watched," receiving lectures about rules, and being told outright "This is NOT a fun park," creating a hostile rather than hospitable environment that drove families away.

2. Zero entertainment facilities for children - no playground, no kids club, no activities, just a very small pool, making this completely unsuitable for families seeking a holiday park experience, with children having nothing to do and parents spending time "trying not to be at the campsite."

3. Severe and constant aircraft noise from Gold Coast Airport flight path - planes flying "quite low over the park" every "5 to 10 minutes" during the day creates an unrelaxing environment that "honestly felt like we were just off the end of a runway," fundamentally undermining the peaceful holiday atmosphere families seek.
