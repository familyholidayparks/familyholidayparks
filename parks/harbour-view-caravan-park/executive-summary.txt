PARK: Harbour View Caravan Park
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-03-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 26

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Multiple recent reviews indicate the pool has safety tape around it and is falling apart, with one visitor stating "pool lets not go there." No evidence of kids' club, playground, waterpark, or organized activities for children.

Nature (12/20) — Located in Hervey Bay with harbour views and proximity to Fraser Island (K'gari) boat access. Several reviews mention the location is ideal for Fraser Island tours and whale watching excursions, with marina adjacent. Not a natural bushland or beach-front setting.

Cleanliness (6/15) — Mixed feedback with recent reviews noting "toilets are cleaned every day" but also "Everything is falling apart," "Old and tired facilities," and one detailed 2023 review describing "camp kitchen does NOT get attended to and is broken, filthy." Grounds described as "not well looked after."

Value (10/15) — Consistently noted as "the cheapest park in town" with rates around $45-60 per night. Reviews acknowledge "great rates" but question value with comments like "Price $60 for what!!" Several note it's acceptable for the low price but facilities don't match expectations.

Site Size (8/10) — Positive mentions of "Huge camp-sites" and "plenty of room," with sites described as "easy to get in to." One review noted difficulty parking big rigs, but overall space receives favorable comments.

Sentiment (4/10) — Recent 2025-2026 reviews heavily weighted show declining satisfaction with multiple 1-star reviews citing rude staff, management issues, and one family cancelling due to feeling unwelcome. Earlier reviews more positive but trend is concerning. Several "would not return" statements.

Location (8/10) — Excellent positioning next to boat harbour and marina with "1 minute walk" to boat club and easy Fraser Island tour access. However, noted as "a bit far from the actual town" and noise complaints about adjacent fish co-op affecting amenity.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment facilities with pool in disrepair and safety tape visible, providing no recreational options for children staying at the park.
2. Significant cleanliness and maintenance issues with facilities described as "very run down," "old and tired," and "falling apart" across multiple recent reviews, indicating poor upkeep.
3. Concerning customer service problems with multiple recent reviews citing rude staff who hang up on customers, unwelcoming management, and a family cancelling because they "didn't feel comfortable bringing our family" due to being made to feel like an inconvenience.
