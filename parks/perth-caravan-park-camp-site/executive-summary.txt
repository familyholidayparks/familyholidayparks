PARK: Perth Caravan Park & Camp Site
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 42

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews highlight an on-site playground described as "clean and safe" and "one of the best in scottish sites," with exceptional off-site options including Wonder World soft play centre, Air Thrill, mini golf, and a golf driving range all within walking distance. However, on-site entertainment is limited to the playground, requiring families to leave the park for activities.

Nature (11/20) — Guests appreciate watching "lots of wild rabbits" and access to a dog park next door with "lots of walks for friendly dogs." The location lacks significant natural beauty, being situated on an industrial estate "under electrical pylons" near the A9, though some found it "quiet" with green spaces for football.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Recent reviews praise cleanliness with "spotlessly clean and tidy" facilities and "lovely clean toilets and showers," especially post-renovation. However, multiple 2024 reviews cite "grass not cut," "uneven and potted pathways," and one extreme complaint about "disgusting" dishwashing facilities with bins not emptied, showing inconsistent maintenance standards.

Value (12/15) — Families describe it as "amazing value" with "great price" for basic camping, though one review noted "high surcharge for electric connection." Most consider it good value as a stopover, though the limited on-site amenities and occasional maintenance issues affect perceived value.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviewers report "well spaced" pitches with an "8m unit easy to get in and out" and "nice open space." Negative reviews mention "tiny untidy pitch" and crowded conditions during peak times with units "touching" each other, suggesting inconsistent pitch allocation.

Sentiment (4/10) — Sentiment is deeply polarized: enthusiastic families call it a "favorite site" they'll return to, while others declare "WONT BE BACK" and "worst campsite yet." Three 1-star reviews in mid-2024 cite serious issues with travellers on site making families "not feel comfortable," significantly impacting family sentiment. Recent 2026 reviews show improvement.

Location (3/10) — The park's proximity to the A9 creates noise issues, with multiple reviews stating "difficult to sleep" and "motorway noise" though some noted "no noise at all." Bus access to Perth city centre is convenient ("15 minutes away"), but the industrial estate setting lacks appeal for destination holidays.

WHY NOT 100:
The park lost significant points for inconsistent maintenance and cleanliness standards, with reviews ranging from "spotlessly clean" to "grass not cut" and "disgusting" dishwashing facilities within the same year. Location next to the A9 dual carriageway creates noise disturbances that multiple families found disruptive to sleep. The presence of travellers housed by council in mid-2024 created safety concerns that made multiple family reviewers explicitly state they "didn't feel comfortable" and wouldn't return.
