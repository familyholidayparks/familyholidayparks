PARK: Whitsunday Holiday Parks - Lake Proserpine
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REWIEWS ANALYSED: 239

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently highlight the free access to the adjacent Olympic-sized heated pool and waterpark as the main attraction for kids. Families mention children swimming in the lake, catching red claws off the bank, and riding bikes around the park. However, there is no dedicated playground or organized kids activities on-site, limiting the entertainment score.

Nature (17/20) — Guests praise the beautiful natural setting beside Lake Proserpine with stunning sunsets over the water, abundant birdlife including swans and ducks, and peaceful surroundings. The lakeside location offers fishing, swimming off the pontoon, and wildlife viewing. Some reviews mention dust and windy conditions, and the lake setting lacks beach access.

Cleanliness (15/15) — This park receives exceptional praise for cleanliness with multiple reviews stating "cleanest amenities I have ever seen," "spotless amenities," and "immaculate facilities." Reviewers consistently highlight the spotless showers, toilets, camp kitchens, and well-maintained grounds as standout features.

Value (14/15) — Families appreciate the excellent value with powered sites at $34-46 per night and unpowered at $10-20 per person, especially considering the free pool access. Multiple reviews mention "great value for money," "very reasonable rates," and note it's significantly cheaper than staying in Airlie Beach while offering comparable or better facilities.

Site Size (9/10) — Reviews consistently mention "big sites," "plenty of space," "large level sites," and "great sized unpowered sites." Families with caravans note adequate room for their setups, though some mention sites are close together in busier periods.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overall sentiment is very positive with numerous repeat visitors and strong recommendations. Phrases like "will definitely be back," "would 100% recommend," and guests extending their stays appear frequently. A few negative reviews about rude staff and noise issues prevent a perfect score.

Location (0/10) — The park loses significant points for location as it's 20-40 minutes drive from Airlie Beach and the Whitsundays attractions. Multiple reviews note you "have to drive for eateries and shops" and it's "a decent distance away on the opposite side of the highway" from main tourist areas.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited on-site entertainment - no playground, organized activities, or kids club; entertainment relies entirely on the adjacent public pool which isn't directly operated by the park.
2. Remote location requiring 20-40 minute drives to reach Airlie Beach, Whitsundays attractions, restaurants, and major shopping areas significantly impacts convenience for families.
3. Several reviews mention issues with staff ("rude staff," "bullying jerk," "half drunk manager"), noise disturbances late at night with no oversight, and inconsistent management policies that detract from the otherwise excellent facilities.
