PARK: NRMA Norah Head Holiday Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-23
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 107

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently mention pool, jumping pillow, and playground as core attractions. School holiday programming included movie nights, pancake vans, coffee vans, and outdoor cinema. However, recent feedback indicates "major decrease in games and activities for young kids" since management changes, with one reviewer noting "little to no games or event for them anymore." Bike/trike hire available but overall entertainment offerings described as "basic" and "dated" compared to other parks.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional coastal location with "short stroll to iconic Norah Head Lighthouse," "beautiful beaches," and "stunning ocean views." Wildlife includes bush turkeys, rabbits, and kookaburras creating a "peaceful" natural atmosphere. Walking distance to Soldiers Beach and rock pools. National park walks nearby. Only minor deduction for lack of shade trees mentioned by some reviewers.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed results with significant variation. Positive reviews praise "spotless amenities," "very clean facilities," and "clean modern amenities." However, concerning recent complaints include "dish rack mouldy and unclean," "floors looked like they had never been vacuumed," "bathroom full of hair," and cleaners signing off sheets before completing work. Some amenity blocks described as "old but clean" while others noted maintenance issues.

Value (12/15) — Generally considered good value at $117/night for cabins sleeping 6, with "amazing value for our family" mentioned. However, price inconsistencies noted (jumped from $33 to $45 in same month), powered sites "maybe slightly overpriced," and concerns about being charged "full price" during service interruptions. Overall "great value for money" for location but some pricing transparency issues.

Site Size (8/10) — Sites described as "very spacious," "huge camp sites," and "big" with generous space. However, some sites "uneven" with slopes that could cause water runoff issues. Infrastructure placement problematic on some sites where "water hose and power cord could not reach." One unpowered site poorly located beside bins. Generally good size but layout inconsistencies.

Sentiment (7/10) — Strong repeat visitation with many "will definitely be back" comments and families visiting multiple times yearly. However, recent sentiment declining with complaints about booking cancellations, aggressive staff incidents, management responsiveness issues, and "NO RESPONSE to our complaint." Security concerns mentioned with theft and unauthorized access. Mixed staff experiences ranging from "lovely and helpful" to "very abusive and aggressive."

Location (10/10) — Perfect marks for location: 90 minutes from Sydney, walking distance to beaches (10 min), adjacent to Norah Heads Sports Club, near lighthouse, cafes, and restaurants. "Amazing location," "awesome location," and "perfect location" repeatedly mentioned. Easy beach access and proximity to attractions consistently praised.

WHY NOT 100:
Cleanliness standards have become inconsistent with recent reports of mouldy items, uncleaned surfaces, and maintenance shortcuts, suggesting quality control issues since management changes. Entertainment and activities have been significantly reduced according to multiple recent reviews, with families noting disappointment in the decrease of kids programs that previously made the park special. Customer service and operational problems have emerged including booking cancellations without notice, unresponsive management, aggressive staff incidents, and security concerns with unauthorized park access.
