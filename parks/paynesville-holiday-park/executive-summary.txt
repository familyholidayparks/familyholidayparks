PARK: Paynesville Holiday Park
SCORE: 84/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-09
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 123

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (15/20) — Reviews consistently praise the "amazing swimming pool and spa" with warm water year-round, excellent games room with table tennis and billiards, and playground with climbing equipment. Families note "best pool setup we've had for a family with two young kids" and "splash area and large spa the highlight of the park." Pool closes at 8pm which some found limiting.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding proximity to Raymond Island for koala spotting, described as "5-10 minute walk to free ferry" and "perfect stop to explore the Koala trail." Beautiful gardens with "immaculate rose bushes" and "loads of well cared for trees." Waterfront location with "lovely walk through town to see the Koalas in the evening."

Cleanliness (15/15) — Exceptional scores with reviews using "spotless," "immaculate," "pristine," and "cleanest caravan park you will ever come across." Facilities described as "super clean," "exceptionally clean and tidy," with "some of the neatest toilets of any caravan park."

Value (14/15) — Strong value perception with "great value for money," "very reasonable priced," and "good value for money overall." Multiple families note "rates are some of the better we've seen these days" and appreciate inclusions like towels and linen in cabins.

Site Size (7/10) — Mixed feedback on site size. Drive-through sites described as "fairly narrow" and "on the smaller side," though "wide enough for van and awning." Some noted "not enough room to open our awning" while others found sites "spacious." Reverse-in sites appear larger.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with high return rates. Families report "second time," "third time," "been here a few times" with phrases like "will definitely be back," "can't wait to come back," and "already booked another week stay." One significant negative review about staff behaviour towards pool guests.

Location (10/10) — Perfect scores for location accessibility. "5-minute walk into town," "short stroll to main street and ferry," "walking distance to Paynesville shops." Close to attractions, restaurants, IGA, and "easy access to Raymond Island for koala spotting."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site sizes are inconsistent with drive-through sites described as "fairly narrow" and some guests unable to open awnings, limiting appeal for larger rigs and families wanting more space.
2. Entertainment facilities close at 8pm which restricted usage for families out exploring during the day, and no jumping pillow despite some earlier advertising (one negative review noted this specifically).
3. One serious customer service incident where staff "yelled at" non-guests attempting to use the pool, handled poorly according to the reviewer, plus isolated issues with hot water temperature control in cabins.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
