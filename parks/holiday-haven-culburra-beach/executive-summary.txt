PARK: Holiday Haven Culburra Beach
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 65

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (18/20) — Exceptional family facilities with splash park, heated pool, jumping pillow, flying fox, mini golf, basketball court, and modern playground consistently praised. Peak season organized activities like paint pouring and tie-dye sessions. One family has returned 11 consecutive years specifically for the entertainment. Kids described as "in heaven" and "never a dull moment."

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding beachfront location with direct beach access 2-5 minutes from sites. Culburra surf beach with lifeguards during peak season, Crookhaven estuary with boat ramps nearby. Stunning sunrises and sunsets repeatedly mentioned. Beautiful natural surroundings with birds and serenity. Easy walk to lighthouse and headland beaches.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed feedback with strong positive commentary about well-maintained grounds, shiny BBQs, and dedicated cleaners, but several concerning reports during peak periods. Two 1-star reviews cited "absolutely disgusting" amenities with bodily fluids and vomit during New Year's. Majority praise cleanliness outside holiday peaks. One reviewer noted 4 staff cleaning daily.

Value (13/15) — Described as "cheap prices, full value" and "very competitive with their pricing." Powered sites at $65/night mentioned. However, some felt $100/night expensive for cramped conditions. Overall consensus is "affordable escape" with extensive facilities justifying costs. Loyalty program offers 2pm checkout.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive with "good sized sites," "great size," and "loads of room for van, car and kids" mentioned frequently. Ensuite sites described as spacious. However, one couple left immediately citing sites "absolutely crammed in with zero privacy." Tent sites received criticism for feeling like "someone else's backyard." Some sites slightly sloped.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with remarkable loyalty (one family 11 years running). Multiple "will definitely be back" and "highlight of our year" comments. However, 2-3 guests left early or expressed regret, preventing a perfect score. Strong repeat visitation and enthusiastic recommendations dominate.

Location (9/10) — Excellent proximity to beach, boat ramp "couple hundred metres," and estuary. Central base for exploring Shoalhaven region. However, 5-minute drive to shops, and shopping in Nowra recommended. Not walking distance to restaurants. Some mention Culburra itself is quiet/remote. Internet/mobile coverage poor (Optus particularly bad).

WHY NOT 100:
Cleanliness standards deteriorate significantly during peak holiday periods with multiple reports of disgusting amenities including vomit and bodily fluids that drove guests to leave early. Site density creates cramped conditions for some visitors with one couple immediately departing after paying $100 citing zero privacy and half the park being a permanent trailer park. Internet and mobile connectivity is severely limited with non-functional WiFi and poor Optus coverage, making it unsuitable for families needing connectivity.
