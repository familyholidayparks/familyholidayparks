PARK: Anglesea Family Caravan Park
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 132

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Strong family facilities including jumping pillow, multiple playgrounds, mini-golf, pool with rock waterfall, and games room consistently mentioned; some criticism that facilities close early (around 5pm) and games room is outdated with limited appeal; toddler room noted as a positive addition.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural setting with kangaroos, wallabies, ducks, cockatoos, and parrots throughout grounds; direct beach access and river frontage; shaded native tree sites; "mobs of roos," "kangaroos lying next to our tents," and "picturesque bush settings" frequently praised; only park in Anglesea with both river and beach frontage.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with recent reviews noting mold in bathrooms, amenities needing more regular cleaning at peak times, toilet blocks closed 8am-10am for cleaning causing inconvenience; older reviews praised cleanliness; "amenities very clean" versus "amenities widely spaced, dirty, lights not working" shows inconsistency; facilities described as "bit old but clean."

Value (10/15) — Significant concerns about pricing with multiple reviews noting "prices have gone thru the roof," "$93-$149 per night for powered sites" considered high; "way overpriced," "outrageous mid week price of $69," and inflexible late checkout policies ($50 extra) criticized; premium sites not delivering value with "dirt and sand" instead of grass; peak period pricing especially questioned.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive with "sites are big," "large space for caravans," "good size site," and "21' van and car fit easy" noted; however, premium sites disappointing with "very small sites with narrow roads" and uneven ground; some sites described as cramped together while others spacious; dog-friendly sites "down the back" needing tidying up.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation indicated ("we come every year," "will absolutely be back," "2nd stay") but tempered by recent negative experiences; families love the location and wildlife but frustrated by pricing, cleanliness lapses, and service issues; "perfect family spot" versus "won't be coming back" shows polarization in recent 2024-2026 reviews.

Location (10/10) — Unanimously praised as "perfect location," "walking distance to beach, shops and river," "3-5 minutes to surf beach," "under 10 minutes to town centre"; "start of the Great Ocean Road," "central location," and "hardly used the car at all" consistently mentioned; beachfront position and proximity to amenities scored maximum points.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Cleanliness and maintenance standards have declined with multiple 2024-2026 reviews reporting mold in bathrooms, amenities not cleaned during peak periods, toilet blocks closed for cleaning during prime morning hours (8-10am), and facilities described as "run down" with lights not working and limited soap dispensers.

2. Value proposition significantly eroded with powered sites ranging $93-$149/night off-peak considered excessive, premium grass sites delivering "dirt and sand" instead of advertised grass, only one dump point for entire large park, inflexible late checkout policies charging $50 extra, and long weekend minimum stays of 4 nights creating affordability barriers.

3. Infrastructure limitations including very small pool that "don't be misled by photos," no proper sewer connections with pipes on ground, poor lighting creating safety concerns at night, locked gates blocking advertised access to river/beach, kitchen utensils removed due to theft, inconsistent hot water, and WiFi described as "inexistent" or "pretty bad."
