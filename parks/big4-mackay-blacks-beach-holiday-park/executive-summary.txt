PARK: BIG4 Mackay Blacks Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 110

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (15/20) — Families consistently praise the "nice pool" and "water play area for the little kids" with multiple reviews mentioning children having "a ball in the pool" and the "splash pool for kids is a big hit." Activities include fishing, ping pong, beach play, and peddle carts. The park offers a relaxed atmosphere where "kids run around and just be kids" though lacks structured entertainment programs.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural setting with direct beach access being the standout feature. Reviews repeatedly highlight being "right on the beach," "beachfront sites," and "pristine beach" walks. Families love the "stunning sunrise views," beach fishing, and "beautiful ocean views." The coastal environment with shaded trees creates a "relax and enjoyable environment."

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently rated as "very clean," "spotless," and "well maintained" throughout recent reviews. Amenities described as "extremely clean," "super clean," and "clean and tidy." Minor points lost for one 2025 review noting "maintenance is not a high priority" and another mentioning facilities "looking tired" though still clean.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising reasonable rates, but several reviews note cabins are "very pricey" and "very expensive." One guest mentioned "prices double in the holidays" and powered sites being "a little pricey." The beachfront location provides strong value proposition, but accommodation costs concern some families.

Site Size (7/10) — Described as a "small park" and "smaller caravan park" with "compact" sites. Multiple reviews mention drive-through sites being "stacked in" and "very tight" with difficulty maneuvering caravans. However, beachfront sites receive praise for being "very long" with adequate space. The compact nature is noted but not universally problematic.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with families reporting "we extended our stay," "we will be back," and "return to each year." Strong recommendation rate with phrases like "highly recommend," "would definitely stay again," and guests who "kept extending our stay." A few negative experiences with reception staff prevent perfect score.

Location (7/10) — Excellent beach access but trade-offs with town proximity. Located "about 4kms" from shopping with "20mins to centre of Mackay." Reviews note it's "quite a long way from Mackay cbd" requiring driving for supplies. The quiet residential beachfront setting is praised, though convenience to amenities is limited.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site size constraints with drive-through bays being "very tight" and "stacked in" making maneuvering difficult for larger rigs, limiting space for awnings and reducing privacy between sites.
2. Value concerns with multiple families noting cabins are "very expensive" and "very pricey," plus holiday period price doubling, making it less accessible for budget-conscious families.
3. Several service complaints about reception staff being "quite rude," "unfriendly," and "dismissive," with specific negative experiences documented in 2024-2025 affecting the family-friendly atmosphere.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"right on the beach," "kids had a ball in the pool," "water play area for the little kids," "amenities were spotless," "happy atmosphere with kids laughing and families enjoying themselves," "splash pool for kids is a big hit," "beachfront site," "we extended our stay longer than planned"
