PARK: Ingenia Holidays Kinka Beach Yeppoon
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 106

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Families praised the jumping pillow, two pools (one adults-only), Easter Bunny visits, outdoor movies with popcorn, games room with pool table, and ice cream van visits. Some noted games room equipment wasn't always working and entertainment offerings were more limited than larger resort parks.

Nature (17/20) — Park is directly across the road from Kinka Beach with easy beach access, surrounded by lush gardens, mature trees providing shade, interesting vegetation, and wildlife including peacocks and chickens roaming. Beach has mudflats at low tide but beautiful at high tide. Close to Great Keppel Island ferry and Emu Park.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelming praise for "spotless amenities", "crystal clean pools", "almost up to standard for heart surgery" cleanliness from dedicated cleaners Casey and Kim. However, some recent reviews noted cabin floors weren't mopped, one toilet/shower block not cleaned all week, and laundry facilities had issues with pet hair in dryers.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback: many found it "value for money" at $48-50/night for families, with 2 nights get 1 free deals. However, some felt $70+/night for powered sites was expensive given amenities, with complaints about pricey gas refills, washing machines, and minimum night policies during peak periods.

Site Size (8/10) — Reviews vary significantly: many praised "huge sites", "big sites", "spacious sites", but multiple families complained about extremely narrow sites ("500mm wider than van", "very tightly fitted", "cramped like sardines"), particularly sites that had been subdivided. Site allocation appears inconsistent.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong positive sentiment with many "will definitely return" comments, extended stays (6 nights became 30, 2 days became 6), and "nicest caravan park in 6 months of travel" testimonials. However, 6-8 very negative 1-star reviews citing rude staff, lack of empathy, and poor customer service during ownership transition to Ingenia.

Location (10/10) — Perfect beachfront location across the road from Kinka Beach, short drive to both Yeppoon and Emu Park (10 minutes), close to Great Keppel Island ferry, handy to shops/cafes/restaurants, yet "out of the hustle and bustle" for a peaceful stay.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Inconsistent site sizing with some families allocated cramped, narrow sites while larger sites remained vacant, and subdivided sites causing space issues for families with larger rigs.
2. Recent cleanliness concerns under new Ingenia management including cabin floors not being mopped, toilet blocks not cleaned regularly, and laundry facilities with excessive pet hair despite signage.
3. Limited kids' entertainment compared to larger holiday parks (no jumping pillow found in 2023, games room equipment not working, minimal activities at night), and several 1-star reviews citing very rude staff and poor customer service particularly around refund/booking flexibility issues.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"spotless amenities almost up to standard for heart surgery", "kids loved the jumping pillow pool and Easter Bunny", "outdoor movies with popcorn were great", "crystal clean pools", "directly across the road from the beach", "value for money highly recommended", "very tightly fitted in like sardines", "rude staff avoid at all costs"
