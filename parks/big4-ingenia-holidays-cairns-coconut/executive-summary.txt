PARK: BIG4 Ingenia Holidays Cairns Coconut
SCORE: 88/100
DATE SCORED: 2025
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 359

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (19/20) — Reviews consistently praise the exceptional range of activities including multiple pools with water slides, splash park with giant bucket, jumping pillows, mini golf, basketball and tennis courts, pedal carts, outdoor cinema, and gym. Families note "so much to do we didn't leave," "kids paradise," and "everything you could want." Free activities include pancake Thursday mornings, aqua aerobics, snorkelling lessons, and kids entertainment programs during school holidays.

Nature (12/20) — The park features tropical landscaping with palm trees creating a pleasant aesthetic, but is located next to the highway without bush, beach or creek access. Reviews mention "beautiful grounds," "tropical feel," and "lush and inviting" landscaping. Mountain views are visible from some areas. The nature score is moderate as it's a manicured resort environment rather than natural bushland.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Outstanding cleanliness noted across hundreds of reviews with terms like "spotlessly clean," "immaculate," "pristine," and "not a leaf out of place." Amenities, pools, camp kitchens, and cabins are consistently described as very well maintained. A few isolated reviews mention cockroach issues in cabins or minor cleaning oversights, preventing a perfect score.

Value (12/15) — Reviews are mixed on value with many noting it's expensive but worth it for the facilities. Prices range from $300-500+ per night for cabins. Families appreciate that most activities are free once on-site. Some reviews mention it's "not the cheapest" but "good value considering all the activities and facilities available." A few feel it's overpriced for the accommodation quality.

Site Size (9/10) — Caravan sites are described as "spacious," "large," "massive," and "roomy" with good spacing between sites and trees providing privacy. Angle parking makes access easier for big rigs. Some reviews note sites are "on the small side" or "packed in," and the park is very large requiring walking distances to facilities.

Sentiment (10/10) — Exceptional sentiment with families returning annually for 10+ years, describing it as "the best park" they've stayed at and already planning return visits. Overwhelmingly positive with phrases like "can't wait to come back," "would never stay anywhere else," and "will definitely be back." High recommendation rate to friends and family.

Location (8/10) — Located 10-15 minutes drive from central Cairns, close to airport and shopping centres. Convenient highway access but not within walking distance to city attractions. Reviews note "easy access to Cairns," "close to everything you need," but mention needing a car. Highway noise is noted in some reviews. Bus stop at entrance is a positive.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Highway noise and exhaust fumes affect some sites, with multiple reviews mentioning traffic noise and the park being "right next to the highway" - some guests found it disruptive especially in tents.
2. Several concerning security incidents including car thefts, bikes stolen, and reports of people looking through windows at night, with reviews noting "theft is a big problem at this park" and security presence needs improvement.
3. WiFi and mobile signal are severely limited with multiple reviews stating internet "does not work," speeds are "capped," and mobile signal "grinds to a halt" in evenings, making remote work or staying connected difficult despite being advertised as available.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
