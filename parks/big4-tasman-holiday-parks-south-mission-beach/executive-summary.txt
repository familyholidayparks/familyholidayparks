PARK: BIG4 Tasman Holiday Parks - South Mission Beach
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-14
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 145

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Families consistently highlight the resort-style pool with waterslide as a major drawcard with "kids didn't want to get out," well-maintained playground, onsite cafe with entertainment including karaoke nights, and the unique experience of cassowaries and wallabies roaming through the park providing natural entertainment for children.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural setting with park positioned directly opposite patrolled beach with stinger net for safe swimming, lush tropical gardens with "beautiful lipstick palms," cassowaries with chicks regularly visiting, wallabies and diverse birdlife throughout, proximity to Kennedy walking track, and views of Dunk Island creating an immersive tropical Far North Queensland environment.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Strong majority of reviews praise cleanliness with terms like "spotlessly clean," "immaculate," and "super clean" for amenities blocks, camp kitchen, and pool area, though some reviews from 2023-2024 mention occasional maintenance issues with fans, camp kitchen carpet, and muddy sites after rain indicating some inconsistency in upkeep standards.

Value (13/15) — Generally considered "reasonably priced" and "affordable" for the beachfront location and facilities offered, with positive mentions of cafe pricing and family budget suitability, though some reviews cite rates of $67-69 per night as overpriced when maintenance issues were present, and restaurant pricing for condiments received criticism.

Site Size (6/10) — This is the park's most consistent weakness across reviews with frequent mentions that "sites are ridiculously small," "VERY tight to get into," and "quite small for motorhomes," with multiple families noting difficulty parking vehicles alongside caravans and concerns about crowding, particularly challenging for full-size vans and during peak season.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive family sentiment with numerous "will definitely return" statements, many calling it their "favourite park" or "best holiday park in Australia," high recommendation rate, and repeat visitors over many years, though a few negative experiences with site flooding and cassowary incidents slightly temper the perfect score.

Location (10/10) — Perfect beachfront location with beach access "100 metres away" or "40 steps from cabin," patrolled swimming area with stinger net directly opposite, peaceful South Mission Beach setting away from crowds, reasonable distance to Mission Beach town (7-10km) and Cairns area attractions, with consistent praise that location cannot be faulted.

WHY NOT 100:
Site size remains a significant constraint with cramped caravan sites creating access difficulties and limited parking space, particularly problematic for larger rigs and during busy periods when "they keep piling in vans." Some reviews from 2023-2024 documented maintenance inconsistencies including muddy sites with poor drainage after rain, dirty camp kitchen areas, and rusty bathroom fans, suggesting standards fluctuated during that period. The cassowary presence, while magical for many, created safety concerns for some guests with one caravan door kicked hard enough to cause the family to leave early, indicating inadequate risk briefings at check-in.
