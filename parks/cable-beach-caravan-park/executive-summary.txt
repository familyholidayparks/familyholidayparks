PARK: Cable Beach Caravan Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 115

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently highlight an exceptional "resort style" pool described as "the best pool in Broome" and "better than Crown Towers," but the playground is "small, run down and not usable for young children." No organized kids activities or entertainment programs mentioned. Pool is the sole family entertainment feature.

Nature (16/20) — Excellent scores for mature shade trees throughout ("beautifully shady," "lots of beautiful big trees"), walking distance to famous Cable Beach (8-10 minutes), and "beautiful plants and birds." Wildlife sightings mentioned. Loses points as no bush setting or creek access beyond beach proximity.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with recent reviews noting decline. Positive mentions of "very clean," "amenities always clean and tidy" balanced against "kitchen and bathrooms were a bit dirty," "bandaids on ground in showers," "dead cockroaches," "toilets blocked for whole stay," and "camp kitchen has used old rubbish everywhere." Inconsistent standards.

Value (10/15) — Significant concerns with "$73-80 per night" described as "biggest rip off," "way too expensive," "greedy," and "overpriced." Some found it "reasonably priced" and "half the price" of competitors. Value diminished by small sites charged same as large, inconsistent pricing, and dated amenities for premium rates.

Site Size (6/10) — Major weakness with repeated complaints: "squeezed in like sardines," "very small sites," "felt like we were on top of our neighbours," "packed to the rafters," "overcrowded," "narrow roads." Some large sites exist but allocation inconsistent. High-density 500-site park with tight maneuvering.

Sentiment (8/10) — Polarized responses. Many 5-star reviews praising staff and location ("came for 1 night stayed 2 weeks," "can't wait to come back"), but significant 1-2 star reviews citing poor management response, rude customer service, and declining standards. Repeat visitors note park has deteriorated over 6 years.

Location (9/10) — Excellent proximity to Cable Beach (walking distance), close to brewery, restaurants, pubs, and town (5 minutes drive). Bus stop at entrance. Perfect location for Broome attractions. Only detriment is no longer a shortcut through adjacent property to beach.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site sizes are consistently too small for a premium-priced park, with families feeling "squeezed like sardines" and caravans "on top of neighbours" with inadequate space for rigs and vehicles spilling onto driveways.
2. The park mixes long-term workers and backpackers with holiday families, causing noise issues ("vehicles coming and going late at night and early morning"), hygiene problems in shared facilities, and an uncomfortable atmosphere that families report has significantly declined in recent years.
3. Cleanliness and maintenance standards are inconsistent with aging amenities needing upgrade, blocked toilets going unrepaired, dirty camp kitchens with inadequate facilities for 500 sites, and customer service failures where management "clearly couldn't care less" about legitimate complaints.
