PARK: Mudjimba Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 143

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — No on-site playground or kids club facilities mentioned. Families rely on playground across the road, pool, and beach for activities. Several reviews note lack of facilities compared to BIG 4 parks for the price. Food vans occasionally visit. Kids bring bikes to ride around park.

Nature (18/20) — Excellent beach location with walking distance access (2-3 minutes). Dog-friendly beach nearby. Close to cafes and natural environment. Shaded sites under paperbark trees. Bike paths available. Some mosquito issues in back sections near reserve.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed feedback with modern, clean amenities praised in many recent reviews, but significant complaints about filthy camp kitchens (dirty fridges, cockroaches, moldy equipment). Amenities "impeccably clean" per some, but consistency varies by cleaning staff. Grounds maintenance issues with leaf debris.

Value (8/15) — Major pain point with widespread complaints about pricing. $80-$100 per night described as "over the top," "unethically high," and "way overpriced." 26% price increase without facility improvements noted. Dog fee of $5/night seen as money grab. Many say not value for money compared to alternatives.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive with "spacious" and "generous" sites mentioned. Drive-through options for big vans. Some poor sites (like site 1, 188) that are uneven or cramped. Grass sites advertised but dirt patches allocated. Sites close together in some areas.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitor base with families returning yearly, but sentiment declining in recent reviews. Many 5-star reviews offset by growing frustration with staff attitudes, pricing, and cleanliness. Loyal customers appreciated over new guests. Management responsiveness poor.

Location (8/10) — Excellent proximity to beach (2-3 min walk), cafes, restaurants within 5 min walk. Close to Coles, zoo, and Sunshine Coast attractions. Airport noise noted but manageable (no late flights). Walking distance to all amenities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Persistent staff attitude problems with multiple reviews citing rude, unhelpful reception staff who lack interpersonal skills and make guests feel unwelcome rather than greeting them warmly.
2. No dedicated on-site children's entertainment, playground, or activities — families must rely entirely on external beach/playground for kids, limiting appeal for families with young children seeking park-based activities.
3. Significant cleanliness failures in camp kitchens with cockroaches, moldy equipment, dirty facilities, and inconsistent maintenance that contradict the premium pricing and create health concerns.
