PARK: Ingenia Holidays Kingscliff
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-06-09
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 118

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Reviews consistently noted "kids loved the pool" and there were "lots of kids playing together," but multiple families complained of "no green space for kids to play," no pool table or table tennis, and "nothing for the kids" beyond the pool. Limited on-site kids' entertainment options.

Nature (13/20) — Guests appreciated "short walk to beach and cafe," "easy 10mint walk to beach," and "beautiful views and sunsets over the water." However, proximity to nature is offset by "constant traffic noise from M1 freeway" and "flights over head from Coolangatta airport starting at 6am."

Cleanliness (9/15) — Reviews are highly inconsistent. Many praised "super clean" amenities and "well maintained" grounds, but numerous recent reviews cited "cabin was absolutely filthy," "floors were filthy," "amenities are dated and not cleaned in the 4 days we were there," "no soap for the 3 days," and "getting very run down."

Value (10/15) — Mixed sentiment: some found it "ok for $40 pn" and "excellent price for school holiday time," but others complained "way overpriced," "$130/night for a family of 5" with nothing for kids, and "overpriced park for the location, quality of amenities and facilities."

Site Size (6/10) — Multiple complaints about "sites in this area of the park are ridiculously small," "very small sites," ensuite site "almost impossible to fit our caravan on," and "allocated a small site for a larger caravan, with our van actually extending onto the roadway." Some sites described as adequate.

Sentiment (7/10) — Overall sentiment is mixed-to-positive with many loyal guests saying "we go every year" and "will definitely be back," but tempered by substantial negative experiences citing poor cleanliness, uncomfortable stays, and unresponsive staff. Staff praised frequently ("Eadie was absolutely lovely," "Rachel was extremely helpful").

Location (7/10) — "Great location near Kingscliff," "close to beaches, cafes," and "a short walk to the river" received praise, but location negatives include "constant traffic noise," airport flight path noise, "it's also no where near anything," and "way too far to get to shops."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe noise issues from M1 freeway traffic "right into early hours of morning" and Coolangatta airport flights from 6am significantly impact guest experience and sleep quality.
2. Inconsistent and declining cleanliness standards with multiple recent reports of filthy cabins, dirty amenities, no soap, inadequate cleaning, mould on sheets, and overall "getting very run down."
3. Minimal on-site kids' entertainment beyond a pool—no green space for play, no indoor activities like pool tables, and limited playground facilities, with families reporting "nothing for the kids" and conflicts with permanent residents over space.
