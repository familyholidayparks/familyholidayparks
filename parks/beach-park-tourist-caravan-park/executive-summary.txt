PARK: Beach Park Tourist Caravan Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 26

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Limited kids entertainment with table tennis and pool table mentioned, but reviews note broken equipment including "table tennis bats were all broken" and pool cue tip broken. Solar heated pool receives positive mentions but no structured kids activities or programs evident.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional beachfront location with "direct access to the beach" via private path repeatedly praised. Located on Phillip Island coastline with beach only 30 metres away. Natural setting is the park's strongest feature with consistent mentions of beach proximity and coastal environment.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed reviews with recent feedback showing "amenities were spotless and constantly clean" and "toilets and showers where spotless", but significant concerns raised including "most unkempt caravan park we have ever stayed at", "under the bed and lounge were filthy", and "camp kitchen was unclean and pathetically equipped". Shows inconsistency over time.

Value (12/15) — Positioned as budget accommodation with "prices are good" and "Budget great for family" noted positively. Price mentioned at $50 AUD, described as "priced accordingly" for basic facilities. Families appreciate affordability but expect basic amenities only.

Site Size (5/10) — Consistent complaints about cramped sites: "sites are quite small", "sites a little difficult to get the van into", "minimum afmetingen", "small and awkwardly shaped". One loyal customer mentions adequate space for 21ft caravan but this is not the common experience.

Sentiment (6/10) — Polarized sentiment with loyal repeat visitors ("coming for over 15 years", "regularly visit") contrasted against very negative recent experiences including staff rudeness reported in multiple 2026 reviews ("rude to myself and my wife", "my wife was abused by one of the staff"). High repeat visitation offset by serious service concerns.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location "right on the beach" and "easy walk into Cowes" with "walking distance to the main streets". Phillip Island setting provides access to attractions. Only minor points lost as not directly in town center.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant staff behavior issues reported in recent 2026 reviews with multiple incidents of rudeness and one report of verbal abuse, creating serious customer service concerns.
2. Cramped, awkwardly shaped sites consistently criticized as too small for modern caravans, with guests needing help to maneuver into tight spaces, limiting suitability for larger family rigs.
3. Maintenance and cleanliness inconsistencies evident with broken equipment throughout (table tennis, pool cues, kitchen appliances), dirty camp kitchen, and facilities showing age despite some areas being well-maintained.
