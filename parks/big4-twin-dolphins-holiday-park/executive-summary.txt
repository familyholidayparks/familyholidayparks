PARK: BIG4 Twin Dolphins Holiday Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 54

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (9/20) — Reviews consistently note limited kids entertainment with "nothing for kids to do apart from pool," no jumping pillow, basic playground needing updates, and paid game tables ($2 per game). Several families specifically mentioned the lack of facilities compared to other BIG4 parks, though one family praised the playground for their 5-year-old.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting with "rain forest" surrounds, "heaps of birdlife" especially kookaburras, beautiful palms and trees creating a bush atmosphere. Multiple reviews highlight the peaceful, tranquil environment "like youre in the bush" despite being close to town. Some mention mosquitoes and sandflies near cabbage palm forest areas.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed feedback with recent improvements evident. Renovated amenities receive praise as "top class" and "very clean and modern," but several reviews from 2024-2025 cite uncleaned amenities, overflowing bins with blowflies, mold in cabins, and dusty rooms. Inconsistent cleaning standards during busy periods.

Value (10/15) — Reviews split between "great affordable price" and "very overpriced." Some found it cheaper than other area parks and appreciated prices kept down by fewer facilities. However, multiple complaints about pricing inconsistencies, being charged more than advertised rates, and poor refund policies that left guests feeling exploited.

Site Size (8/10) — Positive feedback on "caravan sites are big," sites "weren't on top of each other," and good spacing. Grass sites in rainforest setting particularly praised. However, limited specific detail about large rig accommodation or exact site dimensions in reviews.

Sentiment (5/10) — Highly polarized reviews ranging from "will definitely come back" to "not family friendly at all." Recent reviews (2024-2026) show declining satisfaction with management changes, service quality issues, and maintenance concerns. Multiple complaints about rude staff, poor customer service, and guests feeling unwelcome despite some praising friendly staff.

Location (7/10) — Central Tuncurry location praised as "5mins from everything," close to shops, beach, rock pool, and clubs. However, park is "away from the water and walks" with no waterfront access. Several reviews note it's not ideal for those wanting beach proximity, recommending Reflections parks instead for water views.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severely limited kids entertainment infrastructure with outdated playground, no jumping pillow, no waterpark features, and minimal organized activities—falling well short of BIG4 standards and family expectations.
2. Significant service and management issues with multiple recent reviews citing rude reception staff, poor customer service, inflexible refund policies, booking errors, and guests being "yelled at" or made to feel unwelcome.
3. Inconsistent cleanliness and maintenance standards with reports of uncleaned amenities for extended periods, mold in cabins, overflowing bins, and general run-down appearance indicating the park "needs an update and alot of love."
