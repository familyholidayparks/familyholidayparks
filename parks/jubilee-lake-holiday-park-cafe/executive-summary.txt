PARK: Jubilee Lake Holiday Park & Cafe
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 112

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Reviews mention playground, footy oval, outdoor theatre, paddle boats/canoes for hire, and fishing opportunities. Families appreciated these basic facilities but noted lack of structured kids' programs or modern entertainment infrastructure. One reviewer mentioned "playground was blast for our kids" and another highlighted "boat bicycles" as a positive, but several noted "a game room will be nice" suggesting gaps in entertainment options.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural environment with consistent praise for the beautiful lake, abundant wildlife including peacocks, wallabies, ducks, and numerous bird species. Reviews repeatedly mention "wildlife everywhere," "gorgeous lake," "beautiful natural setting," and stunning walking tracks around the lake. Close proximity to Hepburn Springs and Daylesford attractions. Minor deduction for bugs/mosquitos mentioned in warmer months.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Generally strong cleanliness standards with frequent mentions of "very clean" amenities, "immaculately clean" cabins, and "always clean and serviced regularly." However, several reviews noted issues: unwashed dishes on arrival, inconsistent bathroom cleaning during peak times, empty soap dispensers, and overflowing sanitary bins. Winter heating in shower blocks also criticized.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback on value. Positive reviews mentioned "very reasonably priced," "super cheap especially the lollies," and "great prices." However, multiple reviews noted concerns: "over priced very simple cabins," "disappointed at winter prices," and "bit expensive" for basic facilities. Cafe pricing received positive mentions.

Site Size (8/10) — Reviews consistently mention "generous size sites," "sites are big with a great view," and "lovely open spaces." Some criticism for unpowered sites being unlevel and "no effort has been made to level off sites." Distance to bathrooms (5+ minute walk) from some sites mentioned as inconvenient.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong positive sentiment with many return visitors and families saying "will definitely be back" and "highly recommend." However, sentiment impacted by several negative experiences regarding staff rudeness, noise issues, and maintenance concerns. Some reviewers stated "won't be back" after disappointing experiences.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location praised as "five minutes into Daylesford," "close to town," and "central for many diverse activities." Ten-minute drive to hot springs mentioned positively. Some sites too close to main road with tourist traffic on weekends. Overall very accessible while maintaining peaceful bushland setting.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited structured kids' entertainment beyond basic playground and boat hire - no waterpark, jumping pillow, or organized activities that families expect from higher-tier holiday parks.
2. Inconsistent maintenance and aging facilities with multiple reports of cold showers, facilities needing upgrades, muddy sites, and occasional cleanliness lapses during busy periods.
3. Mixed customer service experiences with several reports of rude staff interactions, poor noise management between sites, and unhelpful responses to guest concerns.
