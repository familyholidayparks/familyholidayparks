PARK: Parry Beach Campsite
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 84

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Reviews indicate no playground, kids club, or organized activities. One family mentioned hosting "kids parties here" and activities are limited to natural beach play, fishing, and bushwalking. This is a basic campsite, not an entertainment-focused holiday park.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding natural setting with "camping in the forest with heaps of shade," "beautiful beach," direct beach access for walking and 4WD, bush walking trails including the Bib Track, wildlife sightings, and "all you can hear at night is the ocean and the wildlife." Reviews consistently praise the stunning natural environment.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback on cleanliness. Many reviews mention "spotless toilets," "very clean camp," and "well maintained/serviced" facilities. However, significant complaints about "yellow water," bore water quality, inconsistent hot water from solar showers, and occasional issues with locked facilities. Some reviews note "amenities are old but very clean."

Value (12/15) — Strong value proposition at $23-24 per night with most reviews stating "good value for money," "cheap campsite," "not too pricey and very fair." Some complaints it's "bit rich for $23" given basic facilities and no power. Overall, families appreciate the affordable pricing for beachside location.

Site Size (6/10) — Limited information on site spaciousness with some reviews mentioning "sites a little small," concerns about being "bunched together like sardines," and a 2.7m height restriction limiting larger vans. Positive mentions include "lots of sites (40+), nicely spaced" and "spacious sites," but overflow areas are needed during busy periods.

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive with many saying "will definitely be back," "stayed longer than planned," and "come twice a year." However, significant negative sentiment around caretaker interactions in 2023 with multiple 1-star reviews citing "rude caretaker," though more recent 2025-2026 reviews show improved caretaker service. Strong repeat visitation among those who appreciate basic camping.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location with beach "just at the door," "minute walk to beach," and "20min drive to Denmark town." Close to attractions like Green Pool, Elephant Rock, and good for fishing. Only minor deduction for remoteness and lack of immediate town amenities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment facilities - no playground, kids club, pool, waterpark, jumping pillow, or organized activities that Australian families with young children expect from a holiday park.
2. Inconsistent and basic facilities with unreliable solar hot water ("shower was cold," "solar so a bit hit n miss"), yellow bore water requiring families to bring drinking water, no powered sites, and no booking system causing uncertainty during peak periods.
3. Multiple complaints about rude caretaker interactions particularly in 2023-2024, with families reporting being "bunched together like sardines," unwelcoming behavior, and poor communication, though this appears to have improved in recent reviews.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
