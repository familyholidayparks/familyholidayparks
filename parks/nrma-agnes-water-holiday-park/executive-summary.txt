PARK: NRMA Agnes Water Holiday Park
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2025
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 167

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Reviews consistently note there is "no playground or pool" with facilities described as "limited." Entertainment comes entirely from the natural beach environment, with families praising it as "Great for surfing and swimming" and "perfect for surfers of all ages." The on-site cafe provides meals but no structured kids' activities or entertainment programs are mentioned.

Nature (20/20) — Perfect score for exceptional beachfront location with "beach right at your doorstep," "Amazing location directly on beach," and "stunning ocean views." Families describe it as "the most beautiful beach" with "large shady grass park between cabins and beach," nearby playground next door, and access to discovery trails. The natural coastal environment is the park's standout feature.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Generally positive with many noting "Very clean park," "spotless," and "amenities cleaned daily." However, several reviews mention inconsistencies: "amenities were humid and filthy," "shower had significant mould," "sanitary facilities dirty," and items like "empty water bottle beside sofa." Recent reviews show improvement under new management.

Value (13/15) — Mixed feedback with many finding it "well above avg" at "$70 a night for powered site," "reasonable cost," and "good prices." However, some felt pricing high: "$388 per night" for cabins with "tired amenities," "$70 bloody dollars" for one night with beach issues, and concerns about "value for money? No" in premium cabins.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews indicate varied site sizes with "roomy site," "good size," and "enough room" mentioned positively. However, concerns include "site was a bit small," "parking was a little tight," "not much room between vans," "cramped like most CP," and some sites described as struggling to "accommodate a tent." The newer Spring Road section has tighter spacing.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very high sentiment with numerous "will definitely be back," "12th year coming back," "repeat customers every year," and "one of our favourite parks." Recent management changes created issues with multiple 1-star reviews citing "aggressive," "rude," and "confrontational" behaviour, though newer 2025/2026 reviews show improvement with praise for "new management" being "welcoming, friendly and considerate."

Location (10/10) — Perfect location score with unanimous praise: "walking distance to everything," "5 minute walk to supermarket and shops," "short walk to beach, shops, fooderies," "close to cafes, takeaways," and proximity to 1770. Families note you can "park the car, throw away the keys" and access everything needed on foot.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of dedicated family entertainment infrastructure (no playground, no pool, no kids' club, no entertainment programs) limits appeal to families seeking structured activities beyond beach play.
2. Inconsistent cleanliness standards with multiple reports of mould in cabins, dirty amenities during peak periods, and maintenance issues including broken appliances and inadequate cleaning between guests.
3. Management and customer service issues particularly during 2024-2025 period with multiple serious complaints about aggressive, rude, and confrontational behaviour toward families with children, plus booking allocation problems and poor communication during check-in.
