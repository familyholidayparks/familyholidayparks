PARK: BIG4 Mudgee Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-06-04
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 156

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews mention games room, basketball court, pool and playground as positives for families, though multiple families were disappointed that the jumping pillow advertised has been removed. Some reviews note "plenty of activities to do even on a rainy day" and kids enjoyed the facilities, but the play area is described as "a bit tired" and "very very ordinary not much for a child to do there really."

Nature (11/20) — The park's semi-industrial location is a significant detractor, with reviews stating "in an industrial area so very noisy early from trucks" and "felt like we were in an industrial caravan park." Multiple guests note "you just do not get a relaxed nature feel here at all vs other caravan parks" and "minimal grass, a lot of gravel/tar." Some sites have tree cover and filtered sun, but the overall environment lacks the natural bush or waterfront setting families seek.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Reviews are mixed, with many praising "very clean, well maintained" amenities and "spotless" facilities, while others report significant issues: "cabin it was dirty, stove didn't work walls where dirty and marked," "floors were very dirty," "camp kitchen and BBQ sinks filthy and never cleaned," and maintenance issues like "severe effluent/water leak across our site" that went unaddressed for days.

Value (11/15) — Families generally find the park "good value" and "affordable," with positive comments about pricing for Big4 members. However, some feel it's "overpriced for what they are" given the dated cabins, and specific complaints about "$7.48" laundry costs and "$25.00 to get beds made" in cabins. The inconsistency between renovated and old cabins affects perceived value.

Site Size (8/10) — Caravan sites receive consistent praise: "Good size sites with room for those that want to stay hitched up," "All caravan sites are large with plenty of room behind," "very flat for caravans," and "Sites are far enough apart so that we didn't feel crammed in." However, cabin size is noted as small with "compact" rooms and "tiny" showers.

Sentiment (8/10) — Family sentiment is moderately positive with many return visitors and recommendations, particularly from caravanners who value the spacious sites and helpful staff. However, negative experiences around cleanliness inconsistency, cabin quality variation, and industrial noise create a mixed picture. Several families explicitly state they won't return, balanced by many who would "definitely come back."

Location (8/10) — The park is "5-10 minutes from main town," convenient for accessing Mudgee attractions, wineries, and the golf club. However, it's "a bit far to walk to town centre" and the semi-industrial surroundings detract. Families appreciate proximity to Bunnings and Harvey Norman, and buses run past the entrance, making it a practical base despite lacking a scenic location.

WHY NOT 100:
1. The park's semi-industrial location with early morning industrial noise (log splitting at 7am, concrete business noise measured at 75db) significantly detracts from the relaxation and nature experience families expect from a holiday park.
2. Significant inconsistency in cabin quality and cleanliness, with many older cabins described as "outdated," "dirty," and "not what they show on their website," creating a disappointing experience for families who don't get the renovated units.
3. The advertised jumping pillow no longer exists, disappointing many families with young children, and the playground is described as "tired" and "very very ordinary," falling short of Big4 entertainment standards.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
