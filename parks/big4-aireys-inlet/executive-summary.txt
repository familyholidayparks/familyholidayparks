PARK: BIG4 Aireys Inlet
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-23
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 370

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (15/20) — Reviews highlight heated pool, adventure playground, jumping pillow, pedal carts, outdoor chess, and safari glamping tents. Families appreciate kids' activities and the games room, though some note the pool is small and a few suggest adding basketball courts or upgrading facilities. Nightly communal firepits receive strong praise for creating social atmosphere.

Nature (17/20) — Excellent natural setting with walking distance to Split Point Lighthouse, clifftop coastal walks, secluded beaches at Fairhaven and Eagle Rock, and surrounded by native birdlife. Reviews consistently mention beautiful bush setting with trees throughout the park, morning bird chorus, and easy access to Great Ocean Road attractions and Otways waterfalls.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Outstanding cleanliness scores with "spotlessly clean," "immaculate bathrooms," and "best showers we've ever seen" appearing frequently. Amenities described as modern, well-maintained with excellent water pressure. Occasional mentions of spider issues in specific cabins and some maintenance delays during peak periods prevent perfect score.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing. Many find it reasonable and good value, but multiple recent reviews note significant price increases—from $70 to expensive peak rates ($300-$730/night for cabins), with some feeling it's overpriced for what's offered. Long-term guests particularly disappointed by dramatic cost increases under new ownership.

Site Size (7/10) — Sites described as "fairly compact" with some reviews noting tight car parking and difficulty backing into certain sites. Concrete slabs praised, but unpowered sites become dusty without grass. Some larger rigs find sites challenging. Ensuite sites and double-wide slabs receive positive mentions.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong positive sentiment with many repeat visitors and high recommendation rates. Phrases like "will definitely be back," "5th time staying," and "count the sleeps until we return" common. However, some long-term loyalty lost due to management changes, pricing increases, and inflexible rebooking policies affecting multi-generational families.

Location (10/10) — Perfect location scores consistently. Walking distance to general store, cafes, pharmacy, Aireys Pub, and beaches. "Stones throw" to Split Point Lighthouse and coastal walks. Central for exploring Great Ocean Road between Lorne and Anglesea. Right on highway but quieter sites insulated from noise. "Can't miss it" accessibility praised.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Pool consistently described as "small," "tiny," "dinky" with some families choosing beach instead—limits appeal for families expecting typical BIG4 water facilities.
2. Significant value concerns with recent dramatic price increases (reports of $3,000 to $11,000 for same booking) alienating long-term loyal customers and pensioners who previously found it affordable.
3. Limited on-site entertainment variety compared to larger BIG4 parks—no waterslides, mini-golf, or basketball court, with some reviews noting kids need additional activities beyond basic playground and jumping pillow.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
