PARK: Toorbul Caravan Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 47

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Reviews contain no mention of kids' clubs, playgrounds, jumping pillows, or organized activities. One family noted "our kids enjoyed the place" due to waterfront access, but entertainment relies entirely on natural environment and fishing rather than facilities.

Nature (16/20) — Strong praise for coastal location with "right on the passage" access, "as soon as you drive out of it, you see ocean", "feel very close to nature", and mentions of "beautiful main Esplanade" and "wild kangaroos". Proximity to Australian Zoo and Glasshouse Mountains noted. Midges mentioned as afternoon issue.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Exceptional consistency with "incredibly clean facilities", "amenities totally spotless", "best shower pressure I've ever had", and "grounds and amenities spotless" appearing frequently. One review noted cleanliness varied by staff member, and drainage issues mentioned after rain.

Value (11/15) — Multiple mentions of "great price", "reasonable prices", "good price", though one review criticized $800/week cabin pricing. Generally viewed as affordable, but limited facilities reduce overall value proposition for active families.

Site Size (9/10) — Reviewers praised "big flat sites", "good size sites", "sites nicely spaced", and "grassy sites". Small park with mostly permanent residents noted, but touring sites adequately sized.

Sentiment (7/10) — Overwhelmingly positive sentiment from couples and retirees ("never want to leave", "wish I wasn't leaving"), but three 1-star reviews cite booking issues, maintenance problems, and poor customer service. Strong repeat visitors among fishing/quiet seekers, less enthusiasm from families needing activities.

Location (7/10) — Walking distance to beach, pub, and shop praised. Convenient to Australian Zoo (30 mins), Bribie Island, and Caboolture. However, described as "out of the way" and "nothing much to see in town" - good for peaceful stays but lacking nearby family attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of kids' entertainment facilities (playground, pool, games room, activities) makes this unsuitable for families seeking on-site entertainment, losing significant points in the entertainment category.
2. Administrative and maintenance issues documented in multiple reviews including booking mix-ups, site reassignments without notice, broken laundry equipment, drainage problems, and inconsistent customer service.
3. Very limited local amenities beyond a pub and corner shop, with no significant family attractions in immediate vicinity requiring 30+ minute drives for activities.
