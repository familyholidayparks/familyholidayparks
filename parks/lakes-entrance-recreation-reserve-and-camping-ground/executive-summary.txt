PARK: Lakes Entrance Recreation Reserve and Camping Ground
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 109

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note this is a basic camping ground with limited dedicated kids' facilities. Families mention "kids loved the space and the ability to run around on the field and kick a footy" and watching local AFL games, but multiple reviews specifically state "not much for kids to do there" beyond the open sports oval and natural environment.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional waterfront location with "sites right on the waters edge" and "direct access to the beach inlet." Families frequently mention abundant birdlife including "black swans every day," pelicans, and ducks. Reviews describe "gorgeous family of swans," "beautiful views and sunset," and the ability to "swim directly from your camp site." The natural setting is a major highlight.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive feedback on "brand new showers and toilets," "spotlessly clean," and "modern and spotless." Multiple reviews call it "the cleanest" campground they've experienced. Recent renovations (12 months old) clearly elevated standards. Minor deductions for occasional complaints about bathrooms being "dirty regardless of what time of day" and inadequate toilet/shower numbers during peak periods.

Value (14/15) — Exceptional value with "$35 per night power & water" making it the "cheapest caravan park" many have stayed at and "1/4 of the price any caravan park had available." Families consistently praise "great value," "value for money," and "best prijs kwaliteit." Small deduction as one reviewer felt "a bit over priced" compared to other regions.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant concerns raised with "sites are very small," "a little squashy," "slightly cramped," "densely packed," and "sites are tight." Multiple families note sites are "close together," though some waterfront sites described as "nice and big" and "good large spot."

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong positive sentiment with families stating "will comeback here many times," "we will definitely be back," "highly recommend," and "would stay there again." High repeat visitation indicated. Minor deductions for a few negative experiences with staff and management issues.

Location (9/10) — Outstanding central location with "5 min walk to pretty much everything you need, Kmart, woolies, maccas, cafes," "walking distance to shops," "4 mins walk to woolies," and "10 minute walk to everything." Easy access to beach (15 minutes), town centre, and attractions consistently praised.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Minimal dedicated children's entertainment or playground facilities - reviews explicitly state there's "not much for kids to do there" beyond the open field, lacking the waterparks, jumping pillows, or activity programs found at higher-scoring parks.
2. Site sizes are consistently described as small, cramped, and tightly packed, particularly problematic for families with larger rigs or those seeking more private space during busy periods.
3. Insufficient amenities capacity during peak times with only one amenities block serving approximately 72 sites, leading to complaints about overcrowded facilities and occasional cleanliness issues when busy.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
