PARK: Noonamah Tourist Park
SCORE: 48/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-09-04
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 28

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Reviews make no mention of kids' clubs, playgrounds, waterslides, or organised activities. The park offers a basic pool but lacks family entertainment infrastructure, with reviewers describing it primarily as a convenient stopover location.

Nature (12/20) — Guests appreciated "lovely greenery", "lush and green all year round", and "shady" surroundings. Located 30 minutes from Darwin and "very close to berry springs and Litchfield and Kakadu", offering gateway access to NT natural attractions, though the park itself sits beside Stuart Highway with noted road noise.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed reviews with recent guests praising "AMAZINGLY clean" cabins, "extremely clean bathrooms", and "crystal clear" pool, but one 2025 review complained "toilet/shower blocks were old and dirty". Positive mentions of grounds maintenance and modern amenities, but inconsistency evident.

Value (8/15) — Limited family value discussion in reviews. One guest stated "Not value for money at $55, there are much nicer places to stay for less or the same money", while another praised pricing compared to other parks. One owner offered a free night's stay, suggesting flexible approach, but no clear family package mentions.

Site Size (9/10) — Strong positive feedback with "large van sites", "hugh site for the van", and "very good caravan space" mentioned consistently. One reviewer noted "our site was tight" but most praised spaciousness and drive-through capability.

Sentiment (7/10) — Generally positive for transit stays with guests extending bookings and calling it their "number one stop", but one family left early due to theft concerns and cleanliness issues. Not specifically family-focused sentiment, more adult/grey nomad appreciation.

Location (7/10) — Convenient highway location "40k trip into Darwin" with tavern, servo and small supermarket within 300m. Good base for exploring Litchfield and Kakadu. However, persistent mentions of "noise from stuarts highway" and "Truck Stop" description indicate roadside setting rather than peaceful family destination.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of children's entertainment, playgrounds, or kids' activities mentioned across all 28 reviews, making this unsuitable as a family holiday destination park.
2. Security concerns raised with one family experiencing theft of belongings from their site and leaving early, plus conflicting cleanliness reports suggesting inconsistent maintenance standards.
3. Highway noise repeatedly mentioned as an issue, with roadside "truck stop" character limiting the peaceful, family-friendly atmosphere Australian families typically seek for holidays with young children.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
