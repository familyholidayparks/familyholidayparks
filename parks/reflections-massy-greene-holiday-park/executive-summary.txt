PARK: Reflections Massy Greene - Holiday Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-06-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 68

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Multiple recent reviews explicitly state "no facilities for kids" and "playground has been removed with no replacement." One 2023 review mentioned "play area for kids" but 2024-26 reviews confirm it's now "fenced off lots not working." No pool, waterpark, or organized activities mentioned. Families report nothing for children to do on-site.

Nature (17/20) — Outstanding natural setting with "exceptional location right on the river front," "protected beach for kids to fish and swim," and "magnificently clear" river for kayaking. Reviews praise "beautiful water views," beach access, rainforest walks nearby, and safe swimming areas. Loses points for one muddy campsite with drainage issues and "sharp grit" on riverfront sites.

Cleanliness (9/15) — Highly inconsistent. Some reviews praise "spotlessly clean" grounds and "amazing facilities perfect," while multiple recent family reviews report "floors were not clean," "my crawling baby picked multiple things up from the floor," "feet were black," "smelly bins," and amenities "in need of upgrade" and "more regular cleaning." Older standard cabins had cleanliness issues.

Value (6/15) — Overwhelming negative sentiment on pricing. Called "most expensive caravan park from Victoria to top end Queensland," "wildly over priced," "$93.00 price gouging," "massive price gouge," and "$100 a night in off peak." Multiple families state poor value for facilities provided, with "very expensive park for the facilities" and pricing "equal with 5 star parks" but "2 star bracket" quality.

Site Size (5/10) — Significant concerns with "packed in like sardines" appearing in multiple reviews, "cramped sites," "tiny campsite that couldn't fit our mini camper and car," "caravan sites are pretty tight," and "difficult to access." Premium unpowered site "barely had 2mx2m of flat ground." Some waterfront sites praised but access and size problems widespread.

Sentiment (8/10) — Mixed sentiment with loyal long-term visitors loving the location while recent families express disappointment. Multiple "will definitely be back" from couples, but families report "won't be back," "last time," and "major decline over the past few years." Security incidents, theft reports, and management concerns noted. Strong loyalty from permanent residents and couples, frustration from families.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location scores with "walking distance to town," "300m to town, pub, bakery," "easy walk into town," and proximity to Brunswick Heads facilities praised universally. Direct river frontage and beach access major drawcard. Minor deduction for noise from busy road backing some sites and crowded feel during peak periods.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of kids' entertainment facilities with playground removed and no replacement, making this unsuitable for families seeking on-site activities for children.
2. Severe value perception issues with numerous families calling it the "most expensive" park while receiving poor maintenance, aging amenities, cramped sites, and facilities declining rather than improving despite premium pricing.
3. Inconsistent cleanliness particularly in family cabins with dirty floors affecting crawling babies, aging amenities needing refurbishment, and maintenance issues including non-working camp kitchen equipment and poor shower pressure.
