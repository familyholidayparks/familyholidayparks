WHY THIS SCORE:

Value (4/15) — Poor value with $270 charged for 30-minute stay, $120/night for poorly maintained attached cabin with no refund policy, though some mention "can't beat the price" for permanent residents.
Site Size (3/10) — Cramped cabins with "bathroom isn't big enough to swing a cat", attached cabins rather than standalone, no mention of spacious sites suitable for families.
Sentiment (2/10) — Overwhelmingly negative recent reviews (4 of 5 most recent are 1-2 stars), guests leaving early, booking elsewhere, stating "would not stay here again" and "cannot in all conscience recommend it".
Location (6/10) — Walking access to bakery, medical center, IGA, chemist, and shops; bus service 4 times daily; convenient Maleny location but park itself unsuitable for holiday families.

WHY NOT 100:
1. This is fundamentally a permanent residential home park for retirees, not a holiday park, with multiple reviews explicitly warning "not for holiday camping" and "false advertising" - completely unsuitable for families seeking entertainment and activities.
2. Severe cleanliness and maintenance failures across recent reviews including plumbing/effluent problems under council investigation, mould, leaking showers, dirty facilities, and no air conditioning making cabins uninhabitable in Queensland heat.
3. Zero family amenities with no playground, pool, kids activities, or entertainment; combined with extremely poor value (guests leaving after 30 minutes, no refunds) and overwhelmingly negative recent sentiment from actual holiday visitors.
