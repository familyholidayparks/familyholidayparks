PARK: Lakeview Tourist Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 50

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (5/20) — No evidence of playground, jumping pillow, games room, or organized kids activities; reviews confirm there is no pool; park is purely accommodation-focused with no on-site entertainment facilities for children.

Nature (16/20) — Strong lakefront position with "amazing views across to the lake" and location "steps from the lake" for fishing and swimming; "less than 10 mins drive to beautiful beaches"; walking paths along Tuggerah Lake; peaceful natural setting praised frequently.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Exceptional performance with "one of the cleanest parks we've come across," "amenities were kept spotlessly clean," "cleanest bathrooms I've seen in any camper park," and "so clean that you could sleep in them"; consistently immaculate presentation.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback with some praising "cheap" and "great rates" while others found it "quite expensive for $50 a night" given lack of facilities; "more expensive than we thought looking at the website"; reasonable pricing acknowledged but value questioned without pool or entertainment.

Site Size (7/10) — Limited specific feedback; one family noted cabins "perfect size for family of 3-4"; complaints of "camping spot was too small" and sites feeling cramped with "everyone look like siting each other"; drive-through sites available for larger rigs.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive guest sentiment with frequent "will definitely be back" comments; guests extending stays; "treated like family" service; two theft incidents noted; strong recommendation rate despite facility limitations.

Location (9/10) — Excellent Central Coast positioning "close to everything," "very close to shops and beaches," shopping centre nearby, local clubs for dining; main drawback is "very close to a main road which is very noisy" for some sites.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of children's entertainment facilities including no pool, no playground, no jumping pillow, and no organized activities, severely limiting appeal for families with young children seeking on-site fun.
2. Security concerns with two separate reviews reporting theft of personal items (chairs, tent ladder, shoes, caravan step), suggesting inadequate site security for a family park.
3. Significant road noise issues for sites near the main road, small cramped sites in some areas, and discrepancies between website promises and actual waterfront access affecting guest expectations.
