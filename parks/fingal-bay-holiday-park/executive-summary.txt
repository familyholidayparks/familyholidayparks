PARK: Fingal Bay Holiday Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-01-12
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 113

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently mention a large heated pool that's popular with kids despite being "bloody freezing," a games room with activities (though some noted too many gambling-style machines), playground equipment, tennis courts next door, and BBQ areas. Families appreciated "plenty for the kids to do" and the activity room being "great for all kids, small and large alike."

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural setting with the park "directly opposite" Fingal Bay beach which reviewers called "stunning," "spectacular," and "magnificent." Beach access is literally across the road with "beautiful coastal vistas," surrounding national park, opportunities to see koalas, and excellent snorkeling and swimming. The park itself features "lots of shady trees and shrubs," "leafy areas," and "well-kept gardens."

Cleanliness (13/15) — Most reviews praised "very clean" and "spotlessly clean" facilities with amenities "cleaned regularly," though several recent reviews noted cleanliness inconsistencies including "communal areas and toilets could be kept cleaner," hot water issues, maintenance delays, and one long-term visitor noting "appalling filthy mess left in toilets by some campers."

Value (13/15) — Reviews highlighted "a fraction of the price of the Ingenias in the area" and fair pricing compared to competitors who "double their prices." However, some felt it was "a bit pricey" at $65-90 per night for basic sites, with complaints about public holiday minimum stay requirements and pricing being "quite expensive" for the amenities provided during some periods.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive feedback on "good sized caravan sites," "spacious" sites, and "well separated" pitches, with excellent reviews for the drive-through ensuite and RV sites. However, several reviewers with larger vans (6.9m-7m) struggled with "very tight" older sites and narrow roads, with one noting they received a 5m slab when needing more space.

Sentiment (9/10) — Outstanding repeat visitation with families returning for "30 years," "10+ times," and "regularly for many years." Strong recommendation language including "best holiday park ever," "will definitely be back," and "highly recommended." Some detractors cited security concerns, service issues, and one serious noise complaint about a generator.

Location (6/10) — While the beach location is pristine and "perfect," the park is "a little out of the way" and "50 minutes from the main highway," requiring drives to Nelson Bay and Shoal Bay for more amenities. Within walking distance are some shops, cafes, and takeaways, but it's more secluded than central, which some families loved but others found limiting.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant hot water system issues mentioned across multiple recent reviews with families reporting "only 2 hot showers in 6 nights," "no hot water pressure" in winter, and the hot water system being "a nightmare," impacting basic comfort for families.

2. Inconsistent cleanliness and maintenance standards with reports of dirty floor mats, cobwebs and bugs in cabins, squeaky doors, maintenance delays of 20+ hours, and declining toilet block maintenance from long-term visitors, suggesting quality control gaps.

3. Serious security and service concerns including no after-hours support ("zero after hours service"), unattended holiday period security issues with bike theft and dangerous e-bike riding, uncontactable security numbers during late-night disturbances, and a persistent generator noise complaint that management dismissed, showing gaps in guest safety and service responsiveness.
