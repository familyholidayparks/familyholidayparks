PARK: Burnett Heads Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-26
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 162

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note "not much to do for young families" on-site with "NOTHING to do for kids in the park," though Easter/Christmas saw "fun family entertainment," "kids activities, music and Easter bunny," and "colouring competition." Occasional events like "BBQ, live music, face painting and movie night" were praised but are not regular features. Most families rely on nearby playground, skatepark, and natural activities.

Nature (16/20) — Strong natural setting with "kangaroos and birds around for the nature enthusiasts," "nice view of the water," "close to the Burnett River," and "walking distance to beaches." Proximity to "Mon Repos Turtle Centre" and "fantastic bike rides to Bargara through Mon Repos turtle area" highly valued. "Peaceful and relaxing" environment with "shady trees," "leafy sites," and "lovely setting by the water." Minor sandfly issues noted.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive: "very clean amenities," "spotless amenities," "extremely clean amenities, grounds, and camp kitchen," "always clean and tidy." Cabins described as "immaculate" and "lovely and clean." Some older reviews mention "amenities are a bit old but spotless" and occasional concerns about mould/outdated facilities, and one noted "lack of cleaning by managers" during a 5-day stay.

Value (13/15) — Generally seen as "great value," "well priced," "reasonably priced," and "cheap rates." Multiple mentions of "good value" for the location and quality. One review questioned value at "$69 for a caravan site," citing poor shower quality. Most families found it fair for a council-run park with good facilities and location.

Site Size (8/10) — "Roomy sites," "large and shady," "good sized sites," "spacious tent sites," and "big drive through sites" frequently mentioned. Some sites have "concrete slab and a hedge for privacy." Negative feedback includes "sites are small and congested," "non powered sites are very small," and challenges maneuvering larger rigs. Overall adequate but variable.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation: "will definitely be back," "being staying here for years and will continue to," "our 3rd visit and we'll be back for more." However, several negative experiences with previous management (shouting, rudeness, unwelcoming) impact sentiment. Current management (Jess and Ross) praised as "amazing hosts" and "friendly, approachable." Mixed experiences lower overall sentiment.

Location (9/10) — Excellent proximity: "short 200 metre walk to shops," "walking distance to IGA, cafes, pub," "close to the port for Lady Musgrave trip," "short drive to Bargara," and "15 minutes drive from Bundaberg CBD." "Close to the water" and "lovely walkway along the coast." Only minor issue is "not really close to a beach but close enough if you don't mind a trudge."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited on-site children's entertainment — multiple reviews explicitly state "NOTHING to do for kids in the park" and "not much around or to do for young families," with activities mostly limited to occasional seasonal events rather than permanent facilities like a playground or daily kids' club.
2. Management inconsistency and negative service experiences — several reviews describe "rude" and "unwelcoming" behaviour from previous caretakers, including shouting at guests in front of children, aggressive interactions over parking, and making families feel they are "putting them out by simply arriving."
3. Ageing amenities and facilities — while clean, amenities are "extremely dated and desperately needs to be replaced," "need an upgrade from the 50's," with complaints about poor shower pressure, mouldy shower blocks, outdated laundry facilities, and no pool to compensate for the coastal location.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"Kangaroos and birds around for the nature enthusiasts," "very clean amenities and friendly helpful staff," "not much around or to do for young families," "fun family entertainment over the Easter weekend," "roomy sites, very clean amenities," "short 200 metre walk to shops," "NOTHING to do for kids in the park," "management are unwelcoming and make you feel as though you are putting them out"
