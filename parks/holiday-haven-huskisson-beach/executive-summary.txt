PARK: Holiday Haven Huskisson Beach
SCORE: 73/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-05-30
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 127

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Families consistently mention two playgrounds that young children love, table tennis, tennis courts, basketball court, a small pool, and cycling around the park. Football teams and sporting groups frequently use the facilities. However, entertainment is more outdoor/self-directed rather than organized kids' clubs or extensive waterpark features.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding natural setting directly on beautiful Huskisson Beach with white sand and calm waters, described as "pristine" and "gorgeous part of the world." Easy access to stunning Jervis Bay, walking tracks along the foreshore, bike paths with coastal views, nearby Moona Moona Creek and lagoon. The bushland camping area offers some nature immersion, though several reviews note it's less "bushland" than expected.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "spotlessly clean" cabins and "one of the cleanest" amenities, while concerning reports include ants in bunks, dirty cabin with hair and stains on mattress, towels smelling like vomit, socks and water bottles left under beds, and toilet facilities not serviced for days in bushland area. Recent reviews generally more positive about cleanliness standards.

Value (10/15) — Significant concerns about pricing with multiple reviews calling it "overpriced" ($75-90/night for camping, $150 for 2 nights bushland), "expensive," and "prices have gone up making it challenging for our family." Some note good off-season deals (30% off), but peak season pricing doesn't always match facility quality. Competitors nearby offer better rates.

Site Size (7/10) — Powered sites described as "large enough," "spacious," and "good sized," particularly those with artificial turf. However, several reviews warn sites are "very very tight" requiring careful planning to get rigs in, bushland sites are "squeezed" and "limited," and sloping sites require leveling chocks. Adequate but not exceptional for larger rigs.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitor base with families returning annually for Christmas and multiple stays, describing it as "home away from home" and "our go to park." Exceptional staff service frequently praised. However, sentiment damaged by several very negative 1-star experiences involving booking policy issues, road noise complaints, safety concerns in bushland area, and poor service recovery on cleanliness issues.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for location. Universally praised as "unbeatable," "best location you could hope for," right on Huskisson Beach (30 seconds to sand), easy 5-15 minute walk to town center along beautiful coastal path, close to shops, restaurants, and cafes. Main criticism is bushland sites are separated from beach and affected by road noise.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Inconsistent cleanliness standards with concerning reports of ants in bunks, dirty mattresses, poor cabin cleaning, and inadequate toilet servicing in bushland areas, plus lack of service recovery when issues reported.
2. Value concerns are widespread with multiple families noting overpricing that doesn't match facility quality, particularly for bushland sites and peak periods, with competitors offering significantly better rates nearby.
3. Significant road noise issues affect bushland camping areas backing onto the main Huskisson-Vincentia road, described as "incredibly loud," "like sleeping on the street," and "feels criminal to charge people to sleep next to a main road."
