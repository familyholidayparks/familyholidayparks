PARK: Tweed Holiday Parks Fingal Head
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 76

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Multiple recent reviews confirm the playground is "non existent" with only "2 swings" remaining, baby swing non-operational, and families reporting the park is "not very kid friendly" with management and regulars who "don't seem to like kids being there."

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting directly on beautiful surf beach with "easy access to both beach and bush," lighthouse walks, whale watching from sand dunes, "amazing wildlife including nesting curlews and so many dolphins," shady trees throughout, and bird life abundance.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed reports with some praising "outstanding cleanliness in facilities" and "spotless" cabins, but recent reviews note "lawns had not been cut in weeks," out-of-service showers left uncleaned, "long grass growing through everything," and soap frequently running out during busy periods.

Value (8/15) — Consistently criticized as "highly overpriced" at $38-70/night for unpowered sites described as "paddock with a few taps" or "swamp paddock," with multiple reviewers stating "not worth" the price and recommending going elsewhere for better value.

Site Size (6/10) — Frequently mentioned concern with sites being "a bit small and can feel very tight," "not a lot of room between Van sites," and guests feeling "very cramped in when full," though some positive mentions of "good sized sites" in certain areas.

Sentiment (5/10) — Severely impacted by repeated complaints of "abusive, threatening & intimidating park manager," staff being "exceedingly rude," families with children being "scolded" and made to feel "unwelcome," creating a divided sentiment between loyal repeat visitors and those who vow never to return.

Location (4/10) — While beachfront position is praised, accessibility suffers from being "off the beaten track" with no local shops requiring 4-5km trips to Kingscliff, poor mobile reception and wifi, and remote feel that doesn't suit families needing convenient amenities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Catastrophic customer service failures with multiple documented incidents of manager being abusive toward families with children, including yelling, threatening eviction, and refusing late-arriving guests within reception hours.
2. Complete absence of family entertainment infrastructure with playground effectively abandoned (only 2 broken swings) and atmosphere that actively discourages children, making it unsuitable for young families.
3. Poor value proposition with premium pricing ($38-70/night) for unpowered sites in overflow paddocks separated from amenities, described as "swamp" areas with midges that even management admits aren't worth the price.
