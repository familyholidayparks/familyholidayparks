PARK: Great Ocean Road Tourist Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 66

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Limited onsite entertainment with reviews mentioning only a small games room, basketball court, and jumping pillow; several families noted "kids playground is quite small (only a swing and the jumping mattress was deflated when we arrived)" and "not much of a playground for the children, only 2 swings." No pool or waterpark facilities mentioned.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting with consistent praise for "right next to the water," "beautiful spot down by the river," river walks with "abundance of birdlife," "short walk to the beach," and "safe kids beach" access. Ideal location for exploring coastal wonders including the 12 Apostles, Bay of Islands, and cliff top walks.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive feedback with repeated mentions of "very clean," "spotless amenities," "clean facilities," and "everything pristine." Minor concerns included one review noting "cleanliness could be improved - bathroom and floors" in cabins and amenities "could use renovation" though still clean.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing; some found it "best price we have gotten along the great ocean road" and "well priced," while others noted "still more expensive than NRMA parks at Port Campbell" and "50 Dollar un peu élevé par rapport à la moyenne" for powered sites.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews describe "reasonably spacious" sites with "level sites," "lush grass," and "wide roads, easy to manoeuvre the van." Some mentioned "small caravan park" overall and issues with "spot was not flat" requiring repositioning. Cabins described as "very small" in several reviews.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong positive sentiment with "one of my favourite spots that I keep coming back," "highly recommend this place," and "1000000% recommend." However, two significant negative reviews about cold cabins, WiFi issues, and after-hours unfriendliness impacted the score. Family-run operation praised as "amazing people."

Location (10/10) — Perfect marks for location with "ideal spot" positioning, "5 mins to Bay of Islands," "10-15 minutes to 12 Apostles," "close to everything," and "perfect position to go sightseeing along the great Ocean road." Easy walking distance to town amenities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Very limited children's entertainment infrastructure with only basic playground equipment (2 swings, jumping pillow with maintenance issues) and no pool or water play facilities for young families.
2. Small cabin sizes and some site spacing concerns with reports of cramped accommodation and uneven ground requiring repositioning of caravans.
3. Inconsistent service quality including WiFi problems not being resolved, cold cabins without compensation offered, and one serious complaint about after-hours arrivals being turned away.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
