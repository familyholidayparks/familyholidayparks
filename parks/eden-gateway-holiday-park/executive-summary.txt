PARK: Eden Gateway Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-19
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 144

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (13/20) — Reviews consistently mention the "massive jumping pillow," outdoor pool, playground, games room, and pedal karts for hire at $5/30min. Families appreciated movie nights and communal campfire activities during peak times. However, some noted entertainment facilities are "a little on the decline" and pool had "dirty bits and bobs in it."

Nature (17/20) — Exceptional natural setting with kangaroos grazing throughout the park daily, native bellbirds singing, and bushland surroundings. The boardwalk across the road provides immediate lake access, with Aslings Beach within walking distance. Multiple reviews highlight "surrounded by birds and kangaroos" and "gorgeous walk to the sea through a reserve with much wildlife."

Cleanliness (10/15) — Highly polarized feedback. Positive reviews praised "spotlessly clean," "very clean amenities," and "meticulously maintained" facilities. However, significant negative reviews described "filthy villa," "shower full of hairs," "dusty rooms and beds," "mouldy shower," and "stains on mattresses." Cleanliness appears inconsistent across different cabins and management periods.

Value (11/15) — Mixed value perception. Many found it "affordable," "reasonable prices," and "value for money." However, peak season pricing drew criticism: "$275 per night for one bedroom is ridiculous," "$900 for powered site over NYE," and "$80 a night totally overpriced." Several mentioned unexpected charges including $100 cleaning fees and extra charges for children over 6 months.

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised for "large sites," "massive so you don't feel boxed in," "big spaces for parking motorhomes and caravans," and "huge space for our family of 4." Sites accommodate large vans well, though some mentioned "very large rocks in the way of turning a large van around."

Sentiment (7/10) — Overall positive sentiment with many "will definitely stay again" and "highly recommend" comments. However, significant detractors stated "would never stay here again," "avoid this place," and "seriously disappointing experience." Management transitions appear to have impacted consistency. Repeat visitors of 20+ years noted declining standards.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location "just off the highway," with boardwalk and lake across the road, Eden town centre 1km walk, and close to Aslings Beach, whale museum, and mountain bike park. Easy access to Merimbula and Bega. Some noted highway noise as expected trade-off for convenient location.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Cleanliness standards are highly inconsistent, with multiple detailed complaints about filthy cabins, mouldy showers, dusty surfaces, broken appliances, and unclean amenities during peak periods, suggesting management and quality control issues across different accommodation types.

2. Entertainment and recreational facilities show signs of age and decline, with an outdated camp kitchen, aging amenities, dirty pool conditions reported, and limited organized activities outside peak periods, falling short of modern holiday park standards.

3. Customer service experiences vary dramatically depending on management periods, with complaints about rude staff, inflexible policies, poor communication (water shut-offs, booking errors), unreliable WiFi capped at 500mb, and unexpected charges that damage trust and repeat visitation.
