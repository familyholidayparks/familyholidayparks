PARK: Discovery Parks - West Beach Parks
SCORE: 84/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-17
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 275

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (19/20) — Reviews overwhelmingly praise the "incredible" range of activities including heated pool, toddler pool, splash park, multiple playgrounds with flying fox, jumping pillows, kids club with daily activities (bingo, cookie decorating, craft, taekwondo, cooking classes), outdoor cinema, arcade room, mini golf, gym, and basketball courts. Families report "so much to do you don't need to leave the park" and "kids had the best time." Special mention for activity coordinator "Miss Deb" who "brightened everyone's day."

Nature (17/20) — Direct beach access via secure gates to "beautiful" West Beach with walking/cycling paths extending "tens of kilometres" in both directions. Park features "bright green grass," mature trees, and sea breezes. Reviews note "beach at your door step" and "1 minute and your feet are in the sand." Some mention the beach is occasionally affected by sand dumping works and sewage concerns, preventing a perfect score.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Park grounds consistently described as "immaculately clean," "spotless," and "very well maintained" with "army of maintenance staff." However, multiple reviews note amenity blocks are "outdated," "need a refresh," with "cracked terrazzo floors," broken fixtures, and insufficient daily cleaning during busy periods. Some cabins show mould, dust buildup, and maintenance issues. One review mentioned ringworm outbreak closure.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback on value. Many find it "reasonable" for what's offered, others note it's "expensive" and "the most expensive caravan park" they've stayed at. Long weekend price increases of 44% criticized as "a rip off." WiFi issues and broken laundry machines during peak times frustrate guests paying premium rates. Some cabin quality doesn't match pricing.

Site Size (9/10) — Powered sites consistently described as "good size," "generous," "large and well kept grassed," and "huge sites." Caravan sites praised for space with concrete pads available. Minor criticism that ensuite sites are "tiny" and unpowered sites "a little bit small." Overall strong performance in this category.

Sentiment (9/10) — Extremely positive with families returning year after year ("4th year in a row," "stay here every spring"). Phrases like "one of our most favourite places in the world," "best park we've stayed at," and "in our top 3 of Oz" common. However, some serious concerns about theft (multiple bike thefts), noise from kids/planes, and staff service issues prevent a perfect score.

Location (7/10) — Excellent beach access and only 15 minutes to Adelaide CBD, close to Harbour Town shopping, Glenelg, and airport. Public transport at gate. However, airport noise (planes from 5-6am until 10pm-2am) is a major complaint. Traffic noise from Military Road disturbs some cabins. Location is more suited to families than those seeking peace.

WHY NOT 100:
1. **Airport and noise disruption** — Consistent complaints about aircraft noise starting at 5-6am and continuing throughout the day/night, with helicopters flying until 2am. Traffic noise from Military Road keeps guests awake "ALL night." This significantly impacts the relaxation factor for many guests.

2. **Theft and security concerns** — Multiple serious reports of bike thefts (e-bikes, regular bikes) even when locked, with police confirming it's "a common occurrence at this park." Goods stolen from vehicles. No warning signs or guest notification despite ongoing issues. Poor CCTV coverage and staff response.

3. **Amenity maintenance deficit** — While grounds are pristine, amenity blocks are "outdated," "atrocious," need "complete renewal" with broken fixtures, leaking taps, inadequate cleaning frequency during peak times, no towel hooks, and old terrazzo floors that are "cracked and tired." Laundry facilities consistently have multiple machines out of order during busiest periods.
