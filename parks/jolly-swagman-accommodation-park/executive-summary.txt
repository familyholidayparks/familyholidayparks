PARK: Jolly Swagman Accommodation Park
SCORE: 52/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 78

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Reviews consistently note "Not a lot here for kids," "No playground for our 3 year old which was dissapointing," and no park nearby to walk to. No mention of any on-site kids activities, pool, or entertainment facilities.

Nature (12/20) — Park benefits from proximity to natural attractions with "walking distance to Picnic Point where you can enjoy excellent views over greenery forests," "plenty of walking tracks across the road," and access to Queens Park. However, the park itself has limited natural setting beyond "beautiful maintained garden" areas.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed feedback with many noting "clean amenities" and "lovely and clean place," but concerning recent reviews citing "rusty kettle," "tomato sauce under plate," "dust under bed," "not very clean or tidy grounds," and "No cleaning of the amenities for 2 days." Standards appear inconsistent.

Value (13/15) — Strong value proposition with "4 nights over the Christmas period under $200," "Fantastic for the price," "cheapest place in Toowoomba," and "weekly rate was extremely cheap." However, one review complained room "definitely not worth $130" due to size and condition.

Site Size (4/10) — Consistently described as having space issues: "Caravan spots were a little tight," "Sites are a bit tight," "Caravan sites are close together," and "Small park." Room sizes also criticized with "My laundry is bigger" and "a bit on the small side."

Sentiment (7/10) — Predominantly positive staff feedback ("friendly," "helpful") but marred by serious complaints about rude staff in recent reviews. Mix of repeat visitors and those who "Won't be back." Good recommendations for budget stays but not for family holidays.

Location (10/10) — Excellent central location consistently praised: "Great location in the centre of town," "close to CBD," "handy to town," "walking distance to Dan Murphy and pub," and "Tourist information centre is across the road." Strong accessibility to Toowoomba attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment facilities—no playground, no pool, no kids activities—making it unsuitable for families with young children seeking holiday park amenities.
2. Inconsistent cleanliness and maintenance standards with recent reviews highlighting dirty dishes, rusty appliances, uncleaned amenities, and untidy grounds suggesting declining upkeep.
3. Tight, cramped caravan sites and very small cabin rooms that limit comfort for families and larger rigs, with multiple reviews comparing accommodation size unfavourably to mining camps or laundries.
