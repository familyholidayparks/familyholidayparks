PARK: BIG4 Hervey Bay Holiday Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 273

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently mention limited on-site entertainment with a basic games room that was often closed, dirty, or relocated to a hot tin shed during renovations; pool table and equipment frequently non-functional; however, staff organized free sausage sizzles, Easter egg hunts, and scavenger hunts that families appreciated.

Nature (12/20) — Park is located in a residential area but within walking distance (2-5 minutes) to Point Vernon beach and dog-friendly off-leash areas; beach is rocky with limited sand; nice bike paths and esplanade walks nearby; some trees and birdlife on-site but not a heavily natural setting.

Cleanliness (9/15) — Mixed reviews with many praising clean amenities and staff efforts, but significant complaints about dirty cabins with expired milk, mouldy showers, grout buildup, hair in drains, lack of basic supplies, and floors so dirty feet turned black; amenities described as old but "always kept clean" by some, "dirty" and "inadequate" by others.

Value (8/15) — Reviews cite "dynamic pricing" charging up to $85-95/night for powered sites despite advertised rates from $63; many felt overpriced for the dated facilities and lack of entertainment; some appreciated "cheap rates" and "excellent value" especially given the location, but majority felt it didn't match typical Big4 standards.

Site Size (5/10) — Frequent complaints about "very small" and cramped sites, narrow roads making reversing difficult, sites "too close to each other" where neighbours could be heard talking; staff helped with parking but multiple reviews describe sites as inadequate for modern caravans; some permanents have junk around sites.

Sentiment (6/10) — Polarized reviews with enthusiastic repeat visitors praising staff friendliness and community atmosphere, but substantial negative sentiment from families who left early, felt deceived by online photos, encountered rude permanent residents or staff, and would "never stay there again"; many recommend alternative parks in Hervey Bay.

Location (10/10) — Strong consensus that location is excellent: quiet residential area away from main Hervey Bay hustle, short walk to beach and convenience store, easy 5-10 minute drive to town center, shops, restaurants, whale watching, and Fraser Island tours; dog beach and esplanade walks highly praised.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Extensive negative feedback about run-down, dirty cabins and amenities that don't meet Big4 standards, with guests reporting expired food, mouldy bathrooms, inadequate cleaning, broken equipment, and floors so dirty their feet turned black.
2. Severely limited on-site entertainment and facilities for children, with games room often closed/relocated during renovations, broken pool tables, no fans in summer, no pool lights for evening swimming, and nothing to keep kids occupied on-site requiring constant day trips.
3. Very small, cramped caravan sites with narrow roads that make parking difficult even with staff assistance, permanent residents creating a "trailer park vibe," and ongoing construction disruption with no advance warning to guests about renovation noise and mess.
