PARK: BIG4 Townsville Gateway Holiday Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 178

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note "only has a basic pool" with "no parks inside" and "not family friendly" being key criticisms. Multiple families reported the pool being down during school holidays, and one review specifically stated "This caravan park unfortunately doesn't accommodate for young children there is only a swimming pool." No mention of waterpark, jumping pillow, playground, kids club, or organized activities in any reviews.

Nature (12/20) — Park backs onto a creek with "quite a lot of shady spots near the creek" though crocodile warning signs present. Reviews mention "lots of shady trees," "mango trees around perimeter," and "plenty birdlife" including curlews. Natural setting scores moderately but highway proximity detracts significantly. One reviewer noted "backing onto a serene little stream added a lovely touch of nature."

Cleanliness (9/15) — Major concerns across recent reviews with "facilities are dirty & run down," "sheets were dirty," "mould in the aircons," "floors sticky and dirty," and "garbage bins overflowing." Multiple reviews cite "tiles missing in showers," "toilet block roof leaked," and maintenance neglect. Some positive mentions of clean amenities exist but heavily outweighed by negative cleanliness reports, particularly in cabins.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback: budget sites praised as "great value" at "$30 a night" and "$25 for a powered site is cheap," but cabin pricing criticized as "$170/night was definitely a let down" and "extremely overpriced at close to $200 night." Several reviews note "good value for money caravan park" for sites but "overpriced for what it offered" for cabins. Laundry costs of $7 per load called "a rip off."

Site Size (9/10) — Consistent praise for "Big grassy sites," "enormous powered and unpowered grass sites," "very spacious park," "generous sized sites," and "large spots for big vans." Multiple reviews mention "big rig sites" and "drive thru sites." One reviewer with a "13.5-meter rig" found backing in "stress-free thanks to the generous site space."

Sentiment (5/10) — Highly polarized with many negative recent experiences. Multiple reviewers state "Will not be staying here again," "Would not stay there again," and "Wouldn't recommend." Security concerns raised with car theft and break-in reports. Management issues noted with "4 managers in 4 months." Some repeat visitors exist but overall sentiment trending negative in 2024-2026 period.

Location (4/10) — Consistently described as "10km out of Townsville CBD," "a bit out of town," and "quite far from town with no local shops." Major issue is proximity to Bruce Highway with "A LOT of noise from the highway," "trucks and traffic ALL day and ALL night." Positive mentions include "5 minutes to Fairfield shopping complex" and "easy access off Bruce Hwy" but distance from city attractions and beach significant drawback for families.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Minimal entertainment facilities for families with young children—no playground, no kids club, no waterpark, just a basic pool that reviewers report was frequently unavailable during peak family times like school holidays, making it unsuitable as a destination park for families seeking activities.

2. Serious and recurring cleanliness and maintenance issues documented across multiple recent reviews, including mould in cabins, dirty sheets, broken facilities, leaking roofs, overflowing bins, and management's apparent unwillingness to invest in repairs, with one staff member stating "the owners don't want to spend any money."

3. Location compromises family holiday experience with significant highway noise pollution affecting sleep, distance from Townsville attractions and beaches requiring constant driving, and concerning security issues including documented theft and break-ins that left families feeling unsafe.
