PARK: Ingenia Holidays Noosa
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-23
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 350

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (15/20) — Strong family entertainment offerings with heated pool, jumping pillow, outdoor movie nights on big screen, games room, and popular pizza van twice weekly. Some reviews noted pool closes early at 5:30pm and games room TV was broken. Kids disco and balloon man mentioned during peak periods.

Nature (13/20) — Park features palm trees providing shade and tropical feel, with hundreds of rainbow lorikeets roosting nightly creating atmosphere. Located near golf course with some kangaroo sightings, but 15-20 minute drive to beaches and national park. Not a natural bush setting but pleasant green spaces.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Generally praised for clean facilities and amenities, with spotless bathrooms and well-maintained grounds. Some negative reviews mentioned overflowing sanitary bins, spiders in bathrooms, dirty camp kitchen at times, and shower blocks needing deep cleaning. Staff clean facilities regularly but maintenance issues noted.

Value (10/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing, with costs ranging $44-137 per night depending on site type. Some felt it was expensive for cramped sites, while others found good value especially for cabins. Comparable to other Noosa parks but some disappointed given price versus site size.

Site Size (6/10) — Most consistent complaint was tight, cramped sites with caravans "squished like sardines" and "you can hear neighbours breathe." Powered sites described as smallest some guests had stayed on. Ensuite sites and alphabet-named sites reported as more spacious. Very challenging for larger rigs.

Sentiment (8/10) — Majority positive with many repeat visitors and recommendations, praising friendly staff especially park manager Nicole and helper Rob who guides vans in. Some very negative experiences with rude staff, refund issues, construction noise, and feeling unwelcome. Overall sentiment warm but not exceptional.

Location (8/10) — Located in Tewantin, 15-20 minute drive to Noosa Main Beach and Hastings Street, 5 minutes to local shops and marina. Bus stop directly outside park. Away from hustle of Noosa but requires driving everywhere. Not walkable to beach or major attractions but convenient access.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site sizes are consistently described as too small and cramped, with caravans packed tightly together making it difficult for larger rigs and reducing privacy—a major complaint across many reviews.

2. Location requires 15-20 minute drive to Noosa beaches and main attractions rather than being walkable, and pool closes early at 5:30pm which disappoints families wanting evening swims.

3. Inconsistent service quality with multiple reports of rude reception staff, refund problems, maintenance issues in cabins, and cleanliness lapses in bathrooms and kitchen areas during busy periods.
