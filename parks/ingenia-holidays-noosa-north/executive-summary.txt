PARK: Ingenia Holidays Noosa North
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-26
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 343

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Strong family entertainment with heated pool, jumping pillow, games room, outdoor cinema nights, and popular twice-weekly pizza van visits. Kids activities and movie nights consistently praised. Some noted pool closes early at 5:30pm and games room could use updating.

Nature (12/20) — Located in suburban Tewantin with palm trees and birdlife (rainbow lorikeets noted frequently), but no beach or natural water features on-site. Requires 15-20 minute drive to Noosa beaches and national park. Adjacent to golf course. Limited walking access to nature from park itself.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Generally clean and well-maintained with spotless amenities receiving frequent praise. However, multiple reviews mentioned bathroom bins overflowing, inconsistent kitchen cleaning during peak times, and some maintenance issues. Staff described as keeping grounds tidy with regular bin collection.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback on value with many noting premium pricing ($100+ per night for sites, $127-200+ for cabins). Some felt it expensive for the compact sites and suburban location. Others praised it as good value for the facilities offered, particularly cabins. Free activities appreciated.

Site Size (6/10) — Consistent criticism of cramped, tight sites with caravans/vans very close together. Reviewers noted difficulty parking larger rigs, feeling "like sardines," and hearing neighbours easily. Described as "smallest sites I've ever stayed on." Staff assistance with parking into tight sites frequently mentioned and appreciated.

Sentiment (9/10) — Predominantly positive sentiment with many return visitors and recommendations. Staff friendliness consistently praised. Families particularly happy with kids amenities. Some notably negative experiences with noise, site allocation, and staff rudeness brought overall sentiment down.

Location (8/10) — Convenient suburban location in Tewantin, 5-10 minutes to shops/marina, but 15-20 minutes drive to Noosa Heads beaches and main attractions. Bus stop outside park entrance noted positively. Described as "away from hustle and bustle" but this means limited walking access to attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site size is the biggest limitation with consistently cramped, tight spacing causing families to feel "squished like sardines" and hearing neighbours through thin cabin walls or close caravan proximity.
2. Location requires 15-20 minute drive to Noosa's main beaches and attractions, with limited nature access or activities within walking distance of the park itself.
3. Inconsistent service quality with multiple reports of rude reception staff, aggressive cleaning staff, poor refund handling, and maintenance issues during peak periods that detracted from otherwise positive experiences.
