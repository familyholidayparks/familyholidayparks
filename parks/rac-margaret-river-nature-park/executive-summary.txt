PARK: RAC Margaret River Nature Park
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 121

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (13/20) — Reviews mention "daily kids activity" and "great bike tracks for the kids through the bush" with extensive mountain bike trails accessible from the park. The communal fire pit at 4pm and playground are highlighted, though some families noted "not much for kids to do" at the accommodation itself, requiring daily trips out.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural setting with reviews praising "beautiful setting among the trees", "gorgeous sites right in the middle of nature", and "walk through the forest from the park into town". The forest environment features native birdlife including kookaburras, walking trails to old mill sites, and direct access to extensive mountain bike trails through bushland.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive feedback with "spotlessly clean", "very well maintained amenities", "cleanest toilet and shower facilities", and "amenities always clean and maintained". A few isolated incidents mentioned dirty cabins on arrival and leaking taps, plus occasional issues when school groups stayed.

Value (13/15) — Most reviews indicate good value with pricing around $37-50 for powered sites, described as "not pricey" and "price was as good as government bush camps". Some guests felt pricing was high for accommodation with cleanliness issues, and bike rental prices varied unexpectedly ($30-50).

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised with "large sites with plenty of space between sites", "very generous site sizes", "roomy sites", and "large, covered with trees, plenty of space between each one". Sites provide good privacy among trees, though safari tents noted as "extremely close together".

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong positive sentiment with many repeat visitors stating "been here every year for the past few years", "we will be back", and "favourite park on our 3 month road trip". A few negative experiences with staff interactions and accommodation cleanliness prevented a perfect score.

Location (9/10) — Well-positioned "super close to margs" with "5 minute drive from Margaret River town" or "20-25 minute walk" through forest trails. Centrally located for exploring wineries and caves, though the single-lane gravel access road and distance from some attractions noted as minor drawbacks.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited on-site entertainment for children beyond the playground and daily activity sessions, with several families noting kids needed daily trips out to stay engaged.
2. Multiple reports of inconsistent service quality including rude staff interactions, unresponsive emergency calls, and poor customer service in specific incidents that left guests feeling discriminated against.
3. Accommodation cleanliness issues in cabins and safari tents including dirty bedding, maintenance problems (broken locks, holes in screens), and occasional noise disturbances from school groups using dorm facilities.
