PARK: NRMA Cairns Holiday Park
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-25
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 246

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Reviews consistently note "limited facilities for kids," "not much to do with kids," and no mention of organized activities, waterpark, or kids' club. One reviewer mentioned a "TV room for kids" but entertainment options are minimal. Thursday pizza van was a rare positive mention.

Nature (10/20) — The park features "lovely shady trees," "tropical foliage," and a "resident bat colony," creating a leafy urban environment. However, vegetation is "clearly not maintained," "unkempt," with "tall grasses" and "hanging branches" causing issues. Close to Botanical Gardens but no beach or creek access. Natural setting is compromised by airport and highway proximity.

Cleanliness (4/15) — Overwhelmingly negative feedback dominates: "dirty camp kitchen is the worst I have ever seen in Australia," "schmutzigste Camping Platz," "amenities were unclean and grubby," "Poep in de douches," "toilets n shower blocks weren't cleaned," "shower walls are extremely dirty," and "rats in the kitchen." Some reviews note "facilities were spotless" but these are vastly outnumbered by cleanliness complaints spanning 2023-2026.

Value (7/15) — Mixed reviews on value. Some found it "great value for the price" and "budget friendly" at $30-45/night. However, many felt "very overpriced for the small camp site," "Preisleistung passt nicht wirklich," and "$45! A rip off" given the cramped sites and poor conditions. Value perception heavily dependent on accommodation type and expectations.

Site Size (3/10) — Consistent criticism across reviews: "sites are so small," "Unacceptably small unpowered sites," "extremely tight," "literally a parking space," "sardine tin type parking," "no room to put our awning up," and "people are literally piled up on top of each other." Powered sites slightly better but still described as "compact" and "tight."

Sentiment (5/10) — Deeply polarized sentiment. Positive reviews praise "friendly staff," "great location," and repeat visits ("3rd visit," "stayed 4 weeks"). However, negative reviews are more numerous and passionate: "worst caravan park I have ever stayed at," "Never again," "we will never be back," "worst experience of my trip to Australia." Many warn others to avoid. Security concerns and theft reports significantly damage sentiment.

Location (9/10) — Strongest scoring category. Reviews consistently praise "Great location," "Very convenient location," "perfect location," "close to everything," with "$1 bus to city," "20-30 minute walk to city centre," and "close to airport." Public transport accessibility via bus 123 frequently mentioned positively. Proximity to Cairns CBD, Esplanade, and Botanical Gardens highly valued.

WHY NOT 100:
1. **Critical cleanliness and maintenance failures** — Systematic evidence of "dirty" facilities, infrequent cleaning, "rats," "cockroaches," overflowing bins, and unmaintained vegetation. Reviews from 2023-2026 consistently report amenities "smell like one big piss," kitchen is "disgusting," and cleaning occurs only "once a day" or less, with some reporting no cleaning during multi-night stays.

2. **Severe security and safety concerns** — Multiple theft reports including "$1200 e-scooter stolen," "$3000 mountain bike stolen," "camping stove stolen," "food stolen," "clothes stolen," plus "violent burglary situation," "homeless people wandering through," and "drug offers." Security measures described as "totally ineffective" with minimal cameras, poor lighting, and "easy access over boundary fences."

3. **Exceptionally cramped sites unsuitable for families** — Reviews repeatedly describe sites as "parking space" sized, with families unable to open doors, set up awnings, or have privacy. "Vehicles and people are literally piled up on top of each other," making the park fundamentally unsuitable for family camping with children who need space to play safely.
