PARK: NRMA Shellharbour Beachside Holiday Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-18
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 135

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Reviews consistently note "there wasn't a lot for kids within the park" with only a "great little playground" and "small playground" mentioned. Families report needing to explore surrounding areas for activities. One positive mentions "live entertainment" and "pizza oven" but these appear infrequent. Limited waterpark or structured kids activities.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural setting with families raving about being "right on the water," "20m from the beaches," and "ocean pool a few metres away." Multiple reviews mention whale watching from sites, "dolphins," rock pools, and "beautiful ocean pool with kids pool." Direct beach access is consistently praised as "spectacular views."

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "very clean amenities" and "immaculate" facilities, but significant concerns from families about "bathrooms didn't appear to be cleaned during our stay," "toilet paper, sand and water scattered around," and "bins were overflowing." Some cabin reviews mention "dirty," "cockroach," and "insects."

Value (12/15) — Reviews note "good value" and "not overly expensive" with NRMA discounts appreciated. However, several families felt cabins were "over priced for this park" at "$330 a night" and "$71 night" for powered sites deemed expensive. Mixed value perception overall.

Site Size (6/10) — Consistent complaints that sites are "quite difficult to get into," "small tight park, tricky in a large van," and "tight squeeze." Multiple reviews warn "God help you if you're bigger" and note "close proximity to other vans." Better for smaller setups.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation with "3rd time visiting" and "we keep coming back," but tempered by management issues, security concerns ("things stolen several times"), and some very negative experiences with disability accommodation. Overall positive but not universally glowing.

Location (10/10) — Perfect scores for location: "500m walk to town," "5 minute walk" to shops and restaurants, "walking distance to shops pub," Shell Cove Marina nearby. Families love the accessibility to amenities while maintaining beachfront position.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited on-site children's entertainment and activities — families with young children consistently report needing to leave the park for kids' activities, with only a small playground available and no structured entertainment programs mentioned.
2. Inconsistent amenities cleaning and maintenance — multiple family reviews specifically note bathrooms not being cleaned during multi-day stays, overflowing bins, hot water issues, and broken fixtures that impact the experience for families with babies and young children.
3. Tight sites unsuitable for larger family rigs — numerous reviews warn that maneuvering larger caravans is "extremely hard" with difficult access, making this challenging for families with bigger setups who need space for kids to play around their site.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"There wasn't a lot for kids within the park", "bathrooms didn't appear to be cleaned during our stay", "Amazing location only 20m from the beaches", "sites are a great size but can still be difficult to get into", "right on the water with unrestricted views", "watching a few Whales having a great time", "Great pool and loads of facilities just a short walk away", "tight squeeze, everyone is just a little bit too close"
