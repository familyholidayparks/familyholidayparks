PARK: Tasman Holiday Parks - Fraser Coast
SCORE: 73/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REWIEWS ANALYSED: 500

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews indicate basic entertainment with pools, games rooms and camp kitchens across locations, but limited dedicated kids clubs or structured activities. Some locations mention "games area" and "rec room with pool table" but no waterparks or extensive children's programming. Families appreciated outdoor spaces for kids to explore but noted "no playground" at some sites.

Nature (16/20) — Strong natural environment across the network with multiple beachfront locations, "beautiful beachfront location," "right on the beach," kangaroo sightings, bird watching, and proximity to Fraser Island. Walking tracks along esplanades, "stunning views," beach access, and natural bush settings feature prominently. River Heads location offers unique rustic bush camping experience.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive feedback on cleanliness with "spotless amenities," "immaculate facilities," "amenities cleaned every day," and "exceptionally clean" appearing frequently. New renovations at Scarness and Burrum Heads receive particular praise. Minor issues noted with some older cabins and occasional maintenance delays.

Value (12/15) — Mixed value perception with some praising "great value for money" and "excellent value" particularly at renovated locations, while others found parks "expensive," "overpriced" or "least value for money during our 6 week trip." Pricing varies significantly across the network from budget-friendly to premium rates.

Site Size (7/10) — Site sizes are inconsistent across locations with "spacious sites" and "generous grassed sites" at renovated parks contrasted with "small site," "tight sites," and "very tight for big rigs" at others. Multiple reviews mention difficulty reversing larger caravans, though staff assistance is frequently offered.

Sentiment (7/10) — Generally positive sentiment with many repeat visitors and recommendations, but significantly impacted by management inconsistencies, customer service complaints, and severe issues at specific locations (particularly River Heads midge problems). Strong positive sentiment at Burrum Heads and Scarness post-renovation, but notable complaints about staff attitude and billing issues.

Location (8/10) — Strategic locations across Fraser Coast with beach access, "walking distance to shops," proximity to Fraser Island ferries, and "central location" praised. Most parks offer easy access to Hervey Bay attractions, esplanade, restaurants and shopping. Some locations described as "15 minute walk away" from main areas.

WHY NOT 100:
Inconsistent management quality with multiple reviews citing rude staff, poor customer service, and billing disputes including double-charging complaints that remained unresolved for years. Severe sandfly and midge infestations at River Heads location made stays "unlivable" for many families despite warnings, with multiple early departures and refund requests. Limited dedicated children's entertainment facilities with no proper playgrounds at several locations, games areas "occupied by older kids," and minimal structured activities beyond basic pools and recreation rooms.
