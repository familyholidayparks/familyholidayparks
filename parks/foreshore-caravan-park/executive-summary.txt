PARK: Foreshore Caravan Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-18
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 55

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note "it probably suits the people travelling without kids more than those with kids" with no mention of playgrounds, jumping pillows, or organized activities on-site. One review mentions "great park next door if you have kids" indicating families rely on external facilities. The park offers natural entertainment through fishing, kayaking, and wildlife watching but lacks structured children's amenities.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional river frontage with reviews praising "amazing view out over the water," "beautiful to wake up to an abundance of pelicans and other waterbirds," and "right on the Nambucca River...you couldn't get any closer." Families enjoyed "fishing and crabbing and boating" with "boat and kayak access." Walking paths along the river toward the ocean and evening bat displays provide natural wonder.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive feedback: "amenities spotless," "super clean," "very clean and tidy amenities," "older but spotless amenities." Multiple reviews across all years consistently praise cleanliness despite noting facilities are "older vintage" or "outdated but clean." One negative review about a worn mattress prevents a perfect score.

Value (9/15) — Mixed feedback with several complaints: "$40 for an unpowered campsite is a bit much," "$60.00 per night for powered site...very expensive," and "$40 a night for an un powered is shameful. This is a $25 a night unpowered experience." However, positive mentions of "very reasonably priced" and "good value" from families who stayed longer suggest better value for extended stays.

Site Size (6/10) — Reviews note "reasonably small sites that might be difficult for vans over 8 metres" and "very squashing, water view blocked by trees." While some praised "great sites" and riverfront positions, the limited space and tight layout for larger rigs is a clear constraint mentioned by multiple reviewers.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong repeat visitor loyalty with phrases like "our go to place," "we will definitely be back," "stayed here numerous times," and families extending stays. Very positive tone overall with excellent host relationships, though two one-star reviews (mattress complaint and cramped sites) indicate occasional disappointments that prevent a perfect score.

Location (8/10) — Excellent positioning "close to shops," "easy walk to town, shops, RSL," and "walking distance to town." Beach access via walking paths and proximity to clubs, cafes, and amenities praised. However, being on a river rather than ocean beachfront and requiring walks/drives to main attractions prevents top marks.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of on-site children's entertainment facilities (playgrounds, games room, jumping pillow, kids club) with multiple reviews explicitly stating it suits couples more than families with young children.
2. Pricing perceived as excessive for the level of facilities provided, particularly for unpowered sites at $40-60/night, with no camp kitchen and minimal amenities beyond BBQ plates.
3. Small site sizes problematic for larger caravans over 8 metres, with cramped layouts and limited space reducing appeal for families with bigger rigs and multiple vehicles.
