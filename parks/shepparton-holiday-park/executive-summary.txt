PARK: Shepparton Holiday Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 109

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Limited on-site entertainment with reviews mentioning a small playground, pool with strict rules (no jumping, no balls), tennis court frequently closed, and a sparse recreation room with ping pong table. Multiple families noted lack of activities for children and entertainment facilities feeling empty or non-functional.

Nature (10/20) — Park described as on edge of town in somewhat industrial area with grassy sites and some trees providing shade. Reviews mention bird life and rural setting, but limited natural attractions or nearby nature experiences. No beach, creek, or significant natural features mentioned.

Cleanliness (9/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising clean amenities, spotless showers, and well-maintained facilities, but significant complaints about specific cabins being dirty, dusty, mouldy, with ant problems, blood-stained bedding, and neglected maintenance in older units.

Value (8/15) — Reviews split on value with some praising reasonable pricing and budget accommodation, but others strongly criticizing high prices ("$75 a night", "expensive laundry $7.97", "$20 site increase") relative to aging facilities and limited amenities.

Site Size (7/10) — Positive comments about "large grassed sites", "generous size", "plenty of room", concrete slabs for caravans, and space for vehicles. Sites described as adequate with good accessibility.

Sentiment (6/10) — Highly polarized reviews with loyal repeat visitors praising the park versus strong negative experiences with management. Multiple reports of guests being evicted or asked to leave, strict rule enforcement, and rude management behavior significantly impacting overall sentiment, though some families report wonderful stays.

Location (8/10) — Generally positive feedback on location "4kms from Shepparton", "6 minutes to town", "close to shops, facilities, museums", convenient access while maintaining quiet rural setting away from city noise.
