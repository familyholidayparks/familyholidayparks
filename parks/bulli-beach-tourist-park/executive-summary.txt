PARK: Bulli Beach Tourist Park
SCORE: 71/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-27
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 163

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Limited on-site entertainment with only a basic playground and occasional activities mentioned. One German review referenced daily events like bingo and craft sessions, but recent reviews rarely mention structured kids' activities. The park relies heavily on natural attractions rather than organized entertainment, with families praising the bike tracks, beach, and rock pools rather than park-provided activities.

Nature (19/20) — Outstanding natural environment with direct beach access consistently praised as the park's highlight. Multiple ocean rock pools both north and south, patrolled beaches, extensive coastal walking and cycling paths stretching to Wollongong, and abundant wildlife including cockatoos, lorikeets, and rabbits. Families repeatedly describe it as "right on the beach," "5 second walk from the beach," and "100m to the water."

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed cleanliness standards create inconsistency. The newer southern/top amenities block receives praise for being "modern and clean," while the older front block is repeatedly criticized as "filthy," "dated," "revolting," and having "dirty water bubbling up through sinks." Several cabin reviews mention dirty conditions including "sand on beds," "stinky wet couches," "cockroaches," and "dirty plates." Positive reviews note clean grounds and well-maintained sites, suggesting inconsistent cleaning standards.

Value (12/15) — Pricing viewed as reasonable by some ($55-62 per night for powered sites) but expensive by others given limited amenities. Multiple reviews express frustration paying premium rates without WiFi access, with one stating rates are "comparable to full-service hotels" yet lack basic connectivity. Security concerns and theft incidents also impact value perception. Positive reviews appreciate seniors discounts and affordability compared to Sydney alternatives.

Site Size (7/10) — Sites receive mixed feedback with families noting they're "generous in width but narrow in depth," requiring many larger vans (8m+, 19.6ft) to park at angles. Reviewers mention "sites are hard to access for large vans," "very tight," and "not very long." Positive comments note "large sites," "good size grass sites," and adequate space for smaller rigs. Mud issues in wet weather significantly impact usability.

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive sentiment with many families returning and recommending the location, though recent reviews show declining satisfaction. Multiple guests report it as their "second home" and "favourite place," but recent reviews increasingly mention security concerns (knife incident, theft), early morning noise disturbances (6am rubbish trucks, restaurant staff), and management issues with bookings. Repeat visitation is strong among those prioritizing beach access.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for location with unanimous praise for beachfront positioning. Woolworths 2 minutes away, close to cafes and restaurants, easy access to Wollongong, Sea Cliff Bridge, and regional attractions. Walking and cycling paths provide excellent connectivity. Only minor drawback is the adjacent cemetery, though most families view this as quirky rather than problematic.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness inconsistencies between old and new amenities blocks, with multiple reports of filthy conditions, broken facilities, and inadequate maintenance in older areas that create unacceptable standards for family accommodation.

2. Significant security vulnerabilities with open access from public bike paths leading to theft incidents (stolen food from camp fridge, dirtbike theft with knife threat at 2:30am), creating safety concerns especially for families with young children and solo travelers.

3. Complete absence of WiFi despite premium pricing and lack of structured on-site entertainment for children, forcing the park to rely entirely on natural attractions while charging rates comparable to full-service facilities that provide these basic modern amenities.
