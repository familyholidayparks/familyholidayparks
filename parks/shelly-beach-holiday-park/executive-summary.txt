PARK: Shelly Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 79

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Consistently praised waterpark with slides and splash areas dominates positive feedback; "my 12 kids enjoyed the waterslide very much", "waterpark was the main highlight", families report children loving the pool facilities and not wanting to leave, weekend food van adds convenience, long weekend activities mentioned.

Nature (16/20) — Exceptional beachfront position with Shelly Beach across the road, "stones throw from Shelly Beach", "can hear the ocean from our cabin", beautiful rocky beach, Wyrrabalong Coastal Walk nearby, 5-minute walk to beach with cart access, ocean views from some cabins, "watching sunrise and sunset was the ultimate".

Cleanliness (8/15) — Major inconsistency emerges as critical weakness; newer cabins praised as "super clean", but numerous reports of older cabins being "filthy", "dusty and gross", mould in bathrooms and air conditioners, dirty kitchenware, stained bedding, "feet were black after walking around", shower facilities "major letdown" with maintenance issues, mixed reports on amenity cleanliness.

Value (10/15) — Mixed value perception with some finding it "value for money" and "fantastic for a family holiday without costing an arm or a leg", but multiple complaints about steep pricing ($330-$399/night) versus quality delivered, "not worth $399 per night", "price quite steep for quality of accommodation", reasonable pricing noted by large groups.

Site Size (5/10) — Limited site information but campsites described as "small, like a sandy, dirty parking spot", "quite packed in", parking limited to one car per cabin, narrow bathrooms noted, camp/RV sites on main road, doesn't accommodate full-size caravans only pop-tops and camper trailers.

Sentiment (7/10) — Polarized responses with enthusiastic families returning annually versus others leaving early demanding refunds; strong repeat visitors "go up every year", "we'll definitely be back", "8th Christmas family get together", but also "will never return again", "left a day early", "worst caravan park I have stayed at", newer cabin guests significantly more satisfied.

Location (9/10) — Standout feature with "top notch" positioning, beachfront access, "stones throw from Shelly Beach", walking distance to Bateau Bay shopping centre with Coles/Aldi/Woolworths, cafés nearby, bowling club access with membership discount, golf course next door, short drives to attractions, "awesome location".

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness inconsistency between old and new cabins with multiple reports of mould, dust, filth, and poor maintenance in older beachfront units undermining guest confidence and creating negative experiences worth immediate refunds.
2. Small cramped campsites backing onto busy main road creating excessive traffic noise keeping families awake all night, with park acknowledging issue but continuing to offer these sites.
3. Poor maintenance standards throughout older sections with broken shower facilities, potholes, inadequate lighting, thin walls, uncomfortable old mattresses, and general "run down" appearance suggesting deferred investment.
