PARK: Edgewater Holiday Park
SCORE: 48/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-09
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 32

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Multiple families explicitly noted the absence of kids play equipment, with one stating "I wish they had kids play equipment handy." One family mentioned children enjoying a sandy beach area and watching planes, and there's a pool, but overall entertainment infrastructure for young families is severely lacking with no structured activities mentioned.

Nature (15/20) — The park's strongest asset is its riverside location with "beautiful views of the river" and waterfront access. Reviews consistently praise the "fantastic new public shelter and covered deck directly on the river" for watching boats and enjoying sunsets. Close to national parks with free ferry access for bikes, offering good nature connectivity.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Highly inconsistent across reviews. While some recent visitors found cabins "clean and tidy" and amenities "cleaned every morning," multiple families reported serious issues: "cobwebs in every corner, leaking taps, holes in curtains," "ants crawling under the bunk beds," "dirty linen," "poop dried on the wall," and "pool bottom was grimy." Facilities described as "tired," "outdated," and needing renovation.

Value (7/15) — Mixed feedback with concerns about pricing versus condition. One reviewer stated it was "the most expensive park we used on a five week trip to Queensland and back and was no where near like value for money." Another cited "$10/set of linen needed and it comes dirty." Some appreciated "affordable" rates, but overall the dated facilities don't justify pricing for most family reviewers.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews mention "sites are large" and one family enjoyed the "grassy area" for gatherings. However, safety concerns were raised about being "situated under a huge widow maker gum tree" with branches dropping. Powered sites available but limited specific feedback on site spaciousness for families with children.

Sentiment (3/10) — Predominantly negative family sentiment with multiple 1-star reviews from families stating "will be last time I will be bringing my family back," "I wouldn't bring my dog here," and "never again." Several reviews cite rude staff and poor service. While some positive experiences exist, repeat family visitation intent is very low, particularly from recent reviews.

Location (8/10) — Strong location scores with "less than a 5 minute drive" to town centre, "walking distance to Aldi, food spots & other department stores." Right on the river with boat ramp access. Near airport (small planes noted but not overly noisy). "Central to everything" though "a bit out of town" from main Port Macquarie attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of children's play equipment and entertainment facilities makes this unsuitable for families with young children seeking an active holiday experience.
2. Severe and recurring cleanliness issues including pest problems (ants), unhygienic conditions (dirty linen, poop on walls, pubic hair in drains), and poorly maintained cabins with broken fixtures significantly detract from family comfort and safety.
3. Consistently reported poor customer service with multiple reviews citing "rude staff," unhelpful reception, and inhospitable management creating negative experiences that drive families away permanently.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"I wish they had kids play equipment handy", "run down, unhospitable", "cobwebs in every corner, leaking taps, holes in curtains", "ants crawling under the bunk beds", "dirty linen that wasn't on the beds", "last time I will be bringing my family back", "most expensive park we used", "beautiful views of the river"
