PARK: Snowy River Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 99

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Reviews consistently highlight the natural river environment for play, with families mentioning "children happily playing in the river", rope swings, swimming, kayaking, bike riding, and "two toddlers were perfectly safe walking and riding bikes around the park". A playground exists across the road. However, no on-site structured kids' entertainment, waterpark, jumping pillow, or organized activities were mentioned. One review notes "DVD choices always thrill the kids" in cabins.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural setting with near-perfect scores. "Right on the banks of the Snowy River" appears repeatedly, with families praising "stunning scenery", "picturesque blend of green pastures", direct river access for swimming, platypus sightings, riverside walks, "lush grass, shady trees", and proximity to Kosciuszko National Park. Described as "peaceful", "tranquil", and "beautiful natural setting".

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding consistency across all reviews. "Spotlessly clean", "immaculate amenities", "super clean", "impeccably maintained", "very clean modern facilities", "amenities are spotless" mentioned repeatedly. Staff actively maintain cleanliness with "always be somewhere there cleaning them". Both amenity blocks and cabins praised as "very clean and tidy".

Value (12/15) — Mixed signals on value. Described as "very reasonably priced", "great value for money", and "washing machines so reasonably priced". However, one loyal reviewer notes price increase from "$45 to $65 a night" making them "rethink our holiday", particularly given remote location and lack of dump point. Another mentions "overpriced for camping with no electricity". Generally seen as reasonable but not exceptional value.

Site Size (9/10) — Strong positive feedback. "Very spacious and green", "massive sites", "powered sites are large", "plenty of room", "lot of room and had shade", "spacious" mentioned frequently. Some reviews note "sites need hose/power extensions to make the distance" suggesting variable site configurations, preventing perfect score.

Sentiment (10/10) — Exceptional guest loyalty and satisfaction. "We have been coming here for the past 7 years", "family favourite we keep coming back to year after year", "It's becoming our tradition", numerous mentions of extending stays, and consistent "will definitely be back" statements. High recommendation rate with "highly recommend" appearing throughout reviews.

Location (8/10) — Good accessibility but remote. "30 minutes from Jindabyne", "45 minutes from the SkiTube", "under 30 minutes to Jindabyne", close to Kosciuszko National Park. Pub across the road is major plus. However, described as "very isolated town", "very remote", with limited food options (pub only Wednesday-Sunday). "Not in the middle of town" but proximity to attractions and natural features compensates.

WHY NOT 100:
1. No dedicated on-site entertainment infrastructure for children - no waterpark, playground on property, jumping pillow, kids' club, or organized activities that family holiday parks typically offer.
2. Remote location with limited services - food only available at pub Wednesday-Sunday, no dump point for caravans, and isolation may challenge families wanting easy access to amenities and variety.
3. Value concerns emerging with recent price increases (from $45 to $65) that loyal customers note as potentially prohibitive, plus issues with hot water in showers and lack of air conditioning in cabins reducing comfort value.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"Safe for kids in the shallow sandy rock pools", "children happily playing in the river", "two toddlers were perfectly safe walking and riding bikes", "immaculate amenities", "spotlessly clean", "Right on the banks of the Snowy River", "wholesome family friendly atmosphere", "family favourite we keep coming back to year after year"
