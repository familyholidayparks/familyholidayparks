PARK: Waterloo Bay Tourist Park
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 95

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews mention free go-carts for kids and a jumping pillow, but minimal organised activities or kids entertainment facilities noted. One review mentioned go-carts as "the only good thing" but otherwise entertainment options are scarce.

Nature (16/20) — Strong coastal location with beautiful beach access within walking distance. Multiple reviews praise "tucked in behind the dunes of the most beautiful beach," "walking distance to the beautiful bay," impressive coastline, and scenic bay views. Dog-friendly beach nearby adds appeal.

Cleanliness (3/15) — Significant and consistent criticism across recent reviews: "FILTHY AMENITIES, FILTHY CAMP KITCHEN," "standard of cleanliness was appalling," "amenities haven't been cleaned," "stink of urine," "cabin smelled like strong of males urine," "cobwebs everywhere," dirt, hair, and insufficient daily cleaning. Long-term visitors note dramatic decline from previous standards.

Value (8/15) — Mixed feedback with some finding it "good value considering the price," "affordable cabin," and reasonable at $40/night, while others felt overcharged given the poor cleanliness standards. Budget cabins received better value ratings than premium options.

Site Size (5/10) — Multiple complaints about "very small" sites, "snugly sites if your a big rig," caravans couldn't fully extend slide-outs, and inadequate space allocation. Some larger ensuite sites praised, but general site sizing problematic for bigger rigs.

Sentiment (4/10) — Highly polarised with recent reviews predominantly negative citing rude staff, poor service, and cleanliness issues. Long-term repeat visitors expressed disappointment at decline. Some positive experiences noted but recent trend heavily negative with guests leaving early and not recommending.

Location (10/10) — Universally praised as "excellent location," "perfect location," "best location on beach and in town," close to shops, pub, bakery, jetty, and swimming beach all within walking distance. Beachside position is the park's strongest asset.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness issues dominate recent reviews with filthy cabins, amenities not cleaned for days, urine odours, and general lack of maintenance creating an unhygienic environment that prompted early departures.
2. Consistently poor customer service and rude reception staff who provide minimal welcome, inadequate orientation, dismissive responses to complaints, and unprofessional booking management including last-minute cancellations.
3. Infrastructure deficiencies including no proper dump point (only open drain near dishwashing area), broken laundry machines, inadequate lighting, missing amenities like soap and toilet paper, and poorly maintained facilities creating significant inconvenience.
