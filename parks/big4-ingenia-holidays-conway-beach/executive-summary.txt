PARK: BIG4 Ingenia Holidays Conway Beach
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2024
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 82

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Families consistently praise the twice-daily train rides that children loved, the splash park/waterpark outside the pool fence for anytime access, jumping pillow, playground, and go-cart rentals. The unique 1950s American diner is a huge hit with excellent burgers, milkshakes and pizza nights. Some reviews note the water park is now a splash park with no slides.

Nature (16/20) — The park offers beautiful beach access just 100-150m away, perfect for walks and fishing with views overlooking the Whitsundays. Multiple reviews mention breathtaking wildlife and peaceful natural surroundings. However, the beach is not suitable for swimming (500m to water), and crocodiles are present in the area.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelming praise for spotless grounds, immaculate amenities, clean toilets (described as "best looking toilets I have ever seen" with colorful decor), well-maintained facilities, and "cleanest BBQs I've ever seen." One negative review mentioned a septic tank odor issue at site 57, and another mentioned sewage smell.

Value (10/15) — Significant concerns about pricing with one family paying $720 for 2 powered sites for 3 nights due to per-person charges ($75-105/night). Several reviews mention "extremely expensive for camping sites" and pricing discrepancies. However, many found ensuite sites and facilities good value, and the diner offers reasonable prices.

Site Size (8/10) — Reviews consistently mention "spacious sites," "big sites," sites "roomy enough to spread out," and easy parking for a 27-foot van. Sites are nicely separated with palm plantings. One review noted a long walk to toilets from some sites.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong positive sentiment with many "will be back" comments and repeat visitors. Staff described as friendly, welcoming, and accommodating. However, several serious complaints about refund issues, cancelled bookings without notice, poor communication about after-hours check-in, and management disputes significantly impact sentiment.

Location (7/10) — The park is 30 minutes from Airlie Beach in a quiet, isolated location. While this appeals to those seeking peace, it's remote with no nearby shops or services. Reviews note you must be "prepared for days at a time" and it's better suited for fishing trips than typical family holidays.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Value concerns are significant with multiple families reporting extremely expensive pricing due to per-person charges rather than per-site rates, with some feeling misled about actual costs versus advertised rates.

2. Several serious management and customer service issues including disputed refunds requiring Fair Trading complaints, bookings cancelled without proper notice, poor after-hours communication, and confrontational interactions that left families extremely disappointed.

3. The remote location 30 minutes from Airlie Beach with no phone/internet connectivity, no nearby amenities, mosquito problems, and heat/humidity issues (pool water too hot to cool down) makes it challenging for families seeking a typical holiday park experience rather than a fishing-focused getaway.
