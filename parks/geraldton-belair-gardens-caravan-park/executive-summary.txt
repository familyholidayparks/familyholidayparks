REVIEWS ANALYSED: 323

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews mention pool, jumping pillow, and playground facilities with bouncing cushion for kids, but limited structured activities or water features beyond basic pool; majority of reviews focused on beach access rather than on-site entertainment.

Nature (14/20) — Park is directly across from Pages Beach with walking paths to lighthouse, dog beach nearby, dolphins spotted, shady casuarina trees throughout sites; however, industrial port setting significantly detracts from natural ambience.

Cleanliness (6/15) — Deeply polarised reviews: newer amenities blocks described as "spotless" and "very clean" but older blocks repeatedly called "disgusting," "ancient," "cracked walls," "1960s prison amenity block," with sewage smells, flooding showers, and dirty water flowing between cubicles; potholes throughout roads.

Value (5/15) — Numerous reviews cite overpricing for condition: "$60 powered site fee...a rip off," "$200.00 for this is a disgrace," "most expensive ensuite site" with no concrete slab, "way too expensive for what it is," with theft costs adding to guest expenses.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews mention "large spots," "good sized sites," "roomy" sites with concrete slabs, "double concrete site slabs," and "lots of space," though some note "tight" caravan sites and narrow spots in older sections.

Sentiment (4/10) — Severely damaged by security concerns with multiple reviews stating "never stay again," "won't be back," "would not recommend," "avoid this place," "notorious for burglaries"; theft incidents include car break-ins, smashed windows, stolen bikes, generators, and belongings; positive comments from repeat visitors outweighed by safety fears.

Location (4/10) — Beach access praised as "just across the road" with lighthouse and walking paths, but industrial port setting with 24-hour train shunting, cargo ships, rail yards, and truck noise heavily criticised; 5-minute drive to town but isolated from restaurants and shopping; "middle in industriegebied."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Persistent and well-documented theft problem with car break-ins, smashed windows, stolen bikes and belongings occurring regularly despite warnings from staff; multiple reviews report police attendance and describe theft as "notorious" and "very regular occurrence."

2. Severely outdated and poorly maintained older amenities blocks described as "disgusting," "worst toilets I have used," with flooding showers, sewage smells, dirty water flow between cubicles, broken locks, crumbling walls, and facilities dating back to the 1970s requiring complete rebuilding.

3. Intrusive 24-hour industrial noise from adjacent port operations including train horns, rail shunting, cargo loading, truck traffic, and reverse beepers making sleep extremely difficult, with numerous families reporting being woken "2 to 5 times a night" despite the park having no control over this.
