PARK: Beerwah Caravan Park
SCORE: 63/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-25
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 108

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Reviews consistently note there are "no special offers for children," no pool, no playground, and no jumping pillows. One German review explicitly states "Man sollte wissen, dass es für Kinder auf diesem Platz keine speziellen Angebote gibt" (no special offers for kids). This is clearly an adults-oriented park with zero on-site entertainment.

Nature (15/20) — The park is set "amongst strawberry fields" in a "rural part" of Beerwah with "beautiful quiet spot" character. Reviewers mention it's close to "Glasshouse Mountains" for hiking and "Australia Zoo" (8 minutes away). While peaceful and surrounded by working farms, it's not directly in nature but rather rural agricultural land.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Overwhelmingly consistent praise: "Cleanest amenities in any caravan park I've seen," "spotlessly clean," "immaculate," "the cleanest we have seen in a whole lap of Aus," "facilities are very clean," "cleaned daily." This is the park's strongest feature with near-universal acclaim.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback on value. Several reviews mention $50/night pricing with comments like "a bit high for where it is," "overpriced," though others note "good value," "excellent value for money," and "cheaper than closer to the coast." The 10% Australia Zoo discount was appreciated by multiple reviewers.

Site Size (9/10) — Reviews consistently describe "spacious sites," "large sites," "roomy," "10m X 13m with a solid concrete pad," "adequate space between vans," and "catered for our 31ft motorhome easily." Very strong feedback on site sizing.

Sentiment (8/10) — Largely positive with hosts Fay and Peter receiving exceptional praise ("wonderful hosts," "incredibly friendly," "super helpful"). However, 7 negative reviews (1-star ratings) citing rudeness, management issues, and unfriendly experiences drag down the score. Most guests would return but some had poor interactions.

Location (2/10) — Major weakness for families: "far from everything," "a bit out of the way," "in the middle of nowhere," "7kms off the Bruce Highway." While close to Australia Zoo (positive), it's distant from beaches, town amenities, and major attractions that families typically seek.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Zero on-site entertainment facilities for children — no pool, playground, jumping pillow, games room, or organized activities, making this unsuitable as a destination holiday park for families with young kids.
2. Remote rural location far from beaches, shopping, and family attractions beyond Australia Zoo, requiring significant driving for most activities families would want.
3. Multiple concerning reviews about management being "rude," "bossy," "unprofessional," with incidents including water being turned off during showers and defensive behavior, suggesting inconsistent customer service experiences despite many positive reviews of the hosts.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"Cleanest amenities in any caravan park I've seen," "no special offers for children on this site," "doesn't have a pool," "Fay and Pete are absolutely wonderful hosts," "8 minutes away from Australia Zoo," "far from everything," "mostly permanents," "surrounded by strawberry fields"
