PARK: Discovery Parks - Coolwaters, Yeppoon
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-06-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 155

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Consistently praised as having exceptional facilities with "quite possibly one of the best" pool/water park areas including multiple pools, water slides, splash park, jumping pillow, tennis court, basketball court, and organized school holiday activities. Families report kids "loved all play equipment" and "there is so much to do." Minor points lost for occasional facility closures and noise from water slide stairs.

Nature (14/20) — Good natural setting with beach access across the road, adjacent to Causeway Lake with boat ramp and fishing opportunities, and proximity to Byfield National Parks and Great Keppel Island. However, some reviews note occasional "rotting kelp smell" from the Causeway, and the park is 10-15 minutes from Yeppoon town center, limiting immediate nature immersion.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Highly variable feedback with many praising "immaculately clean" facilities and "best showers," but significant negative reports of dirty amenities, ensuites needing "really good clean," "mouse poo on floor," inconsistent cleaning frequency, and sewage smells in some areas. Recent reviews suggest ongoing maintenance but inconsistent standards.

Value (9/15) — Major concern across reviews with frequent complaints about pricing: "extremely over priced," "$65 per night for unpowered dirt/mud sites," "$77.60/night too expensive," unexpected price jumps ("$47 vs $30 standard"), and charging by van size. Some found deals through G'day Parks, but overall consensus is pricing doesn't match site quality or amenities provided.

Site Size (7/10) — Mixed feedback with some reporting "nice big sites" and "massive for our 24ft van," but others noting "packed in like sardines," "sites are quiet jam packed," and smaller camping sites. Ensuite sites generally better, but standard powered and unpowered sites vary significantly in size and quality (grass vs dust/sand).

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitor base with many families returning annually and praising staff friendliness ("AMAZING SUCH A FANTASTIC TEAM"). However, significant negative experiences around management decisions (evictions, refund policies), inconsistent customer service, and unresolved complaints moderate the overall positive sentiment.

Location (7/10) — Good base for exploring region (Great Keppel Island, Byfield, fishing spots) with beach access across road and lake adjacent. However, "nowhere close to Yeppoon," "10-15 mins into Yeppoon," "no food options within walking distance," and some isolation noted. Position works well for touring families but less ideal for those wanting walkable amenities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Cleanliness and maintenance are highly inconsistent—while some praise spotless facilities, numerous recent reviews report dirty amenities, inadequate cleaning frequency, broken equipment, and sewage odors, indicating quality control issues.
2. Value for money is poor with widespread complaints about high pricing that doesn't match site quality, unexpected charges, Saturday premiums, and unpowered sites on dirt/mud at premium rates creating significant dissatisfaction.
3. Site allocation quality varies dramatically from excellent grassed ensuite sites to dusty bowls, sites near dump points, sandy patches with no grass, tree root hazards, and poor drainage, with management seemingly unable or unwilling to address placement issues fairly.
