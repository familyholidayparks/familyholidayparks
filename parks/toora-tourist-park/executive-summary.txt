PARK: Toora Tourist Park
SCORE: 83/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-04
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 80

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (18/20) — Exceptional variety consistently praised: heated indoor pool and spa, jumping pillow, two playgrounds, mini golf, trampoline, tennis/basketball courts, games room with extensive DVD library and board games. Families report 'kids never ran out of fun things to do' and 'didn't need to leave the park'. Multiple reviews cite it as perfect entertainment for children with activities keeping families occupied for entire stays.

Nature (16/20) — Set in beautiful South Gippsland with scenic mountain backdrops and 'spectacular autumn foliage'. Close proximity to Wilson's Promontory (30 mins), beaches, Agnes Falls, Port Welshpool Long Jetty, and Great Southern Rail Trail (750m). Described as 'quiet, secluded feel sheltered from wind' with nice greenery throughout park. Natural environment praised though park itself is more manicured than bushland.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently described as 'one of the cleanest parks, toilets and showers we've ever seen', 'immaculate', 'very clean amenities', and 'extremely well kept and clean'. Camp kitchens 'very well equipped and maintained'. Minor issues noted with one shower fixture and occasional ensuite flooding, plus two negative reviews about maintenance needs during peak periods.

Value (13/15) — Repeatedly praised as 'very competitive pricing', 'better value than Big 4', and 'without making a huge hole in your wallet'. All facilities included with stay (pool, spa, tennis, mini golf, games). One significant complaint about $92/night long weekend pricing with 3-night minimum described as 'gouging'. Overall strong value perception for families.

Site Size (7/10) — Mixed feedback: sites described as 'well maintained', 'flat and shady', with drive-through options praised. However, multiple reviews note 'caravan sites are very small', 'narrow powered grass sites', 'cramped sites' and 'sardine city'. Some sites not level. Ensuite sites rated more spacious. Cabins praised as 'most spacious in Victoria'.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with high repeat visitation ('second time stay', 'will be back again', 'we will be regulars'). Described as 'top 3 stays in all Australia', 'hidden gem', 'fantastic place', '10 out of 10'. Owners Jenny and Marshall consistently praised for hospitality. Few negative reviews (2 low ratings from 80 total). Strong recommendation rate for families.

Location (6/10) — Walking distance to Toora town (IGA, pub, pharmacy, cafes) is convenient. Excellent base for Wilson's Promontory and Gippsland exploration. However, town itself is quiet with 'not much happening' and 'most sightseeing places more than 1 hour drive'. Not a beach/coastal location. Good for touring base but not destination location itself.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site size issues are consistently mentioned - caravan sites described as narrow, cramped, and some not level, limiting appeal for larger rigs despite the excellent facilities.
2. Remote location means the actual town of Toora has limited attractions and most sightseeing destinations require 1+ hour drives, reducing spontaneous exploration opportunities.
3. Minor but recurring maintenance issues noted including aging facilities, some equipment wearing out, limited pool hours (closing at 5pm in winter), shower fixture problems, and occasional cleanliness lapses during peak periods.
