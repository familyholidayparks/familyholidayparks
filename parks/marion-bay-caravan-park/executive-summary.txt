PARK: Marion Bay Caravan Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-08
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 86

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Limited structured kids entertainment with a bouncy pillow/jumping castle mentioned frequently and a small playground with chess set. Holiday periods feature occasional live music and food trucks. Free pancake breakfasts delighted families. No pool or waterpark facilities.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding natural setting with beach access literally steps from sites, located at the gateway to Dhilba Guuranda-Innes National Park. Families praised the "beautiful natural beach," excellent fishing opportunities, and spectacular coastal scenery. Perfect for exploring pristine beaches and national park wilderness.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "spotless" amenities and "very clean" facilities, but concerning reports of dirty cabin floors, sewage smells, poor cabin maintenance, uncleaned camp kitchens over multi-day stays, and amenities "not cleaned in 5 days."

Value (12/15) — Generally described as "great value for money" with reasonable pricing for powered sites. Some felt it "a bit expensive" and cabin issues undermined value. Special touches like bread delivery and pancake breakfasts added value for families.

Site Size (8/10) — "Good size sites" and "fairly large spaces" mentioned regularly with "generous space between sites" appreciated by families with larger rigs. Mix of grassy and gravel sites with artificial grass pads on some sites.

Sentiment (7/10) — Strong repeat visitors and many "will definitely be back" comments, but significantly impacted by multiple 1-star reviews citing aggressive management behavior, inconsistent rule enforcement, poor communication, and allegations of discriminatory treatment that created uncomfortable stays.

Location (9/10) — Exceptional beachfront location "right on the beach" with direct national park access. Remote setting means limited nearby services (1km to nearest food), but families valued the peaceful escape and proximity to fishing spots and scenic drives.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant management and customer service issues documented in multiple reviews including aggressive confrontations, inconsistent policy enforcement (particularly around vehicle rules), poor communication regarding booking extensions, and allegations of discriminatory behavior toward families.
2. Inconsistent cabin maintenance with reports of non-working appliances (ovens, stoves, lights, air conditioning), poor cleanliness, sewage smells, broken doors, and inadequate response when issues reported to reception.
3. Limited on-site entertainment for children beyond a bouncy pillow and small playground, no swimming pool, and very basic facilities for families seeking structured activities during their stay.
