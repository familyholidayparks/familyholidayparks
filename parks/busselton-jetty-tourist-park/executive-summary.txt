PARK: Busselton Jetty Tourist Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 90

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (5/20) — Multiple reviews explicitly state "no pool, jumping pillows or play equipment for the kids" and "No kid related playground for that price either". One review mentions "playing fields opposite" but no on-site entertainment facilities exist, making this a basic park for families seeking external activities.

Nature (16/20) — Excellent beach access with "lovely beach 2 min walk away", "100m to beach", and "walking distance to jetty, foreshore". The park features "lovely shady spots", "lots trees", and "well shaded by trees". Located opposite the iconic 1.8km Busselton Jetty with water views and natural coastal environment throughout.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Generally positive with "facilities were always clean", "clean amenities", "spotlessly clean cabin", and "very clean" repeated across reviews. However, concerns noted including "dirty kitchen", "horrible smell" in one cabin, "possum mess", and "sewerage smells around the camp" bring the score down from excellent.

Value (10/15) — Mixed feedback with some praising "cheap price", "excellent value", "very good price", while others complained "way over priced for wat u actually get", "$169 per night didn't feel it was value for money", and "overpriced for the size". Two-night minimum policy frustrated some guests.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews describe "big van sites", "spacious enough", "good size", and "large shady sites", but multiple complaints about "very uneven site", "tight spaces", "sites are uneven and too close together", and difficulty leveling motorhomes. Some sites challenging for larger rigs.

Sentiment (7/10) — Positive location praise throughout but marred by complaints about "rude staff", "reception absolutely vile on check out", theft issues ("2 gas bottles stolen", "e-bikes and trikes taken"), safety concerns with "unsupervised children running around", and "no fencing and no security". New management receiving better recent feedback.

Location (10/10) — Universally praised as "fantastic location", "perfect location", "awesome location" with "walking distance to the beach, town and restaurants", "right opposite Busselton Jetty precinct", "5 minutes walk to Busselton Jetty", and "short walk to town centre". Ideal base for exploring Margaret River region.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of on-site kids entertainment facilities (no pool, playground, jumping pillow or activities) severely limits appeal for families with young children seeking self-contained holiday experiences.
2. Security concerns with multiple theft reports (gas bottles, heaters, bikes) and open access with "no fencing", "no gate", and "random people walking through the park" creating safety issues for families.
3. Inconsistent staff service with several reports of "rude", "dismissive", "unfreundly", and "abrupt" reception staff, plus aging facilities with "tired" amenities, uneven sites difficult for larger rigs, and maintenance issues affecting family comfort.
