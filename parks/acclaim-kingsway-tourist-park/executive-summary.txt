PARK: Acclaim Kingsway Tourist Park
SCORE: 32/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-19
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 130

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Reviews consistently report playground closed for renovations, games room closed, tennis courts unusable and "cracked covered in weeds", basketball court "below average". One family noted "expectations there would be a lot for the kids to do" but found facilities unavailable. The only positive is the pool, with bees and wasps reported around it.

Nature (8/20) — Limited natural environment mentioned. Reviews note "giant gum trees" and "love hearing the birds, especially the kookaburras". One review mentions "nature reserve walk trail across the road". However, grounds described as "overgrown", "trees need trimming badly", and "not much gardening done". Location is 15 minutes to beach and 20 minutes to CBD.

Cleanliness (2/15) — Overwhelmingly negative feedback. Multiple families report "dirtiest caravan park", "absolutely disgusting inside and out", "toilets and showers were disgusting" with "hair and sand throughout", "not cleaned once during our 8 day stay". Specific reports include "cockroach crawling on me", "dead or alive cockroach in the shared fridge", "bathroom shower had so much grime", "floors were so filthy my socks were black", "mould, rotting wood and mildew". While some recent reviews mention new management improving cleanliness, the majority of family experiences were extremely poor.

Value (6/15) — Mixed feedback on value. Some praise "great value for money" and "very affordable" with "stay 7 pay 6 deal". However, multiple families found it "overpriced" at "$50 a night for an unpowered site" given the condition, with one stating "can't believe the prices they ask for with it looking the state it's in". The consensus is you get budget pricing but facilities don't match even that level.

Site Size (7/10) — Positive feedback on "large concrete sites", "huge slabbed sites", "nice large site", and "spacious sites". Several reviews mention sites are adequate with "plenty of parking". However, issues noted with "broken concrete pavers everywhere", "caravan pad was broken", and some permanent resident areas being messy.

Sentiment (2/10) — Extremely poor family sentiment. Multiple reviews from families state "would never stay again", "do not waste your time", "worst caravan park we have ever stayed in", "avoid at all costs", "we cut our stay short", "didn't stay our two nights we had booked". One family specifically noted "we are a family of four and did not go out after the sunset" due to safety concerns. Very few families would recommend or return.

Location (8/10) — Strong positive aspect. Reviews consistently praise "great location", "close to everything", "walking distance to shops", "shopping centre 10 minutes walk away", "5 mins from train station and bus stop right outside", "cycling path into perth was superb". Location rated as convenient for Perth CBD access and northern beaches.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness and maintenance failures make this unsafe and unsuitable for families with young children, with multiple reports of uncleaned facilities, pests, broken amenities, and safety hazards like broken concrete and poor lighting.
2. Complete lack of functional children's entertainment facilities, with playground closed, games room closed, tennis courts unusable, and only a poor basketball court available, leaving families with nothing for kids to do.
3. Overwhelmingly negative family sentiment with multiple reports of families cutting stays short, feeling unsafe, and experiencing accommodation that was "inhabitable" with cockroaches, mould, sewage smells, and filth throughout cabins and facilities.
