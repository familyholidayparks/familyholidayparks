PARK: Toowoomba Showgrounds Caravan Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-11
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 202

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews consistently note "nothing much to do there" with minimal on-site entertainment beyond an obstacle course mentioned once; primarily a working showground hosting events rather than a dedicated family holiday park with kids' activities or programs.
Nature (14/20) — Guests appreciate the "peaceful" lakeside setting with "hundreds of corellas," ducks, turtles, and birdwatching opportunities; "quiet treed setting" and "beautiful area" with countryside views, though it's a showground environment rather than pristine natural bushland.
Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "clean amenities" and "well maintained grounds," but frequent mentions of "amenities need an upgrade," "smelly" facilities, "outdated plumbing," and complaints about insufficient cleaning during busy periods like Flower Festival.
Value (13/15) — Strong positive sentiment about pricing at "$35 a night" (formerly $30) described as "great value," "well priced," and "cheap" for power and water; some felt it was "bit high on price" given dated amenities but generally considered reasonable for a stopover.
Site Size (10/10) — Exceptional praise for "huge area," "plenty of space," "heaps of room," "spacious sites," and ability to "pick your own spot" with level sites accommodating large motorhomes; consistently rated as having abundant space throughout the showgrounds.
Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive with many repeat visitors and recommendations for "overnight stay" or short visits; however, mixed feelings about amenity quality and some negative experiences with staff/access issues temper overall enthusiasm for longer stays.
Location (7/10) — Situated on Toowoomba's outskirts, "close to Coles" but "so far out of town" and "not walking distance" to main attractions; convenient for highway travellers but requires driving to access city amenities and Flower Festival venues.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severely lacking family entertainment infrastructure with no kids' club, playground equipment, waterpark, or organized activities—this is a working showground offering camping, not a purpose-built holiday park for families.
2. Amenities consistently described as dated, outdated, smelly, and in desperate need of renovation, with specific complaints about poor shower privacy in men's facilities, inadequate cleaning during peak times, and bore water quality.
3. Location disadvantage for families wanting walkable access to attractions, being significantly removed from Toowoomba's main tourist areas and requiring vehicle transport for all activities and shopping.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"nothing much to do there," "amenities need an upgrade," "plenty of space and well maintained," "dated amenities but clean," "park by dam so peaceful," "great value for money," "so far out of town," "not walking distance," "huge area for camping," "facilities a bit dated but clean and well maintained"
