PARK: Burleigh Beach Tourist Park
SCORE: 73/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 67

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Limited on-site entertainment with a kids TV/rec room for games and activities during holidays; no pool or waterpark; kids reported riding bikes around the park; one reviewer noted "would be a 10/10 if there was a pool for the kids"; entertainment zone mentioned but minimal structured programs; families rely heavily on beach activities just across the road.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting with direct access to Burleigh Beach (100m away), Burleigh Head National Park walking tracks, and coastal paths to Tallebudgera; "wonderful coastal walks," "stunning views of the ocean," "best sunset you'll see on the Coast"; families praised the park/beach combination for children; "when you're on the beach's doorstep, you won't need" a pool.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Consistently described as "very clean amenities," "always clean and tidy," "super well maintained"; some concerns emerged with "damaged floors and walls," "pots and pans filthy," "walls inside our villa could do with a good scrub"; older facilities noted as "quite dated" but functional; "amenities are some of the best you'll find" though "could also be cleaner."

Value (12/15) — Mixed value perception with "very reasonable tariff" and "value for money" mentioned positively; "dose get expensive in peak times"; one reviewer felt "expect more for the price" given maintenance issues; location premium acknowledged; overall solid value for beachfront position but not exceptional.

Site Size (7/10) — "Large sites," "spacious," "plenty of room for kids to roam around," "sites are roomy and easy to get into and out of"; however multiple mentions of "very tight camp sites," "roads were extremely narrow," "struggled to get our 21ft caravan out"; concrete slabs noted but space constraints evident for larger rigs.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive repeat visitor sentiment: "our go-to place," "stay here all the time," "our favourite Gold Coast tourist park," "will be back for sure" appeared frequently; "love, love, love this place"; exceptional staff praise ("managers go above and beyond"); minor deductions for roadwork noise complaints and maintenance concerns.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for "best location on the Gold Coast," "across the road from the beach," "walking distance to multiple cafes, shops, restaurants"; "right in the heart of Burleigh Heads"; easy access to theme parks via public transport; "5-minute walk to the beach"; central to all Gold Coast attractions while maintaining quiet park atmosphere.

WHY NOT 100:
1. No swimming pool or water play facilities — multiple families specifically noted "would be a 10/10 if there was a pool for the kids" and this significantly impacts entertainment value for families with young children who want on-site water activities.
2. Limited on-site kids entertainment infrastructure — only a basic TV/rec room with games; no structured programs, waterpark, jumping pillow, or daily activities that families expect from premium holiday parks.
3. Tight site configurations and narrow internal roads — several reviewers with larger caravans (21ft+) struggled with access and maneuvering; "very tight camp sites" impacts the comfort level for families with bigger rigs and reduces site size appeal.
