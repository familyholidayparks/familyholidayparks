PARK: Reflections Seal Rocks - Holiday Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 101

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Limited on-site entertainment with only a basic playground suitable for older children (nothing under 4 years), occasional live music events, and special activities like Anzac ceremonies and yoga mentioned by some guests, but families noted "not a heap for kids to do inside the park" beyond the playground.

Nature (20/20) — Exceptional natural environment with the park located directly opposite "one of the best beaches in Oz," pristine coastal setting for swimming and surfing, stunning views, whale and dolphin watching opportunities, lighthouse walks, and surrounded by hills and forest creating an "absolutely stunning" and "magical location."

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed cleanliness feedback with recent reviews noting amenities "tired and dirty," facilities "only cleaned once a day" which "isn't enough when the park is completely full," toilet blocks "at most times unclean" during peak periods, though some guests praised "immaculate facilities" and "constantly clean" bathrooms, indicating inconsistency especially during busy times.

Value (9/15) — Significant value concerns with families calling pricing "overpriced" and "ridiculous," powered sites costing $89-$101 per night, standard cabins $200/night described as "unbelievably overpriced for what it was," and premium cabins nearly $1000 for a stay, with many noting costs don't match facility quality and remote location with no nearby services.

Site Size (7/10) — Sites described as "spacious" and "very roomy" for caravans, though some felt "quite close together" making it a "busier park," with specific complaints about back sites flooding when it rains and dirt/gravel sites that become muddy, plus "no shade for tent areas."

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong emotional connection with many families returning annually for 5-20 years saying "our go to place" and "we look forward to our annual trip," though recent sentiment declining with repeat visitors noting deterioration over last 2 visits and saying they'll "go elsewhere next time" due to cleanliness and value concerns.

Location (10/10) — Perfect beachfront location "across the road from the most amazing beach," short walk to multiple beaches including gentle swimming spots and surf breaks, lighthouse walking distance, described as "THE PERFECT location," though very remote with nearest supermarket 40+ minutes away requiring visitors to bring all supplies.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Cleanliness standards are inconsistent and particularly problematic during peak periods, with amenities blocks not cleaned frequently enough for capacity, toilet facilities unclean with "toilet paper on floors, faeces on walls," and accommodation units with dirty cutlery, mouldy caravans, and "dead cockroaches" at check-in.

2. Value proposition is poor with premium pricing ($89-$200+ per night) that doesn't match facility quality, particularly for standard cabins that are "beyond dingy and extremely old" with water damage, broken features, and "rundown" conditions, making guests feel "ripped off."

3. Virtually no on-site kids entertainment beyond one basic playground, no water play facilities or pool, no kids club, and minimal organized activities, making it unsuitable for families seeking structured children's programming or wet weather alternatives to the beach.
