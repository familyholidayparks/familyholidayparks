PARK: Beachside Holiday Caravan Park Yeppoon
SCORE: 56/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-05-28
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 164

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Reviews repeatedly mention no playground, no pool, and no on-site kids entertainment. Families report children running unsupervised through the park and cite nearby Yeppoon lagoon and parks as alternatives. One review bluntly states "nothing here for kids to do it's more retirement people" and "no playground or pool for the kids so they run wild."

Nature (18/20) — The park's standout feature is its absolute beachfront location with direct beach access, island views, and huge sandy beaches ideal for walking, biking, and sandcastles. Guests consistently praise the "right on the beach" setting, ocean sounds, and stunning sunrises. Proximity to natural attractions like Capricorn Caves is also noted positively.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Most reviews commend spotless and well-maintained amenities, with phrases like "amenities are spotless," "super clean," and "immaculate facilities." However, some guests reported dated facilities, a dirty laundry room with broken machines, occasional lack of hot water, and uneven maintenance across different blocks.

Value (7/15) — Reviews are polarised: some find it "great price" and "very reasonable rates," while many families describe it as "very expensive," "overpriced," and not worth $60–$81/night for basic grass sites with no pool or playground. Strict cancellation policies (70% fees, no refunds) frustrate multiple guests.

Site Size (7/10) — Many reviews praise "very big" and "generously sized" sites with plenty of room. However, several guests report tight, cramped sites requiring staff assistance to park, with one family unable to fit despite booking 11 months in advance. Experiences vary significantly by site allocation.

Sentiment (5/10) — Sentiment is sharply divided. Positive reviews highlight repeat visits, beautiful beachfront stays, and helpful staff. However, a significant volume of recent 1-star reviews detail rude, inflexible management (especially "Kath"), strict rules, poor customer service, aggressive staff behaviour, late check-in refusals, and families being turned away or forced to leave. Many families state they will not return.

Location (9/10) — Guests consistently praise the location: beachfront with direct access, 10–15 minute walk to Yeppoon CBD, cafes, restaurants, lagoon pools, and shops. Easy access to Great Keppel Island ferries is also noted. Some road noise is mentioned, but overall location is a major strength.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe customer service and management issues dominate recent reviews, with multiple families reporting rude, aggressive, inflexible staff (especially management figure "Kath"), strict enforcement of rules, and distressing experiences including being screamed at, refused entry for minor delays, denied refunds, and forced to leave—utterly unacceptable for a family holiday park.
2. Complete absence of on-site kids entertainment, playground, or pool facilities means families are entirely reliant on the beach and must travel to nearby Yeppoon lagoon/parks for structured play, significantly limiting appeal for families with young children.
3. Inconsistent value and facility quality: while some guests find it reasonable, many families feel the park is overpriced for basic, dated facilities with no entertainment, particularly given punitive cancellation policies, variable site quality, and reports of poor WiFi, dirty laundry, and occasional hot water issues.
