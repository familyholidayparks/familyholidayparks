PARK: BIG4 Ulladulla Beachside Holiday Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-23
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 47

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Reviews consistently highlight the "new pool and playground" as a "huge asset when travelling with kids," with families noting "the kids thoroughly enjoyed the play park & swimming pool." Multiple 2026 reviews reference these facilities being newly completed. The park offers grassed areas for children and direct beach access for endless entertainment, though no organized activities program was mentioned.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional beachside location with "direct access to Racecourse beach" mentioned in numerous reviews. Families describe the beach as "absolutely beautiful," "perfect," and note special experiences like "watching dolphins at sunrise" and "listening to waves overnight was bliss." The natural setting is the park's standout feature with beach access literally a "1 minute walk" or "less than a minute" from accommodation.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelming praise for cleanliness with reviews using terms like "spotless," "very clean," "clean and tidy," and "facilities clean at all times." The new villas are described as "clean and modern," "brand new," with amenities consistently noted as well-maintained. One minor mention of older facilities in some areas, but overall cleanliness standards are exceptional.

Value (13/15) — Reviews mention "great value" and "best caravan park cabins I have stayed in" for the 3-bedroom villas. The modern renovations and beachside location provide strong value proposition. No specific pricing complaints, though the focus is more on quality than budget positioning.

Site Size (9/10) — Multiple reviews describe "roomy" and "generous" sites with "huge grass sites" and "large enough that we did not feel cramped." Powered sites noted as "very gross" (large). Minor mentions of some sites being "a little bit uneven" but overall space receives positive feedback.

Sentiment (9/10) — Extremely positive family sentiment with numerous reviews stating "will definitely stay again," "we'll be back," and "came back a week later with friends." Families express strong emotional connection, particularly the Christmas story about last-minute booking becoming "one of the best spots we ever." Managers Alex and Sean receive exceptional praise for hospitality.

Location (9/10) — Excellent beachside position with beach access as primary drawcard. Located "close to Ulladulla" township (1km, 5-minute walk mentioned). The trade-off is highway location causing some noise and being "not in walking distance to any pubs and restaurants," though families appreciate the peaceful setting.

WHY NOT 100:
Highway noise is mentioned by multiple reviewers as a drawback, with one noting "traffic noise 24/7" from the busy road location. While the park has recently added a pool and playground, there's no evidence of organized kids' entertainment programs, activities, or kids' club that would maximize the entertainment score. Some reviews from late 2024 mention facilities still being under construction, and one reviewer felt the park had become more cabin-focused with "many permanent occupants," reducing the traditional caravan park atmosphere.
