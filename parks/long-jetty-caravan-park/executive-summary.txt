PARK: Long Jetty Caravan Park
SCORE: 79/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-06-02
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 448

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Reviews mention a playground, occasional cinema nights, and movie nights for kids, but no waterpark, jumping pillow, or extensive structured activities. The focus is on natural entertainment like fishing off the jetty and exploring the beach.

Nature (18/20) — Excellent natural setting with immediate access to the Long Jetty (800m pier), beach, coastal walks, Great Southern Rail Trail, and proximity to Agnes Falls, Tarra Bulga National Park, and Wilson's Promontory. Reviews consistently praise the "beautiful scenery", "peaceful coastal atmosphere", and "stunning bay with views".

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding cleanliness scores throughout reviews: "spotless grounds", "amenities always clean", "immaculate", "very well maintained", "clean facilities", "toilets cleaned regularly", "you could nearly eat off them". Modern, new amenities blocks frequently mentioned.

Value (13/15) — Generally positive value perception with comments like "great value", "not over the top", "stay for 6 pay for 4", "reasonable price", though some noted "a little pricey at $70 per night" and "expensive for middle of winter" for older cabins. Overall good value for the quality offered.

Site Size (10/10) — Consistently exceptional site sizes: "sites are huge", "biggest site we have ever had", "absolutely massive", "VERY large sites", "extra large caravan sites", "generous in size", "spacious and private", "plenty of room for caravan and car and boat".

Sentiment (10/10) — Overwhelmingly positive sentiment with high repeat visitation: "will definitely be back", "already booked again", "we will return", "highly recommend", "love it here", "can't wait to come back". Staff consistently praised as "friendly", "helpful", "welcoming", and "accommodating".

Location (10/10) — Perfect location scores: "2min walk to Long Jetty", "right next to the jetty", "short walk to beach and pub", "walk straight out of park onto beach", "stones throw from the shore", "perfect base" for exploring Gippsland region, Wilson's Prom, and rail trails.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited on-site entertainment facilities - no pool, waterslides, jumping pillow, or extensive organised kids' activities that families with young children often seek at holiday parks.
2. Some sites experienced waterlogging and drainage issues after heavy rain, with multiple reviews mentioning "boggy spots", "water laying in sites", and "low spots" needing fill.
3. Sandflies and midges were a significant complaint in multiple reviews ("blood sucking midges left red bite marks", "sand flies are the issue", "midges were not good"), though some guests reported no problems.
