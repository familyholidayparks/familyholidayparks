PARK: Broken Head Holiday Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 85

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — No on-site kids entertainment, playground, or pool mentioned; families rely entirely on beach and nature walks for entertainment; one review mentioned "good facilities for kids" at amenities but no structured activities; Aunties Cafe food van provides coffee and food but not entertainment.

Nature (20/20) — Perfect score with reviews consistently praising "1 minute walk to beautiful beach", "surrounded by peaceful rainforest", "on the edge of national park", "headland walks", "stunning Broken Head beach", "wallabies and bird life", "waking up to sound of ocean", and "like being in the middle of the jungle".

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive with "modern and spotless amenities", "facilities are so clean", "toilets and showers always clean", "one of the cleanest best kept Parks"; lost 1 point for isolated incidents of cabin cleanliness issues (sand in room, dirty microwave, dated cabin bathrooms).

Value (11/15) — Mixed reviews with $57/night powered sites praised as "very fair" and "reasonable price", but complaints about $550 for 4 nights tent camping being "expensive", "most expensive holiday park in Australia" ($75/night unpowered), plus extra $22 parking fees; generally considered good value for location despite premium pricing.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant weakness with multiple reviews stating "sites are very tiny", "fairly small, gravely and hilly", "lots are very close together", "caravan sites are tight", "bearly fitted two tents"; positive mentions of "generous space" for tent plots and "spacious grassy camp sites" were outnumbered by complaints.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation ("3rd year in a row", "29 years", "keep coming back every single year", "annual tradition") but significantly impacted by serious reception staff complaints ("always unbelievably rude", "appalling attitude", "very rude never go there EVER") and multiple 2024 management incidents involving screaming at guests.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score with universal praise: "unbeatable location", "right on the beach", "10min drive to Byron Bay", "close to Byron and Lennox", "away from hustle and bustle but close enough", "excellent location 200m to gorgeous beach", ideal proximity to attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. No on-site children's entertainment facilities (no playground, no pool, no kids club) limiting appeal for families wanting structured activities beyond beach access.
2. Persistent and serious customer service issues at reception with multiple recent reviews describing staff as "unbelievably rude", "dismissive", "condescending", and several 2024 incidents of management screaming at guests with children present.
3. Site sizes are consistently described as too small, particularly for tents and larger caravans, with gravel surfaces causing tent damage and uneven ground requiring wheel chocks.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"1 minute walk from the beach", "amenities are modern and spotless", "sites are very tiny", "reception staff are always unbelievably rude", "we keep coming back every single year", "one of the cleanest best kept Parks", "surrounded by peaceful rainforest", "most expensive holiday park in Australia"
