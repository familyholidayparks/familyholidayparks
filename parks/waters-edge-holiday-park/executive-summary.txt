PARK: Waters Edge Holiday Park
SCORE: 52/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-08
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 164

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently mention a jumping pillow that was frequently closed ("closed since December 2023", "not opened till 9am"), a games room with mostly coin-operated machines, and a small pool. Playground equipment was often broken or taped off as unsafe. Families noted "half of the equipments were not working" and "everything was switched off". Holiday events like Easter egg hunts were appreciated by some families.

Nature (12/20) — Park is located directly across from Lakes Entrance waterfront with "lovely walks along the water wall with wood ducks, black swans, plovers". Easy access to "90 Mile beach walk" and "footbridge across to the surf beach". Natural setting appreciated but park itself described as functional rather than picturesque, lacking significant on-site natural features.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Highly polarized reviews. Recent positive reviews praise "spotless amenities", "beautifully clean", "very clean campground". However, numerous negative reviews cite "dirty swimming pool", "shower blocks were disgusting", "pool had dead cockroaches", "smelt of cat piss", and maintenance issues like "diarrhea splotches" left unattended. Cleanliness appears inconsistent and deteriorated during peak periods.

Value (9/15) — Reviews describe as "well priced", "good value for money", "really good value" and "budget friendly". However, many felt it was "not worth the $53 per night" given the cramped sites and dated facilities. Guests noted it's cheaper than other Lakes Entrance parks but "far below big4 standards". Value perception depends heavily on accommodation type (cabins rated better than caravan sites).

Site Size (3/10) — Overwhelmingly negative feedback: "Smallest sites I have experienced in 20 years", "very cramped small sites", "sites are too small", "pitch was so small I couldn't get my awning out", "Sites are not big enough for a caravan and your car". Multiple reviews describe having to ask neighbors to move vehicles just to fit in. Roads described as "very narrow with tight corners" making maneuvering difficult.

Sentiment (5/10) — Mixed sentiment with passionate supporters and strong detractors. Positive reviews emphasize location and helpful staff (John and Kelly frequently praised). Many negative reviews express strong disappointment, with phrases like "worst Big4 experience", "will never stay here again", "wouldn't recommend". Recent management changes (new owners ~2024) appear to have improved some aspects. Repeat visitors mostly stayed in cabins rather than caravan sites.

Location (7/10) — Consistently praised as the park's strongest asset: "Excellent position", "fantastic location", "walking distance to EVERYTHING", "right across from the water", "close to shops, bars, beach". Guests appreciated easy access to Lakes Entrance main street, supermarkets, restaurants, and beach/waterfront. Location directly on main road provides convenience but some noise concerns.

WHY NOT 100:
Site sizes are severely inadequate for modern caravans, with the majority of reviews from caravan users describing extreme difficulty accessing sites, insufficient space for vehicles and awnings, and constant conflicts with neighboring sites over parking—this is a fundamental infrastructure problem affecting most guests. Entertainment facilities for children are poorly maintained with the jumping pillow frequently closed for extended periods, playground equipment broken and taped off as unsafe, pool maintenance issues including green water and dead insects, and games room mostly coin-operated with limited free activities. Cleanliness and maintenance standards are inconsistent and appear to deteriorate significantly during peak periods, with multiple reports of disgusting bathroom conditions, broken fixtures, poor maintenance response times, and facilities that fall well below expected Big4 standards despite the affiliation.
