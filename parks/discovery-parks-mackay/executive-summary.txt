PARK: Discovery Parks - Mackay
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 126

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Families consistently praised the water slide pool, jumping pillow, mini golf, pedal carts, splash park, and kids club with daily activities (9am-12pm). Several reviews mentioned children "loved" the facilities and wanted to return. However, the slide was frequently out of service ("shut the whole time we stayed", "wasn't running half the week") and pool closures occurred due to bacteria issues, reducing reliability.

Nature (15/20) — The park backs onto wetlands with "abundant bird life", ducks, pelicans, and barramundi fishing in the lake which families enjoyed. Reviews mentioned "beautiful" sunset views and wildlife watching. However, the major drawback was "mosquito infested lake" with multiple reviews warning about severe mosquito problems ("10+ bites in 5 minutes", "absolutely carried away by mosquitoes day and night"), and limited shade from palm trees.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Cleanliness feedback was highly inconsistent. Positive reviews noted "spotless", "super clean", and "very clean amenities". However, numerous negative reviews cited "cabin was dirty, dust and dog hair", "mouldy windows", "dust clinging to walls", "70% of tiles cracked badly", "bathroom smelt like sewerage", dirty camp kitchen with "old food covered in mould" in fridges, and non-functional washing machines ("4 washers 2 out of order").

Value (12/15) — Many families found it "Great Value for money" with "really good" pricing and "reasonably priced". The $10 day pass for facilities was called "cheap and amazing". However, some felt $600 for a week on a powered site was expensive given maintenance issues, and the strict cancellation policy with full forfeiture frustrated guests.

Site Size (8/10) — Caravan sites received positive feedback as "large", "spacious", "big flat sites", and "easy access" including drive-through options. Ensuite sites by the lagoon were particularly praised. Minor complaints about limited parking for multiple vehicles at cabins and tight maneuvering for larger rigs in some spots.

Sentiment (7/10) — Sentiment was polarized. Many families loved their stay ("We will definitely book here again", "Can't recommend it enough") and staff like Jayde received specific praise. However, significant negative reviews described "worst one we have ever stayed at", "truly awful", staff being "unbelievably RUDE", and serious service failures (flood incident with no support, being called "hillbilly's"). Mixed consistency across visits reduced overall sentiment.

Location (3/10) — The park's location was its weakest aspect. Located on a main road causing noise ("trucks all night with the brakes going off", "traffic noise", "too close to the highway"), it's "bit out of town" (10 minutes drive to shops), and "you can't walk to anywhere". While close to the marina and harbour, it lacks walkable amenities and proximity to beaches, reducing convenience for families without vehicles.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Inconsistent cleanliness standards with multiple reports of dirty cabins (dog hair, dust, mould, broken items) and poorly maintained shared facilities (mouldy fridges, broken washing machines, sewerage smells) significantly impacted the family experience.
2. Severe mosquito infestation near the wetlands made outdoor activities uncomfortable for many families, with multiple warnings in reviews, and frequent facility closures (pool bacteria issues, non-operational slide for extended periods) reduced the reliability of advertised entertainment.
3. Poor location on a noisy highway far from walkable amenities, combined with polarized customer service experiences ranging from "amazing" to "appalling", "RUDE", and "unprofessional" (particularly around incident management and policy enforcement), created an inconsistent guest experience.
