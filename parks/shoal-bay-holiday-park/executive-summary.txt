PARK: Shoal Bay Holiday Park
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-06-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 129

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Multiple reviews note the arcade was removed and replaced with a basic playground, with recent visitors commenting "not much for the kids to do" and "work being done on basketball courts." Limited on-site kids activities beyond the playground, though families mention nearby beach activities and lolly shop within walking distance.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding beach location with reviews consistently praising "literally a stones throw from the beach," "calm stunning Shoal Bay," and "8-minute stroll across the road" to pristine beaches. Families love the "walking/bike tracks," cycle paths to Fingal Bay and Nelson Bay, and "relaxed coastal atmosphere" with natural beauty, though some note limited dog-friendly beach areas.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Exceptional standards with "cleanest bathroom facilities I've ever been to in all of Australia," "cleaned 2 to 3 times a day," "comparable to a 5 star hotel," and "spotlessly clean and modern." Facilities consistently described as immaculate with rain head showers and modern design.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "great value for money" and "very reasonably priced," but recent reviews cite "$70 for an unpowered site. Highway robbery" and complaints about pricing compared to other states. Some families question value given tight sites and basic on-site entertainment.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant concern with "sites are on the small side," "tiny and very on top of each other," "bit snug," "really only for smaller caravan," and "sites a little tight to get into." Multiple warnings about cramped conditions during peak times, though some mention "spacious" ensuite sites.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very positive with "will be 100% booking it again," "we'll definitely be back," "attended for nearly three decades," and "our new family get together place." Strong repeat visitation and recommendations, though some negative experiences with noise, weekend crowds, and management response.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score with "absolutely perfect location," "didn't need the car once parked," "walk to shops beach and restaurants," "2 minute walk to beach shops restaurants," and proximity to Shoal Bay Country Club, IGA, cafes. Families love the convenience and beach access.

WHY NOT 100:
Limited on-site kids entertainment with the arcade removed and only a basic playground, leaving families reliant on beach activities rather than park facilities during downtime or poor weather. Sites are consistently described as small and cramped, particularly during peak periods, making it challenging for larger rigs and families wanting space. Weekend noise issues, security concerns (bikes stolen, non-guests accessing facilities), and inconsistent management response to disturbances detract from the family experience.
