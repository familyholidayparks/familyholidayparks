PARK: Pier Caravan Park Hervey Bay
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-16
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 146

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews mention a pool, playground for kids, and games room with ping pong and arcade games, but entertainment is modest compared to larger holiday parks. One family noted "My daughter (almost 3) and I had such an amazing time" and multiple reviews reference the games room and pool area, but there's no waterpark, jumping pillow, or extensive kids' club activities. Entertainment is adequate but not the park's main drawcard.

Nature (17/20) — Strong nature score with consistent praise for the location near Urangan Pier ("10 minutes walk"), beach access ("right next to the beach"), and proximity to Fraser Island/K'gari tours. Multiple reviews highlight "fantastic sunset views", "off leash beach nearby", and "close to the water" with esplanade walking paths. The coastal environment and access to natural attractions like whale watching are major strengths.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding cleanliness across all reviews with phrases like "spotlessly clean", "immaculate", "super clean", and "everything is kept spick and span tidy" appearing consistently. Amenities described as "immaculate regardless of what time I went" and "cleaned every day". One reviewer said "this caravan park has to be the cleanest and most organised I have ever visited."

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback on value. Multiple reviews note "getting a little bit expensive", "$62 a night pretty exy", and "a bit on the expensive side", though most add qualifiers like "but it is worth it" and "can't complain about the quality". Free fifth night deals mentioned. Premium pricing reflects high standards but doesn't represent budget value for families.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally good site sizes with "large sites", "spacious sites", and "plenty of room" mentioned frequently. However, several reviews note challenges: "tight for average backers", "if you have a van 21ft plus, it's very very tight", and "sites are close to each other". Mix of grass and concrete slabs. Good but not exceptional space.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very positive sentiment with high repeat visitation: "Our family have agreed that it is traditional to go to the Pier once a year", "we will be back for sure", and "staying always here when in Hervey Bay". However, three 1-star reviews (April 2026) citing "rude and aggressive behaviour from staff" and security issues (bike theft) prevent a perfect score. Most recent reviews remain positive.

Location (10/10) — Perfect location score. Consistently praised as "great location", "perfect location", "walking distance to everything", "5 minute walk to the pier", close to shops, restaurants, markets, and boat harbour. Convenient for Fraser Island tours and central to Hervey Bay attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited onsite entertainment facilities—the park has basic amenities (pool, small playground, games room) but lacks the waterparks, kids' clubs, jumping pillows, and structured activities that families with young children expect from top-tier holiday parks.
2. Premium pricing without commensurate value perception—multiple reviews note the park is "expensive" or "exy" relative to facilities offered, with costs reflecting cleanliness standards and location rather than entertainment value for families.
3. Concerning security and service incidents—several reviews document bike thefts ("lost my $2500 e-bike"), aggressive staff behaviour requiring police involvement (April 2026), and tight site access for larger vans, indicating operational inconsistencies that undermine the otherwise positive experience.
