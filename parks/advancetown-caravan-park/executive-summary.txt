PARK: Advancetown Caravan Park
SCORE: 52/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-05-09
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 29

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Reviews explicitly state this is "an adult park" with one reviewer noting the location is "without being subject to the theme parks, the traffic and children's entertainment." No playground, kids club, or children's activities mentioned across any reviews. One French reviewer mentioned a dirty BBQ in the camp kitchen as the only communal facility detail.

Nature (16/20) — Multiple reviews praise the "quiet bush setting," "amongst the treetops," and "mountain setting" with views overlooking the Gold Coast. Proximity to Hinze Dam (5 minute drive) and Gold Coast Hinterland setting provide natural surroundings. Bush turkeys spotted on-site. However, no beach access or significant nature-based activities mentioned.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Exceptional and consistent praise: "the most spotless amenities," "impeccably clean," "not a cobweb or spec of dust," "cleanest we've come across in our travels," "meticulously kept." Management personally maintains facilities to very high standards throughout the 3-year review period.

Value (10/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing. Recent reviews cite $65 weekdays/$85 weekends as "expensive" and "a lot more than we would normally pay." Earlier reviews from 2023 noted $58-62 as "reasonably priced." Repeat visitors acknowledge quality justifies cost but pricing has increased significantly, reducing family value proposition.

Site Size (6/10) — Limited evidence: one detailed review notes "some big rigs would have difficulty" though a 6-metre van fit easily. Multiple reviews emphasize "very few sites available for travellers" and "only a few sites" as the park is "mainly permanents." No specific mentions of spacious family sites.

Sentiment (8/10) — Very positive sentiment from those who stayed, with multiple repeat visitors (one on their 6th visit over 6 years). However, park orientation toward adults and permanent residents limits family appeal. One 1-star review criticized it as just "a permanent caravan park." French reviewer warned families seeking quiet to avoid due to pub noise.

Location (9/10) — Excellent strategic location: 30 minutes to Surfers Paradise, 6km to Nerang shopping, close to Gold Coast theme parks (30-60 minutes), next to Beechmont Hotel. "No traffic noise," "peaceful" setting while maintaining easy Gold Coast access. "Hidden gem" for avoiding touristy areas while staying connected.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Zero on-site children's entertainment or activities - reviews explicitly identify this as "an adult park" with no playground, kids club, or family-focused facilities, making it unsuitable for families seeking holiday park entertainment.
2. Very limited transient sites available - primarily a permanent resident park with "only a few sites available for travellers," restricting family accessibility and booking flexibility.
3. Noise issues from adjacent pub - French reviewer specifically warned that live music from Beechmont Hotel resonates through the park until 11pm, problematic for families with young children needing early bedtimes.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"An adult park," "spotless amenities," "impeccably clean," "mainly permanents," "very few sites available for travellers," "quiet bush setting," "without being subject to children's entertainment," "close to all Gold Coast Theme Parks"
