PARK: Enclave at Healesville Holiday Park
SCORE: 48/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-25
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 39

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — No on-site kids entertainment, playground, or activities mentioned. One discriminatory incident regarding a family birthday barbecue. Reviews confirm this is primarily a retirement village with minimal family facilities. No games room, jumping pillow, or organised activities for children evident in any reviews.

Nature (16/20) — Strong natural setting with "outstanding flora and fauna" including native birds, kangaroos, and possums. Beautiful community gardens and reflective garden praised. Located in the scenic Healesville region with easy access to wineries and natural attractions. "Beautiful area" mentioned multiple times, though primarily a village setting rather than bushland.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Highly inconsistent. Recent reviews report serious issues: "ants in bunk bed room", "mouse in my cabin", "pool was dirty I had to clean it myself", "mould in shower", "cleanliness of the bathrooms is non existent", "facilities not cleaned every day", "ensuite had not been cleaned after previous campers". Older reviews (2023-2024) praised cleanliness, indicating decline under new ownership from 2026.

Value (7/15) — Multiple complaints about pricing: "expensive for the facilities provided", "$54 per night for a powered site, perhaps $40 per night would suit", "overpriced for what it was". One review noted "reasonable price" but most recent family reviewers felt facilities didn't justify cost, especially given cleanliness and maintenance issues.

Site Size (6/10) — Very limited sites available ("only 8 sites", "4 ensuite sites, 10 powered"). Sites described as "spacious enough" but "weirdly set up for vans" with "unusable space at the back" and "front very close to the road". Small showers noted as problematic for families: "awkward if you're a parent trying to supervise a young child in the shower".

Sentiment (4/10) — Severely damaged by recent negative family experiences and management issues. Recent reviews show decline: "gone down hill with new owners", discrimination incident, poor staff attitude, mice and ants. Positive sentiment mainly from couples and grey nomads, not families. Several reports of "owners not on site or available much" and "staff rarely accessible".

Location (9/10) — Excellent location "very convenient and close to Healesville town centre", "very close to the town and wineries", "central to many attractions and points of interest". Quiet neighbourhood setting. Only limitation is "no public transport" requiring a car. Easy access to Healesville Sanctuary and Yarra Valley attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Critical cleanliness failures for families including mice, ants in children's beds, mould, dirty pool, uncleaned bathrooms, and hair/crumbs in bedding - completely unacceptable for young children.
2. Zero on-site entertainment or play facilities for kids (no playground, no kids club, no activities), making this fundamentally unsuitable as a family holiday park despite the name.
3. Significant management and maintenance decline under new ownership with owners rarely available, discrimination reported, poor staff attitudes, facilities "old and neglected", and operational issues like "no vacancy signs when sites empty".
