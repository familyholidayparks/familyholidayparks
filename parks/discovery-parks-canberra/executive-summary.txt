PARK: Discovery Parks - Canberra
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-23
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 153

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (3/20) — Reviews consistently note the complete absence of family entertainment facilities with repeated mentions of "no playground, no pool," "nothing for kids," and "no games rooms." Multiple families specifically criticized the lack of on-site activities and entertainment options, with several Easter event activities being the only exception mentioned.

Nature (8/20) — The park is described as "devoid of any personality," "very open," and lacking trees throughout reviews. Multiple guests note "no shade trees," "minimal trees," and describe it as a "concrete jungle" with an unattractive outlook over a recycling yard. The exposed nature makes it very windy, though there is an open field next door for dogs.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive feedback on cleanliness with reviews praising "immaculate" facilities, "spotless" amenities, "very clean" bathrooms, and "modern and clean" everything. The park is repeatedly called "the cleanest caravan park we have ever been to" with exceptional maintenance standards throughout.

Value (10/15) — Mixed feedback on value with several reviews noting it's "not the cheapest," "on the pricey side," and "charging top dollar" at $58 for powered sites. While most accept the pricing given the quality facilities, multiple reviewers suggest the Epic showground as a cheaper alternative and some feel value has declined.

Site Size (10/10) — Exceptional praise for site size with consistent mentions of "huge slab sites," "massive" spaces, "generous sites," and accommodation for "the biggest rigs." The drive-through concrete sites are repeatedly highlighted as outstanding, well-spaced, and easy to navigate even for large motorhomes.

Sentiment (7/10) — Generally positive sentiment from caravanners praising facilities and cleanliness, but significantly impacted by numerous negative school camp reviews complaining about food quality, squeaky beds, and poor experiences. Many adult travellers say they'll return, but families note they wouldn't recommend it.

Location (10/10) — Strong positive feedback on location with reviews noting it's "close to the city," "10 minutes to city," "within walking distance to light rail," and provides "easy access to all the attractions." Convenient to shops, showground, and all Canberra tourist destinations while remaining quiet.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment facilities (no pool, playground, or activities) makes this unsuitable for families wanting on-site entertainment, with multiple reviews specifically criticizing "nothing for kids."
2. The stark, treeless environment with no shade or landscaping creates an unattractive "concrete jungle" atmosphere that lacks character and exposes guests to harsh winds and weather.
3. Significant negative feedback from school camp groups about food quality issues (reports of food poisoning, raw food, mouldy items) and accommodation problems (squeaky beds, broken fixtures, no locks on doors) damages overall reputation.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"Huge slab sites catering for the biggest rigs," "camp kitchen is beyond 5 star," "no playground, no pool," "lacks any personality and feels dull," "cleanest caravan park we have ever been to," "like being in a carpark for caravans," "no trees or shade," "staff were friendly and helpful"
