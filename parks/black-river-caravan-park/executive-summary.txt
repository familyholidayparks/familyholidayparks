PARK: Black River Caravan Park
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-01-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 34

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — This is an over 50s only park with no children permitted. Reviews explicitly state "great no kids" and "Caravan Park for over 50s". Activities mentioned are adult-focused happy hours and social gatherings, making it completely unsuitable for families with young children.

Nature (14/20) — Reviews mention "lots of birds and wallabies around", "spectacular sunsets", and proximity to Black River. The natural setting is appreciated by guests, though the park is described as "30 mins away from Townsville" and "quite a way out of town around 30kms", limiting easy access to beaches and major nature attractions.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Consistently praised with phrases like "beautifully clean amenities", "spotless bathrooms", "Never seen a cleaner, more inviting camp kitchen", and "amenities always clean". One negative review cited "shower pressure is terrible" and another mentioned "amenities were poor" and "needs a good spruce up", preventing a perfect score.

Value (14/15) — Excellent value noted with "$38 powered and $35 unpowered", "very reasonably priced", "great value sites", and "$30 a night". Pricing is consistent year-round and considered cheap. Minor deductions for one complaint about customer service regarding email responses.

Site Size (9/10) — Reviews consistently praise "big spacious sites", "loads of room", "plenty of room to spread out", and "large sites". One concern raised about sites being "packed in tight in busy season" where "some of us couldn't put out our awnings", and "sites are a bit tight width wise".

Sentiment (7/10) — Overall positive sentiment with many 5-star reviews and guests saying "will definitely be back". However, significantly impacted by two very negative 1-star reviews citing rudeness, poor customer service, and management issues. The over 50s restriction also limits broader appeal.

Location (6/10) — Located "30 mins away from Townsville", described as "out of the way" and "quite a way out of town around 30kms". Some guests specifically chose it to "avoid staying in Townsville" and appreciated being "outside of TSV crime central". The distance from town and attractions reduces convenience for day trips.
