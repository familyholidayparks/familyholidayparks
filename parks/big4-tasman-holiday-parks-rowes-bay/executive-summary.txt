PARK: BIG4 Tasman Holiday Parks - Rowes Bay
SCORE: 52/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 259

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews rarely mention organized kids entertainment or activities; basic amenities include a pool, tennis court, playground, and giant chessboard, but minimal programming. Several families noted the park has "plenty to do" with beach access, but on-site entertainment is limited to self-directed play facilities.

Nature (15/20) — Strong beachfront location directly across from Rowes Bay with park, walking paths to The Strand, and views of Magnetic Island. Guests appreciate proximity to beach, coastal walking trails, and natural setting. However, pest issues (sandflies, mosquitoes, midges) significantly detract from outdoor enjoyment, with multiple reports of severe bites requiring medical attention.

Cleanliness (6/15) — This is the park's most critical weakness. Extensive evidence of poor cleanliness: "filthiest facilities I've ever been in," showers with mould and hair, dirty cabins with ant infestations, bed bugs, cockroaches, blocked drains, overflowing bins, and unhygienic amenities. While some reviews mention clean facilities, the overwhelming majority from recent years report significant hygiene concerns.

Value (5/15) — Strong consensus that the park is overpriced for quality delivered. Guests report paying $85-$100+ per night for powered sites with poor maintenance, dynamic pricing that increases rates unexpectedly, and premium charges for substandard facilities. Multiple reviews state "way too expensive," "rip off seriously," and "not worth the money at all."

Site Size (6/10) — Mixed feedback with some praising "huge sites" and "large sites," while others report cramped conditions, sites too small for caravans booked, uneven terrain, potholes, and fake grass "evergreen" sites with no shade. Several families had to request site changes due to inadequate space.

Sentiment (5/10) — Deeply polarized reviews. Some guests had excellent experiences praising staff and location, but a substantial proportion report terrible stays with phrases like "worst accommodation experience," "never stay here again," "disappointing stay," and "would not recommend." Recent reviews (2024-2026) skew significantly more negative than older ones, suggesting declining standards.

Location (7/10) — The park's strongest feature. Excellent beachfront position across from Rowes Bay, walking distance to The Strand, close to Townsville CBD, near Magnetic Island ferry terminal, and good access to local attractions. However, road noise, proximity to airport, and security concerns (theft, unauthorized access) reduce the score.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe and persistent cleanliness and maintenance issues across accommodations and amenities, with extensive evidence of unsanitary conditions, pest infestations (ants, bed bugs, cockroaches), mould, blocked drains, broken equipment, and inadequate housekeeping that fundamentally compromise family safety and comfort.

2. Extremely poor value proposition with premium pricing ($85-$100+ per night) that does not reflect facility quality, coupled with controversial dynamic pricing, hidden fees, refused refunds for legitimate complaints, and significantly lower standards than comparable parks at half the cost.

3. Inconsistent and often dismissive customer service, with multiple reports of rude staff, ignored complaints, refused assistance, mocking guests, aggressive management behavior, and zero accountability for serious issues including pest infestations and security breaches, creating a hostile environment particularly problematic for families with young children.
