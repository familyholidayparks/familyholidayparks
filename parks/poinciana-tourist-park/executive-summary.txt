PARK: Poinciana Tourist Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-19
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 116

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews indicate minimal on-site entertainment for kids with only a swimming pool mentioned; families noted the glamping area has a separate pool, but no playground, kids club, waterpark or organized activities were mentioned, with families relying on nearby Yeppoon lagoon and beach activities for children's entertainment.

Nature (14/20) — Strong access to natural environment with Cooee Bay beach a 5-minute walk away, dog-friendly beach access, Wreck Point lookout nearby, and proximity to Great Keppel Island ferry (5 mins drive), though multiple reviews noted the park itself lacks "nice gardens or shady trees" with "cracker dust" grounds rather than natural landscaping.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Exceptional cleanliness scores with recent reviews consistently describing "immaculate" amenities, "spotless" facilities, and bathrooms "like they are from a hotel"; the new amenities block receives outstanding praise as "next level" and "best bathrooms I've seen in a caravan park", though older caravan area infrastructure shows signs of age.

Value (11/15) — Mixed value perception with rates ranging $42-$58 per night for powered sites; some families found it "well priced" and "affordable" while others noted it was "very expensive" for an unpowered site, and concerns about restricted washing machine use requiring $4 laundry fees reduced perceived value for longer-staying families.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant criticism of caravan site sizing with repeated mentions of "sites are way too small", "very tight sites", "narrow" spaces where "if it was full we would of been on top of the next caravan", and difficulty maneuvering with families needing neighbors to move cars to park or leave; cabin and glamping areas received better space ratings.

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive sentiment with many families describing "wonderful time", "loved our stay", and multiple return visits mentioned, though enthusiasm notably higher for cabin/glamping guests than caravan users, with some families stating they "won't be back" due to site issues, and overall recommendation rate strong but not overwhelming.

Location (7/10) — Good location score with walking distance to beach, shopping center across the road with bakery and shops, 2-minute drive to Yeppoon esplanade/waterpark, 5-minute drive to marina for Great Keppel Island, and close to cafes and tavern; however, glamping tents suffer from road noise, and the park lacks scenic views or peaceful bushland setting.

WHY NOT 100:
[1] Complete absence of on-site kids entertainment facilities—no playground, kids club, jumping pillow, or organized activities meant families with young children had to rely entirely on external attractions for entertainment, limiting appeal for family holidaymakers. [2] Caravan site infrastructure significantly underperforms with consistently criticized narrow, unlevel sites that are difficult to access, lack grey water drainage, have dusty unsealed roads, and old power/water facilities, creating frustration for families touring with their own vans. [3] Limited natural amenity within the park itself with multiple reviews noting lack of gardens, shade trees, or attractive landscaping, presenting views of permanent resident structures and "cracker dust" rather than the tropical, green environment families expect at a coastal holiday park.
