PARK: Amberlee Family Holidays
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2025
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 17

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (18/20) — Outstanding variety with four playgrounds, huge sandpit, mini golf, rope adventure playground, splash park, pool with water features, tennis and basketball courts. Multiple reviews highlight entertainment facilities with "wow factor" and kids so excited they "made us stop before we even arrived at our site."

Nature (14/20) — Located in Rosebud's green belt with "lots of green spaces to relax" and described as a "quiet location." Short drive to beaches and Mornington Peninsula attractions, though primarily a managed park environment rather than natural bushland setting.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently praised with "grounds were immaculate," "super clean" facilities appearing in multiple reviews. Recent renovations noted with "newly renovated" cabins and "excellently maintained" ensuite facilities. One point deducted for mention of construction materials visible on one site.

Value (11/15) — Good perceived value with families returning and recommending, though ensuite sites mentioned at "high additional cost" and one reviewer noted cramped RV sites. No specific pricing complaints but limited explicit value mentions in recent reviews.

Site Size (6/10) — Powered ensuite sites described as "very spacious" by some, but significant concerns from RV users about sites "squished between bungalows" and "better parking spaces at Woolworths." Park transitioning from 50+ sites to mostly permanent structures with only 4 touring sites remaining.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with multiple "definitely coming back" statements and 17-year repeat visitor. Two recent 1-star reviews (one customer service, one site size complaint) prevent perfect score but majority express love for the park.

Location (6/10) — Good Mornington Peninsula location with "short drive to the beach and centre of Rosebud" and "close to all amenities." Described as quiet but not beachfront or immediately adjacent to major attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site size issues for RV/caravan users with only 4 touring sites remaining among 50+ sites, with complaints about cramped spaces "squished between bungalows" affecting traditional campers.
2. Customer service inconsistency with one detailed complaint about unprofessional phone handling and another negative booking experience impacting first impressions.
3. Limited WiFi/internet connectivity explicitly mentioned as problematic, and value perception affected by high additional costs for ensuite sites without clear pricing transparency.
