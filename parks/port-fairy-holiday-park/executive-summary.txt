PARK: Port Fairy Holiday Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 53

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Reviews consistently praised the heated indoor pool with mini waterslide that kept kids entertained for days, plus jumping pillow, giant playground, tennis and basketball courts, games room, and occasional pancake breakfasts. Families noted 'always something happening for the kids' and children spending entire days at facilities. One reviewer mentioned free kids movie nights in the lighthouse room.

Nature (16/20) — The park is beautifully positioned between Moyne River and Eastern beach with 'beautiful gardens' creating a peaceful atmosphere. Multiple reviews highlighted the scenic location with views of neighboring cows from safari tents, easy beach access, and positioning on the Great Ocean Road. The gardens were described as exceptionally well-maintained, creating private, spacious sites with natural shelter from winds.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding cleanliness scores with 'spotless amenities' mentioned repeatedly across reviews spanning all three years. Multiple reviewers called it 'the cleanest and most well cared for park' with 'immaculate' facilities and 'very clean neat and tidy' grounds consistently maintained to Big4 standards.

Value (13/15) — Most families found good value with well-equipped cabins and safari tents, though pricing wasn't extensively detailed. One reviewer noted '1br standard cabin was well priced for a summer peak budget accommodation option.' The range of included facilities (pool, playground, camp kitchen) added value, though one review suggested missing features like pizza oven or weekend activities.

Site Size (9/10) — Sites described as 'generous,' 'spacious,' 'big flat electrical sites' with 'extra space' and privacy. Multiple reviews noted 'no one is on top of each other' and accommodation for big rigs with drive-through capability. One minor complaint about being 'pushed together' when park was mostly empty prevented a perfect score.

Sentiment (10/10) — Exceptional sentiment with multiple families returning for second, third, and fourth stays. Reviewers used phrases like 'will definitely be back,' 'one of the best camps we've stayed at,' and 'must stay if in Port Fairy.' The overwhelmingly positive 5-star ratings and high recommendation rate demonstrate strong family satisfaction.

Location (9/10) — Great location on outskirts of Port Fairy, 1.5-2km walk to town (20 minutes) with convenient access to beaches, shops, and Great Ocean Road attractions. Reviewers appreciated the quiet setting despite proximity to amenities. Minor road noise mentioned occasionally, and walking distance to town may be far for some families with young children.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited organized entertainment programming - one review specifically noted the park was 'missing...something at weekends such as movie night or pancakes in morning,' suggesting entertainment relies more on facilities than structured activities.
2. Distance from town centre requires walking 1.5-2km (20 minutes) which some families with young children found inconvenient, preferring driving or biking to shops and beach.
3. Minor maintenance issues reported including inconsistent hot water in cabins, aircon dripping water, and difficulty reaching staff by phone, though these were isolated incidents.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"spotless amenities", "always something happening for the kids to do", "kids spent two days in and out the pool", "cleanest and most well cared for park", "beautiful gardens it's like staying in someone's well maintained yard", "one of the best camps we've stayed at", "plenty of facilities for the kids", "private and quiet sites"
