PARK: Lakes Main Holiday Park
SCORE: 48/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 40

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — No on-site kids entertainment, playground, or activities mentioned in any reviews. One family with "two small children" noted the cabin was suitable for quiet relaxation but no mention of any child-focused amenities or entertainment options. Multiple reviewers specifically warned families with children to avoid this park due to lack of facilities and safety concerns.

Nature (14/20) — Located in Lakes Entrance near water and foreshore, with reviews noting "close to the beach," "close to the water," and "5 minutes from the foreshore path into town." Guests can borrow crabbing equipment. However, one serious safety incident mentioned a guest falling in a hole in the grass requiring hospitalization. Natural setting is accessible but not the park's primary feature.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Highly polarized reviews with recent feedback showing major concerns. Multiple reviews praise cleanliness ("very clean," "extremely clean," "cleaning is really well done"), but several serious complaints in 2024-2026 cite "dirty and smelly place," "cabin beds had bedbugs," "sheets looked old and unwashed," and facilities "falling to bits." Maintenance issues include faulty lights, broken toilets, and wrapped smoke detectors.

Value (9/15) — Described repeatedly as "very good value for money" and "budget accommodation," though recent critical reviews call it "pricey for what you get" and "expensive for what we received." Cabins are basic with some lacking essential items (chopping board, toilet paper, adequate paper towels). One guest felt "scammed $550."

Site Size (3/10) — Cabins consistently described as "small," "tiny," "not huge," with beds taking up "almost the entire width of the room." One review noted inability to stand next to bed to take photo. Adequate for overnight stays but cramped for families. One review mentioned camping ground had "expanded into the area directly outside our room."

Sentiment (5/10) — Mixed sentiment with significant family safety concerns. While some repeat guests ("stayed here 4 times") express satisfaction, multiple families with young children had negative experiences. Several reviews warn others not to stay, particularly families. Positive reviews mostly from couples or solo travelers on brief stopovers.

Location (7/10) — Good location "close to shops and lake," "few minutes away from the shops," "close to services," and "20 minutes walk from the centre of Lakes." Quiet street location away from main road praised. Dog-friendly nearby amenities. Easy access to Kmart, Woolworths, Aldi.

WHY NOT 100:

2. Serious cleanliness and safety issues including bedbug reports, non-operational smoke detectors wrapped in plastic, maintenance neglect (faulty lights, broken toilets), and a hospitalization from a hole in the grass - these are fundamental failures for families with young children.
