PARK: Acclaim Pine Grove Holiday Park
SCORE: 28/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-08
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 114

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Reviews consistently report no swimming pool (permanently closed and filled in), only one old swing for playground equipment, and zero on-site kids entertainment or activities. Multiple families expressed disappointment about the pool shown in photos no longer existing.

Nature (8/20) — Park features some mature shade trees and grassed areas with pine trees creating a bush setting feel. Located near Esperance beaches (3-5 minute drive) and national park access, though the park itself sits in an industrial area directly on the highway with no natural barrier or security fencing from the road.

Cleanliness (3/15) — Reviews overwhelmingly report severe cleanliness failures: raw sewage smells throughout the park, black mould covering shower cubicles, fly larvae in toilets and showers, dead bugs left uncleaned for days, dirty ensuites, cockroaches in cabins, overflowing bins, and filthy camp kitchens. Recent 2025-2026 reviews show ongoing sewage system failures with open sewerage pits and blocked drains overflowing with human waste.

Value (3/15) — Consistently described as overpriced ($48-$160 per night) for the condition provided. Multiple reviews state "total rip off", "not worth the price", and that other parks in the area are cheaper and better equipped. Families felt charged premium prices for severely substandard facilities.

Site Size (7/10) — Positive mentions of "large sites", "spacious", "good sized caravan sites", and "marvellous large sites". Some sites described as tight fits for camper trailers, and uneven with tree roots, but generally adequate space when level.

Sentiment (2/10) — Overwhelmingly negative family sentiment with phrases like "worst caravan park", "never coming back", "avoid at all costs", "last resort", and "should be condemned". Very few families would return, with most warning others to exhaust all other options. New management mentioned positively in late 2025 reviews but infrastructure issues persist into 2026.

Location (3/10) — Located directly on busy highway with road trains passing all night creating constant noise. No sound barrier or fencing between sites and road, making families feel unsafe. Close to town (5 minutes) and beaches, but in industrial area. Many reviews cite noise and safety concerns as deal-breakers.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe ongoing sewage system failures with raw sewage smells, open sewerage pits near campsites, blocked toilets, and drains overflowing with human waste that management has known about for months without adequate resolution.
2. Catastrophic cleanliness standards with black mould covering amenities, fly larvae infesting toilets and showers, dead bugs left uncleaned for days, and only 2 showers/toilets for the entire park that are rarely cleaned properly.
3. Complete absence of family entertainment infrastructure - no pool (permanently closed), virtually no playground (one old swing), no kids activities, misleading photos showing facilities that no longer exist, and extremely poor value for money charged.
