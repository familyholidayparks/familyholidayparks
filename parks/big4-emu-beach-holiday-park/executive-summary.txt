PARK: BIG4 Emu Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 87/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 232

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (19/20) — Reviews consistently highlight exceptional facilities including splash water park with slides and tipping bucket, two jumping pillows, mini golf, nature playground, theatre room with movies, recreation room with pool tables and ping pong, kids club activities during school holidays, and pedal carts. Families report children entertained "all day" with "hours of entertainment" and "so much to do on site."

Nature (18/20) — Beachfront location with direct access to Emu Beach, calm safe swimming, scenic walking and cycling trails along the coast to Emu Point cafe and Middleton Beach, surrounded by tall peppermint trees and peaceful bushland, wildlife sightings including bandicoots and rabbits, guests love "falling asleep to the sound of waves" with "beautiful beach clean, bluest of blue water."

Cleanliness (15/15) — Overwhelmingly praised as "immaculate," "spotlessly clean," "meticulously maintained" with modern amenities blocks cleaned daily, "some of the nicest ablutions" experienced, camp kitchens well-equipped and clean, grounds beautifully manicured with lush green lawns, staff visibly maintaining throughout the day.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing with several reviews noting "expensive" or "most we have paid in 8 weeks," powered tent sites $150/night, family apartments over $300/night, though many feel facilities justify the cost; some concern about extra charges for mini golf and laundry requiring coins not cards.

Site Size (8/10) — Good-sized sites with concrete slabs and beautiful green grass surrounds, though some reviews mention sites "just too small" or "a little tight" especially for larger vans, laneways can be narrow when busy requiring careful maneuvering, big rig area available with immaculate surroundings.

Sentiment (10/10) — Exceptional positive sentiment with high repeat visitation, families returning year after year calling it their "favourite park over 5 years of caravanning," "100/10" ratings from children, strong recommendations as "number one recommendation for kid friendly caravan parks," visitors "regretted not booking longer" and "will definitely be back."

Location (10/10) — Prime beachfront position barely 100m to beach, 10-15 minutes from Albany CBD, walking distance to Emu Point cafe, short drive to Middleton Beach and major attractions including The Gap and Natural Bridge, Woolworths complex 3km away, peaceful setting yet accessible to everything.

WHY NOT 100:
Some negative staff interactions reported with reception described as "rude and unhelpful" on multiple occasions, impacting customer service experience despite many praising staff friendliness. Value concerns with pricing considered high by some families, particularly budget cabin quality not matching $300+ nightly rates, and additional charges for activities and coin-operated laundry. Site access challenges for larger rigs with narrow internal roads, tight turning circles, and some concrete pads too short for longer caravans requiring leveling blocks.
