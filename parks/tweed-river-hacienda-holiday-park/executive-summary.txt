PARK: Tweed River Hacienda Holiday Park
SCORE: 35/100
DATE SCORED: 2025
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 9

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Only a "basic pool" mentioned; no kids clubs, playgrounds, or organized activities noted. One family mentioned kids enjoyed fishing, but no structured entertainment exists.
Nature (16/20) — Strong natural setting with "beautiful foreshore of the Tweed River," river frontage for fishing, views of Mount Warning, and short bike ride to beaches and lighthouse. Beautiful sunsets noted.
Cleanliness (5/15) — Significant concerns with "amenities aren't cleaned properly" and "grounds not much better." Park described as needing "a face lift" and "some love" due to aging condition.
Value (4/15) — No specific pricing feedback, but limited availability for holidaymakers and aging facilities suggest poor value. Park transitioning to permanent residents over holiday stays.
Site Size (7/10) — "Generous in size backing onto the Tweed River" with approximately 20 riverside sites noted. Good spacing mentioned for available camping sites.
Sentiment (3/10) — Mixed to negative sentiment with major disappointment about no longer accepting holidaymakers. Customer service described as "extremely rude." One long-time visitor "heartbroken" about policy change.
Location (8/10) — Excellent location "just off the highway," less than 10 minutes to Kingscliff, walking distance to pub, fish and chips shop opposite, cyclable to beaches.

WHY NOT 100:
1. The park no longer accepts holiday makers according to multiple recent reviews, fundamentally eliminating its usefulness as a family holiday destination.
2. Minimal entertainment facilities for children with only a basic pool and no playground, kids club, or organized activities mentioned across any reviews.
3. Serious cleanliness and maintenance issues with amenities not properly cleaned, aging facilities needing renovation, and concerning management practices reported by multiple reviewers.
