PARK: Margaret River Tourist Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 270

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently praise the jumping pillow, playground, pool, BBQ areas, ping pong table, and communal TV/games room. Families report "kids absolutely loved it," "made new friends instantly," and "plenty of things to do for the kids." Pool is unheated with 1-2m depth, which some found limiting for young children.

Nature (13/20) — Park features mature shady trees, grassed areas, and is near river/woodlands for walks. Several reviews mention "lovely bird life," "beautiful at this time of year," and "surrounded by trees." Located near beaches (~10km) and bush walks, though the park itself is in a more developed area near industrial zones and a primary school.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Majority of reviews describe facilities as "clean and tidy," "spotless," and "well maintained." Bathrooms and amenities "always very clean" with regular upkeep noted. Points lost for some reports of dated facilities, inconsistent cleaning schedules, dirty kitchen areas during busy periods, and complaints about "boogies in shower" and "sticky floors" in isolated cases.

Value (11/15) — Pricing described as "reasonable," "great value for money," and "worth it" by many families. However, concerns raised about dynamic pricing ($42-$63 per night fluctuations), high peak season rates, and dated facilities not matching price point. Some felt "overpriced" for what was offered, particularly with limited services like no toiletries provided.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant concern across reviews: "sites are small," "extremely tight," "smallish," "not enough room," and "a bit tight getting our 19.5ft van in & out." Powered sites particularly cramped with limited space for awnings and vehicles. Some ensuite and grassy sites offer more room, but overall space is a recurring complaint.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation noted ("2nd Christmas here," "stayed many times," "coming here for nearly 20 years"). Many would "definitely stay again" and "highly recommend." However, sentiment impacted by noise issues ("kept awake by campers," "loud until 2am"), inconsistent service experiences, and some guests leaving early due to dissatisfaction.

Location (8/10) — Excellent proximity: "walking distance to town" (10-20 min walk), "close to main street," "easy walk 500m to town." Near attractions, wineries, caves, beaches. Minor drawbacks: located near industrial area and primary school, causing some daytime noise. Exit gate described as "bottleneck" by some.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site size is consistently problematic, particularly for caravans — multiple reviews cite tight spaces, difficulty maneuvering larger rigs, and insufficient room for awnings alongside vehicles, limiting comfort for families with bigger setups.

2. Facilities are dated and require modernization — bathrooms need updating, some cabins have old pipes causing odors, water pressure/temperature inconsistencies, and worn fittings detract from the overall experience despite regular cleaning.

3. WiFi is extremely poor to non-existent — described as "extremely weak," "practically unusable," "grottenschlecht," and "patchy," forcing guests to rely on mobile data which also has poor reception ("only 1 bar of 4G"), creating frustration for modern families.
