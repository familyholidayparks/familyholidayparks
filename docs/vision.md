# Product Vision

## What Family Holiday Parks is building

Family Holiday Parks should become the first place Australian families check before booking a holiday park.

The platform is not just a directory. It should combine:

- trustworthy family reviews
- transparent scoring
- park comparison
- destination guidance
- useful family-specific detail
- memorable, shareable review outputs

## Core promise

> Find better family holidays.

Families should be able to understand quickly:

- whether a park suits their children
- what the park is genuinely good at
- what may disappoint them
- whether it offers value
- whether its location suits the trip they are planning
- how it compares with nearby alternatives

## Competitive advantage

The long-term advantage is a structured review system that captures more useful family context than generic review sites.

That includes:

- who travelled
- the ages of the children
- what the family actually used
- what the children loved
- what the adults valued
- where the park disappointed
- how the stay scored across the same seven criteria used throughout the site

This turns loose review text into comparable, family-specific evidence.

## Success looks like

- Families trust the score.
- Reviews feel human rather than robotic.
- The platform explains who each park is best for.
- Reviewers enjoy contributing.
- Families share their Holiday Reports.
- Parks cannot buy a better score.
- Private reviewer data stays private.
- The scoring method remains consistent as the platform grows.
