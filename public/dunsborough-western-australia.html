If We Were Booking...

  • Facilities and space: We'd choose BIG4 Dunsborough Southern Stars for the adventure playground, flying fox and BMX track on spacious sites, accepting a short drive to the beach.
  • Beachfront position: We'd choose Tasman Holiday Parks Yallingup Beach for direct sand access and dramatic coastal views, trading facilities for location.
  • Our tip: Swim at Meelup or Eagle Bay rather than the more exposed surf beaches, the water here is exceptionally calm and clear, genuinely some of the best family swimming conditions in WA.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Dunsborough good for families?
Yes, BIG4 Dunsborough Southern Stars is one of the best-equipped parks in the south west, and the calm bays at Meelup and Eagle Bay offer excellent swimming.
Which Dunsborough park is best for kids?
BIG4 Dunsborough Southern Stars, scoring 88/100, with an adventure playground, flying fox and BMX track.
Where is the best swimming for young children?
Meelup Beach and Eagle Bay, just north of Dunsborough, offer exceptionally calm, clear water.
Is BIG4 Dunsborough Southern Stars close to the beach?
It's 10 to 15 minutes drive from the beach, trading beachfront position for spacious sites and comprehensive facilities.
Which park is right on the beach?
Tasman Holiday Parks Yallingup Beach sits directly on the sand, suited to families with smaller rigs prioritising location.
Can you see whales from Dunsborough?
Yes, whale watching cruises operate from Dunsborough and nearby Busselton during the migration season.
What are the best free family activities?
Swimming at Meelup and Eagle Bay, and the walk to the Cape Naturaliste lighthouse.
When is the best time to visit?
Summer for swimming, or the whale season months for an additional wildlife activity.
What should families do when it rains?
BIG4 Dunsborough Southern Stars' games facilities cover a wet day, and the Margaret River region's indoor attractions are a short drive away.
How far ahead should you book?
BIG4 Dunsborough Southern Stars books out well ahead for school holidays given its reputation.
