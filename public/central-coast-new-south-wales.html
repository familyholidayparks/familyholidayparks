Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best family holiday parks on the Central Coast NSW?
NRMA Toowoon Bay, Canton Beach, and Norah Head holiday parks are top choices for families. Each offers direct beach access, modern facilities, and activities suited to children. Shelly Beach Holiday Park is another excellent option with a sheltered beach perfect for younger kids. All four parks provide safe, family-friendly environments with amenities like playgrounds and pools.
Which caravan park on the Central Coast is best for kids?
NRMA Toowoon Bay Holiday Park stands out for families with its playground, shallow beach access, and proximity to Toowoon Bay's rock pools. The park's layout keeps children safe, and staff are experienced with family groups. Canton Beach and Norah Head also cater well to kids with similar facilities and beach activities nearby.
Are there holiday parks with pools near the Central Coast?
Yes, several parks feature pools. NRMA Toowoon Bay and Canton Beach have swimming pools, making them ideal during warmer months or as backup rainy-day activities. These pools supplement the beaches and are maintained to high standards. Contact parks directly to confirm seasonal pool operating hours before booking.
Which Central Coast holiday parks are closest to the beach?
All four parks offer beachfront or beach-adjacent locations. Toowoon Bay has direct beach access with safe swimming areas. Norah Head sits near Norah Head Beach and lighthouse. Canton Beach is steps from its namesake beach. Shelly Beach Holiday Park offers direct access to Shelly Beach, known for calm, protected waters ideal for families with young children.
What Central Coast caravan parks offer powered sites?
NRMA Toowoon Bay, Canton Beach, and Norah Head holiday parks all provide powered caravan sites with full hookups. Shelly Beach Holiday Park also offers powered options. Powered sites include 15-amp or 20-amp connections. Book ahead during school holidays, as powered sites fill quickly on the Central Coast, particularly December-January.
Do Central Coast holiday parks have cabins available?
Yes, all four parks offer cabin accommodation ranging from basic to deluxe options. NRMA parks typically provide self-contained cabins with kitchens and bathrooms. Shelly Beach also offers cabin options. Cabins suit families wanting comfort without caravan setup. Off-peak rates offer better value. Book several months ahead for school holiday periods.
Which holiday parks on the Central Coast are pet-friendly?
NRMA Toowoon Bay, Canton Beach, and Norah Head holiday parks accept pets in designated areas, though restrictions apply. Some parks allow dogs in certain cabin types or caravan sites only. Shelly Beach Holiday Park also accommodates pets. Always confirm pet policies, size restrictions, and additional fees when booking, as these vary between parks.
What's the best value holiday park on the Central Coast?
Shelly Beach Holiday Park generally offers competitive rates, especially for powered caravan sites and basic cabins. NRMA parks provide good value considering facilities and beach access, with discounts for members. Off-peak visits (March-May, September-November) offer better rates across all parks. Weekly stays typically reduce nightly costs compared to short-term bookings.
What's the best area to stay on the Central Coast for families?
Toowoon Bay is excellent for families with rock pools, calm beach areas, and nearby shops. Norah Head offers lighthouse visits and scenic walks suitable for all ages. Canton Beach provides quieter beach access. Shelly Beach suits families wanting sheltered, protected swimming. Your choice depends on whether you prefer beach activities, exploration, or peaceful relaxation.
What rainy day activities are available near Central Coast holiday parks?
Norah Head Lighthouse has indoor exhibits and stories. Central Coast Leagues Club offers family entertainment. Gosford has the Australian Reptile Park and cinemas. Ocean Beach pools provide indoor options. Beaches offer rock pool exploring even in light rain. Many parks have indoor game rooms. The Central Coast offers sufficient indoor attractions to prevent holiday disruption during wet weather.
