If We Were Booking...

  • Farm-stay charm: We'd choose Kookaburra Park for the daily animal feeding, fairy garden and genuinely warm family atmosphere that beats most beach resorts for entertainment value.
  • Coast base, range days: We'd choose BIG4 Caloundra or Ingenia Rivershore for pool afternoons and easy day trips up to Maleny, Montville and Australia Zoo.
  • Our tip: Split your days: mornings up the range for Mary Cairncross's pademelons or Kondalilla Falls, afternoons back at the coast pool. The range delivers nature, the coast delivers recovery, and neither feels rushed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best family holiday parks in Sunshine Coast Hinterland?
BIG4 Caloundra, Kookaburra Park, Ingenia Holidays Rivershore, and Landsborough Holiday Park are top choices for families in the Hinterland. Each offers different amenities—BIG4 features excellent facilities, Kookaburra Park provides quiet bushland settings, Ingenia Holidays Rivershore offers riverside locations, and Landsborough Holiday Park delivers affordability with family-friendly grounds.
Which caravan park in Sunshine Coast Hinterland is best for kids?
BIG4 Caloundra stands out with dedicated kids' facilities, playgrounds, and organized activities. Kookaburra Park offers nature-based exploration opportunities with native wildlife viewing. Both parks provide safe, supervised environments where children can play while parents relax.
Are there holiday parks with pools near Sunshine Coast Hinterland?
Yes, BIG4 Caloundra features multiple pools including heated options perfect for families. Ingenia Holidays Rivershore also has pool facilities. These amenities are essential during warm Queensland months and provide reliable entertainment regardless of weather conditions.
Which holiday parks are near beaches in Sunshine Coast Hinterland?
Caloundra beaches are accessible from BIG4 Caloundra, offering the closest coastal access. Ingenia Holidays Rivershore provides riverside swimming instead. For beach visits, these parks offer convenient proximity to stunning Sunshine Coast waters within 15-20 minutes' drive.
What powered sites are available in Sunshine Coast Hinterland parks?
All four parks—BIG4 Caloundra, Kookaburra Park, Ingenia Holidays Rivershore, and Landsborough Holiday Park—offer powered caravan sites with varying amperage. BIG4 typically provides full-service powered spots with additional amenities, while other parks offer reliable power connections at competitive rates suitable for extended stays.
What cabin options are available in Sunshine Coast Hinterland?
BIG4 Caloundra offers luxury cabins with modern amenities. Ingenia Holidays Rivershore provides comfortable self-contained options. Landsborough Holiday Park and Kookaburra Park also feature cabin accommodation. Choices range from basic budget cabins to fully equipped family units with ensuite bathrooms and kitchenettes.
Are there pet-friendly holiday parks in Sunshine Coast Hinterland?
Several parks welcome pets with designated pet-friendly sites. Confirm specific pet policies directly, as restrictions vary regarding breed, size, and number of animals allowed. Most parks require advance notification and may charge additional pet fees to accommodate your furry family members.
Which holiday park offers best value in Sunshine Coast Hinterland?
Landsborough Holiday Park typically delivers excellent value without compromising on essential family facilities. Compare nightly rates across BIG4 Caloundra, Kookaburra Park, and Ingenia Holidays Rivershore for weekly discounts and shoulder-season specials that significantly reduce accommodation costs.
What's the best area to stay in Sunshine Coast Hinterland for families?
Caloundra offers the best balance—close to beaches, hinterland attractions, and family-friendly restaurants. BIG4 Caloundra positions you perfectly for accessing both coastal activities and inland rainforest walks. Landsborough provides quieter, more affordable Hinterland experiences with excellent day-trip accessibility.
What can families do with kids in Sunshine Coast Hinterland?
Enjoy bushwalking at national parks, wildlife spotting at Austinvilla Estate Vineyard, visiting the Sunshine Coast Wildlife Centre, exploring mountain villages, and browsing local markets. Rainy days suit indoor attractions like the Eumundi Markets area shops, local cafes, and park movie nights at some holiday parks.
