If We Were Booking...

  • All-weather facilities: We'd choose BIG4 Ingenia Holidays Inverloch for the indoor heated pool and splash park, a genuine wet-weather insurance policy on this coastline.
  • Beach and paddleboarding: We'd choose Ingenia Holidays Inverloch Foreshore for a more natural setting with easy access to beach activities and paddleboarding.
  • Our tip: Do the Screw Creek Nature Walk boardwalk with younger kids, it's flat, easy and shaded, then head to the indoor pool if the Gippsland weather turns, which it often does even in summer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Inverloch good for families?
Yes, particularly given BIG4 Ingenia's indoor heated pool, which makes it a reliable choice regardless of South Gippsland's changeable weather.
Which Inverloch park is best for kids?
BIG4 Ingenia Holidays Inverloch, with an indoor heated pool, splash park, jumping pillow and pump track.
Is the beach at Inverloch safe for swimming?
Yes, the main beach is patrolled in season.
What is the Screw Creek Nature Walk?
An easy boardwalk through wetlands near Inverloch, well suited to young children and a good option for a calm morning activity.
Is Cape Paterson worth a visit?
Yes, it's a short drive away with good rock pool exploring at low tide.
What are the best free family activities?
The Screw Creek boardwalk, the main beach, and rock pooling at nearby Cape Paterson.
When is the best time to visit?
Summer for the beach, though the indoor pool at BIG4 Ingenia makes it a workable destination in cooler months too.
What should families do when it rains?
BIG4 Ingenia's indoor heated pool and splash park are the reliable wet-weather option here.
How far is Inverloch from Melbourne?
About 2 hours drive south east, a manageable weekend trip.
How far ahead should you book?
BIG4 Ingenia books out for school holidays given its strong facilities, so plan ahead.
