Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best family accommodation in Inverloch?
Inverloch offers fantastic family-friendly options along the Bass Coast. BIG4 Ingenia Holidays Inverloch is brilliant for families wanting pools and playgrounds, while Ingenia Holidays Inverloch Foreshore puts you steps from the beach. Inverloch Surfside Holiday Retreat is another top choice with spacious sites. All three parks offer cabin and camping options, so whether you're towing a van or want a comfy bed, you're sorted for a memorable coastal getaway.
Which is the best caravan park in Inverloch?
BIG4 Ingenia Holidays Inverloch consistently rates as the top choice, offering excellent facilities including jumping pillows, heated pools, and entertainment for the kids. If beachfront location matters most, Ingenia Holidays Inverloch Foreshore can't be beaten for proximity to the sand. Inverloch Surfside Holiday Retreat is another cracker option, particularly for families wanting a quieter, more relaxed vibe while still enjoying quality amenities and easy beach access.
What activities are there for kids in Inverloch?
Inverloch keeps kids brilliantly entertained! The calm waters at the main beach are perfect for swimming and paddling. Head to Point Smythe at dusk to watch the penguin parade - it's less crowded than Phillip Island. The Bunurong Coastal Walk offers easy sections for little legs, and Anderson Inlet is fantastic for spotting dolphins and rays. Don't miss fossicking in the rock pools at low tide or hiring bikes to explore the coastal trails.
Which beach in Inverloch is best for kids?
The main Inverloch Beach near the foreshore is absolutely perfect for families with young kids. The water stays shallow for ages and the gentle waves make it ideal for safe swimming and paddling. There's a natural lagoon area near the inlet that's particularly calm. The beach has grassy areas nearby for picnics, plus it's walking distance to cafes and the playground. Low tide reveals brilliant rock pools for exploring too.
Does the Esplanade Hotel Inverloch have a kids menu?
The Esplanade Hotel Inverloch is family-friendly and offers kids dining options. It's a popular spot right on the foreshore with views across Anderson Inlet. The pub has a relaxed atmosphere where families feel welcome. For the freshest feed though, many locals grab fish and chips from the Inverloch fishmongers and enjoy them on the beach - the kids can run around while you watch the sunset over the water.
What things can we do in Inverloch with kids this weekend?
This weekend, start with a swim at the main beach, then explore the rock pools at low tide. The Bunurong Coastal Walk offers stunning views and easy sections perfect for families. Visit Point Smythe at sunset to watch the little penguins waddle ashore. Anderson Inlet is brilliant for spotting stingrays from the jetty. Grab fish and chips for tea, and if weather's fine, hire bikes to cruise the coastal paths around town.
What indoor activities are there in Inverloch for kids?
When the weather turns, Inverloch's indoor options are a bit limited as it's a small coastal town. Your best bet is staying at BIG4 Ingenia Holidays Inverloch which has indoor recreation facilities. The Inverloch Library runs children's programs during school holidays. Otherwise, it's a short drive to Wonthaggi for tenpin bowling and indoor play centres, or head to Phillip Island's many indoor attractions like the Penguin Parade Visitor Centre.
