If We Were Booking...

  • Wildlife and the wild west end: We'd choose Discovery Parks Kangaroo Island for cabins near Flinders Chase, kangaroos through camp at dusk and short runs to Remarkable Rocks and Admirals Arch.
  • Town and beach base: We'd choose KI Caravan Park at Kingscote with Brownlow Beach across the road, shops nearby and wildlife spotting from the park itself.
  • Our tip: Board sandboards for Little Sahara and swim at Emu Bay, the locals' beach. And book Seal Bay's guided tour rather than just the boardwalk, walking the beach among wild sea lions is the memory the kids keep.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kangaroo Island good for kids?
Excellent for wildlife-mad kids. Wild sea lions at Seal Bay, kangaroos and echidnas around the parks, sandboarding at Little Sahara and calm swimming at Emu Bay.
How do you get to Kangaroo Island with a car or van?
The SeaLink ferry from Cape Jervis to Penneshaw, about 45 minutes. Book vehicle spots well ahead for school holidays, they genuinely sell out.
Can you take a caravan on the ferry?
Yes, priced by length. Many families leave big vans on the mainland and take the car plus a cabin booking instead, it's often cheaper than shipping the van across.
What is the one thing families shouldn't miss?
Seal Bay's guided tour. You walk the beach among a wild sea lion colony with a guide. The boardwalk alone is good, the beach walk is unforgettable.
How big is Kangaroo Island really?
Bigger than people expect, 155 kilometres end to end. Kingscote to Flinders Chase is about 90 minutes, so split your stay or accept some driving.
Where is the best swimming for kids?
Emu Bay, calm, shallow and you can drive onto the beach. Stokes Bay's hidden beach through the rock tunnel is the adventure option.
How many nights do you need?
Three nights minimum, five is comfortable. Day trips from the mainland spend most of the day on the ferry and miss the dusk wildlife, which is the best part.
What wildlife will kids actually see?
Kangaroos and wallabies at dusk around the parks, sea lions at Seal Bay, koalas in the trees around Flinders Chase and, with luck, echidnas crossing the road.
What should families do when it rains?
The island is quiet in rain. Seal Bay tours still run, Clifford's Honey Farm and the Emu Ridge eucalyptus distillery are undercover, and cabin board games fill the gaps.
Is Kangaroo Island expensive?
The ferry adds real cost for vehicles, and groceries run higher than the mainland. Fuel up and stock up in Kingscote, and budget the ferry as part of the trip cost.
