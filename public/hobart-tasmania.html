If We Were Booking...

  • City base: We'd choose BIG4 Hobart Airport Tourist Park, 15 minutes from town with the jumping pillow and basketball court kids love, and painless early flights when the trip ends.
  • Animals or beach: We'd choose Huon Valley Caravan Park for the daily devil feeding and animal shows on-site, or Snug Beach and White Beach for calm sand plus the Port Arthur day trip.
  • Our tip: Do Salamanca Market on Saturday morning, then drive up kunanyi/Mount Wellington in the afternoon. Take jumpers whatever the season, it can be 15 degrees colder at the summit than on the waterfront.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hobart good for families?
Yes. Salamanca Market, the kunanyi summit, Bonorong's kangaroo feeding, Richmond's bridge and lolly shop, and beach or bush parks within 30 minutes of everything.
Which Hobart holiday park is best for kids?
BIG4 Hobart Airport for facilities, its jumping pillow and basketball court are consistently praised, or Huon Valley Caravan Park where daily animal shows and devil feeding make the park the attraction.
Is there a park near Port Arthur?
White Beach Tourist Park on the Tasman Peninsula, with a playground, beach swimming and about 20 minutes to Port Arthur's historic site.
Can you take kids up Mount Wellington?
Yes, kunanyi's summit is a 30-minute drive from the city with boardwalks and shelters at the top. Pack warm layers year-round, the summit is dramatically colder.
Is MONA suitable for children?
Parts of it, and the museum flags adult content clearly. The ferry ride to MONA is a hit regardless, and younger kids often enjoy the grounds more than the galleries.
What is Bonorong Wildlife Sanctuary?
A wildlife sanctuary at Brighton, 25 minutes from Hobart, where kids hand-feed kangaroos and meet devils, wombats and koalas. It's the most reliable wildlife hit near the city.
What are the best free family activities?
Salamanca Market on Saturdays, the kunanyi summit, Richmond village and bridge, and the beaches at Kingston, Snug and Seven Mile.
What should families do when it rains?
The Tasmanian Museum and Art Gallery is free, Mawson's Huts Replica Museum is compact and good, and the park games rooms and indoor play centres cover the rest. Hobart rain rarely lasts all day.
When is the best time to visit Hobart with kids?
December to March for beach weather and long evenings. Winter is cold but comes with snow on the mountain and the Winter Feast if you time it right.
How far in advance should you book?
Summer school holidays and the late-December festival period book out ahead. The rest of the year is generally easy.
