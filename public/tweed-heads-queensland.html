If We Were Booking...

  • Waterpark and theme park access: We'd choose BIG4 Tweed Billabong Holiday Park for the massive waterpark and treehouse playground, using it as a lower-cost base for Gold Coast theme park days.
  • Quieter beaches: We'd choose Tweed Holiday Parks Pottsville South for a calmer coastal pace away from the border bustle.
  • Our tip: Base at Tweed Billabong and treat it as your Gold Coast theme park headquarters. You get the same 20-minute drive to Dreamworld and Movie World, but sites typically price below the Gold Coast strip itself.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Tweed Heads good for families wanting Gold Coast theme parks?
Yes, it's a genuinely cheaper base. Dreamworld, Movie World and Sea World are all 20 to 30 minutes away, often for lower accommodation prices than staying on the Gold Coast strip.
Which Tweed Heads park is best for kids?
BIG4 Tweed Billabong Holiday Park by a distance, with a massive waterpark, heated pool, multiple waterslides and a treehouse playground.
Can you swim at Tweed Heads beaches?
Yes, Coolangatta and Kirra beaches are a short drive north and patrolled in season, with generally calmer conditions than further up the Gold Coast.
Is the Tweed River good for families?
Yes, it offers calm paddling, fishing and river swimming spots, a gentler alternative to the ocean beaches for younger kids.
How far is Tweed Heads from the Gold Coast theme parks?
About 20 to 30 minutes to Dreamworld, Movie World and Sea World, similar drive times to staying at many southern Gold Coast parks.
What are the best free family activities?
The river foreshore parks, Coolangatta and Kirra beaches, and the Boyd Street lookout over the Tweed River mouth.
When is the best time to visit?
School holidays if theme parks are the focus, book Tweed Billabong well ahead for those windows. Shoulder seasons are quieter and cheaper.
What should families do when it rains?
Tweed Billabong's games room covers a wet morning, and the Tweed Heads and Coolangatta shopping and cinema precincts are close by.
Is Tweed Heads walkable to the border with the Gold Coast?
Yes, it blends seamlessly into Coolangatta, you can walk across the border on the beach without noticing.
How far ahead should you book?
Tweed Billabong books out for school holidays given its waterpark reputation. The quieter parks further south are usually easier.
