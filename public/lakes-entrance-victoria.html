If We Were Booking...

  • Facilities and position: We'd choose NRMA Eastern Beach Holiday Park for the jumping pillow, heated pool and movie nights, a short walk from the beach.
  • Animal encounters: We'd choose Echo Beach Tourist Park for the on-site petting animals alongside its trampoline and playground, walking distance to town.
  • Our tip: Swim the calm lakes with younger kids in the morning, then walk the boardwalk across to the patrolled ocean beach in the afternoon for a proper surf swim. Two different water experiences in one easy day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lakes Entrance good for families?
Yes, the combination of calm lake water, a patrolled ocean beach and a walkable town centre makes it a well-rounded family destination.
Which Lakes Entrance park is best for kids?
NRMA Eastern Beach Holiday Park, with a jumping pillow, heated pool and movie nights.
Is the ocean beach safe for swimming?
The Ninety Mile Beach section is patrolled in season for surf swimming.
Are the lakes good for young children?
Yes, the lakes are generally calm and well suited to paddling, kayaking and younger swimmers.
Can you do boat cruises on the lakes?
Yes, cruises run regularly and suit families of all ages, a relaxed way to see the lake system.
What are the best free family activities?
The boardwalk across to the ocean beach, the town's foreshore, and lake swimming.
When is the best time to visit?
Summer school holidays for full beach and lake conditions, though the town is pleasant across most of the year.
What should families do when it rains?
The park games rooms and Echo Beach's animal encounters cover a wet morning.
How far is Lakes Entrance from Melbourne?
About 3.5 hours drive east, a popular Gippsland coast destination.
How far ahead should you book?
NRMA Eastern Beach books out for school holidays given its facilities, so plan ahead.
