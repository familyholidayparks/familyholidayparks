Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best accommodation in Maleny for families with kids?
Kookaburra Park offers excellent family-friendly accommodation right in the hinterland, while BIG4 Maroochy River and Cotton Tree Holiday Park provide brilliant bases just 20 minutes away on the Sunshine Coast. For something closer to Maleny's village charm, Beerwah Caravan Park and Landsborough Holiday Park are solid options. Visit between April and October for the most comfortable weather – mild days perfect for exploring without the sticky summer humidity.
Where can families stay in Maleny?
Families love staying at Kookaburra Park in the Maleny area, or heading to nearby BIG4 Maroochy River and Cotton Tree Holiday Park on the coast. Beerwah Caravan Park and Landsborough Holiday Park offer great alternatives close to the hinterland attractions. Maleny's a ripper for kids! Explore the stunning lookouts along the Blackall Range, visit local dairy farms for hands-on experiences, and wander through the quirky village shops. The region's playgrounds and parks are beautifully maintained, and you're only 20 minutes from Sunshine Coast beaches when the little ones need a splash. Grab morning treats from Maple Street Co and enjoy a relaxed hinterland pace that kids absolutely love.
