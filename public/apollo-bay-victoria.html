Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best family holiday parks in Apollo Bay, VIC?
BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park, Apollo Bay Holiday Park, and Marengo Family Caravan Park are top choices. Each offers different amenities—Pisces features pools and modern facilities, Apollo Bay Holiday Park provides beachside access, and Marengo suits families wanting a quieter setting. Apollo Bay Recreation Reserve offers budget-friendly camping. Compare based on your priorities: pools, beach proximity, or budget.
Which caravan park in Apollo Bay is best for kids?
BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park leads for families with children, offering swimming pools, playground facilities, and organised activities during peak seasons. Marengo Family Caravan Park also caters to families with dedicated play areas. Both parks provide structured entertainment options that keep kids engaged beyond standard accommodation.
Are there holiday parks with pools near Apollo Bay?
BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park has dedicated swimming pools, making it ideal for families prioritising water activities. This is the primary park in the area featuring pool facilities. During warmer months, pools provide entertainment on hot days and are particularly valuable for younger children needing supervised water play.
Which holiday parks in Apollo Bay are near the beach?
Apollo Bay Holiday Park sits closest to the beach, offering direct beach access for families. BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park is also beachside-adjacent with bay views. Both parks let children easily access sandy shores, rock pools, and water activities without lengthy walks, ideal for families with young kids.
What Apollo Bay parks have powered sites for caravans?
All major parks—BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces, Apollo Bay Holiday Park, and Marengo Family Caravan Park—offer powered caravan sites with full hookups. BIG4 Pisces provides the most extensive powered site options with modern infrastructure. Apollo Bay Recreation Reserve offers basic powered sites at lower costs, suiting budget-conscious families.
Where can families rent cabins in Apollo Bay?
BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park offers self-contained cabins ranging from basic to luxury options. Apollo Bay Holiday Park provides cabin accommodation with ensuite facilities. Marengo Family Caravan Park has cabin options for families avoiding campervans. All three parks offer year-round cabin availability, though peak season booking ahead is essential.
Are there pet-friendly holiday parks in Apollo Bay?
Most Apollo Bay parks accommodate pets on-site, including BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces and Apollo Bay Holiday Park, though specific pet policies vary. Check directly as some areas restrict pets, and many charge pet fees. Apollo Bay Recreation Reserve generally allows pets. Always confirm breed restrictions and leash requirements before booking with pets.
What's the best value holiday park in Apollo Bay?
Apollo Bay Recreation Reserve offers budget camping at lower daily rates than commercial parks. Basic powered and unpowered sites suit families watching costs. For better facilities at reasonable prices, check off-season rates at BIG4 Pisces or Marengo, where discounts apply outside school holidays and peak periods.
What's the best area to stay in Apollo Bay for families?
The beachfront precinct near Apollo Bay Holiday Park suits families wanting beach access and town amenities within walking distance. BIG4 Pisces offers quiet, facilities-rich stays slightly removed from town hustle. Marengo provides peaceful countryside settings. Choose based on whether you prefer beach proximity or quieter, family-focused park atmospheres.
What rainy day activities can families do near Apollo Bay?
Visit the Apollo Bay Seafood Festival precinct, indoor shopping areas, or local cafes. The nearby Great Otway National Park offers sheltered rainforest walks with kids. Check for indoor attractions in nearby towns like Lorne. Parks with pools provide weather-independent entertainment. Plan indoor crafts or games at your accommodation for extended wet periods.
