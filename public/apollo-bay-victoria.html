Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best family accommodation in Apollo Bay?
Apollo Bay offers brilliant family accommodation options right where the Great Ocean Road meets the Otways. BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park and Apollo Bay Holiday Park both provide excellent facilities close to the beach, while Marengo Family Caravan Park offers a quieter spot just down the coast. Most parks feature playgrounds, camp kitchens, and easy access to both beaches and rainforest walks – perfect for keeping the kids entertained.
Which family caravan parks in Apollo Bay are worth staying at?
Marengo Family Caravan Park is a cracker choice for families wanting a more relaxed vibe, while BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park delivers top-notch amenities if the kids need plenty of action. Apollo Bay Holiday Park sits beautifully close to town, making bakery runs and beach access a breeze. The Apollo Bay Recreation Reserve also offers budget-friendly camping for families who love the outdoors.
What's the best caravan park in Apollo Bay?
BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park consistently ranks as the top pick for families, with excellent facilities and a location that's hard to beat. That said, your 'best' really depends on what matters most – Marengo Family Caravan Park wins for peaceful coastal stays, while Apollo Bay Holiday Park can't be beaten for walking into town. Visit during shoulder season (April-May or September-November) for the best availability.
What are the best kids activities in Apollo Bay?
The rockpools at the eastern end of Apollo Bay beach are absolute gold for little explorers, and the calm waters make it perfect for swimming. Kids love the Maits Rest Rainforest Walk – it's an easy 30-minute boardwalk through ancient trees that feels like stepping into a fairy tale. Don't miss the Apollo Bay Bakery for treats, and check out the local playgrounds along the foreshore for sunset play sessions.
What family activities are there in Apollo Bay?
Apollo Bay delivers the lot – beach swims, rockpool exploring, and rainforest walks all within minutes of each other. Families love the Great Ocean Road Wildlife Park for meeting koalas and kangaroos, while the Otway Fly Treetop Walk offers an unforgettable canopy adventure. Grab fish and chips from the local shops and watch the sunset from the breakwater, or tackle one of the easier Otways waterfall walks together.
Which is the best family park in Apollo Bay?
BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park leads the pack for families wanting comprehensive facilities and activities on-site. Apollo Bay Holiday Park offers excellent value with a more intimate feel and prime location near town. Marengo Family Caravan Park suits families after a quieter, beachside experience. Each park has its own character, so choose based on whether the kids need constant entertainment or you're planning your own adventures.
What are the best things to do near Apollo Bay with kids?
The Otways rainforest is your playground – Triplet Falls and Hopetoun Falls are manageable walks with spectacular payoffs. Great Ocean Road Wildlife Park lets kids get up close with native animals, while Cape Otway Lightstation offers history and wildlife spotting. Back in town, the foreshore playground and calm swimming beach keep little ones happy. Time your visit for shoulder season to enjoy everything without the summer crowds.
