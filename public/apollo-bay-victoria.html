Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best family holiday parks in Apollo Bay?
BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park, Apollo Bay Holiday Park, and Marengo Family Caravan Park are top choices. Each offers different facilities suited to families. BIG4 Pisces features pools and entertainment, Apollo Bay Holiday Park provides beachfront access, and Marengo offers spacious grounds. Check each park's current amenities as they vary seasonally. Apollo Bay Recreation Reserve also accommodates families seeking basic facilities at lower costs.
Which caravan park in Apollo Bay is best for kids?
BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park stands out for families with children, offering swimming pools, playgrounds, and organised activities during peak seasons. Their facilities cater specifically to keeping kids entertained. Apollo Bay Holiday Park's beach location appeals to families preferring water-based activities. Consider your children's ages when choosing—some parks suit toddlers better while others entertain teenagers.
Are there holiday parks with pools near Apollo Bay?
BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park has heated pools, making it the primary option for families prioritising swimming facilities. Pools operate seasonally, so verify opening dates when booking winter stays. Other parks focus on beach access rather than pools. If pool access is essential, BIG4 Pisces is your best choice for guaranteed swimming facilities during warmer months.
Which holiday parks in Apollo Bay are near the beach?
Apollo Bay Holiday Park sits closest to the main beach, offering direct beach access—ideal for families wanting easy shoreline visits. Marengo Family Caravan Park and BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces are within walking distance to beaches. Apollo Bay Recreation Reserve has reasonable beach proximity. All parks benefit from Apollo Bay's coastal location, though proximity varies—confirm exact distances when booking.
What caravan parks in Apollo Bay have powered sites?
BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park, Apollo Bay Holiday Park, and Marengo Family Caravan Park all offer powered caravan sites. Powered site availability varies seasonally—book ahead for peak periods. Apollo Bay Recreation Reserve provides basic facilities including some powered options at competitive rates. Compare pricing across parks as powered site costs differ significantly during school holidays.
Where can families find cabin accommodation in Apollo Bay?
BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park offers cabins ranging from basic to luxury options. Apollo Bay Holiday Park and Marengo Family Caravan Park also provide cabin and self-contained accommodation. Cabin types vary—some include ensuites and kitchens while others share facilities. Book well ahead for school holiday periods. Cabins suit families preferring more comfort than camping.
Are any Apollo Bay holiday parks pet-friendly?
Several Apollo Bay parks accept pets; however, policies vary significantly. BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park typically allows pets in designated areas. Apollo Bay Holiday Park and Marengo Family Caravan Park have pet-friendly options. Always confirm current pet policies, breed restrictions, and additional fees before booking. Some areas within parks may be pet-free, so clarify exactly where your pet can stay.
Which holiday park in Apollo Bay offers best value for families?
Apollo Bay Recreation Reserve provides budget-friendly options with basic facilities suitable for budget-conscious families. BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park offers reasonable mid-range pricing with excellent facilities included. Compare nightly rates across all four parks—prices fluctuate seasonally. Value depends on your priorities: basic comfort, pools, beach access, or entertainment. Off-season visits typically offer better rates across all parks.
What's the best area to stay in Apollo Bay for families?
Apollo Bay's compact size means all parks are near attractions. Apollo Bay Holiday Park's beachfront location suits families wanting immediate beach access. BIG4 Pisces appeals to families prioritising pools and activities. Marengo offers quieter, spacious grounds. Central locations provide easiest access to shops and restaurants. Consider your family's interests—beach lovers choose differently than those wanting pool facilities and entertainment.
What are the best times to visit Apollo Bay with kids?
School holidays—April, July, and December-January—see peak activity but higher prices and crowds. Summer (December-February) offers warm weather and beach swimming, though facilities get busy. Shoulder seasons (March-April, September-October) provide pleasant weather with fewer crowds and better rates. Winter suits families enjoying cool coastal walks. Check school term dates and book accommodation early for holiday periods when availability is limited.
