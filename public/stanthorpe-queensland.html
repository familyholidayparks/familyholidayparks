Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best family holiday parks in Stanthorpe, QLD?
Country Style Holiday Park, Top of the Town Tourist Park, and Tiny Away Escape @ Granite Belt are top choices for families visiting Stanthorpe. Each offers different accommodation styles, from powered caravan sites to comfortable cabins. They're centrally located, making it easy to access local wineries, orchards, and scenic walks that appeal to families exploring the Granite Belt region.
Which caravan park in Stanthorpe is best for kids?
Top of the Town Tourist Park offers excellent facilities for families, with spacious grounds and proximity to local attractions. Country Style Holiday Park provides a relaxed, family-friendly atmosphere with good amenities. Both parks put families near Stanthorpe's child-friendly activities like fruit picking and scenic drives through the Granite Belt.
Are there holiday parks with pools near Stanthorpe?
Country Style Holiday Park features a swimming pool, making it ideal for families wanting water-based recreation during their stay. This is particularly appealing during warmer months. The pool provides a great way for kids to cool off while parents relax, adding value to your Stanthorpe holiday experience.
Is there a holiday park near the beach in Stanthorpe?
Stanthorpe is inland in the Granite Belt region, so there are no beachside parks. However, the nearest beaches are at the Gold Coast, approximately two hours away. These family-friendly parks are better suited for exploring Stanthorpe's mountain scenery, wineries, and outdoor activities rather than beach holidays.
Which parks offer powered sites in Stanthorpe?
Country Style Holiday Park and Top of the Town Tourist Park both provide powered caravan and motorhome sites with full hookups. These powered sites are essential for families with larger caravans or those needing air conditioning during summer. Book ahead during peak seasons to secure your preferred powered spot.
What cabin options are available in Stanthorpe holiday parks?
Tiny Away Escape @ Granite Belt specializes in cabin accommodations, offering comfortable self-contained options. Top of the Town Tourist Park and Country Style Holiday Park also provide cabin choices ranging from basic to deluxe. Cabins suit families preferring furnished accommodation over caravan camping.
Are there pet-friendly holiday parks in Stanthorpe?
Several Stanthorpe parks accept pets, though policies vary. Check directly with Country Style Holiday Park, Top of the Town Tourist Park, and Tiny Away Escape regarding pet accommodations and any associated fees. This allows families to bring their furry companions on Stanthorpe holidays without leaving them behind.
What's the best value holiday park in Stanthorpe?
Country Style Holiday Park offers competitive rates for powered sites and cabins, providing good value for families. Prices vary seasonally, so visiting outside school holidays and peak fruit-picking season typically offers better rates. Compare nightly costs across all three parks to find the best deal for your dates.
What's the best area in Stanthorpe to stay with family?
Top of the Town Tourist Park's elevated location provides excellent views and easy access to town amenities and attractions. Country Style Holiday Park offers a more relaxed rural setting while remaining convenient to Stanthorpe's main activities. Both locations put families within 10-15 minutes of wineries, orchards, and scenic lookouts.
What can families do with kids in Stanthorpe during rainy days?
Indoor options include Stanthorpe Wine Centre tastings (non-alcoholic options available), local museums, and covered farm attractions. Many orchards and farms offer indoor activities like fruit picking sheds. Parks' indoor facilities and nearby cafes provide shelter, making rainy days manageable when visiting Stanthorpe's Granite Belt region.
