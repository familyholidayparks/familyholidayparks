If We Were Booking...

  • Cable Beach position: We'd choose Cable Beach Caravan Park or RAC Cable Beach Holiday Park to walk to the beach for sunset and the camel trains, with Cable Beach CP's shaded pool the standout.
  • Space and value: We'd choose Broome Caravan Park for its resort-style pool with a lap lane and roomier sites, accepting the 10-minute drive to Cable Beach.
  • Our tip: Check the Staircase to the Moon calendar before booking. It only happens on a handful of nights each month through the dry, and the Town Beach markets that run with it are the best family night in Broome.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to take kids to Broome?
The dry season, May to September. Warm days, no humidity, safe swimming at Cable Beach and every outdoor activity running. The wet season brings storms, humidity and stingers.
Which Broome caravan park has the best pool?
Cable Beach Caravan Park's shaded resort-style pool is the standout, with Broome Caravan Park's pool, complete with a lap lane, a close second. In Broome heat the pool is half the holiday.
Is it safe to swim at Cable Beach?
In the dry season, yes, at the patrolled section. Stinger season runs roughly November to May, and locals take crocodile advice seriously year-round, so swim where patrolled and heed the signs.
What is the Staircase to the Moon?
A natural effect where the full moon rising over the mudflats creates a staircase illusion, visible a few nights a month from March to October. Town Beach runs night markets to match.
Where are the dinosaur footprints?
Gantheaume Point, at low tide. Check the tide chart and go at the lowest tide you can, the footprints sit in the reef rock below the cliffs.
Are the camel rides worth it?
The sunset camel trains along Cable Beach are a genuine bucket-list experience for kids. Book ahead in peak season, or just watch them pass for free at sunset.
How far ahead should you book for the dry season?
For June to August, several months at minimum; many regulars book a year out. May and September are noticeably easier.
Do kids need anything special for Broome?
Serious sun protection, reef shoes for the rocky points, and a hat that stays on. The UV is extreme even in winter.
What should families do in the heat of the day?
Pool time. Structure the day like locals: beach and activities early, pool through the middle, Cable Beach for sunset.
Is Broome expensive?
In peak dry season, yes, sites price well above southern equivalents and flights fill early. The shoulder months soften both.
