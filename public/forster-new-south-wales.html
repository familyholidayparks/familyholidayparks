If We Were Booking...

  • Waterpark facilities: We'd choose NRMA Forster Tuncurry Holiday Park for the multiple pools including a waterpark with slides, or Discovery Parks Forster for the splash park and pump track.
  • Lakeside calm: We'd choose Lakeside Forster Holiday Park for direct lake access and kayaking, a gentler pace for families with younger children.
  • Our tip: Do the lake in the morning while it's glassy calm for paddleboarding, then head to the surf beach in the afternoon once the sea breeze picks up. Forster genuinely gives you two different water holidays in one stay.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Forster good for families?
Yes, the combination of calm lake swimming and proper surf beaches, plus strong entertainment-focused parks, makes it one of the mid-north coast's best family bases.
Which Forster park has the best facilities for kids?
NRMA Forster Tuncurry and Discovery Parks Forster both run strong entertainment programs, with waterparks, jumping pillows and school holiday activities.
Is Wallis Lake safe for young children?
Yes, it's generally calm and shallow in the shoreline areas, well suited to paddling, kayaking and safe swimming.
Are the ocean beaches patrolled?
Forster Main Beach and Nine Mile Beach are patrolled in season for surf swimming.
What is Cape Hawke lookout?
A short, steep walk to a tower with sweeping views over Forster, the lake and the coastline, manageable for older kids.
What are the best free family activities?
Lake swimming and paddling, Cape Hawke lookout, and the walking track around Bicentennial Park by the water.
When is the best time to visit?
School holidays for the full activity programs, or shoulder season for a quieter, still-mild stay.
What should families do when it rains?
The park games rooms and school holiday indoor activities cover most wet days at the bigger parks.
How far is Forster from Sydney?
About 3.5 hours drive north, making it a popular weekend and school holiday destination for Sydney families.
How far ahead should you book?
The entertainment-focused parks book out well ahead for school holidays given their popularity.
