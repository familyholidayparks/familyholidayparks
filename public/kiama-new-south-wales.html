If We Were Booking...

  • Full resort facilities: We'd choose BIG4 Easts Beach Holiday Park for the waterpark, jumping pillow, arcade and kids club, all steps from the beach.
  • Quiet beach walk: We'd choose Surf Beach Holiday Park or Kendalls On The Beach for a quieter, more natural beachfront setting.
  • Our tip: See the blowhole first thing in the morning before the tour buses arrive, then settle in at Easts Beach for the rest of the day. The waterpark keeps kids busy for hours and the beach is right there when they want a change.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kiama good for families?
Yes, BIG4 Easts Beach offers some of the best resort-style facilities on the South Coast, and the town's famous blowhole is a quick, free attraction.
Which Kiama park is best for kids?
BIG4 Easts Beach Holiday Park, with a waterpark, jumping pillow, arcade and kids club right on the beachfront.
What is the Kiama Blowhole?
A natural sea cave that forces water into a dramatic spray, most impressive with decent ocean swell. It's free and located right in town.
Are Kiama's beaches safe for swimming?
Easts Beach, Surf Beach and Kendalls Beach are all patrolled in season and suit family swimming.
Is Werri Beach at Gerringong worth visiting?
Yes, it's a long, patrolled beach a short drive south, popular for its space and consistent surf.
Can you walk between Kiama and Gerringong?
Yes, a coastal walking track links the two towns with clifftop views, doable in shorter sections with younger kids.
What are the best free family activities?
The blowhole, the coastal walk, and the various patrolled beaches along the strip.
When is the best time to visit?
Summer school holidays for full beach conditions, though the blowhole and walks work year-round.
What should families do when it rains?
BIG4 Easts Beach's arcade and indoor facilities cover a wet day, and Kiama's small cinema is another option.
How far ahead should you book?
BIG4 Easts Beach books out well ahead for school holidays given its facilities reputation.
