Local Knowledge

G'day! Clare Valley might be famous for its Riesling, but families will fall head over heels for the Riesling Trail – that brilliant 35km cycling path where even your little ones can pedal safely from town to town, stopping for bakery treats along the way. The cooler months from April to October are absolutely magic here, with perfect weather for bike rides and those stunning autumn colours painting the vineyards gold and red. Here's an insider tip: grab breakfast at Seed Winehouse in Sevenhill before you start your ride – they've got a gorgeous lawn where the kids can run around, and the staff are always happy to fill up your water bottles for the trail ahead.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best family accommodation in Clare Valley?

Discovery Parks Clare, Green Acres Caravan & Camping Park, and Clare Valley Caravan Park are all top choices for families. They're perfectly positioned for exploring the Riesling Trail by bike, and the kids will love having space to run around after a day of adventures. Book ahead during school holidays, as Clare Valley's become quite the family favourite for its safe cycling and relaxed atmosphere.

Which is the best caravan park in Clare Valley?

Discovery Parks Clare consistently rates highest with families, though Green Acres Caravan & Camping Park and Clare Valley Caravan Park both offer excellent facilities too. All three are well-located for accessing the famous Riesling Trail. The choice often comes down to whether you prefer being closer to town or having more bushland surroundings – each park has its own charm for different family styles.

What are the best kids activities in Clare Valley?

The Riesling Trail is an absolute winner – 35km of safe, sealed cycling path perfect for little legs, connecting towns with bakeries and playgrounds along the way. Kids love the freedom of riding between stops. Visit during April to October for the best weather, and don't miss breakfast at Seed Winehouse in Sevenhill before hitting the trail. Pack a picnic and make a full day of it.

Which Clare Valley wineries are kid-friendly?

Seed Winehouse in Sevenhill is a fantastic family spot with delicious breakfast options the kids will actually eat. Many Clare Valley cellar doors have outdoor spaces where children can play while parents taste, though it's worth calling ahead to check facilities. The real family magic here isn't in the wineries though – it's cycling the Riesling Trail between towns and stopping for treats at local bakeries.

What children's activities are available in Clare Valley?

The Riesling Trail is purpose-built for families – a safe, sealed 35km cycling path connecting Clare's towns, perfect for children of all ages. Little ones can pedal at their own pace, stopping at playgrounds and bakeries along the way. The cooler months from April to October offer perfect riding weather, and autumn brings those spectacular vineyard colours that make every photo Instagram-worthy.

What are the best things to do in Clare Valley with kids?

Base yourselves at Discovery Parks Clare, Green Acres, or Clare Valley Caravan Park. Visit April to October for brilliant weather, and grab breakfast at Seed Winehouse before starting your ride. The autumn colours are absolutely stunning.

What things can you do in Clare SA with kids?

Stay at one of the top family parks like Discovery Parks Clare or Green Acres, and explore at your own pace. April to October brings cooler weather perfect for cycling, plus those gorgeous autumn vineyard colours the kids will remember forever.
