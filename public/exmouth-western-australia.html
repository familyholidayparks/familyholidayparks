If We Were Booking...

  • Facilities in town: We'd choose RAC Exmouth Cape Holiday Park for the two warm resort-style pools, games room and nightly movies, with town's shops and tour departures minutes away.
  • The outback station experience: We'd choose Bullara Station Stay, an hour south, for farm animals, campfire damper and the kind of station atmosphere kids remember for years. No pool, and it doesn't matter.
  • Our tip: Split the trip. A few nights at Bullara on the way in, then the rest in Exmouth for the reef. You get two completely different holidays, and the contrast is the magic.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Exmouth good for kids?
For water-loving families it's one of the best trips in Australia. Snorkelling straight off the beach at Turquoise Bay, turtles, and whale sharks in season. It suits school-aged kids best.
What age can kids snorkel Turquoise Bay?
Confident swimmers from around 8 handle the drift snorkel on calm days. Younger kids can paddle the sheltered bay end. Always check conditions, the current is the whole point of the drift.
When is whale shark season?
Roughly March to July. Tours run daily in season and take children, though minimum ages and prices vary by operator.
Which Exmouth park is best for families?
RAC Exmouth Cape has the best facilities in town, with two warm pools, a games room and nightly movies. Bullara Station, an hour south, is the memorable outback alternative.
What is Bullara Station like with kids?
A working station with animals, open space, campfire nights and a strong community feel. No pool and limited playground, and families rate it a trip highlight anyway.
How far ahead should you book?
For April to October, months ahead at minimum. Exmouth's beds are limited and the season is short, so accommodation is the first thing to lock in.
Is Coral Bay or Exmouth better for families?
Both sit on Ningaloo. Exmouth has more accommodation, services and Cape Range access; Coral Bay is smaller with reef right off the main beach. Many families do both.
What should families do away from the water?
Cape Range National Park's gorges, the Yardie Creek boat tour, the lighthouse lookout for whales in season, and Exmouth's playgrounds and skatepark in town.
Is the water safe for young children?
The sheltered corners at Turquoise Bay and town beach are calm in good weather. Watch current advice at the drift sections and swim within the sanctuary zones.
How remote is Exmouth really?
It's a 13-hour drive from Perth, groceries and fuel cost more, and phone coverage fades in the national park. Stock up and plan like it's remote, because it is.
