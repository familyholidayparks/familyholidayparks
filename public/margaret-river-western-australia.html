If We Were Booking...

  • Farm-stay experience: We'd choose Discovery Parks Margaret River for the daily 3:30pm animal feeding, the huge jumping pillow and the free school holiday activities. Kids structure their whole day around feeding time.
  • Nature and town access: We'd choose RAC Margaret River Nature Park for bush and bike trails, or Margaret River Tourist Park to walk into town for the bakery and playground.
  • Our tip: Do Mammoth Cave self-guided with the audio headsets rather than a scheduled tour. Kids move at their own pace, nobody shushes them, and it's the most family-friendly of the caves.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Margaret River good for kids or just wine?
Genuinely good for kids. Farm animal feeding, caves, calm river-mouth swimming and free school holiday programs at the parks. The wine is just what the parents do at lunch.
Which Margaret River holiday park is best for children?
Discovery Parks Margaret River. Daily animal feeding at 3:30pm with kangaroos and camels, a huge jumping pillow, go-karts and free holiday activities including craft sessions and treasure hunts.
Where can kids swim safely?
The river mouth at Prevelly and the sheltered bay at Gnarabup are the calm options. The open surf beaches have serious swell and rips, so treat them as spectator beaches with young kids.
Which cave is best for families?
Mammoth Cave. It's self-guided with audio headsets so families move at their own pace. Lake Cave is more spectacular but runs on scheduled guided tours.
Do the wineries welcome children?
Many do, and the ones with lawns, playgrounds or resident animals make family lunches easy. Ask at your park for the current family favourites.
How far is Margaret River from Perth?
Around three hours drive south. Most families make it a five-plus night trip rather than a weekend.
What are the best free family activities?
The animal feeding if you're staying at Discovery, the river walk in town, watching surfers at Surfers Point, and the beach at Gnarabup are all free.
What should families do when it rains?
The caves don't care about weather, the chocolate and cheese factories north of town are undercover, and the park games rooms fill the gaps.
Do any parks have pools?
Margaret River Tourist Park has an unheated pool. Most families rely on the river mouth and Gnarabup for swimming, or pick a park for its other facilities.
How far in advance should you book?
WA school holidays and the Easter period book out well ahead. Winter is quiet, green and still worth it for the caves and farm stays.
