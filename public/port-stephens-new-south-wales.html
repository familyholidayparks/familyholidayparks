If We Were Booking...

  • All-weather facilities: We'd choose BIG4 Ingenia Holidays Soldiers Point for the indoor heated pool, bowling alley and under-5s play area. It's the park that saves a rainy week.
  • Beach-first holiday: We'd choose Halifax Holiday Park, Fingal Bay Holiday Park or Shoal Bay Holiday Park if calm bay swimming across the road mattered more than on-site entertainment.
  • Our tip: Book a sandboarding tour on the Stockton dunes early in your stay. If the kids love it, and they will, you can go back. Quad and camel tours on the dunes book out in school holidays.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Port Stephens good for families?
Yes. The bay beaches are calm enough for toddlers, dolphin cruises run year-round, and the Stockton sand dunes give older kids a genuine adventure day.
Which Port Stephens holiday park is best for young children?
BIG4 Ingenia Soldiers Point has an indoor heated pool, a bowling alley and a dedicated under-5s play area, making it the strongest all-weather pick for young families.
Which beaches are calmest for little kids?
Shoal Bay, Nelson Bay foreshore and Little Beach are flat-water bay swimming. Fingal Bay is gentle surf and patrolled in season.
Are the parks close to the dolphin cruises?
Yes, cruises leave from Nelson Bay marina, about 5 to 15 minutes drive from most parks. Morning cruises usually have the calmest water.
When can you see whales in Port Stephens?
May to November, with the peak from June to October. Several bay cruises switch to whale watching in season.
What are the Stockton sand dunes and are they worth it?
The Worimi Conservation Lands hold the largest moving sand dunes in the southern hemisphere, about 30 minutes from Nelson Bay. Sandboarding tours are usually the highlight of a family trip.
What are the best free family activities?
Swimming at the bay beaches, the Tomaree Head summit walk, Fly Point snorkelling and the foreshore playgrounds at Nelson Bay and Shoal Bay.
Do the parks have pools?
Soldiers Point and Fingal Bay do. Halifax and Shoal Bay skip the pool and rely on their beach positions instead, so pick based on how your family swims.
How far in advance should you book?
School holidays book out months ahead, especially at the beachfront parks. Summer weekends fill fast too because Port Stephens is an easy drive from Sydney and Newcastle.
What should families do when it rains?
The indoor pool and bowling at Soldiers Point, Irukandji Shark and Ray Encounters at Anna Bay, and the cinema at Nelson Bay cover wet days.
