Frequently Asked Questions

Is Albany good for families?
Yes, BIG4 Emu Beach and BIG4 Middleton Beach both offer strong facilities, and the town's dramatic coastal scenery adds genuine adventure.
Which Albany park is best for kids?
BIG4 Emu Beach Holiday Park, with two jumping pillows, a nature playground, mini golf and a splash water park with slides.
What are the Gap and Natural Bridge?
Dramatic granite rock formations along Albany's coast, free to visit and genuinely spectacular for families.
Can you see whales from Albany?
Yes, whale watching cruises operate from the harbour during the migration season.
Is the swimming safe for young children?
BIG4 Middleton Beach offers netted swimming for extra safety; check conditions at other beaches given the more exposed Southern Ocean coastline.
What is King River Palms known for?
Free canoes and kayaking on the river, a nature-based, hands-on activity style rather than resort facilities.
What is the National Anzac Centre?
A museum in Albany telling the story of the town's significant role as the departure point for WWI troops, engaging for older children.
What are the best free family activities?
The Gap and Natural Bridge rock formations, and Middleton Beach's foreshore.
When is the best time to visit?
Spring through autumn for the mildest weather, or whale season for an additional wildlife activity.
What should families do when it rains?
The National Anzac Centre offers a good indoor option, and both main parks have covered games facilities.
