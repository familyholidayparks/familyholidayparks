Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best family holiday parks in Perth, WA?
Banksia Tourist Park, Discovery Holiday Park, and Perth Caravan Park & Camp Site offer excellent family facilities across Perth's key locations. Discovery Parks - Swan Valley suits families wanting vineyard region access. Each provides playgrounds, clean amenities, and kid-friendly atmospheres. Compare their proximity to beaches and attractions based on your planned activities to choose the best fit for your family's needs.
Which caravan park in Perth is best for kids?
Banksia Tourist Park excels for families with dedicated playground areas, shallow pools, and spacious grounds. Discovery Holiday Park also features family-oriented amenities and activities. Both parks provide safe environments where children can play while parents relax. Check current facility updates before booking, as offerings vary seasonally.
Are there holiday parks with pools near Perth?
Both Banksia Tourist Park and Discovery Holiday Park feature swimming pools suitable for families. These facilities provide excellent cool-down options during Perth's hot summers (December-February). Pools vary in depth and temperature, so confirm specifications when booking if you have young children requiring shallow water areas.
Which holiday parks near Perth are close to beaches?
Perth Caravan Park & Camp Site offers proximity to Cottesloe and City beaches, ideal for beach-loving families. Discovery Holiday Park provides reasonable beach access. Both locations let families enjoy swimming, surfing lessons, and beach picnics without lengthy drives from accommodation, maximizing holiday time.
Where can I find powered sites in Perth holiday parks?
All four parks—Banksia Tourist Park, Discovery Holiday Park, Perth Caravan Park & Camp Site, and Discovery Parks - Swan Valley—offer powered caravan and RV sites with full hookups. Availability varies seasonally, particularly during school holidays. Book early for guaranteed powered site access during peak family travel periods.
What cabin options are available in Perth holiday parks?
Banksia Tourist Park and Discovery Holiday Park offer self-contained cabins ranging from basic to premium specifications. Options accommodate 2-8 people with varying amenities. Perth Caravan Park & Camp Site and Discovery Parks - Swan Valley also provide cabin accommodation. Compare bedding configurations and kitchen facilities before booking based on your family size.
Are Perth holiday parks pet-friendly?
Banksia Tourist Park welcomes pets in designated areas with specific size restrictions. Discovery Holiday Park and Perth Caravan Park & Camp Site also accommodate pets, though policies vary. Confirm pet policies, any additional fees, and designated pet exercise areas when booking. Some restrictions apply during peak periods.
What's the best value holiday park in Perth?
Perth Caravan Park & Camp Site typically offers competitive rates, especially for powered sites and basic cabins. Discovery Parks - Swan Valley provides excellent value for families seeking quieter, regional experiences. Off-peak season visits (March-May, September-November) significantly reduce costs across all parks while maintaining good weather for family activities.
What's the best area to stay in Perth for families?
Cottesloe and City Beach areas suit families prioritizing beach access—stay at Perth Caravan Park & Camp Site. Swan Valley suits families interested in wine region exploration and quieter surroundings—Discovery Parks - Swan Valley is ideal. Perth's central locations near Banksia Tourist Park offer attraction access without coastal travel.
What rainy day activities are near Perth holiday parks?
Perth's numerous indoor options include SEA LIFE Aquarium, Western Australian Museum, and Scitech Discovery Centre. Most parks have community halls and activity centers. Shopping centres like Westfield offer climate-controlled entertainment. Plan indoor activities during winter months (June-August) when rainfall peaks to ensure enjoyable rainy day backup options.
