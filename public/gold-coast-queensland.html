Local Knowledge

The Gold Coast offers something for every type of family. Younger children love the calm water at Tallebudgera Creek, while older kids are drawn to Dreamworld, Movie World, Wet'n'Wild and Sea World. Broadwater Parklands is one of the best free family attractions on the coast, with playgrounds, water play areas and plenty of space to run around. If you're staying during school holidays, book early as the most popular family holiday parks often sell out months in advance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Gold Coast good for families?
Yes. The Gold Coast is one of Australia's most family-friendly holiday destinations, combining beaches, theme parks, wildlife attractions, playgrounds and family holiday parks. There are activities for toddlers, primary-school children and teenagers alike.
Which Gold Coast holiday park has the best facilities for kids?
BIG4 Gold Coast Holiday Park is the standout choice for families thanks to its waterpark, resort pool, jumping pillow and easy access to nearby theme parks. It consistently receives one of the highest family scores on the Gold Coast.
What are the best things to do on the Gold Coast with kids?
Popular family activities include Dreamworld, Movie World, Wet'n'Wild, Sea World, Currumbin Wildlife Sanctuary, Tallebudgera Creek and Broadwater Parklands. Many families combine theme park days with beach and pool days at their holiday park.
Where should families stay on the Gold Coast?
Families wanting resort-style facilities often choose BIG4 Gold Coast Holiday Park or NRMA Treasure Island Holiday Resort. Families looking for calm water swimming frequently choose Tallebudgera Creek Tourist Park, while Broadwater Tourist Park is popular for its waterfront location and family-friendly facilities.
What is the best caravan park on the Gold Coast for families?
BIG4 Gold Coast Holiday Park ranks highest overall for families thanks to its waterpark, pool, playgrounds and proximity to major attractions. Tallebudgera Creek Tourist Park and Broadwater Tourist Park are also excellent options depending on whether you prefer creek access or waterfront camping.
Are there family caravan parks on the Gold Coast with pools?
Yes. BIG4 Gold Coast Holiday Park, Broadwater Tourist Park, Tallebudgera Creek Tourist Park, NRMA Treasure Island Holiday Resort, Main Beach Tourist Park, Kirra Beach Tourist Park and Nobby Beach Holiday Village all offer pool facilities.
What are the best beaches on the Gold Coast for families?
Tallebudgera Creek is one of the safest swimming spots for young children thanks to its calm water. Families also enjoy Broadwater Parklands, Kirra Beach, Main Beach and Burleigh Heads, depending on the age of their children and preferred activities.
