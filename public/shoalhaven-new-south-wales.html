Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best caravan park in the Shoalhaven for families?
Holiday Haven Culburra Beach consistently tops the list for families, with its beachfront location and excellent facilities. Holiday Haven Shoalhaven Heads is another cracker, offering river access and playgrounds. For something more secluded, Treehaven Tourist Park delivers a bush setting while still being close to beaches. Each park has its charm – beach lovers gravitate to Culburra, while fishing families adore Shoalhaven Heads.
How can I support Shoalhaven Kids in Need?
Shoalhaven Kids in Need is a wonderful local charity supporting disadvantaged children throughout the region. They accept donations of new toys, books, and sporting equipment year-round, with collection points at various local businesses in Nowra and surrounding towns. Many holiday parks run donation drives during school holidays, or families can contact the organization directly through their Facebook page to arrange donations or find out about current needs.
What are the best kids activities in the Shoalhaven?
The Shoalhaven delivers endless adventures for kids! Jervis Bay's crystal-clear waters are perfect for snorkelling and spotting dolphins, while Huskisson's calm beaches suit little ones. The Nowra Wildlife Park lets kids hand-feed kangaroos, and Coolangatta Estate offers family-friendly grounds for picnics. Rock pooling at Culburra Beach and kayaking the Shoalhaven River are absolute winners. Late spring offers ideal conditions before summer crowds arrive.
Where can I book kids parties in the Shoalhaven?
The Shoalhaven offers brilliant party venues for kids! Nowra's Trampoline Park and Flip Out are popular indoor options, while many holiday parks like Holiday Haven locations offer party packages with playground access and BBQ facilities. Beach parties at patrolled spots like Culburra or Shoalhaven Heads are quintessentially Aussie, and venues like Coolangatta Estate provide beautiful outdoor spaces. Local businesses in Huskisson and Nowra also offer mobile party services.
What are the best things to do in Nowra with kids?
Nowra punches above its weight for family fun! The Nowra Wildlife Park is a must-visit for close encounters with native animals, while the Shoalhaven River offers fantastic kayaking and fishing spots. Nowra Fresh Shopping Centre keeps kids entertained on rainy days, and the nearby Riverside Tourist Park provides great playground facilities. The Shoalhaven Zoo and historic Fleet Air Arm Museum make excellent half-day adventures for curious minds.
What things can we do in Nowra with kids this weekend?
This weekend in Nowra, check out the Growers Market on Saturday morning for fresh treats and local atmosphere. The Showground often hosts family events, while the Shoalhaven River foreshore is perfect for bike rides and picnics. If the weather's dodgy, head to the library's kids programs or Flip Out trampoline centre. Nowra Riverside Tourist Park makes an excellent base, and nearby beaches are just a 15-minute drive for spontaneous adventures.
