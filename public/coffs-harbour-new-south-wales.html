If We Were Booking...

  • Beachfront position: We'd choose BIG4 Park Beach Holiday Park for direct access to patrolled Park Beach and an easy walk to town.
  • Facilities and quiet: We'd choose Sapphire Beach Holiday Park for the two pools, games room and mini golf in a calmer setting.
  • Our tip: Do the Big Banana in the morning before the crowds build, and finish the afternoon with a walk out to Muttonbird Island. It's a short, flat walk with a genuine payoff, wide harbour views and the chance of dolphins below.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Coffs Harbour good for families?
Yes, it's a reliable, easy family stop with patrolled beaches, the Big Banana Fun Park and whale watching cruises in season.
Which Coffs Harbour park is best for kids?
BIG4 Park Beach Holiday Park for beachfront position, or Sapphire Beach Holiday Park for two pools and more on-site entertainment.
What is the Big Banana Fun Park?
An attraction park built around the giant banana landmark, with waterslides in summer, ice skating, a toboggan ride and mini golf.
When can you see whales from Coffs Harbour?
Roughly May to November, with cruises departing the marina during the migration season.
What is Muttonbird Island?
A small island at the end of the Coffs breakwater with an easy walking track, harbour views and genuine seabird nesting colonies.
Are the beaches safe for young children?
Park Beach and Jetty Beach are patrolled in season and suit family swimming well.
What are the best free family activities?
Muttonbird Island, the Botanic Garden, and the Sealy Lookout forest walks above town.
When is the best time to visit?
Whale season, May to November, adds an extra drawcard, though Coffs works as a family stop year-round given its position on the highway.
What should families do when it rains?
The Big Banana's indoor sections, the Coffs cinema and the North Coast Regional Botanic Garden's covered areas cover wet days.
How far is Coffs Harbour from Sydney and Brisbane?
Roughly halfway between the two, about 5.5 to 6 hours drive from either city, making it a natural overnight stop.
