If We Were Booking...

  • Walk to the beach: We'd choose Tweed Holiday Parks Kingscliff Beach, 200 metres from the sand, for a genuinely walkable family holiday with no car needed most days.
  • A pool for the hot days: We'd choose Ingenia Holidays Kingscliff for the pool, accepting the slightly longer walk to the beach.
  • Our tip: Explore the rock pools at the Kingscliff headland at low tide, then walk the beach path south towards Cabarita for a change of scenery. It's flat, easy and doubles as the day's exercise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kingscliff good for families?
Yes, particularly for families who want to walk everywhere. Both main parks sit close to the beach and the town centre.
Which Kingscliff park is best for young children?
Tweed Holiday Parks Kingscliff Beach for beach proximity, or Ingenia Holidays Kingscliff if a pool matters more than walking distance to the sand.
Is the beach safe for swimming?
Yes, it's patrolled in season and generally a gentle, family-friendly beach compared to some of the more exposed surf beaches nearby.
Do the parks have pools?
Only Ingenia Holidays Kingscliff has a pool. The Tweed Holiday Parks properties rely on beach access instead.
What is there to do beyond the beach?
Rock pool exploring at the Kingscliff headland, walking the coastal path towards Cabarita, and the town's cafes and Saturday markets.
How far is Kingscliff from the Gold Coast?
About 20 to 30 minutes south of the Gold Coast border, making it an easy day trip for theme parks if needed.
What are the best free family activities?
The beach, the headland rock pools, and the coastal walking path linking Kingscliff to Cabarita Beach.
When is the best time to visit?
School holidays if you want the town buzzing, or shoulder season for a quieter, still-warm stay.
What should families do when it rains?
Limited undercover options in Kingscliff itself; Tweed City shopping centre, 15 minutes away, has a cinema and food court.
How far ahead should you book?
The walkable parks book out for school holidays given the position, so plan ahead for peak periods.
