If We Were Booking...

  • Close to the attractions: We'd choose Alivio Tourist Park for the resort-style pool, water play area and multiple playgrounds, ten minutes from Questacon and the museums.
  • Space and pets: We'd choose Capital Country Holiday Park for the bigger sites, putt-putt and ball-game space, accepting the 20-minute drive into the attractions.
  • Our tip: Do Questacon first, not last. It sets the bar for the trip, the kids will want a second visit, and morning sessions beat the school-group rush. Almost everything else in Canberra is free, so the budget survives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Canberra actually fun for kids?
Genuinely, yes. Questacon alone justifies the trip, and the War Memorial, National Museum, National Zoo and lake foreshores fill the rest of a week, mostly for free.
Which Canberra holiday park is best for families?
Alivio Tourist Park for facilities and proximity, with a resort-style pool, water play area and multiple playgrounds ten minutes from the main attractions.
What is Questacon?
The national science and technology centre, packed with hands-on exhibits, a free-fall slide and live science shows. It's the best paid attraction in Canberra for kids, book mornings.
What can families do for free in Canberra?
The War Memorial, National Museum, National Gallery, Parliament House, the lake foreshores and playgrounds, and Floriade's flower festival each spring.
Where can kids swim in Canberra?
The park pools in summer, plus river swimming at Casuarina Sands and the Cotter, 25 minutes from the city. The lake itself is for boats and bikes rather than swimming.
How cold does Canberra get?
Winter mornings regularly sit below zero, and days stay cold. Pack proper warm gear from May to September, then enjoy the crisp blue-sky days.
When is the best time to visit?
March to April and October to November for mild weather, or September school holidays for Floriade. Summer works too, with pool afternoons built in.
How many days do you need?
Three full days covers Questacon, the War Memorial and the museums. Five lets you add the zoo, Cotter swims and Tidbinbilla Nature Reserve.
What should families do when it rains?
Canberra is the best wet-weather city in Australia. Every major attraction is indoors, so rain barely changes the plan.
Is Canberra expensive?
The attractions being free makes it one of the cheapest family trips going. Park sites and cabins price reasonably outside event weekends and Floriade.
