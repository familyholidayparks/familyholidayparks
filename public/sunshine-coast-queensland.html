If We Were Booking...

  • Waterslides and activities: We'd choose BIG4 Caloundra or Ingenia Holidays Rivershore for the heated pools, waterslides and daily kids programs. Caloundra also puts you 300 metres from calm swimming at Golden Beach.
  • Classic beachfront: We'd choose Dicky Beach Family Holiday Park or Alex Beach Cabins and Tourist Park if we wanted to fall out of the van and onto the sand, with the skatepark out the front of Dicky Beach a bonus for older kids.
  • Our tip: Golden Beach at Caloundra is the best young-kid swimming on the coast. Calm, shallow and protected. If your kids are under 8, base yourself at that end and day-trip north.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Sunshine Coast good for families?
Yes, it's one of Australia's most reliable family destinations. Calm swimming at Golden Beach, patrolled surf beaches, Australia Zoo, Sea Life Mooloolaba and a strong line-up of holiday parks with pools and waterslides.
Which Sunshine Coast holiday park has the best waterslides?
BIG4 Caloundra has a heated pool complex with slides suitable for all ages, and Ingenia Holidays Rivershore runs two waterslides and a splash pool.
What is the best area of the Sunshine Coast for young children?
Caloundra. Golden Beach is calm and shallow, the foreshore has playgrounds and BBQs, and BIG4 Caloundra is a short walk away.
Are there beachfront caravan parks on the Sunshine Coast?
Yes. Dicky Beach Family Holiday Park sits 200 metres from the sand and Alex Beach Cabins is 400 metres from Alexandra Headland Beach.
How far is Australia Zoo from the holiday parks?
About 20 minutes drive from Caloundra and 30 to 40 minutes from Mooloolaba and Maroochydore. Allow a full day.
What are the best free family activities on the Sunshine Coast?
The Kings Beach saltwater pool, Golden Beach foreshore playgrounds, the Coastal Pathway for bikes and scooters, and Mooloolaba's beachfront parks are all free.
How far in advance should you book?
Beachfront parks book out months ahead for school holidays, especially Christmas. Book as early as your plans allow.
Which parks suit caravans with big rigs?
BIG4 Maroochy River has spacious riverside sites and most of the larger parks handle big rigs. Call ahead for slab sites over 10 metres.
What should families do when it rains?
Sea Life Mooloolaba, the cinema at Maroochydore, indoor play centres at Warana and Kawana, and the games rooms at the bigger parks cover most wet days.
Is the Sunshine Coast cheaper than Noosa?
Generally yes. Comparable powered sites and cabins run noticeably cheaper, and there are more parks to choose from, which helps in peak periods.
