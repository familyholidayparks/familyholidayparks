Why Families Love Sunshine Coast

  • Safe beaches for swimming and sand play
  • Australia Zoo and family attractions nearby
  • Holiday parks with pools, waterslides and jumping pillows
  • Great weather year-round
  • Beaches, hinterland and wildlife all in one destination

Local Knowledge

The Sunshine Coast is one of Australia's best family holiday destinations because everything is close together. Families can spend the morning at Australia Zoo, the afternoon at the beach and be back at their holiday park pool before dinner. Mooloolaba, Caloundra and Coolum all offer excellent family beaches, while the Glass House Mountains and hinterland provide easy day trips when you want a break from the sand.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Sunshine Coast good for families?

Yes. The Sunshine Coast combines safe beaches, family attractions, wildlife experiences and some of Australia's best family holiday parks. It is ideal for toddlers, primary-school children and teenagers alike.

Which Sunshine Coast holiday park has the best facilities for kids?

BIG4 Caloundra ranks highest thanks to its waterslides, heated pool, jumping pillow and daily activities. Ingenia Holidays Rivershore is another standout with resort-style facilities and family entertainment.

What are the best things to do on the Sunshine Coast with kids?

Popular family activities include Australia Zoo, SEA LIFE Sunshine Coast, Aussie World, Kings Beach, Mooloolaba Beach, Currimundi Lake and the Glass House Mountains.

Where should families stay on the Sunshine Coast?

Families wanting the best facilities generally choose BIG4 Caloundra or Ingenia Holidays Rivershore. Families wanting a beachside holiday often choose Alex Beach, Dicky Beach or Coolum Beach Holiday Park.

Are there family caravan parks on the Sunshine Coast with pools?

Yes. BIG4 Caloundra, Ingenia Holidays Rivershore, Alex Beach Cabins, BIG4 Maroochy River, Dicky Beach and Mudjimba Beach Holiday Park all offer pool facilities.

What is the best caravan park on the Sunshine Coast for families?

BIG4 Caloundra is the highest-ranked family caravan park thanks to its facilities, location and consistently strong family reviews. Ingenia Holidays Rivershore and Alex Beach Cabins are also excellent choices.

What are the best beaches on the Sunshine Coast for families?

Kings Beach, Golden Beach, Mooloolaba Beach, Cotton Tree Beach and Currimundi Lake are among the most popular family-friendly beaches thanks to calmer water, playgrounds and nearby facilities.
