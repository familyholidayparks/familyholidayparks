If We Were Booking...

  • Kids entertained on-site: We'd choose Anchor Belle Holiday Park for the heated indoor pool, games room and scavenger hunts. It's the park families return to for 15 years straight.
  • Beach and nature: We'd choose NRMA Phillip Island Beachfront or BIG4 Ingenia Phillip Island for beach access, kids clubs in holidays and a quieter pace between penguin and koala outings.
  • Our tip: Book the Penguin Parade for your first night, not your last. If weather turns it into a washout, you've got spare nights to rebook, and the kids will want to go twice anyway.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Phillip Island good for families?
Yes, it's one of Victoria's best family destinations. Penguins every night of the year, koalas at the conservation reserve, calm northern beaches and parks built around kids.
Which Phillip Island holiday park is best for young kids?
Anchor Belle Holiday Park. The indoor heated pool is genuinely warm, there's a toddler pool, and the games room and organised activities keep under-12s busy whatever the weather.
How do Penguin Parade tickets work?
Book online ahead, especially for school holidays and weekends. Penguins arrive after sunset year-round, so the start time shifts with the season.
Which beaches are safe for young children?
The north-facing beaches near Cowes, including Childrens Beach and Chicken Beach, are calm and shallow. Avoid the ocean beaches like Woolamai with young kids, the rips are serious.
What else is there beyond the penguins?
The Koala Conservation Reserve, the Nobbies boardwalk with summer seal watching, Churchill Island's heritage farm with daily animal demonstrations, and A Maze'N Things for a rainy day.
How far is Phillip Island from Melbourne?
About 90 minutes to two hours drive from Melbourne via the bridge at San Remo. No ferry required.
What should families pack that they usually forget?
Warm layers, even in summer. The Penguin Parade stands are exposed and cold after dark, and island weather changes fast.
What are the best free family activities?
The beaches at Cowes, the Nobbies boardwalk, watching the pelican feeding at San Remo at noon, and the rhyll wetlands walks are all free.
How far in advance should you book a park?
Summer school holidays book out months ahead, and Anchor Belle's returning families snap up peak weeks early. Winter is quiet and easy.
What should families do when it rains?
A Maze'N Things, the games rooms at Anchor Belle and BIG4 Ingenia, and the Phillip Island Chocolate Factory cover most wet days.
