If We Were Booking...

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Denmark good for families?
Yes, BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach has excellent facilities, and nearby Greens Pool is among the best family swimming spots in WA.
Which Denmark park is best for kids?
BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach, with a massive playground featuring a flying fox and bouncy pillows, plus regular kangaroo encounters.
What is Greens Pool?
A naturally sheltered swimming spot in William Bay National Park, with calm, crystal-clear water protected by granite boulders, a short drive from Denmark.
Will kids see kangaroos in Denmark?
Sightings around BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach are common, though as with all wildlife, not guaranteed on every visit.
Is the inlet or ocean better for young children?
The Wilson Inlet offers calmer water than the ocean beaches, generally better suited to younger swimmers.
What is Elephant Rocks?
A striking rock formation near Greens Pool in William Bay National Park, another excellent, sheltered family swimming spot.
What are the best free family activities?
Greens Pool and Elephant Rocks, both free to visit and genuinely spectacular.
When is the best time to visit?
Spring through autumn for the mildest weather, though the region can be cooler than other WA coastal destinations year-round.
What should families do when it rains?
BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach's covered play areas offer some shelter, and the town's cafes provide a relaxed indoor option.
How far is Denmark from Albany?
About 30 to 40 minutes drive, making it easy to combine both destinations in one trip.
