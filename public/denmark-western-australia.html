The Denmark Holiday Park Scene

Denmark's holiday parks sit in a genuinely special pocket of WA's south coast, where the Denmark River meets the Southern Ocean. What sets this area apart is the sheer variety packed into a small geographic footprint – you've got beachfront options like BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach Holiday Park literally metres from the sand, riverside parks such as Denmark Rivermouth Caravan Park where you can launch a kayak straight from your site, and bush-based options like Denmark Forest Retreat and Riverbend Chalets tucked into the native forest. The landscape here actually delivers on what the brochures promise: proper old-growth karri and marri trees, walking tracks that lead somewhere worth going, and genuine tidal river access. Most parks are small to mid-sized operations, which means you avoid the carpark-style sprawl you get in some destinations while still having decent facilities.

You'll see the same mix of people every season – caravanners doing the south coast loop in their 20-footers, families with young kids during school holidays (particularly January and winter breaks, which book out months ahead), couples in motorhomes wanting to base themselves for a week, and genuinely surprising numbers of repeat visitors who've been coming to the same park for a decade or more. The school holiday peaks are real enough that experienced travellers book by April if they want January, but the shoulder seasons remain pretty accessible. Denmark attracts families specifically because the river is safe for young swimmers, the beaches are protected and patrolled, and there's legitimate activity nearby – fishing, kayaking, wine region drives – without feeling like you need to fill every minute.

What brings people back year after year is that Denmark works equally well for doing nothing and for staying active. You can sit under a karri tree at your site with a book for three days, or you can kayak the river, walk the headland trails, hit William Bay for snorkelling, and drive into town for decent coffee. The parks themselves tend toward the practical rather than flash – you get what you need without paying resort prices – and the river and beach access is legitimately exceptional. Unlike destinations that rely entirely on artificial attractions or theme parks, Denmark's appeal is anchored in actual landscape and water access that doesn't get old.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best family holiday parks in Denmark, WA?
BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach and Denmark Rivermouth Caravan Park are top choices for families. BIG4 offers comprehensive facilities including pools and activities, while Rivermouth provides excellent amenities near the river. Both parks have excellent reviews from families and offer diverse accommodation options. Riverbend Chalets and Denmark Forest Retreat also cater well to families, with quieter settings if you prefer a more relaxed atmosphere away from busier holiday park crowds.
Which caravan park in Denmark is best for kids?
BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach Holiday Park ranks highest for kids, featuring swimming pools, playground facilities, and organized activities during peak seasons. The beachfront location means endless entertainment, while the park's infrastructure supports families well. Denmark Rivermouth Caravan Park also scores highly with families, offering safe grounds and proximity to river activities suitable for children of all ages.
Are there holiday parks with pools near Denmark?
BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach Holiday Park features swimming pools on-site, making it ideal if pool access is important. Several parks offer water-adjacent locations where kids can enjoy river or beach swimming. Pool availability varies seasonally, so confirm directly with parks during off-peak periods. Most parks compensate with beach or river access, providing natural swimming alternatives during warmer months.
Which holiday parks are closest to Denmark beach?
BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach Holiday Park is literally beachfront on Ocean Beach, offering direct sand access from the park. Denmark Rivermouth Caravan Park sits near the river mouth where it meets the ocean. Both provide immediate beach access without needing car transfers, ideal for families with young children wanting easy beach visits throughout their stay.
What caravan parks in Denmark have powered sites?
All four parks—BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach, Denmark Rivermouth, Riverbend Chalets, and Denmark Forest Retreat—offer powered caravan sites. Availability and pricing vary, so book ahead during school holidays. Most parks provide 15-amp or 10-amp power connections. Confirm specific amperage and site sizes when booking, as requirements differ for larger caravans or those with multiple appliances.
Where can we find cabins in Denmark?
Riverbend Chalets & Caravan Park specializes in self-contained chalets, perfect for families preferring cabin accommodation. BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach offers various cabin styles from budget to premium. Denmark Forest Retreat provides cabin options in a peaceful forest setting. Each offers different price points and amenities, so compare facilities like kitchens, bathrooms, and heating when deciding which suits your family's needs.
Are there pet-friendly holiday parks in Denmark?
Most Denmark parks accommodate pets, but policies vary significantly. BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach and Denmark Rivermouth Caravan Park generally allow pets with restrictions on certain areas. Confirm pet policies, sizes, and breed restrictions directly before booking. Some parks charge additional fees. If you're traveling with pets, clarify designated pet areas and whether they're permitted in cabins versus caravan sites only.
What's the best value holiday park in Denmark?
Denmark Rivermouth Caravan Park and Denmark Forest Retreat typically offer competitive pricing compared to BIG4. Off-peak season visits provide significant savings across all parks. Compare nightly rates for your travel dates, as winter months (May-August) are considerably cheaper. Check what's included—some parks bundle activities or facilities—affecting overall value for your family's specific needs and interests.
What's the best area to stay in Denmark for families?
Ocean Beach precinct suits families wanting beach access and activities, making BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach ideal. Rivermouth area offers quieter, nature-focused experiences perfect for families seeking relaxation. Denmark Forest Retreat suits families preferring tranquility among trees. Consider your priorities: beachfront action, river activities, or forest seclusion, then match to park location accordingly for your family's holiday style.
What are things to do with kids in Denmark?
Beaches offer swimming, rock pools, and sand play. Wilson Inlet provides kayaking and wildlife spotting. Denmark Visitor Centre has informative displays. Local shops and cafes cater to families. Hiking trails suit various fitness levels. Check what's nearby your chosen park—BIG4's beach location differs from Forest Retreat's woodland setting. Many parks provide activity suggestions and local recommendations to help plan daily adventures suitable for your children's ages.
