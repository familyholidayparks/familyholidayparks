Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best family holiday parks in Airlie Beach?
Discovery Parks, Tasman Holiday Parks, and NRMA Airlie Beach Holiday Park are top choices for families. Each offers different amenities—Discovery Parks features modern facilities, Tasman provides beachside access, and NRMA offers reliable caravan park infrastructure. Compare their specific offerings like pools, playgrounds, and accommodation types to match your family's needs and budget.
Which caravan park in Airlie Beach is best for kids?
Tasman Holiday Parks stands out for families, offering direct beach access and shallow swimming areas perfect for children. Discovery Parks also caters well to kids with dedicated play areas and family-friendly facilities. Both parks provide safe environments where children can enjoy water activities and outdoor recreation without traveling far.
Do holiday parks near Airlie Beach have pools?
Yes, all three major parks offer swimming pools. Discovery Parks features modern heated pools ideal for year-round swimming. Tasman and NRMA parks also provide pool facilities, though Discovery Parks' pools are particularly suited to families with varying age groups. Always confirm current pool facilities when booking, as upgrades may be ongoing.
Which holiday parks in Airlie Beach are closest to the beach?
Tasman Holiday Parks offers the closest beach access with direct beachfront location. Discovery Parks and NRMA are nearby but require short walks to Main Beach. If beach proximity is essential for your family, Tasman is your best option, though all three parks place you within walking distance of Airlie Beach's main attractions.
What powered sites are available in Airlie Beach caravan parks?
All three parks offer powered caravan and RV sites with 15-amp or 20-amp connections. Discovery Parks and NRMA provide modern powered sites with full hookups available at some sites. Availability varies seasonally, so book early, especially during school holidays when powered sites fill quickly in Airlie Beach.
Are there cabin options at Airlie Beach holiday parks?
Yes, all three parks offer cabin accommodations ranging from budget to luxury. Discovery Parks provides modern self-contained cabins with ensuite bathrooms. Tasman and NRMA also offer cabin options from basic to premium. Cabins are ideal for families avoiding tent camping while enjoying caravan park facilities and beach proximity.
Which Airlie Beach holiday parks are pet-friendly?
All three parks accept pets with restrictions. Discovery Parks allows dogs in designated areas with pet fees. Tasman and NRMA have pet policies requiring advance notification. Confirm specific pet policies—size limits and designated areas vary between parks. Plan accordingly if bringing family pets to ensure your accommodation meets their requirements.
What's the best value holiday park in Airlie Beach?
NRMA Airlie Beach Holiday Park typically offers competitive pricing, especially for members. Discovery Parks and Tasman provide good value with different pricing tiers. Compare nightly rates, included amenities, and off-season discounts. Family packages and longer stays often provide better value across all three parks during quieter periods.
What's the best area to stay in Airlie Beach for families?
Beachfront areas near Main Beach are ideal for families, making Tasman Holiday Parks particularly convenient. Central Airlie Beach locations offer easy access to restaurants, shops, and the beach. Discovery Parks and NRMA are well-positioned for exploring the town. Choose based on whether you prioritize direct beach access or proximity to Airlie Beach's commercial areas.
What rainy day activities are available near Airlie Beach holiday parks?
Airlie Beach offers indoor attractions including the Airlie Beach Lagoon (weather-protected swimming), arcade centers, and shopping precincts. Cinemas and cafes provide refuge during wet weather. Most parks have indoor facilities and entertainment. The nearby Bowen area offers museums and galleries. Plan flexibility into your itinerary, as tropical weather can impact outdoor activities.
