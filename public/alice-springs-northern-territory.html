If We Were Booking...

  • The oasis: We'd choose Discovery Parks Alice Springs without hesitation, waterslides, splash park, kids club, movie nights and astronomy sessions make it the standout family park in Central Australia.
  • Simple stopover: We'd choose Alice Springs Tourist Park for a secure, central base with a clean saltwater pool if we were passing through on the way to Uluru or the Top End.
  • Our tip: Pack swimmers for the West Macs. Ellery Creek Big Hole is a genuine desert waterhole swim 90 minutes from town, and it's the thing nobody expects from the Red Centre. The water is cold even in summer, which is exactly the point.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Alice Springs good for families?
Better than most expect. A standout holiday park with waterslides, waterhole swimming in the West Macs, joey cuddles at the Kangaroo Sanctuary and desert skies made for stargazing.
Which Alice Springs park is best for kids?
Discovery Parks Alice Springs, by a distance. Multiple pools with waterslides, a splash park, two jumping pillows, kids club, movie nights and astronomy sessions.
Can you swim in Alice Springs?
At the park pools year-round, and at West MacDonnell waterholes like Ellery Creek Big Hole when water levels allow. The waterhole water is cold even in summer.
Is Uluru a day trip from Alice?
No, it's 4.5 hours each way. Treat Uluru as its own two-to-three-night trip and give Alice and the West Macs their own time.
What is the Kangaroo Sanctuary?
The rescue sanctuary from the TV series Kangaroo Dundee, running guided afternoon tours where kids meet and sometimes cuddle rescued joeys. Book well ahead.
What are the West MacDonnell Ranges?
A chain of gorges and waterholes stretching west from town: Simpsons Gap, Standley Chasm, Ellery Creek and Ormiston Gorge. It's the best family day-trip country in the Centre.
When is the best time to visit?
April to September for mild days and cold, clear nights. Summer runs into the 40s, which makes the park pools essential and midday outings unwise.
How cold do winter nights get?
Regularly near zero. Pack proper warm layers for evenings and early starts, then shed them by mid-morning.
What should families do in the heat of the day?
Pool and waterslides at the park, the Desert Park's nocturnal house, the Megafauna Central museum in town, and save the gorges for early morning.
Is Alice Springs safe for families?
The parks are secure and family-focused, and the usual travel common sense applies in town. Discovery and the Tourist Park are both gated with good lighting.
