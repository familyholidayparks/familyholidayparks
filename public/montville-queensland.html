Local Knowledge

G'day fellow adventurers! Montville and the surrounding Blackall Range offer families that perfect sweet spot – you're just 30 minutes from the Coast's beaches, yet nestled in cool, misty rainforest where the kids can spot pademelon wallabies at dusk and you'll actually need a jumper year-round. Winter (June to August) is magic up here when the fog rolls through the village and you can cosy up by a fire, though spring brings the most spectacular garden displays if you're planning to visit the famous Flame Tree cottages and Kondalilla Falls. Here's an insider tip: grab fresh scones from the Montville Deli before 10am on weekends (they sell out fast!), then take them down to Lake Baroon for a proper picnic with those jaw-dropping valley views.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best kids activities in Montville?

Montville's a ripper for little ones! The rainforest walks are perfect for spotting wildlife – keep your eyes peeled for pademelons at dusk. The village itself has loads of lolly shops and galleries the kids will love, plus Kondalilla Falls is just down the road for a proper adventure. Base yourself at nearby Coolum Beach Holiday Park or Cotton Tree Holiday Park on the coast, then day trip up to the mountains when you need a break from the beach.

Is there a kids dentist in Montville?

Montville's a tiny village, so you won't find a dedicated kids dentist up on the range. For paediatric dental care, you're best heading down to Caloundra or Maroochydore on the coast – about a 30-minute drive. Most family-friendly dentists along the Sunshine Coast are excellent with little ones. If you're staying at BIG4 Caloundra or Kookaburra Park, there are plenty of family dental practices within a short drive of your accommodation.

Where is the kids park in Montville?

Montville itself doesn't have a traditional playground – it's more about nature-based activities and village exploring. For proper play equipment, head to Lake Baroon foreshore (stunning views!) or drop down to the coastal parks. BIG4 Caloundra, Alex Beach Cabins and Tourist Park, and Cotton Tree Holiday Park all have fantastic playgrounds as part of their facilities, plus you'll be close to Caloundra's excellent beachfront playgrounds and water parks.

What are the best things to do in Montville with kids?

Start with the rainforest walks – they're easy enough for little legs and brilliant for wildlife spotting. The main street's packed with fudge shops and galleries where kids can watch sweets being made. Kondalilla Falls is a must-do for a waterfall swim and picnic. Winter months are magical when the mist rolls in! Stay at Coolum Beach Holiday Park or BIG4 Caloundra to combine mountain adventures with beach days – you're only 30 minutes between the two.

What are kid friendly things to do in PEI?

This one seems off-track for the Sunshine Coast region! If you're after family activities around Montville and the Blackall Range, there's heaps on offer – rainforest walks, waterfall swimming at Kondalilla, and village exploring. Check out Kookaburra Park, Cotton Tree Holiday Park, or Coolum Beach Holiday Park as your base for exploring both the hinterland and gorgeous coastal beaches. The combination of mountains and ocean makes this area absolutely unbeatable for families.
