How it all works

How do Family Holiday Parks scores work?
Every park is scored across 7 criteria: entertainment, nature, cleanliness, value, site size, sentiment and location. Each criterion is weighted based on what matters most to families travelling with kids. Parks are ranked Gold, Silver or Bronze within each location.
Are the rankings sponsored?
No. Parks cannot pay to rank higher. Every ranking is based purely on our scoring methodology applied consistently to all parks. We don't accept payment for placement, reviews or rankings.
How many parks have you scored?
We've scored 500+ holiday parks across 100+ locations in Australia. We analyse hundreds of real Google reviews per park, weighted towards recent family experiences.
How do you choose the best family holiday parks?
We analyse real family reviews and score each park on criteria that matter to families — kids' facilities, water access, cleanliness, value and location. We don't rely on star ratings alone. A park with 4.2 stars and 800 reviews from families will often beat a 4.5-star park with mostly grey nomad reviews.
Can parks pay to rank higher?
No. Our scoring system is completely independent. We built this because we were frustrated by paid placement and misleading listings. The score a park gets is the score it earns.
What is the Family Verified badge?
When we visit a park in person with our kids, we add a Family Verified badge to that listing. It means a real family has stayed there and confirmed the facilities, photos and experience match what's advertised.
