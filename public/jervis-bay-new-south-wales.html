If We Were Booking...

  • Beachfront position: We'd choose Holiday Haven Huskisson Beach to wake up 100 metres from the sand and walk to town, or Holiday Haven White Sands one street back.
  • Pool and facilities: We'd choose Tasman Holiday Parks Jervis Bay for the pool with slide and spa, pedal carts and playground, accepting a short drive to the main beaches.
  • Our tip: Skip the Hyams Beach parking scramble. The sand at Murrays Beach, Green Patch and Huskisson is the same blinding white, and you'll actually get a park. Book Booderee campsites and cruises ahead in summer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Jervis Bay good for young children?
Exceptionally. The bay's water is calm, clear and shallow, more like a giant swimming pool than open ocean, and Huskisson Beach and Callala Bay suit toddlers well.
Which Jervis Bay holiday park is closest to the beach?
Holiday Haven Huskisson Beach is about 100 metres from the sand, with Holiday Haven White Sands a few minutes walk from Huskisson Beach.
Which park has the best pool?
Tasman Holiday Parks Jervis Bay, with a pool, waterslide and spa plus a playground beside it. It's the pick if your kids want a pool as well as the beach.
Is Hyams Beach worth visiting?
The sand is genuinely white, but parking is extremely limited in summer. Green Patch, Murrays Beach and Huskisson offer the same white sand with easier access.
When can you see dolphins and whales?
Dolphins live in the bay year-round and cruises from Huskisson see them most days. Whales pass through from roughly May to November.
What is Booderee National Park and is it worth the entry fee?
It's the national park covering the southern side of the bay, home to Green Patch, Murrays Beach and Cave Beach. For a family it's easily worth a day or two.
What are the best free family activities?
Swimming at Huskisson and Callala, the White Sands walk between Huskisson and Vincentia, spotting stingrays at the wharf, and the playgrounds along the Huskisson foreshore.
How far in advance should you book?
Summer school holidays book out many months ahead, and Booderee's campgrounds run a ballot for peak season. Shoulder seasons are far easier and the water is still swimmable into autumn.
What should families do when it rains?
The Jervis Bay Maritime Museum at Huskisson, the games rooms at the bigger parks, and Nowra's indoor play centres 25 minutes away cover wet days.
Is Jervis Bay suitable for caravans?
Yes, the Holiday Haven and Tasman parks all take vans. Sites at the beachfront parks are tighter, so ask for a larger site when booking if you're over 8 metres.
