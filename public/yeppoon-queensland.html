Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best family holiday parks in Yeppoon?
Ingenia Holidays Kinka Beach, Discovery Parks Coolwaters, NRMA Capricorn, and Poinciana Tourist Park all offer excellent family facilities. Ingenia Holidays and Discovery Parks feature water parks with pools, while NRMA Capricorn provides beachfront access. Poinciana offers good value with basic amenities. Compare facilities like playgrounds, pools, and proximity to beaches when choosing based on your family's needs.
Which caravan park in Yeppoon is best for kids?
Discovery Parks Coolwaters stands out with its waterpark including slides and pools, ideal for children. Ingenia Holidays Kinka Beach also offers excellent kids' facilities with pools and activities. NRMA Capricorn provides beach access and family programs during peak seasons. Check ahead for school holiday entertainment programs at these parks.
Which holiday parks in Yeppoon have pools?
Discovery Parks Coolwaters features a dedicated waterpark with multiple pools and slides. Ingenia Holidays Kinka Beach offers resort-style pools. NRMA Capricorn has a pool facility. Poinciana Tourist Park has a smaller pool. Pool sizes and water play facilities vary, so confirm current features when booking for your preferred activities.
What holiday parks are near the beach in Yeppoon?
NRMA Capricorn Yeppoon Holiday Park offers direct beachfront access on Keppel Bay. Ingenia Holidays Kinka Beach is beachside at Kinka Beach. Discovery Parks Coolwaters is within walking distance of beaches. Poinciana Tourist Park is also close to local beaches. Beachfront or beach-adjacent parks provide easier access for swimming and water activities.
Do Yeppoon holiday parks have powered sites?
All four parks—Ingenia Holidays, Discovery Parks, NRMA Capricorn, and Poinciana—offer powered caravan and motorhome sites. Powered sites typically include 10-15 amp power connections. NRMA and Ingenia Holidays usually have more powered options. Confirm power availability and amperage when booking, especially during peak holiday periods.
What cabin options are available in Yeppoon holiday parks?
All four parks offer cabin accommodations. Ingenia Holidays and Discovery Parks provide modern, family-friendly cabins with ensuite facilities. NRMA Capricorn offers beachfront cabins. Poinciana has budget-friendly cabin options. Cabin types range from basic to luxurious, so check specific amenities like kitchens, air-conditioning, and bedroom configurations for your family size.
Are any Yeppoon holiday parks pet friendly?
Most Yeppoon parks accommodate pets, but policies vary. NRMA Capricorn and Poinciana Tourist Park typically allow pets on leads in designated areas. Ingenia Holidays and Discovery Parks have specific pet-friendly cabins or sites. Always confirm pet policies, breed restrictions, and associated fees before booking to avoid disappointment.
What's the best value holiday park in Yeppoon?
Poinciana Tourist Park generally offers competitive pricing with basic but clean facilities. Off-peak season rates at NRMA Capricorn and Discovery Parks provide good value. Compare nightly rates, included facilities, and proximity to attractions. Budget parks save money but may lack water parks and activities available at premium parks like Ingenia Holidays.
What's the best area to stay in Yeppoon for families?
Beachfront locations at NRMA Capricorn and Ingenia Holidays Kinka Beach offer easy beach access and water activities. Discovery Parks Coolwaters provides pool facilities and is centrally located. The Kinka Beach area suits families wanting beach proximity, while central Yeppoon parks offer closer access to shops and restaurants. Consider your priority: beach access or town convenience.
What are the best rainy day activities in Yeppoon?
Discovery Parks Coolwaters waterpark operates in rain. Capricorn Coast Wildlife Park offers covered areas. Yeppoon Library provides indoor entertainment. Rockhampton (30 minutes away) has museums and indoor attractions. Many parks offer indoor games rooms. Book cabin accommodation to ensure comfortable indoor space during wet weather breaks.
