If We Were Booking...

  • The full package: We'd choose NRMA Victor Harbor Beachfront, the waterpark, waterslide, pump track and games room make it the strongest family park on the south coast, with calm bay swimming across the road.
  • Whale season: We'd book June to September and check the Whale Centre's sighting log each morning. Southern right whales calve in Encounter Bay and the viewing is free from the headlands.
  • Our tip: Ride the horse tram over to Granite Island, then walk the island loop and look for the little penguins' burrows. Time the Cockle Train to Goolwa for a steam-hauled day and the kids get trams, trains and whales in one trip.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Victor Harbor good for families?
It's built for them. A horse-drawn tram, steam train, whales in winter, hand-feeding kangaroos at Urimbirra and one of South Australia's best-equipped family parks on the beachfront.
What facilities does the NRMA park have for kids?
A waterpark with waterslide, splash park, jumping pillow, pump track and games room, directly opposite the bay. It's the strongest family facility set on the south coast.
When can you see whales at Victor Harbor?
June to September, when southern right whales calve in Encounter Bay. The South Australian Whale Centre posts daily sightings and the headland viewing is free.
What is the Granite Island horse tram?
A horse-drawn tram that has crossed the causeway since 1894. Ride over, walk the island loop, and walk back across if the kids still have legs.
Is the swimming safe for young children?
Yes, the bay near the causeway is calm and shallow. It's protected water rather than surf, which suits toddlers and young swimmers well.
What is the Cockle Train?
A heritage railway running between Victor Harbor and Goolwa along the coast, steam-hauled on many services. It's an easy half-day that kids rate surprisingly highly.
How far is Victor Harbor from Adelaide?
About 80 minutes drive, which makes it South Australia's most popular family seaside weekend.
What are the best free family activities?
Whale watching from the headlands in season, the foreshore playgrounds, walking the Granite Island loop and the swimming itself.
What should families do when it rains?
The Whale Centre, the games room at the NRMA park and the cinema in town cover wet days, and the Cockle Train runs regardless.
How far in advance should you book?
School holidays book out well ahead, particularly summer. Winter whale season is quieter and cabins are easier to get.
