If We Were Booking...

  • Pool and play facilities: We'd choose Ingenia Holidays Hunter Valley for the two pools, jumping pillow and pedal karts, the strongest family setup in the region.
  • Space and camp kitchen: We'd choose Wine Country Tourist Park for generous sites and a well-equipped camp kitchen, good for larger family groups.
  • Our tip: Book a morning at Hunter Valley Gardens for the kids, then split the afternoon, one parent takes older kids to TreeTops Adventure Park while the other enjoys a cellar door with younger ones back at the pool.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Hunter Valley good for families?
Yes, if you balance the wine touring with kid-focused activities. The main family park has strong pool facilities, and attractions like Hunter Valley Gardens cater well to children.
Which Hunter Valley park is best for families?
Ingenia Holidays Hunter Valley, with two pools including a heated option, a spa, jumping pillow and pedal karts.
Are the wineries kid-friendly?
Some cellar doors welcome families with grounds to explore, though the tasting rooms themselves are adult-focused.
What is Hunter Valley Gardens?
A themed garden attraction with family-friendly grounds year-round and an elaborate Christmas lights display each holiday season.
What is TreeTops Adventure Park?
A ropes and zipline course through the treetops, suited to older children and adults looking for an active outing.
Can families do a hot air balloon flight?
Yes, dawn balloon flights over the vineyards run regularly, though they come at a premium price and suit families who want to splurge on one big activity.
What are the best free family activities?
Driving the scenic vineyard backroads, and many of the free public areas of the Hunter Valley Gardens.
When is the best time to visit?
Spring and autumn for the mildest weather and prettiest vineyard scenery.
What should families do when it rains?
The park pools and games rooms cover the basics, and the Hunter Valley Gardens has some undercover areas.
How far is the Hunter Valley from Sydney?
About 2 hours drive north, a popular weekend destination for Sydney and Newcastle families alike.
