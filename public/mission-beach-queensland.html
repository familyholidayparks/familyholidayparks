If We Were Booking...

  • Beachfront with a pool: We'd choose BIG4 Tasman South Mission Beach, 100 metres from the sand with the resort pool, waterslide and a playground the kids rate.
  • Wildlife and quiet: We'd choose Tropical Hibiscus for the wallabies, spotless amenities and easy walk to Wongaling Beach, or Mission Beach Camping for long beach walks straight off the site.
  • Our tip: Take the water taxi to Dunk Island for a day. The sheltered side snorkels well for beginners, the Muggy Muggy track is short enough for young legs, and being on an island beats almost anything on the mainland for a seven-year-old.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mission Beach good for families?
Yes, if your family's speed is beaches, wildlife and quiet rather than waterparks. It's the most laid-back stretch of the tropical coast.
Will kids really see a cassowary?
There's a genuine chance, this is the best wild cassowary territory in Australia. Drive slowly, especially at dawn and dusk, and never approach or feed one.
Which park is best for families?
BIG4 Tasman South Mission Beach, 100 metres from the sand with a resort-style pool, waterslide and playground. Tropical Hibiscus is the quieter wildlife-rich alternative.
Can you swim in the ocean at Mission Beach?
Yes, inside the netted enclosures during stinger season, roughly November to May, and at patrolled sections in season. Outside those months the open beach swims beautifully.
What is Dunk Island and how do you get there?
A rainforest island 4 kilometres offshore, reached by water taxi from Wongaling Beach. Day trips cover snorkelling, short walks and a beach barbecue.
When is the best time to visit?
May to September for dry days and easy swimming. The wet season is lush and dramatic but humid, with some businesses winding back.
What are the best free family activities?
The beach itself, cassowary spotting, the Kennedy walking track's coastal section and the markets at Porter Promenade twice a week.
Do the parks have pools?
BIG4 South Mission and Tropical Hibiscus do. Ocean Beach Tourist Park and Mission Beach Camping rely on their beach positions instead.
What should families do when it rains?
Not much is undercover here, which is part of the charm. Showers pass fast; if rain sets in, Tully's Golden Gumboot and sugar mill tours are 25 minutes away.
How far is Mission Beach from Cairns?
About two hours south, which keeps the crowds away and is exactly why families who find it keep coming back.
