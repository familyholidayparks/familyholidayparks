The Gold Coast delivers the full family package within a tight radius. Theme park thrills, patrolled surf beaches and rainforest walks are all within 30 minutes of each other. April to October is the sweet spot for visiting. The humidity drops, the skies clear and the summer storms that can disrupt beach days are largely gone.

One practical tip worth knowing: if theme parks are on the itinerary, consider staying in the Helensvale to Oxenford corridor rather than on the coast. You will halve your morning drive time and actually get to the gates before the queues build.
