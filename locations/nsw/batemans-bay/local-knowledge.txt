G'day! Batemans Bay sits right where the Clyde River meets the ocean, which means the kids can splash in calm river beaches one day and surf proper waves the next – it's this mix of waterways that makes family holidays here so brilliant. We always plan our trips for the shoulder seasons of March-April or October-November when the weather's still gorgeous but the crowds thin out and you can actually get a park by the river without booking six months ahead. Here's a local tip that's saved us countless times: grab your fish and chips from Innes' Boatshed right on the Old Punt Road bridge at sunset, then feed the leftover chips to the massive stingrays that cruise underneath – the kids absolutely lose their minds watching those gentle giants glide past.
