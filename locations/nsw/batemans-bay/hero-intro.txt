Batemans Bay is a relaxed coastal town on the NSW south coast that ticks the boxes for families wanting beach time without the crowds. The beaches here are genuine drawcards – Batemans Bay Beach itself is patrolled and perfect for younger kids, while Pebbly Beach and Sunshine Bay offer rockpools and quieter swimming spots when the swell picks up. Beyond the sand, you've got the Eurobodalla National Park on your doorstep with walking trails through spotted gum forests, and the Old Mogo Town theme park if the kids need a bit of themed fun. The town's compact enough that you can walk the waterfront, grab fresh fish and chips, and actually enjoy a holiday without spending half your time driving.

For accommodation, you're spoilt for choice with several quality caravan parks that understand what families need. BIG4 Batemans Bay at Easts Riverside Holiday Park, Batemans Bay Holiday Park, Rio Rita Caravan Park, and Batemans Bay North Tourist Park all offer powered sites, on-site amenities, and enough space for kids to run around safely. The real advantage is location – you're never more than a few minutes from either the beach or the bush, so whether your crew wants swimming, rock-hopping, or hiking, it's all within easy reach. It's the kind of place where families actually relax instead of just changing locations.
