PARK: Garden of Eden Caravan Park
SCORE: 89/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 95

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (18/20) — Exceptional family facilities with pool featuring waterslide, toddler pool with fountains, tennis court, free mini golf, trampolines, basketball court, playground equipment, giant chess, and photo props. Reviews consistently highlight "lots to do if you have kids," "really kid friendly," and families describe it as having "more facilities and activities than any other park we've stayed at."

Nature (19/20) — Outstanding natural setting backing onto lake with boardwalk access, surrounded by trees with bellbirds singing, abundant birdlife and wildlife throughout. Beach is short walk away (500m), park described as "very green, with lots of trees and backing onto bushland and a lake" with "beautiful leafy environment" and "lush green surrounds."

Cleanliness (15/15) — Universally praised as "spotlessly clean," "immaculate," and "best we have seen on our travels." Amenities described as "so spotless you could perform open-heart surgery with no risk of infection." Multiple reviews confirm "extremely clean amenities" and "very well maintained."

Value (14/15) — Excellent value with powered sites around $45 for two people, described as "extremely reasonable," "great value for money," and "underpriced in my opinion." Reviews note it's "affordable compared to other campsites" with comprehensive facilities included.

Site Size (9/10) — Sites consistently described as "huge," "good size," "spacious," with "heaps of room" and "lots of space." Grassy, level sites with "beautiful green grass" and shaded areas available. Large unpowered sites backing onto lake particularly noted.

Sentiment (10/10) — Outstanding guest satisfaction with numerous repeat visitors ("third year," "been back three times") and emphatic recommendations. Described as "one of the best parks," "campers heaven," and "one of the best parks by far" with families specifically planning return visits.

Location (10/10) — Perfect location with walking distance to Eden town, beach (500m), shops (600m), and boardwalk access directly from park. Adjacent to lake with easy access to whale festival and local attractions. Described as "stones throw from everything in Eden."

WHY NOT 100:
1. One negative review regarding late arrival policy when guest arrived after 6pm and was denied access despite perceived availability, suggesting after-hours access could be improved.
2. Some sites experience mosquito issues near the lake during certain seasons as noted by multiple guests.
3. Jumping pillow was not inflated during at least one family's peak season stay, indicating occasional inconsistency with all advertised entertainment features being operational.
