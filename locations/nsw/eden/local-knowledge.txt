G'day! Eden's stunning Sapphire Coast location gives families the rare combo of proper beaches, actual whale watching (September to November is absolutely magic), and holiday parks tucked into bushland where your kids can spot kangaroos before breakfast. We've stayed at parks all along this coast, and what makes Eden special is how you can be building sandcastles at Aslings Beach one hour, then watching humpbacks breach from the Killer Whale Museum lookout the next. My top tip: grab fish and chips from Wheelers Oyster Farm just north of town on your way in – the kids can feed the massive resident stingrays while you order, and it's become our family's Eden arrival tradition.
