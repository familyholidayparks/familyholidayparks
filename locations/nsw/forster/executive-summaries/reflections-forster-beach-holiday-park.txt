PARK: Reflections Forster Beach - Holiday Park
SCORE: 56/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-13
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 97

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Multiple families explicitly stated "nothing to do for young children I.e no playground, pool, water play etc" and "Not kids friendly park it is for the elderly." One reviewer noted "A few activities for kids & they had an afternoon of live music" during Christmas, but the overwhelming consensus is "No on-site facilities for families to enjoy (no pool, playground or games room etc)." Several reviews described it as "Definitely not a family oriented or kid friendly holiday park."

Nature (16/20) — Excellent beachfront location with reviews praising "only a short walk to beach," "close by the Forster beach, very nice view and can hear the sound of waves," and "You can walk to a few restaurants in just a few minutes. We were told you can see dolphins there sometimes." The park offers "ocean view" sites and proximity to "Forster has the best Ocean pool to swim." Natural environment scores well despite some sites being "mostly dirt" with minimal grass.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed feedback with recent improvements noted: "Super modern and clean amenities block and camp kitchens. 5 star amenities" and "impeccably clean, facilities well priced." However, significant concerns include "room was infested with ants and cockroaches," "site hadn't been cleaned prior to arrival, rubbish and random rusted bits of metal throughout," and "The new amenities block is awesome but it's always dirty and never cleaned." Older cabins described as "outdated" with maintenance issues.

Value (8/15) — Price concerns dominated reviews: "$650 for 2 nights and only had got the basics," "$63 per night was extremely expensive," "overpriced for what you get," and "At $400 a night we should have had gas and access to wifi." Some found it "well priced" and "Good value for being a few minutes walk to the beach," but majority felt pricing didn't match facilities, especially given lack of pool/playground.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant site size complaints: "Very tight packed sites," "Site we were given was too small for our van," "camp site was so small, our camper trailer only just fit but we couldn't set the whole thing up," and "sites are very cramped up the far end." Positive mentions of "Our caravan site was large & really level" and "Spots are generous with three car widths each" were counterbalanced by access issues and uneven surfaces.

Sentiment (6/10) — Polarized reviews with loyal returners contrasting frustrated families. Positive: "Our family stays here every Easter," "coming here 3 times a year for 33 years," "Would return again." Negative: "Won't be coming back again," "will never take family back there," "I wouldn't recommend this park to anyone." Recent management changes noted as improvement but offset by "parks in the area" comparison unfavorably.

Location (10/10) — Universal praise for location: "Awesome location in the heart of Forster. 2 mins walk to beach & restaurants," "Right in the middle of everything," "perfect location minutes from beach, marina, shops in main street," "walking distance to town and fishing," and "Close to shops cafes and beach." Location consistently rated as the park's strongest asset.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment facilities - no pool, no playground, no water play, no games room - making it unsuitable for families with young children seeking on-site activities.
2. Significant cleanliness and maintenance issues including pest problems (ants, cockroaches), poor site conditions (dirt, mud pits, rubbish), dangerous roads with potholes, and inconsistent cleaning of amenities despite premium pricing.
3. Poor value perception with high pricing ($400-650/night for cabins, $63/night for sites) not justified by dated facilities, minimal WiFi, cramped sites, and lack of family amenities compared to competing parks in the area.
