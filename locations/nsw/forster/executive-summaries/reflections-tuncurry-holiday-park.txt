PARK: Reflections Tuncurry - Holiday Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-19
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 194

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews consistently note "no playground, no pool, water play etc", "nothing to do for young children", with only pedal karts and occasional activities like "live music" and "pizza van" mentioned. Multiple families specifically stated this is "not a family oriented or kid friendly holiday park".

Nature (16/20) — Outstanding natural location with "short walk to beach", "rock pool", "breakwall", and "off leash beach within walking distance". Guests loved being "right on the beach", hearing "sound of waves in the night times", and the "beautiful setting" on the water, though pine trees dropping nuts were mentioned as a nuisance.

Cleanliness (9/15) — Highly polarised feedback: some praised "impeccably clean", "super modern and clean amenities", "CLEANEST cabin accommodation", while many complained of "black mould", "discussing toilets", "dirty and never cleaned", "facilities poorly maintained", and amenities "well past their use by date". New amenities blocks received praise but older facilities were criticised.

Value (8/15) — Mixed feedback with concerns about "overpriced" rates, "expensive" pricing at "$63 per night" and "$72 per night for pensioners", especially given "lack of facilities". Some noted "good value" for the location, but many felt the fees didn't match the aging infrastructure and limited amenities.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant complaints about "sites are very small", "dirt sites", "tight packed sites", "cramped", "our van only just fit", and "uneven, unlevel" sites. Some premium double slab sites praised, but many standard sites described as inadequate for larger vans with poor drainage and "mud pit" conditions after rain.

Sentiment (7/10) — Divided sentiment: loyal repeat visitors ("coming here 3 times a year for 33 years", "visited 12 times") love the location and "will definitely return", but numerous 1-star reviews cite poor customer service, safety concerns including theft, and deteriorating conditions. Many stated "won't be back" or "will not be returning".

Location (10/10) — Universally praised as "best location in Forster", "location location location", "prime beachside location", "walking distance to beach, shops, restaurants", "2 mins walk to beach", and "central to everything". The proximity to rock pool, breakwall, marina, cafes and town is the park's standout feature.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment facilities - no pool, no playground, no splash park, no jumping pillow, nothing for kids to do onsite, making it unsuitable for families wanting onsite entertainment during their stay.

2. Significant maintenance and cleanliness issues throughout the park including black mould in cabins, outdated and "tired" facilities, poor road conditions with potholes, uneven sites, drainage problems creating mud pits, and inconsistent cleaning standards in amenities blocks.

3. Poor value perception with high pricing that doesn't match the aging infrastructure, combined with numerous customer service complaints including inflexible cancellation policies, booking errors, theft/security concerns, and management described as "rude" and unhelpful in multiple reviews.
