PARK: Discovery Parks - Forster
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-08
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 163

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (18/20) — Reviews consistently praise exceptional facilities including splash park with 3 waterslides, heated pool, jumping pillow, pump track, playground, and pedal karts. Families highlight "loads of free activities" during school holidays including scavenger hunts, fishing competitions, and organized events. Multiple reviews call it a "kids dream" with "heaps for the kids to do."

Nature (17/20) — Stunning riverfront location on Wallamba River with "beautiful river views and sunsets," boat ramps, sandy beaches, fishing, and kayaking. Reviews praise the "peaceful" natural setting and wildlife including dolphins. Park borders bushland though some note encounters with spiders and lizards near bush.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "immaculate" new cabins and "very clean" facilities, but several critical reviews mention dirty older cabins with mould, poor cleaning of common areas, litter in pool areas, and maintenance issues. Recent renovations improving standards.

Value (13/15) — Generally considered "great value" with "reasonable prices" especially for powered sites at around $23-50 per night. Some feel premium pricing not justified given distance from town. Free activities and equipment hire add value.

Site Size (8/10) — Caravan sites described as having "good access" with "reasonable space between adjacent sites" and "massive van sites." Some sites concrete slabbed. Newer areas still developing infrastructure.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with many return visitors ("30+ years," "25 years") and strong recommendations. Phrases like "will definitely be back," "can't wait to come back," and "best caravan park" common. Some negative experiences with specific staff and cancellation policies.

Location (5/10) — Major consistent criticism: park is in Darawank, 10-20 minutes drive from Forster, "miles from anywhere," requiring car for all shopping and activities. Reviews note "false advertising" of location. Peaceful setting but inconvenient for town access.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Location significantly misleading - park is 10-20 minutes from Forster in Darawank, requiring driving for all shopping and town activities, with multiple reviews citing "false advertising" and inconvenience.
2. Inconsistent cleanliness standards with critical reviews detailing filthy older cabins with mould, dirty floors, hair in bathrooms, and poor common area maintenance despite many praising new facilities.
3. Some significant service failures including rude reception staff, lack of empathy for emergency cancellations, group bookings separated without communication, and smoking allowed in pool areas affecting families.
