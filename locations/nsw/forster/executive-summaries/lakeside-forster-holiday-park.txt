PARK: Lakeside Forster Holiday Park
SCORE: 73/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-08
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 199

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews consistently note limited entertainment for older children with a "small playground mainly suitable for toddlers," no trampoline or community hall, and basic facilities. The heated pool receives praise, and some mention pedal go-karts and basketball court, but families felt it "lacks basic facilities especially for kids" beyond toddler age. The lake itself provides natural entertainment for fishing and kayaking.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding natural setting with "beautiful lake views," "stunning" sunsets over Wallis Lake, abundant wildlife including deer, dingos, swans, pelicans, and "lots of bird life." Direct lakefront access for kayaking, fishing, and swimming. Reviews emphasize the "serene view of the lake" and "peaceful and serene surroundings," though some note a smelly creek/swamp area near certain sites.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "very clean park," "immaculate" facilities, and "clean amenities," but notable complaints about "amenities toilet were always dirty," "facilities unclean," insufficient shower blocks (only 3 showers per gender), and during busy/wet periods "needed more than one clean per day." Some cabin cleanliness issues mentioned.

Value (13/15) — Generally positive with "fairly priced," "reasonable rates," "excellent value," and "unbeatable" pricing mentioned frequently. Families appreciated the spacious sites for the price, though some felt premium lakefront sites with swamp smells didn't deliver value, and at $80/night for poor sites, some felt it was overpriced during peak.

Site Size (10/10) — Universally praised with "HUGE sites," "massively generous," "enormous," "largest ever been on" appearing throughout reviews. Sites described as "large and grassy" with plenty of space so families "don't feel like your on top of each other."

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong positive sentiment with many return visitors ("return each year for the last 6 years," "16 years"), though some negative experiences around management communication, racial harassment incident, and weather-related site issues. Most reviews express intent to return and highly recommend.

Location (0/10) — Park is "a bit out of town" (10 minutes to Forster centre, 7 minutes to shopping), described as "quiet part of town" and "away from main township." While close to Stockland shopping centre, it's removed from beaches, cafes, and main attractions, requiring driving for most activities.

WHY NOT 100:
Limited entertainment infrastructure for older children beyond the toddler playground and heated pool, with no trampoline, limited activities, or kids club programming consistently available. Significant drainage and site maintenance issues during wet weather, with multiple reports of sites becoming "boggy mess," "swamp like," and "completely washed out," plus inadequate shower facilities (only 3 per gender) for busy periods. Some lakefront sites suffer from unpleasant swamp/sewerage smells that significantly impact the experience, and cleanliness standards are inconsistent across amenities blocks.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"HUGE sites," "heated pool," "playground mainly suitable for toddlers," "beautiful lake views," "sunsets over the lake are sensational," "amenities toilet were always dirty," "boggy mess after rain," "friendly staff"
