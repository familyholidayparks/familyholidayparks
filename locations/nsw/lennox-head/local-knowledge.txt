G'day! If you're hunting for a family holiday park base near Lennox Head, you're in for an absolute treat – this stunning stretch of the NSW North Coast gives you those famous seven-mile beaches, rock pools the kids will obsess over, and some of the calmest swimming conditions you'll find anywhere. We reckon April through October is your sweet spot for visiting, when the humpback whales cruise past (often visible right from the beach!) and you'll dodge the summer holiday crowds. Here's an insider tip: grab takeaway from the Lennox Village shops and head to Boulders Beach at low tide – the natural rock pools there are like nature's own paddling pools, and your littlies can safely splash about while you actually relax with a cuppa.
