# Lennox Head, NSW

Lennox Head is a laid-back coastal village on the far north coast of NSW that ticks all the boxes for a family getaway. The area boasts some genuinely great beaches – Seven Mile Beach and Lennox Head Beach itself are perfect for swimming, with patrolled lifeguard areas and reliable waves that suit everyone from timid paddlers to keen surfers. The town sits right next to the Minyon Falls hinterland, so you've got rainforest walks, swimming holes, and proper nature experiences within an hour's drive. The local parks and reserves are well-maintained and family-friendly, including Reflections Lennox Head Holiday Park, which offers comfortable accommodation options if you want the convenience of staying put.

What makes Lennox Head work for families is the no-fuss vibe and the range of activities on offer. Kids can spend hours at the beach or exploring the rock pools, while you've got decent fish and chip shops, a few cafes, and enough going on to keep everyone entertained without the chaos of busier tourist spots. The hinterland offers proper adventure – think rainforest walks at Minyon Falls or kayaking on the Richmond River – so you can mix beach days with exploring. It's the kind of place where families actually relax rather than spend the whole time chasing attractions, which is half the battle when you're travelling with kids.
