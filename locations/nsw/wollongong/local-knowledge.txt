G'day! The Wollongong region is an absolute cracker for families who want beaches, rainforest walks, and proper coastal adventures all within a stone's throw of each other. We love visiting between March and May when the summer crowds have thinned out but the ocean's still warm enough for the kids to splash about – plus you'll often score better rates at the holiday parks. Here's a local tip: grab fish and chips from Corrimal Beach on a Friday arvo and let the kids play at the adjacent playground while you watch the hang-gliders land on the headland – it's become our family tradition every time we're up this way.
