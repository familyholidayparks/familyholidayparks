# Wollongong: A Family Holiday Destination

Wollongong sits on the New South Wales south coast, offering families a genuine seaside getaway without the crowds of Sydney. The region boasts excellent beaches perfect for kids—Bulli Beach and Corrimal Beach are patrolled by lifeguards and backed by grassy parkland, while Windang Beach offers sheltered swimming ideal for younger children. The hinterland is equally impressive, with rainforest walks, lookouts, and coastal trails that keep everyone engaged. Key family attractions include the iconic Sea Cliff Bridge, the interactive Illawong Museum, and Wollongong Botanic Garden, which gives kids room to run around while adults enjoy the scenery.

For practical family holidays, the area has solid accommodation options across several parks and caravan grounds—NRMA Shellharbour Beachside Holiday Park, Bulli Beach Tourist Park, Corrimal Beach Tourist Park, Windang Beach Tourist Park, and Lake Windemere Caravan Park all cater specifically to families with on-site facilities and easy beach or nature access. The region strikes a real balance between outdoor adventure and downtime; you can spend mornings at the beach, explore nature in the afternoon, and still be back for a quiet evening. Local cafes and restaurants are genuinely good, and there's enough variety to keep families of different ages entertained without feeling like you're constantly chasing activities.
