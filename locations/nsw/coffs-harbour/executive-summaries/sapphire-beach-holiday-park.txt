PARK: Sapphire Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 110

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Families praised the two beautiful pools (one warmer), games room, putt-putt golf, pedal karts, playground, tennis court and basketball court. Several reviews noted "kids loved the pool area" and activities kept children entertained, though some mentioned paying extra for mini golf and table tennis was unexpected. Coffee van on-site was a bonus.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional beachfront location with "100 meters to beach" repeatedly mentioned. Direct access through vegetated dunes to uncrowded, dog-friendly beach. Park "nestled amongst towering trees" with "magnificent birdlife" including black cockatoos. Shaded, tree-lined sites with "peaceful" natural bush setting. Nearby coastal walks across headlands praised.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive feedback on "spotless," "brand new," "second to none" amenities. Recent renovations delivered "modern," "immaculate" bathrooms with "excellent water pressure" and hot showers. However, multiple reviews from 2024-2025 cited specific cleanliness failures including mouldy cabins, dirty pools, unsanitary outdoor showers, and sand/hair in beds.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback with some finding it "great value" at $50-100/night, while others noted it was "a bit on the pricy side" and complained about extra charges for activities. Several felt $170/night cabin rates were high given dated interiors and requirement to do dishes. Positive comments on fair pricing balanced against premium location costs.

Site Size (7/10) — Multiple reviews mentioned "spacious caravan sites," "sites were a generous size," and "big" with concrete slabs. However, one stark 1-star review complained "site is really small and parked very close to another van" even when park nearly empty, and another mentioned sites "didn't fit the van and car."

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong repeat visitation intent with phrases like "will definitely be back," "booked for three nights, stayed three weeks," and "extended our stay." Highly recommended by families. Some negative experiences with management communication, strict policies (no refunds), and "robotic" staff interactions detracted from otherwise positive sentiment.

Location (9/10) — Prime beachside location praised as "perfect," "right on the beach" yet "quiet" and peaceful. Short drive to Coffs Harbour (10-15 mins) and Moonee Beach shops (2km). Easy highway access but some noted "highway noise continues all night." Walking distance to Beachstone Cafe. Felt secluded despite proximity to amenities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Serious cleanliness inconsistencies with multiple 2024 reviews reporting mouldy cabins, dirty pools, unsanitary showers, and hair/sand in beds despite generally excellent amenity feedback.
2. Site size and allocation issues with guests placed in cramped sites even when park nearly empty, and some forced to move sites after setup causing frustration and damage.
3. Management and customer service concerns including strict no-refund policies even for medical emergencies, poor communication, "robotic" staff interactions, extra charges without notice, and arrogant responses to legitimate complaints.
