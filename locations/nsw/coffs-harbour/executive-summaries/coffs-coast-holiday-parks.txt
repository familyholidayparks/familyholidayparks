PARK: Coffs Coast Holiday Parks
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2025
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 12

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews indicate minimal on-site kids entertainment infrastructure. One family noted "kids enjoyed themselves and always had a smile" but no specific mention of playgrounds, jumping pillows, or activity programs that characterize higher-rated family parks.

Nature (16/20) — Strong natural setting with "5 minutes walk to the beach" and proximity to Coffs Creek repeatedly praised. Beach access is excellent and food options nearby contribute to the coastal location appeal.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Consistently positive feedback with "Bathrooms were awesome and clean," "Toilets are close and cleaned regularly," "clean and tidy," and "great and clean" laundry facilities mentioned across multiple reviews.

Value (8/15) — Mixed signals with one reviewer noting $43/night for unpowered sites and expressing frustration about lack of pricing transparency. No strong value endorsements from families in reviews.

Site Size (4/10) — Significant concern with "Campers are on top of each other," "lack of large sites for all of us that have larger length vans," and "No, you cannot fit multiple vehicles on one site" noted across reviews.

Sentiment (6/10) — Polarized sentiment with five 5-star reviews but also concerning 2-star reviews citing smoking issues and construction. One family said they'd return, but smoking concerns led to a "would not recommend for Kids" review.

Location (9/10) — "Excellent" and "great location" mentioned repeatedly. Close proximity to beach, Coffs Creek, and food options consistently praised across multiple reviews.

WHY NOT 100:
1. No evidence of dedicated kids entertainment facilities (jumping pillows, playgrounds, games rooms) that families expect from holiday parks
2. Cramped site spacing with campers "on top of each other" and inability to accommodate larger rigs or multiple vehicles
3. Smoking concerns raised by families and construction disruption during stay periods impacting the family-friendly atmosphere

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"GREAT for young families with kids", "5 minutes walk to the beach", "Bathrooms were awesome and clean", "Campers are on top of each other", "would not recommend for Kids as there are a lot of smoking people", "lack of large sites", "kids enjoyed themselves and always had a smile", "Staff are incredibly helpful and accommodating"
