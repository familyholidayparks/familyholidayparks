PARK: Reflections Coffs Harbour - Holiday Park
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 164

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Multiple families explicitly stated there are no facilities for children: "No pool, No playground, No recreational activities at all", "nothing for the children at all except to look at a few rabbits", "not enough for young children". One review mentioned occasional events like Easter egg hunts and pancake breakfasts, and there are resident rabbits that children enjoy, but no permanent play equipment or entertainment infrastructure.

Nature (8/20) — Reviews mention the park feels like "being in the bush" with "beautiful tall trees throughout" and a creek-surrounded unpowered camping area with "vast green lawns". The location offers access to Boambee Beach (5 minutes), beaches, and parklands, but the park itself is sandwiched between the Pacific Highway and a creek with limited natural setting on-site. Wildlife includes rabbits and some birds.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Consistent praise for cleanliness with reviews stating "amenities are the cleanest I've seen for a long time", "extremely clean", "spotless", "best amenities I've ever seen in a caravan park", and "brand new, luxurious amenities". However, multiple cabin reviews mentioned dirt, mould, dog hair, insects in toaster, and poor maintenance which significantly impacts this score.

Value (5/15) — Highly polarized feedback: some found it "overpriced" at $145-250/night for cabins with issues ("too expensive for no sleep", "not worth even a quarter of it"), while others praised "reasonable" pricing at $54-65/night for powered sites. Common sentiment was poor value for cabin accommodation given condition and noise, but acceptable value for caravan/motorhome sites.

Site Size (6/10) — Mixed feedback with some reporting "sites are smallish and close together", "not much room to put awning out and have car on site", while others praised "great size sites", "lots of room", "very large" sites. Unpowered sites described as "spacious" but some were "uneven and sloping". Generally adequate but not exceptional.

Sentiment (4/10) — Deeply divided reviews with many 1-star ratings citing "worst experience in 70 years", "DONT DO IT", "never again", contrasted with 5-star reviews from caravan users. Negative reviews dominate from cabin guests and families with children. Several mentions of management issues and rude service. Limited evidence of repeat visitation from families.

Location (7/10) — Excellent proximity to "central Coffs", "walking distance to shopping centre, pubs clubs", "close to shops and city", "handy to everything". However, the highway location is a double-edged sword causing severe noise issues. Close to attractions including Big Banana and beaches, but the traffic noise significantly detracts from the location advantage.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Catastrophic noise issues from Pacific Highway location - overwhelmingly the most mentioned complaint with dozens of reviews citing "absolutely terrible" road noise, "trucks roaring all night", "noisiest caravan park we have ever stayed at", making sleep nearly impossible especially in cabins near the highway.

2. Complete absence of family entertainment facilities - no pool, no playground, no kids activities, no recreational facilities whatsoever for children, making this unsuitable as a family holiday destination despite being marketed as a "Holiday Park".

3. Significant cabin maintenance and cleanliness issues - multiple detailed reports of mould ("black mould everywhere"), dirty conditions ("dirt all over bed frames, insects in toaster"), broken fixtures, old dated cabins "well past their used by dates", creating health and safety concerns that severely impact the family experience.
