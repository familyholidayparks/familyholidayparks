PARK: Greenmans On The Hawkesbury - Caravan Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 47

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — No structured kids entertainment or playground mentioned; families praised wildlife encounters with peacocks, goannas, guinea fowls and ducks as natural entertainment; small pool mentioned positively; children enjoyed bush trails and seeing animals; emphasis on nature-based activities rather than organised entertainment.

Nature (20/20) — Exceptional natural environment with consistently glowing reviews; stunning location on Hawkesbury River surrounded by pristine bushland; abundant wildlife including peacocks, goannas, guinea fowls; beautiful birdlife; kayaking on glassy water; excellent fishing for bream and flathead; bush trails for exploring; described as "totally wild and unspoilt", "paradise", and "nature resort".

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed reviews with concerning consistency issues; several recent reviews cited "bathrooms very old and quite dirty", "showers not great bathroom messy"; one reviewer mentioned facilities "in awful state" after busy weekend; positive mentions of "immaculate grounds" and "clean amenities" primarily from 2024-2025; amenities described as "dated but adequate" and "functional but tired".

Value (12/15) — Generally considered reasonable but some concerns about pricing; described as "reasonably priced" and "great camping spot" by multiple reviewers; firewood noted as "very reasonably priced"; however one recent review stated "money is a bit pricey" for the facilities provided; overall decent value given the unique riverside location despite aging infrastructure.

Site Size (8/10) — Positive mentions of spacious sites; reviewers noted "spacious and well-grassed tent site", "camping grounds are bloody great"; sites directly on river mentioned favorably; described as "small campground" overall but individual sites appear adequate; suitable for big vans with one wheelchair-accessible guest successfully accommodated.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive family sentiment with strong repeat visitation; multiple reviews stating "kids already asked to go back again", "will return for sure", "can't wait to go back"; one reviewer on 5th visit; hosts consistently praised as "lovely", "wonderful", "friendly"; described as "hidden gem" and "best campsite in NSW"; families genuinely love the experience despite facility limitations.

Location (7/10) — Good accessibility being one hour from Sydney near M1 motorway; convenient for weekend getaways; steep, narrow dirt road descent creates access challenges with some nervous about towing; very secluded and remote once down the mountain; "perfect place isolated from outside"; no phone signal mentioned as both positive and negative; beautiful river location.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of structured kids entertainment facilities - no playground, no kids club, no activities program, relying entirely on natural environment for child engagement which limits appeal for families wanting organized entertainment.
2. Significant cleanliness and maintenance concerns with aging amenities requiring urgent updates - multiple recent reviews cite dirty, messy bathrooms, inconsistent hot water supply, and facilities in poor condition, particularly during peak periods.
3. Challenging access via steep, narrow dirt road creates towing anxiety and limits accessibility for some families, plus issues with ants in cabins, mosquitos, and occasional noise from other campers not being managed effectively by staff.
