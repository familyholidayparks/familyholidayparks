The Hawkesbury region sits just an hour north of Sydney, where the mighty Hawkesbury River carves through sandstone valleys creating natural playgrounds that kids absolutely love – think calm water beaches perfect for little swimmers, riverside parks with barbecues, and those iconic Brooklyn fish and chips eaten straight from the paper while watching the boats go by. We've found autumn (March to May) delivers the sweet spot: the river's warm enough for swimming, the scorching summer crowds have thinned out, and you'll actually get a park at Dangar Island wharf on weekends. The holiday parks here range from riverside campgrounds where you can kayak right from your site to cabin-style stays with pools, most clustered around Wisemans Ferry and Lower Portland. Here's an insider tip: always fill up with fuel and grab supplies before you head into the region – the closest full supermarket to most parks is back at Windsor, and those little riverside general stores charge accordingly!
