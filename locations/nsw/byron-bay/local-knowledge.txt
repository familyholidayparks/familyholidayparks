Byron Bay is busiest during school holidays and can book out months ahead, especially beachfront parks. Main Beach and Clarks Beach are patrolled and family-friendly, while Belongil Beach offers quieter surf. The Cape Byron Walking Track is manageable for most kids and offers dolphin and whale watching from the clifftops. If you're staying in peak season, expect crowds in town — many families prefer Suffolk Park or Broken Head for a quieter base with beach access. The farmers market on Thursday mornings is a local favourite.
