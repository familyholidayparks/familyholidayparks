G'day! The Ballina region is absolutely brilliant for families who want that sweet spot between beach vibes and river adventures—your kids can be building sandcastles at Shelly Beach in the morning and spotting dolphins from the Richmond River breakwall by afternoon. We reckon late September through November is the magic window here, when the humpback whales are heading north with their calves (grab a coffee at Missingham Lookout for front-row seats), the water's warming up nicely, and you'll dodge both the summer holiday crowds and the stinger season. Here's an insider tip: pick up fresh prawns straight off the trawlers at the Ballina Fish Co-op on a weekday afternoon—the kids love watching the boats come in, and you'll have the makings of the best barbie your caravan park has ever seen.
