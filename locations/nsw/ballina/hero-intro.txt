# Ballina, NSW – A Family Holiday Destination

Ballina is a laid-back coastal town on NSW's far north coast that ticks all the boxes for a family getaway. The beaches here are brilliant for kids – Shelly Beach and Lighthouse Beach offer calm, patrolled waters perfect for swimming, while the town's rock pools are great for exploring rock pools at low tide. Beyond the sand, you've got the Brunswick River for kayaking, walking trails through coastal bushland, and plenty of green spaces where children can run around without worrying about crowds. The town centre is compact and easy to navigate with families, and locals are genuinely friendly.

For accommodation, you're spoilt for choice with several quality holiday parks: BIG4 Tasman Holiday Parks, Discovery Parks, and the two Reflections properties (Shaws Bay and Ballina) all offer family-friendly facilities with pools, playgrounds, and powered sites. Key attractions include the Big Prawn (a quirky photo stop the kids will love), the Ballina Naval and Maritime Museum, and the nearby Cape Byron lighthouse and headland walks. Restaurants and takeaway spots cater well to families, and the slower pace means you can actually relax while the children enjoy proper outdoor time without expensive theme parks or crowded tourist traps.
