PARK: BIG4 Tasman Holiday Parks - Ballina
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-16
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 99

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Consistently praised as having "one of the best play areas" with playground, jumping pillow, heated spa, pool, games room with pool table and table tennis, cinema room, big chess, go-kart hire, and cart hire. Multiple reviews highlight "SO much for the kids to do" with facilities catering to different age groups. Activity packs provided on arrival.

Nature (16/20) — Located near Boulder Beach with coastal walking/cycling trails connecting Ballina and Lennox Heads. Reviews mention "beautiful beaches," "gorgeous views," whale watching opportunities, and "shady landscaped surroundings" with "well-maintained gardens and palms." Beach access requires short walk or 5-10 minute drive.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly praised for "spotless amenities," "immaculately maintained grounds," "crystal clear swimming pool," and "modern and clean facilities." A few isolated negative reviews mentioned mould on bedding, cockroaches in one cabin, and maintenance issues, preventing a perfect score.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many positive comments about "good deal via website" and comprehensive facilities, but several reviews complained about high costs relative to issues experienced, cramped sites during peak periods, and unexpected maintenance closures without compensation.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive with "generous" and "good size" caravan sites, though one strong negative review described layout as "horrible" with people "crammed so close together." Drive-through sites available with machine-assisted parking praised.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong repeat visitation intent with phrases like "already booked for next year," "will definitely be back," and "can't wait to go back." Excellent staff mentions (Kim, Kara, Cathryn, Erin). However, 3-4 very negative reviews citing poor customer service and maintenance issues prevent a perfect score.

Location (3/10) — Significant weakness identified across reviews: "a bit out of town," "had to drive everywhere," "10 min drive from the beach," "need a car to access closest towns," "beaches not walkable despite photos in ad." Located at Skennars Head between Ballina and Lennox Head requiring driving for beach/shops.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Location requires driving to beaches (10 mins) and shops despite being marketed as beach-accessible - multiple reviews expressed disappointment that beaches "none of which are walkable" contrary to advertising photos.
2. Several serious service/maintenance complaints including mould on bedding, broken dryers, washing machines out of order (3 of 4), poor staff attitude in handling complaints, and prioritization of permanent residents over tourists.
3. Cramped caravan site layouts during peak periods with inadequate facilities for tent campers ("0 undercover areas," "toilets far from tent section"), and some cabins described as "very small" with quality issues in older units.
