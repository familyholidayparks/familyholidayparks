G'day! Jervis Bay's combination of powder-white beaches and calm, crystal-clear waters makes it absolutely brilliant for families with little ones who are just getting confident in the ocean – the protected bays mean you can actually relax while the kids play in the shallows. We reckon May through October is your sweet spot for visiting, when the weather's still lovely but the summer crowds have thinned out, plus you've got a decent chance of spotting migrating whales from the shore. Here's a local secret: grab your fish and chips from the Huskisson bakery mid-afternoon, then head to Plantation Point for sunset – the kids can play on the grass while you watch the parrots come in to roost, and you'll have earned yourself a proper breather after a big beach day.
