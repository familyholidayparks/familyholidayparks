PARK: Tasman Holiday Parks - Jervis Bay
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 268

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (15/20) — Reviews consistently mention the pool with slide and spa, playground area near the pool, pedal carts and kayaks for hire, coffee van on site, and occasional live entertainment and activities during holidays including bug shows and musicians. Several families note kids loved the pool and playground facilities, though some mention the pool and spa were cold/not heated.

Nature (17/20) — Guests praise the peaceful riverside/creekside location with beautiful wildlife including kangaroos, possums, parrots, peacocks, ducks, and bull rays visible from the jetty. The park offers boat ramp and jetty access for fishing and kayaking. Reviews note it's close to Jervis Bay's stunning white sand beaches (3-5 minute drive), though not walking distance. The setting is described as tranquil with views over Falls Creek/Woolamia creek.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Most reviews describe the park as "spotless," "immaculate," "pristine," and "exceptionally clean" with well-maintained facilities and amenities. However, some recent reviews note issues with cleanliness in specific cabins (blowflies, dirty paw marks, wasp nests, cockroaches mentioned in multiple reviews), and a few guests found amenities smaller than needed for the number of visitors.

Value (12/15) — Reviews are mixed on value. Many describe it as "great value," "reasonable price," and "budget friendly" with rates around $125-195 per night. However, several guests felt it was overpriced given the requirement to strip beds and remove linen at checkout, and some premium waterfront villas at $450+ per night received complaints about noise disturbance. The requirement for $1 coins for hot showers in some accommodation types was also noted negatively.

Site Size (9/10) — Reviews consistently praise the site sizes as "huge," "spacious," "large," and "roomy" with plenty of space for caravans up to 24ft. Sites feature artificial turf or astroturf which guests appreciate for keeping vans clean. Some premium sites and waterfront locations are particularly noted for their generous space.

Sentiment (8/10) — Overall sentiment is positive with many guests saying they'll return and recommending the park. Staff are frequently praised as "friendly," "helpful," "welcoming," and "amazing." However, sentiment is lowered by notable complaints about noise disturbance from other guests, smoking issues between sites, checkout requirements, and some negative staff interactions regarding bookings and policies.

Location (4/10) — This is the park's weakest area. While praised for being close to Jervis Bay attractions and beaches (3-5 minute drive), it's not within comfortable walking distance of Huskisson town (described as 2-3km, 20-30 minute walk, or 5 minute drive). Some guests were disappointed it wasn't beach-front. However, proximity to two local breweries within walking distance is a positive. The location is described as peaceful and away from busy streets.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Location requires driving to access Huskisson town and main beaches rather than walking distance, which disappointed families expecting easier beach access for young children.

2. Unusual checkout requirements (stripping beds, removing all linen, taking out garbage) with threats of $50-100 cleaning fees created negative experiences for many guests who felt this was inappropriate for paid accommodation at these price points.

3. Noise management issues with multiple reviews citing disturbances from other guests (drinking, loud conversations, kids on bikes/scooters at 6-7am), smoking between sites, and lack of effective enforcement of quiet hours despite stated policies, significantly impacting family stays.
