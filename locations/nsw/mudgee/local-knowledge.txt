G'day! The rolling vineyard hills around Mudgee create such a beautiful backdrop for a family holiday, and what many visitors don't realise is that most of the cellar doors here are genuinely welcoming to kids – many have sprawling lawns, farm animals, and even playgrounds while the adults taste wine. We reckon April through May is absolutely magic for families: the autumn colours are stunning, it's perfect weather for bushwalks and bike riding, and you'll dodge both the summer heat and the winter chill that can nip at you out here on the Central Tablelands. Here's a local tip that'll save you a few dollars – grab your free fossicking licence from the Mudgee Visitor Centre, then head to Hargraves (about 30 minutes out) where the kids can pan for real gold in the creeks, and they actually find little specks if they're patient!
