WHY THIS SCORE:

Cleanliness (14/15) — Exceptional cleanliness standards with reviews consistently praising "cleanest I've seen," "immaculate amenities," "spotless amenities block," "modern very clean amenities," and "always clean" throughout 2023-2025; one minor lighting issue noted in March 2024.

Value (10/15) — Mixed value perception with one March 2026 complaint about "expensive for day visitors," February 2025 noting "fairly good price," and general consensus of reasonable pricing but not exceptional value; quality facilities justify cost for most but premium location pricing evident.

Site Size (8/10) — Good-sized sites with multiple reviews confirming "sites are a good size," "reasonably sized," "large and well kept," "plenty of room," with slabs for caravans and level pitches; not oversized but adequate for most family setups.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong positive sentiment with many "will definitely return" statements and one family visiting for 10 years; however, significant detractors in December 2024 ("cranky old lady at reception") and February 2025 ("very rude and uninviting") impact score, plus one February 2024 1-star review citing "horrible camp ground."

Location (10/10) — Perfect 10 for location with unanimous praise for being directly across from beach, on Cudgera Creek, near excellent general store, 5 minutes to Cabarita and Pottsville, with easy access to swimming, fishing, and coastal walks.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Minimal dedicated kids entertainment infrastructure beyond a small playground - no waterpark, jumping pillow, games room, or organized activities program that families with young children increasingly expect at quality holiday parks.

2. Inconsistent management experiences with several recent reviews (Dec 2024, Feb 2025) citing rude reception staff and unwelcoming attitudes that significantly detracted from otherwise beautiful facilities and location.

3. Unavoidable highway road noise affecting daytime experience for multiple guests, plus some site privacy concerns with one Feb 2024 reviewer calling out "no privacy between camp sites" as a dealbreaker.
