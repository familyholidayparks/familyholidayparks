PARK: Tweed Holiday Parks Tweed Heads
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-14
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 146

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Reviews consistently highlight the heated pool with waterslide as a major drawcard for kids, with families praising it as "great for kids" and noting children had "amazing time in the pool." The playground is described as large and good quality, plus there's a games room with massage chairs. Food trucks visit regularly (Friday/Saturday nights) adding dining entertainment. However, no organized activities or kids club programs were mentioned, and one Easter visitor noted disappointment at lack of holiday activities.

Nature (14/20) — Located directly on the Tweed River with boat ramp access, small river beach, and designated swimming area. Families appreciated the "beautiful little beach on the river" and proximity to Gold Coast surf beaches (3 minutes to Coolangatta). The park has shady trees, well-maintained gardens, and bird watching opportunities. However, multiple reviews noted surroundings "not that interesting" and the bay setting rather than ocean beachfront limits the natural appeal.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Reviews show highly inconsistent cleanliness. During quiet periods, amenities described as "spotlessly clean," "sparkling clean," and "immaculate." However, multiple recent reviews from peak periods report "disgustingly dirty facilities," toilets "not cleaned as frequently as they should," "stale urine smell," and "no toilet paper." One reviewer couldn't bathe their baby due to "horrendous uncleanliness." The inconsistency during busy times significantly impacts the score.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback on value. Positive reviews note it's "relatively cheap compared to other places in the area," "reasonably priced," and "great value for money." One family paid $400 for 6 people for two nights calling it a "bargain." However, several recent reviews mention "price gouging, too expensive" and the park being a "ghost town" due to pricing. The value appears reasonable outside peak periods but questionable during high season.

Site Size (8/10) — Most reviews describe sites as "good sized," "spacious," "generous," and able to accommodate large rigs (22ft van plus Ford Ranger mentioned). The artificial grass sites receive particular praise. However, some reviews note sites "weren't big" and one mentioned "small sites" during busy periods. Tent sites had issues with "rock hard ground" making pegs impossible to install.

Sentiment (6/10) — Highly polarized sentiment. Many 5-star reviews from loyal repeat visitors (one coming 35+ years, another since 2008). However, numerous recent 1-star reviews cite they'll "never return" due to rude staff, sandfly problems, or poor service. Multiple families report leaving early and cutting stays short. The severe staff rudeness complaints ("rudest person I have ever dealt with," "belittled and spoken down to," "abused by reception") significantly damage overall sentiment.

Location (10/10) — Excellent location scores consistently across reviews. Just 2 minutes to major shopping (Tweed City), 3 minutes to Coolangatta beaches, walking distance to pub and coffee shops, 15-minute bike ride to town via cycleway. Close to Gold Coast, Broadbeach, and Surfers Paradise. The convenience to amenities while maintaining a riverside setting earns full marks despite some road noise complaints.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe and recurring sandfly/midge infestation that has forced multiple families to leave early, with reviews describing being "eaten alive," requiring "medication for allergic reaction," and suffering bites for weeks afterward—management provides no warning or mitigation strategies.
2. Significant customer service problems with multiple recent reviews describing reception staff as "rudest person I have ever dealt with," being "belittled," "abused," and management showing defensive, dismissive attitudes toward legitimate safety and accommodation concerns.
3. Inconsistent cleanliness standards, particularly during peak periods when amenities deteriorate from "spotlessly clean" to "disgustingly dirty" with insufficient cleaning frequency, broken equipment going unreported, and basic supplies like toilet paper missing.
