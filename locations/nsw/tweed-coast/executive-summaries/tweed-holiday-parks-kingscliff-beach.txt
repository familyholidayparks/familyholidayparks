PARK: Tweed Holiday Parks Kingscliff Beach
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-01-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 89

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — No dedicated kids' entertainment facilities, playground, or waterpark mentioned. Families noted "Kids can ride around on the concrete paths" as the main activity, with entertainment coming from the beach itself and nearby bowling/surf clubs rather than on-site facilities.

Nature (19/20) — Outstanding natural environment with "beach directly in front of the park," "crystal clear water," whale watching from sites, and "fabulous beaches to walk on." Multiple reviews praised the beachfront location as the park's standout feature, with easy beach access and coastal walks to Fingal Head.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently described as "extremely clean," "spotlessly clean," "immaculate," with "amenities super clean" and "toilets and showers spotless all the time." Minor deductions for aging ensuites in some areas and occasional maintenance issues like broken BBQs.

Value (9/15) — Significant concerns about pricing with reviews stating "very expensive," "$131 for a powered caravan site," "way overpriced," and "council have gotten greedy" with "doubling the nightly rate." While some felt the prime location justified costs, many families found it poor value.

Site Size (9/10) — "Generously sized sites," "large caravan sites," "double slabs," "plenty of room," and "easy to navigate into." Some reviews mentioned tight fits for larger rigs, but overall very positive about space.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong repeat visitation with "Every Christmas holiday for the last 51 yrs," "will definitely be back," and "10/10 would highly recommend." Family reviews frequently mentioned kids having a great time, though some families were deterred by pricing and security issues.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for location: "across the road from great restaurants," "Woolworths is a 5 minute walk," "walking distance to shops, hotel," bowling and surf clubs adjacent, with "beach right at our door step." Universally praised as the park's greatest strength.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of on-site kids' entertainment, playground equipment, or water play facilities beyond beach access, limiting entertainment options for families.
2. Recurring security issues with thefts from sites, unlocked perimeter gates, poor fencing, and no security cameras, particularly affecting beachfront sites.
3. Premium pricing that multiple reviewers found excessive ("$131 for powered site," "way overpriced," "Un Australian"), significantly impacting value perception for families.
