PARK: Ingenia Holidays Kingscliff
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 118

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (9/20) — Limited on-site kids entertainment with most reviews mentioning only the pool and basic playground facilities; families noted "no green space for kids to play" and "nothing for the kids" beyond the pool, with one reviewer disappointed by lack of indoor activities like pool tables or table tennis during rainy weather.

Nature (12/20) — Situated near the Tweed Coast with "short walk to beach" and river access within 10 minutes, though the natural environment is compromised by proximity to M1 freeway and Coolangatta airport; some guests enjoyed "beautiful views and sunsets over the water" but location is more suburban than bushland.

Cleanliness (9/15) — Mixed feedback with recent positive mentions of "super clean" amenities and "spotlessly clean" facilities, but consistent complaints about dated bathrooms needing upgrades, unmowed grass sites, dirty cabins (particularly cabin 18), and inconsistent cleaning standards with several reports of filthy accommodations and poor maintenance.

Value (10/15) — Pricing described as "excellent price" and "good value" at $40-69/night for basic sites, but multiple reviewers called it "overpriced" and "way overpriced" for cabin stays at $89-130/night given the aged facilities and maintenance issues; value perception depends heavily on accommodation type chosen.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant concerns about site sizing with reviews stating "ridiculously small" ensuite sites, caravans extending onto roadways, and "very small sites" though some powered sites described as adequate for "caravan, annexe and car all side by side with room to spare" for 20ft vans.

Sentiment (6/10) — Polarized guest sentiment with enthusiastic 5-star reviews praising staff friendliness and facilities alongside scathing 1-star reviews about cleanliness and maintenance; repeat visitors exist but many stated they would "never come again" or seek alternatives; permanent residents create mixed atmosphere.

Location (6/10) — Convenient access just off M1 with beaches and Kingscliff township within 5-10 minutes, but major downside of "constant traffic noise from M1 freeway right into early hours" and "many flights over head from Coolangatta airport starting at 6am"; location trades convenience for peace and quiet.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant noise pollution issues from M1 freeway traffic and Coolangatta airport flight path severely impact guest experience, with multiple reviews citing constant traffic noise into early morning hours and flights starting at 6am as major detractors.

2. Inconsistent cleanliness and maintenance standards with numerous reports of filthy cabins, dated bathroom facilities needing urgent upgrades, unmowed grass, broken fixtures, and poor housekeeping creating hygiene concerns that drove guests to leave early or avoid return visits.

3. Minimal on-site kids entertainment beyond a basic pool and small playground, with no green space for active play, no indoor wet-weather activities, and complaints from families that children had nothing to do, leading permanent residents to complain about kids trying to play in common areas.
