PARK: Tasman Holiday Parks - North Star
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 125

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (18/20) — Reviews consistently highlight the "insane" waterpark with 8m tower slides, dedicated toddler splash zones, daily kids club, indoor and outdoor play areas, marine discovery centre with rock pool tours, reptile shows, movie nights, and arcade - creating an entertainment-rich environment where "kids spent all their time in the pool and slides" and parents appreciate activities that "give you some adult time."

Nature (17/20) — The park sits in a prime coastal location "nestled between a beautiful beach and estuary" with Hastings Point beach just a "short 10-minute toddler-friendly walk" away, access to a "beautiful safe creek" for swimming and paddle boarding, opportunities for whale watching, sunrise walks around the headland, and well-maintained landscaped grounds with palms - though some felt it "felt very claustrophobic" when crowded.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Most reviews praise "spotless" amenities blocks and "super clean" facilities with "plenty of staff maintaining the park throughout the day," but multiple recent complaints detail "filthy cabin floors," "huge cockroaches in our cabin every night," "dirty carpet," "broken tiles at reception," and "BBQ area and toilets were quite dirty" - indicating inconsistent cleaning standards, particularly in accommodation.

Value (11/15) — Value perception is polarised - many feel it offers "value for money" with extensive included facilities like kids club, marine centre, adult spa area, and tractor service, but numerous reviews express shock at pricing: "just shy of $1300 for 2 nights," "$175 a night during school holidays - literally DOUBLED," "very expensive" with increases of "$100s from last year," and "cafe prices were expensive" - families with caravans particularly feel squeezed by high site fees.

Site Size (8/10) — Caravan sites receive positive feedback as "large sites easily accommodated our 10m van," "spacious sites," "great size, clean and tidy" with ensuite options and astro turf, though some at the front were "so small and hard to get in and out of," and one reviewer noted "every square inch of land was occupied" - the tractor parking service is widely appreciated as a "marriage saver."

Sentiment (9/10) — Overall sentiment is strongly positive with numerous "will definitely be back" comments, families returning 3-5 times, and many calling it "one of the best holiday parks" they've visited - however, recent negative reviews about cleanliness, service issues at Ripples cafe, pest problems, and aggressive price increases have dented confidence, with some stating "won't be going back" and "wouldn't recommend."

Location (9/10) — Location scores highly for being "only 90 mins from Brisbane," "walking distance to the beach, sports ground, shops," with "cafe and petrol station right there at the park," "Woolies a short drive away," and proximity to Cabarita Beach (5 minutes) - families appreciate not needing to drive anywhere during their stay, though some note it's "a tad out of the way."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Inconsistent cleanliness standards - multiple families reported filthy cabin floors requiring deep cleaning, cockroach infestations in accommodation, broken fixtures, and poorly maintained BBQ areas and toilets despite premium pricing.
2. Significant price increases have created poor value perception - families report prices doubling under new management to $175/night for campsites and $650+/night for cabins, with many feeling "exploited" and questioning whether they're "getting what you pay for."
3. Service and maintenance gaps - complaints about Ripples cafe staff being "rude and unwelcoming," failed room servicing, overcrowded facilities during peak times making the waterpark feel "unsafe," limited indoor play centre access, and site allocation issues despite advance bookings.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"water park was insane," "kids club every day," "best room we've experienced at a holiday park," "staff go above and beyond," "cabin floors were filthy," "huge cockroaches in our cabin every night," "prices have skyrocketed to an unbelievable $175 a night," "one of the best holiday parks I've ever visited"
