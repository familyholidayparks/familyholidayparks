G'day! Nambucca Heads sits right where the Nambucca River meets the Pacific, giving families that brilliant combo of calm river beaches for little ones and proper surf beaches for older kids – all within a five-minute drive of each other. We reckon the sweet spot for visiting is April through June when the whale watching is phenomenal from the headland and you'll dodge both the summer crowds and winter chill. Here's a local secret: grab fish and chips from the Nambucca RSL and head to the V-Wall at sunset where the kids can spot stingrays in the shallows while you watch the sky turn pink over the river – it's become our family's favourite tradition every time we roll through.
