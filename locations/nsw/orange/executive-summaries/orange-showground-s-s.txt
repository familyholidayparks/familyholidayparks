PARK: Orange Showground s/s
SCORE: 28/100

REVIEWS ANALYSED: 44

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — No evidence of any kids entertainment, playground facilities, or organised activities. Reviews mention events like shows, markets and circus performances held at the venue, but no dedicated family entertainment infrastructure for campers.

Nature (8/20) — Reviews describe "beautiful grounds", "lots of grass area", "looks so lovely in autumn" and mention a "large fenced in, off lead dog exercise area" though lacking trees/shade. Located near Borenore Caves for walking. Basic natural setting but limited engagement opportunities.

Cleanliness (5/15) — Mixed reports with some noting "clean" and "well kept" grounds, but significant complaints about locked toilet blocks creating hygiene concerns. One reviewer mentioned "garbage bin not near camping area" and another warned of potential flooding in lower sections.

Value (8/15) — Strong value proposition as completely free camping, with multiple reviews thanking Orange Council. However, comparisons made to nearby Manildra showground offering "5 star treatment" for only $5/day with full amenities. One reviewer noted event entry at $20 adult seemed expensive.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews confirm "plenty of room", "lots of level places to put your van", "most sites big suit most vans", and you can "park where you like". Some mention moderate flatness with tilt toward showground and flooding risk in lower areas.

Sentiment (3/10) — Highly negative family sentiment with multiple "never again" comments, people leaving for other locations, and 1-star reviews citing unwelcoming atmosphere, poor signage, security concerns ("dodgy fella on motorbike"), and severe disappointment with facilities. Primarily suited for overnight stops only.

Location (5/10) — Described as "close to the Orange town centre", "convenient to Orange CBD", "handy and safe" location. However, strict 1-night maximum stay policy frustrated visitors wanting to explore the area, causing them to spend tourism dollars elsewhere.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Critical absence of toilet and shower facilities - consistently locked or unavailable, making it completely unsuitable for families with young children who aren't fully self-contained with onboard bathrooms.
2. Zero family entertainment infrastructure - no playground, pool, kids club, jumping pillow, or any activities that would engage children during a holiday stay.
3. Designed only as overnight stopover (1 night maximum), not a holiday destination - strict stay limits, no power hookups, no water access, and basic amenities prevent extended family vacations.
