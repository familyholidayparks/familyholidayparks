PARK: Canobolas Caravan Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 75

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Multiple reviews explicitly state "absolutely nothing for kids, no pool, no park not even a swing" and visitors were "surprised it was a Big 4" given the complete absence of entertainment facilities. No activities, playground equipment, or water features mentioned across all reviews.

Nature (12/20) — Park features "lush green grass," "lovely grassy caravan spot," and "plenty of grassed areas for kids and dogs to play." Located in Orange with some natural surroundings but described as "a bit industrial rather than rural." Decent green space on-site but not a bushland or waterfront setting.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Highly variable feedback: many praise "spotless amenities," "amenities cleaned multiple times a day," and "super clean facilities." However, serious concerns include "filthy accommodation," "cutlery draw caked in mould," "maggots in bins," "dated facilities need refurbishment," and laundry seals "full of mould/grime/black gunk."

Value (10/15) — Mixed value perception: some found it "good value for money" with reasonable rates, while others complained cabins at $160-195 were "overpriced for what we received" and "not worth it" given cleanliness and maintenance issues. Powered sites generally considered affordable.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews mention "decent size site for our van," "lots of room for vans, motorhomes and tents," and "lovely big, grassy caravan spot with slab." However, some noted "tight spacing for caravans" and the park is "not so good for big rigs."

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive sentiment with many "would recommend" and "will be returning" comments, plus praise for friendly staff. However, several strongly negative reviews about cleanliness and cabin conditions, with some stating "will not stay again" and "would never think of staying there again."

Location (9/10) — Excellent location feedback: "very close to everything," "5 mins into town," "convenient for Orange town centre only 2km away," "close to shops," and "walking distance to local takeaways." Well-positioned for accessing Orange attractions and facilities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment infrastructure—no pool, playground, kids' club, or activities, which is critical for families with young children seeking an engaging holiday experience.
2. Inconsistent cleanliness standards with multiple reports of mould, filthy cabins, maggot-infested bins, and poorly maintained facilities that directly contradict other positive cleanliness reviews.
3. Lack of natural environment appeal—the park is in an industrial area rather than a scenic bush, beach, or rural setting that families typically seek for nature-based holidays.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
