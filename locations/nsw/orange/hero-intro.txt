# Orange, NSW – A Family Holiday Destination

Orange sits in the Central West of New South Wales and offers families a refreshing mix of outdoor activities and attractions without the crowds you'll find on the coast. While Orange itself isn't beachside, it's surrounded by stunning natural bushland and excellent parks where kids can burn energy and families can enjoy picnics and walking trails. The region is known for its apple orchards, cool-climate wineries, and volcanic landscapes, with Mount Canobolas offering fantastic hiking and scenic lookouts that reward even little legs with spectacular views. Accommodation options include Canobolas Caravan Park, Colour City Caravan Park, and the Orange Showgrounds, all offering family-friendly facilities and easy access to attractions.

What makes Orange great for families is the relaxed pace and variety – there's enough to keep everyone entertained without feeling rushed. Kids will love exploring the local wildlife, visiting the Orange Regional Museum, and checking out the historic attractions around town. The cooler climate means summer holidays are actually comfortable, and the community-focused events throughout the year give you plenty of reasons to visit different seasons. It's the kind of place where you can take a long weekend or a proper break, stay in a comfortable caravan park, and let the kids experience nature and regional Australian culture at a genuine pace.
