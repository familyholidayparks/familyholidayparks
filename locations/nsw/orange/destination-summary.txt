Orange sits in the central tablelands where the volcanic peaks of the Canobolas region create a natural backdrop that sets it apart from the typical caravan park circuit. The three main parks—Canobolas, Colour City, and Orange Showgrounds—each offer distinct experiences within the same landscape. Canobolas leans toward the bush park experience with proximity to walking trails and volcanic geology, while Colour City accommodates the full spectrum from caravans to motorhomes with solid on-site facilities. The Showgrounds park handles overflow during peak periods and suits those after straightforward, no-frills stopover accommodation. What makes Orange genuinely different isn't beachside or riverside views—it's the cool climate, the wine region setting, and genuine access to bushwalking without the coastal crowds.

The clientele here tells you something real about Orange's appeal. You'll see long-term caravan regulars who've been returning for a decade or more, families hitting the parks as a base for school holidays, and motorhomers using it as a circuit stop between Sydney and further inland. During school holiday periods—particularly Easter and winter break—all three parks fill up completely, and locals know to book months ahead or you won't get a site. The draw isn't novelty; it's repetition. Families come back because the parks work logistically—kids can run around safely, the town has decent shops and services, and there's enough to do without being overwhelming.

The natural access around Orange is genuinely the hook. Canobolas Regional Park sits minutes from the top-ranked caravan park, offering proper day walks through native bush, volcanic lookouts, and creeks. The town itself has become a legitimate food and wine destination in recent years, which keeps repeat visitors returning with different intentions—some years it's purely family, others it's couples seeking something beyond typical caravan park entertainment. You get reliable facilities, stable pricing, easy access to actual activities that aren't manufactured, and a town that functions as a proper regional centre rather than a tourist trap. That's why caravanners and motorhomers keep coming back to Orange rather than chasing flashier destinations.
