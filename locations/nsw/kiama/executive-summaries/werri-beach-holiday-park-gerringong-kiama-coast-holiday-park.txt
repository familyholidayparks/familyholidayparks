PARK: Werri Beach Holiday Park Gerringong [Kiama Coast Holiday Park]
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2025
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 75

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Very limited on-site kids entertainment mentioned; one review noted pool available and kids using it even in rain, one mentioned lack of full tennis court, but no playground, kids club, or organized activities referenced. Main entertainment is the beach itself.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural environment with park positioned directly across from Werri Beach with ocean views, walking tracks along esplanade, nearby ocean swimming pools and rock pools. Reviews consistently praise "incredible beach," "rolling green hills meet the ocean," and beautiful coastal scenery.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed results with many praising "spotless amenities," "super clean toilet buildings," and well-maintained grounds, but significant negative reviews about "filthy cabin floors," "sand/dirt all over the floor," "dirty bugs over kitchen benches," "spa bath was filthy," and ants in beds. Amenities generally clean but cabin standards vary considerably.

Value (11/15) — Mixed value perceptions with several complaints about pricing: "$280 per night for three to three and half star quality," "Highway robbery for $60 pn in a paddock," "$220 dollars a night this is a ripp off," and "unpowered sites were very expensive at $55." Some found it "very good value" compared to Kiama, but consensus suggests pricing doesn't always match quality delivered.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive feedback on site size with reviews noting "large, flat site," "big caravan sites," "good sized grassed sites perfect for our large Oz tent," "plenty of space," and "spacious" sites. Some noted sites are "narrow but long" and adequate for larger rigs.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong positive sentiment with many return visitors and recommendations, excellent staff praise ("Jackie," "Dave and Jade" mentioned positively), but dampened by several very disappointed 1-star reviews about cleanliness, check-in issues, and unmet expectations. High repeat booking intention among satisfied guests.

Location (10/10) — Outstanding location scores with park directly opposite beach, walking distance to rock pools, close to bowling club, shops 5 minutes away, short drive to Kiama, and easy access to Gerringong town. Consistently described as "perfect location," "couldn't be better," and "amazing location."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Inconsistent cabin cleanliness with multiple reports of dirty floors, filthy spa baths, ants, unwashed surfaces, and poor pre-arrival preparation despite charging premium prices up to $280/night.
2. Virtually no on-site kids entertainment infrastructure beyond a basic pool (which has had maintenance issues), with no playground, kids club, jumping pillow, or organized activities mentioned across 75 reviews.
3. Value concerns with pricing perceived as too high relative to quality delivered, particularly for older-style facilities and basic amenities that several reviewers compared unfavorably to other parks and accommodation options in the area.
