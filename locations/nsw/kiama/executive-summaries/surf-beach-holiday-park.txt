PARK: Surf Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 76/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-30
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 71

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews consistently mention a playground that "kids loved" and proximity to beaches and coastal walks, but explicitly note "not a significant amount for young kids to do" on-site. No evidence of kids clubs, organized activities, or water entertainment beyond the pool. Families rely heavily on nearby beaches and walking tracks for entertainment.

Nature (19/20) — Outstanding natural environment with "stunning ocean views," positioned between two beaches (Surf Beach and Kendalls Beach), direct access to the Kiama coastal walk, and proximity to Minnamurra rainforest. Multiple reviews praise "gorgeous location with many exits straight out to lovely grassed areas and the beautiful beach" and "walk for miles from there."

Cleanliness (14/15) — Exceptional cleanliness with reviews calling amenities "always spotless," "immaculately clean," and "the cleanest I have ever seen on a caravan park." However, recent 2026 review cited "cleaning standards inside the cabin were below par" and 2024 review mentioned "hygienic standards were slightly below par," suggesting some inconsistency.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback on value with comments that it's "a bit pricey" and one family felt supplies were insufficient for a weekend stay. However, others found "excellent value" and "good price." The council-run facility offers various accommodation options "priced accordingly."

Site Size (7/10) — Caravan sites described as "big" and "very spacious," but multiple reviews warn it's "little bit tight for larger vans," "tight access to caravan sites," and "parking spaces are pretty tight." Manoeuvring challenges noted frequently, though concrete slabs appreciated.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with multiple families returning for 3+ consecutive years. Phrases like "will definitely be back," "can't wait to come back," and "we even extended our stay" common. One serious security concern (bike theft, 1-star review) and occasional service complaint prevent perfect score.

Location (10/10) — Perfect location score with "walking distance from two beaches," "10 min easy walk" to Kiama town centre, easy access to blowhole, markets, shops, and cafes. Consistently praised as "unbeatable" and "close to everything."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited on-site kids entertainment beyond a basic playground - families with young children specifically noted there's "not a significant amount for young kids to do" on-site, requiring reliance on beaches and external attractions.
2. Security and maintenance concerns emerged in recent reviews including one serious theft incident with non-functioning security booms/cameras, plus inconsistent cabin cleaning standards in 2026 reviews after years of perfect cleanliness ratings.
3. Site accessibility challenges for larger rigs with multiple warnings about "tight access," narrow roads, and difficult maneuvering that can cause stress during busy periods, limiting appeal for families with large caravans.
