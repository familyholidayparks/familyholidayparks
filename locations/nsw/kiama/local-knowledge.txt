G'day! The Kiama region is an absolute cracker for families, with its famous blowhole putting on free shows that'll have your little ones squealing with delight, plus rock pools so perfect for tiny explorers you'll wonder why you ever bothered with hotel pools. We reckon April through October is your sweet spot – you'll dodge the peak summer crowds, snag better accommodation rates, and still catch plenty of sunshine (plus the blowhole performs best with those winter swells). Here's a local tip that'll save you a bundle: grab your fresh fish and chips from Kiama Harbour instead of the main blowhole area, where it's half the price and twice as good, then picnic at Bombo Beach while the kids clamber over those incredible hexagonal rock columns.
