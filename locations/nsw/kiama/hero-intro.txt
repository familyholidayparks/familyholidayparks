# Kiama, NSW – A Family Holiday Destination

Kiama on the South Coast of New South Wales offers families a genuine beach holiday without the crowds and chaos of busier coastal towns. The region boasts a string of beautiful beaches perfect for swimming, surfing lessons, and long afternoon walks – Werri Beach and Seven Mile Beach are particular favourites for families wanting safe swimming spots and great facilities. Beyond the sand, the hinterland reveals native bushland ideal for exploring, with walking tracks suitable for kids and plenty of spots to spot local wildlife. The nearby towns have good cafes, fish and chip shops, and local produce markets, so you can eat well without leaving the area.

What makes Kiama genuinely family-friendly is the range of quality holiday parks designed specifically for people travelling with children. Options like BIG4 Easts Beach Holiday Park, Surf Beach Holiday Park, and Kendalls On The Beach offer everything from ensuite cabins to powered sites, plus playgrounds, swimming pools, and organised activities to keep kids entertained. Most parks sit right on or steps from the beach, which means less faffing about with packed cars and more time actually enjoying the ocean. School holidays here feel manageable – you get proper outdoor space, affordable accommodation, and the kind of relaxed pace that lets families actually switch off.
