G'day! The Blue Mountains is an absolute cracker for families who want that perfect mix of bushwalking adventures and cosy cabin nights – your kids will be spotting lyrebirds on the trails one minute and toasting marshmallows the next. We reckon April through May is pure magic here, when the autumn colours are stunning and the parks aren't chockers with school holiday crowds. Here's a local tip that'll save you some grief: if you're planning bushwalks from your holiday park, always check with the Blue Mountains Lithgow & Oberon Tourism office before you head out, as tracks can close suddenly after heavy rain, and the last thing you want is to drive the family to a locked gate at Echo Point!
