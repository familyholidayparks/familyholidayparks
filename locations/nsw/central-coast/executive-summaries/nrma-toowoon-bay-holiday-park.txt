PARK: NRMA Toowoon Bay Holiday Park
SCORE: 73/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-22
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 175

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently mention jumping pillow, resort-style pool with lazy river, playground, and pedal cars. Families note "endless entertainment for the kids" and children staying "permanently occupied." Some reviews mention jumping pillow being deflated or out of service during stays, and one long-term visitor noted activities "use to be so good but now it's bad." Food trucks visit on weekends (pancake van, fresh seafood van) adding to the experience.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional beach location with direct access to Toowoon Bay Beach and nearby Shelley Beach. Multiple reviews praise "stunning beach," "beautiful beach views," "calming sound of waves," and "ocean views from cabin balconies." The park backs directly onto beaches with "short walk down dune paths." Dog-friendly off-leash beach at Shelley Beach is repeatedly highlighted. Natural environment includes rabbits and magpies throughout the park.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed feedback with significant recent decline noted. Positive reviews mention "immaculately clean," "cleanest bathrooms I have seen in many a caravan park," and newly renovated facilities. However, multiple recent reviews report "amenities are not very clean," "pool was dirty and smelt weird," "toilet block near us was old and dirty," cleaner finishing in "7 minutes," and "decline in maintenance." Some blocks updated while others remain dated with mould issues reported.

Value (11/15) — Generally considered reasonable value with reviews noting "extremely reasonable compared to other local parks" and "great value for money." However, several reviews mention premium cabins being "very very over priced" at $4500 for 6 nights. NRMA membership discount changes caused confusion. Peak season pricing described as "premium rate" with sites around $55/night off-peak.

Site Size (8/10) — Sites described as "great size, not on top of each other," "spacious with plenty of room to accommodate large vans," and "huge camp spots." However, significant complaints about "sites are crammed in" especially near gates, some sites "a bit tight to get into," and concerns about electrical cords running through neighboring sites. Site mats have "big holes" and are "worn, torn and a trip hazard" according to multiple reviews.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation with families returning for 3+ years and one reviewer "coming here every year" for a decade. Many "will definitely be back" comments. However, several emphatic "won't be back" reviews citing cleanliness, staff rudeness, and maintenance decline. Recent sentiment shows declining satisfaction compared to historical visits. Families appreciate the safe environment for children's independence.

Location (9/10) — Outstanding location praised as "perfect" and "second to none." Beach access within 2 minutes walk, cafes 500m away, 5 minutes to The Entrance, close to Shelly Beach Golf Club. Central Coast NSW location provides easy access to shops, clubs, and multiple beaches. Only minor complaint about "steep long walk down and up" to beach access and 1.5 hour drive to Sydney Airport.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Inconsistent cleanliness with significant maintenance decline reported in recent reviews - amenity blocks showing neglect, pool not cleaned regularly, site mats with holes, and rushed cleaning noted by multiple families visiting during peak season.
2. Overcrowding during peak times creates cramped conditions with sites "jammed in," pool becoming overcrowded and dirty, insufficient amenity cleaning frequency, and late-night arrivals (11pm) disturbing other guests without proper management oversight.
3. Premium accommodation overpriced relative to condition with cabin issues including non-working BBQs, mould in blinds, missing basic supplies (towels, tea towels), poor wifi/Foxtel service despite advertising, and ant infestations reported in multiple beachcomber cabins.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
