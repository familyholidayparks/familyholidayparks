G'day! The Central Coast sits in that perfect sweet spot between Sydney and Newcastle, where the beaches are less crowded, the rock pools are brilliant for little ones, and you can actually find a park on a weekday without doing seventeen-point turns. We've found September through November absolutely magic here – the water's warming up, the whales are still migrating past, and the school holiday chaos hasn't hit yet. My insider tip? Grab your fish and chips from any local shop and head to the rock baths at MacMasters Beach right on sunset – the kids can splash about safely while you actually sit down for five minutes, and it won't cost you a cent. The holiday parks here strike that lovely balance between proper facilities and that classic Aussie coastal vibe where kids can ride their bikes around freely and make friends with the crew from three cabins over.
