PARK: Reflections Brunswick Head - Holiday Park
SCORE: 61/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 62

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Very limited on-site entertainment with only pedal cart hire mentioned; no pool, playground, or organized activities noted; one review mentioned a former bird aviary that's no longer present; families rely on external attractions and natural environment for entertainment.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding natural setting right on Simpson's Creek with river frontage; immediate access to shallow river swimming suitable for kids; 5-minute walk to beach; beautiful bird life including bush turkeys; scenic sunrise views; dual option of creek and ocean swimming highly praised by families.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Generally positive feedback on amenities being "spotless," "immaculate," and cleaned multiple times daily; family bathrooms available with baby bathtubs; some reviews noted older amenities blocks weren't as impressive; isolated complaints about bird droppings on concrete pads and gravel sites.

Value (7/15) — Significant concerns about pricing with multiple reviews calling it "overpriced," "shameful," and "way overpriced"; powered sites ranging $72-$90 per night considered excessive given compact sites and limited facilities; several families noted pricing doesn't match experience; monopoly on Brunswick parks mentioned.

Site Size (5/10) — Consistent complaints about sites being "tiny," "cramped in like sardines," and "right on top of each other"; sites described as smaller than quoted; some concrete slabs without proper setup space; typical of older coastal parks but limiting for larger rigs.

Sentiment (6/10) — Mixed sentiment with many repeat visitors and positive family experiences offset by significant issues; theft problems impacted multiple families; security concerns, inflexible cancellation policies, and customer service issues noted; families with positive experiences loved the location but those with problems were highly dissatisfied.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location with walking distance to town center, shops, cafes, restaurants, bakery, IGA, BWS, and Brunswick Hotel; footbridge access to beach; close to everything without excessive noise (except highway truck traffic noted); convenient for exploring Brunswick Heads.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Serious and recurring security issues with multiple reports of theft (eskies, alcohol, bikes) from campsites that management doesn't adequately warn guests about or address, creating safety concerns for families.
2. Very limited on-site entertainment and facilities (no pool, no playground, no activities) for the premium pricing charged, forcing families to rely entirely on external attractions.
3. Cramped, undersized sites that don't provide adequate space for families with caravans or camper trailers, with sites described as "tiny" and "crammed in like sardines" throughout reviews.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
