G'day! Brunswick Heads sits right where the Brunswick River meets the Pacific, and it's this gorgeous river-meets-ocean setup that makes it absolutely brilliant for families – the kids can paddle in calm river waters one day and surf gentle beach breaks the next. We reckon April through October is your sweet spot for visiting, when the humpback whales cruise past during their migration and the summer holiday crowds have thinned right out. Here's a local tip that'll save you a packet: grab your fish and chips from the Brunswick Heads Hotel (the pink pub on the river), then stake out a spot on the grassy banks for sunset – the kids can run around while you actually enjoy a warm meal, and the local pelicans put on quite a show at feeding time around 3:30pm daily.
