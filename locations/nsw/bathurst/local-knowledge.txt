G'day! Bathurst and its surrounding region punch well above their weight for family holidays, with the kids absolutely loving the mix of gold rush history, motor racing excitement at Mount Panorama, and proper country experiences like farm stays and fossicking. We've found September to November is the sweet spot for visiting – the weather's brilliant for outdoor adventures, and you'll catch the Bathurst 1000 buzz in early October if that's your thing. Here's a local tip that's saved us more than once: grab your fresh produce and picnic supplies from the Bathurst Farmers Market on Saturday mornings at the Bathurst Showground, because the holiday parks here have fantastic barbecue areas but the closest supermarkets can be a bit of a drive depending on where you're based.
