PARK: Blacksmiths Beachside Holiday Park
SCORE: 73/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-14
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 98

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently highlight "great kids activities during holidays" and "free activites" for children, with mentions of a "new children's playground and rec room," playground facilities, and table tennis. However, entertainment appears seasonal and less structured than top-tier parks, with limited year-round programming.

Nature (18/20) — Excellent natural setting with the park "200 metres from the beach," "walking distance to Blacksmiths Beach," access to "grannies pool" (natural inlet), breakwall for fishing, and "great sunset views back toward Lake Macquarie." Multiple reviews praise the beach access and natural swimming areas.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "beautifully clean amenities" and "spotless cabins," but significant recent complaints about "toilets and showers were very dirty," "blood on toilet doors," "amenities smelt," and pool tiles needing cleaning. Inconsistency in standards is evident.

Value (11/15) — Limited direct pricing commentary, with one review noting "overpriced" and another mentioning "decent rates." Most families found it acceptable but not exceptional value, with crowding issues during peak periods affecting perceived value.

Site Size (6/10) — Major consistent concern with "sites were very small," "sites not on the generous side parking is squeezed," "sites too small for our van," and "some sites too close to each other." Many noted sites are "oddly shaped and a bit small" though "there's room for modern, bigger, vans."

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive with many stating "will definitely return" and "always come back to," but tempered by complaints about management handling of issues, aggressive interactions between guests, and some families saying "will not be returning again." Strong staff praise for Brae and Beau.

Location (9/10) — Outstanding location "close to beach and shops," "short walk to beach," "5 min drive to Swansea" for major supermarkets, walking distance to Orana Hotel and cafes, and "easy drive into Newcastle and Belmont." Central to attractions with excellent beach access.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Persistent sandfly/midge problem with multiple families reporting severe bites ("6 family members have bites all over us," "horrible camping experience" due to sandflies), requiring "strong deet" and better site management.
2. Small site sizes are a recurring major complaint affecting families with larger caravans, with sites described as "very small," "squeezed," and "oddly shaped," limiting the park's suitability for modern large rigs.
3. Inconsistent cleanliness standards with recent reviews reporting very dirty amenities including blood on doors, smelly facilities, and unsupervised dogs pooping on sites, contrasting sharply with earlier positive cleanliness reviews.
