PARK: Teralba Lakeside Caravan Park
SCORE: 18/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-13
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 47

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — No dedicated kids entertainment facilities, playground, or organized activities mentioned; one reviewer noted "kids enjoy the pool and the bbq area" in a positive review, but the vast majority found no family entertainment infrastructure at all.

Nature (8/20) — Located next to Lake Macquarie with walking access ("lake is less than 5 minutes walk"), offering some natural appeal; however, grounds described as "unkept," "overgrown grass," and "super untidy" detract significantly from the natural environment experience.

Cleanliness (1/15) — Overwhelmingly consistent reports of severe hygiene issues: "filthiest amenities," "toilets have not been cleaned in God knows how long," "mould in the sinks," "cockroach infested cabin," "pool is green," "pubic hair in the shower," and "bins weren't emptied all week." Multiple families left within hours of arrival due to uncleanliness.

Value (2/15) — Extremely poor value with prices ranging $40-$195 per night; reviewers consistently stated "way overpriced," "no where value for money," and "I've stayed in 4 star hotels with full service for less money." Families felt deceived by website photos versus reality.

Site Size (3/10) — Limited sites available ("only 4 sites for vans"), caravan sites "very small" requiring repositioning to avoid hitting neighbouring vans, though drive-through sites were described as "large" by some reviewers; bathrooms "way too small to service 4 adults."

Sentiment (1/10) — Extremely negative family sentiment with phrases like "worst caravan park ever," "would give 0 stars," "DO NOT GO HERE," "never be back," and families leaving after only 3 hours of a multi-day booking; very few repeat visitors, with most actively warning others away.

Location (1/10) — Newcastle/Teralba area offers lake access within walking distance and proximity to local pub, but overshadowed by safety concerns ("unusual people walking around," "permanent residents looked dodgy and scary"), highway noise, and families feeling "unsafe."

WHY NOT 100:
Cleanliness is catastrophically poor with consistent reports spanning 3 years of toilets never cleaned, mould throughout facilities, green pools, cockroach infestations, and families using nearby Bunnings toilets instead—this represents a fundamental failure in basic hygiene standards for family accommodation. Management issues are severe with reports of hostile, rude, abusive behaviour, poor customer service, threats of police involvement, yelling and swearing at guests, and unresponsive refund processes creating an unsafe and unwelcoming environment for families with children. There is a complete absence of family entertainment infrastructure with no playground, kids club, activities, or maintained recreational facilities, while the pool—when not locked—is frequently described as green and filthy, making this fundamentally unsuitable for family holidays.
