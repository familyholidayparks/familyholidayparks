The Snowy Mountains region is absolutely brilliant for families who want to mix alpine adventures with proper Aussie bush experiences – think skiing and toboarding in winter, then wildflower walks and trout fishing come summer. We reckon late spring (November) or autumn (March-April) are the sweet spots when the parks aren't packed, the weather's mild, and accommodation prices drop significantly compared to peak ski season. Here's a local tip that'll save you heaps: if you're visiting in winter, book your holiday park in Jindabyne or Cooma rather than up at the resorts themselves – you'll pay half the price and it's only a 30-40 minute drive to the slopes each morning.
