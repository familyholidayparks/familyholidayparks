WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Limited on-site entertainment facilities mentioned; kids enjoyed exploring the park grounds, riding bikes around the property, visiting chickens, and games were available. No waterpark or structured kids club mentioned.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting in the Snowy Mountains with stunning scenery and wildlife viewing opportunities. Families praised the peaceful atmosphere surrounded by beautiful nature, proximity to Kosciuszko National Park with wild brumbies, access to Lake Eucumbene for fishing and picnics, Yarrangobilly Caves with thermal pool, and lovely grassy sites with countryside views. "Breathtaking natural surroundings offering plenty of space for kids to play and explore."

Cleanliness (13/15) — Consistently praised for cleanliness with descriptions like "spotless," "super clean," "exceptionally clean," and "immaculate" appearing frequently. Amenities, showers, and cabins received particular praise. However, one recent critical review (March 2026) noted grass wasn't mowed and general maintenance issues, suggesting some inconsistency, though bathrooms were still described as "absolutely spotless" even by that reviewer.

Value (13/15) — Many reviews praised value for money with phrases like "best value for money," "affordable," "reasonably priced," and "great value for money." However, two reviews mentioned being "overpriced" and one critical review about budget accommodation falling below expectations. Overall sentiment was positive about pricing relative to the experience provided.

Site Size (8/10) — Sites described as having "plenty of space," "spacious camping sites," "flat well kept" sites, and "enough grassy area." Cabins were consistently described as "spacious," "roomy," and having "everything needed." No complaints about cramped conditions, though detailed site size specifications weren't frequently mentioned.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive sentiment with extremely high repeat visitation rates and strong recommendations. Phrases like "we'll definitely be back," "can't wait to return," and "will stay here on our visits" appeared frequently. Multiple reviews from returning guests. Only 3-4 negative reviews out of 90, with most being 5-star ratings. Families consistently praised the welcoming hosts Andrea and John.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for location accessibility and nearby attractions. Walking distance to Adaminaby town, pub, bakery, and shops (5-minute walk). Short drive to Lake Eucumbene (4km), 30-45 minutes to Selwyn Snow Resort and Yarrangobilly Caves, close to Kosciuszko National Park. Described as "ideal base for exploring," "fantastic location," and "convenient location to high country."
