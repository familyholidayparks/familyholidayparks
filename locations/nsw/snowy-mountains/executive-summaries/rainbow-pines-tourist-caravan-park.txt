WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — No mention of any kids' entertainment, playground, games room, or organized activities for children. Reviews focus entirely on adult activities like fishing and peaceful relaxation with no evidence of family-oriented entertainment infrastructure.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting on Lake Eucumbene's shore with "breathtaking views," "spectacular sunsets," "great walking tracks, heaps of wildlife," and "crystal-clear" water. Close to Snowy Mountains with access to both lake and mountain environments. Strong nature score reflects consistent praise for the serene lakeside location.

Communal facilities appear better maintained than cabin accommodation.

Value (12/15) — One review mentions "good price" for families and another notes "$39 for 1 night, off peak, non powered site." However, two negative reviews describe cabins as an "absolute rip off," suggesting value varies significantly by accommodation type. Reasonable pricing but quality concerns impact overall value perception.

Site Size (8/10) — Limited direct evidence but one recent review notes "two tent spots, which were more than spacious enough for our group of 8 adults, 1 child, and a dog," indicating generous site sizing. Amenities noted as "little far from our caravan site" suggests decent spacing between sites.

Sentiment (7/10) — Overall positive sentiment with many 5-star reviews and repeat visitors, but tempered by several concerning 1-star and 3-star reviews in 2024-2025. Phrases like "going back up soon," "we stayed an extra 3 days," and "coming back for sure" indicate loyalty, but recent quality concerns from families drag sentiment down.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location directly on Lake Eucumbene with easy fishing access. Close to Snowy Mountains for skiing, 15km from Adaminaby for supplies. Peaceful and remote setting praised consistently. Only minor concern is distance from major towns, but this suits the park's target market.

2. Significant cleanliness and maintenance inconsistency, particularly with cabins and en-suites, with multiple recent reviews reporting dirty conditions, cobwebs, maintenance issues, and accommodation that required cleaning before use.
3. Accommodation quality varies dramatically, with some cabins described as "dirty and run down" and "absolute rip off" while amenities receive praise, creating an unreliable experience for families not bringing their own caravan.
