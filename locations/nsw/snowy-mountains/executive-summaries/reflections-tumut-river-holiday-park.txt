PARK: Reflections Tumut River - Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-01-03
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 147

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Limited structured entertainment with a playground for kids, open grassy areas for bike riding and play, adjoining walk/bike paths to wetlands, and river access for swimming (with life jacket warnings for fast current). No pool, waterpark, or kids' club activities mentioned. Coffee van on-site.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural setting directly on the Tumut River with stunning views, mature shade trees, abundant wildlife (ducks, magpies, kookaburra, cockatoos, possums, platypus), walking tracks to wetlands, and proximity to Snowy Mountains exploration. River described as "spectacular," "beautiful," and central to the park experience.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed reviews with many praising "spotless" and "immaculate" amenities, but recurring complaints about inconsistent cleaning during peak periods, particularly when park is full. Some cabin cleanliness issues noted with "grubby marks," "dust," and deep cleaning needed. Amenities described as "older style" requiring refurbishment.

Value (10/15) — Several reviews mention pricing concerns including "overpriced for the facility" at $160/night for dated cabins, "top price for a second grade park," and unusual policies like charging toddlers as adults. Recent Reflections takeover criticized as "severely overpriced." However, positive mentions of well-stocked facilities and good site value.

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised for "large sites," "massive sites," "spacious" powered and unpowered options, "big sites, shady and cool," with riverfront sites particularly sought after. Some sites "a bit squeezed" and water connections not available at every site without extensions.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation with many saying "will definitely be back," "love this place," though tempered by management change concerns and several very negative service experiences. Scott the groundskeeper receives exceptional praise. Mixed feelings about new Reflections management.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location "walking distance to town" (10-15 minutes), close to shops, brewery, pubs, pool, library, and serves as base for exploring Snowy Mountains and Mt Selwyn. Some road noise mentioned near highway-adjacent sites, but generally quiet.

WHY NOT 100:
[1] Inconsistent cleanliness standards during peak periods with amenities requiring more frequent cleaning shifts, spider webs, and some cabins needing deep cleaning and refurbishment rather than basic maintenance.
[2] Minimal on-site kids' entertainment infrastructure beyond a basic playground, with no pool, kids' club, waterpark, or structured activities that modern families increasingly expect from holiday parks.
[3] Service inconsistency following management changeover to Reflections, with multiple complaints about rude staff interactions, inflexible booking policies, understaffing issues, and pricing increases affecting the previously strong family-friendly reputation.
