PARK: Holiday Haven Shoalhaven Heads
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-14
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 81

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Reviews consistently praise the jumping pillow, large pool with toddler splash park, games room, playground with pirate ship, tennis court, and mini golf. An 11-year-old reviewer loved "heaps of stuff to entertain" kids, and families mention bike tracks and pump track access across the road. Holiday activities are provided during peak periods.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional location with beach access on one side and Shoalhaven River on the other. Families mention easy 5-minute walks to beach, river views from sites, Native Botanic Gardens nearby, bandicoot sightings at night, and proximity to Berry, Kangaroo Valley and Jervis Bay region for exploration.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Most reviews praise "spotless amenities," "immaculate" ensuites, and exceptionally clean facilities. However, some reviews note women's bathrooms more modern than men's, dirty utensils in one cabin, and older men's facilities "very alt and riechen oft streng." General maintenance is well-regarded with consistent positive mentions.

Value (12/15) — Reviews mention "great value" and "reasonable fees" with families returning for 10+ years. However, some note "price is quite high for one night" at $50+ and strict cancellation policies disappointed guests during family emergencies. Overall perceived as fair pricing for location and facilities offered.

Site Size (7/10) — Significant variability mentioned: "generous" and "good sized" sites praised by many, but specific sites 364-367 criticized as "crammed in like sardines" with inability to fit Ford Ranger properly. Several reviews note some sites "very limited in size" and cramped conditions during peak periods requiring car parking outside gates.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation with families returning for 10+ years and many "will definitely be back" comments. However, recent reviews cite serious concerns about bike and item theft, with multiple families stating "never coming here again" due to security issues and dismissive management response to theft incidents.

Location (10/10) — Perfect scores for location with beach and river access, 5-minute walk to shops/cafe/bakery/IGA, walking distance to pub and bowling club, and easy drives to Nowra (15 minutes) and regional attractions. "Location is unsurpassed" and "perfect location" mentioned repeatedly.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Security concerns with multiple reported thefts of bikes and valuables, with management reportedly dismissive and refusing to check security cameras, creating an "unsettling environment especially for families with children."
2. Site size inconsistency with specific sites (364-367) being extremely cramped, requiring cars parked outside gates during busy periods, and difficulty maneuvering larger rigs in certain areas of the park.
3. Cleanliness disparity between upgraded women's facilities and dated men's amenities which are "very old," don't lock properly, and sometimes smell, plus insufficient dryers (only 2) for peak holiday periods and some cabin cleaning issues.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
