PARK: Holiday Haven Currarong
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-22
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 59

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Reviews consistently highlight excellent kids facilities including splash park, jumping pillow, large swimming pool, mini golf, putt putt, and basketball court. Multiple families mention "kids loved it" and school holiday activities. Some reviews note organized events on special weekends. Lost points due to limited structured programming details and one safety incident with playground gate locking.

Nature (17/20) — Outstanding beach access with "literally 100 metre walk to the lovely beach" repeatedly mentioned. Guests praise the beach, inlet, rock pools, Currarong River, and bush walks all within easy reach. "Wake up to birds singing sounds of surf" captures the natural setting. Lost points as approximately 80% of park contains permanent caravans which some felt created a less natural atmosphere.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Strong positive feedback with "super clean," "very clean amenities," and "grounds incredibly neat and clean" appearing frequently. Modern ensuite sites described as "spotless." Great shower pressure praised. Lost points due to mentions of older amenities block being "basic and tired" compared to ensuites, drainage/flooding issues in some sites, and permanent caravan areas described as "rundown, old and dingy."

Value (12/15) — Described as "cost-effective family summer holiday" and "well priced" by multiple families. Cabins noted as good value for families. Lost points as no specific pricing mentioned, location requires 25-minute drive to proper supermarket adding costs, and some peak period booking issues affected perceived value.

Site Size (8/10) — "Large size sites available" and "plenty of room even for larger vans" noted positively. "Very well spaced out" during Christmas. Lost points as some reviews mention "sites very close together" affecting privacy, and site cramming issues during one booking dispute.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation with guests booking "twice a year now" and "coming here for 15 years." Many "will be back" comments. Lost points significantly due to serious management complaints in recent reviews including allegations of unprofessional behavior, discrimination complaints from multiple families, noise management issues, and safety concerns with locked gates.

Location (9/10) — Excellent coastal position near beaches, rock pools, and creek. Walking distance to bowling club. "Central to some absolutely stunning spots" around Jervis Bay area. Lost minimal points as location is quiet/remote with nearest supermarket 25 minutes away in Nowra, and described as less vibrant than nearby Huskisson.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Serious recent management and customer service issues with multiple detailed complaints about unprofessional staff conduct, booking disputes handled poorly, and allegations of discriminatory treatment during peak periods that significantly damaged guest confidence.
2. Approximately 80% of park occupied by permanent caravans described as "rundown, old and dingy" creating a "rough vibe" that concerned parents about children wandering independently and detracted from the holiday park atmosphere.
3. Inconsistent maintenance standards with drainage/flooding issues on some sites, amenities block described as "basic and tired" compared to newer ensuites, and noise management problems with guests reporting loud music after midnight without intervention.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"Perfect place to take young kids", "splash park, jumping pillow, big swimming pool", "literally 100 metre walk to the lovely beach", "super clean", "kids loved it", "cost-effective family summer holiday", "80% permanent, rundown, old and dingy caravans", "appalling, unprofessional, irresponsible customer service"
