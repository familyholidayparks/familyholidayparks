G'day! The Shoalhaven region is an absolute cracker for family holidays, stretching from the white sands of Jervis Bay down to the relaxed riverside towns near Nowra, with holiday parks that put you within minutes of both pristine beaches and the stunning Shoalhaven River. We've found late spring (November) hits the sweet spot – the water's warming up, school crowds haven't arrived yet, and you'll snag better rates before the Christmas rush. Here's a local tip that's saved us countless times: if you're planning beach days at Huskisson or Vincentia, grab your morning coffee and provisions at the Huskisson bakery before 9am, because once the day-trippers arrive from Canberra (it's only a 2.5-hour drive), you'll be queuing out the door. The parks here range from bushland hideaways perfect for exploring to beachfront spots where the kids can be in the water before you've finished your first cuppa.
