PARK: Tasman Holiday Parks - Merimbula
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-19
REWS ANALYSED: 123

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews frequently mention jumping pillow, mini golf, indoor pool, games room, and basketball courts, but multiple families noted "limited activities for children" especially in wet weather, with games requiring coins and "not much to do within the park" being common complaints. Some praised "lots of fun things for the kids" while others found facilities "dull no colour or fun things about it".

Nature (16/20) — Strong wildlife presence with "lots of kangaroos around, water hens and ducks", "kangaroos hopping around regularly", and bushland backing. However, park is "nowhere near the beach" requiring a separate trip, and wildlife comes with "kangaroo poo everywhere". Creek with "lizards and birds" adjacent provides natural appeal.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Major concern across reviews with "dirty", "hair everywhere", "floors were wet and still very dirty", "cobwebs", "shower was dirty", "pathetic condition of rooms", and "disgusting" appearing frequently. Some praised cleanliness but inconsistency is evident with "toilets often not very clean" and "bathrooms only cleaned once a day".

Value (7/15) — Significant value concerns with "overpriced", "not worth the money", "over $300 dollar a night", "$170 for a night", pricing inconsistencies noted, and "disgrace what they charge compared to what they provide". Multiple mentions of better value parks nearby and "simply unaffordable with 3 kids".

Site Size (7/10) — Mixed feedback with "sites are good size" and "spacious" from some, but others complained about "smallish site", "packed in like sardines", "way to small a site", and rigs "half on the road". Ensuite sites received better size ratings than standard powered sites.

Sentiment (5/10) — Highly polarized with loyal repeat guests alongside many "never again" declarations. Recent reviews (2025-2026) show declining sentiment with maintenance issues, cleanliness complaints, and family safety concerns. One scathing review warned park is "not family friendly" with safety issues for children.

Location (9/10) — Consistently praised aspect with "walking distance to shops and town", "woolies across the road", "close to town centre", "2mins drive" to shopping, and "easy walk in to town". Location convenience is the park's strongest attribute though "nowhere near the beach" noted.

WHY NOT 100:
Cleanliness and maintenance standards are consistently problematic with dirty cabins, broken facilities, hot water outages, blocked toilets, and poor upkeep creating negative family experiences especially in peak periods. Value proposition is poor with high nightly rates ($170-$400+) not matching accommodation quality, particularly for older villas and cabins that are "super old, over used, falling in pieces". Limited children's entertainment infrastructure means rainy days are challenging, with paid games room activities and restricted mini golf hours frustrating families seeking all-weather holiday options.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
