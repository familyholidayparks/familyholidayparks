PARK: Merimbula Lake Holiday Park
SCORE: 73/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 111

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note "no playground for kids" with only one mention of a basketball court. The park has a "small nice pool" but lacks structured kids entertainment, waterslides, or organized activities. One reviewer specifically noted this as a drawback for families.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural environment with "mobs of grey kangaroos," "flocks of friendly ducks," and abundant wildlife throughout the grounds. Located beside Lake Merimbula with "lovely view of the lake" and walking trails. Reviews repeatedly mention the "rolling green pastures," "open countryside outlook," and magical wildlife encounters making guests "feel close to nature."

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding cleanliness across all reviews with phrases like "hands down the cleanest campsite I've ever stayed at," "spotlessly clean," "immaculately clean," and "cleanest facilities I've seen in all my travels." Amenities described as "ultra clean," "spotless," and "maintained in a spotless condition" with daily cleaning mentioned.

Value (14/15) — Consistently praised for "very reasonable price," "fair prices," "unschlagbarer/fairer Preis" (unbeatable fair price), "very well priced," and being "way better than the hustle and bustle and crazy expense of the caravan parks close to beach." One mention of paying to use BBQ as "bit rough."

Site Size (10/10) — Universal praise for "large camping sites," "big grassy camp spots," "spacious/large area," "large level sites," and "lots of space." Sites described as "flat," "very level," with "plenty of space between sites" and "good spacing between neighbours." Accommodated 9m motorhomes with room to spare.

Sentiment (10/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with extremely high repeat visitation intent. Phrases include "would 100% stay here again," "we'll be back for sure," "will definitely be back," "can't wait to go back," and "this rates as the best." Multiple reviewers stated it was their favourite park or best experience.

Location (7/10) — Located between Merimbula and Pambula, "3-4min drive to Pambula and 10 mins to Merimbula." Reviews note it's "a bit far from the town and beach," "nothing within walking distance," and requires driving to attractions. However, praised as a "perfect base camp" that's "close enough to the facilities" while being "peaceful" and "away from hustle and bustle."

WHY NOT 100:
1. No playground or structured kids entertainment facilities - multiple reviews specifically note "no playground for kids" which significantly impacts family entertainment value.
2. Location requires vehicle access - the park is 10-15 minutes from town and beaches with "nothing within walking distance" and no safe walking paths along the highway, limiting spontaneous outings.
3. Limited direct lake access - despite the name, reviews note "not really on the lake" with "no actual lake access for swimming" and insufficient signage to lake access points.
