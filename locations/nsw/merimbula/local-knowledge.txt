G'day! Merimbula's Sapphire Coast is where you'll find some of NSW's most stunning family holiday parks, tucked between pristine beaches and the incredible Bega Valley hinterland. We love visiting during the shoulder seasons of late spring and early autumn when the whales are migrating, the beaches aren't packed, and you can still get a decent swim in without the summer holiday prices. The kids absolutely go mad for the Magic Mountain recreation area near town – it's free, has trampolines and waterslides, and it's the perfect spot to tire them out on your first afternoon when you've arrived too early to check in. Pack the esky with local Bega cheese and Tathra oysters from the co-op, and you're sorted for your first night's barbie.
