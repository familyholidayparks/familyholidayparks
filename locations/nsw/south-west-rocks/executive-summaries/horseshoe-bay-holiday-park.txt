PARK: Horseshoe Bay Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 51

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews mention "lots to do" and "good for kids" but provide minimal evidence of structured on-site kids entertainment, playgrounds, or organized activities. One reviewer noted the park "has nothing to offer" beyond the natural landscape, suggesting entertainment relies entirely on external activities like fishing, surfing, and beach play rather than park facilities.

Nature (19/20) — Outstanding natural environment with reviewers praising "beautiful beach", "spectacular views", "lots of whales and pods of dolphins", beachfront positioning, coastal walks at your doorstep, and dog-friendly beach nearby. Multiple reviews call it "one of the best" locations in Australia with direct beach access and stunning natural surroundings.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed feedback with older reviews praising "very clean amenities" and "immaculate" facilities, but recent reviews (2025-2026) report "amenities are outdated", "always Sandy and dirty", "broken ceramic soap holders with sharp edges", cabins with "dampness", "sewage smell", and "walls peeling off". Maintenance appears inconsistent across different blocks.

Value (11/15) — Limited direct value commentary, but one reviewer stated "charge $60 per night is ludicrous" given facility condition. Positive long-term loyalty ("been coming for 40 years") suggests reasonable value for location-focused visitors, though facility condition relative to price draws criticism from more recent guests.

Site Size (6/10) — Reviews describe "roomy" waterfront sites and some with "beautiful big sites", but multiple complaints about "small tight caravan sites", "sites don't have a lot of room to manoeuvre", "little tight getting our 22ft van on it", and mix of "dirt, rocks and weeds" or "mud through your camp-site" on non-turf sites. Artificial turf sites rated better.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong loyalty evident with multi-generational visitors ("been coming for 40 years now my children come each year") and many "definitely coming back" statements. However, recent reviews show frustration with customer service ("lack of flexibility", "no clue about customer service"), cabin conditions ("thank god it was only 3 nights"), and facility maintenance affecting overall sentiment.

Location (10/10) — Universally praised with "perfect location", "best location", "beach front", "shops and pub directly across the road", "right in the heart of town", "close to everything", easy walking access to amenities, and "stunning spot". Location is the park's strongest asset with no negative location commentary.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Virtually no on-site kids entertainment or structured activities mentioned across 51 reviews, with families relying entirely on beach and external activities for child entertainment.
2. Amenities are outdated and inconsistently maintained with recent reports of broken fixtures, sandy/dirty facilities, inadequate laundry equipment, and cabins requiring urgent refurbishment or demolition.
3. Site quality is highly variable with tight manoeuvring spaces, mud/weeds on non-turf sites, and customer service issues around site allocation and inflexibility that frustrate guests despite the premium location.
