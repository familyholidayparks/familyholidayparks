PARK: Ingenia Holidays South West Rocks
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-26
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 198

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Reviews consistently praise the "waterslide in the pool is a win for kids," games room, jumping pillow, and ping pong. Families mention "plenty for the kids to do" and "lots to do for the kids," with the pool being a major highlight. Some noted limited kids club activities during their stay, and entertainment options are more facility-based than organized programs.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting with "Beautiful Creek for water fun," riverside location along Back Creek and Macleay River, and "short walk to beaches." Multiple reviews highlight the "tranquil settings," "abundant birdlife," paperbark trees to explore, and access to Horseshoe Bay beach. The "stunning walks into town" along the river estuary and dog-friendly beaches add significant nature value.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "spotless" and "immaculate" amenities that are "cleaned morning AND night," but concerning reports of bed bugs, cockroaches, dirty cabins with "filthy feet from walking around," and "rusty railings." Recent reviews show improvement but some standard cabins remain "tired" and need deep cleaning.

Value (11/15) — Significant concerns about pricing with multiple reviews stating "way 2 expensive," "$289 a night way 2 expensive," and noting price increases from "$35 last year and $65 a night this year." Several called it "the most expensive" park on their trip and "very overpriced." Positive mentions of "well priced" offset by frequent value complaints.

Site Size (9/10) — Strong performance with "lovely large sites," "generous sites," "huge sites," and "big caravan sites" consistently mentioned. Sites accommodate "big vans with enough room for awnings and car space." Waterfront sites particularly praised as "quite large with good new slabs" and "not packed in like sardines."

Sentiment (9/10) — Very positive overall with many "will definitely be back" and "our favourite family holiday spot" comments. However, sentiment damaged by staff rudeness complaints ("some of the office staff are a tad rude," "obscenely rude"), security issues with thefts reported, and service inconsistencies that left some guests disappointed despite liking the facilities.

Location (10/10) — Excellent location scores with "perfect location," "great location," and "walking distance to town and beaches" dominating reviews. Easy "10-15 minute walk" to town, "short walk (1.5km) to beaches," and proximity to shops, restaurants, and attractions. River access and boat ramp add value for fishing families.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Cleanliness inconsistency with serious reports of bed bugs, cockroaches in cabins, and some standard cabins described as "tired, run down and in need of a deep clean" with "stains on walls, spiders and webs throughout."
2. Security concerns with multiple theft incidents reported (bikes, fishing gear, wallet stolen from inside caravan), "very little security in the park," open access from adjoining "dodgy" neighbourhood, and no security guards despite premium pricing.
3. Staff service inconsistency with numerous complaints about office staff being "quite rude," "dismissive," "obscenely rude," poor communication (double-bookings, checkout confusion), and lack of follow-up on guest concerns despite excellent groundskeeper interactions.
