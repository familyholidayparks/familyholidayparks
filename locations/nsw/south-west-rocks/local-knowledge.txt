G'day! South West Rocks is an absolute cracker of a spot where the Macleay River meets pristine beaches, and the kids can snorkel with friendly grey nurse sharks at Fish Rock Cave – honestly, it's one of those pinch-yourself moments they'll talk about for years. We reckon April to October is your sweet spot for visiting, when the humpback whales cruise past during their migration and the weather's perfect for rock pool exploring without the summer crowds. Here's a local tip that'll save you a packet: grab your fresh fish and chips from Fisherman's Co-op right on the wharf rather than the main street shops – it's half the price, twice as good, and the pelicans put on a free show at 3:30pm when the boats come in.
