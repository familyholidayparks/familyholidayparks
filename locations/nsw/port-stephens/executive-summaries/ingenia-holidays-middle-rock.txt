PARK: Ingenia Holidays Middle Rock
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 175

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (15/20) — Reviews consistently praise the pool facilities with heated sections, jumping pillow, and splash features. Cafe and bar onsite receive positive mentions. However, several families noted the playground was removed or inadequate, with complaints of "no park for kids just sandpit" and limited structured activities for younger children.

Nature (17/20) — Strong natural environment with "beautiful" beach access via 10-15 minute dune walk, abundant wildlife including monitor lizards, kookaburras, and possums. Location next to Koala Sanctuary praised. Bush setting and green grass mentioned frequently. Loses points as beach walk challenging for families with very young children.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Highly inconsistent. Some reviews report "cleanest and most modern" facilities and "immaculate" amenities cleaned twice daily. However, multiple detailed complaints about filthy cabins with "urine and faeces," "mould," dirty dishes, and "bathrooms constantly dirty and full of sand." Maintenance issues with broken fixtures noted.

Value (10/15) — Mixed feedback on value. Prices described as "expensive" ($800+ for 3 nights, $444/night peak), with complaints "not worth the price paid." However, others found it "great value for money" and "very reasonably priced" for unpowered sites. Cafe prices noted as expensive.

Site Size (7/10) — Powered sites described as "tight" with "one way access" and "very cramped." However, unpowered sites praised as "massive spots" with "plenty of space." Caravan sites "nice and wide but not overly long." Mixed feedback on ease of maneuvering large rigs.

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive with many repeat visitors and "will definitely be back" comments. Staff praised as "friendly and helpful." However, sentiment damaged by serious complaints about security staff aggression, noise issues until 2am with "no sleep," and management described as unhelpful ("told too bad").

Location (5/10) — Located near One Mile Beach and close to Nelson Bay attractions. However, beach access "not accurate" and "15 minute walk on soft sand" challenging for young families. Park described as "pretty far from main road" requiring detours. Not directly on beach as expected.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Cleanliness standards are severely inconsistent - from "immaculate" to "living in squalor" with detailed reports of filthy cabins, unclean toilets with human waste, mouldy showers, and dirty floors that required guests to clean themselves despite premium pricing.

2. Noise management and security issues are significant with multiple reports of "music pumping until 2am," kids on bikes/scooters late at night, aggressive security staff mistreating guests including children, and no effective quiet time enforcement despite 10pm policy.

3. Misleading expectations around beach access (15-minute soft sand walk vs. "right next to beach"), removed playground not updated on website, cafe closures not communicated, pool overcrowding from large group bookings, and poor communication about site conditions and facility availability.
