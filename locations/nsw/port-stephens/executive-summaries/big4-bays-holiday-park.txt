PARK: BIG4 Bays Holiday Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 111

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — The pool with waterslide is consistently praised as the highlight with families loving it, jumping pillow gets positive mentions, and activities like volleyball, giant chess, basketball, and 10-pin bowling are available. However, many reviews note facilities are "run down", equipment often broken or closed unexpectedly, pedal carts unavailable in peak season despite advertising, and limited activities compared to photos.

Nature (8/20) — Park has established gum trees and bush setting at rear, but is "in the middle of nowhere" requiring 6-8 minute drive to beaches. No walking tracks or pram-friendly paths around park itself, and location is "off a main road" rather than waterfront. Multiple reviews mention flies, mosquitoes, and bindii infesting grass areas.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Mixed feedback with some praising "spotless" amenities cleaned twice daily, but recent reviews frequently mention "dirty bathrooms", "filthy showers", outdated facilities, cockroaches in cabins, dusty carpets not properly cleaned, and "bare minimum" cleaning. Some amenities "very clean" while others "not well taken care of and very dated".

Value (6/15) — Consistently cited as major concern with "way too expensive", "not worth the price", and rates of $147-$190/night for powered sites and $710-$3800 for cabins feeling excessive given condition. Multiple reviews state "value for money was poor", facilities don't match pricing, and cheaper than other parks but "exorbitant prices" for what's provided. Some found it "affordable" compared to area.

Site Size (6/10) — Sites described as "good size", "very spacious" and "large" by some, but others report "barely fits our site", "sites were very short" with vans hanging over roads, no concrete slabs on many sites (grass/dirt/sand), and limited slab availability. Mixed experiences with site allocation and spacing.

Sentiment (7/10) — Polarized reviews with loyal repeat visitors versus many saying "will never stay here again". Positive mentions of staff (Georgia, Steve, Lynn) going above and beyond, but significant complaints about rude staff, management dismissing concerns, no refunds offered, and families leaving early. Many express disappointment versus expectations.

Location (9/10) — Strong point with "excellent location" close to Nelson Bay, Anna Bay, beaches, shops (Woolworths, Coles, Kmart nearby), attractions (horse riding, shark feeding, sand dunes, camel rides) all within short drive. Easy access to Port Stephens highlights though not beachfront. Main road provides convenience but brings noise.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe maintenance and cleanliness issues with dated, "run down" facilities, broken equipment, bindii infestation, cockroaches, and inconsistent cleaning standards that fall well below BIG4 expectations according to multiple recent reviews.

2. Very poor value perception with pricing $147-$190/night for basic powered sites and up to $3800/week for cabins that don't match photos, have broken appliances, and lack amenities - many families feeling "ripped off" and leaving early.

3. Critical safety and security concerns with no boom gates, no on-site security, serious incident reported in amenities, road noise from drag racing, unsafe playground equipment causing injuries, and doors not locking properly in cabins.
