PARK: BIG4 Karuah Jetty Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 143

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently mention jumping pillow, pool, games room with free arcade games, go-karts for hire, movie nights on projector, fire pit, and daily kids activities during holidays. However, multiple families noted activities were poorly organized or cancelled, pool crowded and needing more cleaning, jumping pillow often too hot or vandalized, and playground needing shade and updates.

Nature (16/20) — Guests praised the peaceful riverside location with long jetty for fishing and crabbing, beautiful river views, wild rabbits, birdlife (lorikeets, kookaburras), and salt marsh/mangrove access. However, the park lost points because beach access requires 30-45 minute drives to Jimmy's Beach or Nelson Bay, water access for kayaks is limited, and some noted sandfly problems.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Recent reviews show mixed results: many praised "spotless" and "very clean" amenities, cabins, and BBQ areas with daily maintenance visible. However, critical reviews cited dirty BBQ grills, broken toilet flush buttons, jammed doors, pool water "opaque," camp kitchen "disgusting" after events, popcorn in bed sheets, cobwebs, and inadequate cleaning during peak periods.

Value (9/15) — Multiple families complained about pricing: "vastly overpriced at $80," "most expensive" compared to Fraser Coast to Jervis Bay, "$1200 for 9 nights in dirty gravel site outrageous," and gravel/dirt sites charged same as concrete. Positive mentions included "great discounts," "reasonable price," and "affordable," but value concerns dominate recent reviews.

Site Size (6/10) — Major inconsistency reported: some praised "large concrete caravan sites" and "big caravan sites," but many complained about "very small ensuite sites," "had to move sites 3 times," "sites squishy," neighbors "couldn't put awning out," and drive-through sites "mainly dirt, very slopey, uneven" rather than the grassed sites shown online.

Sentiment (7/10) — Overall sentiment is mixed-positive with many repeat visitors and recommendations, but also significant disappointment. Positive: "return campers," "favourite spot," "will definitely be back." Negative: "wouldn't stay again," "first and last visit," "worst holiday park," and multiple complaints about refund disputes and misleading site descriptions damaging trust.

Location (5/10) — Reviews note convenient highway access ("short distance off highway") and walk to local RSL/IGA, but location loses heavily because nearest beach requires 30+ minute drive, limited local shops with erratic hours, and families felt too far from Port Stephens attractions. "Fair drive to activities we wanted to do, found ourselves in Nelson Bay."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe site quality inconsistency with gravel/dirt/sloped drive-through sites charged at same rate as concrete slabs, causing flooring damage and customer dissatisfaction - management criticized for lack of transparency about site conditions.
2. Cleanliness standards drop significantly during peak periods with camp kitchens and bathrooms left "disgusting" after events, broken/malfunctioning facilities (toilets, washing machines, keypads), and pool maintenance issues.
3. Location requires 30-45 minute drives to beaches and major Port Stephens attractions, with very limited water access from park itself despite being riverside, disappointing families expecting beach proximity.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
