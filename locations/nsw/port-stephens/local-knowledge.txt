G'day! Port Stephens has been one of our favourite family escapes for years, and it's easy to see why – where else can the kids spot dolphins from the beach in the morning, then be sandboarding down massive dunes by afternoon? We reckon April through October is the sweet spot for visiting, when the weather's still beautiful but you'll dodge the summer holiday crowds and score better rates at the parks. Here's an insider tip: grab fish and chips from The Galley in Nelson Bay (the kids' flathead is always fresh), then head to the grassy foreshore at d'Albora Marina where little ones can feed the pelicans at 3:30pm sharp – it's free, it's memorable, and you'll feel like a local.
