G'day fellow family adventurers! Ulladulla and its stunning Shoalhaven coastline have stolen my heart over countless visits – the combination of patrolled beaches with rock pools for littlies, brilliant whale watching from May to November, and those famous Blessing of the Fleet prawns makes it pure gold for family holidays. We always time our trips for the quieter shoulder seasons of April-May or September-October when the weather's still lovely but the parks aren't bursting at the seams. Here's an insider tip: grab fish and chips from any local shop, but head to Warden Head Reserve for sunset rather than the main Harbour precinct – the kids can run free on the grass while you spot dolphins, and there's rarely another soul there midweek.
