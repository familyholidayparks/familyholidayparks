PARK: Ingenia Holidays Ulladulla
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 119

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Water slides are the standout feature with consistent praise from families: "kids loved the slides", "water slides are fun", multiple pools and splash park. However, limited operating hours frustrated many ("only open 12-1 PM Friday and Saturday", "two hours of the day"), games room has broken coin machines, and facilities often closed unexpectedly. Gym has broken equipment.

Nature (8/20) — Not near beach ("15 min walk or 5min drive"), park located between two major roads creating industrial feel, no natural shade or trees on camping sites, "no character to any site". Walking tracks mentioned but park itself lacks natural environment. Highway dominates the setting.

Cleanliness (7/15) — Major recurring issues: "black mould all over the ceilings", "facilities are filthy", "toilets were absolutely disgusting", "zero ventilation" causing mould growth, "cleaned once in the morning" insufficient during peak times, "duck poo" in pools. Some positive mentions of cleanliness exist but overwhelmed by negative evidence, particularly from recent reviews.

Value (8/15) — Mixed feedback: "extremely overpriced", "very over priced", "great value for money" mentioned by some. Pricing increases noted ("getting more expensive over Easter"). Peak period pricing disappoints given cleanliness and space issues. Off-season rates rated more fairly.

Site Size (4/10) — Consistently criticized: "Sites are VERY small", "packed in like sardines", "tight sites", "couldn't fit our car on our site", "all on top of one another", sites sloping/uneven with potholes, "no room to put awning up". Ensuite sites particularly cramped. Sites 53-58 mentioned as having most space.

Sentiment (6/10) — Polarized responses with many "would not return" statements from recent reviews versus some repeat visitors. Safety concerns raised (child hit by car), management responsiveness criticized, but some praised staff helpfulness. Recent trend notably more negative than older reviews.

Location (7/10) — Easy highway access, "short walk into Ulladulla", close to town and beaches (driving required). However, "constant traffic noise", "loud traffic", "trucks hurl past" significantly impact experience. Not walkable to beach for families. Ex-servos club and amenities nearby.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness and maintenance deficiencies with black mould in bathrooms, insufficient cleaning frequency during peak periods, and broken/poorly maintained facilities creating health and safety concerns that dominate recent family reviews.
2. Extremely cramped caravan and camping sites described as "sardine-like" with many families unable to fit vehicles on-site, combined with sloping/uneven ground and lack of shade making setup stressful and uncomfortable.
3. Highway noise pollution and safety issues including a child being struck by a vehicle, inadequate security (no boom gate), and inconsistent facility access hours that severely diminish the family holiday experience despite good water entertainment.
