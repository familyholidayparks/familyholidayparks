PARK: Discovery Parks - Burrill Lake, Ulladulla
SCORE: 73/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 80

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Families consistently praised three playgrounds, jumping pillow, free mini golf, tennis court, heated pools (one deep, one for kids), games room, and bird feeding activities with rainbow lorikeets. Kids particularly loved non-stop fishing off two jetties with excellent catches reported. Some mentioned games room deteriorating with rubbish and table tennis removed, and one pool being too cold.

Nature (17/20) — Beautiful lakeside setting with direct lake access, boat ramp, and two jetties received consistent praise. Abundant wildlife including lorikeets, ducks, rabbits, and possums delighted families. Short drive to beaches and coastal attractions. Natural environment slightly compromised by proximity to highway for some sites.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed results with recent reviews showing concerning decline. Multiple reports of roaches, ants, dirty cabins with filthy cutlery/crockery, mould on bathroom ceilings, spider webs, and unhygienic conditions. However, amenities blocks described as "exceptionally clean," "updated," and "refurbished to modern standard" with excellent showers. Camp kitchens well-maintained.

Value (10/15) — Several families noted "great value holiday" and "very reasonable rates," but significant complaints about dynamic pricing gouging ($28 to $60 per night over 12 years). Recent guests felt pricing didn't match quality, particularly with cleanliness issues. One noted "a little pricy for the facilities offered." Positive mentions of affordable activities.

Site Size (6/10) — Consistent complaints about cramped sites: "sites are a bit tight to manoeuvre into," "Tents and caravans packed so closely it feels like you're in a refugee camp," and "odd shaped, small" sites. Some lakeside sites were spacious, but overall spacing problematic for larger rigs. Pet sites particularly restricted near entrance.

Sentiment (7/10) — Strong repeat visitors and families extending stays, with many "will definitely stay again" comments. However, recent negative trend visible with long-time guests cancelling future bookings due to cleanliness decline and pricing increases. Noise management issues (late-night music) and poor customer service from some reception staff dampened sentiment.

Location (6/10) — Five minutes from Ulladulla with IGA, cafes, and bakery within walking distance. Easy highway access but traffic noise significant for some cabins. Short drive to beaches rather than beachfront. Convenient location praised but proximity to Princes Highway detracted from tranquility for many guests.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant cleanliness deterioration with multiple reports of roaches, ants, filthy cabins, and dirty kitchen items creating unacceptable conditions for families, particularly those with babies needing clean facilities.
2. Severely cramped site spacing making maneuvering difficult for caravans, creating a crowded "refugee camp" feeling, and restricting pet owners to small entrance area away from desirable lake sites.
3. Inconsistent maintenance standards between cabin types, poor noise control (late-night music disturbances), traffic noise from highway, and deteriorating customer service with rude reception staff and poor communication during maintenance disruptions.
