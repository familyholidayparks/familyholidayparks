PARK: Dolphin Point Tourist Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 36

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews mention "plenty of activities for kids" in natural water activities like "riding the current from the beach," kayaking, swimming, and snorkeling, but there is no evidence of any structured entertainment, kids' club, playground equipment, or organized activities. The entertainment is entirely nature-based and self-directed.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural environment consistently praised across reviews with "spectacular views right on the foreshore," "Beautiful cabin s right on waterfront," direct access to "shallow lagoon for swimming," surf beach within walking distance, whale watching opportunities, and "excellent abundance of water activities on Burrill Lake." The waterfront location is the park's standout feature.

Cleanliness (9/15) — Highly inconsistent with polarized reviews. Multiple recent positive mentions of "spotlessly clean," "clean amenities," and "very clean," but significantly undermined by serious complaints including "black mould" health issues, "sand in the beds, blood on the pillow cases," "dirty windows, filthy oven, dirty marks on walls, mouldy curtains," and "kitchen utensils not cleaned." The Nemo van specifically flagged as uninhabitable.

Value (8/15) — Limited direct value commentary with one concerning review stating "Very overpriced & underwhelming stay" and noting dated, unmaintained accommodation. No reviews specifically praised value for money, suggesting pricing may not align with the variable quality experience.

Site Size (7/10) — Described as "not a huge park" and "we didn't feel jammed in," suggesting adequate but not exceptional spacing. Positive mention that sites don't feel cramped, though one review mentioned parking restrictions requiring guests to move vehicles away from cabins which may inconvenience families with mobility needs.

Sentiment (5/10) — Extremely polarized sentiment damages this score significantly. While many guests express love for the location with "will definitely be back" and repeat visitors evident, recent reviews (2025-2026) show concerning negative patterns around customer service with multiple mentions of "rude," "arrogant," owner being "terrible," and aggressive behavior. Several guests stating they will "never return" and warning others away.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score with unanimous praise for location. "Absolute beautiful location," waterfront positioning, "close to convenience shops," "Ulladulla up the road," "easy walk through a beautiful park into town," and direct beach/lake access. The geographic setting is clearly exceptional for families wanting water-based holidays.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of organized children's entertainment, playground equipment, or kids' club facilities means families with young children requiring structured activities will find limited age-appropriate amenities beyond natural water play.

2. Serious and recurring cleanliness issues documented in multiple recent reviews including health-hazardous black mould in the Nemo van, uncleaned utensils, soiled bedding, and poorly maintained cabins that directly impact family safety and comfort.

3. Highly problematic and inconsistent customer service with multiple 2025 reviews describing the owner as rude, arrogant, and unwelcoming, including hanging up on enquiries, refusing cabin viewings, and aggressive responses to legitimate complaints, creating an uncomfortable environment for families.
