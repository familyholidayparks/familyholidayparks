PARK: Sheralee Tourist Caravan Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-06-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 151

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — No on-site kids entertainment, playground, pool, or activities mentioned. This is purely a stopover park with no family entertainment facilities. One reviewer noted "didn't see any children" and multiple reviews confirm this is not a destination park for families seeking entertainment.

Nature (8/20) — Located beside a river with some improved gardens, but heavily urbanized setting near airport and busy roads. Some reviews mention "beautiful nature near the river" and beach within 15-minute walk, though one negative review described river as sometimes smelly with visible rubbish. Not a nature-focused environment.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed reviews with recent improvement under new management. Many recent reviews praise "spotless bathrooms," "very clean," and daily cleaning, particularly from late 2025/early 2026. However, older reviews and some recent ones mention "dirty bathroom floors," "cockroaches in camp kitchen," weak shower pressure, lukewarm water, and sensor lights going dark mid-shower. Inconsistent cleaning standards.

Value (11/15) — Prices range $40-60 per night which reviewers consider "fair for Sydney" and "extremely reasonable" given proximity to CBD. However, multiple complaints about limited WiFi (1 hour free then $2/hour), small sites for the price, and inconsistent charging policies. Value is location-driven rather than facility-driven.

Site Size (4/10) — Consistently criticized as "very very small," "cramped," "super small spot," with many unable to extend awnings. Sites described as "smaller than the campervans" and "overcrowded car park." 21ft caravan maximum, car must be detached. Permanent residents occupy significant space, limiting visitor sites.

Sentiment (7/10) — Highly polarized. Recent reviews under Maria and Andrew management (Oct 2025+) are overwhelmingly positive praising warmth and hospitality. Older reviews frequently cite rude staff, unhelpful service, and unpleasant experiences. Positive sentiment centers on management kindness and location; negative on cramped sites and past staff rudeness. Family-specific sentiment is neutral to negative as this is not family-oriented.

Location (15/10) — Outstanding location score. Universally praised as "closest campground to city center," 5-7 minute walk to bus, 15-20 minute walk to Rockdale train station with direct 30-minute trains to Sydney CBD. Close to airport (10-20 minutes), beaches, shopping. Perfect for city exploration. This is the park's primary strength.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Zero family entertainment facilities - no playground, pool, kids' activities, or entertainment of any kind, making this unsuitable for families seeking a traditional holiday park experience with children's amenities.
2. Sites are severely undersized and cramped, with numerous reviews confirming inability to extend awnings, caravans overhanging roads, and spaces "smaller than the campervans," creating frustration particularly for families with larger rigs or young children needing outdoor space.
3. Inconsistent service quality and cleanliness issues including cockroaches in kitchen areas, lukewarm/cold showers, weak water pressure, dirty floors, and past reports of rude staff treatment, though recent management has improved hospitality aspects significantly.
