PARK: Ingenia Holidays Sydney Hills
SCORE: 48/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-11
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 142

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Limited on-site entertainment with only a pool, tennis court, and playground mentioned. Multiple families complained the playground has "no shaded area and was way too hot for the kids to play," making it unusable during warm weather. No organized activities or kids club programs evident from reviews.

Nature (8/20) — Located in a tree-studded setting with "plenty of bird life" and some greenery, though reviewers noted it "gave snakey vibes" and one mentioned "lush greenery." However, tent sites face sheds and rubbish bins rather than natural settings. Not near beaches or significant nature attractions.

Cleanliness (5/15) — Significant and consistent cleanliness problems across reviews. Multiple families reported "atrociously dirty and unkempt amenities," "toilets were dirty," "mould on windows and bathroom ceiling," "cabin was not clean at all," "rooms were dirty and very dated," and amenities "appeared not to have had a good clean for quite awhile." Pool frequently "green and dirty."

Value (8/15) — Mixed feedback on value at approximately $200/night. Some found it "great value for money" and "cheap," while families with young children felt "way overcharged for what we got" given the dated facilities and cleanliness issues. Compared favourably price-wise to other Sydney Ingenia parks.

Site Size (3/10) — Major issue for families. Consistently described as "sites are too small," "very tight for larger vans," "not big enough to put our chairs out," "cramped," and "tiny." Multiple reviewers noted difficulty maneuvering caravans and inadequate space for awnings and vehicles.

Sentiment (5/10) — Highly polarized reviews with families either loving or hating their stay. Many stated "we will never return" and "won't be back," while others were repeat visitors. Staff consistently praised as "friendly and helpful," but overall experience marred by facility issues. Mix of 1-star and 5-star reviews.

Location (8/10) — Strong location score for Sydney access with bus stop out front connecting to train station, 15 minutes off M7, convenient to shops, and described as "perfect location." Good base for Blue Mountains and city visits (1 hour away). On busy main road but noise not intrusive.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe and consistent cleanliness issues across cabins, amenities, and pool - multiple families reported mould, dirty surfaces, green pool water, and "atrociously dirty" bathrooms that were clearly not being properly maintained or deep cleaned between guests.
2. Critically undersized sites unsuitable for families with caravans, awnings, and outdoor equipment - repeatedly described as "too small," "tight," and "cramped" with families unable to set up chairs or properly enjoy outdoor space.
3. Minimal kids entertainment infrastructure with an unusable playground (no shade, too hot), frequently unmaintained pool, no kids club or organized activities, and limited on-site amenities that fall well short of family holiday park expectations.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"atrociously dirty and unkempt amenities," "playground had no shaded area and was way too hot," "pool was green and dirty," "sites are too small," "tent area not good at all," "needs upgrading desperately," "mould on ceiling and around toilet," "kids loved the swimming pool"
