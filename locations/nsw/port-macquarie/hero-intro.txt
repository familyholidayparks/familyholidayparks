Port Macquarie is a solid choice if you're after a relaxed family getaway on the mid-north coast. The town boasts some gorgeous beaches – Flynns Beach and Town Beach are perfect for a swim, and the rock pools at Shelly Beach are ideal if the little ones want to explore rock pools safely. Beyond the sand, there's plenty to keep families entertained: the Sea Turtle Hospital lets kids get up close to marine wildlife, the Koala Hospital offers a genuine wildlife experience, and if the weather turns, there's the Port Macquarie Museum or the Regional Art Gallery. The natural setting is excellent too, with the Hastings River perfect for spotting dolphins and the surrounding bushland great for easy walks.

For accommodation, you've got some solid caravan parks in the area – NRMA Port Macquarie Breakwall Holiday Park sits right on the water, Flynns Beach Caravan Park is beachside and family-friendly, and Edgewater Holiday Park offers a quieter vibe with good amenities. What makes Port Macquarie work for families is the mix of beaches, nature and attractions without feeling too touristy or over-the-top. It's got enough going on to keep everyone occupied, but it's still the kind of place where you can actually relax and not feel like you're on a theme park schedule.
