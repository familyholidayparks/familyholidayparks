PARK: Gardens Caravan Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 122

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Multiple families noted "no jumping pillow, no playgrounds which are decent" with one review stating "the campground should have enough entertainment for the kids rather than relying on a book." The park features a football oval and open green spaces, but lacks dedicated kids' facilities like splash parks or adventure playgrounds. Families mentioned it's a "pretty much self serve" park without organized activities.

Nature (16/20) — Excellent natural setting with "walking distance to a spectacular beach" (3-5 minute walk to East Beach), sites "on the banks of the Moyne River" with beautiful water views, and location "close to botanical gardens." Reviews praised the "lush greenery and views" and "gorgeous pine trees for shade." Multiple families mentioned the stunning beach access and natural surroundings as major highlights.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Highly inconsistent ratings ranging from "amenities are spotless" and "very very clean" to "dirtiest amenities I've ever experienced" and "facilities are in really poor condition." Most recent 2026 reviews mention facilities "due to be renovated" with some noting "very dirty and water over the floor, showers dripping, toilets leaking." Older 2024 reviews include extreme complaints about mould and hygiene issues.

Value (11/15) — Generally considered reasonable with "$96 for 2x nights powered" and "good site fees," though some felt it was "a bit expensive for an off season site." Several reviews mentioned "great value" and "reasonably priced," but concerns about "fees go up, but there is no change to amenities." The pricing appears fair for the location but value is diminished by aging facilities.

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised with "huge grassy sites," "BIGGEST and most AMAZING spot I have ever come across," and "large sites" mentioned frequently. Multiple reviews highlight "plenty of room for the caravan and car" and "such a spacious caravan park." Sites along the river particularly noted as enormous with excellent spacing and privacy.

Sentiment (8/10) — Mixed sentiment with many repeat visitors ("been going to gardens for 9 years" and "I've been here every year of my life") expressing love for the park, while others left disappointed. High recommendation rate from satisfied guests but notable complaints about declining standards from long-term visitors. Overall positive but with concerning negative outliers.

Location (10/10) — Outstanding location scores with "perfect location," "short walk to beautiful East Beach," "easy walk into town" (10-15 minutes), and proximity to shops, cafes, and attractions consistently praised. Described as "central position in historic Port Fairy" with beach access across the road and town amenities within walking distance.

WHY NOT 100:
The park offers virtually no on-site children's entertainment facilities, with multiple families specifically noting the absence of jumping pillows, decent playgrounds, or proper camp kitchen facilities with basic appliances - critical amenities expected by modern families with young children. Cleanliness standards have been alarmingly inconsistent, with some 2024-2026 reviews describing facilities as filthy with maintenance issues like leaking toilets, dripping showers, and poor hygiene, though renovations are planned. While the huge sites and excellent location are strengths, the lack of family-focused amenities and unreliable facility maintenance significantly limit the park's appeal for families seeking a comprehensive holiday park experience.
