PARK: Pelican Waters Holiday Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-23
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 23

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Reviews consistently highlighted excellent on-site entertainment with alpacas and chickens that kids can feed, a playground, giant trampoline, and free sporting equipment. The unique train carriage accommodation was specifically mentioned as something 'kids would love' and created memorable family experiences. Multiple families noted plenty of space for children to play and described it as 'children's heaven'.

Nature (18/20) — The park sits alongside the Moyne River with beautiful water views and abundant birdlife. Families praised the peaceful, tranquil setting and direct access to the rail trail. The park is a 5-minute walk to the beach and provides excellent access to Port Fairy's coastal environment. The presence of farm animals adds to the natural experience.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Reviews unanimously praised cleanliness with repeated mentions of 'spotless', 'very clean', and 'well maintained' cabins. Multiple guests commented on the exceptional maintenance standards and well-stocked, well-equipped accommodations throughout their stays.

Value (13/15) — While guests consistently praised the quality and experience, with one reviewer stating this was 'superior' to other 5-star properties, there were no specific mentions of exceptional value or pricing. The well-equipped cabins and facilities suggest good value, though one review mentioned booking directly may be better.

Site Size (9/10) — Reviews noted 'plenty of space for kids to play', 'good amount of room in cabin', and spacious grounds. The presence of various accommodation types including cabins, apartments, and train carriages suggests adequate site sizing, though no specific mention of large powered sites for big rigs.

Sentiment (10/10) — Overwhelmingly positive sentiment with 21 of 23 reviews giving 5 stars. Multiple families described themselves as 'boomerang guests' returning annually, with consistent phrases like 'we will be back', 'can't wait to return', and 'definitely recommend'. The host Julie received exceptional praise across all reviews.

Location (10/10) — Perfect location score with reviews consistently praising the 5-minute walk to town centre, shops, cafes, beach, and wharf area. Direct access to the rail trail and proximity to all Port Fairy attractions while maintaining a peaceful, quiet setting was repeatedly highlighted.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited structured entertainment facilities - while the alpacas, playground and trampoline are great, there's no pool, games room, or organized kids' activities that many top-tier family parks offer.
2. No value pricing mentions - despite high quality, no reviews specifically mentioned affordability or exceptional value for families on a budget, suggesting pricing may be mid-to-premium range.
3. Cabins lack individual ovens requiring use of communal facilities, which while adequate, is less convenient for families wanting full self-contained accommodation.
