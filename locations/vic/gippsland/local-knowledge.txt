G'day! Gippsland's stunning mix of pristine beaches, ancient rainforests, and the incredible Ninety Mile Beach makes it one of Victoria's most diverse regions for a family holiday park stay. We love visiting during autumn (March-May) when the crowds thin out, the weather stays beautifully mild, and you can actually spot wombats wandering near your cabin at dusk in places like Wilsons Promontory. Here's a local tip that'll save you money: grab your fresh fish and chips from the co-op in Lakes Entrance rather than the tourist strip – the locals queue there for a reason, and you'll pay half the price for twice the serving.
