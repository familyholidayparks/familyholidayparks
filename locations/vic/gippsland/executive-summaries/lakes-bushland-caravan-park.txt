PARK: Lakes Bushland Caravan Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-03-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 58

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently mention pool, playground, tennis court, table tennis, giant chess, and dog park as key features. Multiple families noted "lots for kids to do" and children enjoying the play facilities, though entertainment is more basic outdoor play rather than organized activities or elaborate facilities. No waterslides or jumping pillows, but adequate for active families.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional bushland setting with pristine natural environment. Reviews repeatedly praised "beautiful bushland," "gorgeous bird life," "magpies and parrots," wildlife viewing, and peaceful natural surroundings. Close proximity to Raymond Island for koala spotting. Only minor point deduction as it's inland rather than beachfront, 20-25 minutes from Lakes Entrance coast.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Consistently positive feedback about cleanliness with amenities described as "extremely clean," "spotless," "super clean," and "kept spotless." Camp kitchen and bathrooms particularly praised. Some reviews mentioned amenities as "older" or needing "refreshing/updating," though clean and functional. Lost points for occasional management issues and one noise complaint.

Value (14/15) — Outstanding value with pricing cited at $29 per night for powered sites. Multiple reviews called it "best value for money around," "really affordable," "excellent value," and "one of the few honest fares left in Australia." Reviewers noted you'd "pay more than double for a park of this quality" elsewhere. Strong value proposition for families.

Site Size (9/10) — Reviews consistently praised "huge sites," "very spacious," "large campsites," "ample room for your van & vehicle parking," and "so much space." Powered sites described as "bowling green flat with manicured grass." Excellent space for families with larger rigs and multiple vehicles.

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally very positive with many repeat visitors and strong recommendations. However, sentiment impacted by several 1-star reviews citing poor customer service, staff availability issues, noise problems from permanent residents, and management complaints. Recent reviews show management changes. When service is good, guests love it; inconsistency is the issue.

Location (6/10) — Good location for exploring Gippsland region and accessing Raymond Island, but 20-25 minutes inland from Lakes Entrance beaches. Described as "out of town," "off the main road," and "hidden gem" location. Perfect for bushland camping but not ideal for families prioritizing beach access. Trade-off between tranquility and coastal proximity.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Inconsistent management and customer service with multiple complaints about staff unavailability, self-check-in only, cranky phone responses, and lack of evening supervision to enforce quiet hours when permanent residents caused noise issues.
2. Limited organized entertainment - facilities are good but basic (pool, playground, tennis) without the waterslides, jumping pillows, kids' clubs, or organized activities found at premium family parks.
3. Inland location 20-25 minutes from beaches means families wanting beach access need to drive, and amenities described as older/functional rather than modern, though clean.
