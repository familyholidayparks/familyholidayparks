PARK: Loch Sport Holiday Park (Caravan & Camping Ground)
SCORE: 61/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 30

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note very limited playground facilities with one family stating the "play equipment for kids is extremely limited" and children found it "dangerous." One positive review mentions kids were "close to the playground and a place to swim" but another specifically requested the play area "could be a little bigger and accommodating to all ages." No evidence of organized activities, kids' clubs, or entertainment programs.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting with multiple reviews praising "abundant wildlife, beautiful lake views, endless beaches" and "beautiful coastal bush setting." Located between Lake Victoria and Ninety Mile Beach with "beautiful birdlife and kangaroos" visible throughout. Reviewers mention "cute little beaches on walk tracks" and "lots of nearby animals." Beach fishing described as "phenomenal" with easy lake access.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Strong cleanliness marks with repeated mentions of "extremely clean," "pristine amenities," and "very clean" facilities. Multiple reviews specifically thank staff for keeping "everything so clean and neat." Recent reviews maintain consistent praise for "clean amenities" though one 2024 review called facilities "outdated."

Value (9/15) — Mixed value perception with one review stating "Not worth the 60.00 per night" and calling it "outdated." However, positive mentions include "cheap coin laundry" and families planning return visits suggesting reasonable value for nature-focused stays. Limited on-site entertainment may impact perceived value for families expecting more activities.

Site Size (9/10) — Very positive feedback on space with "huge sites," "camping site big," and "sand and grass patch with lots of room to set up." Reviewers appreciated the spaciousness though some noted leveling required "a little bit of patience."

Sentiment (7/10) — Generally positive with many 5-star reviews and repeat visitors, but severely impacted by one family's 1-star experience involving "drunk strange adults" approaching children and permanent residents creating unsafe conditions. Other families report becoming "a favourite spot" and planning "Easter tradition" returns. The safety incident significantly affects family-specific sentiment.

Location (9/10) — Excellent positioning "between Lake Victoria and the famous Ninety Mile Beach" with "easy walk to the lake foreshore" and "couple minutes drive to Ninety Mile Beach." Walking distance to general store, pub, and RSL. Well-located for water activities though the remote Gippsland location may challenge accessibility for some.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Playground facilities are severely lacking with equipment described as "extremely limited," "dangerous," and "not accommodating to all ages" - a critical deficit for families with young children seeking active play options.
2. One family reported a serious safety incident involving intoxicated permanent residents approaching their young children, verbal assault, and threatening behaviour, which management attributed to permanent park residents without adequate resolution.
3. Mosquitoes and flies are persistently problematic across multiple seasons with reviews warning they "can be out of control," are "relentless," and one reviewer noting management's dismissive "what do you expect we are in the bush" response rather than offering solutions.
