G'day families! Ballarat and its surrounds offer that perfect sweet spot of history and nature – your kids can pan for gold at Sovereign Hill in the morning, then be swimming in a heated pool at your holiday park by afternoon. Winter here is absolutely magic (yes, even with the chill!) because the town transforms for the Ballarat Winter Festival, and you'll find holiday parks surprisingly quiet mid-week during school term. Here's a local tip most visitors miss: grab your free Parks Victoria pass from the Ballarat Visitor Centre, then head to Creswick Regional Park – it's just 20 minutes north and your kids can fossick for actual gold in the creeks without the Sovereign Hill ticket price.
