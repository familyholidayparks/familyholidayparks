PARK: BIG4 Kryal Castle Holiday Park - Ballarat
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2025
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 76

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews consistently praised Kryal Castle access with "great shows & entertainment", "jousting is fun", "kids loved the animals", face painting, potion classes, and medieval-themed activities. However, the caravan park itself has "no playground", "no pool or spas", "nothing for kids to do" outside castle hours, and "no play equipment or spaces for kids to play".

Nature (10/20) — Guests appreciated "great views overlooking Ballarat", "awesome view", and "soft grass" at some sites. Location offers countryside setting with horses in paddocks. However, many reviews describe the site as feeling like "a building site" with "construction stuff everywhere, rubbles, rusty iron stuff", detracting significantly from natural enjoyment.

Cleanliness (3/15) — This is the park's most critical weakness. Multiple recent reviews report "toilets and showers were disgusting - poo on floor, toilet seats half hanging off", "no cleaning or emptying", "blood stained sheets", "hair on towels and bedsheets", "black mold over bathroom ceiling", "carpets are stained and dirty with cobwebs", "floor was greasy and sticky", and "not once across 4 nights were amenities cleaned".

Value (5/15) — Highly mixed sentiment. Positive reviews noted "great value inclusive of entry into Kryal Castle", "very reasonably priced", and "extremely good value for money with entry and breakfast included". However, many felt "completely ripped off", cited "$72 per night" as poor value given limited facilities, "$300 a night" for tiny homes with issues, and "this is the WORST experience of all" for a Big4 park.

Site Size (4/10) — Significant negative feedback: "sites are small and poor designed", "had to take up 2 sites", "no room to open our awning", "cabin was extremely tiny", "rooms themselves are cramped", and "family room was very VERY small". Positive mentions included "sites are spacious" and "site was plenty big enough for a 10p tent" from some tent campers.

Sentiment (3/10) — Overwhelmingly negative overall sentiment with many one-star reviews. Common phrases: "worst Big4 experience ever", "DO NOT book your holiday here", "we won't be going back", "lasted literally 2 hours and left", "we lasted 2 nights out of 3 booked", "would visit again but wouldn't stay there". Major issues with last-minute booking cancellations affecting multiple families. Positive reviews mainly from 2024-2026 focusing on castle experience.

Location (5/10) — Reviews praised "great location overlooking Ballarat", "easy access from freeway", and "easy to get to all sites around Ballarat". Castle adjacency is the main draw. However, "reception is actually IN Kryal Castle, which took a while to find", poor signage, "no map of caravan park", and confusion about facilities detracted from location benefits.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe and consistent cleanliness failures across amenities, accommodations, and facilities, with multiple reports of unsanitary bathrooms, uncleaned rooms, and health hazards like black mold and blood-stained sheets that are completely unacceptable for a Big4 standard park.

2. The park appears to have operated while still under construction with building materials, safety hazards, "out of order" signs on most facilities, inadequate camp kitchen ("one cook top, ONE single sink for 100 people"), no playground, no pool, creating a fundamentally incomplete holiday park experience.

3. Systemic operational failures including last-minute booking cancellations (some just 6 days before arrival), poor communication, inadequate staff training, inconsistent facility availability, and management that appears unresponsive to serious complaints, severely damaging trust and reliability.
