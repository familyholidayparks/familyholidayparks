G'day! If you're after a proper family holiday that combines pristine beaches, bushwalking, and wildlife spotting without the crowds, Inverloch and the surrounding Bass Coast absolutely deliver. We love visiting during the shoulder seasons of late spring and early autumn when the weather's still beautiful but the penguins at nearby Point Smythe are putting on their nightly show for smaller audiences. Here's a local tip that'll save you a bundle: grab your fresh fish and chips from the Inverloch Fish Co-op right on the jetty rather than the main street shops – it's where the locals go, the prices are better, and the kids can feed the pelicans while you wait.
