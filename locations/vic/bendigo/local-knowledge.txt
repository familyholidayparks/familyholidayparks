G'day families! Bendigo and its surrounding regions offer that perfect sweet spot for a Victorian getaway – you're close enough for an easy drive from Melbourne (under two hours), yet far enough to feel like you've properly escaped to the country. The cooler months from March to May are absolutely magic here, when the kids can explore the historic gold mining sites without melting in the heat, and the autumn colours around Lake Weeroona are stunning for those family bike rides. Here's an insider tip: grab a Bendigo Joss House Temple family pass before you arrive – the kids go nuts for the dragon stories, and having your ticket sorted means you can pop in during that tricky late afternoon window when everyone's getting a bit ratty and needs something engaging to do before dinner.
