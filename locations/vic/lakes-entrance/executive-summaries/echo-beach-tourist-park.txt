PARK: Echo Beach Tourist Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-02-18
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 45

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews mention pool, playground, trampoline, and petting animals (rabbits, guinea pigs) that kids enjoyed, but entertainment is basic with limited structured activities; several families noted "kids loved the bunnies" and appreciated the "trampoline and basketballs for the kids" though overall offerings are modest for extended stays.

Nature (14/20) — Located in Lakes Entrance with "short walk to beach" and "closeness to the beach" mentioned positively; described as "quiet" and "one street back from the hustle and bustle" but limited evidence of significant natural bush setting or nature-based activities beyond beach proximity.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Highly polarized with recent positive reviews praising "very clean, well maintained" and "super clean rooms" contrasted sharply against multiple complaints of "ants everywhere," "stained/dirty fabric surfaces," "mouse poop in cutlery draw," "dust everywhere," and "revolting stains on the mattress," indicating significant inconsistency across cabins.

Value (10/15) — Described as "very reasonable price" and "affordable" with some acknowledging "you get what you pay for"; mixed value perception with budget-conscious families satisfied but others feeling cabins didn't justify cost given maintenance issues and basic amenities.

Site Size (6/10) — Consistently described as "very small park" with limited evidence of spacious sites; no specific commentary on caravan/RV site dimensions though described as compact and quiet with cabins close together.

Sentiment (4/10) — Deeply divided sentiment with loyal repeat guests ("10 years staying," "many times a year for many years") versus extremely negative experiences; multiple 1-star reviews from 2023-2024 citing cleanliness, service issues, and complaints about "not kids friendly," significantly damaging overall family sentiment score.

Location (8/10) — Strong location score with "walking distance from everything in town," "walk to everything," "short walk to beach and shops," and "very convenient location" frequently mentioned; proximity to Lakes Entrance attractions and beach is a key strength.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness inconsistency with multiple detailed complaints about pest issues (ants, bed bugs, mouse droppings), stained bedding, dust buildup, and poor cabin hygiene that contradict positive reviews and create unacceptable risk for families.
2. Limited kids entertainment infrastructure with only basic playground, small pool, and petting animals—no waterpark, jumping pillow, games room, or structured activities programs that families expect from quality holiday parks.
3. Significant service and management issues documented in 2023-2024 reviews including confrontational owner behavior, complaints about families with children being unwelcome, poor communication, and multiple reports of "rude" interactions that severely damage family-friendliness reputation.
