PARK: North Arm Tourist Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-03-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 29

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Limited entertainment with only a small swimming pool mentioned; kids used the pool even in cold/windy weather, but one family noted confusion about age requirements and called it "really small"; no playground, kids club, or organized activities mentioned in any reviews.

Nature (14/20) — Excellent waterfront position directly across the road from North Arm estuary with water views; easy access to lake, boats, and fishing spots; "great spot for a fish"; opposite water and nice lawns; however, reviewers noted it's "bit hard to just walk over to beach" despite close proximity.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Highly inconsistent cleanliness scores; multiple recent reviews from peak periods report "facilities were disgusting and dirty", "amenities were so dirty" with "no mop, wipe down" during Melbourne Cup weekend; off-peak reviews describe "clean facilities" and "amenities oldish but clean and maintained"; floors need retiling and facilities described as "looking a bit tired".

Value (12/15) — Good value noted with "reasonable prices", "rates reasonable for off season", "cheap powered sites"; families appreciated the pricing for the location; "fees reasonable" mentioned multiple times; good balance of cost versus location benefits.

Site Size (3/10) — Major limitation with multiple reviews warning "sites are quite small, not recommended if your van is over 19ft"; "spots are tiny and extremely tight"; "van sites not large"; "as big as a postage stamp"; "would be hard for large vans to park in"; consistently cited as a significant constraint.

Sentiment (7/10) — Mixed sentiment with strong positive feelings about managers and location offset by cleanliness concerns; many say "will definitely visit again" and "would not hesitate to stay again" particularly for off-peak; however, several recent reviews state "won't stay again" due to cleanliness issues; manager receives praise as "friendly, helpful and engaging" but one negative review claims manager was "rude".

Location (10/10) — Outstanding location scores with "amazing spot with everything in walking distance"; across from estuary, "5 minute walk to shops", "short walk to Aldi", close to pub and restaurants; "perfect location to enjoy all Lakes Entrance has to offer"; easy access to both water and township.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severely limited site sizes make the park unsuitable for larger family rigs over 19ft, with multiple reviews describing spots as "tiny and extremely tight" or "as big as a postage stamp", significantly restricting who can stay comfortably.
2. Critical cleanliness failures during peak family holiday periods (Melbourne Cup weekend, January) with multiple families reporting "disgusting and dirty" facilities, inadequate cleaning routines, and showers that "looked like they have not been cleaned for a long time".
3. Minimal on-site entertainment for children beyond a small pool with no playground, kids activities, games room, or organized entertainment mentioned, leaving families dependent on leaving the park for activities.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
