PARK: Koonwarra Family Holiday Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-09
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 98

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Reviews consistently note the pool is "very small" with "limited or no use" and time restrictions. The games room requires payment for individual games, and the playground is described as "old and not maintained" with "rotted wood and lots of weeds." The arcade "kept kids busy" but "could be upgraded" with some free games. Multiple families report "very less kids activities/playing area."

Nature (14/20) — The park is "across the road from the 'lake'" with "lovely walk" access and "close to walking paths and play areas." Guests enjoy the "lush greenery" and "natural feel" with "beautiful short stroll" to see wildlife and seals. Beach access is approximately "20 mins" walk via the footbridge to 90 Mile Beach, and the location offers "heaps of things to do around the area."

Cleanliness (11/15) — Recent reviews show polarization: many praise "spotless" cabins that are "extremely clean" with "sparkling clean" amenities, while others report "filthy, run-down" conditions, "smelly dirty outdated cabin," and insect infestations. The park shows "signs of being dated/tired" with facilities needing updates, though cleaning staff receive praise when present.

Value (12/15) — Frequently described as "best budget-friendly" with "fantastic price" and "very reasonable" rates. Special deals like "2 nights get 1 free" are appreciated. However, some guests feel rooms are too basic for the price, and extra charges (games room, cleaning fees) detract from value perception.

Site Size (5/10) — Multiple reviews cite "very small sites" where "cars parked in front it extremely tight to get in and out." Guests report vans had to "ask patrons to move cars to get hitched." Some note "generous sized sites" and "plenty of room," suggesting inconsistency across the park. Parking arrangements block cabin entrances making "getting a pram difficult."

Sentiment (6/10) — Mixed sentiment with polarized experiences. Positive reviews praise staff (especially Monica and Bill) and cleanliness, with repeat visitors and cabin owners. However, significant negative reviews cite "abusive" management, "controlling" behavior, racist treatment, and "terrible operators who are control freaks." Management style creates friction with some guests.

Location (4/10) — Located "2 mins drive to the centre of Lakes entrance" and "1km from town centre," requiring walking or driving to attractions. While "close to everything" with good access to the waterfront walk and footbridge, it's "bit out of the shopping area" and not truly central for families wanting walkable access to town amenities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Kids' entertainment facilities are severely outdated and inadequate — the playground has "rotted wood," the pool is "very small" with limited use, and the games room requires payment for each game, leaving families with "very less kids activities" on-site.
2. Site size and layout issues create significant practical problems, with "very small sites" where cars block access and families struggle to maneuver, requiring other guests to move vehicles.
3. Management approach and cleanliness consistency are major concerns, with multiple serious complaints about "controlling" and "abusive" behavior, plus stark cleanliness variations from "spotless" to "filthy" and "infested with insects."
