G'day! Wilsons Prom and the surrounding South Gippsland region is where my own kids first fell in love with camping – there's something magical about waking up to kookaburras and spending your days between pristine beaches and rainforest walks that even teenagers will admit are "pretty cool". The shoulder seasons of autumn and spring are absolutely perfect here, with milder weather for hiking and far fewer crowds than the packed summer holidays. Here's a local tip that'll save you hassle: if you're planning to explore the Prom itself, book your Tidal River accommodation or day pass months ahead, but stay at one of the excellent holiday parks in nearby towns like Foster or Fish Creek – you'll get better availability, often better facilities, and you're still only 30-40 minutes from the park gates.
