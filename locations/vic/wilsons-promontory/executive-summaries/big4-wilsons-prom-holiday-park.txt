PARK: BIG4 Wilsons Prom Holiday Park
SCORE: 73/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-25
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 405

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note minimal on-site entertainment with only a basic playground, basketball hoop, and occasional movie nights or coffee vans; families specifically mention missing typical BIG4 facilities like jumping pillows or pools, with one reviewer stating "the only thing missing was one of those giant jumping pillows" and another noting "no jumping pillow, pool etc....very very small camp kitchen."

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural environment with reviews praising "stunning waterfront location on Corner Inlet," "spectacular sunrise views," "peaceful bush setting with established vegetation screening sites," "abundant wildlife including black swans, kangaroos, wombats," "beautiful banksia forest walks," and "direct beach access" with the park serving as the perfect gateway to Wilsons Promontory National Park just 20-30 minutes away.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Reviews are mixed: many praise "clean amenities," "well maintained," "spotlessly clean" facilities with regular cleaning schedules posted, but others note "amenities need upgrading," "toilet blocks are outdated," "older amenity block needs facelift," and occasional complaints during peak periods about insufficient toilets and cleanliness issues, with one noting "only 6 male toilets for all sites and cabins."

Value (10/15) — Significant concerns about rising prices with multiple reviews mentioning "getting expensive," "$149/night for waterfront site," "campsites gone up $15 per night in two years," "$70 for powered site without water not value for money," "$380 per night for cabin absolutely ridiculous," and "pricing not representative of facilities" with families feeling "priced out" of a previously loved destination.

Site Size (10/10) — Outstanding praise for site sizes with reviews consistently using terms like "enormous," "huge," "very spacious," "generous," "big enough to fit caravan, car and tent," "roomy sites with privacy," "never seen sites as big as these," and "lovely to spread out rather than be packed in like sardines" with natural bush screening providing excellent privacy between sites.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strongly positive overall sentiment with many families returning multiple times and already rebooking, phrases like "will definitely be back," "loved our stay," "can't wait to return," "one of our favourites," though some loyal long-term visitors express disappointment over recent price increases and management changes affecting their ability to return.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location praised as "perfect base for Wilsons Prom," "only 20-30 minutes to Tidal River and park entrance," "right on Corner Inlet waterfront," "peaceful setting away from crowds," though some note it's "long way from town amenities" and "remote with limited phone reception" which can be either positive or negative depending on preferences.

WHY NOT 100:
1. **Minimal on-site entertainment** - The park lacks typical BIG4 facilities that families with young children expect, with no pool, water park, jumping pillow, mini golf, or organized kids' activities beyond a basic playground and basketball area, limiting entertainment for families staying multiple days.

2. **Aging amenities infrastructure** - Multiple reviews note that older toilet/shower blocks need renovation with complaints about "tired amenities," "outdated facilities," "insufficient toilets for peak periods," "no family bathrooms," and facilities that don't meet modern BIG4 standards, though one amenity block has been recently renovated.

3. **Significant value concerns** - Families express frustration that prices have risen substantially ("tripled" according to some) without corresponding improvements to facilities, with powered sites lacking water, no dump point on site, limited camp kitchen capacity, and pricing that many feel exceeds what's offered compared to other parks or even the national park campground at Tidal River.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
