PARK: Toora Tourist Park
SCORE: 83/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 80

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (19/20) — Outstanding range praised universally: heated indoor pool and spa, two playgrounds, jumping pillow, trampolines, mini golf, tennis/basketball courts, games room with huge DVD library, table tennis. Families report kids "never ran out of things to do" and many stayed on-site their entire holiday. One point deducted for some games being "a little outdated" and limited pool hours in winter.

Nature (16/20) — Located 30 minutes from Wilsons Promontory with beautiful autumn foliage and scenic hill views. Close proximity to Agnes Falls, Port Welshpool pier, Great Southern Rail Trail (750m). Park described as "sheltered from wind" with "nice greenery" but the town of Toora itself has limited natural attractions. Lost points for being a base for nature rather than immersed in it.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently praised as "one of the cleanest parks we've ever seen" with "very clean amenities" and "immaculate" facilities. Camp kitchen and bathrooms specifically highlighted. Modern amenities with bathmats and good water pressure. One negative review cited maintenance issues, and a few mentioned minor problems (shower flooding, broken clothesline wires) preventing perfect score.

Value (13/15) — Described as "much better value than Inverloch Big 4" and "competitive pricing for holiday season." All facilities included free with stay. However, one reviewer complained about $92/night during long weekend with 3-night minimum being "price gouging." Generally considered excellent value but peak pricing affects score.

Site Size (7/10) — Multiple reviews mention "narrow powered sites," "cramped sites," "very small caravan sites" and "sardine city." Some sites not perfectly level. Drive-through sites available. Cabins praised as "most spacious in Victoria" but camping/caravan sites consistently described as smaller than ideal, affecting the score significantly.

Sentiment (9/10) — Exceptionally strong with numerous "will definitely be back" and "highly recommend" statements. Described as "top 3 stays in all of Australia" and many repeat visitors. Owners Jenny and Marshall praised by name. Minor deductions for two 1-star reviews citing maintenance and appliance issues, and noise complaints about barking dogs and roosters.

Location (5/10) — Walking distance to small town with IGA, pub, cafe, pharmacy. Easy drive to Wilsons Prom (30 min), Foster (10 min), Agnes Falls, and Port Welshpool. However, Toora itself "doesn't have much" and "most sightseeing places more than 1 hour drive." Not beachfront. Good as a touring base but not a destination location, limiting score.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site sizes are consistently described as narrow, cramped, and small for caravans/campers, with some levelling issues, preventing higher scores for families with larger rigs.
2. Location in small Toora town with limited local attractions requires driving 30+ minutes for major sights, and is not waterfront/beachfront despite being marketed near Wilsons Promontory.
3. Maintenance inconsistencies noted in several reviews including broken appliances, faulty shower roses, non-working WiFi/TV, and aging facilities, though generally well-maintained overall.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"top 3 stays in all of Australia", "kids never ran out of fun things to do", "didn't need to leave the park", "one of the cleanest parks we've ever seen", "much better value than Inverloch Big 4", "owners went above and beyond", "caravan sites are very small", "sardine city cramped sites"
