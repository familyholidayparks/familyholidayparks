# Lorne, Victoria – A Family Holiday Worth the Drive

Lorne is a proper seaside town on Victoria's famous Great Ocean Road, and it ticks the boxes for families looking to mix beach time with bush walking. The main beach is patrolled by lifeguards during summer, with gentle waves that suit younger swimmers, while Lorikeet Beach offers rockpools perfect for kids to explore. Beyond the sand, the hinterland is packed with walking tracks through tall timber and fern gullies – some short enough for little legs, others better for parents needing a proper stretch. Popular spots include Sheoak Falls and Erskine Falls, where you can swim in freshwater pools on warmer days.

The town itself is manageable for families without being too touristy, with fish and chips shops, cafes, and a decent range of accommodation including the Lorne Foreshore Caravan Park for those after budget-friendly beachfront stays. You'll find enough to keep kids entertained – rock hopping, spotting local wildlife like kookaburras and wallabies, and having a hit at mini golf – without the crowds you'd get at more famous destinations. It's the kind of place where you can actually relax while the kids burn off energy, and you're positioned perfectly for a day trip along the Great Ocean Road if you fancy it.
