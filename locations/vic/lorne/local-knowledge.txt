G'day! The stretch of coast around Lorne is absolutely magic for families – where else can you spot koalas snoozing in the manna gums right above your cabin, then walk five minutes to patrolled surf beaches? We've found autumn (March-May) is the sweet spot for visiting, when the summer crowds have thinned but the weather's still lovely and the rock pools at Teddy's Lookout are teeming with life. Here's an insider tip: grab your fish and chips from Lorne Beach Pavilion on a Wednesday or Thursday evening when the local cockatoos put on their cheeky sunset show along the foreshore – it's become our family's favourite tradition, and it won't cost you a cent beyond your dinner.
