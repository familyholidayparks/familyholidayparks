PARK: Amaroo Park Phillip Island
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 153

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Reviews note limited on-site entertainment with only a basic pool and no dedicated playground; one review mentioned a games room but very few families commented on kids' activities; "the only thing we could think of that would improve this place would be if there was a play park for the little kids"

Nature (12/20) — Location on Phillip Island provides access to natural attractions like Penguin Parade (10 mins drive), beaches, and wildlife including rabbits hopping around the property; walking distance to Cowes beach; however the park grounds themselves show poor maintenance with overgrown gardens and weeds

Cleanliness (4/15) — Overwhelming evidence of severe decline since ownership change in 2024/2025; "amenities block was consistently dirty throughout our stay", "black mould covering the window seals", "pool was green", "dirty undies under bed", "floors have not been cleaned very well at all", "hairy shower", "food on wall, stains on couch"; consistent reports of uncleaned amenities, bins overflowing, and lack of attention to detail

Value (5/15) — Multiple reviews cite poor value with high prices for declining standards; "$240 per night" for basic cabin with maintenance issues, "$330/night" described as "rip off", "$220 per night for a powered site is absolutely ludicrous"; pricing not justified by current condition or facilities

Site Size (7/10) — Powered sites described as "good size" and "spacious camping sites"; however some sites have dirt instead of grass and ditches that caused guests to trip; "larger sites leave you stepping out on to dirt"

Sentiment (3/10) — Sharp division between older reviews (pre-2024) praising the park and recent reviews (2024-2026) expressing extreme disappointment; "since the change in management the overall standard has declined significantly", "this is the worst run park we have ever stayed at", "would rather sleep in my car"; very low repeat visitation intent from recent guests; multiple reports of management unresponsiveness to complaints

Location (7/10) — Consistently praised as "great location", "walking distance to shops, beach, restaurants", "right behind Coles", "10 min walk to the beach", "close to all Phillip Island activities"; central Cowes location is the park's strongest asset

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness and maintenance decline under new management since 2024/2025 with consistent reports of dirty amenities, mould, uncleaned cabins, green pool, overflowing bins, and poor grounds maintenance
2. Complete absence of on-site kids entertainment, activities or playground facilities making it unsuitable for families seeking entertainment beyond location
3. Poor customer service and management responsiveness with multiple reports of rude staff, communication barriers, unresponsiveness to complaints, and unprofessional behaviour including guests being "kicked out" mid-dispute
