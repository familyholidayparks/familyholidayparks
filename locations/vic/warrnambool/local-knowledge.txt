G'day! Warrnambool's stunning Shipwreck Coast offers families that perfect mix of wildlife encounters and beach adventures – where else can you watch Southern Right whales from the shore between June and September, then pop over to Tower Hill to spot emus and koalas on the same afternoon? The shoulder seasons of late spring and early autumn are absolute gold here: mild weather, quieter beaches, and the kids can actually enjoy the rock pools at Logan's Beach without fighting the summer crowds. Here's an insider tip – grab fish and chips from Whalers Inn on a Friday arvo and eat them at the Stingray Bay playground while the little ones burn off energy; the locals have been doing this for years, and you'll see why.
