PARK: Capel Caravan Park
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-03-25
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 37

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Reviews consistently mention the "good size pool for kids and adults," spa, "well-equipped games room," and playground. Families report "kids had so much fun" and "kids had a blast playing the games," with spontaneous cricket matches adding to community atmosphere. Limited structured activities compared to larger parks.

Nature (17/20) — "Beach is just 5-7 mins walking distance" via scenic "gravel road with no cars that runs alongside a small creek." Guests enjoyed "amazing sunset and fairy floss skies over Port Phillip Bay" and describe proximity to "breathtaking nature reserves and beaches." The Capel foreshore provides beautiful bay access with warmer water than nearby Phillip Island.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding across all recent reviews: "toilets and showers so clean all the time," "facilities are the cleanest we have ever seen," "impeccably clean," "very clean amenities." Bins emptied daily, grass mowed regularly, amenities "well taken care off" under current management.

Value (14/15) — Multiple reviews highlight "good price," "cheaper than anywhere else," and "reasonable price." Families appreciate the affordability for the quality delivered, though no specific pricing details provided in reviews.

Site Size (8/10) — "Unpowered spot was quite big" fitting "car, swag and gazebo comfortably." Sites described as "lovely thick soft grass" with space for "two tents (and cars!)" though no mention of extra-large sites for bigger rigs.

Sentiment (10/10) — Exceptional loyalty with numerous "will definitely visit again" and "we'll definitely be back" statements. Under Anthony and Sarah's management (2025-2026), reviews show overwhelmingly positive sentiment: "by far the friendliest and most accommodating staff" from travelers across Australia. Strong repeat visitation indicated.

Location (8/10) — "Perfect base for exploring the Peninsula" with "Rosebud Plaza less than 5 minutes drive" and "Capel Sound shops and beach less than 10 minutes walk." Walking distance to cafés and restaurants. Good accessibility though not in a premium beachfront position.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Facilities are "a bit dated" and "old but still very functional" — while clean and well-maintained, the infrastructure lacks modern amenities and contemporary design that families increasingly expect.
2. Limited entertainment variety with only basic facilities (pool, games room, playground) and no organized kids' clubs, jumping pillows, waterslides, or structured activity programs mentioned.
3. Only "one camp kitchen" noted by reviewers with "no cooktop" (BBQ only), limiting meal preparation options for families during busy periods or poor weather.
