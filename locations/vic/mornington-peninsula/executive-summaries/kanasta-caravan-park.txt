PARK: Kanasta Caravan Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 68

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently mention a games room with pool table, ping pong table, and coin-operated arcade games that kids enjoy, plus a playground better suited to older children. One 2-year-old still enjoyed it, but parents noted it's "definitely designed for older kids." No pool or major entertainment facilities, but enough to keep kids occupied on rainy days.

Nature (15/20) — Located 600-800m from Rye beach and pier with "lots of fishys" for fishing. Families appreciated the quiet, peaceful setting "tucked away" in a residential area. Close proximity to Peninsula Hot Springs (4-10 min drive) is frequently mentioned. The park features plants and succulents throughout, but limited natural bush setting. Beach access is the main nature drawcard.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed results with strong recency bias toward concerns. Many positive reviews cite "super clean," "spotless," and "incredibly clean bathrooms" that appear newly renovated. However, recent 2024-25 reviews flag issues: "toilet rim not cleaned for a long time," "furniture very badly stained," "ants on cushions," "exhaust fan thick with dust," and games room "smells of urine." Cleanliness appears inconsistent between cabins.

Value (12/15) — Several families mention "good value" and "affordable stay," with prices around $165-176 per night. Positive comments on "great value" and "decent priced basic stay." However, one reviewer felt it was "way too expensive for expectation" at $176/night given the basic facilities and close quarters. Pet-friendly policy adds value for dog owners who save on kennels.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant concerns about space throughout reviews. Multiple mentions of "small park," "bit small and tight," "space is at a premium here," and "very close proximity to others." One reviewer felt like "an intruder amongst a close knit community of perm. residents." Cabins described as "small" and "perfect size" depending on expectations, but clearly compact. Only one car park per cabin noted as limitation.

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive with strong repeat visitor intentions from many families. Common phrases: "will definitely stay again," "we will absolutely be back," "would definitely come back." Staff praised as "lovely," "friendly and helpful," "go above and beyond." However, sentiment dampened by some significant negative experiences (no hot water, couldn't contact staff, security concerns) and several 3-star reviews from families who "wouldn't choose to stay here again."

Location (9/10) — Excellent location scores highly. Described as "walking distance to the Main Street of Rye," "10 minute walk to beach and shops," "close to Peninsula Hot Springs," and "600 metres to both sea & shops." Families could walk to pub for meals. Quiet residential setting appreciated, though some found it too "hidden away." Easy access to Rosebud, Sorrento for day trips.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant cleanliness inconsistency flagged in recent reviews (2024-25) with stained furniture, uncleaned toilets, ants, and dusty facilities in some cabins, indicating quality control issues despite older reviews praising spotlessness.
2. Very limited site space with cabins in extremely close proximity, only one parking space per cabin, and a cramped layout that makes some families feel like "outsiders" or uncomfortable with permanent residents walking past frequently.
3. Basic entertainment facilities with no pool, limited playground suitable mainly for older kids, and maintenance issues noted (playground needs maintenance, games room hygiene concerns), plus one critical service failure with no hot water and inability to contact emergency staff overnight.
