G'day! The Mornington Peninsula is where Melbourne families come to exhale – think calm bay beaches on one side for the littlies, surf beaches on the other for adventurous teens, and enough strawberry farms and hot springs to keep everyone happy in between. We've found September through April gives you the best run, though savvy locals know the Easter school holidays get absolutely packed, so booking ahead is non-negotiable. Here's an insider tip: grab fish and chips from one of the bay-side spots near Rosebud or Rye, then let the kids run wild at the shallow, knee-deep water that stretches out for ages – it's basically nature's own toddler pool and you'll actually get to eat your meal warm for once.
