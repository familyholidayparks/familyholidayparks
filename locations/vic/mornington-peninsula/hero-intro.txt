# Mornington Peninsula: A Family Holiday Destination

The Mornington Peninsula offers everything families need for a proper beach holiday without the fuss of travelling too far from Melbourne. You've got sheltered beaches perfect for younger kids at places like Rosebud and Dromana, plus rockier spots at Portsea for rock pooling and exploring. The natural landscape is genuinely impressive – think coastal heathland, native bushland trails, and plenty of space for children to burn off energy. Key attractions like the Mornington Peninsula Hot Springs, wildlife parks, and casual beachside adventure golf keep kids entertained without draining the budget, while parents can relax knowing there's good infrastructure and plenty of family-friendly cafes dotted along the foreshore.

Getting accommodation sorted is straightforward with several reliable caravan parks in the area including BIG4 Mornington Peninsula Holiday Park near Frankston, Peninsula Holiday Park, Capel Caravan Park, Amberlee Family Holidays, and Kanasta Caravan Park – all offering the basics families need with on-site facilities and easy access to beaches and attractions. What makes the Peninsula genuinely good for families is the variety; whether you're after a quiet day at the beach, hiking through nature reserves, or checking out local markets and wineries (the grown-ups' bonus), there's something for everyone. It's close enough for long weekends but far enough away to feel properly removed from the city grind.
