G'day! The Daylesford region is an absolute ripper for families who want to swap beach holidays for something a bit different – think mineral springs the kids can actually swim in, forest walks that don't require marathon fitness levels, and old-fashioned lolly shops on every corner. We reckon April to June is your sweet spot for visiting, when the autumn leaves turn those gorgeous gold and copper colours around the lake, and the weekend crowds thin out just enough to actually get a table at the bakeries. Here's an insider tip: grab your swimmers and head to the Hepburn Bathhouse on a weekday morning when they open at 9am – you'll have the family-friendly Sanctuary Pool practically to yourselves, and the kids love the lazy river more than any fancy waterslide park.
