G'day families! Shepparton and the Goulburn Valley are an absolute ripper for a holiday park getaway, especially if your kids are into fruit picking – come during stone fruit season (December to February) and you can hit the orchards before cooling off at the park's pool. The region's flat terrain makes it perfect for little ones learning to ride bikes, and you're smack bang in the middle of some brilliant farm gate trails. Here's a local tip: grab your breakfast supplies from SPC Factory Sales on Welsford Street – the kids go mad for picking their own discontinued jam flavours, and you'll pay half what you would at the supermarket.
