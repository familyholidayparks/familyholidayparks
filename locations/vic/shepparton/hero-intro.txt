# Shepparton Family Holiday Guide

Shepparton is a laid-back regional destination in Victoria's heartland that punches above its weight for family getaways. While it's not beach country, the town sits right on the Goulburn River with plenty of water-based activities to keep kids entertained, plus sprawling parks and gardens that make outdoor days easy. The area's real strength is its mix of nature trails, orchards, and farmland where families can slow down and actually enjoy time together without fighting crowds or traffic.

The town's got solid infrastructure for families, with several reliable caravan parks and holiday venues scattered throughout, including BIG4 Shepparton Park Lane Holiday Park, Shepparton Holiday Park, and Victoria Lake Holiday Park, all offering the kind of grass and facilities that parents appreciate. Kids will find plenty to do at local attractions like the Shepparton Showgrounds and nearby nature reserves, while the farming culture means you can visit orchards, markets, and farm shops for genuinely good produce. It's the kind of place where families can camp, explore by bike, fish in the river, and actually disconnect without losing creature comforts.
