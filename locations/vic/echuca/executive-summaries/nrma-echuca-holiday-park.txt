PARK: NRMA Echuca Holiday Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-22
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 250

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (18/20) — Reviews overwhelmingly praise the "spectacular" splash park and heated pool as standout features, with families noting "kids went absolutely bonkers," "insanely fun," and "adults had as much fun as the kids." Camp kitchen described as "magnificent" and "well equipped." School holiday activities and water park consistently mentioned as reasons for repeat visits.

Nature (16/20) — Located directly on the Murray River with walking tracks along the riverbank, easy access to paddle steamer viewing, and proximity to surrounding Riverina natural areas. Reviews mention "beautiful setting by the Murray River," "right next to Murray River," and "5-minute walk to river." Limited on-site bushland but excellent access to river environment.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed feedback with newer facilities and some amenity blocks described as "spotless" and "immaculate," but older blocks show inconsistency with reports of "cobwebs everywhere," "mould in showers," "dirty toilets," and maintenance issues. Some cabin cleanliness concerns noted. Regular cleaning witnessed but quality varies by location within park.

Value (13/15) — NRMA members receive excellent value at $33/night for powered sites. Most reviews praise "great value," "reasonable pricing," and facilities-to-price ratio. Some guests felt "overpriced at $51" for non-members or noted inconsistency where all sites charged same price despite quality differences between modern synthetic grass and dusty dirt sites.

Site Size (7/10) — Significant variation reported: some "large" and "generous" drive-through sites with synthetic grass praised, but many complaints about "tight," "tiny," "packed in," "short" sites where motorhomes "could only just squeeze on." Site allocation inconsistency is a recurring theme with some guests receiving inappropriate-sized sites for their rigs.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong loyalty evident with multiple families returning for 30-37 years and consistent "will definitely be back" statements. High recommendation rate from families. Some negative experiences with staff attitude, security concerns (theft reports), and site allocation issues prevent full marks. Overall very positive family sentiment toward facilities and location.

Location (7/10) — Exceptional proximity to Port of Echuca (5-10 minute walk), Murray River, paddle steamers, and town centre repeatedly praised as "perfect location," "walking distance to everything," "no need to drive." Points lost for noise concerns (nearby street racing, road traffic), lack of on-site natural bushland, and some sites backing onto main roads.

WHY NOT 100:
Inconsistent site quality and allocation creates frustration - families booking modern synthetic grass sites sometimes allocated to dusty, dirt sites at the same price, or sites inappropriate for their rig size despite advance bookings being confirmed. Older amenity blocks show significant maintenance issues with mould, cobwebs, broken fixtures, and cleanliness concerns that contrast sharply with newer facilities, creating an uneven guest experience. Security concerns reported by multiple guests including theft from sites, boom gates frequently stuck open or broken, holes cut in fences, and insufficient management response to safety issues diminish the family-safe atmosphere.
