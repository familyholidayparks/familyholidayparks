PARK: Rotary Park - Self Contained Caravan and RV Park
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 70

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Reviews consistently note this is a basic self-contained park with absolutely no on-site entertainment, playground equipment, or kids activities. One review mentions "Some weekends they have the trains running" nearby, but there are no family-oriented facilities whatsoever at the park itself.

Nature (14/20) — Multiple reviews praise the riverside location "alongside the river bank" and "on the bank of the Campaspe River" with "Lovely Birdlife" and opportunities to "sit and relax" by the water. However, reviews note "Very dusty no grassy areas" and "dirt gravel parking" rather than natural camping settings.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Reviews frequently describe the park as "clean tidy expansive camping area", "Well maintained", and "nice & clean" with accessible dump points and rubbish skips. However, multiple reviews complain about dust issues and note "Site very muddy after rain", and importantly "Toilets are locked" due to vandalism.

Value (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive feedback on the $10 per night fee with phrases like "rippa bewdie bargain", "great value for money", "Cheap camping", and "couldn't ask for more" for the price point. The only criticism is one reviewer who felt charged for basic facilities.

Site Size (8/10) — Reviews consistently mention "plenty of room", "Large open area", "heaps of space", and "room for larger Motorhomes" and "big rigs". Sites described as "Flat sites" and "large gravel sites" with easy access.

Sentiment (6/10) — While many reviews are positive about value and convenience, overall sentiment for families is mixed. One concerning review warns about "young locals causing trouble with campers at night", and the lack of facilities means it's unsuitable for most family stays. Repeat visitors are mainly self-contained travellers.

Location (7/10) — Reviews note the park is "close to town" (1-2km), "walking distance to town along a cycle track", "handy to local attractions" and "Close to the Port of Echuca". Good for accessing Echuca's attractions but not a destination park itself.

WHY NOT 100:
1. This park has zero family-friendly facilities - no toilets, showers, playground, pool, or entertainment of any kind, making it completely unsuitable for families with young children who require amenities and activities.
2. Multiple reviews warn about safety concerns including "young locals causing trouble with campers at night and no support from authorities" and locked toilet facilities "Due to vandles", creating an unsuitable environment for families.
3. The park is strictly for self-contained vehicles only with basic dirt/gravel sites that become "very muddy after rain" and "A bit too dusty" - there's no grass, no powered sites, and limited infrastructure beyond dump points and water access.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"must be fully self contained", "No toilet facilities available for campers", "only $10 per night", "A bit too dusty", "young locals causing trouble with campers at night", "Toilets are locked because they don't want to pay for cleaning", "Lovely Birdlife", "close to town"
