PARK: Discovery Parks - Echuca
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-25
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 173

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (19/20) — Outstanding feedback on kids facilities with splash park, waterpark with slides, indoor heated pool, outdoor pool, jumping pillow, BMX track, mini golf, basketball court, playgrounds, and school holiday activities. Reviews consistently mention "so much for the kids to do," "kids will never be bored," and "splash park was a huge hit." Easter activities, food trucks, and daily entertainment programs highly praised.

Nature (17/20) — Located directly on the Murray River with "stunning views," walking/cycling trails along the river, abundant birdlife including kookaburras, beautiful natural bush setting. Multiple reviews mention "lots of birdsong," "beautiful natural environment," and peaceful surroundings. Some concerns about lack of shade on powered sites and distance from amenities for some sites.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "very clean amenities" and "spotless" ensuites, but significant complaints during peak periods about "putrid, dirty facilities," "clumps of hair," toilets without soap/paper, and inconsistent cleaning standards. Several reviews mention filthy cabin floors, urine-stained mattress protectors, and smelly splash park toilets during busy times.

Value (13/15) — Generally considered "great value" and "much cheaper than all the others" with reasonable rates ($37-55/night for sites). However, multiple complaints about poor value in cabins ($330-400/night) with minimal supplies (two dishwashing tablets for a week), dated furnishings, and additional costs. Laundry facilities described as expensive and ineffective.

Site Size (8/10) — Positive feedback on "big sites," "great sized sites," and "plenty of room" for big rigs. Ensuite sites particularly praised. However, drive-through sites described as "pretty narrow" with awnings nearly touching neighbors. Some powered sites "a bit tight" and unpowered sites noted as more spacious.

Sentiment (7/10) — Strong repeat visitation with families returning yearly and extending stays. Many "highly recommend" comments and "we will be back" sentiment. However, offset by several extremely negative 1-star reviews about negligence, poor customer service, and guests stating they won't return. Staff reviews mixed between "sensational" and "extremely rude."

Location (2/10) — Consistently described as "too far out of town" (5-10 minute drive), "decent drive from town," and isolated location. While proximity to river is positive, distance from Echuca CBD and attractions is a significant drawback. No busy road noise mentioned as positive but limited access to town amenities without driving.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe and persistent WiFi/connectivity issues across all accommodation types, with reviews stating "terrible phone reception and NO WIFI," "couldn't even send texts," and "absolutely useless" internet despite being advertised as available.
2. Inconsistent cleanliness standards particularly during peak periods, with multiple reports of dirty facilities, toilets without supplies, lack of hot water, filthy cabins, and amenities running out of soap and toilet paper for extended periods.
3. Remote location 5-10 minutes from town creates access challenges, with limited on-site food options (self-catering required) and guests feeling isolated when reception/amenities are needed, particularly problematic given poor phone connectivity.
