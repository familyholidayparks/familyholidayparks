# Echuca Family Holiday Guide

Echuca is a charming riverside town on the Murray River in Victoria that punches above its weight as a family destination. While it doesn't have ocean beaches, the river itself is the main drawcard – you can swim in designated areas, take paddlewheel boat cruises, and explore the historic port precinct that kids find genuinely interesting. The town's walkable layout means families can easily stroll between attractions without fussing with the car constantly, and there's a solid mix of things to do whether your kids are into history, nature or just mucking about outdoors.

For families wanting to stay a few days, there are good caravan park options dotted around town including NRMA Echuca Holiday Park, Discovery Parks, Rich River Tourist Park, and Riverlander, all offering the flexibility you need with kids. The surrounding area has plenty of parkland and easy walking trails along the river, and you're close enough to spots like Barmah State Park for a bit of proper nature exploration. The slower pace suits families – there's no pressure to rush between attractions, and the local food scene has moved beyond the usual servo fare, so everyone can eat reasonably well without breaking the bank.
