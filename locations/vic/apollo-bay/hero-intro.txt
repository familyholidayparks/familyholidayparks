# Apollo Bay, Victoria

Apollo Bay is a laid-back coastal town on Victoria's famous Great Ocean Road, offering families the perfect mix of beach time and nature exploration. The sweeping sandy beach is ideal for swimming and building sandcastles, while the rock pools at nearby Marengo are great for spotting marine life without venturing too far from shore. The town itself has good amenities without being touristy, and plenty of local fish and chip spots to grab dinner after a day outdoors. Kids can burn energy at the Apollo Bay Recreation Reserve or explore the walking trails through the surrounding national park without needing to drive far.

The real drawcard for families is the natural playground on your doorstep. Walks range from short coastal strolls to more substantial hikes through rainforest, where you might spot native wildlife. Several well-maintained caravan parks operate in the area, including BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park, Apollo Bay Holiday Park, and Marengo Family Caravan Park, all offering family-friendly facilities and easy beach access. If you're after a holiday where kids can actually get outside and tire themselves out, Apollo Bay delivers without the stress of crowded tourist traps.
