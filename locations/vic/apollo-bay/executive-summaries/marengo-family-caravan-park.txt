PARK: Marengo Family Caravan Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 108

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Limited on-site entertainment with only a small playground mentioned; one family from Jan 2024 praised "daily activities they were putting on for the kids" but this wasn't consistently referenced across reviews. Most entertainment comes from natural environment rather than organized activities or facilities.

Nature (19/20) — Outstanding natural setting with direct beachfront access, stunning ocean views, seal colony visible from rocks, rock pools for children to explore, koalas in park areas, and access to Great Ocean Road Walk. Reviews consistently praised "hearing the waves at night," "watching the sunrise," and wildlife viewing opportunities.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed feedback with significant concerns during peak periods. While some praised "spotless" cabins and "very clean" facilities, multiple recent reviews described amenities as "disgusting, old and not cleaned near enough," "dirty and rundown," and facilities becoming "filthy" when park is full. Older amenities struggle to stay clean under heavy use.

Value (8/15) — Considerable dissatisfaction with pricing, particularly during peak season. Reviews cited $154/night for powered sites, $73-98 for basic sites with "old and run down" facilities. Many felt it was "overpriced for the amenities available" and "very expensive considering the state of the park." Off-season pricing ($33/night) received better feedback.

Site Size (4/10) — Consistent complaints about cramped sites: "site was tiny with no space to park the car," "unable to extend awning," "had to park our car where the awning should be," "adjoining sites overlapped each other." Multiple reviews mentioned difficulty maneuvering caravans and needing neighbors to move vehicles.

Sentiment (7/10) — Mixed overall sentiment with passionate lovers and disappointed visitors. Many expressed intent to return for the location, but several stated they would "never stay here again" due to facilities and value concerns. Family reviewers were more positive than general travelers.

Location (6/10) — Excellent coastal position on Great Ocean Road with beach access, but 5 minutes drive from Apollo Bay shops/restaurants. Some felt nearby Apollo Bay offered better value. Great for nature access but not walking distance to town amenities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severely inadequate amenities for park capacity with only 3 showers and 3 toilets for entire park, causing long waits during peak periods and cleanliness issues
2. Extremely cramped site sizes that prevent awning extension, require parking on roads, and create difficult maneuvering situations that frustrated many caravan owners
3. Poor value proposition with premium pricing ($73-154/night) for aging, basic facilities that multiple reviewers described as "old," "run down," and "needing TLC urgently"
