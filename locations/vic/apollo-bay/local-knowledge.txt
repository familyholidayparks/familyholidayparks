G'day! Apollo Bay sits right where the Great Ocean Road meets the stunning Otways rainforest, giving families that magical combo of beach days and forest adventures all in one spot. We reckon April to May or September to November are your sweet spots – the weather's still lovely, the holiday parks aren't packed to the rafters, and you'll actually snag a table at Apollo Bay Bakery without the summer queue snaking down the street. Here's a local tip that'll save you some drama: if you're planning to explore Maits Rest Rainforest Walk with the kids, head there before 10am when the light filters through the tree ferns beautifully and you'll have the boardwalk practically to yourselves.
