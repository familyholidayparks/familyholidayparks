Apollo Bay is one of the best family destinations on the Great Ocean Road, combining safe beaches, rainforest walks and wildlife encounters in one easy-to-explore town. Families love spotting koalas around Cape Otway, exploring rock pools along the coastline and visiting the glow worms at Melba Gully after dark. Maits Rest Rainforest Walk is an easy, family-friendly boardwalk through giant tree ferns, while the harbour is a great spot for an afternoon stroll and fish and chips. Between June and September, whales can often be spotted from the shoreline.
