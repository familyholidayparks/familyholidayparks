G'day! Apollo Bay sits right where the Otways rainforest meets the Southern Ocean, giving families that rare combo of beach days and forest walks without having to pack up and move locations. We've found April to October is magic here – the winter swells bring in whales you can spot from the shore, and the kids go mad for the glow worms in Melba Gully after dark. Here's a local trick: grab your fish and chips from Apollo Bay Fishermen's Co-op right on the harbour rather than the main street spots – it's where the locals queue up, and the kids can watch the fishing boats unload while you wait.
