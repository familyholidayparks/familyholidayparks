PARK: Marengo Family Caravan Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-06-14
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 118

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Limited on-site entertainment with one family noting "small playground" and "toddler loved the play equipment"; one review mentioned "daily activities they were putting on for the kids" but this appears inconsistent; most families rely on beach and nature-based activities rather than structured entertainment

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional natural environment with "ocean at our doorstep", "direct beach access", "spotting the seals from the rocks nearby", "beautiful rock pools for small children", "koalas in their natural habitat", and "Great Ocean Road Walk runs through the camp"; stunning coastal headland location praised across majority of reviews

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "clean amenities", "spotless" cabins, and "very clean" facilities, but notable complaints about "dirty and rundown" amenities, "disgusting, old" showers/toilets in some peak periods, and facilities "quickly becoming filthy" when park at capacity; cleaning frequency appears inconsistent

Value (9/15) — Significant concerns about pricing with "peak fees of $154 per night", "$73 for one night", complaints it's "overpriced for the amenities available", though off-season deals noted as "$100 for 3 nights" considered reasonable; value perception hampered by small sites and aging facilities relative to cost

Site Size (5/10) — Consistent criticism of "extremely small sites", "sites too small", "unable to extend awning", "had to park our car where the awning should be", "very small and difficult to get vans in"; major issue for families with larger rigs

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong emotional connection with "favourite stay on the Great Ocean Road", "didn't want to leave", "will definitely be back", though tempered by families who "left early" or "won't be coming back again" due to site size and facility issues; location creates loyalty despite operational shortcomings

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for "stunning location", "right on the beach", "5 mins drive to Apollo Bay shops", easy access to Great Ocean Road attractions, Otway National Park, and coastal walks; universally praised aspect

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severely undersized caravan sites create maneuvering challenges and cramped conditions, with multiple families reporting inability to extend awnings and cars blocking roads
2. Grossly inadequate amenity capacity with only 3 showers and 3 toilets serving the entire park, creating queues and cleanliness issues during peak periods
3. High pricing relative to aging facilities and small sites, with peak season rates up to $154/night not matching the basic amenity standard and tight site configuration
