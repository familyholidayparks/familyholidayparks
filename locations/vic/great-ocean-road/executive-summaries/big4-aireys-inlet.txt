PARK: BIG4 Aireys Inlet
SCORE: 76/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-23
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 370

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently mention playground, jumping pillow, heated pool, pedal carts, games room, and outdoor chess, though several families noted the park "needs a basketball court" and some felt entertainment options were limited compared to larger BIG4 parks. The small pool size was frequently mentioned as adequate for kids but "tiny" or "dinky" by adult standards.

Nature (17/20) — Exceptional natural setting with reviews praising "waking up to birds chirping", "beautiful location surrounded by nature", "lovely bird life", and "tree-lined sites". The park offers walking distance access to beaches, lighthouse, cliff-top walks, and coastal trails, with many noting the "beautiful easy walk to the lighthouse" and "stunning beaches" nearby.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive feedback with phrases like "spotlessly clean", "immaculate amenities", "best shower since we left home", "cleanest toilet/shower block we have ever seen", and "exceptionally clean". A small number of isolated complaints about spiders, hair, or maintenance issues prevented a perfect score.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing, with many finding it "reasonable" or "great value", but notable complaints about costs: "$730 per night is overpriced", "most expensive caravan park we stayed in", "cost has skyrocketed", and one extreme price increase complaint ($3000 to $11,000). Peak season pricing particularly noted as high.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews describe sites as "spacious", "generous", and "level" with concrete slabs available, though some noted they were "fairly compact", "cramped", or "tight to get in". Unpowered tent sites received comments about being "small" or "dusty" with limited grass coverage.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong positive sentiment with many repeat visitors ("4th stay", "fifth time there", "17 years", "2 years over Xmas") and high recommendation rates. However, several disappointed long-term guests mentioned policy changes, staff rudeness incidents, and service issues that detracted from otherwise positive experiences.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location praised extensively: "walking distance to everything", "cafes just across the road", "2 minute walk to general store", "short walk to beach, shops and pub", "perfect location on Great Ocean Road". Only minor criticism was road noise for cabins closest to the highway.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited entertainment infrastructure compared to larger BIG4 parks (no basketball court, small pool, fewer activities) with multiple reviews specifically requesting more on-site entertainment options for families.
2. Value concerns with significant pricing complaints during peak seasons and documented price increases that have deterred long-term loyal customers, with ensuite sites and cabins considered expensive relative to facilities offered.
3. Inconsistent customer service experiences including documented staff rudeness, checkout conflicts, booking disputes, and policy inflexibility that left negative impressions despite otherwise positive park qualities.
