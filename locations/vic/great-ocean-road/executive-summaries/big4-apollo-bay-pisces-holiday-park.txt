PARK: BIG4 Apollo Bay Pisces Holiday Park
SCORE: 92/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-23
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 428

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (19/20) — Reviews overwhelmingly praise the "next level" entertainment including a spectacular nightly light and sound show ("$750,000 investment"), covered jumping pillow, heated pool, pump track, water play area, games room, flying fox playground, kids club activities, scavenger hunts, face painting, live music on weekends, and food trucks. Families describe children as "entertained for days" with "so much for the kids to do."

Nature (18/20) — The beachfront location is repeatedly highlighted with "stunning ocean views," direct beach access "across the road," and the "sound of waves" creating ambiance. The park sits between ocean and Otway Ranges with sheep in back paddocks and koalas spotted in trees. Reviews mention "spectacular" sunrise views and scenic walking along the foreshore into town.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Consistent 5-star feedback describes the park as "spotlessly clean," "immaculate," and "pristine" with "heated bathrooms," "excellent" amenities, and "impeccably clean camp kitchen." Staff maintain facilities with "daily cleans across the whole park" and "everything spotless and well maintained."

Value (12/15) — Reviews acknowledge premium pricing ($80-$230 per night for sites depending on season, $500+ for cabins during peak) as "expensive" and "way too high" by some, though many state "worth every cent" and "you get what you pay for." Several note it's "by far the most expensive" they've paid but justify it with exceptional facilities and service.

Site Size (9/10) — Powered sites described as "huge," "spacious," "very large," and "easy to access" with synthetic grass, level surfaces, and "plenty of room for our large van." Ensuite sites particularly praised. Some mention caravan sites are "difficult to get into" initially but well worth it once positioned.

Sentiment (10/10) — Exceptional repeat visitation and recommendation rates with phrases like "will definitely be back," "already booked again next year," "best big 4 we've stayed at," "my favorite of all," and "can't wait to return." Multiple reviewers extending stays and many describing it as annual tradition. Overwhelmingly 5-star ratings.

Location (9/10) — Positioned at northern end of Apollo Bay with "perfect central location" for Great Ocean Road exploration. Beach "directly opposite" is major asset. Distance to town (1.5km walk/5 min drive) noted as minor inconvenience by some, though others appreciate the "quieter" position. "Ideal location" for Otways and coastal attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
Premium pricing during peak seasons ($229 for powered site, $500+ nightly for cabins) is repeatedly mentioned as "too expensive," "insane amount," and "greed" with some families stating it's "way too high" for a caravan park, though offset by exceptional facilities. Distance to Apollo Bay town centre (1.5km) requires walking or driving for shops and restaurants, which doesn't suit all families wanting immediate amenities. A few isolated negative experiences mention booking confusion, site allocation issues, noise complaints handled poorly by staff, and lack of ensuite heating in winter impacting guest satisfaction.
