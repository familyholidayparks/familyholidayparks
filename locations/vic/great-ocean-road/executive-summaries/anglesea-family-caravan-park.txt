PARK: Anglesea Family Caravan Park
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-28
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 132

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Families consistently highlight the giant jumping pillow, mini-golf, multiple playgrounds, games room, pool with rock waterfall, and toddler room. Many note "heaps to keep the kids entertained" especially during peak periods, though some mention facilities close early (around 5pm) and the games room has limited appeal with only basic activities.

Nature (19/20) — Outstanding natural setting with kangaroos, wallabies, ducks, cockatoos and parrots throughout the grounds. Direct beach and river frontage with "kangaroos lying next to our tents" and wildlife encounters consistently described as magical. Bush camping sites under native trees provide authentic coastal atmosphere, with walking tracks to beach and clifftop walks nearby.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with recent reviews noting "amenities need to be cleaned more regularly at peak times" and some reports of mold, duck droppings around pool area, and toilet blocks needing refresh. Off-peak reviews praise cleanliness with "cleaned few times a day" but consistency drops during busy periods. Several 2024-2026 reviews mention facilities showing age.

Value (10/15) — Significant concern with "prices have gone thru the roof" — powered sites $93-$149/night, cabins $280-$550/night. Multiple reviews cite "overpriced" and "way overpriced in Summer Holiday period" with "value for $$ is not there now." Four-night minimums on long weekends add to cost concerns, though location partly justifies premium pricing.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive with "sites are big" and "lots of space" mentioned frequently. Large sites accommodate 21' vans easily, though some premium sites disappointed with "dirt and sand, very uneven" rather than grass. Bush camping sites praised for generous spacing that "don't feel crammed."

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation with "we come every year" appearing multiple times, and many saying "will absolutely be back." However, sentiment dampened by pricing concerns and service inconsistencies. Family reviewers overwhelmingly positive about kids' experiences, but solo travelers and those seeking premium facilities express disappointment.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for "only park in Anglesea with both river and beach frontage." Walking distance to beach (3-5 minutes), town, shops, IGA, cafes, and bowls club. Ideal Great Ocean Road base location with 12 Apostles within 1-hour drive. "Everything is walking distance" praised universally.

WHY NOT 100:
Cleanliness inconsistency during peak periods with multiple 2024-2026 reviews reporting amenities not cleaned properly, mold in bathrooms, duck droppings around pool, and toilet blocks closing for 2-hour cleaning windows (8-10am) causing significant inconvenience. Premium pricing not matched by premium maintenance standards, with several reviews noting facilities need refresh and modernization. Value concerns are substantial with prices increasing dramatically ($93-$149 for powered sites, up to $550/night for cabins) while service quality declined, leading multiple families to state they won't return or will seek free camping alternatives despite loving the location.
