PARK: NRMA Port Campbell Holiday Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-06-16
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 168

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Reviews consistently note "no playground for the kids", "nothing for kids", "no activities for the kids", and "no swimming pool". Families report the park offers virtually no on-site entertainment, relying entirely on the town and natural environment for activities.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting with "walking distance to the beach", "lovely walk just outside the park with views over the town, beach, and ocean", "bridge over the creek", and "lots of cute rabbits everywhere". Multiple reviews praise "best location on the Great Ocean Rd" and proximity to Twelve Apostles. The creek, suspension bridge, and coastal walks provide genuine nature immersion.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Significant concerns with "amenities are incredibly dirty and they smell", "toilets constantly dirty", "park has fallen into disrepair", "cabin was full of spider web, the floor was a little dusty", "mould in the corners and ceiling", and "dirty bedsheets". However, some recent reviews note improvements under new management with "clean amenities" and "heated and very clean amenities".

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback with some praising "very reasonably priced" and "cost only $41 with power", while others complain "83 Dollar für eine Person und ein Auto" (overpriced for solo travelers) and "wasted $107" on unsuitable tent sites. Families note decent value when facilities work but poor value given maintenance issues.

Site Size (6/10) — Frequent complaints about "sites are tiny, ours was terribly uneven and dusty", "put us on a tiny site not suitable for a caravan", "sites smallish and quite close with no screening", and "parking was a bit tight". Premium sites receive better feedback with "big sites" and "spacious", but standard sites are clearly problematic for larger rigs.

Sentiment (7/10) — Polarized sentiment with loyal returners ("been coming here for over 30 yrs", "will be back") alongside strong negative experiences ("worst ever Holiday Park", "never coming back here again", "avoid this park if possible"). Recent reviews show improving sentiment under new management. Many appreciate location despite facility issues.

Location (10/10) — Unanimous praise for "perfect location", "in my opinion, the best location on the Great Ocean Rd", "walking distance to the beach, cafes, restaurants and shops", "short drive to the apostles", and "right next to the centre of town and the beach". The park's positioning makes it an ideal base for Great Ocean Road exploration.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of kids' entertainment facilities (no playground, no pool, no organized activities) loses 16 points in entertainment scoring, making this unsuitable for families seeking on-site activities.
2. Persistent cleanliness and maintenance issues including "dirty amenities", mould in cabins, broken fixtures, "park has fallen into disrepair", and inconsistent cleaning standards lose 7 points, with multiple reviews spanning years noting deterioration.
3. Inadequate site sizing and layout for caravans with "tiny sites", poor leveling, tight maneuvering, and lack of grass on standard sites loses 4 points, creating frustration for families with larger rigs despite premium sites being better.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"best location on the Great Ocean Rd", "walking distance to the beach", "amenities are incredibly dirty", "no playground for the kids", "park has fallen into disrepair", "needs a lot of TLC", "lots of cute rabbits everywhere", "sites are tiny, ours was terribly uneven"
