G'day families! The Great Ocean Road stretch between Torquay and Port Campbell is bloody brilliant for a family holiday – you've got those iconic limestone stacks the kids will actually remember, plus rock pools, safe swimming beaches, and koala spotting that doesn't feel forced. We reckon April to May or September to November are your sweet spots: the crowds thin out after school holidays, the weather's still decent for beach days, and accommodation prices drop considerably. Here's an insider tip most visitors miss – stop at the Kennett River General Store early morning (around 8am) to grab your koala-spotting map, then head straight up Grey River Road where the koalas are so close to the walking tracks that even toddlers can spot them without binoculars.
