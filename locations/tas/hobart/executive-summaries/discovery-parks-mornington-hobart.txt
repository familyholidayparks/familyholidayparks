PARK: Discovery Parks - Mornington Hobart
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2024
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 45

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Multiple reviews explicitly note the absence of a playground with one stating "it would be even better if there were a playground." No mention of kids' club, activities, jumping pillow, or any organized entertainment. One family noted "Internet wasnt great so kids were a little unhappy" suggesting children had limited on-site options.

Nature (8/20) — The park is described as having "nice and neat" grounds with some reviewers mentioning it's a "lovely caravan park." However, the dominant environmental feature is the adjacent freeway, not natural surroundings. Good location for day trips to Tasmanian nature attractions (7 mins to Hobart) but the park itself lacks significant natural features or bush setting.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Mixed feedback with many recent reviews praising cleanliness: "clean and comfortable," "very clean," "well maintained cabins." However, concerning reports include "Full of mould," "room smelt heavily of mold," "bunk bed sheet and mattress protector seems to have been changed or clean. It has all kind of stain and some dirt on top of it looks like it was cockroaches egg," and "found some chicken bones and dirty cutlery." Also issues with "shower drain was blocked" and maintenance problems.

Value (10/15) — Strong value proposition with many reviews highlighting "budget friendly," "great budget friendly option," "affordable price," "Decent price." Multiple mentions of reasonable rates around $150/night for families. However, some felt cleanliness issues and lack of amenities didn't justify pricing. Overall consensus is good value for basic, no-frills accommodation near Hobart.

Site Size (4/10) — Significant concerns about space: "barely enough room to swing a cat in there," "bathroom/toilet was tiny," "really small," "table du Bungalow familial soit minuscule" (family bungalow table is tiny), "parking spaces are barely big enough for a small car." Some positive mentions of "Spacious cabin for four" but economy cabins clearly cramped for families.

Sentiment (5/10) — Polarized reviews with loyal long-term users ("Used them for 40 years") alongside very negative family experiences. Several "never staying again" comments. Families with young children particularly disappointed. Some positive return visits but overall sentiment mixed, with recent reviews showing ongoing issues affecting family satisfaction.

Location (5/10) — Excellent proximity to Hobart (7-10 mins), airport (10 mins), and amenities, with "great location for day trips" and "Close to all amenities." However, the freeway location is a major detractor: "right next to the freeway," "traffic noise doesn't stop till 1:00am and starts back at 5:00 am," "Katastrophaler Lärm durch die Autobahn" (catastrophic highway noise). Public bus access next door is a plus.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of children's entertainment facilities - no playground, pool, kids' club, or organized activities, making it unsuitable as a family holiday destination rather than just a stopover.
2. Severe traffic noise issues from the adjacent freeway that multiple families described as "catastrophic," "diabolical," causing sleep disruption with noise continuing until 1am and resuming at 5am.
3. Inconsistent cleanliness standards with multiple family reviews reporting mould, stained bedding that appeared unchanged, dirty cutlery, chicken bones left in cabins, and maintenance issues including blocked drains and poor waterproofing.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"it would be even better if there were a playground," "budget friendly option," "right next to the freeway," "barely enough room to swing a cat in there," "Full of mould, traffic and emergency services near by," "Internet wasnt great so kids were a little unhappy," "We stayed for 2 nights as a family of 4," "Never ever going back to this place"
