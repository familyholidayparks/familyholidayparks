G'day! If you're chasing a family holiday that mixes proper Tassie wilderness with surprisingly manageable drives, the parks around Hobart absolutely deliver. We've found autumn (March-May) is magic here – the kids can actually enjoy bushwalking without melting, the beaches aren't freezing, and you'll dodge the summer crowds at Port Arthur. One tip from our many visits: grab fresh fish and chips from Barilla Bay just north of the airport on your way to whichever park you're staying at – the oysters are stunning if your little ones are adventurous eaters, and there's a jetty where kids can spot rays and pufferfish while you devour lunch.
