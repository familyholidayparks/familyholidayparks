PARK: Swansea Holiday Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2025
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 126

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Multiple reviews explicitly note "nothing for kids to do, no ball games no bikes, no park" and "don't expect bouncy pillows etc." One family stated it's "not very child-friendly" with "no playground or area for children to play" and strict signage about no ball games anywhere. The park appears focused on quiet amenity rather than children's activities.

Nature (18/20) — Overwhelmingly praised for its beachside location with "direct access to the beach," "right on the beach," and "beach just a pathway away." Located on Tasmania's scenic East Coast, it's "1 hour drive to Freycinet National Park" and near Wineglass Bay. Guests loved "hearing the sound of the ocean and waves" and the "lovely walk around the headlands."

Cleanliness (15/15) — Exceptional consistency across reviews: "the cleanest we've seen in Tasmania," "immaculately clean bathrooms," "spotless amenities," "cleanest holiday park we have ever seen," "probably cleaner than my own house." Facilities described as "pristine clean," "super clean," and "spotlessly clean" throughout the 3-year period.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback with some praising "very affordable prices" and "priced well," while others complained about "expensive" rates and "$162 for 1 person in a Hiace van for 3 nights. Getting costly." Several negative reviews about strict cancellation policies keeping deposits. Generally considered fair but not exceptional value.

Site Size (9/10) — Sites consistently described as "spacious," "very well maintained, spacious & flat," "large easy to park van sites," with "decent spacing between vehicle spots." Gravel pads with grass sections noted. Cabins criticized as "very small" and "tiny" by families, though caravan sites received strong praise.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong positive sentiment with numerous "highly recommend," "will definitely be back," and "loved our stay" comments. However, several serious complaints about "extremely uncouth owners," "rude and abusive" staff interactions, and poor customer service in cancellation situations dragged down the otherwise excellent sentiment.

Location (10/10) — Perfect scores for location: "great location," "perfect location next to the beach," "easy walk to town," "close to cafes, restaurants and supermarkets," and "40 minutes from Freycinet National Park." Convenient access to Swansea town centre and major East Coast attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of children's entertainment facilities with explicit rules against ball games, bikes, and no playground, making it unsuitable for families seeking on-site activities for kids.
2. Several disturbing reports of extremely rude and abusive owner/staff interactions, including swearing at guests, aggressive checkout enforcement, and complete inflexibility with cancellations even for family emergencies.
3. Cabins are notably small and cramped for families, with reviews stating "you couldn't even close the door to the main bedroom because the bed was in the way" and uncomfortable for stays longer than one or two nights.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"the cleanest we've seen in Tasmania", "spotless amenities", "nothing for kids to do, no ball games no bikes, no park", "right on the beach", "immaculate showers and toilets", "extremely uncouth owners", "cleanest holiday park we have ever seen", "perfect location next to the beach"
