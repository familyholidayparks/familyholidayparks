# East Coast Tasmania: A Family Holiday Destination

The East Coast of Tasmania is a ripper for families wanting a genuine beach getaway without the crowds. Long stretches of golden sand at places like Scamander and Swansea are perfect for kids to swim, paddle and build sandcastles, while the water stays beautifully clear throughout summer. The region's dotted with quality caravan parks—think Scamander Sanctuary, BIG4 Tassie Getaway Parks St Helens, and Swansea Holiday Park—that cater specifically to families with on-site facilities, playgrounds and easy access to the beach. It's close enough to Hobart for a weekend dash, but feels worlds away from the hustle.

Beyond the beach, there's genuine nature that'll keep kids engaged without needing screen time. You've got the Bay of Fires for exploring rock pools, Freycinet National Park nearby for easy walking tracks, and the Scamander River for spotting platypuses if you're lucky. The towns themselves are low-key and friendly, with good local fish and chips spots, and plenty of wildlife encounters that kids find genuinely exciting. Whether you're after lazy beach days or active exploration, the East Coast delivers solid family value with minimal fuss.
