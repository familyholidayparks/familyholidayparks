G'day! If you're planning a Tassie adventure with the kids, the Cradle Mountain region delivers that rare combo of jaw-dropping wilderness and surprisingly accessible family fun. We've found September through April offers the most reliable weather for tackling the easier walks with little ones, though you'll want to pack layers year-round since conditions can shift faster than a wombat scurrying for cover. Here's a local secret that's saved us countless times: grab your groceries and any forgotten camping essentials in Sheffield or Devonport before you head up the mountain, because the nearest proper supermarket to Cradle is over an hour's drive away, and the small stores near the park charge accordingly.
