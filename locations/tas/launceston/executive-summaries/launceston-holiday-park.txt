PARK: Launceston Holiday Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-25
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 395

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews consistently mention a jumping pillow and small playground that kids enjoy, plus natural wildlife entertainment (wallabies, pademelons, rabbits) around the cabins. However, families note limited organized activities and entertainment facilities compared to other BIG4 parks, with some stating "not much to do at the Big 4 itself" beyond basic play equipment.

Nature (14/20) — The park offers regular wildlife sightings with families reporting "wallabies and pademelons at night," "lots of rabbits," and "cute little rabbits scampering around." Located on a hillside with established trees providing shade, though proximity to highways and industrial areas limits the natural bush feel. Close access to Cataract Gorge (walking distance) provides excellent nature activities nearby.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Overwhelming praise for cleanliness with repeated phrases like "spotlessly clean," "very clean facilities," "amenities were clean and modern," and "the cleanest we had ever hired." Modern bathrooms with "hot showers, good pressure" consistently highlighted. Minor deductions for occasional reports of shower cleaning issues and camp kitchen cleanliness during renovations.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing. Powered sites $55-85/night described as "overpriced" and "expensive" by tent campers, with some calling it "the most expensive camp-site in Australia." However, cabin guests found it "budget friendly," "good value for money," and "reasonably priced" given quality of modern accommodation. Pricing reflects cabin focus over camping facilities.

Site Size (8/10) — Powered sites described as "spacious," "generous space," and "large enough for our van (22')," with "good space between sites." Some sites on hillside terraces are level and well-maintained. Occasional complaints about sloping sites and limited tent-suitable ground. Generally adequate for caravans and motorhomes.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong positive sentiment from cabin users with many stating "will definitely stay again," "would highly recommend," and "one of the best Big 4 Parks." Excellent staff reviews: "friendly and helpful," "went above and beyond," "exceptional customer service." Lower scores from tent campers citing noise and value issues. Repeat visitors common among cabin guests.

Location (6/10) — Convenient location "close to town," "walking distance to city," and near Cataract Gorge, airport, and shops. However, major criticism is highway proximity causing "incredible noise all day and night," "constant truck noise," and "logging trucks air brakes." Located in "semi commercial region" rather than natural setting. Easy highway access is double-edged.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Highway noise is the single biggest complaint across hundreds of reviews, with families reporting "trucks using exhaust brakes," "cargo jets 2-3am," and "constant horrendous truck noise" that significantly impacts sleep quality, particularly for tent and caravan users.

2. Limited on-site entertainment for children beyond a jumping pillow and small playground, with families noting the park lacks the extensive kids' facilities (pools, waterparks, organized activities) found at other BIG4 parks, making it primarily accommodation-focused rather than a destination park.

3. Camp kitchen facilities described as "inadequate," "tiny," "third world," "barely usable," and "open to elements" during lengthy renovations, forcing tent and caravan families to cook in poor conditions or rely on cabin kitchenettes, reducing value for non-cabin guests.
