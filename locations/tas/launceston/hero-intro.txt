Planning a family trip to Launceston? We've rounded up the best family holiday parks in Launceston so you can skip hours of research and get straight to the good part. Families love Launceston because the kids go wild for close encounters with Tasmanian devils at the wildlife park, there's proper space to run around at City Park with its cheeky monkeys, and you're only a short drive from Cataract Gorge where they can ride the chairlift and spot peacocks roaming free.
