G'day! Launceston and the Tamar Valley are absolutely brilliant for families who want that perfect mix of nature and easy-going fun – the kids can feed wallabies in the morning and splash in Cataract Gorge's swimming basin by lunch. We reckon April through May is magic here: the autumn colours along the Tamar are stunning, the crowds have thinned out, and you'll still get those crisp, sunny days perfect for exploring. Here's a local secret – grab fresh berries and cherries from the roadside stalls along the West Tamar Highway (the kids love picking their own), then have a picnic at one of the river beaches where the water's actually swimmable unlike most Tassie spots.
