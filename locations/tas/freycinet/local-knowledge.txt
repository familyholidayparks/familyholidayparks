G'day! If you're after that perfect mix of pristine beaches and bushwalking without the crowds, the holiday parks near Freycinet will absolutely blow your mind – my kids still talk about the day we saw wombats waddle past our cabin at dusk. The sweet spot for visiting is November through April when the water's swimmable and you've got long daylight hours to tackle the Wineglass Bay lookout (though honestly, March and April mean fewer school holiday crowds and still cracker weather). Here's something the locals know: grab your fresh crayfish and oysters straight from the boats at Coles Bay jetty around 2pm most afternoons – you'll pay half what the restaurants charge and the kids get a kick out of meeting real fishers.
