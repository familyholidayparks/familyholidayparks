PARK: Bicheno East Coast Holiday Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 154

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews consistently note there is no pool and the small playground is "obsolete, worn out and not working properly" with a flying fox that's "very hard for the kids to get flying." The games room is reported as mostly "out of order" with minimal on-site activities for children beyond the basic playground equipment.

Nature (16/20) — The park excels in natural setting with the beach "one block away," "100 metres walk to the beach," and easy walking access to Waub's Bay, the blowhole, Whaler's Lookout trail, and penguin tours right next door. Multiple reviews praise the "stunning bay for a swim" and "beautiful view of the sea."

Cleanliness (9/15) — Reviews are mixed, with many praising "spotlessly clean" cabins and "very clean toilet and shower rooms," but numerous complaints about cabins needing "a bit of a clean," sofas with "mystery stains" and "food scraps," rubbish left in bins, and areas "under the sofa had not been swept in ages." Amenities described as needing renovation with inconsistent cleaning standards.

Value (13/15) — Strong consensus on excellent value with phrases like "Great value for money," "Very cheap," "reasonably priced," and "$35 for an unpowered site" featuring prominently. Families appreciate the budget-friendly pricing despite dated facilities, with many noting it's perfect for short stays when prioritizing location over luxury.

Site Size (7/10) — Powered sites receive mixed reviews with some noting "large sites" while others complain about cramped conditions, sites "on a bit of a slope," and unpowered sites being "just a piece of grass in between cabins" that are "small and you can't fit your car and tent." Caravan sites described as "potholed" and difficult to level.

Sentiment (7/10) — Overall sentiment is cautiously positive for budget stays, with many families indicating they "would stay again" for short visits. However, limited enthusiasm for longer stays, with several noting it was "sufficient" or "adequate" rather than exceptional, and multiple warnings to "avoid" or try alternatives nearby.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for location with unanimous praise: "right middle of the town," "walking distance to everything," "2 mins walk" to IGA supermarket and cafes, "across the road from beach and shops," and "perfect location in the middle of the town." Proximity to all Bicheno attractions is the park's strongest asset.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severely limited on-site entertainment with no pool, a broken/worn-out playground, and non-functioning games room facilities that leave families entirely dependent on external attractions for children's activities.

2. Inconsistent cleanliness standards and significant maintenance issues including dated cabins with mould, broken appliances, collapsed couches, worn mattresses, faulty toilets, and general neglect that multiple families found unacceptable despite the budget pricing.

3. Cramped, poorly maintained sites with uneven ground, limited space for caravans and tents, broken laundry facilities, unreliable WiFi that "basically doesn't work," and poor customer service when issues arise, creating frustration for families beyond just the dated aesthetics.
