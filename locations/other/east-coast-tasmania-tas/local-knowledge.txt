G'day! East Coast Tasmania is where your kids can spot dolphins from pristine white beaches in the morning and feed friendly pademelons at dusk – it's genuinely one of Australia's most unspoiled coastal stretches. We've found April to May offers the sweet spot: you'll dodge the summer crowds, enjoy mild weather perfect for rock pool exploring, and score better rates at the parks. Here's an insider tip that's saved us countless times: stop at the Freycinet Marine Farm at Coles Bay before you settle into your park – the fresh oysters and mussels are ridiculously cheap, and there's nothing better than a seafood feast at your cabin after a big day at Wineglass Bay.
