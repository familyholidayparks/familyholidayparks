G'day! Albany's stunning coastline and fascinating whaling history make it one of WA's most underrated spots for a family holiday park getaway, and I reckon the kids will love scrambling over the granite formations at The Gap and Natural Bridge just as much as mine did. You'll want to visit between November and April when the water's warm enough for swimming, though we always pop down in late June too – the southern right whales putting on a show from the clifftops is absolutely unforgettable. Here's a local tip that'll save you some dollars: grab your fresh fish and chips from Emu Point Cafe on a Friday arvo, then let the kids feed the massive stingrays that cruise into the shallows at sunset – it's become our family tradition every time we're in town.
