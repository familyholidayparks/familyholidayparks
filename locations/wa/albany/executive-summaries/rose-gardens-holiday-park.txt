PARK: Rose Gardens Holiday Park
SCORE: 38/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 111

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Reviews mention a basic playground for kids that is described as "tired and dated" with equipment needing attention. The games room is repeatedly criticized as "disgustingly dirty and neglected" with "spider webs, broken furniture" and no maintenance. No organized activities, movie nights, or entertainment programs were mentioned. Multiple families specifically warned others to "go to the big4 next door" if travelling with children.

Nature (16/20) — The park's standout feature is its exceptional oceanfront location at Emu Point with "unobstructed ocean views," direct beach access to Middleton Beach and Frenchman's Bay, and "beautiful birds" throughout the grounds. Families appreciated the "calm, shallow waters ideal for young children" and dog off-leash beach access. Walking and bike paths along the coast received consistent praise. The natural setting with rabbits and coastal environment scored well despite site maintenance issues.

Cleanliness (2/15) — This was the park's most severe failure point. Recent reviews (2024-2026) overwhelmingly describe "absolutely disgusting" and "revolting" amenities with "faeces on walls," "soap scum," "mould in showers," and facilities "not cleaned properly in a while." Multiple guests reported toilets cleaned "only once a day for 5 minutes" or going "14 nights with only 2 cleanings." The widespread ant infestation affecting "multiple tents and caravans" was a recurring complaint with management appearing unresponsive.

Value (8/15) — Reviews show mixed value perception. Some praised "exceptional value for money" at $54/night compared to Big 4 at $130/night, calling it "reasonably priced." However, many felt the poor condition didn't justify the cost, describing it as "overpriced" for the amenities provided. Families paying $40-60/night during peak periods felt "there is absolutely no excuse for amenities this dirty" given the revenue generated.

Site Size (6/10) — Reviews describe "large spacious sites" and "generous" site sizes with some concrete slabs available. However, many complained about "tiny sites," uneven ground "full of holes from rabbits," sandy sites instead of grass, and poor site allocation with guests "squeezed into the worst three spots" when the park was empty. Some sites were difficult to access and manoeuvre caravans into.

Sentiment (3/10) — Overall sentiment was predominantly negative in recent reviews with many stating "never going back," "wouldn't wish the experience on my worst enemy," and "worst caravan park I've ever stayed in." Multiple long-term repeat visitors said "this will be our last" stay. The few positive reviews praised location only. Evidence of management changes (RAC takeover pending) and owner disengagement contributed to negative sentiment.

Location (9/10) — The location at Emu Point received universal praise as "unbeatable," "perfect," and "the only good thing about this park." Guests valued being "right on the water," 10 minutes from Albany city centre, 30 minutes to The Gap and Natural Bridge, with easy access to beaches, coastal walks, and local cafe. The oceanfront position was consistently the highest-rated aspect.

WHY NOT 100:
The park suffers from catastrophic cleanliness failures with amenities described as "absolutely disgusting," "beyond filthy," and health hazards, with inadequate cleaning schedules and a severe ant infestation that management failed to address despite widespread complaints affecting families with young children. Facilities and infrastructure are severely dated, run-down and neglected with broken equipment, derelict buildings, no maintenance between guests, blocked dump points, non-functional dryers, and a recreation room with "spider webs and broken furniture" creating an unsafe environment for children. Customer service and management response was repeatedly criticized as "extremely rude," "dismissive," and showing complete lack of care, with reports of shouting at guests, refusing refunds despite serious issues, and failing to address safety concerns including multiple thefts with no security measures in place.
