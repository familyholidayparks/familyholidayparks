PARK: Tasman Holiday Parks - Albany
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 193

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Excellent feedback on the bounce pillow/jumping cushion which was consistently mentioned as a highlight for kids, pool facilities praised as 'amazing' and well-maintained, rec room with billiards/table tennis/basketball available, though some noise complaints from families near the bounce pillow area

Nature (12/20) — Urban park setting with 'lots of shade', 'lovely grassy and treed sites', 'beautiful trees surrounding the sites', but limited natural bushland or direct nature access; 30-minute walk to city centre, close to beaches and shops but not a true nature-based location

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising 'pristine bathrooms', 'immaculately clean' facilities, 'very clean amenities and grounds', but concerning number of reviews citing dated facilities, inconsistent cleaning (ensuites not cleaned for 14+ days), 'dirty bathrooms', floor cleaning issues, and laundry cleanliness problems

Value (11/15) — Divided opinions: many found it 'well priced', 'great value for money', 'very reasonable price compared to other caravan sites', while others complained it was 'extremely overpriced', '$129 per night expensive for average site', '$90 for tent site inadequate given condition'

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised for 'very large sites', 'spacious sites', 'large drive through sites', 'plenty of space between sites', 'lots of space in our lot', with concrete slabs for easy setup, though a few mentioned some tent sites were 'a bit small'

Sentiment (7/10) — Generally positive with many 'will definitely stay again' and 'highly recommend' comments, but sentiment significantly impacted by serious safety concerns (break-in attempts, theft from fridges), dismissive staff responses to safety issues, and inconsistent service experiences

Location (6/10) — Central Albany location praised as 'close to everything', '1km from major retailers', 'walking distance to town' (though 30 minutes), but several reviews noted it's 'a little out of town', 'have to drive to town', and location doesn't offer scenic/beachfront appeal

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant security and safety concerns with multiple reviews detailing break-in attempts, food theft from communal fridges, and dismissive staff responses to safety incidents, creating serious concerns for families
2. Inconsistent maintenance and cleanliness with dated facilities (especially ensuites), cleaning staff not arriving for days, poor water pressure, washing machines staining clothes, and general maintenance issues in some cabins
3. Value perception issues with pricing considered high by many reviewers ($110-129/night) relative to the dated condition of facilities, lack of natural setting, and urban rather than resort-style experience
