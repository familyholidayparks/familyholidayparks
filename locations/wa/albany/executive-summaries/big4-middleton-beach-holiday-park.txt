PARK: BIG4 Middleton Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 81/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 162

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Families consistently praised the daily kids club with different activities, heated pool and spa, two playgrounds, jumping pillow, games room with pool/table tennis/arcade, go-kart hire, and recreation room. Multiple reviews highlighted "entertainment for kids is top notch" and children having "the best time" with "so much for them to do."

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional beachfront location with direct walkway access through sand dunes to Middleton Beach. Guests loved "falling asleep to the sound of the waves," spectacular gardens with "exuberant vegetation," native wildlife including bandicoots, and the "rhythmic lullaby of waves." Walking paths along the boardwalk and proximity to Albany's natural attractions highly praised.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently described as "spotlessly maintained," "immaculate," and "clean as a whistle." Amenities, bathrooms, and cabins received repeated praise for being "impeccable," though a few reviews mentioned older bathroom facilities needing updates and one isolated maintenance issue with locks.

Value (8/15) — Significant concern with recent pricing under new ownership. Multiple long-term guests reported 32-55% price increases, with quotes of $679-$1250 per night for peak season. Reviews frequently mentioned "overpriced," "price gouging," and being "twice that of equal parks," though some acknowledged "you pay for what you get" given the premium location.

Site Size (8/10) — Sites described as "decent size," "spacious," with concrete slabs for vans and grass areas. "Lots of grass" and "roomy" sites noted positively, but some reviews mentioned "incredibly tricky spots" for caravan manoeuvring on outer edges and middle rows when cars parked opposite.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong repeat visitation with families returning "year after year" and calling it "one of our all-time favourites" and "the best caravan park we've stayed at." High recommendation rates, though recent pricing changes caused some loyal customers to state they "won't be back." Overall sentiment remains very positive with families noting they "can't wait to come back."

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for beachfront position "right on the beach," walking distance to Middleton Beach cafes, tavern, and restaurants, close proximity to Albany town, ANZAC memorial, and regional attractions. Described as "unbeatable" location that "sets the benchmark."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Value concerns are substantial - multiple reviews documented steep price increases of 32-55% under new ownership, with guests calling it "price gouging," "extortion," and stating similar accommodation is "over $200 per night cheaper" elsewhere, making it prohibitive for many families.

2. Security issues raised by several guests noting thefts during peak times "on multiple visits," with the park being unsecured from both main entrance and ocean side, and management reportedly doing little to improve safety with CCTV or locked gates.

3. Some maintenance and facility concerns including bathroom facilities described as "very ordinary" and "very old" needing updates, one major lock issue leaving ladies without amenities access overnight, games room equipment 50% broken with poor maintenance, and inconsistent policies causing a catastrophic dog booking failure that left a family stranded.
