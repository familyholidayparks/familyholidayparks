PARK: Albany Happy Days Caravan Park
SCORE: 64/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 125

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note this park has no dedicated kids' entertainment, playground, or waterpark facilities. One reviewer explicitly stated "It's not ideal for kids" while another defending the park mentioned families were criticized for children making noise. Positive family reviews focus on self-directed activities like "fishing in the river," "ride bikes around," and kayaking, indicating families must create their own entertainment.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural environment with the park positioned "on the King River" providing "amazing river views." Multiple reviews mention "plenty of trees and birds around," "watching the kangaroos across the creek at sunset," and the ability to launch "kayaks and paddle boards right outside our site." Located "10km from town" offering peace while maintaining access to Albany's beaches and attractions.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding performance with reviews consistently calling it the "cleanest and best maintained caravan park" with "spotless amenities" cleaned "4 times a day." Reviewers note "immaculately kept park," "manicured lawns like a golf course," and facilities that are "cleaner than hotels." This is the park's strongest attribute across all reviews.

Value (12/15) — Good value mentioned at "$45 per night" and "very good price," though some reviews note billing issues with being "charged extra not once but twice." Generally considered reasonable for the quality provided, particularly given the exceptional maintenance standards, though not outstanding value compared to budget options.

Site Size (9/10) — Reviews consistently praise "spacious and level sites," "big sites," "large grassy sites," and "bays are so large, so you are not on top of each other." Sites feature "slab and green grass" with "plenty of space" for families. Minor deduction as "Site 1 & 2 only suitable for small van or camper trailers."

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong positive sentiment with many "will definitely return" comments and repeat visitors. However, significant concerning reviews about management including "appalling management and customer service," "staff are horrible," "owner was rude," and disputes over bookings and noise policies impact overall sentiment for families.

Location (10/10) — Perfect location score with park situated "only 11 mins drive from town," "10 minutes out of Albany," providing quiet riverside setting while maintaining easy access to "beaches and Albany town, shops." King River Tavern "right next door" and described as "close to everything" while being "far enough away to enjoy the starry skies."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of on-site children's entertainment facilities (playground, jumping pillow, games room, kids' club) which is critical for family holiday parks catering to young children.
2. Multiple serious complaints about management customer service including families with young children being told the park "couldn't care less if you, or your children, lived or died" and disputes over quiet time enforcement even before 10pm official hours.
3. Some operational inconsistencies including booking disputes ("gave the site to a person who just drove up"), billing errors ("charged extra not once but twice"), and mixed messages about family-friendliness versus enforcing strict quiet policies.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"Cleanest and best maintained caravan park I've stayed at", "Camped here with my family", "Kids were welcome and could ride bikes around and had a great time fishing in the river", "It's not ideal for kids", "spotless amenities", "told off for laughing, giggling and talking around 9:10 pm", "beautiful, quiet camp ground on the river", "Very good value at $45 per night"
