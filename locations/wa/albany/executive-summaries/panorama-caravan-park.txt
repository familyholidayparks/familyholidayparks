PARK: Panorama Caravan Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-26
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 221

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews confirm no playground, no kids club, no waterpark, and no structured children's activities; one review mentioned a barn with sitting area and kids corner, but entertainment is entirely nature-based with pelicans and bay views providing the main interest for children.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting right on the waterfront of Frenchman Bay with stunning water views, pelicans, and abundant birdlife; excellent proximity (10 minutes) to Torndirrup National Park featuring The Gap, Natural Bridge, blowholes, Misery Beach, and coastal walks; reviews consistently praise the peaceful waterfront location and natural beauty.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed evidence with many reviews praising "spotless" and "very clean" amenities that are cleaned daily, but multiple reviews cite dated facilities, dirty camp kitchen ("dirtiest we saw"), insufficient toilets leading to wet floors, and only 3-4 showers total for all guests creating queues during busy periods.

Value (13/15) — Strong value proposition with reviews noting "great value for money," "much better pricing than other Albany parks," "very very good price," and "best price you can find in Albany" for waterfront sites; powered sites noted as cheap even during school holidays.

Site Size (9/10) — Reviews consistently praise "huge camp sites," "large area heeps of room to move," "spacious" sites, "generous sized sites," and waterfront pitches described as "HUGE" with plenty of space; easy drive-through access for large vans.

Sentiment (8/10) — Predominantly positive sentiment with many guests extending stays and returning repeatedly ("been visiting for 10+ years"), though somewhat dampened by organizational issues, mixed facility feedback, and isolated negative experiences with management and permanent residents.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location 10-15 minutes from Albany CBD, perfectly positioned for Torndirrup National Park attractions (less than 10 minutes), close to beaches, whaling station, and wind farm; slightly out of town providing peace and quiet while remaining accessible to all attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Facilities are significantly dated with insufficient toilet/shower numbers (only 3-4 showers total) causing queues and complaints, and the camp kitchen described as "dirty," "disgusting," and "basic" by multiple reviewers.
2. Zero dedicated children's entertainment or facilities (no playground, no activities, no kids club) making this unsuitable for families seeking structured entertainment for young children.
3. Operational and management issues including booking system problems, permanent residents creating an atmosphere that detracts from tourist experience, dog management problems, and inconsistent service quality.
