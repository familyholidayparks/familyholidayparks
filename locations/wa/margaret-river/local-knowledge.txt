G'day! Margaret River's stunning stretch of WA coastline offers families the perfect mix of kid-friendly beaches, easy forest walks, and yes – plenty of chocolate and ice cream factories to bribe the little ones with. We've found April to October is ideal for families, as the waves calm down at spots like Prevelly and Gnarabup, making them safe for young swimmers, plus you'll dodge the scorching summer heat. Here's an insider tip: grab your free park pass from the visitor centre and you can drive right onto Hamelin Bay beach – the kids will lose their minds when those friendly stingrays glide up to the shore at sunset.
