Margaret River is a brilliant spot for a family getaway, sitting right on the WA coast between Perth and Busselton. The area's got stunning beaches that suit everyone – think calm sheltered bays perfect for little ones to splash about, and bigger breaks for the older kids to explore rock pools. The surrounding forest is genuinely impressive, with tall karri trees and native wildlife that'll keep the kids entertained without needing a theme park. You can do as much or as little as you fancy, from easy walks to spotting kangaroos and birdlife, plus there's enough going on that boredom isn't really an issue.

The area's got solid family appeal without being overcrowded or pretentious. There's wineries for the adults while kids run around the grounds, a few decent cafes and restaurants, and some good natural attractions like the Margaret River caves and seaside lookouts. For accommodation, you've got proper options in Discovery Parks – Margaret River, RAC Margaret River Nature Park, Margaret River Tourist Park, and Riverview Tourist Park, all offering caravan and cabin setups that make family trips easier. The main town's got everything you need – supermarkets, takeaway spots, petrol – so you're not caught out when someone gets hungry or cranky.
