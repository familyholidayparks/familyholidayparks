PARK: Fourseasons Holiday Park
SCORE: 38/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 81

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews mention a jumping pillow that kids enjoy, small pool, mini golf that's described as 'ghost town' condition and 'hasn't been touched for years', basketball ring broken, recreation hall closed and 'nothing in it for kids', playground equipment 'full of cob webbs'. Families note 'only thing kids had to do was bounce on the bouncing pillow'.

Nature (12/20) — Park features 'lovely lawns & beautiful trees', 'wonderful canopy of peppermint trees', 'shaded sites nestled amongst trees'. Beach access is '200 metres away' across the road, dog beach nearby. However, sites are 'right next to a busy road' with 'heavy traffic day and night', and park has 'abandoned trailers and buildings scattered around the edge'.

Cleanliness (3/15) — Overwhelmingly negative feedback: 'extremely run down', 'absolutely filthy and greasy' pots and pans, 'dirty bathrooms despite claims cleaned twice daily', 'toilets stink and really need a proper clean', 'film of dirt on all surfaces', 'mould in bathrooms', 'poop in all toilets', 'floors haven't seen vacuum or mop in a long time', 'bins so dirty you don't want to touch them'. Multiple reviews describe it as 'most uncleaned accommodation we've ever stayed at'.

Value (5/15) — Mixed but trending negative: described as 'extremely good value' and 'affordable' by some, but many cite '$45 a night for unpowered - what a joke', 'quite pricey for the conditions', prices 'nearly double' what it used to cost, '$500 cleaning fee whether unit is clean or not'. Several note 'an airbnb would've been cheaper'. Value perception severely impacted by poor condition.

Site Size (3/10) — Consistently poor: 'sites very tight, hard to get into', 'powered site was unfortunately quite tiny', 'extremely tight sites', 'could almost reach out the window and touch camper tent beside our van', 'no privacy whatsoever', 'very limited parking space', sites 'wedged in between permanent residents'. Families struggle with space for caravans and setups.

Sentiment (3/10) — Predominantly negative with strong warnings: 'would not recommend', 'never stay here', 'don't waste your money', 'will never book here again', 'couldn't wait to leave', 'save people from getting disappointed'. Long-term guests report 'standards have gone downhill badly over 10 years'. Small number of positive repeat visitors, mostly dog owners who value the location despite issues.

Location (4/10) — Best feature cited: 'excellent location' between Busselton and Dunsborough, 'beach right across the road', 'perfect hub to explore surrounding areas'. However, major negative is 'sites right next to busy road', 'very noisy even at night', 'trucks and cars traffic day and night'. Location advantage significantly undermined by road noise.
