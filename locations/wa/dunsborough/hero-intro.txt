# Dunsborough Family Holiday Guide

Dunsborough is a relaxed coastal town in Western Australia's South West region, perfectly positioned for families seeking sun, sand and straightforward fun. The area is blessed with some of the region's best beaches—Dunsborough Beach itself is patrolled and ideal for younger kids, while nearby Bunker Bay and Eagle Bay offer rockier terrain for exploring rock pools and spotting local wildlife. The town centre is compact and walkable, with fish and chip shops, cafes and a reasonable range of accommodation options including several family-friendly holiday parks like BIG4 Dunsborough Southern Stars and Fourseasons Holiday Park, plus the Tasman Holiday Parks options nearby in Yallingup.

What really sets Dunsborough apart is the combination of easy beach access with genuine natural attractions that keep kids engaged without requiring long drives. The Dunsborough Caves are just a short trip away, offering the novelty of underground exploration, while Cape Naturaliste Lighthouse provides coastal views and a bit of history to chat about on the walk. For families keen on a bit more adventure, the surrounding bushland has decent walking trails suitable for varied fitness levels, and the nearby Yallingup Caves and beaches give you plenty of options to mix things up across a week-long stay. It's the kind of place where you can do very little and have a great time, or keep the itinerary full—either way, the kids will sleep well.
