G'day! If you're hunting for the perfect family getaway, the holiday parks around Dunsborough are absolutely brilliant – we're talking pristine beaches where the kids can snorkel with tropical fish at Meelup Beach, plus those famous Margaret River wineries are just up the road (hello, cheese platters while the littlies play!). Spring and early summer (September to December) is magic here because the weather's warm enough for swimming but you'll miss the January crowds and those eye-watering peak season prices. Here's an insider tip: grab fish and chips from Clancy's in town on your first night, then head to the grassy area at Dunsborough Park for sunset – the kids can run around while you watch the ocean turn pink, and you'll feel like a proper local straightaway.
