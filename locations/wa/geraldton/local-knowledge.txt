G'day fellow family adventurers! Geraldton's stretch of the Coral Coast is where the outback meets some of Western Australia's most stunning turquoise waters, and the holiday parks here put you right in the sweet spot between wild beaches and fascinating history. We absolutely love visiting between April and October when the weather's perfect for snorkeling at the Abrolhos Islands and the wildflowers are blooming inland – plus you'll dodge those summer stinkers that can hit 40 degrees. Here's a local trick we learned from a Geraldton ranger: grab fresh crayfish straight off the boats at the fishermen's co-op on Marine Terrace around 11am, and most holiday parks will let you use their BBQ facilities to cook up your catch for a fraction of restaurant prices.
