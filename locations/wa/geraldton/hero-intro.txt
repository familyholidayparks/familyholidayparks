Geraldton is a genuine family-friendly destination on Western Australia's mid-coast, offering plenty of space, good weather, and plenty to keep the kids entertained. The town boasts some of the region's best beaches – Sunset Beach, Drummond Cove, and Back Beach all have calm, shallow waters perfect for young swimmers. Beyond the water, there's rock pooling at nearby coves, plenty of parkland for running around, and the nearby Abrolhos Islands offering boat trips that most kids find memorable. The town moves at a relaxed pace, so there's no rush – you'll find time to actually enjoy yourselves rather than ticking off a endless to-do list.

For accommodation, you've got solid options with several caravan parks well-suited to families: Sunset Beach Holiday Park and Drummond Cove Holiday Park are right near their namesake beaches, while Geraldton Belair Gardens and Geraldton Caravan Park offer good facilities and central locations. The local museum, botanical gardens, and coastal walks give you easy options on warmer or quieter days. Shopping and decent cafes are straightforward to find without feeling touristy. If you're after a low-stress coastal break where the kids can burn off energy, decent beaches, and manageable facilities, Geraldton delivers without the crowds you'll find further south.
