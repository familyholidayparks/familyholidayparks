PARK: Drummond Cove Holiday Park
SCORE: 69/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 58

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Limited on-site entertainment with a small, dated playground and occasional activities like Easter movie nights and kids activities mentioned by one reviewer; most families noted the park is better for outdoor play with space for bike riding on grassed areas rather than structured entertainment programs.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting overlooking the Indian Ocean with spectacular sunset views, walking trail to the beach (2-10 minute walk depending on fitness), peaceful coastal surroundings, and beautiful open spaces; reviews consistently praised the "stunning views," "beautiful ocean views," and tranquil location surrounded by nature.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed results with recent reviewers praising "spotless clean" amenities and "significantly updated" modern cabins, while older reviews (2023-2024) reported serious cleanliness issues including cockroach infestations, dirty blankets, mouse droppings, and unmaintained facilities; renovations appear to be addressing historical problems.

Value (12/15) — Generally good value with reviewers noting "excellent value for money" and "affordable" rates, though some noted price increases from $40 to $65 for powered sites caused concern; one negative review felt $150-$170 per night was excessive given past cabin conditions, but recent renovations seem to have improved value perception.

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised for "sites are huge," "nice and large" caravan sites, "big enough" spaces, and "plenty of parking"; families appreciated flat, grassed sites with enough room for kids to ride bikes and play.

Sentiment (8/10) — Overall positive sentiment in recent reviews with many saying they'd return and recommending the park, particularly praising staff friendliness and the view; however, several harsh 1-star reviews about past conditions, pricing disputes, and management issues tempered the overall sentiment score.

Location (9/10) — Located 10-15 minutes outside Geraldton with easy highway access, close to beach (2-10 minute walk), peaceful out-of-town setting with minimal road noise despite highway proximity; "great stopover spot" that's "quiet" and "tranquil" but requires driving to town facilities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Entertainment facilities are minimal for families—the playground is described as "small and old, needs updating" with "no playground facilities" mentioned in one detailed cabin review, and organized activities appear rare except for occasional holiday events.

2. Significant historical cleanliness and maintenance issues documented in 2023-2024 reviews including cockroach infestations, bed bugs, flooding bathrooms, power outages, and "dirty old cabins" that created serious concerns, though recent renovations appear to be addressing these problems gradually.

3. Inconsistent accommodation quality with cabin conditions varying dramatically depending on whether renovated or not, lack of wifi access, outdated facilities in unrenovated units, and operational issues like pool closures without advance notice disappointing families who specifically booked for those amenities.
