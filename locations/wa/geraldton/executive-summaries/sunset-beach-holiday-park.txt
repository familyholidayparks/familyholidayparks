PARK: Sunset Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 71/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 157

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Limited structured entertainment with reviews mentioning a playground with equipment for different age groups, BBQ areas, a small pool, and pool table in camp kitchen. No waterpark, jumping pillow, or organized kids activities mentioned. Families appreciate the playground upgrades but note the beach is the main entertainment drawcard.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting with direct beach access being the standout feature. Reviews consistently praise being "right on the beach," "steps from the ocean," stunning sunsets from the viewing platform, and the peaceful coastal environment. Some mention pink sand nearby and beautiful ocean views. Minor concerns about beach erosion and dead lawns in dry weather.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "spotlessly clean amenities" and thorough daily cleaning, but significant concerns raised about inconsistent standards. Multiple reviews mention filthy cabins with dog hair, spiders, cockroaches, mold, dust, and unidentified stains. Amenities described as "dated but clean" by most, though some report wet floors and maintenance issues.

Value (10/15) — Frequent complaints about pricing with reviews calling it "overpriced," "expensive for what it was," and "not worth it." Rates mentioned include $175-$249/night for cabins and $42-$60 for powered sites. Some guests feel facilities don't match the price point, though the beachfront location is seen as offsetting value concerns for many families.

Site Size (7/10) — Site sizes receive mixed feedback with some praising "spacious sites" and "good sized caravan sites," while others complain about cramped conditions, inability to use awnings on drive-through sites, and sites being "too narrow." Several reviews mention sites are "large enough" but just barely, with concrete slabs not always well-positioned.

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive sentiment with many repeat visitors and recommendations, though tempered by frustration about inconsistent service and facility quality. Common phrases include "we will be back," "highly recommend," and "loved our stay," but balanced by "never again" and disappointment from guests expecting higher Big4 standards.

Location (6/10) — Located about 10 minutes north of Geraldton CBD, which is both praised for being away from traffic noise and criticized for being less central. Walking distance to IGA supermarket (15-20 minutes) is appreciated. Direct beach access is the major location strength, though some note the beach itself is "nothing special" compared to town beaches.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant cleanliness inconsistencies with multiple reports of filthy cabins, pest problems (spiders, cockroaches, ants), and unacceptable accommodation conditions that resulted in guests leaving immediately and requesting refunds.

2. Security and theft concerns with multiple documented incidents of items stolen from vehicles including Engel fridges and bicycles, with management providing inadequate warnings or responses to safety issues.

3. Variable and often poor customer service experiences including rude reception staff, interrogation-style check-ins, inflexible policies, lack of warm welcomes, and staff who appear disinterested or unhelpful to paying guests.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
