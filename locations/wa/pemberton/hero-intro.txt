# Pemberton, Western Australia

Pemberton is a relaxed coastal town in the south-west that punches above its weight for families looking to escape the city. You've got beautiful beaches right on your doorstep – think sandy stretches perfect for a swim, rock pools to explore, and enough space for the kids to run around without feeling crowded. The town itself is easy to navigate with everything you need within a short drive, and Pemberton Caravan Park offers solid accommodation options if you're planning to base yourselves here for a few days.

What makes Pemberton genuinely good for families is the mix of natural attractions without needing to trek too far. There's plenty of native bushland to poke around in, walking trails that won't kill your legs, and good spots for spotting local wildlife. The beaches are manageable – not overly touristy, which means fewer crowds during school holidays – and the water's generally swimmable during the warmer months. It's the kind of place where kids can actually be kids, parents can actually relax, and you don't need to spend a fortune to have a decent time.
