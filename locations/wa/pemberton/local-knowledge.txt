G'day! Pemberton's towering karri forests and crisp country air make it one of Western Australia's most underrated spots for families who love the outdoors – the kids will go wild climbing the Gloucester Tree or splashing in the Warren River on a warm afternoon. We reckon autumn (March to May) is absolutely magic here, when the forests turn golden and the weather's perfect for hiking without the summer crowds. Here's a local tip most visitors miss: grab fresh trout from Pemberton Freshwater Research Centre on Friday afternoons and cook it up at your holiday park that evening – the kids can watch the fish being fed before you buy, and it doesn't get much fresher than that!
