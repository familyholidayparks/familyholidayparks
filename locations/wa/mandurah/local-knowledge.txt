G'day! Mandurah's calm waterways and protected beaches make it absolutely brilliant for families with little ones who aren't quite ready for the Indian Ocean's bigger swells – plus the dolphins here are so friendly they'll often swim right up to your boat or paddleboard. We reckon April through May is your sweet spot: the water's still warm enough for swimming, school holiday crowds have thinned out, and you'll dodge the summer's occasional sea breeze that can whip up in the arvo. Here's a local trick we learned from a Mandurah regular – grab fish and chips from Cicerello's, then head to the grassed area at the eastern foreshore where the kids can feed the pelicans at 2:45pm sharp (they turn up like clockwork!).
