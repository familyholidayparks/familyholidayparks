# Mandurah: A Family Holiday Destination

Mandurah sits about an hour south of Perth and offers families a laid-back coastal escape without the fuss. The town's main drawcard is its pristine beaches – Falcon Bay and Silver Sands are perfect for kids, with gentle waters, lifeguard patrols, and plenty of space to run around. Beyond the sand, you've got the Mandurah Estuary for exploring by boat or kayak, and several good walking trails through natural bushland where you might spot dolphins, birdlife, and native wildflowers. The town itself stays pleasantly low-key, meaning fewer crowds than you'd get up in Perth, but with enough cafes, fish and chip shops, and ice cream spots to keep everyone fed and happy.

For families, Mandurah ticks the practical boxes too. The Mandurah Caravan and Tourist Park and Mandurah Coastal Holiday Park both offer solid accommodation options, whether you're bringing a van, caravan, or just looking for self-contained cabins. Key attractions include the Mandurah Performing Arts Centre if you want an indoor option on a rainy day, and the local playgrounds and parks scattered throughout town. The estuary cruises are popular with younger kids, and the foreshore area has good facilities including shaded areas and public toilets. It's the sort of place where you can keep things simple – beach days, nature walks, and early dinners – without feeling like you're missing out on anything.
