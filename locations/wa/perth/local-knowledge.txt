G'day! Perth's holiday parks are an absolute ripper for families, with that unique combo of pristine Indian Ocean beaches, the stunning Swan Valley on your doorstep, and enough space that the kids can actually run around without bumping into crowds. We've found September through November is ideal – the wildflowers are putting on a show, the weather's warm but not scorching, and you'll dodge both the winter rain and the January heat that sends everyone scrambling for aircon. Here's a local tip that'll save you a packet: grab your free transit pass on Sundays and Mondays (yep, it's called the SmartRider Family Funday) and take the kids from your park into the city or down to Fremantle without spending a cent on transport.
