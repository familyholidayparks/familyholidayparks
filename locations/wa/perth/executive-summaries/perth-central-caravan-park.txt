PARK: Perth Central Caravan Park
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 119

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (3/20) — Reviews consistently note there is no playground and "definitely need some things to do for small children besides a pool." The small pool is the only entertainment facility mentioned, with multiple families stating it's inadequate for children's entertainment.

Nature (12/20) — The park benefits from proximity to Swan River with "beautiful little river/bush walk just a hop skip & a jump away" and "surrounded by nature which makes it feel like you're in a rural area." However, immediate surroundings include highway proximity and construction, limiting the natural environment appeal.

Cleanliness (6/15) — Major concerns dominate reviews: "black mould everywhere and even in the air con," "showers & toilets are stuck in the 70s," "bathrooms are filthy and some of the shower heads are broken off," "shower floors have tiles missing," and "the worst i have seen during my 6 weeks of camping trip." While some positive comments exist about cabin cleanliness, amenity blocks receive overwhelming negative feedback.

Value (5/15) — Families consistently report poor value: "most expensive of our stays," "Not worth $270 a night for a family," "expensive in comparison to other parks considering the condition," and "too expensive for very little." The pricing doesn't match facility quality according to family reviewers.

Site Size (4/10) — Reviews repeatedly mention "small spaces," "ridiculously small allocated sites," "very tight to reverse into," "sites are very very small and so hard to get vans in and out," and "parking spots tight to get into." Tree roots and obstacles within sites create additional challenges.

Sentiment (5/10) — Families express significant safety concerns: "very unsafe feel about it," "definitely do not bring families here," "my sister felt so scared at night," and "DO NOT STAY HERE." While some long-term residents and airport users are satisfied, family traveller sentiment is predominantly negative with strong warnings to avoid.

Location (7/10) — Strong proximity to airport ("5 min to airport") and city access via public transport. Close to Woolworths, petrol stations, and Swan River. However, highway noise and urban setting detract from holiday atmosphere.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness and maintenance issues in amenity blocks with widespread reports of mould, broken fixtures, missing tiles, and facilities described as "the worst" and "stuck in the 70s" that are unsuitable for families.
2. Complete absence of children's entertainment facilities beyond a small pool, with no playground, activities, or kids' club, making it inappropriate for family holidays with young children.
3. Safety and security concerns raised by multiple families citing an "unsafe feel," no gates or security, permanent residents creating uncomfortable atmosphere, and sites described as a "trailer park" rather than family holiday park.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"DO NOT STAY HERE", "worst i have seen during my 6 weeks of camping trip", "black mould everywhere and even in the air con", "trailer park not a caravan park for holiday makers", "very unsafe feel about it", "definitely do not bring families here", "ridiculously small allocated sites", "Not worth $270 a night for a family"
