PARK: Crystal Brook Caravan Park
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-06-17
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 66

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (3/20) — Reviews explicitly state "Not kid Friendly" with "nothing flash or any extra amenities available." Multiple families note the complete absence of playground equipment, kids' club, or organized activities. One detailed review specifically warns families that "there might be better options when you consider...park facilities for kids."

Nature (12/20) — Several reviews praise natural elements with mentions of "kookaburras wake us up...Roos hang round at night" and the park being "at the foot of hills walking trails just a short 10min drive away." However, multiple reviews criticize the lack of green space, stating "more grass more trees needed" and sites described as "concrete pad."

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with recent reviews showing decline. While many older reviews praise "very clean," "toilets and showers cleaned 2 times a day," and "spotless" facilities, multiple 2025-2026 reviews report deterioration under new management: "ablution block was unclean and dirty," "cabin we stayed in had mould," and facilities not cleaned for "over 24h" during water issues.

Value (6/15) — Consistent complaints about pricing with reviews stating "expensive for a concrete pad," "quite expensive for what it is," "way too overpriced for what it was," and "for 49$..it's a HUGE NO from me." Several families recommend paying slightly more for Big4 parks with better amenities.

Site Size (4/10) — Significant concerns raised with "unpowered sites were 4 very cramped spots in the corner," "Too Cramped for my Liking," and "no space for anything." One review mentions "huge" powered sites, but the majority of family-accessible sites are described as inadequate with one family "located right next to Dump Point."

Sentiment (3/10) — Overwhelmingly negative family sentiment in recent reviews. Multiple families report feeling "literally unsafe," being "kicked...to the street" with a "5month old daughter," and experiencing "horrible and disrespectful" management. Reviews warn "I don't recommend this park at all" and describe "bad energy." The park is "97% permanent residents" creating an unwelcoming environment for holidaying families.

Location (7/10) — Good proximity advantages with "close to Perth," "7km Perth Airport," and "close to city." Shopping is "ten minutes away" and there's access to Perth hills attractions. However, "no retail services within walking distance" limits convenience for families.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Completely unsuitable for families with young children - explicitly labeled "Not kid Friendly" with zero entertainment facilities, no playground, no kids' activities, and multiple families reporting unsafe feelings and unwelcoming atmosphere from predominantly permanent residents.
2. Severe site cramping and space issues - families describe "cramped spots in the corner," concrete pads with inadequate grass/trees, sites located next to dump points, and "no space for anything," making it impractical for family caravans and outdoor play.
3. Significant management and safety concerns - multiple recent reviews detail families being evicted with babies, "horrible and disrespectful" office staff, feeling "literally unsafe" with residents approaching about car contents, bond money withheld, mould in cabins, and a notable decline since 2024 management change.
