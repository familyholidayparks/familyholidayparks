PARK: Discovery Holiday Park Perth (Swan Valley)
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-05-28
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 354

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Limited on-site entertainment with a small pool that families mention is "lovely and clean" but "on the smaller side" and frequently closed for maintenance. Kids enjoyed the basic playground and some mention a games room and theatre room, but many families noted "not much to do like other parks not child friendly as much" with minimal organized activities or dedicated kids' entertainment areas.

Nature (12/20) — Park situated in Swan Valley wine region with vineyard views from some sites and "surrounded by lovely trees and greenery" creating a "serene environment." Guests appreciate being "close to the beautiful Perth Hills" and nearby Whiteman Park, though the park itself is more suburban than bushland with limited natural exploration opportunities for children.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Highly inconsistent with modern amenities praised as "spotless" and "the best amenities blocks in WAs van parks," but multiple reports of "cabin not cleaned between guests floor was filthy," "ants everywhere," "mould in the shower," camp kitchen "disgustingly dirty bins not emptied," and "toilet paper not replaced for 2 days." Major variance between new and older facilities.

Value (9/15) — Mixed value perception with many finding it "pretty average for the price" and "EXPENSIVE" at $60-70/night for basic sites. Families report "$260 per night" cabins, "almost $900 for three nights," and "not worth the price they charge." Some appreciated "reasonable prices" for location but overall sentiment suggests overpriced for facilities and experience provided.

Site Size (6/10) — Caravan sites described as "very small" with families "unable to pull out awning," "too small for 20ft caravan," "dense layout," and tent sites "super small" on "hard rock dirt." Positive mentions of "great size sites" and "spacious sites" mainly for drive-through and ensuite options, but unpowered and standard sites consistently criticized as cramped.

Sentiment (7/10) — Polarized feedback with loyal repeat visitors praising staff and amenities, but significant negative experiences around theft ("$5000 worth of stuff stolen," "had food stolen from fridge," "stuff stolen of front porch"), safety concerns, and disappointment leading many to state "wouldn't stay here again," "will not be returning," and "would never stay there again." Recommendation rate heavily impacted by security issues.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location "right next to the swan valley," "close to airport," "surrounded by wineries," with easy access to shopping at DFO, Midland Gate, and attractions. Convenient for "fly in fly out days" and exploring Perth, though noise from "planes taking off," "trains with horns blaring," and busy road noted by some families.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Serious and repeated security/theft issues with multiple families reporting stolen belongings, management showing "complete lack of responsibility," no security cameras in key areas, and police confirming "ongoing issues with theft" creating an unsafe environment for family holidays.

2. Severe inconsistency in accommodation quality and cleanliness, with modern cabins praised but numerous reports of ant infestations, uncleaned units, broken fixtures, flooding bathrooms, maintenance neglect, and tent sites described as unsuitable with facilities "over 150m away" and "NO TOILET."

3. Inadequate family entertainment and facilities for a holiday park, with a small pool frequently closed, minimal kids' activities, poor WiFi connectivity throughout ("only works 5m from office"), and a focus on cabin accommodation over caravan/camping facilities leading many families to find it "not child friendly as much" compared to other Discovery Parks.
