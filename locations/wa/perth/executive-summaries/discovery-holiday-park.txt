PARK: Discovery Holiday Park Perth WA
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 354

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Reviews mention a small pool that kids enjoy, playground facilities, and occasional special events like Halloween activities, but limited structured kids entertainment or programs. Theatre room and games room noted but not widely praised. No waterpark or major entertainment facilities.

Nature (13/20) — Located near Swan Valley wineries with vineyard views from some sites, surrounded by trees and greenery creating a "leafy" atmosphere. Adjacent to paddocks and river parks good for dog walking. However, it's essentially suburban/semi-rural rather than natural bushland, and airport proximity noted frequently.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Very mixed feedback with recent reviews praising "spotless" and "immaculate" renovated amenities and "best ameniete blocks in WAs van parks," but multiple reports of dirty cabins, ants, cockroaches, mould in showers, uncleaned camp kitchens with overflowing bins, and maintenance issues. Cleanliness appears inconsistent between different accommodation types.

Value (10/15) — Significant complaints about pricing with terms like "EXPENSIVE," "overpriced," "exorbitant" appearing frequently. Prices range $50-$300+ per night. Many feel it's not good value given issues, though some find it reasonable for the location. G'day Parks membership provides discounts.

Site Size (7/10) — Caravan sites described as "generous," "spacious," and "great size" by many, with good concrete slabs and drive-through options. However, multiple complaints about unpowered tent sites being "super small," cramped, uneven, and some powered sites too small for larger rigs. Site quality varies significantly.

Sentiment (7/10) — Mixed overall sentiment with strong loyal repeat visitors praising staff and facilities, but significant recent negative reviews about theft, poor management response, cleanliness issues, and feeling unwelcome. Approximately 60% positive sentiment with notable recent decline in satisfaction regarding security and customer service.

Location (8/10) — Excellent location close to Perth Airport (5-10 minutes), Swan Valley wineries, shopping centres (DFO, Midland Gate), and central to Perth attractions. Easy highway access. However, airport noise during day, traffic noise from busy roads, and not directly on beach or in pristine natural setting limits the score.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Security is a critical issue with multiple reviews reporting theft of valuables, bikes, and car break-ins, with management showing poor response and no security cameras or guards, creating an unsafe environment that overshadows other positives.

2. WiFi is consistently described as "terrible," "unusable," "extremely slow" or non-existent in cabins, with poor mobile phone reception, making it impossible for families to stay connected or entertained indoors.

3. On-site kids entertainment is minimal with just a small pool and basic playground—no structured programs, limited activities, and facilities that pale in comparison to dedicated family holiday parks.
