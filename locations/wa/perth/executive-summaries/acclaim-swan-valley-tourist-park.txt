PARK: Acclaim Swan Valley Tourist Park
SCORE: 38/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-14
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 80

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Multiple families reported the kids playground as "flooded and not functioning", pool frequently closed with "no shade" and "poorly maintained", games room with pool table mentioned but limited on-site kids activities or entertainment programs.

Nature (14/20) — Located in beautiful Swan Valley wine region with "kangaroos come to say hello at dusk", "hear the sweet song of the Kookaburra at sunrise", unpowered sites with "lots of shade", surrounded by vineyards and natural environment, though near airport flight path.

Cleanliness (3/15) — Overwhelming evidence of poor cleanliness: "toilets disgusting", "missing and cracked tiles, holes in walls", "smell of fish/blocked drains", "everything dirty/broken", "long grass", "spider webs all over", "mould" in bathrooms, "facilities falling apart", many reviews citing unsafe and unhygienic conditions.

Value (7/15) — Mixed value feedback: some praised "35$ unpowered and 45$ powered one of the cheapest around Perth", "great value", but many families felt "overpriced" at "$45-85 per night" given the poor condition, with families leaving and seeking refunds due to conditions not matching the price.

Site Size (6/10) — Sites described as "cramped and very hard to get into" for big rigs, "sites a little tight", "very small turning area for caravans", though some noted "plenty room for van car and awning" and "large enough to also have 2 vehicles parked".

Sentiment (3/10) — Predominantly negative family sentiment with multiple "would never recommend", "do not stay here", "not somewhere you'd want to bring your family", "did not feel safe", families leaving within minutes, though some longer-term guests found it acceptable for basic needs.

Location (9/10) — Consistently praised location: "perfect spot for all the great things the Valley has to offer", "right in the centre of all the action that the Swan Valley offers", "close to all the great vineyards", "15mn drive to Perth and airport", "great location for concerts in the valley".

WHY NOT 100:
1. Critical safety and cleanliness failures with multiple families reporting feeling unsafe, unusable facilities, broken locks, and disgusting bathrooms that are fundamentally unsuitable for young children.
2. Non-functional children's amenities with playground flooded and not working, pool frequently closed or dirty, and no organized kids entertainment or activities for family holidays.
3. Severely deteriorated maintenance across the park with old broken facilities, no hot water incidents, leaking taps, cracked slabs, long grass, spider webs, mould, and infrastructure described as "falling apart" requiring major investment.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"not somewhere you'd want to bring your family", "kids park flooded and not functioning", "did not feel safe", "toilets disgusting", "everything dirty/broken", "pool was closed", "Park needs a big update", "perfect location in the swan valley"
