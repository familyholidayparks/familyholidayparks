PARK: Acclaim Swan Valley Tourist Park
SCORE: 38/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-14
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 80

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews consistently report the kids playground as "flooded and not functioning", pool "closed" during hot weather, or "poorly maintained". One positive noted "games room with pool table and darts" and a 6-year-old loved watching planes, but multiple families specifically warned "not somewhere you'd want to bring your family" due to lack of functioning facilities.

Nature (14/20) — The park is located in the scenic Swan Valley wine region with reviews praising "great nature around", "kangaroos come to say hello at dusk", "sweet song of the Kookaburra at sunrise", and "unpowered sites got lots of shade". The natural surroundings and proximity to vineyards, honey farms, and local markets provide good nature access, though the park itself is aging.

Cleanliness (3/15) — This is a critical weakness with overwhelming evidence: "toilets disgusting", "missing and cracked tiles, holes in walls, smell of fish/blocked drains", "bathroom ceiling full of mould", "spider webs all over", "long grass", "amenities old and falling apart", "deplorable sites", "filthy", "didn't even dare to use amenities", and "cleaner only has 20mins to clean the toilet".

Value (7/15) — Mixed feedback with some praising "affordable", "good price", "35$ unpowered and 45$ powered one of the cheapest around Perth", and "great value". However, many felt it was "overpriced" at "$45 a night. Rip off!", "$85 for 1 night" considered excessive given the poor condition, with several stating they found better accommodation elsewhere for $60.

Site Size (6/10) — Reviews indicate "large enough to have 2 vehicles parked", "plenty room for van car and awning", and "concrete & grass sites". However, multiple warnings about "sites cramped and very hard to get into", "tight for longer vans", "bad layout", roads with no turnaround requiring reversing, and "massive potholes" in the driveway.

Sentiment (3/10) — Predominantly negative family sentiment with serious safety concerns: "did not feel safe", "felt very unsafe", "constantly worried about belongings", "undesirable people", reports of domestic violence incidents, drug use, and "mentally ill man shouting and banging". Several families left within minutes or hours. A few long-term residents and budget travellers had positive experiences, but family recommendation rate is very low.

Location (9/10) — The strongest aspect with consistent praise: "great location", "fabulous location", "perfect spot", "awesome location right in the centre of all the action that the Swan Valley offers", "close to all the great vineyards", "ideal situation for visiting Swan Valley", "nearby Perth and Perth airport (15mn drive to both)", convenient to attractions and wineries.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Critical cleanliness and maintenance failures with disgusting bathroom facilities, broken fixtures, mould, spider webs, and overwhelmingly negative hygiene reports that make this unsuitable for families with young children.
2. Non-functioning or closed children's facilities including flooded playground and pool frequently closed during hot weather when families need it most, with no reliable kids entertainment infrastructure.
3. Serious safety concerns reported by multiple families including permanent residents with concerning behavior, noise at all hours, drug use, domestic violence incidents, and families feeling unsafe enough to leave within hours of arrival.
