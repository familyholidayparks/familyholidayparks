PARK: Acclaim Kingsway Tourist Park
SCORE: 28/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-18
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 130

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (3/20) — Multiple families reported playground closed for renovations, games room closed, tennis courts unusable and run down, basketball court described as "below average". One family specifically noted "expectations there would be a lot for the kids to do" but found facilities non-operational. No evidence of organised kids activities or entertainment programs.

Nature (6/20) — Limited natural environment on site with some large gum trees mentioned positively by a few guests. Nature reserve walk trail mentioned across the road. Located 15 minutes from beaches and 20 minutes from CBD. Traffic noise from adjacent busy road consistently complained about. No creek, bush walks or significant natural features on property.

Cleanliness (2/15) — Overwhelming evidence of poor cleanliness: "dirtiest caravan park", "disgusting toilets", "bathroom shower had so much grime", "floors were so filthy my socks were black", "cockroach crawling on me", "amenities disgusting", "hair and sand throughout shower cubicles", "toilets not cleaned once during 8 day stay", "dead insects everywhere", "mould rotting wood and mildew". Multiple families cited filth as reason for cutting stays short.

Value (5/15) — Described as "overpriced" and "complete waste of money" by multiple families. "$50 a night" with terrible conditions. Several noted paying premium prices for substandard facilities. A few guests acknowledged "great value for money" and "very affordable" compared to Big4 parks, but majority felt prices didn't match the poor condition of facilities and cabins.

Site Size (6/10) — Positive mentions of "huge slabbed sites", "large caravan sites", "nice spacious pitches" and "large concrete sites". However, broken concrete pavers and poor maintenance of pads noted frequently. Mix of permanent residents and tourists with some cramped placement reported.

Sentiment (2/10) — Predominantly negative family sentiment with repeated warnings: "would never stay again", "would never recommend any family staying here", "absolutely unacceptable - avoid", "AVOID AVOID AVOID", "do not waste your time booking here". Multiple families cut stays short. One family of four "did not go out after the sunset" due to safety concerns. Very low repeat visitation and recommendation rate from families.

Location (4/10) — Good proximity to shops (5-10 minute walk), public transport at doorstep, 20 minutes to CBD, 15 minutes to beaches. However, severe traffic noise from adjacent Wanneroo Road consistently complained about affecting sleep. "Road racing/noise at night is beyond a joke" with families unable to sleep.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness and hygiene failures with families reporting disgusting bathrooms, cockroaches, mould, filthy floors and amenities not cleaned for entire stays - creating unsafe conditions especially for young children.
2. Critical safety concerns including broken concrete causing trip hazards, poor lighting at night, non-functioning fire alarms in cabins, and families feeling unsafe enough to not leave accommodation after dark.
3. Children's facilities completely inadequate with playground closed for renovations, games room closed, tennis courts unusable, and no organised kids activities or entertainment - the opposite of what family holiday parks should provide.
