G'day! Kalbarri's dramatic red gorges and pristine coastline make it an absolute cracker for families who love mixing beach days with outback adventures—your kids can be snorkelling with turtles in the morning and scrambling through ancient river gorges by afternoon. The sweet spot for visiting is April to October when the weather's mild and the wildflowers put on their famous show (late July to September is peak), though winter nights can get surprisingly chilly. Here's a local tip that'll save you some tears: if you're planning to visit Nature's Window or the coastal cliffs, go early morning before it heats up, and always pack way more water than you think you'll need—that Kalbarri sun is no joke, and the nearest shop can be a fair drive from the national park lookouts.
