G'day families! If you're dreaming of that postcard-perfect WA coast without the crowds, Esperance is where turquoise water meets powder-white sand in the most spectacular way – and yes, it really does look that blue in person. The sweet spot for visiting is November through April when the water's warm enough for the kids to snorkel at spots like Twilight Beach, though locals will tell you the wildflowers in September make the Cape Le Grand drives absolutely magical. Here's an insider tip: grab your fish and chips from Taylor Street Jetty at sunset, then head straight to the Tanker Jetty where the stingrays glide right up to the pylons – it's free entertainment that beats any theme park, and your kids will talk about it for years.
