PARK: Broome's Gateway Pet Friendly Caravan Park
SCORE: 52/100
DATE SCORED: 2025
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 73

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — No on-site kids entertainment, playground, pool, or activities mentioned. Multiple reviews explicitly state "not family friendly" with additional charges of $20/night for children over 12. No facilities to keep young children entertained.

Nature (14/20) — Strong natural bush setting described as "bush camping with amenities", "rural/station atmosphere", and "country feel". Reviews mention "beautiful waking up to the sound of birds", shady trees, and peaceful environment. However, described as "red dirt" and "dusty with not a single patch of grass".

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed feedback with recent reviews praising "spotless" facilities and "very clean" showers/toilets. However, older reviews mention "infested with ants", "washing machines out of order", and "some areas need a good clean up". Maintenance appears inconsistent.

Value (8/15) — Polarized opinions. Many praise pricing at $36-45/night as "half the cost of town" and "great price". However, multiple families feel it's "very expensive" and "absolute robbery" for unpowered dirt sites with limited facilities. The $20/child surcharge and lack of dump point reduce family value significantly.

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised with phrases like "huge sites", "large sites", "generous sites", "way bigger than any caravan park", and "heaps of room between sites". Clear strength of the park.

Sentiment (5/10) — Mixed sentiment with many positive reviews from couples/retirees but families expressing disappointment. Generator noise complaints common. Some guests extended stays, others said "come here as a last resort" or "spend your money somewhere else".

Location (2/10) — Major weakness with consistent mentions of "20-30km out of Broome", "inconvenient distance", "has to drive 20 mins into broome every day". Not walkable to town or attractions. Dirt access road described as "bumpy".

WHY NOT 100:
1. No family facilities whatsoever - zero kids entertainment, no playground, no pool, no activities, and explicit reviews stating "not family friendly" with surcharges for children over 12
2. Remote location 20-30km from Broome requiring daily drives to access town, beaches, and attractions, making it impractical for families wanting to explore the region
3. No powered sites available, only generators allowed 8am-8pm creating noise issues, plus missing basic infrastructure like dump point, and inconsistent cleanliness with ant infestations reported
