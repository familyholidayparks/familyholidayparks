PARK: Discovery Parks - Broome
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-13
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 193

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews mention proximity to the public splash park, playground, and jetty for fishing. Some families appreciated yoga, kids club, and food vans on-site, but many noted these activities were cancelled or inconsistent, especially off-season. Limited on-site entertainment compared to expectations.

Nature (17/20) — Oceanfront location with direct beach access, turquoise water views, and opportunities to see turtles, dolphins, stingrays, and fish from the jetty. Families enjoyed the natural setting, sunsets, and staircase to the moon viewing. However, swimming not recommended due to crocodile and stinger risk.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback. Many praised modern, clean facilities and spotless ensuites, while others reported cobwebs, dusty restrooms, poor drainage in showers, and backpackers from nearby car park using facilities, leaving them dirty. Cleanliness standards varied across different amenity blocks.

Value (8/15) — Widely criticized as expensive, with many calling it overpriced ($90-$115/night for powered sites). Families felt the price didn't match the experience given security issues, inconsistent service, and facility concerns. Some appreciated the location justified cost, but value concerns were dominant.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive about large, spacious sites with room for caravans, awnings, and setup. Beachfront and ensuite sites especially praised. Some complaints about uneven, sandy, or dirt sites, and tight entry/exit during renovations. Overall, decent space for larger rigs.

Sentiment (6/10) — Sentiment heavily polarized. Many loved the location, views, and staff, with repeat stays planned. However, significant negative sentiment around theft, security, inconsistent service, poor communication, and management attitude. Trust and safety concerns deterred repeat visits for many families.

Location (6/10) — Excellent proximity to Town Beach, markets, Matsos Brewery, jetty, and town (walkable or short drive). Central and accessible. However, recurring complaints about open access from beach allowing trespassers, lack of security fencing, and safety concerns in the area reduced location appeal.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Security is a major, recurring issue with multiple reports of theft, break-ins, trespassers from the beach, and lack of effective fencing, cameras, or night patrols, making families feel unsafe.
2. Value for money is poor, with families consistently calling the park overpriced ($90-$115/night) compared to the experience, especially given security and service issues.
3. Customer service and communication are inconsistent, with reports of rude staff, unanswered phones, unhelpful reception, language barriers with backpacker staff, and management dismissive of guest concerns.
