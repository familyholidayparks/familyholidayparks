Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding cleanliness consistently praised: "spotlessly clean," "cleanest park I have stayed at," "amenities are in fantastic condition they are cleaned daily," "best caravan park amenities ever," "squeaky clean throughout"; new modern bathroom facilities repeatedly highlighted as exceptional; grounds "regularly raked" and "immaculately maintained"

Value (14/15) — Excellent value positioning: "cheapest price in the area," "well priced overall," "best value," "very reasonable price," "$42 per night for two people unpowered"; multiple reviews compare favorably to more expensive parks; only deduction for occasional complaints about cancellation policies and one review calling it "very expensive for a patch of red dust"

Site Size (8/10) — Generally good site sizes: "large sites," "good size sites," "spacious," "reasonably large," "heaps of room for your van"; some reviewers noted "some sites are a tad small" and one mentioned their "first site was a bit tight" requiring a change; variable site quality with some grassy and others bare dirt/sand

Sentiment (9/10) — Very positive overall sentiment with high recommendation rates: "would definitely stay again" appears frequently, "we kept extending our stay," "stayed for 2 months," "stayed here for 3 weeks"; strong repeat visitation indicated; only minor deductions for 4-5 very negative reviews citing aggressive management and poor customer service experiences

Location (10/10) — Perfect location score: "walking distance to Cable Beach," "15-20 min walk to beach," "bus stop right out front," "close to everything," "perfectly located"; easy access to town, restaurants, and Divers Tavern; Broome Explorer bus stop directly at entrance for those without vehicles

WHY NOT 100:

2. Inconsistent site quality with some sites being bare dirt/sand rather than grass, creating dusty conditions in windy weather and reducing comfort for families setting up play areas for children.
3. Several credible reviews cite aggressive management behavior, strict policies (12-point pet agreement, inflexible refund policies), and incidents where guests were asked to leave for posting negative reviews, creating potential stress for families.
