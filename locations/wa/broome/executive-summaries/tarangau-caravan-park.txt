PARK: Tarangau Caravan Park
SCORE: 67/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 99

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Reviews consistently note the absence of onsite entertainment, with multiple mentions of "no pool" and no playground facilities. One review mentions occasional Friday night live music and food trucks. The park positions itself as a quiet, relaxed alternative to larger entertainment-focused parks.

Nature (16/20) — Strong scores for natural environment with "lots of shade," "huge trees," "beautiful frangipani trees," and "loads of trees." The park is 10-20 minutes walking distance to Cable Beach, with multiple reviews praising the easy beach access. Some families note it can be "very dusty when very windy" and a few sites lack shade.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding cleanliness receives universal praise across recent reviews: "spotlessly clean," "cleanest park I have stayed at," "amenities are in fantastic condition they are cleaned daily," "best caravan park amenities ever." The new amenities block is repeatedly described as exceptional with "massive toilet cubicles" and "piped music."

Value (14/15) — Excellent value recognition with "cheapest price in the area," "well priced overall," "best value," and "reasonably priced" appearing frequently. Reviews note unpowered sites at $42-44 for two people. A few negative comments about cancellation policies and one review calling it "very expensive for a patch of red dust" prevent a perfect score.

Site Size (8/10) — Good site sizes with "large sites," "good size sites," and "reasonably large" mentioned frequently. However, multiple reviews note "some sites are a tad small" and concerns about bare dirt/sand on some sites rather than grass. Grassy, shaded sites are available but not universal.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very positive overall sentiment with many extended stays mentioned ("lived there for months," "stayed 3 weeks," "2 months"). Strong repeat visitation intent with "would definitely stay again" appearing frequently. A handful of strongly negative reviews about aggressive management and poor customer service prevent a perfect score.

Location (10/10) — Perfect location scores with Cable Beach consistently described as 10-20 minutes walk, "bus stop right out front," and "close to everything by foot." Multiple reviews praise the quieter location away from town while remaining highly accessible.

WHY NOT 100:
[1] Complete absence of family entertainment facilities (no pool, no playground, no kids' activities) significantly limits appeal for families with active young children seeking onsite activities.
[2] Several credible negative reviews from 2023-2025 describe aggressive management behavior, strict pet policies with 12 requirements, and poor handling of customer service situations including asking guests to leave over negative reviews.
[3] Inconsistent site quality with some sites described as "bare dirt," "patch of red dust," and "no shade" while others are grassy and shaded, plus complaints about excessive speed bumps affecting vehicle access.
