G'day! If you're after a proper family adventure where red dirt meets turquoise water, the holiday parks around Broome deliver something genuinely special – think dinosaur footprints at low tide on Cable Beach that'll blow your kids' minds, and warm dry season weather from April to October when the southern states are getting chilly. The stinger season runs November to May, so most families with little ones stick to those cooler months when the ocean's safe for swimming without stinger suits. Here's a local tip that'll save you dollars: grab your fresh seafood straight from the luggers at the Town Beach boat ramp around 8am rather than paying tourist prices at Chinatown – the kids love watching the boats come in, and you'll get barramundi or coral trout for a fraction of the cost.
