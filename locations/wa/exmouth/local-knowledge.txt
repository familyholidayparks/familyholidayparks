If you're after a properly unique family holiday, Exmouth delivers in spades – where else can your kids snorkel with whale sharks in the morning and spot wild emus wandering through town by afternoon? The Ningaloo Reef sits just metres from the shore here, making it ridiculously easy for even little ones to experience world-class marine life without the boat trips and hefty price tags of other reef destinations. April to July is your sweet spot for visiting, when the whale sharks cruise through and the weather's still gorgeous without the scorching summer heat. Here's an insider tip: grab your snorkel gear and head to the Jurabi Turtle Centre beach access at high tide – the turtles feed on the seagrass there, and you'll often have these gentle giants gliding right past you while the tour groups are all crammed onto boats elsewhere.
