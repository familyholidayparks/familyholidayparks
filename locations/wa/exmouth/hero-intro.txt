Planning a family trip to Exmouth? We've rounded up the best family holiday parks in Exmouth so you can skip hours of research and get straight to the good part. Families love Exmouth because the kids can snorkel with whale sharks and sea turtles at Ningaloo Reef, spot kangaroos hopping around camp at sunset, and swim in the turquoise waters of Turquoise Bay without the crowds of other tourist hotspots.
