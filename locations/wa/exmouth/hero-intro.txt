# Exmouth, WA: A Family Holiday Destination

Exmouth sits at the tip of the North West Cape, offering some of Western Australia's most stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters that kids absolutely love. The town itself is compact and easy to navigate with young children, with everything from the pristine Turquoise Bay to the sheltered swimming spots at Bundegi Beach within a short drive. The waters here are warm most of the year, and the snorkelling is world-class without needing to venture far from shore, making it ideal for families wanting both adventure and safety. The nearby parks like RAC Exmouth Cape Holiday Park and Bullara Station Stay provide comfortable, family-friendly accommodation with facilities designed for kids, from pools to playgrounds.

Beyond the beaches, Exmouth delivers genuine wildlife experiences that hold children's attention. You can explore the Cape Range National Park with its gorges, hiking trails suitable for different fitness levels, and the chance to spot native animals in their natural habitat. The town has genuine attractions too—the Ningaloo Marine Park is right here, where families can swim alongside whale sharks and manta rays during season, and the Exmouth Jetty offers good fishing and spotting opportunities. It's the kind of place where kids tire out in the best way possible, with plenty of outdoor activities that don't require long car trips, leaving parents less frazzled and everyone happier.
