PARK: Sandy Bay Holiday Park
SCORE: 71/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-22
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 71

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Reviews mention basic facilities including a playground, giant chess board, and basketball hoop that children enjoyed, but no pool, waterpark, or organized kids' activities. One family noted "My kids loved the large chess board and spent most of the time at the playground," while another described "plenty of activities to enjoy during our stay — from a basketball hoop and giant chessboard to a playground." The entertainment is simple and unstructured.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional beach access with reviews consistently praising the "100-150 meter walk to a glorious beach" with "clean white sandy shores with turquoise waters." Families loved the "beautiful and peaceful" setting with "bushland between the beach and park," "peppermint trees," and wildlife including possums. The natural environment is the park's standout feature with "stunning beach" access for swimming, sunsets, and dog walking.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding cleanliness scores with reviews repeatedly using words like "absolutely immaculate," "spotless," "impeccable cleanliness," and "never been to such a clean and tidy holiday park." Multiple reviews from 2025-2026 confirm "amenities are regularly cleaned and maintained," "chalets were spotless," and "not an inch was/is dirty."

Value (12/15) — Mixed value perception with several families noting "great value for a family of 5" and "really good value," but concerns raised about camping facilities being "quite expensive for what is offered" with limited kitchen facilities for tent campers. One German review noted facilities were "dated" for the price paid. Chalet guests felt satisfied with value.

Site Size (6/10) — Small boutique park with limited space. Reviews note "only 9 shady caravan sites and 4 camping spots," "very klein und eng" (very small and tight), and "small park." One reviewer mentioned "emplacements pour voiture/caravanes sont très proches les uns des autres" (car/caravan sites very close together). The intimate size suits some but limits space.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very positive family sentiment with high return rates. Multiple reviews state "will definitely be back," "we will be returning for sure," and "re-booked as soon as I left." Families specifically with young children praised it as a "favourite place" and "amazing place for families." One significant negative toilet access incident in 2025 prevents a perfect score.

Location (10/10) — Perfect location scores with universal praise for beach proximity ("seconds from the beach," "stone's throw from the beach"), close to Busselton ("10 minute drive"), bike track to Busselton Jetty, nearby bottle shop and supermarket, and easy access to South West WA attractions. "Perfect location" appears repeatedly.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Very limited on-site entertainment for children beyond basic playground and chess board — no pool, jumping pillow, kids' club, or water activities that families expect from higher-rated parks.
2. Small park size with cramped camping/caravan sites and minimal facilities for tent campers (no proper camp kitchen fridge, limited cooking facilities beyond BBQ hotplate).
3. Dated accommodation in some chalets with old carpets and bathrooms requiring updates, plus one serious incident of toilet facilities being locked at night with malfunctioning access codes causing significant guest distress.
