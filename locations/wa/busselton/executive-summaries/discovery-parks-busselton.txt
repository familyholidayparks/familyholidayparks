PARK: Discovery Parks - Busselton
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 77

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Heated indoor pool (30 degrees) consistently praised as highlight for kids year-round, with playground on-site; however no structured kids clubs, activities programs or entertainment mentioned; coffee van visits and BBQ areas provide some social amenities; limited organized entertainment beyond pool and beach access.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding beachfront location with direct beach access repeatedly highlighted; beautiful peppermint trees provide shade across camping areas; beach perfect for swimming, fishing, sunset watching; bike path to Busselton town praised; natural setting described as "felt like a private beach" and "peaceful".

Cleanliness (10/15) — Amenities blocks generally well-maintained and clean with positive shower feedback; however significant issues reported: safari tents with mould and dust, blocked drains not fixed during stays; cabin 22 had mice droppings, sewerage smell; housekeeping described as "lacklustre" with toilets not cleaned daily; pool area had dirt and dead bugs; mixed standards across accommodation types.

Value (12/15) — Many reviews praised affordability: "much cheaper than many others", "Best value for money we've seen in Months", "great comparing to other places in the neighbourhood"; however some felt pricing didn't match condition of older units; cancellation policy criticized; value strong for location but maintenance issues detract.

Site Size (6/10) — Major recurring complaint: sites "jam-packed together", "very tight roads between sites", "very small and difficult to get on to"; reversing described as requiring "patience" and "nightmare"; power points in odd positions requiring long cables; sites described as standard size but layout cramped; shady and grassy praised but space consistently criticized.

Sentiment (7/10) — Strong repeat visitors with loyal families returning; many "definitely coming back" comments; however tempered by serious complaints about maintenance, noise from maintenance staff during nap times, leavers accommodation experience, theft incident; sentiment positive for location but frustrated by operational issues.

Location (9/10) — Exceptional beach access "100m to beach", "right on the beach"; 8km from Busselton with bike path; walking distance to supermarket and shops; quiet beachside setting away from bustle; entrance difficult to find but accessible once located; proximity to Margaret River region ideal.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant cleanliness and maintenance issues in safari tents (mould, blocked drains, non-working microwaves) and some cabins (mice, sewerage smells, broken ovens) that were reported but not promptly addressed, indicating inconsistent quality control across accommodation types.
2. Site layout criticized extensively with cramped spacing between sites, very narrow access roads making maneuvering difficult even for small rigs, and awkward power point positions requiring cables to stretch across neighboring sites.
3. Limited organized kids entertainment beyond the pool and playground, with no kids clubs, activity programs, or structured family entertainment, plus maintenance noise during children's nap times (10am-2pm daily lawn equipment) frustrating families with young children.
