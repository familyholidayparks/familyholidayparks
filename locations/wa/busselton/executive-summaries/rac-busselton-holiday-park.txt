PARK: RAC Busselton Holiday Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 226

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Reviews consistently praise exceptional kids facilities including two swimming pools (one heated), large bouncing pillow, mini golf, BMX/bike track, games room with Nintendo, movie hub, basketball, volleyball, and pedal cart hire. Families report children "had an absolute ball" and "so much for the kids to do" with daily activities during school holidays. Some concerns about facilities closing early (6pm) on hot nights and occasional equipment closures.

Nature (13/20) — Park features peppermint trees providing shade, grassed areas, lake with ducks and visiting rabbits, and beach access within 5-minute walk with "calm waters and nice white sandy beach." However, multiple recent reviews describe significant grounds deterioration with "sites a dust bowl," "black dusty sad area," "grass virtually non existent," and "ripped matting" replacing what was once "lush green" parkland.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "very clean amenities" and regular cleaning outside peak times, but significant concerns during busy periods. Peak season reviews report "toilets and showers were absolutely revolting," "blood on floor for 30 hours," insufficient cleaning frequency, and "filthy" facilities. Cabins receive praise for surface cleanliness but complaints about deep cleaning needs including mould, grime, and dust.

Value (10/15) — Pricing concerns with families noting "over priced" relative to condition, cabin at "$3,340 for 6 nights" criticized for minimal kitchen equipment, and sites "not up to standard of other cheaper caravan parks." Positive mentions of member discounts ("$28 per night"), stay-pay promotions, and good value outside peak periods. Families question what RAC does with income given declining maintenance.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive with "spacious sites," "large sites for caravan," "big sites and great laid out park," and "lots of room to spread out." Sites feature shade cloth ground covering and shade trees. Some criticism of specific sites being "tiny corner spot" and areas being "on a smaller side for caravans."

Sentiment (7/10) — Polarized feedback with loyal repeat visitors ("3rd year in a row & just keeps getting better") contrasting sharply with recent critical reviews. Major concerns about manager Michelle's enforcement style described as "rude," "aggressive," treating guests "like children," and "discriminatory." Many families report feeling "uncomfortable and unable to relax," with multiple "will Never stay here again" declarations from previously satisfied guests.

Location (7/10) — Situated on outskirts of Busselton CBD providing "quiet stay" away from traffic, 5-minute walk to beach, 20-minute walk to Vasse Village shops/Coles. Benefits from bypass road reducing traffic. Criticized as "a bit out of town" for restaurants and main Busselton attractions, requiring 10-16 minute drive to town centre. Convenient access to Margaret River region and surrounding attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe grounds deterioration with multiple reviews documenting transition from "lush green" to "dust bowl" with dead grass, black sand, ripped matting, and lack of maintenance/watering creating depressing environment that "makes everything black and dirty."
2. Significant management and service issues with numerous detailed complaints about overly strict rule enforcement, staff (particularly Michelle) being "rude," "condescending," and treating guests "like children" or "criminals," creating uncomfortable atmosphere for families.
3. Inconsistent cleanliness standards especially during peak periods with inadequate cleaning frequency, insufficient amenities for capacity, facilities described as "revolting" and "feral" during busy times, and declining attention to deep cleaning in cabins.
