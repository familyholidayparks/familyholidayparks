PARK: BIG4 Breeze Holiday Parks - Busselton
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-22
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 124

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Reviews consistently praised the pool, water playground, jumping pillow, and bike hire as highlights for kids. However, multiple recent reviews noted the waterpark was not working, playground equipment was broken or rusted, and organized kids activities during school holidays had been discontinued, reducing the entertainment score from what would otherwise be excellent.

Nature (16/20) — The park's proximity to the beach is its standout natural feature, with "2 minute walk to the beach" appearing repeatedly. Families loved the calm beach access, dog beach nearby, and coastal bike path to Busselton Jetty. The park has shady trees and established grounds, though reviews noted mostly sand rather than grass, limiting the natural environment score.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "spotless" amenities, clean toilets, and well-maintained grounds, but multiple reviews from 2025-2026 cited cabins needing thorough cleaning (sticky floors, mouldy smells, dirty dishes, sand on beds), aging amenities, and pool maintenance issues. Staff work hard to maintain cleanliness but facilities show age.

Value (10/15) — Reviews questioned value with comments like "$130 for a powered site, not worth the money" and "extremely expensive" alongside broken facilities. Families appreciated well-appointed cabins and good amenities when working, but felt pricing didn't match the aging facilities and maintenance issues, particularly given equipment failures.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews consistently mentioned "campsites are very close to each other," "small place," and sites being "tight with big vans." Ground mats helped, and some praised "good size" powered sites, but the compact nature and people walking through sites near playground reduced space scores. Better suited to smaller rigs.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation with many returning annually and stating "our favourite place." Staff received universal praise for friendliness. However, recent reviews showed declining satisfaction with one family stating they'd "look into other options" after previously loving it, and multiple 1-star reviews about maintenance, cleanliness, and service issues tempered overall sentiment.

Location (9/10) — Excellent marks for beach proximity (150m), coastal path access, and being pet-friendly near dog beach. Short drive to Busselton town, jetty, and shops. Only criticism was distance to main town area for walking (2km) and some road noise for campers, but location clearly a major drawcard.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Maintenance and equipment failures consistently reported across 2025-2026 reviews including broken waterpark, out-of-order washing machines/dryers, rusted playground equipment, and broken spa/jacuzzi features, with slow repair responses.
2. Cleanliness standards in cabins inconsistent with multiple detailed reports of filthy floors, mouldy smells, dirty dishes, unchanged sheets, and grubby bathrooms that required immediate attention or ruined stays.
3. Aging facilities throughout with amenities described as "run down," needing "re vamp," unchanged in "10-15 years," and cabins "needing fresh renos," impacting value perception despite premium BIG4 pricing.
