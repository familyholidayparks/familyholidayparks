# Busselton, WA – A Great Family Escape

Busselton sits on the stunning south-western coast of Western Australia, offering families long stretches of safe, patrolled beaches perfect for swimming, paddling, and building sandcastles. The iconic Busselton Jetty extends 1.8 kilometres into the Indian Ocean and is a must-do for kids – you can walk or ride the train along it, spot marine life, and even dive if the family's keen. The calm, sheltered waters make it ideal for younger children, while nearby beaches like Geographe Bay offer rock pools to explore and plenty of space for beach games without feeling crowded.

Beyond the sand and sea, the region delivers easy access to nature that keeps families entertained without a massive drive. The area's parks and reserves are great for walking trails, spotting native wildlife, and picnicking, while the nearby Leeuwin-Naturaliste National Park offers adventure for slightly older kids. For accommodation, you've got solid options including RAC Busselton Holiday Park, BIG4 Breeze Holiday Parks, Sandy Bay Holiday Park, Discovery Parks, and Busselton Lazy Days Caravan Park – all well-suited to families with the facilities and space kids actually appreciate. It's the sort of place where you can keep days simple, let the kids burn energy, and actually relax without breaking the budget.
