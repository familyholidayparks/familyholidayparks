G'day fellow adventurers! Coral Bay is where the Ningaloo Reef practically kisses the shoreline, meaning your little ones can snorkel with manta rays and sea turtles just metres from the beach – no boat required. The sweet spot for visiting is April to June when the weather's glorious, the humpback whales are passing through, and you'll dodge both the searing summer heat and the winter crowds. Here's an insider tip: grab your snorkel gear and hit the reef at high tide in the morning when visibility is crystal clear and the fish are most active – the local kids call it "magic hour" for good reason.
