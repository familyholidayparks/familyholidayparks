PARK: Denmark Rivermouth Caravan Park
SCORE: 69/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-18
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 128

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Limited structured entertainment with only "some old swings" for playground equipment, though families praised the onsite café, river cruise activity, kayak hire, boat ramp, and walking trails featuring hidden fairy houses that kids loved searching for. No waterpark or kids club mentioned.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural environment "literally on the mouth of the river" with "abundant bird life" including rosellas, ring necks, fairy wrens, ducks, pelicans, and swans. Families loved watching pelicans at the fish cleaning station, feeding ducks, and direct river access. "Wrapped in trees" with mature shady vegetation.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed reviews with some praising "spotlessly clean" and "very modern" amenities, while recent reviews cited concerns about mould, sewage smells, inadequate cleaning frequency ("18 minutes to clean both mens & womens"), and maintenance issues including overgrown sites and dead lawns.

Value (9/15) — Pricing concerns frequently mentioned with "$55 powered site," "$70 for unpowered tent site," and "4 times the price elsewhere." Several reviews noted "overpriced" and café prices "a bit extreme," though long-term guests found chalets "very good value for money."

Site Size (7/10) — Sites described as "spacious" and "good size" by some, but multiple recent reviews warned "sites are very cramped," "van with annex will practically touch the next," "tight parking," and trees overhanging limiting usable space, particularly problematic for larger modern rigs.

Sentiment (7/10) — Polarized sentiment with enthusiastic repeat visitors praising the "family run business" and "annual holiday highlight," but concerning number of recent negative reviews citing "rude staff," "owner very rude and aggressive," and "won't be back" comments suggesting deteriorating service standards.

Location (7/10) — Excellent proximity with "easy walking distance into town" (1.4km riverside walk), "close to town," and "5-10 min drive to CBD." Perfect base for exploring Denmark region beaches and attractions, though some sites have obstructed water views despite premium pricing.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant service quality issues with multiple recent reviews describing staff as "the rudest, shortest and unfriendliest person," owner becoming "aggressive" and "screaming," and inconsistent customer service that drove families to competitors.

2. Maintenance concerns undermining the experience including overgrown sites with bushes blocking space, dated shower facilities lacking basic hooks and curtains, mould in amenities, and grounds needing "a lot of TLC" with dead lawns and poor upkeep.

3. Minimal kids entertainment infrastructure with only old swings for playground equipment, no waterpark, no kids club, and limited organized activities beyond self-directed river access — inadequate for families expecting structured holiday programs.
