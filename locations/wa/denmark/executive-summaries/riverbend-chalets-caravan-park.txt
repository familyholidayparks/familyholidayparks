WHY THIS SCORE:

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently praised for clean amenities, spotless facilities, well-maintained grounds; a few mentions of chalet bathrooms needing attention and mouldy shower curtains
Value (14/15) — Described as "great value for money" and "best value CP in WA" at $39 per night for power and water; reasonable rates for the spacious sites offered
Site Size (9/10) — Very spacious sites repeatedly mentioned, "huge sites," "not crammed in," fire pits 25m apart; occasional complaints about insufficient amenities for guest volume
Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with many repeat visitors over 15 years; however, several concerning reports of rude management interactions and strict policies affecting overall sentiment
Location (9/10) — Excellent location 2-4 minutes from Denmark township, close to beaches, wineries, and attractions; beautiful 20-40 minute river walk into town via Kwoorabup Walk Trail

2. Multiple concerning reviews about rude and aggressive owner interactions, particularly around strict 2pm check-in policy with no flexibility even for special circumstances
3. Chalets are outdated and poorly maintained with mouldy bathrooms, no air-conditioning, inadequate lighting, and insect problems, making caravan sites the only recommended option
