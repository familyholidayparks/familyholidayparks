PARK: BIG4 Denmark Ocean Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 227

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (18/20) — Reviews consistently praised the "huge playground," "flying fox," "pirate ship," "2 bouncy pillows," and "mini golf." Families described it as having "everything" for kids with "plenty to do at the park." The games room/kitchen area and sports courts added further entertainment value. Some noted it's "best" for keeping kids busy.

Nature (18/20) — Guests loved the unique wildlife experience with "kangaroos bouncing by and kookaburras singing in the trees" throughout the park. The location near "Ocean Beach" and "the inlet" (described as "safe and clean for kids to swim") provided excellent nature access. The park sits in "beautiful grounds" with natural bushland setting, though some areas lacked grass.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Major split in experiences: main facilities often described as "spotless," "clean and well maintained," and "excellent." However, unpowered section facilities were "disgusting and dirty 90% of the time," with "mould," "filthy" bathrooms, and sewage smells reported. Peak periods saw "overwhelmed," "dirty" facilities with long queues. Washing machines frequently out of order.

Value (12/15) — Pricing seen as "fair" and "good" during off-peak, but "over price" during school holidays with rates reaching $77-81/night. The $15/night pet fee was controversial. Families appreciated the "very fair price" for what's offered, though some felt it wasn't "worth membership" given facility issues. Generally considered reasonable for a BIG4 with these amenities.

Site Size (8/10) — Powered sites consistently praised as "great size," "huge," "spacious," and "lovely big sites compared to most other camp sites." However, some areas had "pokey" sites or were "packed in like sardines," especially unpowered sections. Site allocation varied greatly with some "generous in size" and others "way too small" for caravans booked.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation with families saying it's become their "annual holiday tradition" and "can't wait to come back." However, significant negative experiences (sewage location, cleanliness, inflexible policies) meant some "won't repeat again" or "left a day early." Mixed but generally positive with loyal fans balanced by disappointed guests.

Location (10/10) — Perfect scores for location: "close to the beach," "10 minute drive to town," "walking distance to ocean beach," and "right by the inlet." Access to Denmark attractions, William Bay National Park (20 mins), and Greens Pool (30 mins) praised. The beachfront position and inlet access were standout features universally appreciated.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness disparity between main facilities and unpowered sections, with multiple reports of "disgusting," mouldy bathrooms, sewage smells, and insufficient facilities during peak periods causing queues and flooding.
2. Site quality extremely inconsistent with unpowered areas suffering from "black sand," "muddy" conditions, "boggy" ground, poor site allocation (some near sewage tanks), and inadequate infrastructure for the number of guests accommodated.
3. Peak period capacity management failures with "overwhelmed" toilets/showers, insufficient washing machines (often 3 of 5 broken), overcrowding in unpowered sections, and facilities unable to meet demand despite obvious staff efforts.
