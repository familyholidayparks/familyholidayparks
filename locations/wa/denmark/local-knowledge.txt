G'day! Denmark on Western Australia's southern coast is an absolute ripper for families who want to mix beach days with proper nature adventures – think towering karri forests, those famous greens pools carved into granite rocks, and calm river spots where the little ones can actually swim safely. We reckon April to October is your sweet spot for visiting, when the weather's mild and you'll dodge the peak summer crowds (plus the wildflowers from August to October are absolutely stunning). Here's a local tip that'll save you some tears: if you're heading to Greens Pool with young kids, park at Elephant Rocks instead and walk the 400m connecting path – you'll have calmer, shallower pools that are much better for littlies, and they'll love clambering over those massive boulders.
