G'day families! Gladstone and its surrounding region is an absolute ripper for families who love mixing their caravan park stays with proper adventures – think turtle nesting at Mon Repos (just down the road), fishing off the marina, and exploring the southern gateway to the Great Barrier Reef. The beauty of this spot is you can visit year-round, though April to October gives you those brilliant blue-sky days without the summer humidity that can make the little ones a bit cranky. Here's a local tip that'll save you grief: if you're planning to visit the Tondoon Botanic Gardens (which the kids will love), go early morning when the kangaroos are out – by midday they've all hopped off into the shade and you'll miss them entirely.
