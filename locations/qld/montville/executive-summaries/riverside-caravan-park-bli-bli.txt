PARK: Riverside Caravan Park Bli Bli
SCORE: 52/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-26
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 58

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Multiple reviews explicitly note the park "doesn't have things like playground, games room or camp kitchen" with families needing to visit nearby attractions like the Aqua Park or park across the river for kids to play.
Nature (14/20) — Strong riverside location with "lovely view of the river" and "great spot near the river," though some complaints about mangrove swamps, dampness, and mosquitoes on certain sites; families can fish at the old boat ramp.
Cleanliness (13/15) — Consistently praised with "spotless amenities," "super clean," and "very clean and tidy," though timed showers frustrate some guests and accessibility concerns noted.
Value (10/15) — Mixed feedback with some finding it "great value" and "inexpensive for the area," while others complained "$42 for an unpowered site is over the top" and "a bit over priced."
Site Size (8/10) — Positive mentions of "great site space (2x normal)," "very large sites plenty of room," though some note sites are "pretty crammed in" with blue metal underfoot.
Sentiment (6/10) — Polarized reviews with loyal repeat visitors ("3rd time back here in 12 mths") versus harsh criticism of management attitude and service recovery during problems.
Location (9/10) — Excellent proximity with "shops galore across the road," "close to Maroochydore, Nambour," and "central to key tourist destinations" making it ideal for exploring the Sunshine Coast.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment infrastructure (no playground, games room, camp kitchen, or BBQ facilities) severely limits appeal for families with young children seeking on-site activities.
2. Inconsistent management approach with multiple reviews citing "rude management," poor service recovery during weather emergencies, and confusion over pet policies creating negative experiences.
3. Environmental issues including mosquitoes requiring spray, damp muddy conditions on some non-powered sites near mangroves, and timed showers that caused accessibility problems for guests with disabilities.
