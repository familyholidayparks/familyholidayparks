PARK: Kenilworth Homestead
SCORE: 52/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-23
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 79

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (13/20) — Multiple families praised Easter activities including kids disco, flying fox, pony rides, petting zoo with horses/donkeys/alpaca, and BMX track, all included in camping fees with food trucks available. However, some reviews noted activities were poorly run or cancelled without warning, and school camp reviews cited terrible instructor experiences.

Nature (18/20) — Consistently praised for stunning location beside the Mary River with crystal-clear water, beautiful swimming holes, kayaking opportunities, and scenic misty mornings. The natural environment receives universal acclaim with families describing it as "absolutely beautiful," though some note lack of shade and privacy between sites.

Cleanliness (4/15) — This is the park's critical failure point with overwhelming evidence of filthy, inadequate amenities. Recent reviews describe "disgusting" toilets, "grim" showers with hair-clogged drains, septic overflow smells, muddy floors, and facilities going uncleaned for entire 4-day stays. Long-term visitors report significant deterioration over recent years.

Value (6/15) — Mixed feedback with concerns about being "overpriced for the facilities" at $230/night for spa rooms, overcrowding during peak times, and inadequate amenities relative to capacity. Some families appreciate included activities, but value is undermined by facility condition and overbooking issues.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive comments about "heaps of room," ability to spread out on the large property, and plenty of grass areas for kids to bike. However, peak periods see families "packed in far too closely" in festival-style camping with insufficient defined spaces.

Sentiment (5/10) — Deeply polarized with loyal repeat visitors of 20-29 years contrasting sharply against recent negative experiences. Multiple families state they won't return until amenities are upgraded, while others cite rude staff and terrible customer service. The sentiment has notably declined in 2024-2026 reviews.

Location (8/10) — Located in scenic Montville/Kenilworth area, close to town with easy access to local bakery and shops. Described as convenient drive from Brisbane with beautiful surrounding countryside, though specific accessibility details are limited.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Catastrophic amenity failure with only 3 showers per gender for 1000+ campers during peak times, leading to 40-minute waits, no hot water, and consistently filthy conditions that multiple long-term visitors describe as the worst deterioration in 20+ years.
2. Severe cleanliness and maintenance neglect with overgrown grass, broken fences, rotting buildings, septic overflow, and amenities going uncleaned for days despite premium pricing, suggesting management prioritises new cabins over basic camping infrastructure.
3. Customer service issues including rude staff interactions, unexplained closures with refusal to refund, inconsistent enforcement of quiet hours allowing noise until 1am, and inadequate supervision creating safety concerns for young children.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
