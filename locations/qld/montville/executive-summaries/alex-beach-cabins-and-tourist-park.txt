PARK: Alex Beach Cabins and Tourist Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 252

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews highlight a heated pool (two connected pools with shade), playground, games room, regular visiting food trucks (wood-fired pizza van especially popular Wednesday/Saturday), and coffee van daily. Some families note the pool is small and can get crowded, and entertainment is more low-key rather than extensive programmed activities or waterpark features.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding beach access with 3-5 minute walk to beautiful, patrolled Alex Beach with dog-friendly areas. Adjacent to a scenic lake/lagoon with birdlife, ducks, and walking/cycling paths. Park features established palm trees creating a tropical feel. Families love the natural surroundings for morning walks and wildlife viewing.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Consistently described as "immaculate," "spotlessly clean," "meticulously maintained" across cabins, amenities, and grounds. Showers praised for great water pressure. Gardens manicured, bins serviced daily, amenities cleaned regularly. Reviewers frequently cite cleanliness as a standout feature.

Value (14/15) — Generally described as "great value," "reasonable prices," and "excellent value for money." Stay 7 pay 6 deal noted positively. Some guests felt pricing was on the higher side for cabin size or ensuite sites, but most agreed quality matched the cost. Central location adds value.

Site Size (9/10) — Powered sites described as "excellent sized," "spacious," "roomy" with privacy hedges between sites. Level concrete slabs accommodate large vans easily. Some mention sites are "not a heap of space" but adequate. Cabins noted as compact but functional, with some feeling cramped for 3-4 people.

Sentiment (10/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with extremely high repeat visitation. Phrases like "will definitely be back," "stay every year," "rebooked for next year," "15 years returning" dominate reviews. Families express genuine affection for the park. Very high recommendation rate despite minor issues.

Location (10/10) — Perfect central Sunshine Coast position. Walking distance (3-5 minutes) to Alex Beach, surf club, cafes, restaurants, convenience store. Short drive to Maroochydore Plaza shopping centre. Between Maroochydore and Mooloolaba. Set back from main roads for peace and quiet while maintaining convenience. Universally praised.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Entertainment facilities are limited to pool and playground with no major waterpark, jumping pillow, mini golf, or organized kids' activities that would achieve a higher entertainment score.
2. Several reviews mention specific maintenance issues including faulty appliances, holes in cabins allowing bugs, dated fixtures, broken fridge/door locks, and poor garden maintenance during one extended stay period.
3. A few negative service experiences reported including rude phone manner from one staff member, refusal to allow booking changes for weather/health, poor handling of a booking error, and alleged serious safety incident with inadequate response.
