PARK: Landsborough Holiday Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 123

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note "no playground area for kids" and "pool super small" with multiple families stating "no catering whatsoever to kids." The park offers a firepit and BBQ area, but families with active children found limited entertainment options beyond feeding ducks at the pond. One family noted their kids "going stir crazy" with nowhere to play.

Nature (16/20) — Strong nature experience with "wooded area, felt nice to be in a natural surrounding" and a lagoon/pond that families loved. Multiple reviews mention "ducks, water birds, eels and water dragons" and "kids loved the ducks and turtles in the pond." The park features "lots of trees" and a "little creek running behind" some sites, providing genuine bushland atmosphere.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "clean amenities" and "beautiful and clean" facilities, but several concerning reports including "mouldy in showers," "facilities were not clean," and "toilets could do with refurbishment and a deep clean." Recent reviews suggest cleanliness has declined, with one guest cleaning the laundry themselves.

Value (9/15) — Value concerns prominent with "overpriced" appearing multiple times, one review noting "$55 for unpowered site completely overpriced." Several guests felt accommodations were smaller than advertised, with one stating "cabin was so little for family of 4" and "pictures were shown to what we got was little disappointed." Laundry at $7 per load considered expensive.

Site Size (8/10) — Powered sites described as "spacious" and "very generous for room" with "plenty of room for a larger van." However, "powered sites small" mentioned by some, and unpowered sites criticized as "muddy parking slot right by the pond." Generally adequate but inconsistent across the park.

Sentiment (6/10) — Polarized reviews with many "will definitely be back" but also significant negative experiences. Permanent residents created friction with holiday families, with one detailed complaint about residents being "verbal abusing" and telling kids to "get away." Several families cut stays short due to various issues.

Location (10/10) — Excellent location ratings with "Australia Zoo is only 500 metres down the road" and "4 mins away." Described as "great location" with "courtesy shuttle bus to Australia Zoo," close to Landsborough shops, and "central location to explore Sunshine coast beaches or glass house mountains."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of children's entertainment infrastructure with no playground, outdated tiny pool, and no kids' activities, making it unsuitable as a holiday destination for families with active children who aren't spending full days at Australia Zoo.

2. Significant conflict between permanent residents and holiday families, with multiple detailed accounts of residents being hostile to visiting children playing, running, or riding scooters, creating an uncomfortable atmosphere that doesn't feel welcoming for family holidays.

3. Inconsistent cleanliness and maintenance with multiple reports of mouldy showers, dirty facilities, deteriorating amenities, broken stove tops, power issues, and a pool desperately needing renovation, suggesting deferred maintenance affecting the guest experience.
