# Montville, QLD: A Family Holiday Destination

Montville sits right on the Sunshine Coast, making it an ideal base for families wanting beach days without the tourist chaos of the major hotspots. You've got the golden sands of Coolum Beach and Alex Beach within easy reach, perfect for swimming, paddling, and building sandcastles when the kids need to burn energy. The hinterland location means you're not far from rainforest walks and nature reserves either, so you can mix beach time with exploring the beautiful bushland that backs this part of the coast. It's the kind of place where you can do a proper beach day in the morning and still be back for a quiet afternoon in the trees.

For families, the real beauty is the range of accommodation options that actually cater to kids and their chaos. Parks like BIG4 Caloundra, Kookaburra Park, Cotton Tree Holiday Park, and Coolum Beach Holiday Park offer proper facilities—playgrounds, pools, and enough space so your family isn't cramped in a tiny room. Alex Beach Cabins and Tourist Park is another solid option if you want something a bit more low-key. The Sunshine Coast hinterland towns nearby are worth a look too for quirky attractions and cafes that don't mind sticky-fingered visitors. It's Australian coastal living without needing to book months ahead or remortgage the house.
