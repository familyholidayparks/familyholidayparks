PARK: Country Style Holiday Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 351

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Limited dedicated kids entertainment with goats and sheep to feed, giant chess board, and communal fire pit being the main activities; no playground, jumping pillow, or waterpark facilities mentioned; families noted kids enjoyed feeding animals and playing in nature but infrastructure for children's entertainment is minimal.

Nature (17/20) — Beautiful riverside location on the Severn River with abundant birdlife, scenic water views, and access to swimming/kayaking; close proximity to Girraween National Park (20 minutes) and surrounded by Granite Belt wineries; river sometimes low or dry during drought but peaceful bush setting with wildlife; families loved the natural environment and tranquil atmosphere.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently described as "immaculate", "spotless", and "pristine" with amenities and grounds receiving exceptional praise; camp kitchen well-equipped and clean; Ben and Narelle clearly take pride in maintenance with daily attention to facilities; rare minor complaints about brown tank water quality and occasional septic smell.

Value (12/15) — Generally considered good value with reasonable rates (powered sites $41-50, showers $1 for 6 minutes); families appreciated spacious sites and clean facilities for the price; some felt shower charges were steep given nightly rates; overall positive value perception though not exceptional compared to amenities offered.

Site Size (9/10) — Large, spacious drive-through powered sites consistently praised; unpowered riverside sites described as roomy with good spacing between campers; suitable for big rigs and motorhomes; occasional mention of tight turning spaces and some sites lacking grass, but overwhelmingly positive feedback on site sizes.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very high satisfaction with hosts Ben and Narelle receiving extensive praise for friendliness and helpfulness; strong repeat visitation and recommendations; some complaints about management attitude toward families with children and handling of minor issues; majority of families felt welcomed and plan to return.

Location (10/10) — Excellent strategic location 5-10 minutes south of Stanthorpe, close to numerous wineries (one directly across road), 20 minutes to Girraween National Park, and convenient to Granite Belt attractions; easy highway access while maintaining peaceful country feel; families valued central positioning for exploring region.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Minimal on-site entertainment infrastructure for children beyond feeding animals and giant chess, with no playground, waterpark, or structured kids activities that families with young children typically seek in holiday parks.
2. River water levels highly variable and sometimes completely dry during drought periods, disappointing families who booked specifically for water activities; some guests felt inadequately informed about water conditions prior to arrival.
3. Management style inconsistencies with several families reporting dismissive or confrontational responses to concerns, particularly regarding families with children, and issues with handling camp rules around fires and site allocation creating negative experiences for some guests.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
