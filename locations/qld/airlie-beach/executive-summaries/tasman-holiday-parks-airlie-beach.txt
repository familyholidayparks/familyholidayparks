PARK: Tasman Holiday Parks - Airlie Beach
SCORE: 71/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 255

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Daily bird feeding at 4pm with rainbow lorikeets is a standout feature that families love, plus jumping pillow, playground, and pool provide good entertainment. Food trucks visit nightly and there's a coffee van each morning. However, no waterpark or structured kids' club activities limit the score.

Nature (14/20) — Beautiful tropical setting with lush gardens, palm trees, and abundant birdlife including cockatoos and bush stone curlews create a lovely natural atmosphere. Location near the Whitsundays provides access to reef tours. However, the park is built near a stagnant creek causing mosquito/midge issues, and it's 2km from the actual beach.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Reviews show inconsistent cleanliness standards. While many praise "spotless" amenities and "clean bathrooms," multiple recent reviews report serious issues including ant infestations, cockroaches in cabins, dirty surfaces, dusty curtains, bins not emptied, and washing machines broken for extended periods. This significant variance cost considerable points.

Value (11/15) — Pricing ranges from reasonable ($35 unpowered sites) to expensive ($87-300 per night for powered/cabins). Several reviews mention "good value for money" and reasonable rates, but others felt prices were "extremely expensive" or "caro" relative to cleanliness/quality delivered. Dynamic pricing criticism noted.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews are mixed: some mention "spacious sites," "roomy sites," and "big spacious sites," while others report sites as "quite small," "tiny very close to neighbours," and "not much room to move." Ensuite sites appear more generous than unpowered. Some muddy/wet site issues reported.

Sentiment (8/10) — Majority positive with many "highly recommend" and "will be back" comments, especially praising friendly staff (particularly Sarah, Rachel, Kaz). However, significant negative reviews about cleanliness, pest issues, and maintenance problems bring sentiment down. Repeat visitors noted declining standards.

Location (7/10) — Located 2km from Airlie Beach centre (20-minute walk or $0.50 bus ride). Good access to Whitsunday tours with pickup from park. Pub across the road is convenient. However, proximity to main road causes some noise, and it's not directly beachfront as some families prefer.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Serious and recurring cleanliness/pest control issues reported in multiple recent reviews including ant infestations, cockroaches, unflushed toilets, flies, and general lack of deep cleaning in cabins significantly impact family safety and comfort.
2. Persistent maintenance problems especially with washing machines (all 8 out of order for extended periods), broken showers, corroded fixtures, and facilities not being repaired promptly show management gaps.
3. Site size inconsistency and muddy/wet conditions on some sites, plus proximity to stagnant creek causing mosquito/midge problems, detract from the outdoor camping experience families expect.
