PARK: NRMA Airlie Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-06-09
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 312

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Reviews indicate two pools (one resort-style lagoon pool) receive praise, with on-site café offering happy hour and occasional live music, but no dedicated playground or organized kids activities mentioned beyond pool facilities; families noted lack of entertainment for children outside swimming.

Nature (14/20) — Park described as tropical setting with palms and native birds (cockatoos), located across from beach with scenic coastal boardwalk to marina; however, multiple reviews note cramped concrete slabs with minimal shade and limited natural greenery on powered sites, though unpowered area has trees.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Major inconsistency evident in reviews with some praising spotless facilities while many recent reviews describe "dirty amenities," "camp kitchen is bedlam with dirty pans," "amenities very dirty," "mud under washing machine doors," and facilities "not cleaned daily" or "only once a day" despite high occupancy.

Value (9/15) — Strong criticism of pricing with reviews noting "$76 is a joke for a run down park," "way to expensive," "totally overpriced," "most expensive sites we've ever been to" at $88 July/$78 August, and "not worth the money" considering condition; however, some found it reasonable for location with unpowered sites at $36-44 considered fair.

Site Size (5/10) — Consistent complaints about being "packed in like sardines," "tiny sites," "jam packed," "ridiculously small," "very very small sites," with powered sites "absolutely jammed together" and caravans having difficulty maneuvering; unpowered described as "uneven paddock" with no demarcation and flooding issues.

Sentiment (6/10) — Mixed sentiment with vocal critics stating "worst caravan park," "DO NOT STAY HERE," "won't be back," "disappointing," alongside supporters praising location and pools; repeat visitors noted decline in standards after NRMA acquisition; many would not return despite appreciating location.

Location (9/10) — Consistently praised for being next door to Coles shopping centre, 5-10 minute walk to shops, 20-30 minute walk or short bus ride to Airlie Beach centre via scenic boardwalk, close to marina for tours; criticized only for being on busy road that's difficult to cross.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severely inadequate amenities for park capacity with only 6 showers per gender and 3 washing machines serving nearly 150 sites, resulting in long waits and overcrowding that multiple reviews describe as unacceptable.
2. Unpowered sites are fundamentally unsuitable being an uneven, sloped paddock with poor drainage that floods badly in rain, creating mud pits that soak belongings, with facilities too far away and only one broken tap.
3. Chronic hot water problems with numerous reviews reporting cold showers especially when park full, broken/dirty washing machines, inconsistent cleaning of amenities, and general maintenance neglect despite premium pricing.
