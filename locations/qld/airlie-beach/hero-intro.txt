# Airlie Beach Family Holiday

Airlie Beach is a laid-back coastal town on Queensland's Whitsunday Coast that ticks all the boxes for a family getaway. The main beach is patrolled by lifeguards during peak seasons and offers calm, shallow waters perfect for younger kids to splash about in. Beyond the sand, you've got the Whitsunday Islands right on your doorstep – think snorkelling, island hopping, and plenty of water-based adventures that'll keep everyone entertained. The town itself is compact and easy to navigate with kids in tow, with plenty of cafes, ice cream spots, and fish and chips shops dotted along the beachfront.

For accommodation, you'll find three solid caravan and holiday park options: Discovery Parks, Tasman Holiday Parks, and NRMA Airlie Beach Holiday Park, all offering family-friendly facilities like pools, playgrounds, and powered sites. Beyond the beach, there's genuine bushwalking through national parks with rainforest trails suitable for different fitness levels, and the natural beauty of the region means kids get proper exposure to Australian wildlife and landscapes. It's the kind of place where you can spend your days swimming and exploring, your evenings relaxing at the park, and actually feel like you've had a proper break rather than just survived a holiday.
