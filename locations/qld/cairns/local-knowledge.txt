G'day fellow family adventurers! Cairns and the tropical north are absolutely magic for kids – where else can you snorkel the Great Barrier Reef in the morning and spot cassowaries in the rainforest by afternoon? We've found May to October gives you the perfect weather without the stingers, plus the winter school holidays mean every park runs brilliant kids' clubs. Here's a local tip that'll save you a packet: grab your fresh fruit and veg from Rusty's Markets on a Saturday morning before you head to your park – the kids go mad for the exotic fruits they've never seen before, and you'll pay half what the tourist shops charge.
