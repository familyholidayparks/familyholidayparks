PARK: Beachside Holiday Caravan Park Yeppoon
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-25
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 164

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — No onsite kids entertainment, playground, or pool facilities mentioned. Multiple reviews note 'no playground or pool for the kids,' 'nothing here for kids to do,' and families must rely on nearby town facilities including splash pools, Yeppoon lagoon, and beach activities like sandcastle building. The park offers beach access but no organized activities.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding natural setting with direct beachfront access, described as 'absolute beachside location with island views,' 'huge beach when tide is out wonderful for walking and biking,' calm swimming beach, and '10m walk onto the beach.' Families praised waking to ocean sounds and beach views, with one noting 'kids could literally wake up, run outside, and start playing in the sand within minutes.'

Cleanliness (13/15) — Generally positive feedback on cleanliness with phrases like 'amenities are spotless,' 'immaculate facilities,' 'very clean and well maintained,' and 'toilets block was the best anywhere.' However, some negative reports mention 'laundry is DRECKIG' (dirty), washing machines out of order, and occasional maintenance issues during peak times.

Value (8/15) — Mixed value perception with multiple families noting the park is 'expensive for what you get,' '$81 a night there are better cheaper places to stay,' 'overpriced,' and 'not worth it for a plot of grass and cement pad.' Positive reviews mention 'great price' and 'reasonable rates' but negative sentiment around strict cancellation policies and fees dominated recent family reviews.

Site Size (8/10) — Most reviews report 'very big' and 'generously sized' sites, 'extra large sites,' and 'nice big space for van.' Some concerns noted about tight sites, parking close to neighbors, and difficulty with larger vans. Family reviewers appreciated having 'plenty of room to park the van and spread out.'

Sentiment (5/10) — Highly polarized sentiment with numerous 5-star reviews from satisfied guests balanced by multiple 1-star reviews from families citing poor customer service, particularly mentioning staff member 'Kath' as 'rude,' 'screaming,' 'inflexible,' and 'unhelpful.' Recent family reviews show declining satisfaction with service quality despite location appeal.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for location with universal praise: 'right on the beach,' '10 minute walk to town centre,' 'walking distance to shops and restaurants,' 'easy access to shops & cafes,' and proximity to Yeppoon lagoon. Families appreciated being able to 'park the car and barely touch it again' with beach and town both accessible on foot.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of onsite kids entertainment facilities (playground, pool, kids club, activities) which is critical for family holiday parks—multiple reviews specifically note this gap with families stating children 'run wild throughout the park with no supervision' and 'nothing here for kids to do.'

2. Significant and recurring customer service issues particularly affecting families, with multiple detailed accounts of rude, inflexible management especially around check-in, setup assistance, cancellations, and problem resolution—these service failures directly impacted family experiences during special occasions like Mother's Day.

3. Strict operational policies (rigid 12-5pm check-in, no late arrivals, no refunds, harsh cancellation fees, vehicle measurement on arrival) that particularly disadvantage families traveling with children who face delays, combined with poor value perception at premium pricing without premium family amenities to justify the cost.
