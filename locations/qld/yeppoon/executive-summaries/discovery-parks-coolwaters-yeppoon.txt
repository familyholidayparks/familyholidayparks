PARK: Discovery Parks - Coolwaters, Yeppoon
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 155

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (18/20) — Reviews consistently highlight exceptional water facilities with "best swimming pool water slide & water park for the little & big kid's," splash park, jumping pillow, tennis court, basketball court, movie nights, bingo, arts and crafts. School holiday activities described as "well thought through." Many families visited specifically for the waterpark and didn't leave the site.

Nature (14/20) — Park sits alongside Causeway Lake with boat ramp access, beach across the road, and proximity to Byfield National Parks, Great Keppel Island, and fishing spots. Some reviewers noted natural surroundings as "beautiful" though beach access from park not straightforward. Negative mentions of sewage/kelp smell from adjacent Causeway in some reviews.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed feedback with recent reviews noting "amenities could have been cleaned more regularly," toilets sometimes "disgusting and unclean," mouse droppings, and ensuites needing "a really good clean." However, many also praised "always clean" amenities and "immaculately clean" park presentation, suggesting inconsistency.

Value (9/15) — Significant concerns about pricing: "extremely over priced for what you receive," "biggest rip off" for unpowered sites at $65/night, unexpected weekend price jumps ($30 to $47), and size-based pricing criticized. Some found deals on G'day Parks, but overall value perception negative relative to condition.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews mention "nice big sites," "massive for our 24ft van," and "spacious" powered sites with ensuites. However, multiple complaints of being "packed in like sardines," sites on "unlevel dirt/mud," "dust bowl with no slab and no grass," and inconsistent site quality (grass vs sand vs dirt).

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation among satisfied families ("4th Christmas break," "third year visiting") with many "will definitely be back" comments. However, tempered by serious negative experiences including evictions, safety concerns, management conflicts, and "would not recommend" reviews from disappointed families.

Location (6/10) — Located at Kinka Beach, "10-15 mins into Yeppoon" and "nowhere close to Yeppoon" per reviews. Praised as convenient base for regional attractions (Great Keppel ferry, Byfield, fishing) but criticized for limited walkable amenities, difficult beach access from park, and isolation from town.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant cleanliness inconsistency—while some found amenities "always clean," multiple recent reviews reported unclean toilets, dirty ensuites needing deep cleaning, and maintenance issues suggesting quality control problems.
2. Poor value perception with unexpected pricing (weekend surcharges, size-based fees), high costs for basic unpowered sites on dirt/mud, and cabin condition not matching premium pricing or website photos.
3. Management and service issues including aggressive evictions (child on e-scooter, cat owners), inflexible refund policies even for medical emergencies, site allocation problems (near dump point, under trees causing van damage), and dismissive staff responses to legitimate complaints.
