PARK: Ingenia Holidays Kinka Beach Yeppoon
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 106

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Families praised the jumping pillow, two pools (including adults-only and baby pool), Easter Bunny visits, outdoor movies with popcorn, and ice cream van. Some noted games room equipment wasn't working and limited entertainment compared to expectations, with one review disappointed by "blow up jumping pillow not much for the kids to do" for the price.

Nature (18/20) — Located directly across the road from Kinka Beach with easy beach access, surrounded by mature trees providing shade, "loads of interesting vegetation," and wildlife including peacocks and chickens roaming the park. Beach has "excessive low tide mark and mud flats" but stretches over 3km for dog walking. Close to Emu Park and Great Keppel Island gateway.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelming praise for "spotless amenities," "crystal clean pools," and cleanliness "almost up to standard for heart surgery." Recent reviews note decline with "floors in our room were filthy," laundry dryers full of dog fur, and "toilet/shower block had been cleaned all week" during one October 2024 stay. Tiles need regrouting with birds causing bathroom issues.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback with many calling it "value for money" and affordable at $48-50/night for families, but others felt $70+ per night "very disappointed for the price." Special deals noted (2 nights get 1 free). Gas refills and washing machines described as "pricey." Some booking flexibility issues with minimum night requirements.

Site Size (8/10) — "Large slab caravan sites" and "huge sites, not crammed in" praised by many, but multiple complaints about specific small sites (21, 86a) where "site was so small our car had to be park partially on the road" and "extremely narrow, only about 500mm wider than our van." Site allocation inconsistency noted.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong repeat visitation with reviewers extending stays ("booked 6 nights, ended up staying 30"), returning for 3+ years, and "will definitely be back." However, several strongly negative 1-star reviews about "VERY RUDE" staff and management inflexibility hurt sentiment score. Transition to Ingenia management in July 2025 noted with concern.

Location (9/10) — "Perfect location" across from beach, 10 minutes to both Emu Park and Yeppoon, close to Great Keppel Island ferry. Some felt "out of the way" and "a bit far from shops." Walking to Emu Park requires walking along 80kph road with no footpath. Quiet location away from "hustle and bustle" appreciated.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site size inconsistency is a significant issue with some families allocated extremely small sites (86a, 21) while larger sites sat vacant, creating frustration about "crammed in like sardines" and poor site allocation practices.
2. Recent maintenance decline noted after Ingenia takeover including dirty cabin floors, laundry facilities with broken dryers, shower tiles needing regrouting, unlocked amenities with bird droppings, and dog fur management issues.
3. Limited structured kids entertainment beyond jumping pillow and occasional events, with games room equipment not working, no camp kitchen in some areas, and disappointing facilities compared to price point for some families expecting more from Ingenia brand.
