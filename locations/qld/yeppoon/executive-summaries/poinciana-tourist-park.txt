PARK: Poinciana Tourist Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 116

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — No playground or kids' club mentioned across all reviews; families relied on nearby Yeppoon waterpark/lagoon (2min drive) and beach access for children's activities rather than on-site entertainment facilities.
Nature (14/20) — Excellent beach proximity with Cooee Bay just 5-minute walk and dog-friendly beach access; however reviews consistently noted "no nice gardens or shady trees" and "cracker dust" with views of permanent residents' structures detracting from natural ambience.
Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive with recent reviews praising "immaculate" and "hotel-like" renovated amenities; "cleanest amenities ever" and "impeccably clean" repeated frequently, though older caravan sections show maintenance lag.
Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback with newer facilities praised but caravan guests questioned value at $42-58/night given cramped sites and infrastructure issues; glamping at $120/night considered good value; cabins well-regarded for quality.
Site Size (6/10) — Consistent criticism that "sites are way to small with no room from your neighbours" and "very tight sites" especially in older caravan areas; newer drive-through sites better but width remains problematic for larger rigs.
Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation evident with positive staff mentions (particularly Wayne); families appreciated location and cleanliness but caravan users expressed frustration with site limitations and washing machine restrictions.
Location (10/10) — Perfect scores for location with beach, shopping centre, bakery and cafes within walking distance; 2-5 minute drive to Yeppoon attractions, marina for Great Keppel Island, and lagoon/waterpark.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of children's playground or on-site entertainment facilities means families must rely entirely on external attractions for kids' activities.
2. Older caravan site areas have significant infrastructure issues including narrow, unlevel sites, dusty unsealed lanes, no sullage drains, and broken slabs that create difficult maneuvering and poor camping experience.
3. Restrictive washing machine policy (no personal machines allowed, $4/load communal only) frustrated multiple families who felt this reduced value given powered site pricing.
