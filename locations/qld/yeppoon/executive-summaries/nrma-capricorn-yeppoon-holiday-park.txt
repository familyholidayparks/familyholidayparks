PARK: NRMA Capricorn Yeppoon Holiday Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-23
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 162

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Strong family entertainment with jumping pillow consistently praised, two swimming pools, playground, outdoor movie nights, games room with retro arcade, pedal cart hire, and occasional food trucks and live music. However, the advertised water slide has been removed and recent reviews note declining activities compared to previous years with less communication about events.

Nature (14/20) — Pleasant natural setting with palm trees throughout, beach access 900m away (though beach not ideal for swimming), walking distance to Mulambin Beach, near Great Keppel Island ferry access, and turtle lookout nearby. Some sites have nice garden separation but overall natural environment is suburban coastal rather than pristine bushland.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Significant decline noted in recent 2026 reviews with multiple complaints about dirty amenities "haven't see cleaning for weeks", calcified showers, overgrown gardens, broken toilet buttons, and poor maintenance. Earlier reviews praised cleanliness as "spotless" and "cleaniest we've ever seen" but standards have clearly slipped.

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback with some calling it "overpriced for what you get" and "too expensive at this park", while others found rates "amazingly cheap" and "excellent price". Ensuite sites seen as worth extra money, but removal of advertised features and declining standards have impacted value perception.

Site Size (8/10) — Consistently praised for "spacious sites", "good size sites", and "large sites". Ensuite sites described as large, flat and well-grassed. Some sites described as "snug" or tight, particularly closer to park center, but back sites very spacious.

Sentiment (7/10) — Mixed sentiment with passionate advocates calling it "one of the best caravan parks" and "INCREDIBLE" but declining repeat visitor satisfaction evident. Multiple reviews compare unfavorably to previous visits noting deterioration. High number of 5-star reviews balanced by concerning 1-2 star reports about service and maintenance decline.

Location (5/10) — Major weakness with park located 11km from Yeppoon town requiring frequent driving, described as "lots of driving in and out of town which was annoying", "not close to anything", and "25 minutes drive from any supermarket". Positive access to Great Keppel Island ferry and some beaches but overall convenience limited.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Significant cleanliness decline in 2026 with multiple reports of dirty amenities, broken facilities, overgrown gardens, and maintenance neglect after management changes
2. Advertised water slide removed long ago but still shown in photos creating false expectations, plus declining activities and communication about events compared to previous years
3. Poor location 11km from Yeppoon requiring constant driving with no nearby shops, limited security with no gates or fencing allowing public access to sites
