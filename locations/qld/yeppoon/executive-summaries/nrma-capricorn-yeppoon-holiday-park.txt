PARK: NRMA Capricorn Yeppoon Holiday Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-25
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 162

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (15/20) — Strong family focus with jumping pillow consistently praised, two pools, outdoor movie nights, pedal cart hire, games room with retro arcade games, and organised activities during peak periods. However, waterslide has been removed despite still appearing in photos, and several reviews from 2025 note declining entertainment offerings and lack of shade over jumping pillow.

Nature (14/20) — Beautiful palm-tree-lined sites with tropical gardens, beach walking distance (900m) across the road, close to Great Keppel Island ferry and Turtle Lookout, well-maintained cycle tracks nearby. However, Mulambin beach noted as not ideal for swimming, and some reviews mention mosquitos, ticks, and flies near creek area.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "cleaniest toilets & showers" and "immaculate facilities," but recent 2026 reviews report "haven't see cleaning for weeks, everything is dirty, calcified, grubby" and "camp kitchen neglected and very unclean." Maintenance issues include broken toilet buttons, jammed doors, and overgrown gardens.

Value (10/15) — Reviews split on value with some calling it "amazingly cheap" and "great price," but multiple recent reviews state "too expensive at this park for what it is" and "overpriced for what you get," especially given removed facilities and 25-minute drive to supermarkets.

Site Size (8/10) — Sites consistently described as "spacious," "good size," and "roomy" with level grass sites. Ensuite sites particularly praised. Some sites noted as "snug" or narrow (site 33 specifically mentioned), and unpowered sites in back paddock have limited facilities.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong positive sentiment from families with many "will visit again" and "highly recommend" comments. However, notable decline in repeat visitor satisfaction comparing 2024 vs 2025/2026 stays, with comments about park "gone down hill" and atmosphere missing compared to previous visits.

Location (6/10) — Major weakness is distance from Yeppoon town (8-11km) requiring "lots of driving in and out of town which was annoying." Beach walkable but not ideal for swimming. Positives include proximity to marina (3 mins), Great Keppel Island ferry access, and quiet location away from traffic.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Cleanliness has deteriorated significantly in recent reviews (2025-2026) with amenities described as dirty, not cleaned for weeks, and maintenance issues throughout the park including broken facilities and overgrown gardens.
2. Distance from Yeppoon town centre (8-11km) and supermarkets (25 minutes drive) creates constant inconvenience requiring frequent driving for supplies and dining.
3. Entertainment facilities have declined with waterslide removed (still advertised), no shade over jumping pillow, worn-out equipment, and reduced organised activities compared to previous years under former management.
