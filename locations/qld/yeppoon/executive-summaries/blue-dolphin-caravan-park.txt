PARK: Blue Dolphin Caravan Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 62

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Reviews consistently note this is a "no-frills" park with no pool, playground, or kids entertainment. Multiple reviews explicitly state "without pool/playground" and one family reviewer advised "if you want high tech park for kids go to the discovery one down the road." One French family with four children praised facilities but mentioned no entertainment aspects.

Nature (14/20) — Park is 650 meters from Yeppoon Lagoon and walking distance to beach and foreshore. Reviews mention "close to blue lagoon swimming pool and beach," "walking distance from the beach," and nearby "footy ground for morning and evening stroll." Natural coastal environment scores well but park itself has limited green space with cramped sites.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Exceptional cleanliness praised throughout reviews: "spotless amenities," "immaculately clean," "amenities were spotlessly clean," "amazing clean facilities," and "BEST SHOWERS EVER." Even critics acknowledged "facilities older but immaculately clean." Minor points lost for aging infrastructure.

Value (10/15) — Mixed value perception. Positive: "great value for money," "not too pricey," "affordable," "reasonable prices." Negative: "quite expensive 60$ for just a pitch," "bit expensive," "cost lots $45/nt." One reviewer complained about being placed in unpowered area despite paying for powered site. Rates reflect basic amenities.

Site Size (7/10) — Significant concerns about cramping. Reviews note "incredibly cramped caravan park," "unpowered plekken worden zo volgepropt dat wij met onze buren niet tegelijk de auto deur open konden doen," "you are very close to your neighbours." Positive mentions of "large powered site with concrete slab" and "AWESOME" site size, but cramping issues prevalent.

Sentiment (7/10) — Generally positive with many repeat visitors and recommendations, but notable negative experiences. Multiple "will definitely be back" comments, but serious concerns about rude staff (2 reviews), intoxicated staff harassment, and inflexible refund policy. Small park atmosphere appreciated by some, criticized by others.

Location (4/10) — Location praised as "perfect" and "central" but with caveats. Reviews emphasize 10-minute walk to lagoon, restaurants, and shops. However, one reviewer felt misled: "not centre of town or SHORT walk to cafes and lagoon this was misleading." Reception "behind bars" concerned some guests about area safety. Minor road noise mentioned.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment facilities (no pool, playground, or kids activities) eliminates this park for families seeking on-site entertainment, with multiple reviews directing families to competitor parks for these features.
2. Significant site cramping issues during busy periods, with guests unable to open car doors simultaneously and feeling "volgepropt," plus concerns about permanent residents mixing with tourists creating space constraints.
3. Several serious safety and management concerns including reports of intoxicated staff harassment, rude ownership, inflexible cancellation policies even for illness, and security bars on reception creating apprehension about the area.
