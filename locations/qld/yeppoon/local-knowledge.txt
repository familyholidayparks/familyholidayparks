G'day! If you're after a proper family holiday that won't break the bank, the holiday parks around Yeppoon are absolute crackers – you've got stunning beaches that stay calm enough for little ones, plus those incredible Capricorn Caves just 20 minutes north that'll have your kids talking about glowworms for months. We always visit between May and October when the stingers are gone and the weather's dry and gorgeous, perfect for beach days without the sweltering summer heat. Here's a local tip: grab fish and chips from Meggsie's on Anzac Parade, then head to the Foreshore for a sunset picnic – the playground there is brilliant and the kids can burn off energy while you actually finish a meal in peace.
