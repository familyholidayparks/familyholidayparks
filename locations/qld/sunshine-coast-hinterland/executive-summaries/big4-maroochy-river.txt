PARK: BIG4 Maroochy River
SCORE: 77/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 416

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently mention jumping pillow, putt putt mini golf, small pool, playground, and excellent school holiday programs with daily activities including discos, reptile shows, science shows, movie nights, and marshmallow roasting at the fire pit. Parents appreciated the Wednesday pancake breakfasts and visiting food trucks, though some noted the pool was "very small" and facilities were more suited to younger children than teenagers.

Nature (14/20) — The park sits directly across from the Maroochy River with easy walking access, river walks, and sunset viewing spots. Families enjoyed the well-maintained gardens, herb gardens, tropical plantings, and shaded trees throughout the park. However, the location is dominated by proximity to the Sunshine Coast Motorway rather than being immersed in nature, with several reviews noting it feels more suburban than natural.

Cleanliness (14/15) — This was the park's standout strength with overwhelmingly consistent praise. Reviews repeatedly used phrases like "cleanest park we've ever stayed in," "immaculately maintained," "spotless amenities," "cleaned twice daily," and "exceptional cleanliness." Staff were frequently named for their meticulous work, particularly Lisa, with amenities, camp kitchens, grounds, and cabins all receiving top marks for presentation.

Value (11/15) — Mixed reviews on value with some families finding it reasonably priced for the Sunshine Coast location and service level, while others felt it was "expensive for what it was" or "not worth the money we paid." Several reviews mentioned the $10/night dog fee seemed excessive, and one reviewer called a $350 cabin rate "ridiculous." The free marriage saver service and Wednesday pancakes were seen as value-adds.

Site Size (8/10) — Caravan sites received mixed feedback, with many praising the artificial turf, level sites, and adequate space, while others noted sites were "very close together," "a bit tight," or required the tractor service because of narrow access. Cabins were described as "spacious" and "well appointed." The park itself is noted as "small" and "intimate" rather than sprawling.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very high repeat visitation rate with many families stating they return regularly, have stayed multiple times, and "won't stay anywhere else." Staff warmth and helpfulness were praised extensively. However, a small number of very negative reviews about noise, size, and specific incidents prevented a perfect sentiment score. Overall recommendation rate is strong among family travellers.

Location (7/10) — Excellent proximity to Sunshine Coast beaches, shopping (Sunshine Plaza), IGA within walking distance, Flume Cafe nearby, and easy motorway access for touring. However, this convenience comes at the cost of constant highway noise that was the single most common complaint, with reviews mentioning "very very loud traffic noise," "like staying in a breakdown lane," and traffic starting at 4am. River access is a short walk across a busy road.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Persistent and significant highway/motorway noise from the adjacent Sunshine Coast Motorway affects the majority of sites, with multiple families leaving early or losing sleep due to traffic noise beginning at 4-4:30am and continuing throughout the day, particularly impacting those in tents or light sleepers.

2. Limited facilities for older children and teenagers, with a very small pool that becomes overcrowded during busy periods, minimal activities beyond the jumping pillow and putt putt for bigger kids, and several families with teens expressing disappointment at the lack of age-appropriate entertainment options.

3. Value concerns with pricing perceived as high relative to park size and facilities, particularly the $10/night pet fee, cabin rates reaching $350/night, and higher costs than nearby competitors, with some families feeling the small pool, limited space, and highway noise didn't justify premium BIG4 pricing for this location.
