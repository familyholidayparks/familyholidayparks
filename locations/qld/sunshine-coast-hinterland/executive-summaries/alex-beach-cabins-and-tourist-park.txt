PARK: Alex Beach Cabins and Tourist Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-27
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 253

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews highlight a heated pool with children's wading area, playground, and games room. Families love the on-site coffee van and regular food truck visits (especially wood-fired pizza). Limited structured kids entertainment or activities beyond pool and playground, though proximity to beach and parklands provides natural play opportunities.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional location with 3-5 minute walk to beautiful Alexandra Headlands Beach, adjoining lake with duck pond and bird life, coastal walkway, and expansive parklands. Families appreciate dog-friendly beach and lake walks, tropical palm tree surroundings, and peaceful setting away from main road noise.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly praised as "spotlessly clean," "immaculate," and "meticulously clean." Amenities consistently described as modern, well-maintained, and cleaned daily. Grounds beautifully kept with manicured gardens. A few isolated complaints about cabin maintenance issues (faulty appliances, holes in screens) but these appear to be exceptions.

Value (14/15) — Described as "great value," "excellent value for money," and "reasonable prices." Special deals like "stay 7 nights, pay for 6" appreciated. Generally seen as affordable for the location and quality, though a few guests felt prices were high for short stays or basic cabins.

Site Size (9/10) — Caravan sites consistently praised as "excellent sized," "spacious," "roomy" with "plenty of room to move around." Privacy hedges between sites highly valued. Double concrete slabs noted. Some cabin guests found rooms compact or cramped, especially for larger families.

Sentiment (9/10) — Extremely high repeat visitation with many families returning annually for 5-15+ years. Strong recommendation rate with phrases like "will definitely be back," "highly recommend," and "best park on the Sunshine Coast." Staff friendliness and helpfulness consistently praised. Few negative experiences related to booking errors or service issues.

Location (10/10) — Perfect scores for location with universal praise for proximity to beach (3-5 min walk), shops, cafes, restaurants, surf club, and shopping centres. Central position between Maroochydore and Mooloolaba ideal for exploring Sunshine Coast. Quiet despite central location.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited organized kids entertainment or structured activities beyond pool and playground—no jumping pillow, waterpark features, or kids club mentioned.
2. Several reports of maintenance issues in older cabins including faulty appliances, poor wifi connectivity, dated fixtures, and small cramped bathrooms, though staff generally responsive to issues.
3. Some operational complaints including inflexible cancellation policies, booking system errors, noise from unsupervised children, and a few reports of poor customer service interactions.
