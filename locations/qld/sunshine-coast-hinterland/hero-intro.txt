# Sunshine Coast Hinterland Family Holiday Guide

The Sunshine Coast Hinterland offers families the perfect balance of beach access and rainforest escape, all within an easy drive from each other. You've got stunning patrolled beaches at Caloundra, Mooloolaba and Noosa just minutes away, where kids can safely swim and build sandcastles while parents relax. Inland, the hinterland delivers cool temperate rainforests, hidden waterfalls and adventure activities that keep children entertained for days. The region genuinely caters to families—there's everything from wildlife parks and theme parks to farm experiences and adventure playgrounds, so bored kids just aren't part of the equation.

For accommodation, you'll find solid park options across the area including BIG4 Caloundra, Kookaburra Park, Ingenia Holidays Rivershore, Landsborough Holiday Park, and Alex Beach Cabins and Tourist Park. These parks offer family-friendly facilities like pools, playgrounds and on-site activities, which means you can let the kids burn off energy without leaving the grounds. The real advantage here is the location flexibility—pick a coastal park if beach time is your priority, or go inland for rainforest walks and cooler temperatures during the warmer months. It's the kind of destination where a week never feels quite long enough.
