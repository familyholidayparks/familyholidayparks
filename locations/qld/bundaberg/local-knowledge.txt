G'day! If you're chasing that perfect blend of turtle encounters, uncrowded beaches, and proper Aussie coastal charm, Bundaberg's holiday parks put you right in the sweet spot. We love visiting between April and November when the weather's brilliant for beach days and the stingers stay away – plus you might catch the turtle hatching season at Mon Repos if you time it right. Here's a local tip that'll save you a packet: grab your fresh produce and those famous Bundy ginger products directly from Bargara Farmers Gate Markets on Sunday mornings, then head straight to your park for a ripper barbie lunch with the kids.
