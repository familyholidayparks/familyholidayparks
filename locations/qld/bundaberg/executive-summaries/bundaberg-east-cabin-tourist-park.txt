PARK: Bundaberg East Cabin & Tourist Park
SCORE: 48/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-02-03
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 42

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Only a pool mentioned for kids; one family noted "pool was lovely and it was nice to be able to bbq beside it while the kids swam" but no playground, activities, or kids club mentioned across all reviews.

Nature (8/20) — Limited natural environment; park is located on a main road near industrial areas with road noise mentioned. Close to beach (10 mins) and Bundaberg attractions but not surrounded by nature. "Not in a nice location" noted by reviewers.

Cleanliness (8/15) — Mixed feedback with recent reviews showing improvement. Positive mentions of "clean and comfortable" and "sparkly pool" but multiple concerning reports of "dirty ensuite with broken vanity door & mouldy internal area," "rotting benches and cupboards," and "needs a really good clean up."

Value (11/15) — Strong value proposition for families with "Value for money especially for larger families" and cabins sleeping up to 8 people. However, one recent review said "Quite expensive" while others found it "reasonably cheap," suggesting inconsistent pricing perception.

Site Size (5/10) — Sites described as "very tight" with concrete slabs. One review mentioned "Plenty of room for a family for a week" in cabins, but caravan sites had drainage issues and cramped spacing noted by multiple reviewers.

Sentiment (5/10) — Heavily polarized reviews with very negative experiences ("one of the worst caravan parks," "scary place") balanced against positive stays. High proportion of 1-2 star reviews in recent period undermines family confidence. Many note facilities are "dated" and "need updating."

Location (5/10) — Convenient to Bundaberg Rum Distillery and fast food, "10 mins to the beach and 10 mins to town," but located on busy main road with industrial noise starting at 7am. Not a scenic or peaceful family holiday location.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe maintenance and cleanliness issues documented over time including "rotting benches," mouldy ensuites, broken vanity doors, potholed roads, and poor drainage that created "wading pools" during rain.
2. Complete absence of kids entertainment facilities beyond a basic pool—no playground, games room, jumping pillow, or organized activities for children mentioned in any review.
3. High proportion of permanent residents changing park atmosphere, significant noise issues (industrial shed, road traffic, motorbikes, "very noisy all night"), and safety concerns ("scary place to stay") make it unsuitable as a family holiday destination.
