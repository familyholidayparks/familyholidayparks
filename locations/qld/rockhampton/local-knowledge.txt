G'day families! Rockhampton and the Capricorn Coast offer that perfect sweet spot where the tropics begin – you're close enough to the Great Barrier Reef for day trips, yet the holiday parks here cost a fraction of what you'd pay further north. We love visiting between April and October when the humidity drops and the beaches are pristine, plus the local wildlife (hello, kangaroos at sunset!) is incredibly active during the cooler months. Here's an insider tip: grab your free beef tasting vouchers from the visitor centre on Gladstone Road – Rocky is Australia's beef capital, and several local butchers offer samples to tourists, which the kids think is hilarious and makes for brilliant barbies back at your park.
