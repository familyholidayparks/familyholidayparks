# Townsville Family Holiday Guide

Townsville is a laid-back North Queensland coastal town that ticks all the boxes for a family getaway. The city sits right on the water with stunning beaches perfect for kids – Strand Beach is a local favourite with its patrolled swimming areas, rock pools, and a beachfront promenade lined with parks and playgrounds. Beyond the sand, you've got the Great Barrier Reef literally on your doorstep for snorkelling and diving adventures, while the nearby wetlands and national parks offer brilliant opportunities to spot wildlife in their natural habitat. It's the kind of place where you can do as much or as little as you like, whether that's reef trips, nature walks, or just mucking about by the water.

For accommodation, you've got solid options across several caravan parks and holiday resorts. BIG4 Ingenia Holidays Townsville, BIG4 Townsville Gateway Holiday Park, and BIG4 Tasman Holiday Parks at Rowes Bay all offer family-friendly facilities with pools, playgrounds, and on-site activities. If you prefer something different, Discovery Parks Townsville and Coral Coast Tourist Park are equally well set up for families. The town itself has loads of kid-friendly attractions including the Reef HQ Aquarium, Castle Hill for panoramic views, and plenty of restaurants that actually welcome children without fuss. It's unpretentious, reliable, and genuinely good value for money – exactly what you want from a family holiday destination.
