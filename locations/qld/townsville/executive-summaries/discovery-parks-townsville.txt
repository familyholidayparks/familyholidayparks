PARK: Discovery Parks - Townsville
SCORE: 52/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-11
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 142

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (13/20) — Reviews consistently praise the pool area with water slides and splash park as a highlight, with families reporting "kids had a blast in the pool and on the slides" and "water park was amazing and kept the kids busy." Jumping pillow and playground mentioned positively. However, facilities sometimes out of service (pool drained, equipment broken), and some reports of children's facilities being "run down and nowhere near the standard advertised."

Nature (6/20) — Park is located on a busy highway with "relentless traffic noise day and night" and sits behind a service station. Very limited natural environment, with reviews describing it as urban and industrial rather than natural. Gardens mentioned as "well maintained" by some but overall minimal nature or bush setting. No beach, creek or natural attractions nearby.

Cleanliness (5/15) — Major concern across reviews. Numerous detailed complaints about "absolutely disgusting and dirty" cabins with "filthy floors," "mouldy showers," "disgusting grout," "walls filthy and covered in stains," and "bins full of maggots." Bathrooms described as having "infestation of bugs," "cockroaches in beds," and "toilets not clean with no toilet paper." Some positive mentions of cleanliness exist but heavily outweighed by negative evidence. Amenities maintenance inconsistent.

Value (7/15) — Mixed feedback on value. Prices ranging from $45-$220+ per night with many feeling overcharged for condition received. Comments like "for 220 per night expect a little bit more" and "$781 for 3 nights" with poor quality. Some found it "affordable" and "preiswert" (good value) for basic overnight stays. Value perception heavily damaged by cleanliness issues not matching pricing.

Site Size (6/10) — Some reviews mention "very large site" and "generous site for caravan" with concrete slabs, but others describe it as "very pokey for van parks." Mixed evidence with some having adequate space and others finding sites cramped. Ensuite sites mentioned but described as needing major makeover.

Sentiment (4/10) — Heavily polarized reviews with strong negative sentiment dominating recent feedback. Multiple "worst caravan park we have ever stayed at" and "I will never return" statements. Safety concerns prominent with break-ins, aggressive behaviour, intimidating permanent residents, and "unsafe" environment repeatedly mentioned. Some very positive reviews from families enjoying facilities but overwhelmed by negative experiences. Low repeat intention from many reviewers.

Location (11/10) — Strong point for the park. Excellent highway access at "crossroads of the A1 and A6," convenient for travellers. "Shopping centre 15 min walk" or "across the road," Ampol service station next door, "15 minutes from the CBD and ferry to Magnetic Island." Easy access to Townsville amenities and hospitals. Location convenience frequently praised even by negative reviewers.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe and widespread cleanliness issues with cabins, amenities, and public areas described as "absolutely disgusting," "filthy," with mould, cockroaches, ants, and inadequate cleaning between guests creating an unhealthy environment for families.

2. Serious safety concerns including multiple reports of break-ins ("someone try break into our cabin on two occasions"), theft, aggressive drunk residents, dogs attacking other dogs, suspected drug use, police presence, and an overall "unsafe" atmosphere created by problematic long-term residents that makes families uncomfortable.

3. Constant highway noise described as "relentless traffic noises day and night," "sirens, traffic, constant disruption," making proper sleep "impossible" for many families, combined with complete absence of natural environment or peaceful setting.
