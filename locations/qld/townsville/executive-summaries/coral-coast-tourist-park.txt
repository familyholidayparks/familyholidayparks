PARK: Coral Coast Tourist Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-09
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 89

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews consistently note the absence of children's entertainment facilities, playground equipment, or organized activities. One review mentions "small library and tv with Foxtel" and BBQ area, but no evidence of dedicated kids' clubs, jumping pillows, or waterpark features that families seek.

Nature (10/20) — The park features "lots of shade trees," "beautiful garden," and "lots of trees and therefore birds to watch," but is described as being "in the middle of an industrial area" with "nothing in the direct environment." No beach, creek, or significant natural attractions within walking distance, though Castle Hill and The Strand are a short drive away.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Outstanding performance with repeated phrases like "immaculately clean," "spotless," "amenities were always clean," and "impeccably cleaned and maintained." Multiple reviews specifically praise the lack of "cobwebs and spiders" and the cleanliness of shower blocks, though one negative review contradicts this claiming "amenities are tiny and dirty pool was green."

Value (9/15) — Mixed feedback with several reviews noting "quite expensive $55 for 2 people powered site," "a little expensive," and "ridiculous dollars a night." Some appreciated "good prices" and "cost efficient," but the consensus suggests pricing is above average for limited facilities, with "property focuses on the needs of price conscious travellers" suggesting basic amenities at standard rates.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant criticism around "sites are super tiny," "2m2 site," "sites are a little tight getting in," and "sites not overly big." Positive mentions include "roomy" sites, "generous sites," and "big enough for our big rig," but the weight of evidence suggests cramped spaces, particularly challenging for larger caravans.

Sentiment (7/10) — Predominantly positive staff interactions ("super friendly," "very helpful") and high repeat visitation ("we keep coming back," "our go to place") offset by several 1-star reviews citing management issues, booking problems, noise complaints, and "worst place we've stayed in." Sentiment appears divided between regular visitors and disappointed first-timers.

Location (7/10) — Convenient proximity to airport and Domain shopping complex, but compromised by industrial surroundings, busy Ingham Road traffic, and RAAF base aircraft noise. Reviews note "10 mins drive to shops," "easy drive into Townsville centre," but "nothing to walk to" and "location is a bit remote" for family activities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of children's entertainment facilities including playgrounds, jumping pillows, kids' clubs, or organized activities that Australian families expect from holiday parks—critical for family engagement and all-day entertainment.
2. Persistent noise issues from RAAF base aircraft operations, truck traffic, and busy road location significantly impact the restful family holiday experience, with multiple reviews specifically citing "horrible" nighttime disturbances.
3. Cramped site sizes inadequate for modern family setups with "super tiny" spaces making it challenging for families with larger caravans, annexes, and outdoor equipment to comfortably spread out and create a proper holiday space.
