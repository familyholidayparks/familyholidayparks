# Magnetic Island Family Holiday

Magnetic Island, just off the coast of Townsville in North Queensland, is a proper gem for families after a laid-back beach holiday without the crowds. The island's got brilliant stretches of sandy beaches perfect for kids—Horseshoe Bay and Palm Bay are the standouts, with calm waters and plenty of shade. You'll find good facilities at most beaches, including playgrounds and picnic areas, so you can settle in for the day without much fuss. The island's compact size means nowhere's too far from anything else, which is a real plus when you're managing little ones.

Beyond the beach, there's heaps to keep families entertained. The walking tracks are manageable for kids of different ages, offering chances to spot wildlife like rock wallabies and sea eagles. Key attractions include the Forts Walk—an easy historical loop with great views—and the Magnetic Island Koala and Wildlife Park if you want guaranteed close-ups with native animals. If you're after outdoor space, Apex Camps Magnetic Island offers a solid base for families looking to stay active. The island's got a relaxed vibe that means kids can run around freely while you actually get to switch off a bit.
