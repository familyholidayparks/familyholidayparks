G'day families! If you're planning a tropical escape to Magnetic Island, you'll find most of the family-friendly accommodation right across the water in Townsville, where the ferry terminal makes it dead easy to zip over to the island for day trips or longer stays. The dry season from May to October is absolutely perfect – the stingers are gone, the humidity drops, and you can snorkel the Bommies or spot rock wallabies without melting into a puddle. Here's a local tip that'll save you a packet: grab your holiday park's barbecue supplies at Willows Shopping Centre before you head to the ferry, because the island's mini-marts charge tourist prices that'll make your eyes water.
