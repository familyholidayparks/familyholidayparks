G'day! Mackay's stunning stretch of coastline offers families that perfect sweet spot between tropical paradise and proper Aussie beach culture, without the crowds you'll find further north. The winter months from May to September are absolute gold – the stinger season's done, the humidity drops off, and you'll have those gorgeous beaches practically to yourselves on weekdays. Here's a local tip that'll save you a bundle: grab your fresh seafood straight from the trawlers at the Mackay Marina on weekday mornings, then cook it up at your holiday park's BBQ facilities – the kids will think you're a legend, and you'll pay half what the fish and chip shops charge.
