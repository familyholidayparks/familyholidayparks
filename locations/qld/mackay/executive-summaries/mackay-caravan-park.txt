WHY THIS SCORE:

Nature (8/20) — Park is located near a river/wetland area with some birdlife and natural scenery mentioned positively by a minority of guests. However, the dominant review theme is the "unbearable smell from the abattoir" that significantly detracts from any natural environment enjoyment.

Cleanliness (4/15) — Major and consistent concerns across reviews: "filthy dirty cabin," "toilets had excrement on the floor," "shower drains covered in hair," "kitchen full of dirty dishes," "cabins infested with cockroaches." A few positive mentions of clean facilities exist but are vastly outnumbered by negative reports.

Value (5/15) — Pricing around $40-45/night for powered sites considered reasonable by some, but many reviewers felt "not worth the money" given the conditions. Multiple reviews mention feeling "ripped off" or that it's "overpriced" for the experience delivered.

Site Size (6/10) — Limited specific feedback on site sizes. Some mention "fair size" and "large enough," while others note it's "mostly long term residence" with limited space for travelers. Mixed reports suggest adequate but not generous site sizing.

Sentiment (3/10) — Overwhelmingly negative sentiment dominates recent reviews with warnings like "DO NOT STAY HERE," "stay away from this place," "worst place I've ever been to," and "would rather spend a night in a septic tank." Very few positive experiences or repeat visitor intentions expressed.

Location (14/10) — The one consistently positive aspect: "5 minute drive to shopping," "10 minutes to Mackay CBD," "conveniently positioned," "close to everything." Easy highway access and proximity to amenities is the park's strongest feature according to reviews.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Critical safety and security concerns with multiple reviews mentioning threatening behavior, intoxicated maintenance staff, drug activity, and feeling "unsafe" - completely unsuitable for families with children.
2. Severe hygiene and cleanliness failures including reports of excrement, cockroach infestations, filthy amenities, and an "unbearable smell from the abattoir" that permeates the entire park making outdoor time unpleasant.
3. Complete absence of family-friendly facilities with no pool, no playground, no kids entertainment, and a resident mix of long-term workers and transient populations that multiple reviewers specifically warned is "not child friendly" and "not suitable for families."
