# Bribie Island Family Holiday

Bribie Island is a laid-back coastal gem just north of Brisbane that ticks all the boxes for a proper family getaway. The island's beaches are the real drawcard – golden sand stretches for miles with gentle waters perfect for littlies to splash about in, while the bay side offers calmer swimming spots ideal for younger kids building confidence in the water. Beyond the sand, there's genuine nature to explore: coastal walks, bird watching spots, and plenty of room to run around without feeling cramped. The pace here is refreshingly slow, which means less stress and more actual relaxation for parents.

For families wanting the full package, Bribie Island delivers without breaking the bank or your sanity. The caravan parks – Bongaree, Bribie Island, and Bellara – all offer solid facilities and plenty of space for kids to roam safely, plus they're close enough to the beaches and attractions to cut down drive time. You'll find everything from fishing spots and playground areas to local shops and takeaway joints, so you're not stuck cooking elaborate meals if you don't fancy it. It's the kind of place where a week disappears quickly, the kids actually tire out from fresh air and activity rather than screen time, and everyone heads home genuinely better for it.
