G'day! Bribie Island and its surrounding areas punch well above their weight for family holidays – you've got the calm Pumicestone Passage for littlies to splash about safely, proper ocean beaches on the eastern side, and those brilliant tidal flats at Woorim where kids can spend hours fossicking for soldier crabs and pipis. We reckon April through October gives you the sweetest weather without the summer humidity and stinger worries, plus the water's still warm enough for swimming well into May. Here's a local trick: time your beach visits with the incoming tide at Woorim – the kids can bodysurf the gentle shore break as it rolls in, and you'll avoid that long walk across the sand when the tide's out.
