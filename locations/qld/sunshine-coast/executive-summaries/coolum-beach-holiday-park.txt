PARK: Coolum Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 173

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Reviews consistently note there are no on-site kids facilities, playgrounds, pools, waterparks, or organized activities; one reviewer stated "no pool or kids playground you have to go out of the park" and another noted "park does not offer any extra touches like evening activities"; families must rely entirely on the beach and external attractions for entertainment.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural environment with direct beachfront access; reviews praise "right on the beach," "beautiful long sandy beach," "patrolled swim areas very close by," and "dog friendly beach" access; the park backs onto Coolum's main beach with multiple walking paths; loses points only for limited natural screening between sites.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Strong performance with most reviews praising "super clean" facilities, "spotless" amenities, "very clean and tidy," and "amenities always clean and serviced"; a few recent reviews mention occasional maintenance issues like broken lights and inconsistent cleaning frequency, with one noting "WCs should be cleaned twice a day."

Value (8/15) — Significant concern across reviews; repeatedly described as "overpriced," "way too expensive," "daylight robbery," with specific complaints about $75-$90+ per night for powered sites, $5 per dog per night fees, $10 tractor fees, and $6 washing machine charges; multiple long-term guests mention leaving due to price increases of 30%+; many feel the lack of facilities doesn't justify premium pricing.

Site Size (5/10) — Major weakness with frequent complaints about "sites are too small for large vans," "very tight," "cramped," "almost inaccessible," and requiring paid tractor assistance; some sites described as "width of a footpath"; premium dune sites receive better feedback for space, but standard sites are consistently criticized as inadequate for modern caravans.

Sentiment (8/10) — Mixed but leaning positive; many loyal repeat visitors over 10-30 years love the location, but recent sentiment shows declining satisfaction due to pricing and customer service issues; positive comments about "favourite caravan park" and "will definitely be returning" are balanced by "won't be going back" and "never again" from previously loyal guests.

Location (10/10) — Near-perfect score with universal praise for "best location," "walking distance to everything," "across the road to shops, Coles, restaurants," "minutes to surf club," and "no need to drive anywhere"; beachfront position and town convenience are the park's strongest assets.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment facilities (no pool, playground, kids club, or organized activities) severely limits appeal for families with young children seeking on-site activities.
2. Persistent customer service issues with multiple reviews citing "very rude staff," "unhelpful reception," "abusive and intolerant," poor communication, unreturned calls, and inflexible policies around bookings and health emergencies.
3. Site size and accessibility problems make the park unsuitable for many modern caravans, with paid tractor service required for numerous sites and frequent complaints about cramped conditions and difficult maneuvering.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
