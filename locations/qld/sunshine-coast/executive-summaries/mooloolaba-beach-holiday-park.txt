WHY THIS SCORE:

Value (8/15) — Significant concern across reviews with prices described as "absolute daylight robbery," "comparably expensive," and "too expensive"; rates of $90-95 per night seen as high for a "very basic park" with no pool, though some guests feel location balances cost.
Site Size (7/10) — Small 60-site park with concrete slabs providing "ample room on the provided slab and single car parking"; described as compact but adequate with good site spacing though specific size dimensions not detailed in reviews.
Sentiment (8/10) — Mixed sentiment with many 5-star reviews praising location and cleanliness but recent reviews show declining satisfaction; repeat visitors express disappointment with service decline, pricing policies, and maintenance issues causing some to seek alternative parks.
Location (10/10) — Perfect score for "one of the best locations on the Sunshine Coast" with 5-minute walk to beach, shops, restaurants, cafes, Coles supermarket, and attractions; only negative is road noise on roadside sites and Friday night traffic.

1. Complete absence of pool or water play facilities for a beach holiday park, with no on-site kids entertainment programs or activities to occupy young children on rainy days or hot afternoons.
