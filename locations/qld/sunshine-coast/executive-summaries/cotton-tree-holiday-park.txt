PARK: Cotton Tree Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-11
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 257

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Reviews consistently note complete absence of on-site entertainment facilities. No pool, no playground, no kids club, no organized activities. Multiple families specifically mention "no pool or other entertainment" and "Cotton tree have not much to offer except a beach." Park relies entirely on natural beach/river environment for entertainment.

Nature (19/20) — Outstanding natural environment with direct access to both patrolled surf beach and calm Maroochy River inlet. Reviews praise "best of both worlds, access to the Maroochydore river and the ocean," "calm waters great for kids," "amazing sunrises at your doorstep," and "between the river and the beach." Only minor deduction for beach erosion mentioned in some reviews.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed feedback with recent reviews showing improvement. Positive comments include "amenities are spotless," "impeccably clean," "cleanest caravan park toilet I ever been," and "very clean facilities." However, multiple peak-season reviews mention "amenities were gross," "beyond filth," "constantly running out of toilet paper," and "dump points completely full for days." Cleanliness appears highly dependent on occupancy levels.

Value (8/15) — Significant concern across reviews. Consistent complaints about "$80 a night is outrageous," "very expensive to say the least," "most expensive nightly rate," and "facilities and service do not reflect the price." Many compare unfavorably to Big4 parks offering more amenities at similar or lower prices. Families note "no pool or other entertainment" doesn't justify premium pricing. 35% price increases mentioned in recent reviews.

Site Size (5/10) — Major weakness with overwhelming negative feedback. Reviews repeatedly describe "sites are really small & close together," "crammed in like sardines," "packed in tight," "everyone is squashed in," "way too small," and "sites so tiny." Multiple stories of difficulty maneuvering larger vans, needing neighbors to move vehicles, and vans not fitting allocated sites. Corner sites and waterfront sites particularly tight.

Sentiment (7/10) — Polarized responses. Strong loyalty from long-term visitors ("coming for nearly 20 years," "we keep coming back year after year") contrasts sharply with recent negative experiences citing poor customer service, rigid policies, and management changes. Common phrases: "will never visit this park again," "won't be returning," alongside "can't wait to come back." Many loyal families report declining to return after recent management changes.

Location (10/10) — Universally praised as exceptional. Reviews consistently highlight "perfect location," "location you can't beat," "one of the best caravan parks in Australia by miles," "walking distance to everything," "shops over the road," "cafes, restaurants just outside," and "close to Maroochydore CBD." Direct beach and river access plus proximity to Sunshine Plaza, Cotton Tree village, and local amenities earn full marks.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of on-site entertainment infrastructure (no pool, playground, or kids facilities) at premium pricing - families paying $76-84/night expect more amenities than just location.
2. Severely cramped sites described as "sardine-like" with multiple documented cases of caravans unable to fit allocated sites, creating stressful arrival experiences and safety concerns with children on bikes.
3. Inconsistent and often poor customer service with rigid cancellation policies, staff described as "rude," "unfriendly," "condescending," unresponsive communications, and lack of flexibility even for medical emergencies or extreme weather - creating significant negative sentiment despite excellent location.
