PARK: Cotton Tree Holiday Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-14
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 257

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note the complete absence of onsite kids entertainment, playground equipment, or pool facilities. Families mention "no pool or other entertainment" and "not much to offer except a beach." The park relies entirely on natural beach and river access for recreation, with occasional food trucks mentioned as the only organized activity.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting with reviewers praising "perfect location on the ocean and inlet," "right between the river and the beach," and "crystal clear calm waterways great for kids." Direct access to both patrolled surf beach and calm Maroochy River inlet provides versatile swimming options. Multiple reviews highlight "amazing sunrises," "beautiful beach sunrises," and "stunning sunsets" as standout natural features.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Generally positive cleanliness feedback with frequent mentions of "spotless amenities," "impeccably clean," and "cleanest caravan park toilet I ever been." However, recent reviews note deterioration during peak periods: "amenities were gross," "toilets and showers were dirty," and "amenities beyond filth" during busy school holidays. Cleaning schedules at 8am receive criticism for inconveniencing early risers.

Value (8/15) — Significant concerns about pricing with multiple reviews calling it "very expensive," "overpriced," and "far too expensive." Specific complaints include "$76-$84 per night with no pool or entertainment," "$80 a night is outrageous," and "most expensive nightly rate" compared to parks with more facilities. Families feel the premium charged is purely for location, not amenities or site quality.

Site Size (5/10) — Major recurring complaint across reviews: "sites are really small and close together," "crammed in like sardines," "everyone packed really tightly in," and "way too small." Multiple families report difficulty maneuvering larger vans with "20+ minutes to squeeze our van in" and "had to leave due to not fitting into allocated sites." Corner and waterfront sites receive slightly better ratings.

Sentiment (8/10) — Mixed sentiment with strong location loyalty balanced by management frustrations. Long-term visitors ("coming for 16-20 years") express disappointment with new ownership: "staff are so unfriendly," "rigid enforcement of rules," and "rude staff, awful communication." However, many still return for the location: "our favorite park to stay at," "will definitely be back," and "already booked for next year."

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for location with universal praise: "best location on the sunny coast," "walking distance to cafes and shops," "everything at your fingertips," and "close to Maroochydore CBD." Direct beach access, proximity to Cotton Tree village shops/cafes across the road, and central Sunshine Coast position consistently rated as the park's strongest asset.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment facilities (no pool, playground, or kids activities) makes this purely a beach-access park rather than a full holiday park experience, with families specifically noting "no pool or other entertainment which other parks have."

2. Severely cramped site sizing causes significant stress and safety concerns, with multiple families unable to fit their vans, cars parked blocking roads, and children's safety compromised by tight spacing: "tragic accident waiting to happen because of cars parked blocking vision."

3. Substantial decline in customer service quality under current management, with numerous recent reviews documenting rude staff interactions, inflexible policies, unauthorized credit card charges, poor communication, and dismissive attitudes toward loyal long-term guests, creating a "cult of unhappiness" atmosphere that undermines the beautiful location.
