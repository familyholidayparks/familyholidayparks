PARK: Landsborough Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 123

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — No playground facilities and very small, dated pool requiring upgrade. Entertainment comes entirely from natural surroundings: duck pond with turtles, water dragons, eels, and abundant birdlife that kids enjoy feeding. One family noted staff created cricket pitch for kids' carnival. Limited structured activities but shuttle bus to Australia Zoo (500m away) mentioned positively.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting with wooded area, lagoon/pond central feature, little creek running behind sites. Families loved feeding ducks and wildlife, peaceful setting amongst trees, healthy birdlife. Close to Glasshouse Mountains and hinterland villages. Natural surroundings consistently praised as highlight, though some mentioned pond smell and mosquitoes near water.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed reports with recent reviews showing decline. Many praised "beautiful and clean" facilities, "immaculate amenities", "SO CLEAN" bathrooms. However, concerning reports of mouldy showers, filthy cleaning equipment, dust in cabins, and facilities needing deep clean. Amenities described as needing refurbishment and "run down" by multiple recent guests.

Value (10/15) — Pricing concerns evident with unpowered sites at $55 deemed "completely overpriced", powered sites $48 "a bit too expensive". Several families felt disappointed by what they received versus cost. Positive value mentions for cabin stays and good amenities. Australia Zoo 10% discount offered. Overall mixed with more recent reviews trending negative on value.

Site Size (8/10) — Powered sites consistently described as "spacious", "generous", "good sized" with room for larger vans. Drive-through sites available. However, some powered sites noted as "small" and unpowered sites described as inadequate muddy parking slots. Gravel/road-base surfaces not grass, making tent camping difficult.

Sentiment (7/10) — Positive repeat intention from many guests but significant negative experiences impact score. Permanent residents caused friction with families, verbal abuse reported, kids told to leave areas. Service issues with power failures, refund problems, poor communication. Recent management changes noted. Many 5-star reviews but critical 1-2 star reviews from families highlight serious concerns.

Location (10/10) — Excellent location scores with Australia Zoo 4-5 minutes away (500m), walking distance to Landsborough town and shops, 20 minutes to beaches in either direction, close to Caloundra, Montville, Maleny, and Glasshouse Mountains. Train station nearby. Convenience for Sunshine Coast exploration consistently praised, though traffic noise from major intersection mentioned.

WHY NOT 100:
1. No playground or kids' entertainment facilities with a very small, dated pool needing urgent renovation – families with active children found it "useless as a holiday park" with nothing for kids to do on-site.
2. Significant permanent resident presence (majority of park) creating friction with holidaying families – children told they weren't welcome in certain areas, complaints made about normal kids' play, making families feel uncomfortable and unable to enjoy their stay.
3. Cleanliness standards declining with recent reports of mouldy showers, dirty facilities, dust in cabins, malfunctioning equipment, and amenities needing refurbishment – inconsistent maintenance affecting guest experience.
