PARK: Landsborough Holiday Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 123

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews consistently highlight a critical gap: "Pool super small and no play ground area for kids," "no catering whatsoever to kids," and "the pool is tiny and old looks like the paints all coming off it." Multiple families noted the pool needs renovation and there are no playground or structured activities. One cricket team found staff accommodating by creating a pitch, but no dedicated kids' entertainment exists.

Nature (16/20) — Strong natural setting praised across reviews: "wooded area, felt nice to be in a natural surrounding," "kids loved the ducks and turtles in the pond," "lots of wildlife to see in the pond," "water dragons," "beautiful birds," and "little creek running behind" sites. The lagoon/pond with abundant birdlife is a consistent highlight. However, some noted "it stinks like the pond," and "smell was sickening" from duck droppings.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Mixed feedback with recent concerns. Positive: "Clean ammenities," "beautiful and clean," "SO CLEAN," "facilities were clean." However, multiple recent reviews note decline: "Has gotten mouldy in showers not cleaned as much," "Filthy in corners," "facilities were not clean, No hand soap," "floors had been swished with a dirty mop," and "toilets could do with refurbishment and a deep clean."

Value (9/15) — Several families questioned value: "over priced," "completely overpriced for what we got" ($55 unpowered), "$48 a night for a van site is a bit too expensive," "price should definitely be lower for what you get i feel ripped off honestly." Some found it reasonable: "Great value," "Affordable price," "Good value for money," but negative value comments are weighted more heavily in recent reviews.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive: "Powered sites were spacious," "generous sites easy access," "plenty of room for a larger van," "good sized camping sites," "Camping sites are fairly good size." Some concerns: "Powered sites small," "cabin was so little for family of 4," and gravel sites noted as not tent-friendly.

Sentiment (6/10) — Sentiment is mixed. Many positive: "will definitely be back," "would stay again," "highly recommend." However, significant negative experiences impact families: "permanent residents ruined this one for us," "didn't feel like we could really enjoy our caravan park," "permanent resident were verbal abusing us," "Never stay again," "would not stay again," "useless as a holiday park." Several families with active kids had poor experiences.

Location (9/10) — Excellent proximity to Australia Zoo praised extensively: "Perfect distance if you are heading to Australia Zoo. 4 mins away," "only 500 metres down the road," "5min drive to zoo," "courtesy shuttle bus to Australia Zoo." "Central location to explore the Sunshine coast," "walking distance to Landsborough town." Minor negatives: "beside a major intersection," "trains honking all night."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Critically inadequate family entertainment infrastructure: no playground, very small and dated pool "with paint coming off," no kids' activities or programs, making it unsuitable as a destination family holiday park.
2. Significant permanent resident conflicts limiting children's freedom: multiple families reported being told kids "weren't welcomed," couldn't ride scooters or play freely, with residents complaining about normal childhood activity, creating an uncomfortable environment for young families.
3. Declining cleanliness standards and maintenance issues: recent reviews note mouldy showers, filthy corners, dirty cleaning equipment, broken stoves, power outages without compensation, and facilities needing refurbishment, indicating maintenance has slipped.
