PARK: Mudjimba Beach Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-09
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 143

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Minimal on-site kids entertainment noted; families mention 'playground across the road' near beach and pool available but 'no improvements in the park or any new facilities' with complaints about lack of 'playgrounds for kids and water activities' compared to BIG 4 parks. Pool described as basic with 170cm depth, primarily used for cooling off rather than entertainment.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting with 'walking distance to beach, cafes and park', 'beach is just down the road', '2 minutes from the beach', 'beautiful beach on the sunny coast', and 'dog friendly beach just across the road'. Park has shade from paperbark trees though this creates leaf debris. Close to Maroochy River (2-3km) and off-leash dog beach areas.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Highly inconsistent feedback; amenities frequently praised as 'the cleanest I've experienced at a van park ever', 'impeccably clean', 'spotless and new', but camp kitchens heavily criticized with 'filthy kitchen by the pool - dirty, moldy, smelling', 'cockroach running under the fridge', 'floor tiles need to be scrubbed as soap scum is building up'. Grounds maintenance issues with leaves and debris on sites upon arrival.

Value (8/15) — Significant concerns about pricing with numerous mentions of '$80-$100 a night' being 'over the top', 'unethically high prices', 'way overpriced for quality', '26% price increase' without improvements, '$5 per dog per night' fee seen as 'money grab'. Some acknowledge prices 'on par with other options' on Sunshine Coast but many felt poor value compared to what's offered.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive with 'spacious sites', 'big sites', 'generous and well kept', 'larger than normal sites', 'plenty of space and drive through site'. However some complaints about 'dirt sites' instead of advertised grass, uneven sites, and 'sites are quite close together'. Some tent campers placed 'down the back' away from facilities.

Sentiment (7/10) — Mixed sentiment; many loyal returners who 'come back year after year' and 'love everything about this place', but significantly undermined by repeated complaints about 'rude' reception staff with 'attitude adjustment' needed, described as 'rudest woman', 'aggressive', and 'unfriendly'. Management issues with refunds, communication, and flexibility. Positive mentions of specific helpful staff (Hasini, Anna) but pattern of poor customer service interactions.

Location (8/10) — Excellent beachside location 'walking distance to beach, cafes and park', 'close to coffee shops, eateries', '5 minutes drive to Coles', 'great for visiting the zoo', 'close enough to access all of the Sunshine Coast'. Airport proximity noted with 'planes flying low all through the day' but 'no late planes' and 'quiet at night'. 'Great community feel' in small surf village setting.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness inconsistency - while amenity blocks are praised, camp kitchens repeatedly described as 'filthy' with cockroaches, mold, broken equipment, and inadequate responses to complaints, creating hygiene concerns for families.
2. Persistent reception staff attitude problems with multiple reviews over 3 years citing 'rude', 'aggressive', 'unfriendly' interactions, poor refund handling, and making guests feel unwelcome, significantly impacting the family holiday experience.
3. Minimal on-site kids entertainment with no playground on property, basic pool only, no kids activities or programs, and premium pricing ($80-$100/night) that families expect would include better facilities and value for children.
