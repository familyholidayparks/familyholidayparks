PARK: Alex Beach Cabins and Tourist Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 252

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews mention a heated pool that's "great for kids," a playground described as "cute little playground," and a games room, though some noted the arcade room "was a let down" needing upgrades. Coffee van and visiting food trucks (especially wood-fired pizza) add family convenience. Limited structured kids' activities or entertainment programs mentioned.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional beach access with "5 minute walk to patrolled beach," backing onto "lovely parkland" and a "pet friendly walk around lake full of bird life." Guests loved the "beautiful palm tree surroundings" and "amazing coastal walkway." The natural setting between beach and lake is a major drawcard.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding cleanliness across the board: "spotlessly clean," "immaculate grounds," "amenities were modern, spotlessly clean," "meticulously clean," and "facilities always clean and very well maintained" are consistent themes. Perfect score reflects exceptional standards.

Value (14/15) — Strong value proposition with "excellent value for money," "great value powered sites," "reasonable prices," and "affordable" pricing noted frequently. The "stay 7 nights, pay for 6" deal appeals to families. Minor deductions for some guests finding it "expensive" or "stiff price."

Site Size (9/10) — Very positive feedback on "excellent sized caravan sites," "spacious sites," "roomy" with "plenty of room to move around," and privacy hedges between sites. A few noted "not a heap of space" and cabins can be "small" or "cramped" for larger groups.

Sentiment (10/10) — Exceptional guest sentiment with countless repeat visitors (10-15 years), many rebooked "for same time next year," "will definitely be back," and "wouldn't keep coming back if it wasn't exceptional." Staff praised as "friendly," "helpful," going "above and beyond."

Location (10/10) — Perfect location score: "great location," "walking distance to beach, cafes, shops," "central to everything on the Sunshine Coast," close to Maroochydore and Mooloolaba, yet "nice and quiet" set back from main roads. Ideal positioning.

WHY NOT 100:
On-site entertainment is limited with basic playground facilities and a games room that disappointed some families expecting more activities — no waterpark, jumping pillow, or organized kids' programs mentioned. Some cabins and sites are quite compact with guests noting "small" spaces, cramped rooms for 3-4 people, and tiny bathrooms that felt restrictive. A handful of negative experiences included maintenance issues (faulty appliances, holes in screens, dirty rooms), refund disputes, and reports of poor customer service responses to complaints, though these were exceptions to generally excellent feedback.
