G'day families! Agnes Water and the neighbouring town of Seventeen Seventy sit right where the Great Barrier Reef kisses the coast, giving you Queensland's most northerly surf beach and calm inlet waters all within a 5-minute drive – absolute gold when you've got kids who want different things. We reckon April to October is your sweet spot for visiting, when the stingers have cleared off and you won't need those stinger suits cluttering up your beach bag. Here's a tip the locals shared with us: grab your fish and chips from the Agnes Water Tavern on a Wednesday arvo, then head down to the free sunset turtle talks at the beach where the rangers explain which hatchlings you might spot during summer months.
