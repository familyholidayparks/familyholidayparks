# Agnes Water, QLD – A Family Holiday Spot

Agnes Water is a quiet coastal town on Queensland's southern end that ticks the boxes for families after a proper beach break. The main beach is patrolled by lifeguards during peak times, has calm waters that are good for younger kids, and the sand stretches far enough that everyone can find their own space without the crowds you'd hit at the Gold Coast. The town's got rockpools to explore at low tide, walking trails through bushland that won't knacker the little ones, and the nearby Eurimbula National Park has some lovely spots for a picnic or a gentle hike. You can grab fish and chips from local spots, and there's enough going on to keep kids entertained without needing a packed schedule.

For accommodation, you've got solid caravan park options like NRMA Agnes Water Holiday Park and The Reef Caravan Park if you're self-catering, or Agnes Water Beach Holidays if you want something more straightforward. What makes the place work for families is it doesn't feel overdeveloped – there's proper nature nearby, the beach is accessible and safe, and you can actually afford a week here without remortgaging. It's the kind of place where kids can burn off energy on the beach or trails, parents can relax, and everyone's happy at the end of the day.
