PARK: BIG4 Toowoomba Holiday Park
SCORE: 38/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-22
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 134

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (9/20) — Positive mentions of jumping pillow, pedal go-karts, games room with pinball machines, and occasional kids club events/petting farm during school holidays, but many recent reviews note very basic facilities with no pool slides or water features, gravel roads preventing kids from using scooters/blades, and pool dominated by permanent resident children.

Nature (4/20) — Urban park in residential Toowoomba street with no natural environment, beach, or bushland access; some fruit trees mentioned but overwhelmingly described as suburban setting with limited green space and no nature-based attractions nearby.

Cleanliness (4/15) — Severe and consistent complaints across recent reviews about cockroach infestations, filthy cabins with unwashed cups and hair-covered linen, dirty amenities running out of toilet paper, mold in air conditioners, dog waste on grounds, flooded shower cubicles, and overall maintenance neglect; even positive reviews acknowledge dated and "gross" facilities.

Value (5/15) — Mixed feedback with many families citing overpricing at $140-175 per night for basic/small cabins in poor condition, "not worth $66 fee" for cramped caravan sites, and feeling it "should be half that cost"; some acknowledge reasonable school holiday pricing and "cheaper than average motel" but overwhelmingly poor value for a BIG4 property.

Site Size (3/10) — Numerous complaints about "very small sites," "extremely cramped," caravan sites so tight neighbours had to move cars to allow exit, "packed park" requiring trailers parked on street, concrete tent slabs, and consensus this is unsuitable for big rigs despite advertising.

Sentiment (4/10) — Predominantly negative family sentiment with repeated "never go back," "do not stay here," "worst park in Queensland," comments about feeling unsafe and unwelcome among permanent residents, though scattered positive reviews from 2023-2024 show some families enjoyed stays before apparent recent ownership change and further decline.

Location (7/10) — Consistent praise for proximity to Toowoomba CBD (10-20 minutes), local shops within 5-10 minute walk, quiet residential street setting, and convenient access to parks/attractions, though some note the surrounding area "seems a bit dodge."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe and pervasive cleanliness failures with cockroach infestations, filthy cabins, unmaintained amenities, and mold issues creating an environment multiple families with young children described as unsafe and forcing early departures.
2. Overwhelming "trailer park" atmosphere dominated by permanent residents whose children monopolize facilities, creating an unwelcoming environment where holiday families feel like intruders rather than guests at a family holiday park.
3. Extreme deterioration of infrastructure and maintenance standards unworthy of BIG4 branding, with dated 1980s-style facilities, potholed roads, broken fixtures, cramped sites unsuitable for modern caravans, and absent/rude management failing to address repeated complaints.
