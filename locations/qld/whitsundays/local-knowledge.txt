G'day! The Whitsundays is where you'll find some of Queensland's most stunning family holiday parks, with direct access to those impossibly blue waters and the gateway to the Great Barrier Reef right on your doorstep. We've found April to October gives you the most reliable weather – you'll dodge the stingers and the humidity while the kids can swim freely without stinger suits. Here's a local tip that'll save you a bundle: book your reef trips through the tour desks at Airlie Beach itself rather than pre-booking online, as the walk-in rates are often significantly cheaper and you can check the actual weather conditions before committing.
