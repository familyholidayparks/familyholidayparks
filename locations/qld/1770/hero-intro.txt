# 1770, Queensland – A Family-Friendly Coastal Escape

1770 is a quiet coastal village that offers families exactly what you need for a relaxed holiday without the crowds. The area is blessed with beautiful beaches perfect for swimming and exploring rock pools, while the surrounding bushland and national park give you plenty of room to stretch your legs. You'll find genuine local hospitality here, and kids can burn energy outdoors without you feeling like you're herding cats through a theme park. The pace is slow enough that everyone actually gets to relax, which is half the battle on a family trip.

The main attractions are wonderfully low-key but engaging – think hiking trails through native bush, spotting local wildlife, and plenty of beach time. There are two solid camping options in the area: Traveller's Rest 1770 and 1770 Camping Ground, both offering family-friendly facilities and a genuine outdoor experience. You can grab fresh fish and chips, explore the local history, and let the kids run about without worrying about elaborate entertainment schedules. It's the kind of place where boredom rarely strikes because there's always a beach to walk or a trail to discover.
