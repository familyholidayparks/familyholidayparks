G'day! The twin coastal towns of Seventeen Seventy and Agnes Water offer families something truly special – Queensland's most northerly surf beach, gloriously warm waters, and a laid-back vibe that's worlds away from the Gold Coast crowds. We reckon May through October is your sweet spot for visiting, when the stingers are gone and the weather's absolutely cracking for beach days and exploring the national parks. Here's a local tip that'll save you a small fortune: grab your fresh fish and chips from the Agnes Water Tavern on a Wednesday when they do their family special, then head to the foreshore at 1770 for sunset – the kids can play on the calm beach while you watch the fishing boats come in.
