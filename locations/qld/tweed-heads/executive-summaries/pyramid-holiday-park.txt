PARK: Pyramid Holiday Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-05-23
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 292

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews consistently note limited on-site entertainment with "no playground", playground recently removed, recreational room with pool table/books/DVDs only, "not much to do with kids if it rains", pool mentioned positively but lacks shade coverage, bird feeding mentioned as highlight by some families, overall "not very kid friendly" for a holiday park.

Nature (8/20) — Not a nature-focused park, located in suburban/industrial area near airport, minimal natural environment on-site, close proximity (5-10 minutes drive) to Coolangatta and Kirra beaches noted positively, "close to beautiful beaches" but not beachfront, some mention of bird life (lorikeets), primarily valued for access to nearby nature rather than on-site natural features.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Strong positive sentiment: "spotlessly clean", "exceptionally clean", "cleanest cabin/accommodation I've ever stayed in", amenities consistently described as "very clean" and "well maintained", however some concerning reports of bed bugs, cockroaches, and ants in specific cabins, overall maintenance rated positively but with notable exceptions that impact families.

Value (11/15) — Generally considered "good value for money", "well priced", budget cabins around $110/night appreciated, "great value" and "affordable" mentioned frequently, however washing/dryer expensive at $6 each, some complaints about "very expensive for what you get", value recognized particularly for stopover accommodation rather than destination holiday experience.

Site Size (6/10) — Mixed feedback: "spacious site", "plenty of room" from some, but "tight site for large caravans", "very tight narrow roads difficult to back van", "parking to site is a bit tight", "streets are tight for a larger van (21ft)", suitable for smaller rigs and campervans, larger families with big vans experienced challenges.

Sentiment (7/10) — Divided sentiment: many repeat visitors and "would definitely stay again" from satisfied guests, however significant negative reviews citing safety concerns, poor customer service on refunds, "wouldn't be rushing back", "would never use them again", positive sentiment primarily from those using it as stopover/base rather than destination, airport noise a major detractor for many families.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location reviews: "great location", "perfect location", easy M1 access, "close to everything", 5-10 minutes to beaches, "8 mins to Coles and Woolies", walking distance to IGA and shops, ideal for Gold Coast Airport proximity, convenient for exploring Gold Coast and Tweed regions, only negative is airport flight path noise which is location-related.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Critically low entertainment facilities for families with young children - playground removed, no waterpark, minimal kids activities, making it unsuitable as a destination holiday park despite being marketed as one.

2. Severe aircraft noise issues with planes flying directly overhead every 10 minutes from 6am-10pm daily, with many families reporting disrupted sleep and constant interruptions, particularly problematic for noise-sensitive children or those with autism spectrum needs.

3. Park caters primarily to permanent residents (estimated 80-95% of sites) creating a residential rather than holiday atmosphere, with limited family-friendly amenities, occasional safety concerns mentioned, and a lack of the community/holiday spirit expected at family holiday parks.
