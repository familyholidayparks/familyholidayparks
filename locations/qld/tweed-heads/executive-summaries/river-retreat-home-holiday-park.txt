PARK: River Retreat Home & Holiday Park
SCORE: 47/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-03-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 59

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (3/20) — Multiple families explicitly noted "This is NOT a fun park" with management telling children this on arrival. Reviews confirm only a very small pool, no playground, and zero kids' activities or entertainment options for families.

Nature (14/20) — Strong riverside location on Tweed River praised across reviews with "river straight across the road for fishing which the kids loved", wildlife sightings including kookaburras and ibis, and walks through mangrove forests mentioned positively.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed results with many praising "amenities are very clean", "spotless showers", and well-maintained facilities, but offset by reports of overflowing bins, flooding damage, untidy permanent sites, and poor drainage creating lakes after rain.

Value (6/15) — Significant concerns with "full prices for underrated sites", no refund policy, hidden $25 cleaning fines, inadequate WiFi, one towel for 3-night stays, and families feeling the experience didn't match the price or website promises.

Site Size (7/10) — Contradictory feedback with some praising "huge sites" and "roomy and level" powered sites, but numerous complaints about cramped conditions, difficult manoeuvring, sites too small for booked caravans, and infrastructure too far to reach.

Sentiment (2/10) — Overwhelmingly negative family sentiment with multiple "will never return", "worst caravan park on our 28 day road trip", children feeling watched and intimidated, and explicit warnings to avoid especially with kids. Major safety concerns raised.

Location (4/10) — Right on Tweed River is positive, close to shopping and Gold Coast, but heavily penalized for being "straight under flight path from airport" with constant low-flying aircraft noise every 5-10 minutes that dominated many reviews.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Aggressive, unwelcoming management culture unsuitable for families - multiple detailed accounts of "rudest manager ever experienced", staff telling kids "this is NOT a fun park", threatening guests with removal, refusing bathroom access before check-in, and one parent reporting their child was "physically and verbally threatened" by management over pool use.

2. Zero entertainment or family facilities - no playground, no kids' activities, only a very small pool, with management explicitly discouraging family fun and children reporting they felt "constantly watched and intimated" making families want to avoid being at the park.

3. Severe aircraft noise pollution - park located directly under Gold Coast Airport flight path with "very noisy with low flying aircraft overhead every 5 to 10 minutes" throughout the day, making it feel like "just off the end of a runway" and requiring earplugs for sleep.
