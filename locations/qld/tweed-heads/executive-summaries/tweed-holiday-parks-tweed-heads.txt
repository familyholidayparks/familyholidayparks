PARK: Tweed Holiday Parks Tweed Heads
SCORE: 71/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-06-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 146

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (16/20) — Reviews consistently praise the "great pool with waterslide," "large playground," and "games room with massage chairs." Families report kids had "an amazing time in the pool" and enjoyed the "small river beach." Food trucks visit regularly. However, no Easter activities or holiday entertainment programs were mentioned, and one review noted "no activities for the children" during peak times.

Nature (14/20) — The park backs onto the Tweed River with a "beautiful little beach," boat ramp access, and "calm water for kayaking." Reviews mention "nice shady trees," "lovely river views," and being "right on the water." However, the surroundings are described as "not that interesting," it's a bay location "not surf," and significant road/airport noise detracts from the natural experience.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Most reviews praise "spotlessly clean" amenities and "very clean and well maintained" grounds. However, multiple recent reviews report "toilets and showers weren't very clean," "disgustingly dirty facilities," "stale urine" smells, and amenities "not cleaned as frequently as they should." Inconsistency during busy periods is evident.

Value (12/15) — Reviews describe pricing as "relatively cheap," "reasonably priced," and "fantastic value" with one noting "$400 for two nights for 6 people." However, several reviews complain about "price gouging, too expensive," and one noted the park was "almost a ghost town" due to high prices. Food van prices were criticized as overpriced.

Site Size (8/10) — Most reviews report "large sites," "very big van sites," and "spacious quiet sites." The premium artificial grass sites are particularly praised. However, some reviews mention "small sites," "sites weren't big," and tent sites have "rock hard ground" where "you can't get a peg in."

Sentiment (6/10) — While many reviews are positive with guests saying "will definitely return" and "best park we've stayed at," sentiment is severely damaged by staff issues and sandflies. Multiple reviews state "will never return," "staff are so rude," "never again," and families had to "leave two days earlier" due to midges. The sandfly problem alone caused numerous negative departures.

Location (4/10) — The park is "close to gold coast beaches" (3 minutes to Coolangatta), "2 min drive to Tweed city shopping centre," and "central to a lot of places." However, significant road noise from adjacent highways, airport proximity, and the industrial surroundings mean "location is incredibly noisy" with "frequent air, road and water traffic including night time drag races."

WHY NOT 100:
1. **Severe sandfly/midge infestation**: The overwhelming number of reviews mentioning being "eaten alive," "hundreds of bites," families leaving early, and needing "medication for allergic reaction" represents a serious, unmanaged environmental problem that ruins stays for many families, particularly those camping near the creek.

2. **Inconsistent and rude staff service**: Multiple reviews describe reception staff as "the rudest person I have ever dealt with," being "belittled" and "spoken down to," "verbally aggressive," and "accused of lying." Management showed poor customer service, lack of compassion, and defensive attitudes that directly caused guests to never return.

3. **Cleanliness deterioration during peak periods**: Recent reviews show a pattern of amenities not being cleaned frequently enough when busy, with "stale urine" smells, "no toilet paper," and facilities that "smelt like they have not been scrubbed in months," indicating inadequate cleaning schedules for high-occupancy periods.
