G'day! Tweed Heads sits right on that magic border between Queensland and New South Wales, giving families the best of both worlds – you can literally walk across the street and change states! The beauty here is the calm Tweed River on one side for little ones learning to paddle, while the surf beaches are just minutes away when the older kids want waves. We always visit between April and October when the humidity drops off and the water's still beautiful – plus you'll dodge the January school holiday madness. Here's an insider tip: grab fish and chips from Fishmongers at Tweed Harbour and eat them at the free playground right there while the pelicans put on their 3pm feeding show – the kids absolutely love it and it's become our arrival day tradition.
