G'day! The Fraser Coast is where your kids can spot humpback whales breaching from the beach during their annual migration (July to November is absolute magic), then spend the afternoon building sandcastles on impossibly calm waters that feel more like a giant bathtub than the ocean. We love visiting in the shoulder seasons of May-June or September-October when the holiday parks aren't packed, the weather's still beautiful, and you'll snag much better rates. Here's a local trick: grab your fish and chips from one of the Hervey Bay Esplanade shops and head to the Urangan Pier at sunset – the pelican feeding happens at 4:15pm sharp, and your little ones will be absolutely mesmerized watching these massive birds swoop in just metres away.
