PARK: Fraser Coast Beachfront Tourist Parks | Torquay
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-01-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 106

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Limited organized kids' activities with occasional treats like zooper doopers mentioned; main entertainment is beach-based play, swimming, and walking/bike tracks along esplanade. One mention of pool and spa but not consistently referenced. No dedicated playground or waterpark facilities noted.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding beachfront location with direct beach access described as "absolute beachfront" and "right on the water"; families report dolphin sightings, safe swimming in calm ocean, beautiful sunsets, and extensive walking/bike tracks along esplanade. Natural environment consistently praised.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Facilities repeatedly described as "very clean," "spotless," and "cleaned daily" with well-maintained amenities, though some reviews note older amenities and occasional issues with washers. Management praised for maintaining high standards.

Value (12/15) — Positive mentions of "excellent price for powered sites" and "$56 a night" for oceanfront views. Most families report good value, though some felt small sites weren't worth the price. Generally competitive for beachfront location.

Site Size (6/10) — Mixed feedback: some describe sites as "quite large" post-rebuild with room for large vans, while others report being "packed in like sardines" with "small sites no room to park your car." Tight sites especially near fence; beachfront rows appear more spacious.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation with families returning yearly for 30+ years in some cases. Mostly positive sentiment about managers and staff being helpful and friendly. Some negative experiences with booking errors and customer service.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location praised as "walking distance to beach and shops," "close to cafes restaurants," with Foodworks across road and pub nearby. Convenient for Fraser Island day trips. Some road noise from esplanade reported.

WHY NOT 100:
1. No dedicated kids' entertainment facilities or playground—families rely entirely on beach activities with minimal organized entertainment beyond occasional treats from managers.
2. Site size inconsistency creates cramped conditions especially in back rows near fence, with multiple reports of difficulty maneuvering vans and lack of car parking space.
3. Road noise from esplanade hoons and traffic impacts some sites, particularly those away from beachfront, affecting the peaceful atmosphere several families expected.
