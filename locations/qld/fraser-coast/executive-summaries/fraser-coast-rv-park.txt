PARK: Fraser Coast RV Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-08
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 70

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Limited structured kids entertainment; families appreciated the fairy house, exploring acres of land, watching kangaroos and frogs, and occasional visits from Rosie the dog, but no playground, pool, or organized activities mentioned.
Nature (18/20) — Excellent bush setting with kangaroos, birds, frogs, shaded sites, walking trails, and proximity to Fraser Island ferry (3-4 minutes); families loved the natural environment and wildlife viewing opportunities.
Cleanliness (13/15) — Facilities consistently described as "spotless," "immaculate," and "super clean" with creative rustic design; innovative showers highly praised but some maintenance issues noted (gate malfunctions, kitchen light issues).
Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback; some found it "budget friendly" at $39/night with good value, while others felt it expensive for limited amenities, especially given severe insect problems and unexpected storage fees.
Site Size (9/10) — Spacious sites with "heaps of room," "plenty of space to spread out," and "not jammed in like a Big4"; powered sites accommodate 4 vans around central pole with extension cables provided.
Sentiment (5/10) — Highly polarized reviews; many 5-star experiences offset by multiple 1-star reviews citing severe midge infestations forcing early departures and poor customer service responses to complaints.
Location (8/10) — Excellent location for Fraser Island access (right near ferry departure), 20 minutes from Hervey Bay, but isolated bush setting with no nearby beaches or attractions beyond nature-based activities.

WHY NOT 100:
[1] Severe seasonal sandfly and midge infestation renders the park "unlivable" for many families during warmer months, with multiple families forced to leave early despite trying multiple repellents, causing sleep deprivation and extreme discomfort especially for young children.
[2] Inconsistent and reportedly hostile customer service with owners described as "rude and unfriendly," "dismissive," hanging up on guests, refusing refunds for midge-related early departures, and giving impression guests are "bothering her."
[3] No on-site kids entertainment facilities (no playground, pool, or activities), limited operational hours (strict 2pm check-in, owner unavailable after 5pm), no dump point for caravans, and gate access issues reported by multiple guests.
