PARK: Discovery Parks - Fraser Street, Hervey Bay
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-22
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 174

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Exceptional facilities with two heated pools, jumping pillow, games room with ping pong, tennis court, basketball hoop, pedal cart hire, and unique wildlife lagoon where kids feed turtles and ducks with free food from reception. Park runs organized kids club, movie nights, breakfast gatherings, reptile shows, live music, and sausage sizzles creating genuine holiday atmosphere families love.

Nature (16/20) — Beautiful natural lagoon running through park with turtles, eels, ducks and fish provides unique wildlife experience. Abundant birdlife throughout well-landscaped grounds with mature trees. Located just 250m walk to Hervey Bay waterfront and beaches, with easy access to Fraser Island tours and whale watching. Green grassed sites and attractive gardens throughout.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Outstanding cleanliness standards with multiple reviews describing facilities as "spotless," "immaculate," and "the cleanest cabins ever stayed in." Ensuite blocks cleaned daily, pools checked multiple times daily, camp kitchens well-maintained. Staff constantly visible cleaning, maintaining grounds and facilities. Cabins prepared with AC on, music playing on arrival.

Value (13/15) — Generally good value with direct booking best, special deals like "two weeks for price of one" available. Some reviews note higher pricing compared to other parks on Queensland coast, though most feel quality justifies cost. Ensuite sites and safari tents considered excellent value for facilities provided.

Site Size (7/10) — Sites adequate but tight for larger rigs, with multiple reviews mentioning narrow access roads and tight maneuvering. Staff provide excellent assistance with parking and backing in. Ensuite sites well laid out with level pads. Some guests with big vans struggled, though assistance always available.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with many repeat visitors over multiple years. Guests consistently praise staff friendliness and helpfulness. Families book return stays immediately on checkout. Some negative experiences with reception staff and booking issues, plus occasional construction disruption, prevent perfect score.

Location (6/10) — One street back from Esplanade means 4-5 minute walk to beach, cafes, shops, and restaurants. Easy access to Fraser Island tours and whale watching. Quiet location appreciated but not beachfront. Short drive to major supermarkets and marina.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site access is genuinely tight for larger caravans and motorhomes with narrow roads causing difficulty for big rigs, requiring staff assistance for parking
2. Some inconsistent customer service experiences with reception staff described as "rude," "grumpy," or "miserable," plus booking and refund issues reported
3. Ongoing construction and maintenance work creates noise from grinders and chainsaws, with scaffolding blocking sites and insufficient advance notice to guests
