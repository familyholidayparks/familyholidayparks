PARK: Burleigh Beach Tourist Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-20
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 67

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Limited on-site kids' entertainment with only a TV room offering games and activities during holidays. Multiple families noted "would be a 10/10 if there was a pool" and the absence of resort-style entertainment facilities. No waterpark, playground equipment, or structured kids' activities mentioned. Families rely on beach and external activities for entertainment.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural environment with the park located 100m from Burleigh Beach and directly adjacent to Burleigh National Park walking tracks. Reviews consistently mention "coastal walk between Burleigh Heads and Tallebudgerah stunning views," "walk around the mountain to Tallebudgera," and proximity to beautiful beaches and natural surroundings. The beachfront location provides outstanding natural amenity.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently praised with phrases like "very clean amenities," "amenities spacious and clean," and "facilities are always very clean including BBQ and kitchen areas." Some minor concerns noted with older amenities needing cleaning improvements and occasional maintenance issues like "damaged floors and walls" and "pots and pans filthy" in one cabin. Overall maintenance described as "well maintained" and "super well maintained."

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback on value with descriptions of "very reasonable tariff" and "value for money" balanced against comments that it "get expensive in peak times" and "expect more for the price." One family noted meal vouchers for the surf club as a bonus. Generally considered reasonable for the prime location, though some felt pricing didn't match cabin condition issues.

Site Size (7/10) — Sites described as "large sites," "spacious," and "plenty of room for kid's too roam around" with most reviewers satisfied. However, multiple concerns raised about "very tight camp sites," "roads were extremely narrow" for larger rigs, and difficulty maneuvering 21ft caravans. Mix of positive space comments and access challenges for bigger vehicles.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive sentiment with families stating "our go-to place for camping on the Gold Coast," "favourite Gold Coast tourist park," and "will be back for sure." Multiple repeat visitors and strong recommendation rates. Minor detractors include temporary tram construction noise and lack of pool, but location quality drives high satisfaction.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score warranted as location dominates positive reviews: "best location on the Gold Coast," "directly across the road from the beach," walking distance to shops, cafes, restaurants, surf clubs, and public transport. Centrally positioned in Burleigh Heads with easy access to all amenities and beaches.

WHY NOT 100:
1. No swimming pool or waterpark facilities, with multiple families specifically noting this would make the park "10/10" - significant entertainment gap for young families
2. Very limited on-site kids' entertainment beyond a basic TV room with games - no playground equipment, kids' club, or structured activities during peak periods
3. Site access challenges for larger caravans with narrow internal roads and tight maneuvering, plus temporary tram construction noise affecting some stays

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
