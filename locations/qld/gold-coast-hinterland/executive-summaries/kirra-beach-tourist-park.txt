PARK: Kirra Beach Tourist Park
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-25
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 269

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently mention the jumping pillow and heated pool as kid favourites, with playground and climbing frame available. Food trucks visit regularly (pizza, fish & chips, Mexican) which families appreciated, and some holiday activities like face painting and hair braiding are offered. However, the playground is described as "very basic" with no slide or swings, and some note the pool lacks a toddler shallow section requiring parents to be in with young children.

Nature (16/20) — The park features beautiful established paperbark trees and gum trees providing lovely shade across grassy sites, with a natural country feel despite the urban location. Kirra Beach is a 5-10 minute walk away with excellent surf beaches nearby including Greenmount, Snapper Rocks and a beachside esplanade walk to Coolangatta. However, several reviews mention a "swamp" smell near cabins, proximity to highway traffic noise, and a visible homeless encampment behind the park which detracts from the natural atmosphere.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive feedback about spotless, immaculate amenities cleaned three times daily with "the best and cleanest amenities seen in Australia." BBQs kept clean with supplies provided, laundry facilities excellent, and grounds well-maintained. However, a minority of reviews from 2026 mention "dated not very clean" amenities, shower issues with temperature/pressure, and some sites covered in leaves or overgrown grass not mowed for weeks.

Value (11/15) — Reviews are mixed on value with complaints about pricing increasing to $63-80 per night being "expensive" and "overpriced" compared to other parks, plus a $60 half-day rate for late checkout criticized as inflexible. Some mention "good value for money" and reasonable fees, but several note getting "more expensive with less services" including no WiFi, and one review about dryer costs of $9/hour. The 2-night minimum booking policy also frustrated some potential guests.

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised for "huge powered sites," "lovely big sites," "spacious van sites" and "generous good size" with room for vehicle parking beside annex. Many note sites are well-spaced so "not on top of neighbours" with good access for large vans. A few reviews mention some sites being tight around trees or having poor drainage, but overall strong positive feedback about site dimensions.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very high rate of repeat customers with many stating "we'll definitely be back," "stayed numerous times," and "been coming here for over 10 years." Staff consistently described as friendly, welcoming and helpful. However, negative reviews mention rude reception staff, poor customer service, and several serious incidents including bike/car thefts, domestic violence, and broken glass cutting a child's foot which significantly impacted some guests' sense of safety and willingness to return.

Location (5/10) — Excellent proximity to Kirra Beach (5-10 min walk), Coolangatta shops/cafes, and surf clubs with easy highway access. However, the park is directly under Gold Coast Airport flight path with planes taking off from 6am onwards described as "very loud" and "crazy loud" throughout the day, which multiple reviews cite as a significant drawback. While some adapted to the noise, it clearly impacts the overall location experience for families wanting peaceful sleep-ins.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Aircraft noise from Gold Coast Airport is pervasive and disruptive from 6am daily with multiple reviews describing planes as "very loud," "crazy loud" overhead, preventing sleep-ins and impacting ambiance despite the otherwise good beachside location.

2. Security concerns are significant with multiple reports of bike thefts using bolt cutters, stolen cars breaking through boom gates, domestic violence incidents, and broken glass injuring children, with management described as "not surprised" and "not particularly helpful with investigation."

3. Amenities are aging and dated with reviews describing showers needing upgrades (poor pressure, temperature issues, large gaps in doors), small benches, no family showers, poor drainage causing flooding, and overall infrastructure requiring a closure period for proper renovation and updates.
