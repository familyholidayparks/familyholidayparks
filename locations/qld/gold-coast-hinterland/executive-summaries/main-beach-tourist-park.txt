PARK: Main Beach Tourist Park
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 102

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews highlight a games room and exceptional Easter events (food trucks, live music, Easter activities) organised by management, but multiple families noted disappointment that the small playground was removed. Pool has shallow area for young children. Limited permanent on-site entertainment infrastructure for kids.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding beachfront location with beach access directly across the road. Described as "green grassy oasis surrounded by skyscrapers" and "absolutely beautiful location." Easy access to foreshore paths for walking/cycling. Beach is patrolled with surf club nearby.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly praised as "immaculate," "spotless," "beautifully clean and tidy," with "near new amenities" and "best bathrooms on the east coast." One recent review mentioned kitchen/showers needing more cleaning, and one tent site had cigarette butts, preventing perfect score.

Value (13/15) — Generally viewed as good value given prime beachfront location, though several reviews noted "pricey especially over Xmas" and "price is up there." Most felt location justified cost. Described as "very reasonably priced compared to other parks on Gold Coast."

Site Size (6/10) — Consistent feedback about "tight parking spots," "sites are tight in places," "sardines for sites," and "little of a tight fit for the van." Some sites difficult to access for larger rigs. Staff helpful with manoeuvring but space is limited.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very high repeat visitation ("been coming 20 years," "booked three more trips"), strong management praise (Nicky & Al frequently mentioned), and high recommendation rate. A few negative experiences with cabin quality and noise from construction work prevent full marks.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for location: beach across the road, 10-minute walk to tram, bus stop outside, walking distance to Tedder Ave cafes/restaurants, Sea World nearby, easy access to Surfers Paradise. "Perfect location" mentioned repeatedly.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited on-site kids entertainment — the playground was removed and apart from the pool and games room, there are no dedicated children's play facilities, with several families expressing disappointment
2. Very tight sites unsuitable for larger caravans — consistent complaints about cramped spacing, difficult access, and lack of room to manoeuvre or set up around vans
3. Cabin accommodation quality issues — multiple recent reviews cited old uncomfortable beds, broken air conditioning, poor TV reception, rusty equipment, and maintenance issues that haven't been addressed

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
