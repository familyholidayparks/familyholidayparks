PARK: Ocean Beach Tourist Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 111

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Managers organize pancake breakfasts, movie nights, arts and crafts, sausage sizzles, rock painting, and koala hunts that families rave about. The fairy garden and "little Jurassic Park dinosaur area" keep young children entertained for hours. However, there's no pool, playground equipment, or games room, with families noting "no play area or pool for kids" but accepting this given the beach location.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional beachfront location directly across from patrolled Miami Beach with shallow, safe water perfect for young children. Reviews consistently praise waking to "the sound of the ocean" and the convenience of beach access through the back gate. Surrounded by "lush bushland" near the headland, creating a surprisingly peaceful natural environment despite urban setting.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding across all reviews. Described as having "the cleanest bins and laundry facilities we have seen anywhere in the country," with amenities that "sparkle like brand new" and are "immaculately cleaned every day." Multiple reviews call it "the cleanest park on the coast" and "absolutely spotless."

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing. Some found it "extremely affordable for a family" at around $62-69 per night, while others felt $68-120 per night was "pricey" and "not worth the value of money," particularly when experiencing issues like lukewarm showers or power cuts. Location and cleanliness justify pricing for most families.

Site Size (8/10) — Sites described as "quite spacious," "very generous," and "nice and big" with artificial turf covering that families love. However, multiple reviews note roads between sites are "a bit tight to get into" and the park feels like "a glorified carpark" with "narrow roadways" in places.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with exceptional loyalty. Multiple families report returning yearly (4th, 5th visits), with children "begging to come back" and declaring it their "new favourite campsite." Won 2025 ReviewPro People's Choice Award as #1 caravan park in Australia. Few negative experiences centered on specific service incidents.

Location (10/10) — Perfect scores for location. Directly opposite patrolled Miami Beach, walking distance to Coles, Aldi, cafes, surf clubs, and restaurants. Public transport accessible, 15-20 minute bike ride to Surfers Paradise, close to Burleigh Heads. Described as allowing you to "park your car once and literally never move it again."

WHY NOT 100:
1. No pool or traditional playground facilities, which limits entertainment options during non-beach weather and loses points for families expecting resort amenities.
2. Pricing concerns from multiple reviewers who felt $68-120 per night was expensive, particularly when combined with lack of WiFi and occasional maintenance issues like lukewarm showers or power cuts.
3. Tight maneuvering between sites and narrow internal roadways make it challenging for larger rigs, with some describing it as congested with "tiny sites and narrow roadways."

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
