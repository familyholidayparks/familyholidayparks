PARK: Broadwater Tourist Park
SCORE: 86/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-19
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 204

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (18/20) — Exceptional family entertainment with two pools (heated in winter), large covered jumping pillow, playground, games room, and organized activities including free pancake breakfasts, kids disco nights, movie nights with big screen projections, pizza vans, coffee vans, and comprehensive school holiday programs that keep children entertained between theme park visits.

Nature (17/20) — Prime waterfront location directly on the Broadwater with stunning water views, beach access for fishing and swimming, abundant wildlife including swans and ducks that visit sites, beautiful sunrises, and adjacent parklands with excellent walking tracks; close to seaway and wavebreak island.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently praised as "spotless" with amenities cleaned at least 3 times daily (staff observed at 5am topping up supplies), immaculate facilities, beautifully manicured grounds maintained daily, well-kept lawns, and camp kitchens described as "amazing" and fully equipped.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback with most finding prices "very fair for such a great location" and "good value," though some note it's "a bit pricey" and "a little bit expensive but right on the water"; generally considered reasonable for the Gold Coast waterfront position and extensive facilities offered.

Site Size (9/10) — Sites described as "reasonably good sized," "spacious," "generous," and "good sized" with ability to accommodate large rigs, though some reviewers with 26ft+ vans found certain sites tight and had difficulty maneuvering; premium waterfront sites particularly praised for space.

Sentiment (9/10) — Exceptional loyalty with families returning for 10-22+ years consecutively, multigenerational visits where children who grew up visiting now bring their own kids, consistently high recommendations, and strong emotional attachment with reviewers calling it "our favourite," "home," and "the best"; only minor negative incidents regarding reception hours and rule enforcement.

Location (7/10) — Excellent central Gold Coast location with easy walk to shops, restaurants, light rail, and Australia Fair (10min walk), close to all theme parks, SeaWorld, and Surfers Paradise (15min drive), boat ramp on-site, but some noise from nearby Southport sirens and weekend jet skis; ideal for families wanting attraction access while maintaining waterfront tranquility.

WHY NOT 100:
1. WiFi connectivity is non-functional despite being advertised, with multiple reviewers across 2+ years reporting the issue remains unresolved, creating significant frustration for guests needing internet access.
2. Value perception varies with some families finding costs high for the Gold Coast, and isolated incidents of poor customer service including after-hours arrival conflicts and inconsistent reception staff attitudes dampening the otherwise excellent experience.
3. Some site allocation challenges with larger rigs (26ft+) finding maneuvering difficult, road noise affecting sites near the promenade, construction noise from adjacent development not disclosed at booking, and only one dump point for the park size causing inconvenience.
