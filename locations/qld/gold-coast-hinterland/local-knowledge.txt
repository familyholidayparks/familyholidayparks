G'day! The Gold Coast Hinterland is where rainforest meets mountain magic, and honestly, it's my favourite escape when the coastal crowds get too much. The kids will love spotting pademelon wallabies at dusk around your cabin, and the natural rock pools at Cedar Creek are absolutely perfect for a refreshing dip after bushwalking through the Gondwana rainforests. April through September gives you the clearest weather for spotting Brisbane and the coast from the lookouts, plus the waterfalls are still flowing beautifully from the summer rains. Here's my insider tip: grab fresh avocados and macadamias from the honest boxes along Tamborine Mountain Road – they're ridiculously cheap and the kids get a kick out of popping coins in the tin.
