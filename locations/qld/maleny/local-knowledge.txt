G'day! Maleny and the surrounding hinterland sit pretty on the Blackall Range, where you can wake up to misty mountain views one moment and be splashing at a Sunshine Coast beach within 20 minutes the next. We reckon the sweet spot for visiting is April to October when the weather's absolutely cracker – mild days perfect for exploring the village's quirky shops and those stunning lookouts without the summer humidity. Here's a local trick: grab your morning coffee and pastries from Maple Street Co-op before the day-trippers arrive (they open at 7am), then head to Gardners Falls for a peaceful bush walk while the kids burn off energy and you actually get to enjoy your flat white warm.
