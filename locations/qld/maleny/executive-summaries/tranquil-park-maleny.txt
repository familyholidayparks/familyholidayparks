PARK: Tranquil Park Maleny
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-22
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 64

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Reviews consistently note this is motel-style accommodation with no kids entertainment, playground, or activities. Primary use is as a wedding venue and basic overnight accommodation, with one family noting they had spacious rooms for their baby to play but no facilities mentioned.

Nature (18/20) — Overwhelmingly praised for "spectacular," "breathtaking," "magnificent" panoramic views of Glass House Mountains. Multiple reviews highlight beautiful grassland scenery, fresh mountain air, peaceful surroundings, birdsong, nearby Mary Cairncross Rainforest Reserve (1km), Gardners Falls rock pools (5km), and Maleny Birdworld (2km).

Cleanliness (6/15) — Highly inconsistent cleanliness standards emerge across reviews. While some recent guests report "so clean with fresh linen" and "very well maintained," numerous detailed complaints cite mould on ceilings and bathrooms, dirty balconies requiring self-cleaning, nail clippings on floors, food splatter in microwaves, stained sheets, filthy aircon filters, and overall "rundown" conditions requiring "a cleaning course."

Value (8/15) — Mixed feedback on $190-$250 per night pricing. Some families found it "very good value for money" as "motel price with awesome view as bonus," while others felt "not worth $220" given dated rooms and cleanliness issues, calling it "extremely expensive and overpriced" for the condition.

Site Size (6/10) — Reviews describe rooms as "very spacious," "large," and "big" with ample storage, though some note limited parking requiring walking distances from cars. One family with 8-month-old baby appreciated the spacious room for baby to play. Not a caravan/camping park.

Sentiment (5/10) — Divided sentiment with passionate fans praising staff service and returning multiple times, while significant portion express disappointment and explicitly state "would not recommend," "won't be returning," and "best part was leaving." Wedding guests generally positive, accommodation-only guests more critical.

Location (7/10) — Strong location praised as "5min drive into Main Street," "very close to main centre of Maleny," with easy access to Maleny attractions, Montville region, Nambour and Maroochy coastline. Central for exploring Blackall Range with numerous nearby attractions mentioned.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe cleanliness inconsistencies with multiple detailed reports of mould, dirty surfaces, filthy balconies, and inadequate maintenance standards that create health concerns and significantly detract from the experience.
2. Complete absence of family entertainment facilities, playground equipment, kids activities, or swimming pool makes this fundamentally unsuitable as a family holiday park despite some families with young children staying.
3. Highly variable room conditions with many guests receiving dated, poorly maintained rooms with broken fixtures, musty smells, damaged furniture, and maintenance issues including flickering lights, rattling AC units, and leaking plumbing.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"amazing views of the glass house mountains," "staff went above and beyond for us and our children," "room was very spacious, which was perfect for our baby to play," "mould in the bathroom had been free to roam for years," "cleanliness is not their speciality," "filthy balcony you couldn't sit at the table," "dated appearance, slightly musty smell," "not worth over $200 per night"
