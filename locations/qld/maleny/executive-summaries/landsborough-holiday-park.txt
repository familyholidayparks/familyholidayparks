PARK: Landsborough Holiday Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 123

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note "no playground area for kids", "pool super small and no play ground", and "no catering whatsoever to kids". The only natural entertainment mentioned is the duck pond where "kids loved the ducks and turtles", with families able to feed wildlife. Multiple families noted this is "useless as a holiday park" for active children, with one family's kids going "stir crazy in such little space".

Nature (17/20) — Strong nature offering with "beautiful and tranquil gardens", "lovely outlook over the duck pond", "wooded area, felt nice to be in a natural surrounding", and "lots of trees and a lagoon". Families enjoyed "feeding the ducks, water birds, eels and water dragons", with "little creek running behind" some sites and "plenty of wildlife to watch". Location provides access to "Glasshouse Mountains" and hinterland.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "beautiful and clean" and "SO CLEAN" facilities, but significant recent concerns including "mouldy in showers", "facilities were not clean", "layer of dust underneath" beds, and "toilets could do with refurbishment and a deep clean". Appears management-dependent with deterioration noted "since no longer Ingenia".

Value (10/15) — Pricing concerns frequent: "$55 overpriced for what we got", "$48 a night for a van site is a bit too expensive", "feel ripped off honestly". Some found "great value" and "affordable price", but value perception diminished by lack of facilities. Families noted better value when focused on zoo visits with "10% discount for guests".

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive with "powered sites were spacious", "generous sites easy access", "plenty of room for a larger van", and "drive through sites available". However, some noted "powered sites small" and concerns about "muddy parking slot right by the pond" for unpowered sites. Cabin space inadequate for families with "only 2 seater lounge no chairs for kids".

Sentiment (7/10) — Moderate sentiment with many "will definitely be back" but significant family dissatisfaction. Permanent residents created issues with families reporting "verbal abusing us" and children being told "we weren't welcomed". Positive comments like "one of the best caravan parks" offset by families leaving early and "would not stay again". Strong zoo-visitor satisfaction but poor holiday park experience.

Location (9/10) — Excellent proximity to Australia Zoo at "4 mins away" and "500 metres down the road" with "courtesy shuttle bus". "Walking distance to Landsborough town" and "20 minutes in each direction" to Sunshine Coast attractions. Some noted "traffic noise" and "trains honking all night" but most found location "cannot be faulted" for accessing region.

WHY NOT 100:
[1] Complete absence of children's entertainment facilities with no playground, tiny outdated pool, and nothing for active kids beyond feeding ducks, making it unsuitable as a destination holiday park for families with young children. [2] Significant permanent resident population creating tension with holiday families, including reports of verbal abuse and children being told they weren't welcome to play in certain areas. [3] Inconsistent cleanliness and maintenance standards with recent deterioration including mouldy showers, dusty cabins, broken stoves, and power issues that weren't adequately addressed by management.
