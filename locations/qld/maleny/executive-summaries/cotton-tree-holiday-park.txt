PARK: Cotton Tree Holiday Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 256

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews consistently note there is no pool, no playground, and no on-site kids entertainment facilities. Multiple families specifically complained about paying premium rates "with no pool or other entertainment which other parks seem to have which are cheaper." The park offers only basic amenities with no activities beyond beach/river access.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting praised extensively. Families love the "perfect location" between calm Maroochy River inlet and patrolled ocean beach. Reviews highlight "crystal clear calm waterways great for kids," "amazing sunrises at your doorstep," and "best of both worlds access to river and ocean." The beachfront setting is the park's standout feature.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed feedback with recent reviews showing decline. Many praise "spotless amenities" and "cleanest caravan park toilet I ever been," but multiple 2025 reviews report "toilets and showers were dirty," "urinals blocked for days," "amenities were gross," and infrequent cleaning during peak times. Cleanliness appears inconsistent depending on occupancy levels.

Value (8/15) — Significant criticism of pricing. Families repeatedly call it "very expensive" ($76-84/night), "overpriced," and "not worth the money" given lack of pool or entertainment. Reviews note "$80 a night is outrageous" for "a 3x5 patch of dirt." Many feel pricing doesn't match facilities offered compared to other parks.

Site Size (4/10) — Major weakness with widespread complaints. Reviews consistently describe sites as "really small and close together," "crammed in like sardines," "tiny and streets narrow," and "way too small." Multiple families struggled fitting vans, with some unable to fit at all. Corner sites particularly problematic with cars blocking access.

Sentiment (8/10) — Polarized responses. Long-term visitors show loyalty ("coming for nearly 20 years"), but many recent reviews express disappointment with management changes. Common themes: "will not be coming back," "disappointing service," but also "will definitely be back." Repeat visitation declining among formerly loyal families.

Location (10/10) — Universally praised as the park's greatest asset. "Perfect location," "amazing location," "location you can't beat" appear repeatedly. Families love walking distance to Cotton Tree village cafes, shops, surf club, and direct beach/river access. Proximity to Sunshine Plaza and coastal bike paths also valued.

WHY NOT 100:
1. No pool or entertainment facilities whatsoever despite premium pricing of $76-84/night, with multiple families calling this poor value compared to other parks with waterparks and activities at lower rates.
2. Sites consistently described as cramped and too small, with numerous families unable to fit their vans properly, cars blocking roads, and feeling "packed in like sardines" with no privacy.
3. Customer service issues with multiple reports of rude, unhelpful staff, rigid cancellation policies even for medical/weather emergencies, poor communication, and management showing "no compassion or understanding" according to recent reviews.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"perfect location for little humans", "calm waters great for kids", "sites are really small and close together", "very expensive with no pool or other entertainment", "crammed in like sardines", "amenities are spotless", "staff absolutely rude", "right next to the river and the beach"
