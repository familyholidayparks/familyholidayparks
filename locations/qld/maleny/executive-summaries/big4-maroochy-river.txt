PARK: BIG4 Maroochy River
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 416

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews consistently mention jumping pillow, mini golf, and small pool as the main activities. School holiday programs with daily activities, pancake Wednesday, food trucks, live music, and campfire gatherings with marshmallows are highly praised. However, many families note the park is "small" with "not a huge range of activities" compared to other BIG4 parks, and the pool is repeatedly described as "very small" and inadequate when busy.

Nature (15/20) — The park sits directly opposite the Maroochy River with excellent walking and cycling paths, fishing opportunities, and beautiful sunset views. Multiple reviews praise the "beautifully maintained" gardens, tropical plantings, herb gardens, and green spaces. However, the park's location between the Sunshine Motorway and busy roads significantly impacts the natural ambiance, with many noting it feels more urban than bushland.

Cleanliness (15/15) — This is the park's standout strength with overwhelming praise: "cleanest park we've ever stayed in," "spotless amenities," "immaculately maintained," and "amenities cleaned twice daily." Reviews consistently rate cleanliness as exceptional across all facilities including toilets, showers, camp kitchens, and grounds.

Value (11/15) — Reviews show mixed feelings about pricing. Some appreciate the "reasonable rates" and note it's "good value," while others complain of "price hiking," "expensive for what it was," and "$350 for a cabin... ridiculous prices." The $10 per night dog fee particularly frustrated experienced travelers. Many felt the small pool and limited facilities didn't justify BIG4 pricing.

Site Size (8/10) — Most reviews describe sites as "good size," "spacious," and "generous" with artificial turf praised as "perfect." However, multiple reviews note sites are "very close together," "a bit tight" for larger vans, and "squeezed in that close they have to park your van with a tractor." The famous tractor service helps mitigate tight access.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overall sentiment is very positive with high repeat visitation: "we keep coming back," "will definitely be back," "7 years enjoying one of the best managed parks." Staff receive exceptional praise for friendliness and helpfulness. However, some guests left early due to noise, and a few had negative experiences with management or felt unsafe.

Location (6/10) — The location receives split reviews. Positives include being "close to everything," "great location" for Sunshine Coast attractions, walking distance to IGA and cafe, and "right on Maroochy River." However, the proximity to Sunshine Motorway is the most significant negative: "very very loud traffic noise," "right beside the Sunshine Motorway," "noise is constant," with many requiring earplugs and some leaving early.

WHY NOT 100:
1. The Sunshine Motorway location creates persistent traffic noise that significantly impacts many guests' experience, with some leaving early and many requiring earplugs - this is the single most mentioned negative across hundreds of reviews.
2. The pool is repeatedly described as "very small," "tiny," "about the size of a backyard one," and inadequate when the park is busy, with insufficient shade options - a major limitation for a family holiday park in Queensland's climate.
3. Limited entertainment facilities compared to other BIG4 parks, with no water park, small playground, and modest activities that suit young children but disappoint families with older kids or teenagers seeking more extensive facilities.
