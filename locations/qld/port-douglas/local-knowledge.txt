G'day! Port Douglas is where the rainforest tumbles right down to meet the Great Barrier Reef, and honestly, how many places in the world can claim that? The dry season from May to October is your sweet spot – the stingers are gone from the beaches, the humidity drops off, and you'll actually want to explore beyond the pool. Here's a local secret: grab your snorkel gear from one of the shops on Macrossan Street and head to the southern end of Four Mile Beach at low tide, where the kids can spot turtles feeding in the shallows without even booking a tour.
