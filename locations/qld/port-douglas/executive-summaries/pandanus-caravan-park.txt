PARK: Pandanus Caravan Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-22
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 127

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — No dedicated kids entertainment, playground or activities mentioned. One family explicitly warned against staying with children due to noise complaints at the pool. No waterpark, jumping pillow or kids club facilities. The only entertainment mentioned was occasional pizza nights and BBQ events organized by previous management.

Nature (15/20) — Strong natural setting with "beautiful tropical rainforest", mature trees providing shade, sites backing onto forest, and bird watching opportunities. Walking distance to palm beaches (10-20 minutes). Natural environment praised as "very natural and well maintained" and "omgiven av vacker tropisk regnskog". However, some complaints about bat droppings from trees and sewerage smell from nearby treatment plant.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Highly polarized reviews. Many praised facilities as "spotless", "immaculately maintained", "best we've stayed in two months", with daily deep cleaning noted. However, recent reviews (2024-2025) report declining standards: "toilets were not clean, full of hair", "showers not clean", "ablutions quite messy", "facilities desperately need an upgrade" with peeling paint and old equipment. Cabin cleanliness complaints included filthy floors and dirty linen.

Value (10/15) — Mixed value perception. Some found it "reasonably priced" ($46-67 unpowered) and "cheaper than other options". However, multiple recent reviews complained: "far too expensive for poor quality", "not worth more than $30/night" (paid $80), "$70 a night is about $20 too much", "way too expensive for what it was". Price increases without facility upgrades hurt value perception.

Site Size (7/10) — Generally described as "large and easy to get into", "spacious", "good size and level" with powered slabs. However, some noted "very tight caravan spots", "unpowered sites not all equal, some narrow between trees", uneven ground with tree roots making tent camping uncomfortable, and some sites requiring parking over slabs due to tree growth.

Sentiment (8/10) — Highly polarized. Loyal fans gave 5-star reviews praising staff warmth and extended stays (2 months common). However, a concerning pattern of 1-star reviews alleging racist treatment of backpackers and international guests, with multiple reports of rudeness from management (particularly Helen and Scott). Families felt unwelcome. Recent management change to Ash and Jack shows improved sentiment in 2024-2025 reviews.

Location (8/10) — Excellent proximity: 10-20 minute walk to Port Douglas town center, marina, restaurants and beaches. "Perfect location near the marina and all the shops", "conveniently located", "close to everything". However, road noise mentioned from main access road, and noise from adjacent backpacker hostel until 11:30pm reported.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Zero kids entertainment or family facilities - no playground, no kids club, no activities, with at least one family explicitly warned away due to complaints about children playing at the pool, making it unsuitable for young families despite being reviewed as a "holiday park".

Dated, declining facilities desperately needing renovation - despite cleanliness efforts, amenities described as "very tired, old fashioned with peeling paint", "run down", old washing machines not working, tiny shower cubicles, and no capital investment due to pending redevelopment creating a "sinking ship" atmosphere.

Serious customer service concerns with multiple credible reports of discriminatory treatment toward international visitors and backpackers, rudeness from previous management, and families with children made to feel unwelcome, severely impacting the family travel experience despite positive staff reviews under newer management.
