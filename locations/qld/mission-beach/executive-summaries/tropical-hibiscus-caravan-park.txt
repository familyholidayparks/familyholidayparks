PARK: Tropical Hibiscus Caravan Park
SCORE: 74/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-25
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 93

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews mention pool, gym, TV room, pizza nights with music, onsite cafe, and occasional cassowary/wallaby sightings which delight children; one family noted "my toddler had a treat" and another mentioned "lots of games for children," but no major waterpark or dedicated kids club activities were referenced.

Nature (17/20) — Families rave about the tropical gardens, "waking up to wallabies everywhere is magic," cassowaries appearing through the park, 500m walk to "pristine Wongaling Beach" with crystal clear ocean overlooking Dunk Island, and beautiful nearby walks; the natural environment is a standout feature.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly consistent praise: "spotless," "one of the cleanest we've been at yet," "amenities spotless," "camp kitchen spotless," "toilets and showers were spotless every time," with only 2-3 negative reviews (one from Jan 2025 mentioning "Dirty! Lazy cleaners") contrasting 90+ positive mentions.

Value (13/15) — Many families highlight "excellent value for money," "fair price," "good bang for your buck," "very good rates," and reasonable pricing for powered/unpowered sites; 4-bedroom cabins specifically praised for value; a few reviews note competitive pricing compared to other Mission Beach parks.

Site Size (7/10) — Multiple mentions of "good sized sites," "big sites," and "well grassed" sites with concrete slabs; however, several reviews note "site size a little narrow," "tight spaces," and one complaint about site 17 being cramped under berry trees, indicating variability.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong positive sentiment with families saying "we will definitely be back," "wouldn't hesitate to stay here again," "will 100 percent stay," and many extending stays or returning for repeat visits; new owners (Keith & Christine) receive exceptional praise for warmth and hospitality; 3-4 strongly negative reviews prevent a perfect score.

Location (9/10) — Superb location praised repeatedly: "500m walk to beach," "2 mins walk to Woolworths," "close to shops," "walking distance to everything," near Thai restaurant and cafes, Greyhound bus stop nearby; families appreciate the central yet peaceful setting away from highway noise.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited dedicated kids entertainment infrastructure — while the pool, wildlife, and pizza nights are enjoyed, there's no mention of playgrounds with modern equipment, jumping pillows, waterslides, or structured kids programs that top-tier family parks offer.
2. Site size inconsistency and narrowness — multiple reviews mention tight/narrow sites and one specific complaint about site 17 being cramped under berry trees that stained vehicles, indicating layout challenges for families with larger rigs.
3. Three strongly negative reviews citing rudeness, cleanliness issues, and facility access problems (e.g., pool closed for private wedding, one "dirty" mention in Jan 2025) indicate occasional service/maintenance lapses, though these are outliers among 90+ positive reviews.
