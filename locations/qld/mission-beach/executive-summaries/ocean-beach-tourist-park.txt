PARK: Ocean Beach Tourist Park
SCORE: 87/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 500

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews highlight organized activities including pancake breakfasts, movie nights, arts and crafts mornings, and sausage sizzles. The fairy garden and Jurassic Park dinosaur area are beloved by young children who 'spent hours playing there every day.' Managers Bec, Pete, and new managers Jodie and Darren create a 'fun and friendly environment' with planned activities. No pool or playground, but the beach compensates. Lost points for limited fixed facilities.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional beachfront location 'directly across the road from beautiful Miami Beach' with patrolled swimming areas. Reviews consistently praise the 'sound of waves,' sunrise views, and 'surrounded by lush bushland.' Beach access is immediate and safe for families. The coastal pathway offers 'amazing beachfront walks in some of the best beachfront parklands in Australia.' Near-perfect score; minimal nature loss.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Perfect score. Reviews universally describe 'the cleanest bins and laundry facilities we have seen anywhere in the country,' amenities 'sparkle like brand new,' and 'immaculately cleaned every day.' Staff maintain 'spotless' facilities with 'sparkling clean' outdoor kitchen and amenities. 'Cleanest park on the coast' appears repeatedly.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing. Some guests mention '$120 a night' feeling steep, though many find it 'great value' for the location and quality. The organized free activities (pancake breakfasts) add value. Compared to Gold Coast alternatives, reviews suggest fair but not exceptional value. Lost points for price sensitivity.

Site Size (8/10) — Sites described as 'quite spacious' and 'huge clean sites,' though the small boutique park means 'sites can be tight' for maneuvering. The 'small park' feel is intentional and creates community atmosphere. Some mention sites are adequate but not oversized. Artificial turf and concrete pads help maximize usable space.

Sentiment (10/10) — Perfect sentiment score. Families return 'year after year' (one reviewer noted their 5th visit), describe it as 'our favorite park,' and say 'we will definitely be back.' Named '#1 caravan park in the country' in 2025 ReviewPro awards. Overwhelmingly positive recommendations with families feeling 'part of a little family community.'

Location (9/10) — Exceptional beachfront location on Miami Beach with surf club at corner, coffee shop at gate, and 'walking distance to grocery stores,' cafes, and North Burleigh surf club. Easy access to Burleigh Heads, Pacific Fair. Public transport connections. Light rail construction currently impacts access slightly but location otherwise near-perfect.

WHY NOT 100:
1. No pool or major play facilities - reliant entirely on beach and organized activities, which some families with very young kids may find limiting during bad weather or stinger season.
2. Site access can be tight for larger caravans - the boutique park size means maneuvering bigger rigs requires care, and the 'small road between sites makes it tight to get into the site or out.'
3. Pricing sits at premium end without full resort amenities - at $110-120/night, some families feel it's expensive for a park without pool, playground, or games room, despite the exceptional location and cleanliness.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
'cleanest bins and laundry facilities anywhere in the country,' 'directly across from beautiful Miami Beach,' 'feels like a little family community,' 'managers go way above and beyond,' 'fairy garden and Jurassic Park dinosaur area,' 'pancake breakfasts and movie nights,' 'immaculately clean, sparkles like brand new,' 'best Caravan Park on the Gold Coast'
