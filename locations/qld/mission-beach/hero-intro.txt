# Mission Beach, QLD

Mission Beach is a laid-back coastal town about an hour north of Townsville where families can actually relax without breaking the bank. The main draw is the beach itself – a long, golden stretch of sand backed by rainforest that's perfect for swimming, building sandcastles, and letting the kids run around. The water's warm year-round, and lifeguard patrols keep things safe during peak season. Beyond the sand, you've got the Daintree Rainforest practically on your doorstep, offering walks through ancient jungle, platypus spotting, and genuine wildlife encounters that beat any zoo visit.

For families, the real appeal is that Mission Beach doesn't try too hard – there's no carnival nonsense, just good-quality outdoor time. The town has solid accommodation options including Ocean Beach Tourist Park, BIG4 Tasman Holiday Parks at South Mission Beach, Mission Beach Camping & Caravan Park, and Tropical Hibiscus Caravan Park, all of which cater well to families and won't drain your budget. There are rock pools to explore, a handful of decent cafes, and enough walking trails to keep curious kids occupied. It's the kind of place where children can play in the water while parents actually get a moment to breathe.
