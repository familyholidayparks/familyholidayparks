G'day! Rainbow Beach is where the red and gold cliffs of the Cooloola coast meet some of Queensland's calmest swimming waters – and honestly, it's one of those rare spots where your toddlers can paddle safely while the older kids try their hand at bodyboarding in the same stretch of beach. We've found September through November is absolute gold for families here: the humpback whales are cruising past, the weather's stunning, and you'll dodge both the stinger season and the summer holiday crowds. Here's a local tip that'll save your sanity: grab your morning coffee and pastries from Rainbow Beach Bakery before 8am, then head straight to the southern end of the main beach at low tide – the kids can explore the rock pools for hours while you actually finish a hot cuppa.
