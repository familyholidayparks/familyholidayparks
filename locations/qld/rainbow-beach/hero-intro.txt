# Rainbow Beach, QLD

Rainbow Beach lives up to its name with stretches of golden sand backed by those famous coloured sand cliffs that kids find absolutely fascinating. The main beach is patrolled by lifeguards and has calm swimming areas perfect for younger children, while the rock pools at either end are great for exploring marine life. It's the kind of place where you can spend a full day just mucking about in the water and sand without needing much else, though the cafe strip nearby means you won't go hungry.

The real drawcard for families is how close nature feels here. You've got Fraser Island just across the water, the Cooloola National Park right on the doorstep with decent walking tracks, and Carlo Sandblow nearby for a bit of adventure without being too strenuous. The BIG4 Rainbow Beach Holiday Park offers solid family accommodation with all the usual facilities, and from there you're within walking distance of the beach and town. It's the kind of destination where older kids can do their own thing exploring tide pools or hiking, while younger ones are happy just being near the water and seeing what washes up.
