The Gold Coast is one of Australia's best destinations for family holiday and caravan parks, offering everything from beachfront camping to riverside resorts and family-friendly holiday parks with pools, waterslides and jumping pillows.

Unlike many destinations, you're never far from the action. Most parks are within easy reach of beaches, theme parks, playgrounds and major attractions, making it easy to spend less time driving and more time enjoying your holiday.

Whether you're travelling with a caravan, camper trailer or booking a cabin, the Gold Coast has holiday parks to suit every budget and travel style. Families often return year after year thanks to the combination of great facilities, reliable weather and endless things to do with kids.
