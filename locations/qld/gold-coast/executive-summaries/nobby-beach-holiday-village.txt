PARK: Nobby Beach Holiday Village
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-14
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 203

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (15/20) — The standout feature is the large heated pool with an impressive waterslide that kids absolutely love, plus ninja playground and monkey bars. Some events like live music, face painting, and food trucks occur during peak periods. However, slide hours are limited to office hours which frustrated early-rising families, and supervision is lacking.

Nature (11/20) — Located about 10-15 minutes walk from Nobby Beach with reasonable beach access. The park itself has minimal natural features with very few trees and limited green space. Situated on busy Gold Coast Highway under flight path, so natural ambience is compromised. Proximity to Miami Markets and cafes provides some environmental interest.

Cleanliness (8/15) — This is a major concern with highly polarized feedback. Multiple recent reviews describe filthy accommodations with cockroach infestations, dust, grime, sticky floors, and blood stains. Amenities are frequently described as "disgusting," "filthy," "dirty from lunch time on," with toilets not cleaned for days. Some guests report clean experiences, but negative cleanliness reviews are too frequent and severe to ignore.

Value (10/15) — Mixed value perception with many calling it "ridiculously overpriced" especially at $450-500/night for unclean villas. Gold Coast pricing means not cheap for powered sites ($60-80). Some found it "good value" for basic studios and appreciated free upgrades. Location adds value but poor cleanliness significantly undermines it for families expecting holiday park standards.

Site Size (5/10) — Consistent criticism about "very tight space for vans," "super close next to each other," "very cramped," and "could shake hands with neighbor." Caravan sites described as squeezed together with minimal privacy and difficult maneuvering. Some corner sites work better for long rigs. Parking also problematic with limited spaces.

Sentiment (6/10) — Deeply divided sentiment from "absolutely loved everything" to "worst experience ever." Many families return repeatedly and recommend it, praising staff and facilities. However, numerous 1-star reviews express disappointment, anger over cleanliness/cockroaches, poor service, and feeling misled. Recent reviews trend more negative than older ones.

Location (7/10) — Excellent proximity to beaches, Miami Markets (7-minute walk), shops, cafes, and public transport. Central to theme parks (15-30 minutes). Light rail extension coming. However, on busy highway with construction noise, tight entry/exit especially with tram works, and under airport flight path. Location is convenient but not peaceful.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severe and recurring cleanliness issues across accommodations and amenities, with multiple reports of cockroach infestations, filthy bathrooms, dusty surfaces, and inadequate cleaning between guests that fundamentally undermine the family holiday experience.
2. Extremely tight site spacing for caravans with minimal privacy, uneven/muddy ground conditions, and difficult vehicle access that makes the camping experience cramped and stressful, particularly for larger rigs.
3. Inconsistent service quality with reports of rude/unhelpful staff, unreturned phone calls and emails, poor communication, inadequate response to serious maintenance issues, and billing/refund problems that erode trust and guest satisfaction.
