PARK: Tallebudgera Creek Tourist Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-18
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 355

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Consistently praised for heated pools with waterslides that keep kids entertained for hours, rental trikes/bikes, holiday activities during peak periods, and the natural entertainment of creek access for paddle boarding, fishing, and swimming. Food trucks and on-site kiosk add convenience. Some mention playground needs updating.

Nature (19/20) — Outstanding natural setting directly on Tallebudgera Creek with safe enclosed swimming areas, walking distance to surf beaches, coastal walks to Burleigh Heads National Park, and beautiful sunrise/sunset views. Wildlife sightings include rainbow lorikeets, iguanas, bush turkeys. Creek described as clear and beautiful with sandy banks.

Cleanliness (13/15) — Amenities frequently described as "spotless," "always clean," and "very well maintained" with staff cleaning regularly throughout the day. However, some negative reviews cite issues with corroded shower heads, broken toilet seats not fixed promptly, mould in cabin bathrooms, and occasional cleanliness lapses during peak periods.

Value (11/15) — Pricing described as "expensive," "overpriced," and premium with unpowered sites $70-100/night and cabins $400+/night. Many feel the location justifies the cost, but some express disappointment at value given aging cabin conditions and maintenance issues. Described as "paying for the location."

Site Size (9/10) — Powered sites consistently praised as "generous," "really nice sized," "extremely large," and "good sized sites for larger caravans" with easy drive-through access. Some complaints about unpowered tent sites being "tiny" and cramped near highway.

Sentiment (9/10) — Extremely high repeat visitation with families returning for 7-55 years annually. Described as "home away from home," "second home," "favourite place." However, several recent negative reviews cite rude management (specifically "Kate"), poor customer service recovery, and feeling unwelcome, preventing a perfect score.

Location (10/10) — Perfect marks for location directly on creek with underpass to Palm Beach, 5-10 minute walk to surf beach and Burleigh Heads, walking distance to Burleigh village cafes/restaurants, and easy access to Gold Coast attractions. Public transport at park entrance. Central Gold Coast position.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Cabins showing significant age with maintenance issues including mould in bathrooms, broken fixtures, dated interiors, and inconsistent cleaning standards despite premium pricing ($400+/night).
2. Customer service concerns with multiple reviews citing rude management (particularly "Kate"), inflexible policies, poor complaint resolution, and guests feeling unwelcome or dismissed when raising legitimate issues.
3. Cleanliness lapses during peak periods, maintenance items not addressed promptly (broken toilets/showers for weeks), and amenities needing renovation despite generally good upkeep and frequent cleaning efforts.
