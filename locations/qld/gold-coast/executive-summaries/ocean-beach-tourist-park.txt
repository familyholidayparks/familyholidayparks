PARK: Ocean Beach Tourist Park
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-16
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 110

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (15/20) — Reviews consistently praise management-organized activities including pancake breakfasts, movie nights with popcorn and marshmallows, arts and crafts, sausage sizzles, rock painting, and koala chocolate hunts. Families highlight the "fairy garden and little Jurassic Park dinosaur area" where 3-year-olds "spent hours playing every day." Multiple reviews note managers "lend scooters/bikes" and create daily activities during school holidays that compensate for no pool or games room.

Nature (19/20) — Exceptional beachfront location with "direct access to supervised Miami South Beach" literally across the road through a back gate. Families describe patrolled swimming areas, shallow water perfect for kids, abundant shells, whale watching opportunities, and "the sound of waves crashing" at night. The park is "surrounded by lush bushland and sheltered by the headland" with "amazing beachfront walks in some of the best beachfront parklands in Australia."

Cleanliness (15/15) — Overwhelming consensus on exceptional cleanliness. Multiple families who have "travelled all around Australia" call this "the cleanest bins and laundry facilities we have seen anywhere in the country." Reviews repeatedly use "sparkling clean," "immaculately cleaned every day," "spotless amenities," and "honestly sparkles like it's brand new" to describe bathrooms, camp kitchen, and laundry facilities.

Value (12/15) — Pricing at $62-69 per night receives mixed feedback. Some families consider it "extremely affordable" and "worth the money," especially given the beachfront location. However, multiple reviews note it's "pricey" for the Gold Coast, with one family paying $120/night feeling "not satisfied with value" after experiencing a power cut and WiFi issues. Most agree location justifies cost but some "would not stay again" based on price.

Site Size (8/10) — Sites described as "quite spacious," "very generous," and "nice and big" with many featuring artificial turf and concrete pads. However, the small 62-site park has "narrow roadways" and is "a little tight in places" making it "tight to get into the site or out" with larger rigs. One critical review called it "a glorified carpark" though most families found site sizes adequate for their needs.

Sentiment (10/10) — Exceptional repeat visitation with families returning for 4-6+ years consecutively. Reviews use phrases like "our favourite park," "home away from home," "kids begging to come back," and "we will keep coming back." The park "totally deserved winning the 2025 ReviewPro People's Choice Caravan Park of the Year Award and officially being named the #1 caravan park in the country." Very high recommendation rate among family reviewers.

Location (10/10) — Perfect Gold Coast location directly on Miami Beach with patrolled surf club and cafe literally at the gate. "Walking distance to Aldi, Coles, Dan Murphy's, restaurants, and 6 playgrounds" while remaining "tucked away and peaceful." Easy bike/Lime scooter access to Burleigh Heads and Surfers Paradise (15-30 minutes). Public transport connections directly in front. "Park your car once and literally never move it again."

WHY NOT 100:
1. No WiFi or reliable mobile service creates significant frustration for modern families, with multiple reviews calling it a "Telstra black hole" and noting inability to contact reception during emergencies.
2. No pool or traditional playground facilities means the park entirely depends on beach access for entertainment, limiting appeal during poor weather or for families wanting diverse activity options.
3. Value concerns with pricing at $68-120/night considered high by multiple reviewers, particularly when experiencing issues like lukewarm showers, power cuts without explanation, or bikes riding through campsites.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"cleanest bins and laundry facilities anywhere in the country," "fairy garden and Jurassic Park dinosaur area," "pancake breakfasts and movie nights," "park your car once and never move it," "won #1 caravan park in the country," "truly feels like a family community," "directly across from beautiful Miami Beach," "managers go way above and beyond"
