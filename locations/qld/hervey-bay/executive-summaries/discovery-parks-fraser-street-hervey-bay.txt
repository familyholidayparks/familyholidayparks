PARK: Discovery Parks - Fraser Street, Hervey Bay
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 175

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Exceptional family facilities with jumping pillow, games room, tennis, basketball, two pools, and go-karts creating constant engagement. The lake with turtles, ducks, eels and fish (free feed from reception) is a standout attraction. Staff-organized activities include kids club, movie nights, breakfast gatherings, sausage sizzles, live music, and even reptile shows. Minor points lost as games room described as "dated" and one pool occasionally closed.

Nature (16/20) — The natural lake with abundant wildlife (turtles, water birds, eels, fish) provides daily nature encounters that kids love. Beautiful landscaping with trees, green grass, and attractive gardens. Located 4-5 minute walk to Hervey Bay beach and esplanade with walking/cycling paths. Points lost for urban location (not bushland setting) and pigeon noise complaints from some guests.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently praised as "spotless," "immaculate," and "meticulously clean" across amenities, ensuites, pools, and cabins. Ensuites described as luxury standard with waterfall showers and premium Ink & Water toiletries. Staff actively maintain grounds daily. One point lost for isolated incidents of BBQ area being "super filthy," mould in one cabin bathroom, and linen hygiene issue.

Value (13/15) — Generally considered good value with competitive pricing ($38/night for powered sites mentioned). Direct booking through website offers best rates. Special deals available (two weeks for price of one). Some guests felt prices were high compared to other parks on their journey, particularly given tight site sizes and construction disruptions during their stays.

Site Size (7/10) — Sites adequate for most rigs but consistently described as "tight," "cramped," and "narrow" especially for larger vans. Staff assistance offered and appreciated for positioning. Ensuite sites well-sized with level concrete pads. Some guests struggled for 30+ minutes to park due to narrow access roads and scaffolding from construction work.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with numerous repeat visitors ("6th visit," "coming for 3 years," "2nd time," "our go to park"). Many guests extended stays or booked return visits during checkout. Staff praised extensively by name (Min, Ana, Amanda, John, Craig, Dave, Rhi). Minor points lost for handful of negative reception experiences and service issues.

Location (6/10) — Walking distance to beach, esplanade, shops, cafes, restaurants, and mini golf (4-5 minute walk). Short drive to supermarkets and marina. One street back from main esplanade provides quieter setting. Points lost as not beachfront location and some felt "back from beach" was disadvantage despite being close.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site sizes are tight and cramped for larger caravans with narrow access roads, requiring staff assistance and causing parking struggles that lasted 30+ minutes for some guests.
2. Construction work during multiple visits created extreme noise, closed showers, blocked access with scaffolding, and covered vehicles in wood chips without warning or apology from management.
3. Location is one street back from beach rather than beachfront, and some isolated service issues included rude reception staff, poor refund communication, gate timer issues trapping guests, and poor TV/wifi reception.
