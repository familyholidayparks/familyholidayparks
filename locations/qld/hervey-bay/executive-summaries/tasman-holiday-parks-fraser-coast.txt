PARK: Tasman Holiday Parks - Fraser Coast
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 96

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews mention games area, common room for kids, pool with pool cues available, and BBQ areas, but limited structured activities or entertainment programs. Families appreciated kids having space to play but noted lack of holiday atmosphere compared to resort-style parks.

Nature (12/20) — Park described as quiet and well-maintained with lovely gardens, but 15-20 minute walk to beach/esplanade. No creek, bushland or significant natural features on-site. Location serves as base for Fraser Island tours but nature experience requires leaving the park.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Strong mixed feedback. Many praised "exceptionally clean" amenities, "spotless" camp kitchen, and clean cabins. However, multiple recent reviews reported filthy cabins, mould, hair in showers, stained crockery, and ant infestations, indicating inconsistent cleaning standards.

Value (10/15) — Mixed reviews on value. Some noted "outstanding value," "good price," and "very good value for money." Others complained of overpriced cabins at $200-362/night that were "tiny," "basic," and "least value for money park during our 6 week trip."

Site Size (6/10) — Multiple complaints about "tiny" and "small sites," difficulty backing in vans, "tight" spaces with vans on either side. Several reviews mentioned sites had "a lot of room" but negative comments about cramped conditions were more common.

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive with many 5-star reviews and repeat visitors. Families praised friendly staff and quiet atmosphere. However, significant negative reviews about cleanliness, value, and misleading photos. Some stated they won't return.

Location (9/10) — Centrally located between shops and beach, 10-15 minute walk to esplanade, 10 minutes to shopping centre. Close to Fraser Island tour departure. Main road noise noted by some. Convenient but not beachfront.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Inconsistent cleanliness standards with multiple recent reports of filthy cabins, mould, and poor maintenance despite many praising cleanliness.
2. Limited on-site entertainment and activities for children beyond basic games room and pool—lacks holiday atmosphere and structured kids' programs.
3. Tight, small sites causing difficulty for caravans, combined with distance from beach requiring car/significant walk, reducing convenience and space families expect.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
