PARK: Fraser Coast Beachfront Tourist Parks | Torquay
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-19
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 106

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Very limited on-site kids entertainment facilities; one reviewer mentioned managers occasionally providing zooper doopers for kids, but no playground, jumping pillow, or organized activities mentioned. The beach itself provides natural entertainment with dolphin sightings and calm swimming.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding natural environment with absolute beachfront positioning less than 10 meters from the beach, calm ocean swimming, regular dolphin sightings, beautiful sunsets, and Fraser Island views. Direct beach access via stairs, great walking tracks along the esplanade, and natural bird sounds create an excellent coastal experience.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently praised as "spotless," "very clean," and "sparkling clean facilities" with daily cleaning of amenities. A few reviews mentioned older/dated facilities and occasional issues (urine smell in toilets, washing machines not as clean), preventing a perfect score.

Value (13/15) — Generally excellent value with "$56 a night right next to the amenities" for ocean front views and "excellent price for powered sites" mentioned repeatedly. Some felt sites were "not worth the small site for price," and tighter spaces reduced perceived value for some guests.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant mixed feedback: some praised "sites are quite large" post-rebuild with "enough room for a large van, awning and parking," but many complained about being "packed in like sardines," "sites are pretty close together and quite small," tight reversing, and minimal car parking space, especially in back rows.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very strong positive sentiment with many repeat visitors ("3rd revisit," "over 30 years," "twice in 4 years"), enthusiastic recommendations, and high praise for management. A few negative experiences with booking errors and poor customer service prevented a perfect score.

Location (10/10) — Perfect score for location with absolute beachfront positioning, shops/supermarket across the road, cafes and Torquay pub within walking distance, patrolled beach nearby, surflifesaving club, and convenient Fraser Island tour bus pickup. Walking distance to all amenities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. No dedicated children's entertainment facilities — no playground, no kids' club, no jumping pillow, or organized activities that families with young children typically expect at holiday parks.
2. Site spacing issues create significant challenges — multiple reviews cite being "packed like sardines," difficulty reversing vans, minimal parking space, lack of privacy, and tight sites especially in back rows near the road.
3. Road noise from the esplanade — consistent complaints about "hoons" racing cars with loud exhausts, particularly at night around 9pm, with traffic noise intrusive for those in sites near the road.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
