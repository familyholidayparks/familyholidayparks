PARK: Pier Caravan Park Hervey Bay
SCORE: 78/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-06-09
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 146

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (14/20) — Reviews mention a games room with ping pong and three arcade games, plus a playground for kids. Families appreciated these facilities, though there's no waterpark or extensive organised activities. One reviewer noted "Our son loved the games room with ping pong, arcade games" and another praised it as "a very kid friendly park." Entertainment is modest but adequate for younger children.

Nature (16/20) — The park is a short walk to the beautiful Urangan Pier, beachfront walking paths, and an off-leash dog beach. Multiple reviews highlighted "fantastic sunset views and fishing," "close to beach, esplanade," and "walking distance to the SPAR, bottle shop, pier and the market." The natural setting is coastal with easy access to water views, though not bushland or creek-based nature.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Overwhelmingly, reviews praised the park's cleanliness with phrases like "spotlessly clean facilities," "amenities were immaculate," "super clean," and "cleanest and most organised I have ever visited." Staff maintain the park daily and facilities are described as "pristine" and "top class."

Value (11/15) — Value perceptions were mixed. Several reviews noted "getting a little bit expensive" and "$62 a night pretty exy," while others said "great value" and "very clean, safe, and great value." Families appreciated fifth-night-free deals, but recent pricing concerns emerged in 2025-2026 reviews.

Site Size (8/10) — Most reviews praised "large sites," "spacious sites," and "big sites which is great when you have a dog." However, some noted challenges: "a bit tight for average backers," "tight to get into with a larger van," and "trees planted right where a large van needs to reverse." Sites are generally good but not universally easy for all rig sizes.

Sentiment (9/10) — Sentiment is overwhelmingly positive with many repeat visitors and high recommendation rates. Phrases like "will definitely be back," "our family have agreed that it is traditional to go to the Pier once a year," and "best park by far" dominate. However, three recent 1-star reviews (April 2026) cited aggressive staff behaviour and safety concerns, slightly tempering the sentiment score.

Location (10/10) — Location scored perfectly with consistent praise: "great location," "close to everything," "5 minute walk to the pier," "walking distance to town and Esplanade," and "close to the water, pier, great shops and eateries." Families loved the proximity to Urangan Pier, Saturday markets, cafes, and beaches.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Entertainment facilities are basic (playground and small games room) with no waterpark, jumping pillow, or extensive organised kids' activities that families expect from top-tier parks.
2. Value perceptions have declined in recent reviews with multiple families noting increasing costs and describing pricing as "expensive" or "exy," impacting affordability for budget-conscious families.
3. Site access issues for larger caravans (21ft+) were mentioned repeatedly, with poorly placed trees and tight angles making reversing difficult, and one reviewer damaged their wheel on a culvert.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"spotlessly clean facilities," "walking distance to everything we needed," "games room with ping pong arcade games," "close to Urangan Pier," "best park by far," "immaculately kept," "dog friendly," "very kid friendly park," "great location friendly staff," "getting a little bit expensive but it is worth it"
