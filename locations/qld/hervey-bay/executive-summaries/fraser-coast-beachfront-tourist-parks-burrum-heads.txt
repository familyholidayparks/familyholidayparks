PARK: Fraser Coast Beachfront Tourist Parks | Burrum Heads
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 84

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews mention a "great playground" but no pool or structured kids' activities; one family noted "Would be great if park had a pool"; the park relies on natural environment rather than organized entertainment with no waterpark, kids' club, or activity programs mentioned.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural setting "right on the Burrum river" with "beautiful sunsets" and "amazing sunsets and right on the water"; families enjoy river walks with "walk along the Burrum River is very pleasant"; beachfront sites offer "amazing views" and easy water access for fishing and SUP's; walking distance to swimming beach.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Outstanding cleanliness scores with "Best and cleanest toilet and shower facilities I've seen," "immaculate amenities and one of the best designs," "amenities spotless," and "not even a cobweb on the ceiling"; daily cleaning schedules maintained; recently renovated facilities described as "very fresh and clean."

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing with "one of the pricier caravan parks we have stayed in" and "price is somewhat challenging but you get what you pay for"; one review noted "prices have increased by 38% per day"; however, many felt "Happy to pay the extra for a great stay" given the quality; $70 per night mentioned.

Site Size (7/10) — "Spacious sites" frequently mentioned with "large sites" and "generous grassed sites"; however, "Sites are very tight to get into at river side" and "roads between the sites are very narrow so it makes it difficult to get your van in"; back sites described as "roomier" for larger rigs.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very high satisfaction with families returning year after year; "16th year at this park" and "we love coming up this way every year"; strong recommendation rate with "If I could have 10 stars I would"; isolated negative reviews about management conflicts but overwhelmingly positive overall; "We will definitely be back" is a common refrain.

Location (10/10) — Perfect location marks with "Perfect location" and "location is excellent"; "Foodworks, bakery, butcher, take away fish and chips, fishing gear beside the park" all within walking distance; "literally next door" to shops; close to Hervey Bay attractions; river and beach access immediate.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of pool facilities which multiple families specifically noted would enhance the park, particularly for summer stays with children who don't have air conditioning in their vans.
2. Limited structured kids' entertainment beyond the playground, with no kids' club, organized activities, jumping pillow, or waterpark features that young families typically seek.
3. Pricing sits at the higher end of the market with some families noting significant price increases and others finding it "challenging," which affects value perception despite quality facilities.
