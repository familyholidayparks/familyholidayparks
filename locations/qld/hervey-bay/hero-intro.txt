# Hervey Bay Family Holiday

Hervey Bay is a beaut of a spot for families after a laid-back Queensland getaway. The wide, sandy beaches are perfect for kids—the water's generally calm and patrolled by lifeguards, so you can relax while the little ones paddle and play. Beyond the beach, there's heaps of nature to explore: Fraser Island is right on the doorstep for a day trip, and you've got the Great Sandy National Park inland with walking tracks that suit all fitness levels. The town itself is pretty manageable to get around, with good cafes, fish and chip shops, and plenty of spots to grab a feed without fussing about.

For accommodation, you've got solid caravan park options dotted around—Discovery Parks on Fraser Street, Ingenia Holidays, Hervey Bay Caravan Park, Pier Caravan Park, and Fraser Coast Beachfront Tourist Parks at Scarness all offer family-friendly setups with amenities that make school holiday chaos a bit easier. The main draw for families is whale watching season (May to November) when humpbacks migrate north, plus dolphin spotting and glass-bottom boat tours that keep kids entertained without being too touristy. It's the kind of place where you can do as much or as little as you want—perfect if you're after genuine family time without the crowds.
