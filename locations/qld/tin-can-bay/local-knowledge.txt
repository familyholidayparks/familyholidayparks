G'day! Tin Can Bay and the surrounding Cooloola Coast offer families that rare combination of calm, crystal-clear waters perfect for little swimmers and the magic of hand-feeding wild dolphins at Norman Point most mornings around 8am. We've found winter through spring (June to October) is absolutely ideal – the weather's glorious, the water's still swimmable, and you'll dodge the summer stinger season that affects areas further north. Here's a local secret: grab fresh prawns from the trawlers at the boat ramp in the late afternoon, then cook them up at your cabin while the kids spot stingrays from the jetty – it's become our family's favourite Tin Can Bay tradition and costs a fraction of restaurant prices.
