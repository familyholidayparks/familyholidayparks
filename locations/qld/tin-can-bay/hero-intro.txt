Planning a family trip to Tin Can Bay? We've done the legwork to find the best family holiday parks in Tin Can Bay so you can skip hours of research and get straight to the good part. Families love Tin Can Bay because the kids get to hand-feed wild dolphins at the boat ramp each morning, the calm shallow waters are perfect for little ones learning to kayak, and there's something magical about a place where the biggest decision is whether to go crabbing or explore the rainbow beach dunes.
