G'day! Moreton Bay's stunning network of islands and coastal parks creates a protected playground where littlies can safely splash in calm waters while older kids try their hand at fishing off the jetties. We've found September through November is absolutely magic here – the water's warming up nicely, the stingers haven't arrived yet, and you'll dodge both the summer holiday crowds and the winter chill. Here's an insider tip: grab your fresh mud crabs and prawns directly from the trawlers at the Scarborough Boat Harbour around 11am most mornings – the kids love watching the boats come in, and you'll pay half what the shops charge for seafood that was swimming just hours ago.
