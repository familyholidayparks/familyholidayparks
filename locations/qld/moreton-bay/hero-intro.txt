Moreton Bay is a genuine family-friendly destination on Queensland's coast, offering something for every age group without the crowds you'll find further south. The bay has fantastic shallow beaches perfect for kids, and you've got plenty of nature to explore – think mangroves, waterways teeming with birdlife, and easy walks that won't exhaust the little ones. The main attractions include Tangalooma on Moreton Island where you can hand-feed dolphins, and closer to shore you'll find good fish and chip shops, playgrounds, and calm swimming spots that beat fighting the waves at ocean beaches.

For accommodation, the area has solid caravan parks that cater well to families: Beachmere, Bells, Toorbul, and Donnybrook all offer reasonable rates and the kind of setup that lets kids run around safely. You're close enough to Brisbane for a day trip if needed, but far enough away to feel like a proper getaway. The pace here is slower, the air's cleaner, and parents actually get a chance to relax while the kids stay entertained with beaches, exploring rockpools, and spotting local wildlife.
