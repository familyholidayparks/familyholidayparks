PARK: Donnybrook Caravan Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 22

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Limited on-site kids entertainment with one mention of an adjacent playground and BBQ area next door; primarily a fishing-focused park with activities centred on crabbing, fishing off the pier, and waterfront walks rather than structured entertainment facilities.
Nature (16/20) — Excellent natural waterfront setting right on the water with fishing pier access, long walking paths around the foreshore, crabbing opportunities, and boat ramp access; described as feeling "remote" despite being close to Brisbane with peaceful water views.
Cleanliness (13/15) — Consistently praised for clean amenities with multiple mentions of "clean showers and toilets," "clean amenities," "well kept, tidy and clean," and "very clean amenities"; camp kitchen and laundry facilities well-maintained.
Value (12/15) — Described as "good value park" with reasonable pricing for the waterfront location; convenient general store across the road and takeaway options available, though specific pricing not detailed in reviews.
Site Size (6/10) — Mixed feedback with several mentions of "tight sites," "small park," and needing to "do some maneuvering" to access spots, though one recent review noted "good size sites"; approximately 17 casual sites in a compact layout.
Sentiment (8/10) — Positive overall sentiment with families appreciating the "laid back" atmosphere and multiple mentions of return visits ("definitely coming back," "would go back in future"); particularly valued by fishing families seeking relaxation.
Location (8/10) — Strong location 40 minutes from Brisbane on the waterfront with general store, post office, and fish & chips across the road; boat ramp access at rear; bowls club nearby; feels remote while being accessible to major city.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Minimal dedicated kids' entertainment infrastructure with only an adjacent playground mentioned—no pool, waterpark, jumping pillow, games room, or organized activities for children.
2. Tight site sizes with multiple reviewers noting space constraints, limited maneuvering room, and only approximately 17 casual sites in a very small park footprint.
3. Limited family-specific amenities and facilities with primary focus on fishing rather than diverse family entertainment; mosquitoes mentioned as a notable downside.
