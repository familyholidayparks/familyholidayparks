Canberra might not be on the coast, but it's a ripper of a family destination that gives you the best of inland Australia without the long drive. The city's packed with world-class attractions like the National Museum of Australia, Australian War Memorial, and Questacon that'll keep the kids entertained for days. You've got heaps of parks and nature reserves right on your doorstep too – the Canberra Nature Park system offers brilliant walking trails, picnic spots, and chances to spot local wildlife. It's the kind of place where you can easily balance structured activities with outdoor time, and everything's pretty well signposted and easy to navigate with little ones in tow.

For accommodation, you've got solid options if you're after the caravan park experience. Capital Country Holiday Park, Alivio Tourist Park Canberra, Discovery Parks - Canberra, and EPIC Caravan Park all offer family-friendly facilities with playgrounds, pools, and the space kids need to burn off energy. The real magic of Canberra as a family holiday spot is how green and spacious it feels – you get that relaxed outdoor lifestyle without the crowds you'd hit on the coast. Whether you're keen on museums, nature walks, or just letting the kids run around in proper parks, Canberra delivers good value and enough variety to keep everyone happy.
