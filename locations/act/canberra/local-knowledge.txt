G'day! If you're after a family holiday that mixes outdoor adventure with fascinating bits of Aussie history, the parks around Canberra absolutely deliver. The best time to visit is autumn (March to May) when the weather's brilliant for hiking and the tourist crowds have thinned out, plus you'll catch the stunning foliage if you time it right. Here's a local tip that'll save you heaps: grab your free entry passes to national attractions like Questacon and the National Zoo before you leave home through the Book Canberra website – the kids will love these spots, and you'll have more budget left for extending your park stay or treating everyone to a pub meal in one of the charming nearby towns.
