PARK: EPIC Caravan Park
SCORE: 52/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 35

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Only a basic playground mentioned; no swimming pool, jumping pillow, waterpark or organised kids activities. One family noted staff gave kids a memento for their journals, but otherwise minimal on-site entertainment for children.

Nature (10/20) — Exhibition grounds setting with some trees and grassy sites mentioned positively by several reviewers. One review noted "landscape is a bit scarce" and another mentioned "trees planted in awkward spots." Not a natural bush or waterfront environment, but better than concrete.

Cleanliness (9/15) — Mixed feedback with recent reviews showing polarised opinions. Multiple positive mentions of "clean and tidy," "amenities clean," but one scathing 2025 review described "amenities are filthy you do not need soap you could scrape it of the walls." Generally adequate but inconsistent.

Value (13/15) — Strong consensus on good value at $40/night powered sites. Multiple reviews specifically praised "great value," "very happy with the price," and "fantastic value and location." Competitive pricing for Canberra access.

Site Size (8/10) — Several positive mentions including "sites are huge," "spacious," and "lots of powered sites." Some issues noted with muddy puddles and leveling problems, but generally good space for families.

Sentiment (6/10) — Mixed overall with friendly staff praised but significant operational issues noted (hard to find office, poor signage, disorganised check-in during events). Some repeat visitors but also strong negative experiences from experienced travelers.

Location (8/10) — Excellent tram access to Canberra CBD consistently praised. "Close to the city with the Tram nearly at the door step" and "short drive or light train ride" mentioned frequently. Farmers markets on site a bonus.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severely limited on-site entertainment for children with only a basic playground and no pool, waterpark, or organized activities, making it unsuitable for families wanting on-site holiday activities.
2. Dated amenities requiring major renovation as noted across multiple reviews spanning 2023-2025, with one recent review describing facilities as "filthy" with poor maintenance standards.
3. Significant operational and management issues including confusing signage, difficulty locating office during events, poor after-hours access, distorted power sockets requiring tape, and booking errors, suggesting inconsistent management standards.
