G'day fellow family adventurers! Alice Springs and the surrounding Red Centre offer something truly magical – where else can your kids wake up to crimson desert sunrises, spot wild camels on morning walks, and learn about the world's oldest living culture all in one holiday? The cooler months from April to September are absolutely perfect for exploring, when daytime temperatures sit in the low to mid-20s and those famous starry nights won't freeze your toes off. Here's an insider tip: grab fresh date ice cream and meet the resident peacocks at the Alice Springs Desert Park before 11am – you'll beat the tour buses and catch the free-flight bird show when the raptors are most active in the morning thermals.
