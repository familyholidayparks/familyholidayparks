# Alice Springs Family Holiday

Alice Springs sits right in the red heart of Australia and makes a ripper of a family destination if you're after something different from the usual beach holiday. While you won't find ocean swimming here, the region more than makes up for it with stunning natural attractions like the MacDonnell Ranges, the Todd River, and some of the most impressive outback scenery you'll lay eyes on. The town's got solid family-friendly attractions including the Alice Springs Desert Park, the School of the Air visitor centre, and the Railway Museum, so there's plenty to keep kids entertained between exploring the rugged landscape and spotting native wildlife.

For families planning a stay, there's a good range of caravan parks and family accommodation that cater well to travellers with kids. Discovery Parks, Alice Springs Tourist Park, Wintersun Cabin and Caravan Park, and Heritage Caravan Park all offer pet-friendly options and family facilities that take the stress out of managing young ones on the road. The town itself is compact and easy to navigate, the locals are friendly, and there's something genuinely special about watching your kids experience the outback up close. It's the kind of holiday where children actually get tired from adventure rather than screen time, and parents get to tick off some real Australian experiences.
