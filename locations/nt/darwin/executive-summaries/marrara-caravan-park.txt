PARK: Marrara Caravan Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 50

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews consistently note there is no playground, no kids club, and no dedicated children's entertainment facilities. The pool with kiddie section is the only child-focused amenity mentioned, with one family noting "Very safe for the kids with a great pool and a kiddie pool too." Multiple reviews confirm "no shared area/camp kitchen" and describe this as an accommodation stopover rather than an activity destination.

Nature (12/20) — The park has "lots of trees, many birds around" and "big open grassy areas" that children enjoyed, with one review noting "the kids enjoyed some lush grass for a change during the dry." However, it's located in suburban Darwin near the airport with "road noise" and "odd aeroplane" disturbances. Reviews describe it as "not really aesthetically pleasing" with limited natural surroundings beyond maintained grass and some shade trees.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Recent reviews consistently praise cleanliness with phrases like "spotlessly clean," "very clean," "lovely and clean," and "beautifully maintained." However, some ensuite issues emerged in most recent reviews: "lacked shelving and had no toilet paper or hand soap" and "they don't supply any toilet paper." Older facilities are noted as "dated but functioned just fine" with maintenance standards generally high.

Value (8/15) — Significant concerns raised in recent reviews about pricing practices, with guests reporting "bill increased by over $500" after booking and "Prices are at a premium today compared to last year." Multiple reviews describe it as "reasonably priced compared to others" but several note the park is "old" and "needs updating esp for the price." The $100 security deposit for powered sites drew criticism.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews mention "Very Large caravan bays" and "bigger sites" with "good size sites," though some note sites are "a bit crammed in" and "a little small and awkward" for modern large vans. Most sites have concrete slabs with grass surrounds and private ensuites, providing functional space rather than expansive natural sites.

Sentiment (6/10) — Mixed sentiment dominates recent reviews, with multiple 1-star reviews citing "Worst customer service," "Arrogant jerk," and "dodgy management." However, positive reviews show strong repeat visitation with guests extending stays from days to weeks. Several recent (2025-2026) reviews are 5-star, but trust and service issues significantly damage overall sentiment for families.

Location (5/10) — Located 15 minutes from Darwin city center with "Coles to the left Woollies to the right" and "bus stop out the front," providing urban convenience. However, it's "Close to airport and very noisy" with plane noise noted, though some found this "minimal." The location serves as a practical Darwin base rather than a destination with natural attractions nearby.

WHY NOT 100:
[1] Complete absence of children's entertainment facilities, playground equipment, or organized activities means families with young children have only a pool for entertainment. [2] Multiple serious customer service complaints in recent reviews, including rude management interactions, after-hours access problems, and unilateral price increases after bookings were confirmed and deposits paid. [3] The park infrastructure is acknowledged as old and dated throughout reviews, with rough internal roads, aging ensuites lacking basic supplies, and an urban setting near airport noise rather than natural family holiday surroundings.
