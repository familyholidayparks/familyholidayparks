PARK: Ardrossan Caravan Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 166

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Reviews mention a playground, pickle ball, giant connect games, and occasional live music during school holidays with free ice blocks for kids. While families appreciated these touches, there's no waterpark, jumping pillow, or major entertainment facilities. Multiple reviews note the camp kitchen is "currently being built" or "in process," indicating limited communal entertainment spaces.

Nature (16/20) — Strong coastal location with beach and jetty within a 10-minute walk, excellent crabbing opportunities, and access to the Walk the Yorke coastal trail. Families praised the "quiet, sleepy country coastal town" atmosphere and ocean views. The park sits near creek areas and offers good access to explore other Yorke Peninsula beaches within 1-2 hours' drive.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelmingly positive feedback on cleanliness with phrases like "spotless amenities," "very clean and well maintained," "immaculate" showers, and "everything was clean, organised, and welcoming." Two negative reviews mentioned bathrooms not cleaned during stay or "filthy" toilets, preventing a perfect score.

Value (12/15) — Generally described as "affordable" and "great value" with one mention of $30/night for unpowered site. Some guests felt pricing was reasonable for the location and cleanliness. However, one review complained about $140/night for a substandard cabin, and the 3-night minimum booking policy frustrated working families wanting weekend trips.

Site Size (8/10) — Powered sites described as "good sized," "huge," "spacious," with "generous green matting" and "ample space to park your tow vehicle." Drive-through sites available. Some complaints about cramped positioning "in amongst the permanents," posts making parking "annoying," and sites having side slopes requiring chocks.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation indicated with many "will be back" comments and "second stay" mentions. Management (Sonya and Shane) repeatedly praised for being helpful and friendly. A few negative reviews cited noise from semi-permanent residents, one alleged racial incident with management, and complaints about specific cabin conditions, preventing a higher sentiment score.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location praised consistently: 10-minute walk to jetty, beach, town centre, Drakes supermarket, cafes, and pub. Easy access from main road. Ideal base for exploring Yorke Peninsula. Minor complaints about some sites backing onto busy road with traffic noise and wind exposure in coastal location.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited on-site entertainment infrastructure — no waterpark, jumping pillow, or significant kids' activities beyond a basic playground and games, with the camp kitchen still under construction during most review period.
2. Inconsistent cabin quality — several reviews cited uncomfortable beds, poor TV placement, inadequate cabin amenities, and one extremely negative review about a "sad tired cabin" with maintenance issues.
3. Noise and site placement issues — complaints about noisy semi-permanent residents, dogs barking through the night, cramped positioning among permanent vans, and some sites placed near busy roads.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
