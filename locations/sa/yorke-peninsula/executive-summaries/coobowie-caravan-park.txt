PARK: Coobowie Caravan Park
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-08
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 73

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (13/20) — Reviews highlight the creative 'Howie the lost pelican' daily treasure hunt that kids love, plus tennis and bocce equipment available for borrowing. Playground exists but multiple reviews note it "could use a revamp." Families mention bike riding and exploring as main activities. Camp kitchen and BBQ areas support family gatherings. Limited structured entertainment beyond outdoor self-directed activities.

Nature (17/20) — Excellent beach access directly across the road with pelicans and swans visible from sites. Ocean views from grassy sites. Close proximity to Edithburgh tidal pool for swimming, multiple jetties for fishing, and described as "peaceful, right on the shores." Short drive to Stansbury, Edithburgh, and Innes National Park. Coastal setting with sea breezes frequently mentioned.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelming consistency in reviews describing facilities as "spotlessly clean," "impeccably clean," "super clean," and "cleanliness is 10/10." Amenities blocks, camp kitchen, cabins, and School House all praised for cleanliness. Recent management (Michelle and Mark) continues high standards established by previous managers. Minor deduction for isolated complaint about billing dispute.

Value (14/15) — Repeatedly described as "great value," "very reasonably priced," "well priced," and "price wise fantastic." Winter specials mentioned (pay 2 stay 3). Described as "a Great relieve from other parks" regarding pricing. Multiple families extended stays beyond original bookings. Pet-friendly cabins available. Good value acknowledged even during peak summer season.

Site Size (9/10) — Sites consistently described as "roomy," "great sized sites," "spacious," "large and green," and "heaps of room." Grassed pull-through sites available. Big powered sites noted. One review mentioned lack of grey water collection point preventing full 5-star rating. School House has multiple bunk rooms suitable for large family groups.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very high repeat visitation with families returning 3-4 times. Strong recommendations throughout recent reviews. Significant improvement noted with new managers Michelle and Mark after negative experience with previous managers. Two 1-star reviews (2024) cite poor customer service incidents. Otherwise overwhelmingly positive with guests extending stays and calling it "home away from home."

Location (9/10) — Beachfront location praised, walking distance to pub. Central to Yorke Peninsula attractions including Edithburgh, Stansbury, Yorktown, Wool Bay. Quick bike ride to Edithburgh. Close to Innes National Park. Described as "less-crowded vibe" alternative. Food van visits on Fridays. Coobowie pub within walking distance (Tuesday-Sunday dining). Some remoteness from major centres.

WHY NOT 100:
The playground requires updating as specifically mentioned by families, and on-site kids entertainment is limited to the pelican treasure hunt and borrowed outdoor equipment rather than structured programs or waterpark facilities. Management transition issues were evident with two serious customer service complaints in late 2024 under previous managers, though recent reviews show marked improvement under current management. The park's remote Yorke Peninsula location means limited nearby attractions require driving to access, and some basic infrastructure gaps exist (grey water points, air conditioning in some cabin areas).
