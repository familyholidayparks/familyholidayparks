PARK: Corny Point Caravan Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-10
REWS ANALYSED: 45

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (11/20) — Limited onsite entertainment with a basic playground, games room offering table tennis/darts/foosball, and bike pump track across the road; reviewers noted the park is "fairly simple, without many attractions for kids" though families appreciated what was available for younger children.

Nature (16/20) — Strong natural environment with proximity to beautiful beaches ("stunning beaches"), Dhilba Guuranda Innes National Park nearby with "great coastal scenery & lots of emus," peaceful location, and the park itself is "tree-lined" with exceptional grass; perfect for families who enjoy exploring natural surroundings.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising clean facilities, but consistent concerns emerged: "amenities are OK, a bit tired and old but need to be cleaned more regularly, especially when there are large groups," one review mentioned "plenty of birds poo" in kitchen, and facilities described as "a little out dated but mostly clean and tidy."

Value (13/15) — Generally positive value perception with mentions of "great prices," "very affordable," "great rate," and "good rates"; families felt pricing was reasonable for what's offered, though bore water and limited facilities prevented top marks.

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently excellent feedback on "large and grassy" sites, "best grass sites we have seen in a long time," "generous unpowered site," "well spaced," and "thickest grass I've ever seen in a park"; only minor criticism about some sites being "tight to get into."

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive with many repeat visitors and recommendations, though tempered by service issues (one "rude" cancellation interaction), the extreme negative cabin couch review, and several families noting limited facilities; many would return but acknowledged it's not a full-featured park.

Location (8/10) — Good location "just a short drive from the beach," walking distance to pub, well-positioned for exploring southern Yorke Peninsula, but area described as quiet with "ghost towns" in off-season and "not really much there"; convenience store nearby helpful.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Very limited onsite kids entertainment and activities beyond basic playground and games room, with multiple reviews specifically noting lack of attractions for children compared to more fully-equipped holiday parks.
2. Cleanliness and maintenance issues with aging amenities that require more regular cleaning, particularly when school groups present, plus specific hygiene concerns in camp kitchen area.
3. Bore water only (salty, not drinkable) and limited surrounding amenities in the quiet township, with some negative service experiences around cancellation policies impacting overall family experience.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"some of the best grass sites we have seen in a long time", "fairly simple, without many attractions for kids", "playground onsite and a bike pump track right across the road", "amenities are OK, a bit tired and old", "only has bore water, no good for drinking", "great camp kitchen facilities", "very friendly and helpful staff", "peaceful and relaxing caravan park"
