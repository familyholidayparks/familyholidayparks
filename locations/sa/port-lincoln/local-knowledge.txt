G'day! Port Lincoln sits right on Boston Bay – one of the world's largest natural harbours – and it's an absolute ripper for families who love the ocean without the tourist crowds you'll find in more famous spots. We reckon April to October is your sweet spot for visiting, when the weather's mild and you can watch the southern right whales cruising past with their calves (the kids will never forget it). Here's a local tip that'll save you a packet: grab your fresh seafood straight from the boats at the marina around 3pm when they come in, rather than paying restaurant prices – the tuna's so fresh it practically jumps into your esky, and there's nothing quite like cooking up your catch at your holiday park that evening.
