# Adelaide: Perfect for Family Holidays

Adelaide is a laid-back South Australian city that ticks all the boxes for families wanting sun, sand and space to breathe. The beaches are the real drawcard – West Beach, Brighton Beach and the Adelaide Beachfront offer safe swimming, playgrounds and rock pools that kids love exploring. Beyond the sand, you've got the Adelaide Zoo, Cleland Wildlife Park where you can hand-feed kangaroos, and the Botanic Gardens for a peaceful afternoon walk. The city's got that relaxed vibe where you're never far from nature, whether that's the nearby Adelaide Hills or a quick drive to the beaches.

When it comes to where to stay, Adelaide's got brilliant holiday park options that cater specifically to families. Discovery Parks runs several parks right near the action – their Adelaide Beachfront, West Beach and Hahndorf locations all offer everything you need from cabins and caravan sites to playgrounds and camp kitchens. There's also Brighton Beachfront Holiday Park and Marion Holiday Park if you want something closer to the city or with a different feel. The parks are well-maintained, family-friendly, and give you the freedom to come and go as you please without the hefty hotel bills. It's honest, practical accommodation that lets families actually relax and enjoy their time away.
