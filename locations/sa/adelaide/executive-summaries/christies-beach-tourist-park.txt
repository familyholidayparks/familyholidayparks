PARK: Christies Beach Tourist Park
SCORE: 68/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-17
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 125

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Multiple reviews specifically state "needs something for the kids" and describe a "very small play area" that needs fencing. Pedal carts were mentioned positively by one reviewer, but the overwhelming consensus is minimal on-site entertainment. No pool, water park, or organized kids activities mentioned. One review noted "limited facilities for the younger ones."

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional beach location with reviews consistently praising "right across the road from the beach," "beautiful Christies Beach," "pristine water and amazing sunsets," and "beautiful long walks along the esplanade." Direct beach access through secure gates. The beach environment is the park's strongest feature with families loving the coastal walks and swimming beach.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising "amenities were always clean," "spotless" facilities, and daily cleaning. However, concerning negative reviews mention amenities "STINKING of urine the whole time," paint peeling in showers, facilities being "old but clean," and weeds growing on sites. Overall maintained but showing age.

Value (11/15) — Reviews describe prices ranging from $30-70 per night with mixed value perceptions. Some call it "cheap for the area" and "reasonable rates," while others complain it's "pricey," "VERY expensive for a basic park at $70 per night," and "price is steep for amenities offered." Value perception varies significantly.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive with "big sites," "large flat sites," "huge sites," and "no issues with 22ft Van" mentioned frequently. However, some reviews note "sites are extremely small and PACKED" and "some caravan sites are a little tight." The beachfront sites appear larger than interior sites.

Sentiment (7/10) — Positive repeat visitors and recommendations, but significantly impacted by safety concerns. Multiple reviews mention "strange people on the property," residents "clearly on drugs," theft from communal fridges, feeling "unsafe," and disturbances at night. Many loved the location but several wouldn't return due to atmosphere concerns.

Location (9/10) — Outstanding location "2 minutes walk from the beach," "10 minute walk to shops, cafes, supermarkets," and "30-40 mins to most attractions." Only drawback is "bit far away from CBD area" (30km south of Adelaide) and significant road traffic noise mentioned in several reviews.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Severely limited kids entertainment facilities with multiple families specifically noting the lack of activities for children and a very small, unfenced playground that doesn't meet family holiday park standards for younger children.

2. Significant safety and atmosphere concerns raised across multiple recent reviews mentioning problematic long-term residents, substance abuse visible on property, theft from communal facilities, nighttime disturbances, and families feeling unsafe enough to leave early or stay awake to guard belongings.

3. Cleanliness inconsistency with several reviews describing persistent urine odors in amenities blocks, aging facilities needing renovation, poor grounds maintenance with weeds and dead grass, and broken fixtures, despite daily cleaning efforts.
