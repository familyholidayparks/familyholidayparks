PARK: Moana Beach Tourist Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-17
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 144

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews consistently highlight major gaps in kids' entertainment infrastructure, with families noting "no games room, no pool and no playground, just a tennis court," "absolutely nothing for the kids here," and "lack of kid play equipment." Multiple families with young children specifically mentioned struggling to keep toddlers entertained, with one family stating they "found it hard to keep our 2½ year old entertainment." The only entertainment mentioned is go-karts for hire and the tennis court.

Nature (18/20) — The park excels in natural environment with exceptional beach access being the standout feature. Reviews consistently praise it as "one of the nicest beaches I've visited in the Adelaide region," "50m from the water," "best beach in Metro SA," and "one of the best beaches in Adelaide." Families appreciate the "coded pedestrian gate takes you right to the beach" and "beautiful, clean, flat beach." The coastal location provides swimming, snorkeling, beach walking, fishing, and sunset viewing opportunities.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Cleanliness reviews are mixed with significant concerns raised. While some found "very clean amenities" and "spotless" facilities, multiple recent reviews report serious issues: "cabin was very poky, shabby and dirty," "facilities are not well maintained and cleaned enough," "shower cubicle was dirty with sand and bugs," "cockroach in the bed," "mould in shower," and "flea bites during my stay." The toilet block is described as needing upgrades with "missing tiles in some cubicles" and inconsistent cleaning standards.

Value (12/15) — Most families found reasonable value with mentions of "very affordable," "great value for money," "well priced for location and quality," and "good price." However, some felt pricing was high for what's provided, with one reviewer stating "prices are outrageous for what is provided" at $70 per night for powered sites in peak season, and another noting $53 for one adult seemed excessive given the facility conditions.

Site Size (8/10) — Site size receives generally positive feedback with descriptions of "excellent and spacious" caravan sites, "large site with water and drain," "good size sites," "huge drive thru, grassed sites," and "sites were large and spaced out." However, one detailed review noted "sites are very close together, which isn't ideal when it is busy" and most sites lack shade, making it "really unpleasant to be outside at your site on hot days."

Sentiment (7/10) — Overall sentiment is moderate with mixed experiences evident. Positive reviews mention repeat visits and recommendations, but several families explicitly stated they won't return due to lack of kids' facilities, cleanliness issues, noise problems, or customer service concerns. Multiple reviews mention "wouldn't stay here again" or "won't be back," particularly from families with young children. Road noise from "hoons doing burnouts" is a recurring complaint affecting family experience.

Location (9/10) — Location scores highly with "perfectly located" beach access, "walking distance to beach and cafe," "close to shopping centre," "4 min by vehicle to shopping centre and pub," and "10-15min drive to main wineries" in McLaren Vale. The proximity to Adelaide (not too far from city) and local amenities like Deep Blue Cafe and surf club is frequently praised. However, the beachside road location brings significant traffic noise issues.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Critical lack of on-site kids' entertainment infrastructure with no playground, no pool, no games room, making it unsuitable for families with young children who need structured play opportunities beyond beach activities.
2. Significant and recurring cleanliness concerns including mould, cockroaches, flea bites, dirty cabins, poorly maintained shower blocks with missing tiles, and inconsistent cleaning standards that fall below acceptable accommodation standards.
3. Persistent noise issues from road traffic and "hoons doing burnouts" at all hours affecting family sleep and relaxation, with multiple families citing this as a reason they would not return to the park.
