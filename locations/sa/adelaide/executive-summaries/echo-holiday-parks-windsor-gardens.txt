PARK: Echo Holiday Parks - Windsor Gardens
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-10
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 114

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Reviews consistently mention "no place for kids to play on the camp grounds" and "no swimming pool." The park is primarily accommodation-focused with no dedicated kids' entertainment, games room activities, or water facilities for families with young children.

Nature (18/20) — Strong nature offering with "beautiful location on Linear Park and the beautiful river red gums," "koala's were in the trees right behind the van," "beautiful parklands just behind the caravan park," and "many kms of walking/biking tracks." Direct access to Torrens River trails creates an excellent natural environment.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Mixed feedback: many reviews praise "very clean amenities," "spotless amenities," and clean cabins. However, some note "bathrooms could use a little updating," "stained carpets," "carpet was wet in main room floor was dirty walls were dirty," and shower heads needing replacement, indicating inconsistent maintenance standards.

Value (13/15) — Strong value proposition with "reasonably priced for family of 4," "prices better then anywhere else," "good value for money," "excellent value for money," and "price for a powered site is very favourable." Budget rooms particularly noted as economical.

Site Size (6/10) — Significant issues noted: "sites at the front of the Park ones closest to the road are crammed in," "not enough SPACE for you to maneuver," "site was a lot tighter to get on," "carpark is very small and tight," and concerns about fitting larger vans. Back sites appear more spacious.

Sentiment (7/10) — Mixed sentiment with strong positive reviews balanced by serious complaints about management communication, site changes without notice, staff hostility in some instances, permanent resident issues, and thin walls in budget rooms. Many repeat visitors but also "unfortunately not returning" from previous regulars.

Location (10/10) — Excellent location scores: "close to everything," "great central location," "easy access to city bus," "15 minutes from Adelaide oval," "short walk to the klemzig bus interchange," "close to the city of Adelaide," and convenient to shops, hospitals, and attractions.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of family entertainment infrastructure—no playground, no pool, no kids' club, no games room, and no organized activities, making it unsuitable for families seeking on-site entertainment for young children.
2. Significant site management and space issues including cramped front sites, poor communication about site changes, bookings moved without notice, and difficulty maneuvering larger rigs, creating stress for families with caravans.
3. Inconsistent cleanliness and maintenance standards with reports of stained carpets, dirty walls, wet floors, outdated bathrooms, faulty shower heads, and thin walls in budget rooms, alongside service issues including unhelpful permanent residents and occasional staff hostility.
