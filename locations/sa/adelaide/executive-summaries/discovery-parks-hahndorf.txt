PARK: Discovery Parks - Hahndorf
SCORE: 87/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 368

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Reviews consistently highlight excellent kids' facilities including mini golf described as "well maintained," a popular jumping pillow with "massive shade," pump track/bike circuit, games room with activities, and a heated swimming pool. Multiple families mentioned "plenty to do for the kids" and "heaps of activities." A few noted games room machines requiring coins and some equipment occasionally out of order.

Nature (18/20) — Set in the beautiful Adelaide Hills with "stunning views," "gorgeous" lake views with ducks, and "beautiful big trees." Families loved the "red leaves in Autumn," wildlife including friendly rabbits hopping around cabins, and "peaceful" natural surroundings. The "lush green location" and "rolling green views" create a genuine hills getaway atmosphere. Location provides easy access to wineries and nature attractions.

Cleanliness (15/15) — Exceptional cleanliness receives overwhelming praise: "spotless," "immaculate," "pristine," "absolutely immaculate." Amenities blocks described as "extremely clean & modern," camp kitchen "spotless clean," and staff "constantly getting around the park cleaning." Multiple reviews noted it's "the most well maintained and clean park we have stayed in" and "best maintained park across Australia."

Value (11/15) — Mixed feedback on pricing with many saying "worth every dollar" and "you get what you pay for," but multiple reviews mentioned "a little bit expensive," "slightly pricy," "prices are high," and one strongly negative review about "price gouging" and "dynamic pricing." Most families felt the quality justified the cost, but value perception varies.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive with "spacious" sites, "good size sites," "level sites," and "roomy" drive-through sites praised. However, multiple reviews noted some sites are "tight," "packed in like sardines" (sites 19-28 specifically), "sites too close together," and regular sites "seem quite small" compared to drive-through options. The hilly terrain also affects site levelling.

Sentiment (10/10) — Outstanding guest satisfaction with phrases like "will definitely be back," "we will be a return customer," "cannot fault this place," and "highly recommend" appearing throughout. Many described it as "the best" park they've stayed in, with families specifically extending stays and returning multiple times. Overwhelmingly positive word-of-mouth and recommendation intent.

Location (8/10) — Excellent location "walking distance to Hahndorf" (15-20 minute walk), with the famous farmers market "directly across the road," and "perfect" access to Adelaide Hills attractions, wineries, and Adelaide CBD (30-40 minutes). Some noted it's "a bit of a drive to main shops" and "bit out of town," but most found the location ideal for exploring the region.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site size and privacy issues — Multiple reviews specifically mentioned sites 19-28 being "packed in like sardines with no privacy whatsoever," and regular powered sites being "quite small" compared to premium drive-through options, with some allowing neighbors to see directly into caravans.

2. Value concerns and dynamic pricing — Several families found pricing "a little bit expensive" with one detailed complaint about "price gouging" and "dynamic pricing" that "charge obscene pricing," making it less accessible for "everyday families," despite the premium quality.

3. Hilly terrain challenges — The park's hillside setting, while providing beautiful views, creates accessibility issues with "steep entry and exit," "need to walk up the fairly steep hill to amenities," making it "not suitable for kids on bikes or people with mobility issues" and requiring levelling blocks for many sites.
