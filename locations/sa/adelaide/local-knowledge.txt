G'day! Adelaide's holiday parks are an absolute ripper for families, especially when you've got little ones who love wildlife – the region's packed with kangaroos, koalas, and beaches calm enough for confident first-time swimmers. We always plan our Adelaide trips for autumn (March to May) when the weather's beautiful, the school holiday crowds have thinned out, and you'll actually get a powered site without booking six months ahead. Here's a local secret: grab your free city connector bus passes and park at the holiday parks in Glenelg or Brighton – you can zip into the city for museum days without the nightmare of CBD parking, and the kids can snooze on the ride back after a big day out.
