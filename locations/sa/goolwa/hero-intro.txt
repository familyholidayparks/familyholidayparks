# Goolwa, South Australia: A Family Holiday Destination

Goolwa is a relaxed coastal town on the Fleurieu Peninsula that's perfect for families looking to escape without too much fuss. The long sandy beaches are sheltered and great for swimming, paddling, and building sandcastles, while the calm waters of the Murray River Mouth offer a change of scenery for a lazy paddle or short boat trip. You'll find solid amenities spread throughout town – cafes, fish and chips shops, and playgrounds – so you can keep the kids happy without having to plan too far ahead.

The natural attractions around Goolwa do the heavy lifting when it comes to keeping children entertained. The nearby Coorong offers excellent birdwatching and walking trails through different landscapes, while Discovery Parks - Goolwa provides a comfortable base with good facilities for families staying overnight. The town also has easy access to the Cockle Train, a heritage railway that kids usually enjoy, and plenty of quieter spots for picnics by the water. It's the kind of place where you can do as much or as little as you like – ideal if your family prefers a low-key break without the crowds of busier seaside towns.
