G'day! If you're after a proper family holiday where the kids can run wild and you can actually relax, the holiday parks around Goolwa are absolutely brilliant. This stunning corner of South Australia sits right where the mighty Murray River meets the Southern Ocean, giving you everything from calm river beaches perfect for littlies to proper surf breaks, plus those famous Coorong wetlands teeming with birdlife. We love visiting during autumn (March to May) when the weather's still gorgeous but the school holiday crowds have thinned out, and here's a local tip: grab fish and chips from Motherduck on the wharf, then let the kids feed the leftovers to the pelicans at the old Signal Point boat ramp – it's become our family tradition every single trip.
