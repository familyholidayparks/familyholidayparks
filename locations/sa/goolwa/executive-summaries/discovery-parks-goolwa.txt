PARK: Discovery Parks - Goolwa
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-21
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 190

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (18/20) — Reviews consistently praise the "top tier water park," two separate pools (heated), splash park with slides and water bucket, two jumping pillows, two playgrounds, bike/scooter track, basketball court, skate park/pump track, kids club activities, movie nights, pancake breakfasts, and visiting food trucks. Families note kids are entertained "all day long" and "so much for the kids to do."

Nature (14/20) — Park is located on the edge of Goolwa township, approximately 1km from town and requires driving to beach access. Reviews mention the "beautiful green park" with "wonderful lawns and pathways" and mature trees in older section, but note it's "not within walking distance of the beach" and "miles from beach." Nearby attractions include River Murray, Southern Ocean, Coorong, and seals at the Barrage within 20-30 minute drive to Victor Harbour.

Cleanliness (12/15) — Most reviews praise "spotless" facilities and amenities "cleaned daily to the highest level" with "fresh clean smell." However, multiple recent reviews (Easter 2026, January 2025) report significant cleanliness failures during peak periods: "amenities were filthy, only cleaned once per day," "actual poo on the floor on 2 occasions," "faeces in shower" and "dirt mud hair filth" in brand new toilet blocks. Staff responsiveness to cleaning complaints was inadequate.

Value (12/15) — Reviews note the park is "costly" with "camping isnt cheap any more" and sites "going up to $80." Some guests felt it was "very expensive for what we got" and complained about dynamic pricing. However, many families found it "well worth it" and "great value for money" given the extensive facilities, with good winter specials available.

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised for "large sites," "spacious," "HUGE spacious site," "large allotments," and "good size that allow for a bit of personal space." Newer sites feature concrete slabs, ensuite options, and some deluxe sites with individual fire pits. A few mentions of sites being "slightly cramped" during peak capacity.

Sentiment (9/10) — Strong repeat visitation with guests returning for "4 years," "over 10 times in the last 12 months," and many stating "will definitely be back" and "one of our favourites." Described as "probably the best caravan park" and "best we have stayed in." However, multiple serious complaints about staff rudeness (gate interactions, reception), lack of noise enforcement (Easter group disturbance), and cleanliness failures during peak times detract from perfect sentiment.

Location (8/10) — Good access to Goolwa main street (walkable 1km), Woolworths, shops, bakeries, and petrol station. Described as "handy location" and "good base to explore surrounding coastline, McLaren Vale" with easy 20-30 minute drive to Victor Harbour. However, not beachfront and "need to drive to places of interest" with beach access requiring 4WD for actual beach driving.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Critical cleanliness failures during peak periods (Easter 2026, January 2025) with faeces left uncleaned despite guest complaints, inadequate cleaning frequency when park at capacity, and no follow-up action—indicating insufficient staffing or protocols for high-volume periods.
2. Multiple incidents of poor staff behaviour including rude gate interactions ("rudest tone," "spoke to me like I'm the idiot"), miserable reception staff, and complete failure to enforce quiet hours when large group caused 12:30am disturbances over multiple nights—only sending "one generic text" with no in-person follow-up.
3. Location limitations for beach-loving families as park is inland requiring driving to beach access, not within walking distance of ocean, and reviewers note it's "miles from beach" compared to beachfront alternatives—limiting spontaneous beach visits particularly for families with young children.
