G'day fellow travel families! If you're after a proper Aussie adventure that'll have your kids talking for years, the holiday parks near Kangaroo Island deliver something truly wild – think seal pups playing at your feet, echidnas waddling past your cabin, and beaches where you're more likely to spot sea lions than other tourists. Winter months from June to September are brilliant for whale watching and wildlife encounters (plus the accommodation prices drop beautifully), though summer brings calmer seas for the ferry crossing from Cape Jervis. Here's a tip from someone who's learned the hard way: always book your SeaLink ferry tickets well ahead of locking in your park accommodation, especially during school holidays, because there's nothing worse than having a campsite booked and no way to get your crew across the water!
