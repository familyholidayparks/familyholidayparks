PARK: Discovery Parks - Kangaroo Island
SCORE: 82/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 125

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (12/20) — Limited organized entertainment with some special events like koala walks, pancake breakfasts, treasure hunts for kids, and a playground/communal area with activities and TV mentioned; no pool or jumping pillow; one reviewer suggested adding a jumping pillow as improvement; wildlife viewing is the primary activity.

Nature (20/20) — Exceptional natural environment with koalas, kangaroos, wallabies, Cape Barren geese, echidnas, and diverse birdlife throughout the park grounds; immediate access to Flinders Chase National Park, Remarkable Rocks, Admirals Arch; families consistently describe wildlife encounters as "magical," "amazing," and a highlight; natural bushland setting with star-filled skies.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelming praise for cleanliness with cabins and facilities described as "spotlessly clean," "very clean," "exceptionally clean"; amenities "always clean and tidy"; camp kitchen "very clean"; one negative review about "filthy facilities" and another about spoiled food in shared fridge and damaged utensils detract slightly from otherwise excellent standards.

Value (13/15) — Generally good value with families noting "great value for the location," "excellent value approx £100 for one night," and "fair prices"; some concerns about laundry costs ($9 wash, $9 dry) and limited shop stock at higher prices; overall positive sentiment about pricing given the remote location and quality of accommodation.

Site Size (9/10) — Sites consistently described as "spacious," "large and roomy," "generous," "plenty of room to spread out"; cabins "well spaced so not too close to neighbours"; "massive area so you don't have to worry about neighbours making noise"; good spacing between sites noted by multiple families.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with families expressing they "absolutely loved it," "will definitely be back," "cannot wait to come back," and "would live there if I could"; strong repeat visitation intent; staff (Margaret and Chris) receive exceptional praise; two very negative reviews about customer service and soundproofing issues prevent a perfect score.

Location (5/10) — Remote location on far western side of Kangaroo Island is ideal for Flinders Chase but over an hour from ferry terminal and main town; "far from main town so no option to dine out"; perfect positioning for national park access but requires significant travel time; isolation noted as both positive (peaceful, nature) and negative (limited amenities nearby).

WHY NOT 100:
[1] Limited organized kids entertainment and facilities - no pool, no jumping pillow, minimal structured activities beyond occasional special events like koala walks, which doesn't meet the needs of families wanting more active entertainment options. [2] Remote location requires extensive driving (1+ hours from ferry) with no nearby restaurants or services, making it less convenient for families who prefer easier access to amenities and dining options. [3] Some maintenance issues noted including soundproofing problems in motel-style rooms, damaged kitchen utensils, unclear kitchen instructions, poor water pressure, and unstable WiFi connectivity in cabins.
