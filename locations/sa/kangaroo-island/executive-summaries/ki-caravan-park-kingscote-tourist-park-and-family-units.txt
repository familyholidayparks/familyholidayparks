PARK: KI Caravan Park ~ Kingscote Tourist Park and Family Units
SCORE: 71/100
DATE SCORED: 2025-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 152

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Limited on-site kids entertainment with only a basic playground mentioned; families rely on island attractions and beach activities rather than park-based entertainment facilities.
Nature (18/20) — Outstanding natural environment with beach 1-minute walk away, koalas regularly spotted in park trees, wallabies, abundant birdlife, peaceful bushland setting, and breathtaking sunrises; several reviews highlight wildlife encounters as a major drawcard.
Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently described as "spotlessly clean", "very clean and well maintained" with amenities repeatedly praised; new owners clearly prioritise cleanliness with facilities renovated and maintained to high standard.
Value (13/15) — Generally described as "great value", "reasonably priced", and "affordable accommodation for Kangaroo Island"; families appreciate the pricing relative to island alternatives though some note cabins are compact for the cost.
Site Size (9/10) — Sites consistently described as "spacious", "roomy", "large enough", "huge" with plenty of room for caravans and vehicles; flat, grassy sites with good shade from established trees.
Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with many repeat visitors, strong recommendation rate, and guests extending stays; new owners Ros and Steve receive exceptional praise for warmth and hospitality though a few negative reviews from previous management period remain.
Location (10/10) — Perfect central base for exploring Kangaroo Island, 5 minutes from Kingscote town centre, beach across the road, walking/cycling path to town, close to supermarkets, cafes, and all island attractions easily accessible.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Very limited on-site entertainment for children with only a basic playground; families must seek entertainment off-site via island attractions and beach activities.
2. Cabins are dated, cramped for families of four, with one 2-star review citing 6sqm living space and inadequate cooking facilities forcing early departure.
3. Three 1-star reviews cite poor accommodation quality under previous management (pre-2024) including worn mattresses, maintenance issues, and rude service, though recent reviews show dramatic improvement.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"Incredible camp kitchen - in the top 3", "spotlessly clean amenities block", "beach just a 1-minute walk away", "Koalas in the trees", "Ros and Steve are fantastic hosts", "peaceful park full of wildlife", "little playground", "Cabins very dated and cramped"
