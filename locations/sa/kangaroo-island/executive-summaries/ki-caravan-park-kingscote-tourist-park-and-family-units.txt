PARK: KI Caravan Park ~ Kingscote Tourist Park and Family Units
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 152

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Reviews mention a "little playground" that is "basic" with kids enjoying it despite limitations. The main entertainment comes from wildlife spotting (koalas, wallabies, birds) rather than structured activities. Multiple families noted seeing koalas in trees near cabins and the park having "plenty of birdlife." No waterpark, jumping pillow, or organized kids' activities mentioned.

Nature (18/20) — Outstanding natural environment with beach "one minute walk" or "across the road," koalas spotted in park trees, wallabies around the grounds, and "lots of trees." Families praised being "surrounded by plenty of tall trees," "bushy area with many birds," and "breathtaking sunrises" from beach. Walking/cycling paths along coastline to town. "Felt calm and close to nature" is a common theme.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Consistently described as "spotlessly clean," "exceptionally clean," "very clean facilities." New owners (Ros & Steve since 2024) have "completely renovated" amenities. Multiple reviews state "amenities were older but spotless" and facilities "super clean and well maintained." Camp kitchen and bathrooms repeatedly praised for cleanliness.

Value (13/15) — Described as "great value," "reasonably priced," "affordable accommodation for Kangaroo Island," and "best value park on the island." Several reviews note it's a good "value-for-money alternative" to expensive accommodation. However, some felt cabins were "cramped" or "dated" though clean, and one family found a 2-bedroom cabin too small for four people.

Site Size (9/10) — Sites consistently described as "spacious," "large," "roomy," "huge," and "plenty of room." Reviewers noted "big sites," "very spacious," "large flat caravan sites," and "sites large enough that you don't feel squashed in." Grass sites praised. Minor note that "some sites are a bit tight to back into" due to trees.

Sentiment (9/10) — Overwhelmingly positive with hosts Ros and Steve highly praised as "beautiful, friendly, knowledgeable," "amazing humans," "delightful," and "couldn't do enough." High repeat visitation intent with many stating "will definitely be back." One reviewer stayed 6 months, multiple families extended stays. Few negative reviews, mostly pre-new ownership or about cramped cabins.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location "5 mins drive from town centre," "2 minutes from the walking beach," and "centrally located" for island exploration. Close to shops, cafes, supermarket, and ferry. Walking/bike path to Kingscote. Quiet setting yet convenient. Reviewers praised it as "perfect spot to base yourself on KI" and "ideal spot to explore the island."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Very limited on-site children's entertainment facilities - only a basic playground with no jumping pillow, waterpark, games room, or organized kids' activities that families with young children typically seek.
2. Cabin accommodation received mixed feedback with some families finding the 2-bedroom units "disappointingly cramped" (one family of four left after 2 nights), "dated," and with "limited cooking equipment" despite being clean.
3. A handful of pre-2024 reviews mentioned maintenance issues, poor quality mattresses, plumbing problems, and rude staff, suggesting the park had quality concerns before the current ownership took over in late 2024.
