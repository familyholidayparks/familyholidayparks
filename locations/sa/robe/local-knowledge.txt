G'day! The limestone coast around Robe is an absolute cracker for families, with those incredible blue lakes, rock pools teeming with life at low tide, and beaches where the kids can actually swim without you having a heart attack. We reckon April to November is your sweet spot – the summer crowds have thinned out, accommodation prices drop, and you'll still get plenty of sunny days for beach walks and cray spotting. Here's a local tip that'll save you a packet: grab your fresh crayfish straight off the boats at the town jetty rather than paying restaurant prices, then cook them up at your holiday park – the kids will never forget watching those crays come in while the pelicans put on their daily show around 4pm.
