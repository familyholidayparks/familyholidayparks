PARK: Sea Vu Caravan Park
SCORE: 71/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-08
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 137

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Limited organized kids entertainment with one playground mentioned positively by families; no waterpark, games room, or structured activities, though beach access provides natural entertainment for children.
Nature (20/20) — Exceptional beachfront position with direct beach access, spectacular ocean views described as 'million dollar', coastal walking tracks to lighthouse and obelisk, dolphin sightings in the bay, and wildflowers along trails create an outstanding natural environment.
Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many praising 'spotlessly clean' and 'immaculate' amenities, but concerning reports of showers being 'filthy', amenities not cleaned during multi-night stays, and sand management issues in facilities.
Value (13/15) — Generally good value with powered sites at $45 peak season noted as reasonable, families appreciated having 'plenty of cash to explore' Robe, though some felt it was 'overpriced' compared to other parks with better facilities.
Site Size (5/10) — Consistent criticism of 'very tight caravan spots', 'jam packed' sites, difficult reversing access, insufficient space for vehicle parking alongside vans, though beachfront location somewhat compensates.
Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation with many booking return stays, guests love the location and views, but frustration with cramped sites, occasional poor service, and inconsistent cleaning standards prevent universal enthusiasm.
Location (10/10) — Perfect location right on the beach in central Robe, walking distance to shops, cafes, restaurants, bakery, and attractions via beachfront path, easy access to coastal walks and town amenities.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Site size is a major limitation with consistently cramped caravan sites, narrow access roads, and insufficient space for parking vehicles alongside rigs, particularly problematic for families with larger vans.
2. Cleanliness standards are inconsistent, with multiple recent reviews reporting dirty showers, amenities not cleaned during stays, and poor maintenance during peak periods, creating hygiene concerns for families.
3. Lack of on-site kids entertainment beyond a basic playground means no games room, pool, waterpark, or organized activities that family-focused holiday parks typically offer.
