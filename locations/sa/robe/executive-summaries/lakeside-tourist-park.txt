PARK: Lakeside Tourist Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-04-03
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 66

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (6/20) — Reviews show minimal on-site kids entertainment with recent removal of the children's playground during renovations. Multiple families noted "nothing for kids to do except water sports on the lake" and one described the previous playground as "unsafe with an old wooden boat and nettles." The fairy garden received a single positive mention, but playground removal has significantly impacted family entertainment options.

Nature (16/20) — Strong natural setting on the banks of the lake with "beautiful lake views" and sites described as "right on the banks of the lakes." Reviews praise the "peaceful park," "natural setting," and sheltered location compared to the coast. Lake offers water sports opportunities. Minor deductions for occasional odour issues with some noting "lake was a bit pongy" and one describing it as "more like a swamp."

Cleanliness (8/15) — Highly inconsistent cleanliness standards evident across review timeline. Positive recent reviews mention "spotlessly clean cabins" and "amenities were excellent and clean," but multiple negative reports cite uncleaned cabins with "sand and sandy footprints," "hair in bottom of shower," "bird poo" in kitchen areas, dirty ceiling fans, and ant infestations. Under new management, cleanliness appears variable.

Value (11/15) — Mixed value perception with several reviews praising affordability ("super affordable," "reasonable $36," "good value for money") but recent complaints about "over priced" and confusing dynamic pricing where rates varied wildly for similar sites ($55 vs $72). Budget cabins lack hot water. Overall decent value for those with lower expectations.

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised for "spacious" sites, "ample room to move," "large sites," and "very spacious sites." Grassy powered sites by the lake are particularly well-regarded. "Plenty of site room" mentioned multiple times, making this a strength for caravanners.

Sentiment (5/10) — Polarized sentiment with loyal repeat visitors balanced by disappointed recent guests. Some state "always our first place to book" and "will be back again," but multiple recent 1-star reviews from families citing management issues, construction disruption, and poor maintenance. Several explicitly state "wouldn't stay here again" and recommend other Robe parks instead.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location praised as "best location in Robe" with "short walking distance from Robe," "easy walk into town," and proximity to beaches. Sheltered from coastal winds. Close enough to shops and restaurants while maintaining peaceful lakeside setting. Only minor deduction for being slightly out of main town area.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete removal of children's playground during recent renovations has eliminated the primary kids' entertainment facility, with no structured activities or waterpark to compensate - critical for family parks.
2. Significant and recurring cleanliness and maintenance issues across multiple reviews including non-functional laundry facilities (6 of 8 machines out of order), missing toilet seats, no hot water in amenities and cabins, uncleaned accommodations, and general "run down" appearance with construction debris.
3. Management transition causing operational disruptions including major construction during peak family stays, inconsistent pricing policies, poor communication about facility outages, and reports of management being "grumpy when talked to" rather than addressing legitimate concerns.
