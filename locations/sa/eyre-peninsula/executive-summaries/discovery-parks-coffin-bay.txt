PARK: Discovery Parks - Coffin Bay
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-08
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 208

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Reviews consistently note "no playgrounds or jumpy pillows" and "could do with a bigger kids playground." The park offers occasional social events like sausage sizzles, wine tastings, and wood-fired pizza during peak season, plus Easter activities, but lacks permanent kids' entertainment infrastructure like jumping pillows or games rooms that families with young children expect.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural environment with "emus and kangaroos roaming free," "wildlife wonder around daily," and location "right next to the National Park." Families praised "kangaroos everywhere at night," proximity to "Oyster Walk trails along the coast," and being "across the road from the bay." The unique wildlife encounters are a standout feature that "amazed young and old."

Cleanliness (13/15) — Strong majority praised "spotlessly clean facilities," "amenities super clean," and "very clean amenities." However, some recent reviews mentioned "showers have mould," "toilets were dirty," and occasional maintenance issues with blocked sinks. Cleanliness appears generally excellent but with periodic inconsistencies during busy periods.

Value (12/15) — Mixed feedback on value. Off-season pricing praised as "very affordable ($24 per night)," "reasonable rates," and "great winter price." However, peak season drew criticism: "$300 per night" for families, "$50 a night" for basic unpowered sites described as a "rip-off," and "not value for money" during busy periods. Value depends heavily on season and site type.

Site Size (7/10) — Reviews frequently mention "large sites," "spacious drive through van sites," and "big sites," but equally common are complaints about "sites as unlevel as you can really get," "unlevel/uneven is an understatement," and needing "blocks & chocks" for leveling. Site size is adequate but levelness issues significantly impact usability for families with caravans.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong positive sentiment with "will definitely stay here again," "would stay again in a heartbeat," and "we came for 6 days and stayed 6 months." However, this is tempered by concerning negative reviews about rude reception staff, service inconsistency, and families stating "will not be staying here again." Overall sentiment is positive but not overwhelmingly so.

Location (9/10) — Excellent location praised as "perfect spot opposite Oyster HQ," "walking distance to most things," "close to shops," and "easy access to National Park." The beachfront position and proximity to town amenities scored consistently well, with only minor points lost for being slightly removed from major attractions beyond Coffin Bay itself.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of dedicated children's entertainment facilities - no jumping pillows, playgrounds described as basic only, no kids' activities programs, making this unsuitable for families with young children seeking entertainment beyond nature experiences.

2. Significant and consistent site leveling issues with multiple families reporting "unlevel/uneven" sites requiring extensive setup time with blocks and chocks, some even causing damage to vehicles, indicating poor site preparation and allocation practices.

3. Inconsistent customer service with multiple reports of "rude," "grumpy," and "unkind" reception staff, plus concerning incidents like forcing guests with barking dogs to leave while other dogs continued barking, and refusing late checkout requests, suggesting service quality varies significantly depending on which staff members are present.
