PARK: Harbour View Caravan Park
SCORE: 62/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 23

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Limited structured entertainment with a playground mentioned in two reviews; one reviewer specifically noted "great children's play ground" while another described a "trusted family orientated environment" where kids "run free in the park playground." Happy hour at 5pm daily and swimming pool provide basic entertainment, but no waterpark, organized activities, or kids club mentioned.

Nature (16/20) — Excellent natural bush setting with harbour views consistently praised; "beautiful view over the harbour," "peaceful bush surroundings," and kangaroos visible in the mornings. Close to boat ramp (200m), beachside access, and fishing opportunities for crabs, King George Whiting, oysters and squid. Bush setting with "many chirping birds" and lizards, though described as "dry and dusty sites" by some.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with most recent reviews praising cleanliness but one September 2024 cabin review reported "food stuck to the floor, hair on the chairs, unclean dishes, dust." Majority noted "clean amenities," "spotless" facilities, and "super clean amenities, hot showers." Facilities described as "old in need of new but always clean," with one temporary hot water issue noted.

Value (13/15) — Strong value proposition at $35/night for powered sites mentioned multiple times; reviewers noted "Good value" and "one of the best" experiences. One reviewer called it "a hidden gem" though another felt limited pool hours (10am-6pm) reduced value for overnight stays. Overall pricing considered reasonable for the region.

Site Size (10/10) — Consistently excellent ratings for site size; "Massive sites," "super spacious," "Large spread out sites," and "Generous size for powered site" mentioned repeatedly. Sites described as "not too close" with room for boats, trailers, and multiple vehicles. "Plenty of shade" and wind shelter available.

Sentiment (8/10) — Generally positive with many 5-star reviews and repeat visitors, though some concerns about strict check-in times (6pm closure), dated facilities, and distance from town. Recent reviews show improvement under new ownership. Park appears better suited to fishing/boating enthusiasts and repeat guests than casual family tourists.

Location (8/10) — "A few minutes from town" but far enough that biking "is not for the faint hearted" for families. Stone's throw from water with boat ramp 200m away. Quiet location praised but noted as "Sadly to far out of town" by some. Good access to Cowell amenities (bakery, shops, pubs) but isolated feel may not suit all families.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Limited children's entertainment infrastructure - only basic playground mentioned with no organized activities, kids club, jumping pillow, or structured programs that families with young children expect from higher-rated parks.
2. Dated and inconsistent cleanliness standards - facilities described as "old in need of new," one cabin had poor cleaning (food on floor, unclean dishes), and temporary maintenance issues like no hot water in amenities blocks.
3. Location trade-offs for families - distance from town makes bike riding unsafe for children, strict 6pm check-in closure, restrictive pool hours (10am-6pm), and mosquito issues reduce convenience for families seeking easy access and flexibility.
