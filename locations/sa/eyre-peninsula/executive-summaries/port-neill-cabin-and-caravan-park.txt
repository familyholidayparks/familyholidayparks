PARK: Port Neill Cabin and Caravan Park
SCORE: 56/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-01-02
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 25

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (4/20) — Reviews make no mention of any playground, kids club, jumping pillow, or organized activities. One family noted "the boys had a great time squidding off the rocks at the boat ramp" indicating natural activities only. A coffee van visits daily which provides some amenity but no dedicated children's entertainment exists.

Nature (14/20) — Strong coastal location with "easy access to the beach," "short stroll to the beach and Jetty," and "access to beachfront." Reviews mention "good fishing off the Jetty," squidding opportunities, and "lovely walks to be had to the beach or the Wharf area." Park is "nestled in between Cowell, Tumby Bay, and Cleve" for exploring the coastline. Some sites have obstructed water views behind cabins.

Cleanliness (11/15) — Mixed feedback with many noting amenities are "clean and tidy" and "very clean," but consistently described as "old," "tired and in need of a little TLC," and "on the tired side, need upgrading." Recent reviews confirm "clean amenities" though "very old amenities block." Water pressure issues noted when all showers in use.

Value (12/15) — Limited specific pricing feedback but "prices were good" and "good stay, prices were good" mentioned. The park appears budget-friendly for the Eyre Peninsula region, though one customer service complaint about a 10-cent bag charge. Good value for basic coastal access.

Site Size (8/10) — Generally positive with "sites are a great size. Plenty of room," "manicured grass," and "lawned sites." However, some sites described as "undulating & hard to get my van level" and "a little steep back to front so had to be creative when leveling the van." Mix of grass and gravel sites available.

Sentiment (7/10) — Many repeat visitors with "2nd time we have stayed," "Can't wait to come and stay again," and "will return again." However, one recent negative review (Nov 2025) stated "I won't be back there" citing poor customer service. Overall positive but not overwhelmingly enthusiastic for families specifically.

Location (10/10) — Excellent location score with "easy walking distance to Jetty, shop, and pub," "short distance to all in town," and proximity to bowls club with Thursday meals. "Great spot to set up and explore the surrounding coastline" between major towns. Only downside is "extremely poor mobile reception."

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of dedicated children's entertainment facilities (no playground, pool, kids club, or organized activities) makes this unsuitable for families seeking on-site activities for young children.
2. Amenities blocks are consistently described across multiple years as "old," "tired," and "in need of upgrading" despite being clean, indicating deferred maintenance that impacts the family experience.
3. Recent service issues with abrupt staff interactions, site allocation problems, and poor customer service incidents suggest inconsistent management that detracts from the family-friendly experience.
