PARK: Arno Bay Caravan Park
SCORE: 42/100
DATE SCORED: 2024-01-15
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 94

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (8/20) — Limited organized activities with Sunday pancake breakfasts and Saturday happy hour mentioned positively, but no dedicated kids club or structured entertainment. Playground is adjacent to park rather than on-site. Some reviews mention large chess/checkers boards near beach.

Nature (18/20) — Exceptional natural environment with direct beach access, white squeaky sand, jetty right at park corner, and stunning mangrove boardwalk adjacent to property. Reviews consistently praise the beautiful beachside location, turquoise water, and excellent fishing spots. Beach and nature access is the park's strongest feature.

Cleanliness (3/15) — Overwhelming evidence of severe cleanliness issues across multiple years. Reviews consistently report "absolute filth," "disgusting" amenities, camp kitchen "nothing short of DISGUSTING," mould on curtains, cobwebs everywhere, broken toilets, showers not working, and facilities never cleaned during entire stays. Health and safety concerns raised.

Value (9/15) — Mixed feedback with some praising affordability ($35 powered sites) while others report overcharging and poor value given condition. Recent reviews note $310 for 2-bedroom cabin considered expensive for what's provided. Value perception heavily impacted by cleanliness issues.

Site Size (5/10) — Limited specific mentions of site sizing. Some reviews note "small sites," park described as "relatively klein und eng" (small and cramped), with few references to spaciousness. Beachfront sites appear to be the premium option.

Sentiment (5/10) — Highly polarized reviews with loyal repeat visitors contrasting sharply with families leaving early. Many negative reviews from family travellers cite safety/hygiene concerns. Significant number of 1-star reviews from 2024-2026 period indicating deteriorating conditions.

Location (10/10) — Perfect marks for beachfront positioning directly on jetty, walking distance to pub, cafe, mangrove boardwalk, and town amenities. Location consistently rated as the park's best attribute across all reviews.

WHY NOT 100:
Critical cleanliness and maintenance failures dominate recent reviews with families reporting "absolute filth," broken amenities not repaired during multi-week stays, and some leaving early due to unhygienic conditions that reviewers suggest warrant health department attention.

Virtually no organized children's entertainment or activities beyond basic Sunday pancakes, with the adjacent (not on-site) playground being the only kid-specific facility mentioned, making this unsuitable for families seeking dedicated kids programming.

Serious operational concerns including uncleaned facilities for entire stays, non-functional equipment (washing machines, ovens, showers), pest issues (mice, mosquitoes through torn screens), safety hazards (unstable steps, electrical problems), and allegations of overcharging that severely impact family suitability.
