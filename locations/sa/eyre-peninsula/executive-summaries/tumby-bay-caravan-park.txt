PARK: Tumby Bay Caravan Park
SCORE: 74/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 177

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (10/20) — Reviews consistently note the park lacks traditional kids entertainment with families specifically mentioning disappointment over no bouncy pillow or playground. The Friday night pizza van and wine tasting were popular but are adult-focused. One reviewer noted "kids were disappointed there wasn't a bouncy pillow but this just encouraged them to make their own fun."

Nature (18/20) — Excellent beachfront location with the park "right on the beach" and "opposite beach front" mentioned repeatedly. Reviews praise the "lovely walking/bike path into town," foreshore walks, and proximity to water for swimming and fishing. The natural coastal setting and town art trail add to the appeal.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Outstanding cleanliness standards with "spotlessly clean amenities," "super clean bathrooms & laundry," and "meticulously maintained" appearing frequently. A few isolated complaints about dated facilities and one dirty cabin, plus one reviewer noting "grounds maintenance is substandard with dirt and debris" prevent a perfect score.

Value (13/15) — Generally positive value perception with mentions of "cheaper end," "well priced," "good value for money," and "reasonably priced." G'Day Parks discounts noted at $36/night. Two negative reviews complained about $140-190 cabin prices and 3% credit card surcharge, and one mentioned "owners are skimping" on supplies.

Site Size (9/10) — Consistently praised with "large spacious sites," "big sites," "sites are spacious," and accommodation for large rigs (22-26ft vans). A few reviews mentioned sites being "one on top of the other" when full and cramped conditions, preventing full marks.

Sentiment (9/10) — Very positive overall with high return intention: "will definitely stay here again," "we'll be back," "stay here every time we are in tumby." Strong recommendation rate. A handful of negative reviews about dirty cabins, hot water issues, and maintenance complaints prevent full marks.

Location (10/10) — Perfect marks for location with "great location" appearing throughout reviews. Walking distance to town (1-1.5km), "easy walk to the pub," "close to beach," and positioned on the Eyre Peninsula touring route. Access to shops, bakery, and jetty all praised.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Complete absence of children's entertainment facilities - no jumping pillow, no playground, no pool or waterpark, forcing families to rely entirely on beach and external activities for kids.
2. Mixed cabin quality with specific complaints about dirty accommodation, dated furnishings, hair clumps, unusable kettles, scratchy towels, and tiny bathrooms that don't match website photos.
3. Inconsistent maintenance standards with several reviews noting grounds covered in dirt and debris, some sites near sewage tanks with foul odors, and sandy/dusty conditions rather than grass.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"right on the beach," "spotlessly clean amenities," "kids were disappointed there wasn't a bouncy pillow," "mainly with young families," "very friendly and helpful staff," "large spacious sites," "Friday night wood oven pizzas," "cabin was dirty"
