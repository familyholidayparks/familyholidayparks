G'day! The Eyre Peninsula is where the rugged Australian outback tumbles straight into some of the clearest turquoise waters you'll find anywhere – and trust me, watching your kids swim with sea lions at Baird Bay or spot southern right whales from the clifftops is pure magic that'll have them talking for years. We reckon May to October is your sweet spot for visiting, when the weather's mild and the whales are putting on a show, though summer's brilliant if you're chasing those iconic beach days. Here's a local tip most visitors miss: grab your fresh seafood straight from the boats at Coffin Bay or Ceduna in the early morning, because the holiday parks here have fantastic BBQ facilities, and there's nothing quite like cooking up King George whiting you've watched being unloaded an hour earlier.
