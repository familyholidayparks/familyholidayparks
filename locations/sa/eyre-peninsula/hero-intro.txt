# Eyre Peninsula Family Holiday

The Eyre Peninsula offers everything families need for a genuinely great beach holiday without the crowds. The coastline stretches for hundreds of kilometres with pristine sandy beaches perfect for swimming, rock pooling and building sandcastles, while the sheltered bays are ideal for younger kids. Towns like Port Lincoln and Coffin Bay provide solid infrastructure with good cafes, fish and chip shops, and plenty of accommodation options including family-friendly caravan parks like Port Lincoln Tourist Park, Port Lincoln Caravan Park, Tumby Bay Caravan Park, Venus Bay Beachfront Tourist Park, and Discovery Parks – Coffin Bay. You'll find everything you need without the hassle of travelling too far between spots.

Beyond the beaches, the Peninsula delivers genuine natural experiences that kids actually engage with. Seal colonies, sea lion encounters and excellent wildlife spotting opportunities keep children entertained and curious, while the relatively compact geography means you can cover serious ground on a week's holiday. The national parks and coastal trails offer manageable walks for mixed-ability families, and the quieter vibe means less stress than major tourist destinations. It's a place where you can genuinely relax while the kids tire themselves out exploring rock pools, spotting wildlife and running on empty beaches.
