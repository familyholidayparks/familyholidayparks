G'day! The Barossa Valley might be famous for its shiraz, but families will find this stunning region offers so much more than cellar doors – think sprawling parks perfect for picnics, bakeries selling enormous vanilla slices that'll fuel your little explorers, and farm-gate experiences where kids can meet alpacas and sample fresh produce. Autumn (March to May) is absolutely magic here, when the vine leaves turn golden and the weather's perfect for outdoor adventures without the summer scorchers. My top tip? Stop at the Apex Bakery in Tanunda early morning before it sells out – grab a kitchener bun for yourself and let the kids choose from the pastry wall, then enjoy breakfast at the nearby Tanunda Soldiers Memorial Garden where there's plenty of space for them to burn off that sugar rush.
