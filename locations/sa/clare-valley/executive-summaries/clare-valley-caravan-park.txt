PARK: Clare Valley Caravan Park
SCORE: 58/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 200

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (2/20) — Reviews explicitly state the park has "no good if you have children," "not a lot for the kids to do," "no playground," and is "designed for adults." Multiple reviews confirm this is an adults-focused park with zero children's entertainment, no jumping castles, and no kids' facilities.

Nature (16/20) — Reviews praise the "beautiful lush park," location "nestled between vineyards," "backs onto a winery," and proximity to the "Riesling Trail" for cycling. Guests describe "lovely green grass," "beautiful surroundings," and being able to "wake up to the sound of birds chirping." The natural wine-country setting scores well.

Cleanliness (14/15) — Overwhelming praise for cleanliness: "spotless amenities," "immaculate small park," "amenities extremely clean," "absolutely spotless," "cleanest amenities you'll find anywhere." One review noted "dated" facilities and another mentioned insufficient drainage, preventing a perfect score.

Value (12/15) — Generally positive with comments like "reasonably priced," "almost half the cost of the other park," "winter rate of $35 per night," and "standard of service relative the price charged is outstanding." However, one review complained of "well over $200 per night" for a cabin, and the lack of family facilities limits value for families.

Site Size (8/10) — Reviews mention "nice size sites," "ample sized sites," "generous size sites," "large sites," and "really good sized caravan pitches." One negative review stated "sites are not level" and parking for towing vehicles is "a bit tight," preventing a higher score.

Sentiment (9/10) — Extremely positive sentiment with high recommendation rates: "we will be back," "stayed for 3 years in a row," "extended our stay," "best park of our trip." Multiple guests describe it as "one of the best parks we have stayed." A few negative reviews about check-in rules, electrical safety concerns, and rude service prevent a perfect score.

Location (10/10) — Perfect location scores with reviews praising "heart of the wineries," "walking distance to wineries," "close to the Riesling Trail," "perfectly located," "ideally located," and "location is second to none." The park is well-positioned for wine tourism and cycling.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Zero family facilities—the park explicitly lacks playgrounds, pools, water features, or any children's entertainment, making it completely unsuitable for families with young children as stated in multiple reviews.
2. Some operational concerns including inflexible 6pm check-in rules that caused frustration, one serious electrical safety complaint dismissed by owners, and reports of rude service when booking.
3. Dated amenities and minor infrastructure issues including "older facilities," insufficient toilets/showers for peak times, unlevel sites that become "swamp when it rained," and no drainage at grass bays.

NOTABLE REVIEW PHRASES:
"no good if you have children," "not a lot for the kids to do," "spotless amenities," "immaculate small park," "walking distance to wineries," "designed for adults as there is no play ground," "friendly and accommodating," "ideally located for seeing what the Clare has to offer"
