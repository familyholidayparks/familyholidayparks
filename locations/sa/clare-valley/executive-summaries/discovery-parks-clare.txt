PARK: Discovery Parks - Clare
SCORE: 72/100
DATE SCORED: 2026-05-24
REVIEWS ANALYSED: 181

WHY THIS SCORE:
Entertainment (17/20) — Exceptional family facilities with two pools (one heated), splash park with water slides, enormous jumping pillow, pump track, playground, basketball court, and regular organized activities including movie nights, craft sessions, treasure hunts, colour runs, paint and sip, and live music events that consistently delight families with young children.

Nature (15/20) — Beautiful setting among gum trees with abundant kookaburras and birdlife, lovely landscaping, and direct access to the famous Riesling Trail for walking and cycling; surrounded by nature yet close to Clare Valley wineries, though on highway location detracts from pure natural experience.

Cleanliness (10/15) — Highly inconsistent standards with many complaints about dirty cabins (hair in sheets, dust on fans, filthy couches, mouldy showers), amenities not cleaned regularly during busy periods, poor shower pressure, and recurring hot water issues, though some guests report spotless facilities.

Value (10/15) — Mixed value perception with pricing described as "overpriced" and "expensive for what you get" particularly for cramped cabins and basic sites at $60+ per night, though entertainment facilities and wine region location justify cost for some families.

Site Size (6/10) — Consistently criticized as "packed in like sardines," "very tight spaces," "sites stacked next to each other," with many struggling to fit medium caravans and limited room around beds in cabins, though drive-through sites receive better marks.

Sentiment (8/10) — Strong repeat visitation and enthusiastic family reviews ("best caravan park in Australia," "kids had an absolute ball") balanced by serious complaints about cleanliness, wasps, shower pressure, and some families leaving early or avoiding return visits.

Location (6/10) — Well-positioned for Clare Valley wineries with some walking distance, adjacent to tourism centre, and 3km from town, but right on busy highway with truck noise, and distance from town criticized by some guests without vehicles.

WHY NOT 100:
1. Chronic shower pressure and hot water problems reported across multiple years with "trickle of water," "no water pressure," and cold showers during peak times that management failed to adequately address despite widespread complaints.
2. Unacceptable cleanliness inconsistency with cabins containing hair-filled sheets, dandruff, filthy couches, mouldy showers, dusty ceiling fans, and dirty kitchen utensils that fall well below Discovery Parks standards.
3. Severely cramped site spacing with caravans "stacked next to each other" and cabin bedrooms where guests "walk sideways to get into bed," plus dangerous uncontrolled wasp infestations that resulted in child injuries.
