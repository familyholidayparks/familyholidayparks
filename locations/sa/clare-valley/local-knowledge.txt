G'day! Clare Valley might be famous for its Riesling, but families will fall head over heels for the Riesling Trail – that brilliant 35km cycling path where even your little ones can pedal safely from town to town, stopping for bakery treats along the way. The cooler months from April to October are absolutely magic here, with perfect weather for bike rides and those stunning autumn colours painting the vineyards gold and red. Here's an insider tip: grab breakfast at Seed Winehouse in Sevenhill before you start your ride – they've got a gorgeous lawn where the kids can run around, and the staff are always happy to fill up your water bottles for the trail ahead.
