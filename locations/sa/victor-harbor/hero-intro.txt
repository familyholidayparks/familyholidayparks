Planning a family trip to Victor Harbor? We've done the legwork to find the best family holiday parks in Victor Harbor, so you can skip hours of research and get straight to the good part. Families love Victor Harbor because the kids can watch horse-drawn trams clip-clop along the causeway to Granite Island, spot little penguins waddling back to their burrows at sunset, and pat stingrays in the shallows at the beach—it's that rare spot where toddlers and tweens are equally entertained.
