G'day! Victor Harbor and the surrounding Fleurieu Peninsula is where my own family escapes when we need that perfect mix of coastal adventures and proper creature comforts – the kids can spot southern right whales from the clifftops between May and October, then burn off energy at the horse-drawn tram to Granite Island. We've found late spring (October-November) absolutely magic here, with mild weather, wildflowers in full bloom, and the school holiday crowds still weeks away. Here's an insider tip: grab fish and chips from Bay Fish Shop on Albert Place, then head to the grassy reserve at Fisherman's Bay where the kids can feed the (very pushy!) seagulls while you watch the sun set behind Granite Island – it's become our non-negotiable tradition every Victor Harbor trip.
